EXT. EARTH SURFACE - DAY

LUCKY stands on the desolate surface, the polluted air a constant reminder of humanity's fall. The ruins of the city stretch before him, a maze of collapsed buildings and twisted rebar. The distant, rhythmic CLANKING of patrol bots echoes through the silence.

He knows this is just the beginning. Escaping the core was one challenge, but surviving on the surface, a world ravaged by robot wars, is another entirely. He needs to find resources, avoid detection, and somehow, find a way to fight back.

His eyes scan the horizon, a flicker of determination in their depths. He is no longer just a survivor; he is a man with a purpose. The escape from the core has given him a second chance, and he intends to seize it.
