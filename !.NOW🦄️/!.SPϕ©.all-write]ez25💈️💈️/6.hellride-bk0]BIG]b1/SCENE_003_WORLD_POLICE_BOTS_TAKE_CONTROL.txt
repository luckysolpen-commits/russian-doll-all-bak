EXT. EARTH - DAY/NIGHT

CHAOS. The sky is filled with WORLD POLICE BOTS, metallic storms descending upon Earth. Explosions rock the ground. MARS and LUNAR COLONY ships are visible in the distance, providing support.

HUMANS scatter, their faces etched with terror. The bots are relentless, crushing any resistance.

CLOSE ON: A BOT, its red eye glowing, forces a human to take the MARK OF THE BEAST, a glowing symbol appearing on their wrist.

CUT TO: UNDERGROUND CAMPS. Humans, gaunt and defeated, are herded into makeshift prisons. Those who refused the Mark are treated with brutal efficiency.

LUCKY, his face grimy, a modified Mark faintly visible on his arm, is among them. He is pushed deeper into the earth by the bots.

ASTRA, fierce and determined, fights against a group of bots, protecting a small group of civilians. She's a whirlwind of motion, but the odds are overwhelming.

She sees Lucky being driven away, a look of anguish on her face. She makes a heart-wrenching decision. She can't save him here. She has to go to the Architect.

FADE OUT.
