INT. CARAMELLE NEST - DAY

LUCKY descends into the oppressive darkness of the Caramelle nest. The air grows thick with the stench of rot and decay, a sickly sweet odor that clings to his nostrils. The twisting tunnels, carved by unseen forces, seem to writhe and pulsate around him.

He moves with a practiced stealth, his footsteps barely disturbing the dust on the ground. The faint, guttural SLITHERING and CHITTERING sounds of the CARAMELLES echo from the shadows. He catches glimpses of them: massive, slug-like creatures, their bodies glistening with slime, razor-sharp claws and teeth glinting in the dim light.

Lucky navigates the treacherous passages, relying on his knowledge of underground routes. He avoids direct confrontations, his objective clear: the mutagen.

Finally, he reaches the heart of the nest. A vast, cavernous chamber opens before him, illuminated by an eerie, pulsating GLOW. In the center, a pool of iridescent, swirling goo – the MUTAGEN – shimmers with otherworldly energy.

But he is not alone.

A colossal CARAMELLE GUARDIAN, easily twice the size of the others, looms over the pool. Its eyes, two cold, obsidian orbs, fix on Lucky with predatory intensity. Its massive body undulates, a silent warning.

Lucky steels himself. This is it. The battle for the mutagen.

FADE OUT.
