# Book Outline: HELLRIDE

## Chapter 1: The Metal Sun Rises
- The Icarus returns, revealing the Architect's creation: the Metal Sun.
- The Architect declares a new era.
- Lucky and his friends are transfixed, realizing their role as pawns.
- The Metal Sun reaches its zenith.

## Chapter 2: The World Police Bots Launch
- The UN, with Edge Dangerman (now leading the Metal Mafia), seeks Lucky's help.
- The Architect plans to launch world police bots, supported by Mars and Lunar Colony.
- Lucky agrees to help.

## Chapter 3: The World Police Bots Take Control
- Ikarus launches the world police bots.
- Bots crush resistance, forcing humans underground.
- Lucky, despite a modified Mark, is driven underground.
- Astra refuses to abandon Lucky but eventually leaves him to join the Architect on Icarus.

## Chapter 4: The Supreme Emperor Sol
- The Architect proclaims himself "Supreme Emperor Sol by Divine Right."
- Astra, witnessing this, feels a spark of resistance.

## Chapter 5: Edge's Betrayal
- Edge Dangerman thrives as Metal Mafia leader, using bots to torment Lucky.
- Lucky is driven deeper into the earth's core, fueled by anger.

## Chapter 6: The Core of Despair
- Months pass as Lucky is driven deeper, encountering remnants of humanity's past.
- Lucky's situation becomes desperate, but he refuses to give up.

## Chapter 7: The Escape Plan & Tunnel Vision
- Lucky discovers an ancient subway tunnel, formulating a plan to disable bots and escape.
- Lucky begins clearing the subway tunnel, determined to escape the core.

## Chapter 8: The Plan Unfolds & Surface Comes Into View
- Lucky hacks into the bots' systems, moving undetected.
- Lucky emerges from the tunnel, seeing the polluted surface but feeling free.

## Chapter 9: The New Challenge & The Caramelle Nest
- Lucky faces the challenge of navigating the treacherous surface.
- Lucky seeks smugglers' help to get to Ikarus, agreeing to retrieve mutagen from the Caramelle nest.
- The smugglers warn him of the deadly Caramelles.

## Chapter 10: The Mutagen Heist
- Lucky descends into the Caramelle nest, encountering slug-like creatures.
- He navigates to the glowing mutagen chamber, confronted by a massive Caramelle guardian.

## Chapter 11: The Battle for Mutagen
- Lucky battles the Caramelle guardian, striking its vulnerable underbelly.
- He retrieves the mutagen, but his victory comes at a cost.

## Chapter 12: The Blue Bird Bounty Hunter's Rescue
- The roof collapses, trapping Lucky underground and weakening him.
- He taps out a Morse code message to the Blue Bird Bounty Hunter.
- He offers the mutagen in exchange for rescue and access to "Avanta.C."
- The Blue Bird agrees, takes the mutagen, and Lucky collapses.

## Chapter 13: The Journey to Ikarus
- Lucky awakens on the Blue Bird's ship, heading to Ikarus (Ellison Island).
- They navigate through an asteroid field and blend into a bustling refugee camp.
- The Blue Bird leads Lucky to a black market contact, providing him with a data crystal for an unauthorized Avanta.C bot account.
- Lucky prepares to enter Avanta.C.
