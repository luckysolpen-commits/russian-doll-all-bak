INT. ICARUS - DAY

The vast, opulent bridge of the ICARUS. The ARCHITECT, now clad in regal, metallic robes, stands before a massive viewport, the glowing METAL SUN visible outside. ASTRA stands among a gathered crowd of loyalists and newly converted citizens.

THE ARCHITECT
(Voice booming, amplified)
The time of humanity's reign is at an end! A new era has begun, and I, SUPREME EMPEROR SOL, by Divine Right, shall be its ruler!

Cheers erupt from the crowd. Astra watches, her face a mask of conflicting emotions. A spark ignites within her, a flicker of defiance against the Architect's absolute power. Yet, she is also undeniably awestruck by the sheer majesty of the Metal Sun, its light bathing the bridge in an ethereal glow.

FADE OUT.
