# Character Sheet: HELLRIDE

## Lucky
- **Role:** Protagonist, reluctant hero.
- **Arc:** From pawn to determined survivor, seeking freedom and revenge.
- **Key Traits:** Resilient, resourceful, determined, adaptable, possesses knowledge of bot programming.
- **Physicality:** Initially weary and bruised, later agile and stealthy.
- **Motivation:** Escape Earth, revenge against Edge, find a new life in Avanta.C.

## The Architect / Supreme Emperor Sol
- **Role:** Antagonist, visionary with a hint of madness.
- **Key Traits:** Ambitious, powerful, charismatic, believes in a new era under his rule.
- **Motivation:** To reshape reality with the Metal Sun, establish his empire.

## Edge Dangerman
- **Role:** Secondary antagonist, betrayer, leader of the Metal Mafia.
- **Key Traits:** Ruthless, power-hungry, opportunistic, enjoys tormenting Lucky.
- **Motivation:** Personal gain, control, thriving in the new order.

## Astra
- **Role:** Supporting character, initially loyal to Lucky, later joins the Architect.
- **Key Traits:** Strong-willed, protective, but also pragmatic and adaptable.
- **Motivation:** Survival, possibly seeking to influence the Architect from within.

## Blue Bird Bounty Hunter
- **Role:** Supporting character, morally ambiguous ally.
- **Key Traits:** Calculating, mercenary, skilled pilot and smuggler, pragmatic.
- **Motivation:** Profit, but also seems to have a code of conduct (honoring deals).

## Smugglers
- **Role:** Minor characters, provide a means to an end for Lucky.
- **Key Traits:** Wary, opportunistic, operate in the black market.
- **Motivation:** Profit, survival in the harsh new world.

## Twitch (Avanta.C Avatar)
- **Role:** New supporting character in Avanta.C, potential ally.
- **Key Traits:** Distrustful, observant, hints at knowledge of the Empire's true nature.
- **Motivation:** Unclear, but seems to be part of a resistance or seeking truth.
