INT. SUBWAY TUNNEL - DAY

LUCKY, a man reborn with purpose, works tirelessly in the ancient subway tunnel. He uses makeshift tools, pieces of rebar and broken concrete, to clear the rubble and debris.

Dust motes dance in the weak beam of his headlamp, illuminating the sheer scale of the task. The tunnel is choked with centuries of neglect, collapsed sections, and the detritus of a forgotten world.

His muscles ache, sweat streaks his grimy face, but his eyes burn with an unyielding determination. Every dislodged rock, every cleared section, is a step closer to freedom.

He pauses, catching his breath, and looks down the long, dark expanse of the tunnel. It's a perilous journey, he knows, fraught with unknown dangers. But the thought of escaping the core, of seeing the sky again, propels him forward.

FADE OUT.
