INT. CARAMELLE NEST - DAY

Dust and debris rain down as the cavern ceiling GROANS, then COLLAPSES. LUCKY, mutagen container clutched in his hand, is caught in the cascade of rock and earth. He cries out as he's buried, the weight crushing him.

Silence. Then, a faint, desperate SCRAPING. Lucky, barely conscious, struggles to free himself. His body screams in protest, every muscle weak and trembling. He's trapped, going weaker by the second.

He remembers the BLUE BIRD BOUNTY HUNTER, the fleeting encounter on the surface. His only hope. With immense effort, he finds a small, exposed piece of metal from a collapsed pipe. He begins to tap, a slow, agonizing rhythm.

LUCKY (V.O.)
(. . . - . . . - . . . - . . .)
(Morse code for S.O.S.)

Minutes stretch into an eternity. Just as darkness threatens to consume him, a faint HUM grows louder. A shaft of light pierces the gloom, cutting through the dust.

The BLUE BIRD BOUNTY HUNTER descends, her sleek form silhouetted against the light. She lands gracefully amidst the rubble, her gaze sharp, assessing the wreckage and Lucky's dire state.

BLUE BIRD
Well, well, Lucky. Looks like you've found yourself in quite the pickle.

Lucky, barely able to speak, holds up the mutagen container.

LUCKY
(Hoarse, weak)
Mutagen... for rescue... Avanta.C...

The Blue Bird's eyes narrow, a calculating glint in them. She looks at the mutagen, then back at Lucky. She knows the value of what he holds.

BLUE BIRD
Avanta.C, huh? You're desperate. And you're offering me a fortune.

She extends a hand, not to help him, but to take the mutagen. Lucky, with his last ounce of strength, pushes the container towards her. She snatches it, her grip firm.

BLUE BIRD
Alright, Lucky. You've got a deal. Anything to get off this rock, right? It's a wasteland of robot wars now.

As she speaks, she activates a small device on her wrist. A faint, almost imperceptible HUM fills the air. Lucky feels a strange sensation, a pulling, a fading.

His vision blurs. He collapses, unconscious, as the Blue Bird Bounty Hunter, mutagen in hand, prepares to extract her prize and him from the collapsing nest.

FADE OUT.
