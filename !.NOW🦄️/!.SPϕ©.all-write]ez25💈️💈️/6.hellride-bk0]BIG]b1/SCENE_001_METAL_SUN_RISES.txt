EXT. SPACE - DAY

The vast, inky blackness of space. Stars twinkle like distant diamonds.

Suddenly, a blinding light illuminates the horizon. The METAL SUN, a swirling vortex of light and energy, begins to rise.

The ICARUS, a colossal spaceship, appears, its metal hull pulsing with an otherworldly energy.

THE ARCHITECT, a figure of immense pride and a hint of madness, stands tall beside his creation.

THE ARCHITECT
(Voice trembling with emotion, amplified across the solar system)
Behold! I present to you... the Metal Sun! A marvel of human ingenuity, forged from the very fabric of space itself!

Across the solar system, INHABITANTS gaze upon the spectacle in awestruck silence.

LUCKY and his friends stand transfixed, their minds reeling at the limitless possibilities, yet a sense of dread washes over them. They are but pawns in a much larger game.

The Metal Sun continues its ascent, its power growing with each passing moment. The solar system trembles.

In an instant of pure chaos, the Metal Sun reaches its zenith, casting a blinding light that seems to pierce the very soul of the universe.

The Architect's plan is complete. Nothing will ever be the same again.

FADE OUT.
