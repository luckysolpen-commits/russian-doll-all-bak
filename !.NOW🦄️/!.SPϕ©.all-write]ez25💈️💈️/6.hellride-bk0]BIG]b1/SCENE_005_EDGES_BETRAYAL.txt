INT. UNDERGROUND - DAY

Dark, damp tunnels. The air is thick with the smell of damp earth and stale metal. LUCKY, bruised and weary, stumbles through the labyrinthine passages, constantly looking over his shoulder.

His comm unit crackles to life. EDGE DANGERMAN's voice, smug and triumphant, echoes through the confined space.

EDGE DANGERMAN (O.S.)
You should have stayed out of this, Lucky. Now you're just a pawn in my game.

Lucky seethes with anger. He can hear the distant hum of WORLD POLICE BOTS, their presence a constant threat. They are driving him deeper, relentlessly, into the earth's core.

Edge's laughter fills the comm unit, a cruel, mocking sound.

EDGE DANGERMAN (O.S.)
I'm thriving down here, Lucky. The Metal Mafia is stronger than ever. And you? You're just digging your own grave.

Lucky clenches his fists, his determination hardening. Revenge. That's all that matters now.

FADE OUT.
