INT. SMUGGLER'S HIDEOUT - NIGHT

A dimly lit, smoky room. The air is thick with the smell of stale synth-ale and desperation. A group of rough-looking SMUGGLERS, their faces scarred and wary, huddle around a flickering holographic map.

LUCKY, his clothes still grimy from the underground, stands before them. He looks out of place, but his gaze is steady.

LUCKY
I need passage to Ikarus. And I need it fast.

One of the smugglers, a burly man with a cybernetic eye, grunts.

SMUGGLER 1
Ikarus ain't cheap, kid. And it ain't easy. The EMPRIE's got eyes everywhere.

LUCKY
I know. That's why I'm here. I heard you're the best at getting past their eyes.

Another smuggler, a wiry woman with a wicked grin, leans forward.

SMUGGLER 2
Best costs. What you got to offer?

Lucky pauses, then meets their gazes.

LUCKY
Mutagen. From the Caramelle nest.

The room falls silent. The smugglers exchange uneasy glances. The cybernetic-eyed smuggler finally speaks, his voice low.

SMUGGLER 1
You think you can handle that? The Caramelle nest is no joke. Those creatures are deadly.

LUCKY
I've been underground before. I know what it takes.

The smugglers look at each other, a silent conversation passing between them. Finally, the wiry woman nods.

SMUGGLER 2
Alright, we'll give you the job. Bring us the mutagen, and we'll get you to Ikarus. But don't come back empty-handed.

Lucky nods, a grim determination settling on his face. He knows what he has to do.

FADE OUT.
