INT. SUBWAY TUNNEL - DAY

The tunnel is now largely clear. LUCKY moves with a newfound stealth, his movements precise and calculated. He carries a jury-rigged device, a collection of scavenged wires and circuit boards.

He approaches a section of the tunnel where the faint hum of a WORLD POLICE BOT is audible. He presses his device against a conduit, a series of complex calculations flashing across a small, makeshift screen.

With a soft WHIRR, the hum of the bot changes, then fades. Lucky grins, a flash of his old self. He has hacked into the bot's systems, disabling its sensors. He is now effectively invisible to them.

He continues his journey, passing abandoned trains, their metal skeletons rusting in the darkness. The once-thriving cityscape, now buried deep, feels like a ghost.

But Lucky doesn't dwell on the past. His focus is entirely on the present, on the escape. He is determined to see it through, to reclaim his life.

FADE OUT.
