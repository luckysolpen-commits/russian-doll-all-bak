INT. AVANTA.C INTERFACE - DAY

Lucky stands before a shimmering, ethereal interface. Millions of AVATAR options swirl around him, a kaleidoscope of forms, colors, and styles. From sleek cybernetic beings to fantastical creatures, from hyper-realistic humans to abstract constructs, the choices are overwhelming.

He reaches out, his hand passing through the holographic projections. Each avatar pulses with a unique energy, a potential new identity.

LUCKY
(To himself)
A new life. A new me.

He scrolls through categories: Warrior, Hacker, Diplomat, Artist. He sees avatars that are clearly designed for combat, others for stealth, some for pure aesthetic pleasure.

He pauses on one. A lean, agile figure, cloaked in shadows, with eyes that glow with an inner light. It feels right. A reflection of his journey, his resilience, his hidden strength.

He selects it. The interface shimmers, then solidifies. The chosen avatar stands before him, a perfect digital representation of his new self.

FADE OUT.
