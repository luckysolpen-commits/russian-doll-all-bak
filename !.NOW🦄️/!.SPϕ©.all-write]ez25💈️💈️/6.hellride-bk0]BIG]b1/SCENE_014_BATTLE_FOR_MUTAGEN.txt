INT. CARAMELLE NEST - DAY

LUCKY charges forward, a blur of motion against the monstrous CARAMELLE GUARDIAN. The creature roars, a sound that vibrates through the very rock of the cavern, and its razor-sharp claws lash out.

Lucky dodges, a practiced dance of survival. He weaves between the guardian's massive limbs, his eyes fixed on its vulnerable underbelly. Smaller CARAMELLES, drawn by the commotion, slither from the shadows, snapping at his heels.

He parries a sweeping claw with a scavenged piece of rebar, the metal groaning under the impact. He uses the momentum to spin, delivering a powerful kick to the guardian's knee. The creature stumbles, a low growl rumbling in its throat.

The battle is a brutal ballet of agility against brute force. Lucky leaps, rolls, and strikes, his movements precise and economical. He takes hits, grunting as a glancing blow sends him skidding across the slimy floor, but he always recovers, always pushes forward.

He sees his opening. With a desperate surge of adrenaline, Lucky launches himself, aiming for the soft, pulsating flesh beneath the guardian's armored hide. His fist connects with a sickening CRUNCH.

The Caramelle Guardian lets out a deafening SCREECH, its massive body convulsing. It thrashes wildly, its claws flailing, tearing at the cavern walls. Lucky, despite his exhaustion, scrambles towards the glowing pool of MUTAGEN.

He plunges his hand into the iridescent goo, scooping a generous amount into a sealed container he had prepared. The mutagen pulses with a strange, warm energy in his grasp.

He glances back at the guardian, which is slowly, agonizingly, collapsing. Its victory came at a cost, but the mutagen is his.

FADE OUT.
