The Chateau Tivoli was a house of stories. It was
a house that had seen it all, from the grandest
of celebrations to the most intimate of sorrows. It
was a house that had welcomed saints and sinners,
poets and politicians, lovers and dreamers. It was a
house that had absorbed all of their stories, all
of their secrets, all of their ghosts. And now,
it was about to absorb three more.

Three strangers, from three different worlds, brought together by
fate, by chance, by the mysterious workings of the
universe. A famous actress, a brilliant tech mogul, a
reclusive author. They had all come to the Chateau
Tivoli seeking something, something they had lost, something
they had never had, something they couldn't even name.
They had come seeking refuge, seeking a sanctuary, seeking
a place where they could be safe, where they
could be anonymous, where they could be themselves.

But the Chateau Tivoli was not just a place
of refuge; it was also a place of transformation,
a place where secrets were revealed, where truths were
confronted, where lives were changed. It was a house
that had a way of peeling back the layers,
of stripping away the masks, of forcing people to
confront the truth of who they were.

And at the heart of it all was Madame
Dupont, the woman who was its owner, its proprietor,
its guardian, its soul. She was a woman of
immense grace and charm, a woman who had a
knack for making everyone feel at home, for making
everyone feel as if they were the most important
person in the world. She was a woman who
had a deep and abiding love for the house,
for its history, for its ghosts, for its magic.
And she was a woman who knew that the
Chateau Tivoli was more than just a business; it
was a calling, a sacred trust, a legacy that
she was determined to preserve and to pass on
to future generations.

As the first day of the new season came
to a close, a sense of anticipation hung in
the air, a feeling that something momentous was about
to happen. The stage was set. The players were
in place. The curtain was about to rise on
a new and exciting drama, a drama that would
be filled with secrets and lies, with mystery and
intrigue, with the unique and unforgettable magic of the
Chateau Tivoli.

The goings-on at the Chateau Tivoli had begun.
And as the city of San Francisco slept, the
grand old house on Steiner Street kept watch, its
windows like eyes looking out into the night, waiting
for the stories to unfold.
