 What a delightful idea! I'm excited to help you create an outline
for your novel. Here's a possible structure for "The Goings-On at
the Chateau Tivoli B&B (A Duckling Novel)" from the perspective 
of the owner, who lives in the 10th room:

**Chapter 1: Introduction**

* Introduce the protagonist, Madame Dupont, owner and proprietor 
of the Chateau Tivoli B&B
* Describe the majestic home at 1057 Steiner St, San Francisco, 
CA
* Set the tone for the story: a charming, yet slightly 
eccentric, establishment where anything can happen

**Chapter 2: The First Guests Arrive**

* Introduce the first group of guests, including a famous 
actress, a tech mogul, and a reclusive author
* Show Madame Dupont's excitement and nervousness as she prepares
for their arrival
* Hint at the secrets and mysteries that each guest brings with 
them

**Chapter 3: The First Mysterious Occurrence**

* One of the guests (perhaps the actress) has a mysterious item 
stolen from her room
* Madame Dupont becomes concerned and decides to investigate
* Introduce a cast of quirky characters, including the B&B's 
staff, neighbors, and local authorities

**Chapter 4: The Guests' Secrets Unfurl**

* As the days go by, each guest's secrets begin to surface:
	+ The tech mogul has a hidden agenda for his visit
	+ The reclusive author is hiding a famous literary reputation
	+ The actress has a secret that could ruin her career
* Madame Dupont tries to keep everything under wraps while still 
providing exceptional service to her guests

**Chapter 5: A Mysterious Room Appears**

* One of the rooms in the B&B seems to have magically disappeared
* Guests start to get spooked, and rumors spread about the 
haunted house
* Madame Dupont is determined to find out what's going on and put
everyone's minds at ease

**Chapter 6: The Mystery Deepens**

* More mysterious events occur:
	+ A guest finds a cryptic message in their room
	+ A valuable item goes missing from the B&B's collection
	+ Strange noises are heard throughout the night
* Madame Dupont becomes convinced that something sinister is at 
play and decides to take matters into her own hands

**Chapter 7: The Investigation Heats Up**

* Madame Dupont enlists the help of a local detective, who has 
his own secrets and motives
* Together, they start to piece together clues and interview 
guests
* Suspects emerge, but nothing seems to add up

**Chapter 8: A Twist Revealed**

* Just when it seems like everything is going smoothly, a major 
twist is revealed:
	+ One of the guests has a hidden connection to the B&B's past
	+ The reclusive author's true identity is exposed
	+ The tech mogul's true intentions are revealed

**Chapter 9: Confrontation and Chaos**

* Tensions boil over as secrets are spilled, and confrontations 
ensue:
	+ Guests turn on each other
	+ Madame Dupont must intervene to keep the peace
	+ The mysterious room reappears, revealing a shocking truth

**Chapter 10: The Truth Revealed**

* With all cards on the table, the guests must confront their own
secrets and lies
* Madame Dupont reveals her own connection to the B&B's past and 
her reasons for running the establishment
* A surprising revelation about the house itself adds a new layer
of complexity

**Chapter 11: The Aftermath**

* The guests depart, each with a newfound appreciation for 
honesty and humility
* Madame Dupont reflects on what she has learned from the 
experience:
	+ The importance of trust and communication
	+ The power of secrets to tear people apart
	+ The value of forgiveness and moving forward

**Chapter 12: A New Beginning**

* As the guests depart, Madame Dupont looks to the future:
	+ She decides to make changes to the B&B to create a more welco
welcoming atmosphere
	+ She begins to rebuild relationships with her guests and the l
local community
	+ She sets her sights on new challenges and adventures

**Chapter 13: A Final Surprise**

* Just as Madame Dupont thinks everything is back to normal, one 
final surprise awaits:
	+ A mysterious package arrives at the B&B
	+ It contains a cryptic message or a hidden clue that hints at 
more mysteries to come
	+ The door opens for new adventures and possibilities

And that's the outline! I hope this gives you a good starting 
point for your novel. Remember, feel free to add, change, or 
modify elements as you see fit to suit your creative vision. Good
luck with your writing! 🦆️🦙️
