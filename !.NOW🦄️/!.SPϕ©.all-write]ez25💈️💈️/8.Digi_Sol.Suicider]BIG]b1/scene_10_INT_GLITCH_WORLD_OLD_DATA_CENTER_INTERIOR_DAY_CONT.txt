INT. GLITCH WORLD - OLD DATA CENTER - INTERIOR - DAY (CONT.)

The silence in the data center is shattered by the metallic CLANG of approaching Metal Mafia robots. Their red optical sensors pierce the gloom, converging on Lucky and Kael's position. The air, once thick with the hum of servers, now vibrates with the ominous whir of their internal mechanisms.

LUCKY
(Drawing his Blade-Shield, adrenaline coursing)
Looks like we woke them up!

KAEL
(Grabbing Lucky's arm, pulling him towards a narrow gap between two server racks)
No time to fight! We created a ripple, not a wave. They'll be rerouting resources to this sector. We need to get out, now!

They scramble through the tight passage, the robots' footsteps growing louder behind them. Lucky glances back to see a torrent of red lasers lancing through the space they just occupied, scorching the server racks.

KAEL
(Cont., panting)
My disruption won't last. The system will compensate. The brainwashing protocols will re-engage. We have a small window. A moment of clarity for those trapped.

They burst out of the data center's hidden entrance, back into the desolate industrial zone. The sickly grey sky seems to pulse with a faint, green light, a residual effect of Kael's disruption. The distant drones, previously subtle, are now blaring their ALERT SOUNDS, their red eyes sweeping the landscape with frantic intensity.

LUCKY
(Looking back at the data center, which is now glowing with an angry, pulsating red light)
They're really mad.

KAEL
(Scanning the sky, pulling out his frequency scrambler)
They're reacting to a critical system failure. This is unprecedented for them. It means we hit a nerve. But it also means they'll be deploying their heavy units. We need to get back to the city, blend in.

As they run, Lucky notices something. The glitched figures they passed earlier – the worker on the scaffold, the endlessly circling truck driver, the figures huddled in the alleyway – are behaving differently. The worker on the scaffold is no longer hammering; he's looking around, his head tilted, a flicker of confusion in his eyes. The truck driver has stopped, his blurred form momentarily solidifying, his hands gripping the wheel as if waking from a dream. And from the alleyway, a faint, almost inaudible WHISPER, a collective murmur of awakening.

LUCKY
(Pointing)
Kael! Look! They're... they're changing!

Kael glances over. His eyes widen. The subtle green pulse from the sky seems to be affecting them, momentarily overriding the brainwashing. Their glitches are becoming more pronounced, but also more *conscious*. A man's arm stretches, but this time, he looks at it with dawning horror, not oblivious acceptance.

KAEL
(A grim satisfaction on his face)
The ripple. It's working. They're waking up. Even if only for a moment. We need to find them. Those who are fighting. Those who are resisting.

Suddenly, a massive, heavily armored METAL MAFIA ENFORCER, a bipedal robot bristling with weaponry, drops from the sky with a THUD that shakes the ground. Its optical sensors glow with an intense, unwavering red. It locks onto Lucky and Kael.

ENFORCER (V.O.)
Unauthorized system interference detected. Targets identified. Termination protocols initiated.

Kael shoves Lucky forward.

KAEL
Run! I'll create a diversion! Find the others! Find those who are waking up! They'll need guidance!

Kael turns, his frequency scrambler glowing brightly, and unleashes a torrent of digital noise at the approaching Enforcer. The robot stutters, its targeting systems momentarily overwhelmed, but it quickly recalibrates.

LUCKY
(Hesitating)
Kael! What about you?!

KAEL
(A determined glint in his eye)
I'm a ghost in the machine, Lucky! I'll find my way! Go! This is our chance! The resistance starts now!

Lucky, with a heavy heart, turns and runs, leaving Kael to face the formidable Enforcer alone. He glances back once, seeing Kael engaging the robot, a whirlwind of digital disruption against overwhelming force. The green pulse in the sky flickers, then begins to fade, the Glitch World's system fighting back, trying to reassert its control.

Lucky runs towards the city, his mind racing. He has to find them. The awakened. The ones who are fighting. The ones who can help him break free from this digital nightmare.

FADE OUT.
