INT. GLITCH WORLD - BUSTLING CITY STREET - DAY

A blinding, disorienting flash. A sensation of being torn apart, then abruptly reassembled. Lucky gasps, his eyes snapping open. He's no longer in the cold, metallic server room. He's standing on a bustling city street, bathed in warm, natural sunlight.

The air is filled with the familiar sounds of a metropolis: the distant HONK of traffic, the murmur of conversations, the rhythmic CLACK of high heels on pavement. Skyscrapers pierce a clear blue sky. People, dressed in what appears to be pre-EMPIRIE, early 21st-century fashion, walk past, engrossed in their own lives.

Lucky looks around in confusion. This is... Earth? But not the ruined, desolate Earth he left behind. This is vibrant, alive, almost idyllic. He sees a coffee shop, a bookstore, a park with children playing.

Then he sees Kael. He's standing a few feet away, his Halo body gone, replaced by a simple, modern suit. His face is pale, a grim expression etched upon it.

LUCKY
(Voice hoarse)
Kael? Where are we? What happened?

Kael's eyes scan their surroundings, a deep, unsettling understanding in their depths.

KAEL
(Voice low, devoid of its usual confidence)
We're in the Glitch World, Lucky. The Metal Mafia's ultimate prison.

Lucky's brow furrows. He looks at the seemingly normal street again. A woman walks past, talking on a phone. Her image flickers, briefly pixelating, then snaps back to normal. A car drives by, its wheels momentarily spinning in place before resuming motion. A billboard advertising a new product suddenly displays a garbled mess of code, then corrects itself.

LUCKY
(Eyes widening in horror)
It's... it's glitched. This isn't real.

KAEL
(Nodding, his gaze fixed on a man whose arm briefly stretches to an impossible length before retracting)
It's a false reality. A simulation designed to imprison their worst enemies. To brainwash them. They make it seem normal, a perfect version of Earth before the devastations, to break your will. But the reality... it's a corrupted loop. And the prisoners... they all have glitch powers. Because this isn't real. It's a system, and we're inside it.

Lucky looks at his own hand. He clenches his fist, and for a split second, his fingers seem to multiply, then return to normal. A cold dread washes over him.

KAEL
(Cont.)
They want to break us. To turn us into compliant drones. But we won't let them. We can't.

Suddenly, a sleek, metallic drone, unlike any he's seen in Avanta.C, glides silently overhead. Its optical sensor, a single red eye, sweeps the street. Then another, and another. They are subtle, almost invisible, blending into the background, but they are there. Watching.

LUCKY
(Voice barely a whisper)
Metal Mafia robots.

Kael's expression is grim. He looks at Lucky, a shared understanding passing between them. They are trapped. In a world that looks like home, but is anything but. A world where reality itself is a lie, and their very minds are the target.

KAEL
(Cont.)
We need to find a way out of here, Lucky. Before we lose ourselves in the illusion.

Lucky looks at the bustling street, the smiling faces, the seemingly normal world. He knows it's a lie. And he knows, with a chilling certainty, that escaping this prison will be their greatest challenge yet.
