## Character Sheet - Book 8: Digital Soul Suicider

### Lucky
*   **Appearance:** (In Glitch World: Appears as a normal human, but can manifest visual glitches on his own avatar, like translucency).
*   **Personality:** Determined, adaptable, and empathetic. He quickly grasps the nature of the Glitch World and uses his abilities to fight for freedom.
*   **Skills/Abilities (Glitch World):** Can manipulate visual rendering of his own avatar (e.g., translucency). Can cause terrain instability. Can overload emotional suppression nodes. Gains ability to transfer energy to others.
*   **Motivation:** To escape the Glitch World, free those trapped, and continue the fight against the Metal Mafia.

### Kael (Nova Spetsnaz Unit 734)
*   **Appearance:** (In Glitch World: Appears as a normal human, but can manifest powerful digital glitches).
*   **Personality:** Highly intelligent, resourceful, and driven by vengeance for his enslaved comrades. Acts as a guide and mentor to Lucky in the Glitch World.
*   **Skills/Abilities (Glitch World):** Highly skilled in digital infiltration and bypassing security. Can manipulate system corruption, targeting overrides, visual distortions. Can project consciousness/energy (e.g., via frequency scrambler).
*   **Motivation:** To dismantle the Metal Mafia's operations from within the Glitch World and ultimately free his Nova Spetsnaz unit.

### Anya
*   **Appearance:** Appears as a normal human in the Glitch World, but her movements are sharp and purposeful. Her eyes are intelligent and weary.
*   **Personality:** Resilient, observant, and determined. She has been silently resisting the brainwashing for a long time. Becomes a key ally in the resistance.
*   **Skills/Abilities (Glitch World):** Highly attuned to subtle glitches and anomalies. Can identify disguised enforcers and hidden clues. Skilled with an energy pistol. Provides tactical support.
*   **Motivation:** To fight against the Metal Mafia's brainwashing and find a way out of the Glitch World.

### Elara
*   **Appearance:** Young woman, initially disheveled and wild, body flickering violently with digital static. Later, gains a healthy glow, and her hands glow faintly.
*   **Personality:** Initially fearful and overwhelmed, but gains strength and resolve. Possesses a unique and powerful Glitch power.
*   **Skills/Abilities (Glitch World):** **Manifestor** - can pull data directly from the simulation's core and render it into physical form (e.g., holographic butterfly, data bypass). Can siphon energy from conduits. Remembers critical information suppressed by the Metal Mafia. Can stabilize unstable areas.
*   **Motivation:** To escape the Glitch World and help others remember and resist.

### Metal Mafia Entities (Glitch World Specific)
*   **Disguised Enforcers:** Metal Mafia agents or robots disguised as normal citizens, blending into the illusion. Their eyes may glow, or they may emit a subtle whirring sound.
*   **Combat Models/Enforcers:** Heavily armored robots designed for suppression and combat within the Glitch World.
*   **Corrupted Enforcers:** Brainwashed prisoners twisted into monstrous, glitched guardians, their bodies amalgamations of corrupted data and physical form.
*   **Soul Guardians:** Elite Metal Mafia enforcers, their bodies composed of pure, corrupted data, extensions of the Digital Soul Suicider itself. Designed to protect the Core.
*   **Digital Soul Suicider:** The core program of the Glitch World, designed to fragment and overwrite consciousness. Not a character, but a central antagonist entity.
