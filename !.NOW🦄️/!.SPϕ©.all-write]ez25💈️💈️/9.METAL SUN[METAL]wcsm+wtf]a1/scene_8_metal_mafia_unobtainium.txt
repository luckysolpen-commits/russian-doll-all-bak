INT. METAL SUN - PUBLIC SQUARE - DAY

As Lucky, Astra, and Gigishixmix begin to decipher the cryptic messages from the ancient documents, a chilling event unfolds in the public square.

SOL, his eyes gleaming with an unsettling intensity, gathers his latest batch of CLONE SONS. The crowd watches in horror as Sol, with a twisted sense of purpose, begins to slaughter them one by one.

SOL
(Voice amplified, echoing)
These are the wages of SIN! Let this be a warning to all who stray from the path of purity!

The air is thick with tension as the onlookers struggle to comprehend the senseless brutality. Gem, who has arrived, watches, her worst fears realized as her son RILEY is spared. His rapid growth and advanced abilities are a stark reminder of Sol's twisted intentions.

Lucky feels a deep sense of unease, knowing that he has grown too close to Gem and her troubled past. Astra and Gigishixmix stand frozen in shock, their bond with the clones shattered by Sol's monstrous act. The once-peaceful Metal Sun is now tainted by the darkness of Sol's obsession.

As the dust settles on the carnage, Sol vanishes into the shadows, leaving behind a trail of devastation and a chilling warning to the people: "SIN" would not be tolerated, and he would stop at nothing to eradicate it.

FADE OUT.
