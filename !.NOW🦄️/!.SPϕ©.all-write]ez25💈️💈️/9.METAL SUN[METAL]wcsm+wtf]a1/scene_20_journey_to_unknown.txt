EXT. PLANET X - CAVE SYSTEM - DAY

Months have passed. Lucky has adapted to Planet X. He's found a network of caves, rich with strange, glowing flora that provides sustenance and a faint, natural light. His Unobtainium suit, though still incomplete, is now a testament to his resourcefulness, patched with alien materials.

He's learned to hunt the local fauna, small, scuttling creatures that glow in the dark. He's built a rudimentary shelter, a testament to his will to survive.

He holds the glowing crystal from the Extremophiles. The Halo connection to Astra is stronger now, a steady hum in his mind, though still no direct communication.

LUCKY
(Telepathically, to Astra)
I'm still here. I'm surviving. I'm learning.

INT. METAL SUN - ASTRA'S SHELTER - DAY

Astra, her face showing the strain of months of hardship, feels Lucky's message. A tear rolls down her cheek. Gigishixmix, now a faint, almost translucent outline, smiles weakly beside her.

ASTRA
(Telepathically, to Lucky)
We know, Lucky. We feel you. We're fighting too. We'll find a way.

The Metal Sun is slowly healing, but it's a long, painful process. The wormhole remains blocked, a constant reminder of Lucky's absence.

INT. METAL SUN - GEM'S QUARTERS - DAY

GEM, her eyes hardened by experience, works tirelessly, trying to find a way to bypass the Metal Mafia's blockade. She's no longer torn between Sol and Lucky; her focus is solely on getting Lucky back and rebuilding what's left of their world. Riley's image, though still present in her mind, is now a motivation, not a distraction.

EXT. PLANET X - CLIFFSIDE - SUNSET

Lucky stands on a cliffside, looking out at the alien landscape. The twin suns of Planet X dip below the horizon, casting long, purple shadows. He is alone, but not broken. He has faced the ultimate challenge of survival and emerged stronger.

He knows the journey back to the Metal Sun, if it's even possible, will be long and arduous. But he has hope. He has the faint, persistent hum of the Halo, a lifeline to the people he loves. And he has himself, a survivor, forged in the crucible of a new world.

FADE TO BLACK.
