EXT. PLANET X - CRASH SITE - DAY

Lucky's world is a blur of light and sound as he is propelled through the wormhole. He crashes onto a desolate, alien planet, the impact jarring his bones. His UNOBTAINIUM SUIT, still incomplete, is battered and dented, leaving him vulnerable.

The landscape is unforgiving: twisted metal trees, jagged rock formations, and a sky that burns with an eternal, sickly green fire. The air is thin and acrid.

Lucky struggles to his feet, his head throbbing. He checks his SOULPEN, but it's shattered, useless. A wave of despair washes over him.

Suddenly, a faint, ethereal glow emanates from his wrist. It's the HALO, a mystical connection he shares with Astra. He focuses, and her voice, distant but clear, echoes in his mind.

ASTRA (V.O.)
Lucky? Are you there? What happened?

LUCKY
(Weakly, telepathically)
Astra... I'm on some planet. My SoulPen is broken. I don't know where I am.

ASTRA (V.O.)
Don't worry, Lucky. I'll find you. Just... stay safe.

Lucky looks around the desolate landscape. Survival here will be a daunting challenge. He's alone, with limited resources, and the Metal Mafia is likely blocking the wormhole, preventing Astra from reaching him.

FADE OUT.
