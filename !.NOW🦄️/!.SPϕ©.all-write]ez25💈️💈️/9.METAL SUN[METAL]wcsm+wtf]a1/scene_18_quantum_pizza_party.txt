INT. METAL SUN - RUINED SECTOR - DAY

The Metal Sun is a shattered husk. Entire sectors are gone, replaced by gaping voids that expose the raw, quantum-warped core. Debris floats in zero-G pockets, illuminated by the dying glow of the central spire.

ASTRA and GIGISHIXMIX huddle in a makeshift shelter, a section of a collapsed building that somehow remained intact. Gigishixmix, for once, is silent, her usual effervescent energy replaced by a quiet, almost mournful hum. Her Halobody flickers weakly.

ASTRA
(Whispering, to herself)
Lucky... are you out there?

She tries to reach him through the Halo, but the connection is fractured, barely a whisper. The catastrophic event caused by Edge's obsession has not only torn the Metal Sun apart but has also disrupted the delicate quantum fabric that allows for such communication.

GIGISHIXMIX
(Softly, her voice devoid of its usual burps)
He's strong, Astra. Lucky-wucky is strong. He'll find a way.

Astra looks at Gigishixmix, surprised by her clarity. The AI humanoid, usually a source of comic relief, now offers a strange, profound comfort.

INT. METAL SUN - GEM'S QUARTERS - RUINED - DAY

GEM stands amidst the wreckage of her quarters. The holographic projection of Riley is gone, replaced by static. She clutches the locket with baby Riley's image, her face streaked with tears.

She sees the devastation outside, the direct result of Sol's unchecked ambition and Edge's reckless pursuit of power. Her own complicity, her navigation of feelings for both men, now feels like a betrayal of the Metal Sun itself.

GEM
(To herself, a raw whisper)
This is my fault. All of it.

She looks at the shattered comms device, a desperate need to reach Lucky, to apologize, to explain, burning within her. But the Metal Mafia's blockade, now reinforced by the chaos, is impenetrable.

FADE OUT.
