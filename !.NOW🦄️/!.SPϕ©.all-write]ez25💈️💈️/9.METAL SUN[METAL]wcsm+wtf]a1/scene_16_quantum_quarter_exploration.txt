INT. METAL SUN - GEM'S QUARTERS - NIGHT

GEM stares at a flickering holographic image of LUCKY, his face etched with concern. She reaches out, her fingers passing through the light. The guilt is a heavy weight in her chest.

Her comms device buzzes. It's SOL. She hesitates, then answers. His face, cold and calculating, appears.

SOL
(Holographic)
Riley's power is escalating. He is a force. You understand what this means for our plans, Gem.

GEM
(Voice strained)
He's your son, Sol. And mine. This... this isn't what we discussed.

SOL
(A chilling smile)
Plans evolve. Power demands sacrifice. You chose your path, Gem. Remember your loyalty.

He disconnects. Gem slams her fist on the table. She looks at the image of Lucky again, then at a small, hidden locket she wears, containing a tiny, almost imperceptible image of RILEY as a baby, before Sol's manipulations.

She tries to use her own Sol Pen to send a message through the wormhole, a desperate attempt to reach Lucky. The energy flickers, then dies. The Metal Mafia's blockade is absolute.

Gem paces, torn. Her feelings for Lucky are genuine, a warmth she rarely feels with Sol. But Sol holds Riley, and a part of her, a dark, ambitious part, is still drawn to his vision of power. She is caught between two worlds, two men, and the fate of her son.

FADE OUT.
