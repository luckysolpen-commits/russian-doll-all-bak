INT. METAL SUN - TRAINING ARENA - DAY

RILEY, now appearing as a young man, moves with incredible speed and precision in a vast training arena. He effortlessly disarms and incapacitates a squad of advanced combat drones, their metallic forms crumpling under his blows.

SOL watches from a viewing platform, a rare, almost paternal pride in his eyes. Riley's abilities are far beyond anything Sol had anticipated, even with the genetic manipulation.

SOL
(To himself, a hint of awe)
He truly is... exceptional.

Riley finishes his training, his chest heaving slightly. He looks up at Sol.

RILEY
Is that all, Father? I feel... I can do more.

Sol descends to the arena floor, a calculating look replacing his pride.

SOL
Indeed, my son. Your potential is limitless. But with great power comes... great responsibility. And great danger.

He gestures to a holographic display showing the Metal Sun's core, then the surrounding solar system. Energy readings spike erratically.

SOL (CONT'D)
Your very existence, your rapid growth, is causing ripples. The Metal Sun's stability... it is being threatened.

Riley looks at the display, a flicker of concern in his eyes. He senses the truth in Sol's words. His power, while immense, is also a destabilizing force.

INT. METAL SUN - GEM'S QUARTERS - NIGHT

Gem paces nervously. She has heard whispers of Riley's escalating power, the tremors felt throughout the Metal Sun. She tries to contact Sol, but he ignores her.

She looks at a framed photo of Lucky, Astra, and herself, a pang of longing in her heart. She knows she needs to warn Lucky, but the Metal Mafia's blockade of the wormhole is absolute.

FADE OUT.
