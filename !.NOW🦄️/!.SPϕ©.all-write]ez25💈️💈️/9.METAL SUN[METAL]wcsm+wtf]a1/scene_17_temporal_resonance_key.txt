INT. METAL SUN - CORE CHAMBER - DAY

Edge, inside the METAL MAN MECHA, cackles maniacally as he pushes the mecha's power core beyond its limits. The Unobtainium, instead of being harnessed, is reacting violently, creating massive energy surges.

EDGE
(Through the mecha's speakers, distorted)
Witness true power! The Metal Sun will be reborn in my image!

Outside the core chamber, the Metal Sun groans. Massive fissures spiderweb across its metallic surface. Sections of the city begin to buckle and collapse, sending debris hurtling into the void. The quantum cooling systems fail, and localized areas begin to heat up to extreme temperatures, then rapidly cool, causing violent atmospheric shifts.

INT. METAL SUN - ASTRA AND GIGISHIXMIX'S LOCATION - DAY

Astra and Gigishixmix cling to a collapsing walkway, dodging falling debris. The entire structure of the Metal Sun is tearing itself apart.

ASTRA
(Shouting over the roar of destruction)
He's destroying it! He's going to rip the whole planet apart!

GIGISHIXMIX
(Her usual goofy grin replaced by genuine terror)
This is bad, Lucky-wucky! Very, very bad!

INT. METAL SUN - SOL'S SANCTUM - DAY

SOL, in his SUPER SON POWERSUIT, stands in his sanctum, observing the chaos on a holographic display. His face is grim, a flicker of concern in his eyes. Even he is surprised by the scale of Edge's recklessness.

RILEY, now appearing as a powerful, almost adult figure, stands beside him, his own eyes wide with a mixture of awe and fear.

RILEY
Father... the Metal Sun is dying.

Sol clenches his fist. His obsession with purging sin has led to this. Edge, a product of the very chaos Sol sought to control, is now destroying his domain.

SOL
(To Riley, a cold resolve in his voice)
Then we shall rebuild. Stronger. Purer.

FADE OUT.
