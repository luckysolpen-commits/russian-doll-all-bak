INT. METAL SUN - QUANTUM QUARTER - DAY

Lucky, Astra, and Gem find themselves in the Quantum Quarter, a district unlike any other. Gravity warps and twists, creating impossible angles and perspectives. They walk through winding streets, taking in the sights and sounds.

They pass by shops selling exotic crystals and quantum-entangled trinkets. The air is thick with the scent of ozone and ionized gases, a reminder that they are still deep within the Metal Sun's core.

After their intense discussion about Solenoid Industries' secrets, Lucky suggests a break.

LUCKY
Let's grab some grub at that new quantum café.

He nods toward a nearby establishment. The sign above the door reads "Schrödinger's Diner" in flashing neon lights.

Astra's eyes sparkle as she pushes open the door. Inside, the diner is a marvel of culinary innovation. The menu features dishes with names like "Entangled Eggs Benedict" and "Schrödinger's Soup."

Lucky orders the "Quantum Quesadilla," which arrives at their table in a puff of misty quantum fog.

As they eat, Gem pulls out her cybernetic implant and begins to scan the surrounding area, searching for clues that might lead them closer to uncovering Solenoid Industries' secrets. The others chat casually, enjoying their meal and the unusual atmosphere.

LUCKY
(Whispering to Astra)
Hey, have you ever tried the 'Gravity Well'?

ASTRA
(Excited)
No way! I've heard it's a local favorite.

Their server, a being with skin like molten lava, smiles as he leads them to the back of the diner. A small door disappears into the quantum fog. The sign above the door reads "Gravity Well: Where Down is Up and Up is Down."

The trio steps inside, finding themselves in a gravity-free environment. They float together, laughing and playing in the zero-G setting. It's a brief respite from their mission.

As they leave the Gravity Well, Gem turns to her companions, a thoughtful expression on her face.

GEM
I think I might have found something.

She holds up her implant.

GEM (CONT'D)
It seems that Solenoid Industries is experimenting with quantum-entangled particles in an attempt to create a stable wormhole.

Lucky's eyes widen.

LUCKY
That's insane! If they succeed, it could change the entire course of human history.

Astra nods gravely.

ASTRA
We need to get to the bottom of this before they do something that can't be undone.

The three friends continue their journey through the Quantum Quarter, their minds racing with the implications of Solenoid Industries' research.

FADE OUT.
