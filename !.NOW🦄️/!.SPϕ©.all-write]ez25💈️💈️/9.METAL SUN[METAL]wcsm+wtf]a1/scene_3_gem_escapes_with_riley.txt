INT. METAL SUN - CENTRAL HUB - NIGHT

Lucky, Astra, and Gem move with caution through the winding tunnels of the Metal Sun's central hub. Surveillance drones patrol the corridors, their red eyes scanning for anomalies.

As they descend deeper into the planet's core, the temperature drops precipitously. The air grows thick with a cold, quantum mist that stings their skin. It's a strange sensation, as if they are walking through a dense fog of pure energy.

INT. METAL SUN - SHADOW NET RENDEZVOUS POINT - CONTINUOUS

They arrive at a hidden chamber. The walls seem to shift and ripple, an illusion created by the extreme quantum fluctuations that keep the planet's interior at a chilly -200°C.

The air grows colder still as they enter. A masked figure emerges from the shadows, its features obscured by a featureless mask.

LUCKY
(Direct)
We're not here for small talk. What do you have?

The figure hands Gem a data crystal. She plugs it into her cybernetic implant. Information flows onto her retina, and she relays it to Lucky and Astra.

GEM
(Voice laced with concern)
Solenoid Industries is working on a top-secret project codenamed 'Eclipse.' I think Sol intends to use Riley as the key to unlocking its true potential.

Lucky's expression turns grim.

LUCKY
We need to get to the bottom of this before it's too late.

Astra nods, her eyes flashing with determination.

ASTRA
Let's take a closer look at Solenoid Industries' files and see what we can dig up.

The three of them vanish into the shadows, leaving behind a trail of speculation and intrigue in the Metal Sun's labyrinthine corridors.

FADE OUT.
