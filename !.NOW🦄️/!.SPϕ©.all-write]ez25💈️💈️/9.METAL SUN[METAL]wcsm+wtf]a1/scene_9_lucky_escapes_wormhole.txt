INT. METAL SUN - ANCIENT RUINS - DAY

Days pass. The Metal Sun struggles to come to terms with the horrors that unfolded. Sol's twisted actions have left a deep scar. Many question his motives.

GIGISHIXMIX, still reeling from the trauma, becomes increasingly clingy towards Lucky, often "drunkenly burping" and showering him with affection. Astra and Gem try to comfort her, but Gigishixmix's mental state is fragile.

Meanwhile, Sol continues to manipulate Gem, using Riley as leverage to further his twisted agenda. His obsession with creating sons has reached a fever pitch.

As tensions escalate, the group finds themselves on the brink of collapse. Lucky, Astra, and Gigishixmix are torn between their loyalty to each other and their unease towards Sol's actions.

Lucky, seeking solace, wanders through the ancient ruins of the Metal Sun. He stumbles upon a hidden chamber, untouched for millennia. Inside, he finds an ANCIENT ARTIFACT, glowing faintly.

He examines it. Cryptic messages are etched into its surface, hinting at the existence of WORM HOLE X, surrounded by UNOBTAINIUM PARTICLES.

Intrigued, Lucky shares his findings with Astra and Gigishixmix. Together, they begin to decipher the ancient text, slowly uncovering the secrets of Worm Hole X. The text speaks of the EXTREMOPHILES, a quantum-warped civilization rumored to have seeded life on Earth itself. The implications are staggering.

Their discovery sparks a mix of excitement and trepidation. What lies at the other end of the wormhole? And what would be the consequences for those who dared to venture forth?

FADE OUT.
