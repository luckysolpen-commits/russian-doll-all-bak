INT. METAL SUN - UNOBTAINIUM SHIPMENT DOCK - DAY

An UNOBTAINIUM SHIPMENT, glowing with an ethereal light, is being loaded onto a transport vessel. Lucky, Astra, and Gigishixmix are overseeing the operation, their nerves on edge.

LUCKY
(To Astra)
This feels too quiet. Edge is going to make his move.

Suddenly, the ground trembles. A colossal MECHA, the METAL MAN MECHA, bursts through a wall, piloted by EDGE. Its eyes glow red, and it unleashes a volley of energy blasts.

METAL MAFIA THUGS, armed and dangerous, swarm the dock, engaging the Metal Sun's security forces. Chaos erupts.

Edge, inside the Metal Man Mecha, targets Lucky.

EDGE
(Voice distorted by the mecha's speakers)
That Unobtainium is mine, Lucky! And so is your life!

Lucky, still without his fully functional Unobtainium suit, is caught in the crossfire. He dodges a blast, the heat searing his skin. Astra and Gigishixmix try to cover him, but the sheer force of the attack is overwhelming.

LUCKY
(To Astra, shouting over the din)
I have to go! Get out of here!

He sees it – a shimmering distortion in the air, a nascent WORM HOLE X, unstable and dangerous, but his only escape. It's the wormhole he discovered, now manifesting due to the immense energy fluctuations from the battle.

With a desperate leap, Lucky dives towards the wormhole, narrowly avoiding a crushing blow from the Metal Man Mecha. He disappears into the shimmering vortex just as Edge's fist slams into the spot where he stood moments before.

Edge roars in frustration. The Metal Mafia continues their assault, but Lucky is gone.

FADE OUT.
