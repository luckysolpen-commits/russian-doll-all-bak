INT. METAL SUN - LUCKY'S QUARTERS - DAY

LUCKY steps out of the metal sun's gates, stretching his arms and letting the warm sunlight wash over him. It's good to be back on familiar ground, surrounded by the hum of machinery and the chatter of the inhabitants.

He walks towards his quarters, a sense of relief washing over him. The Metal Sun is a place where he can escape the dangers and uncertainties of the outside world. Here, he is just Lucky, a normal citizen living among others like him.

He slips into his apartment, shedding his outerwear as he enters. ASTRA, his partner, greets him with a warm smile from the kitchenette.

ASTRA
(Winking)
Hey, handsome. Welcome back.

Lucky chuckles and ambles over to her.

LUCKY
Thanks, I needed this break.

He wraps his arms around her waist, pulling her close. Astra leans into him, her eyes sparkling with affection.

ASTRA
I'm glad you're home safe. But you know Gem's been acting strange lately...

Lucky's brow furrows in concern.

LUCKY
What do you mean?

ASTRA
She's just...different. She won't talk about it, but I can sense something's bothering her.

Lucky's gut tells him that Astra is right. He makes a mental note to keep an eye on Gem and try to figure out what's wrong.

Just then, the COMMS DEVICE beeps. Lucky answers, and a holographic projection of GEM appears before them.

GEM
(Serious)
Lucky, I need to talk to you. It's about Sol...

Lucky's instincts tell him that this conversation is going to be a wild ride.

FADE OUT.
