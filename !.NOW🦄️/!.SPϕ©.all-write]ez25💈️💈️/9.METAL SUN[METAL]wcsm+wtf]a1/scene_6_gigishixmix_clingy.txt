INT. METAL SUN - REVERSE CEILING ROLLER SKATE DANCE CLUB - NIGHT

Gem, ever the scientist, is fascinated by the subtle changes in perception brought about by the Soda. She spends hours exploring the club's air hockey tables and roller skating rinks, marveling at the ways in which her human brain can be tricked into experiencing the world in a completely new way.

As the night wears on, the trio finds themselves lost in a sea of quantum probabilities, their minds expanding to comprehend the infinite possibilities hidden within the Metal Sun's quantum realm. They laugh, dance, and skate through the endless hours, their bond growing stronger as they explore the limits of their newfound temporal resonance.

And so, with Gigishixmix's Soda-fueled guidance, Lucky, Astra, and Gem spend their week-long pizza party night lost in the swirling vortex of quantum probabilities, forever changed by the experience.
