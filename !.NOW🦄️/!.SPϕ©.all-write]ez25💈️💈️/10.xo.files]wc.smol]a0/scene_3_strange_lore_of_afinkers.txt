SCENE 3: THE STRANGE LORE OF THE AFINKERS

[INT. AFINK VILLAGE - ELDER'S HUT - NIGHT]

LUCKY sits cross-legged on a woven mat inside GORN's (the ELDERLY AFINK) hut. The air is warm, filled with the earthy scent of dried herbs and a faint, sweet aroma. Lyra, the YOUNG AFINK GIRL, sits beside Gorn, translating.

Lucky has shared his story – his crash, his origins from a distant world, his mission to find a way home. Gorn listens intently, his ancient eyes never leaving Lucky's face.

GORN (O.S.)
(Translated by Lyra)
Your world sounds... different. Here, our lives are woven with color.

Lucky looks around the hut. The walls are adorned with intricate murals, swirling patterns of vibrant hues that seem to shift and breathe in the dim light. Even the simple utensils are etched with colorful designs.

LUCKY
I've noticed. The art here is... unlike anything I've ever seen.

LYRA
It is more than art. It is life. Our inks are not merely pigments. They are... essence.

She picks up a small, smooth stone, painted with a deep, shimmering blue. She holds it out to Lucky.

LYRA (CONT'D)
This is Zhilak. The ink of strength. When applied, it enhances our muscles, our speed. It allows us to build, to hunt, to endure.

Lucky takes the stone, feeling a faint warmth emanating from it. He's a scientist, but this defies his understanding of biology.

LUCKY
Biologically enhancing? How?

Gorn chuckles, a low, rumbling sound.

GORN (O.S.)
(Translated by Lyra)
It is the wisdom of our ancestors, passed through generations. Each color holds a specific power. Korvath, the green, grants wisdom. Ember, the fiery orange, ignites courage. They are the threads of our existence.

Lucky's curiosity is piqued. He asks more questions, delving into the intricacies of their ink lore. He learns how they mix the inks with various organic compounds, creating dyes for textiles, ceramics, and even for their own skin, forming the intricate patterns he'd observed.

Later, Gorn leads Lucky and Lyra to a secluded, sacred area within the village. In the center stands a large, pulsating ORB, radiating a soft, ethereal glow. It hums with a low, resonant frequency that vibrates through the ground.

GORN (O.S.)
(Translated by Lyra)
This is the Heart of the Ink. The source of our people's power and wisdom. It holds the secrets of our ancestors.

Lucky approaches the orb cautiously, sensing its immense energy. The air around it feels charged, almost alive. Gorn, with reverence, carefully lifts the orb. It hovers above his palm, shimmering.

GORN (O.S.)
(Translated by Lyra)
A vision will soon unfold before you.

The humming intensifies. The orb begins to glow brighter, a blinding flash of light erupting from its core. Lucky instinctively closes his eyes.

When he opens them, he's no longer in the hut. He's standing on an ethereal landscape, suspended in a shimmering void. Below him, far below, is the vast, swirling sea of Planet X.

And floating above that sea, like a mirage, is an INVISIBLE NATION. A city of impossible architecture, towers of gleaming, dark material reaching into the sky, reflecting the colors of the Heart of the Ink. This is the realm of the EXTRMOPHILES.

GORN (V.O.)
(Translated by Lyra)
These are the guardians of the deep. They possess secrets and knowledge passed down through eons.

Lucky's mind reels. The city is immense, alien, and strangely alluring. He feels an inexplicable pull towards it, a sense of destiny.

As quickly as it began, the vision fades. Lucky finds himself back in Gorn's hut, the Heart of the Ink resting gently in the Elder's hands. The air is still, but the hum of the orb seems to echo in Lucky's mind.

GORN (O.S.)
(Translated by Lyra)
This is a sign. The Heart of the Ink has chosen you for a great purpose.

Lucky looks at Lyra, then back at the orb. The Extrmophiles' city, a shimmering enigma, beckons.

[FADE OUT.]
