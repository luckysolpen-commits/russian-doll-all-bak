CHARACTER SHEET - BOOK 10: EXTREMOPHILES

**LUCKY**
*   **Original State:** Human scientist, polygamist (implied, though not explicitly shown in action). Crash-landed on Planet X.
*   **Transformation:** Transformed into an Extrmophile-like being (scaly dark skin, single golden bug eye, sharp turtle-like mouth, thick leather scales) after touching the contents of a mysterious box. Gained telepathic abilities and enhanced physical attributes.
*   **Current State:** Undercover operative, maintaining his human will despite alien instincts. Escaped Planet X through the "Soul's Destiny" portal with Zig and Zack. Still in Extrmophile form.
*   **Key Traits:** Intelligent, resourceful, adaptable, determined, scientific curiosity, resilient.

**LYRA**
*   **Original State:** Young Afink girl, translator for Gorn, possesses ancient wisdom.
*   **Transformation:** Transformed into an Extrmophile-like being (similar to Lucky) after touching the contents of the mysterious box. Gained telepathic abilities and enhanced physical attributes.
*   **Current State:** Betrayed Lucky and the Fuzzballs, aligning with the Extrmophile Kings. Became a queen, breeding with the Kings to create more Extrmophiles. Remains on Planet X.
*   **Key Traits:** Cunning, ambitious, pragmatic, adaptable, manipulative, possesses ancient wisdom.

**GORN**
*   **Original State:** Elderly Afink, village elder, keeper of ancient lore.
*   **Current State:** Remains in Afink Village. Introduced Lucky to the "Heart of the Ink" and the lore of the colored inks.
*   **Key Traits:** Wise, traditional, spiritual, hospitable.

**ZYRA (KHA'ZIX WOMAN)**
*   **Original State:** Extrmophile merchant, leader of the Kha'Zix faction.
*   **Current State:** Remains in the Extrmophile City. Facilitated Lucky and Lyra's transformation.
*   **Key Traits:** Calculating, shrewd, opportunistic, ancient, powerful.

**ZHA'ZIX (HIGH PRIESTESS)**
*   **Original State:** Towering, insectoid High Priestess of the Temple of Whispers.
*   **Current State:** Remains in the Temple of Whispers. Formed an alliance with Lucky, intrigued by his scientific knowledge.
*   **Key Traits:** Intellectual, ancient, powerful, curious.

**KRIX'TIK**
*   **Original State:** Charismatic beetle-like leader of an insectoid colony.
*   **Current State:** Remains in the Extrmophile City. Formed an alliance with Lyra, becoming her consort.
*   **Key Traits:** Ambitious, charismatic, powerful, romantic (towards Lyra).

**ZIG (FUZZBALL)**
*   **Original State:** Frozen Fuzzball, part of the ancient Fuzzball civilization.
*   **Current State:** Freed by Lucky. One of two Fuzzball brothers who became Lucky's companions. Escaped Planet X with Lucky through the "Soul's Destiny" portal.
*   **Key Traits:** Energetic, loyal, telepathic, agile.

**ZACK (FUZZBALL)**
*   **Original State:** Frozen Fuzzball, part of the ancient Fuzzball civilization.
*   **Current State:** Freed by Lucky. One of two Fuzzball brothers who became Lucky's companions. Escaped Planet X with Lucky through the "Soul's Destiny" portal.
*   **Key Traits:** Energetic, loyal, telepathic, agile.

**EXTRMOPHILE KINGS**
*   **Original State:** Three powerful, malevolent Extrmophile rulers.
*   **Current State:** Remain in the Fuzz Castle on Planet X. Betrayed Lucky, allied with Lyra, and are now breeding with her to create more Extrmophiles.
*   **Key Traits:** Ruthless, cunning, powerful, enslavers, telepathic.
