INT. EARTH - ABANDONED WAREHOUSE - NIGHT

Rain lashes against the corrugated iron roof of a dilapidated warehouse. Inside, LUCKY (40s, gaunt, desperate), lies slumped against a stack of rusted barrels, a faint, rhythmic BEEPING emanating from a crude, jury-rigged device strapped to his wrist. His eyes are glazed, fixed on nothing.

Suddenly, a sleek, dark form drops from a high vent, landing with a soft thud. It's the BLUE BIRD BOUNTY HUNTER (BB) (30s, agile, clad in form-fitting, dark blue armor with glowing accents). Her movements are fluid, predatory. She carries a compact, high-tech medical kit.

BB
(Voice, a low, synthesized purr)
Lucky. You're a hard man to find. And even harder to keep alive, apparently.

Lucky barely stirs, a weak groan escaping his lips.

BB
(Kneeling, assessing his condition)
EMPRIE's got a bounty on your head the size of a small moon. But I'm not here for that. Not yet, anyway.

She quickly administers a shot from her kit. Lucky's eyes flutter, a flicker of awareness returning.

BB
(Cont.)
You're lucky I intercepted that distress signal. Most people just fade away out here.

She helps him to his feet, his body trembling.

BB
(Cont.)
Now, let's get you off this rock. We've got a long, quiet journey ahead. The EMPRIE's listening. Always listening.

EXT. SPACE - SMUGGLER'S SHIP - LATER

The small, nondescript smuggler's ship, "The Whisper," glides through the inky blackness of space. Its hull is coated in a special EMPRIE-detection-dampening material, making it almost invisible to long-range scans.

Inside, Lucky is recovering, a nutrient paste tube in his hand. BB is at the controls, her fingers dancing over a holographic console. Stars streak past the viewport, a dizzying blur.

BB
(Without looking at him)
They're everywhere. Imperial patrols, surveillance drones, even civilian vessels are often running passive EMPRIE scans. One wrong move, one stray energy signature, and we're dust.

Lucky looks out at the vast emptiness, a sense of overwhelming insignificance washing over him.

LUCKY
Where are we going?

BB
(A slight smile plays on her lips)
Ikarus. The Ellison Island of Earth refugees. A place where those who can afford to leave, do. And many who can't, still try. Illegally. Despite the risks. Viruses, hacking, getting caught by the EMPRIE... it's a gamble. But it's a chance.

The ship shudders slightly as BB executes a complex evasive maneuver. A faint, red BLIP appears and quickly vanishes on her console.

BB
(Cont.)
Close one. They're getting better.

Lucky watches her, a newfound respect for her skills.

LUCKY
Why are you helping me?

BB
(Turns, her eyes meeting his, a hint of mischief)
Let's just say I have a soft spot for lost causes. And a healthy disdain for the EMPRIE. Besides, you owe me. Big time.

The ship continues its silent, dangerous journey through the void, a tiny speck of defiance against the vast, oppressive EMPRIE.

EXT. IKARTUS - LANDING PAD - DAY

The Whisper descends onto a makeshift landing pad on Ikarus, a sprawling, chaotic settlement built on what looks like a repurposed asteroid or large space debris. Makeshift habitats, shimmering energy domes, and ramshackle structures are crammed together. The air is thick with the hum of machinery and the murmur of countless voices.

BB brings the ship down smoothly, landing on the outskirts of a bustling refugee camp.

BB
(Voice low, urgent)
Time to get moving, Lucky. We need to blend in and avoid drawing attention from the EMPRIE patrols. They have eyes everywhere, even here.

Lucky follows her, keeping close as they make their way through the crowded streets. The air is thick with the smell of smoke and cooking oil, mingling with the sounds of laughter and desperation. Makeshift markets and black markets spring up everywhere. People haggle over goods and services, their faces a mix of hope and despair.

BB leads him to a secluded alleyway, where a group of shady characters lurk in the shadows. Cyber-gear and contraband are on display.

BB
(Voice dripping with menace)
Time for you to meet your contact.

She hands him a small, glowing data crystal.

BB
(Cont.)
This contains your authorization code. You'll need it to jack into Avanta.C and start your new life.

Lucky takes the crystal, his heart racing. This is his ticket out.

BB turns to leave, disappearing into the crowded streets as quickly as she appeared. Lucky watches her go, a mix of gratitude and trepidation.

As the sun dips below the horizon, casting Ikarus in a warm orange glow, Lucky makes his way to the designated meeting point. His destination awaits: Avanta.C, the fabled virtual world where humanity's future lies hidden behind a veil of code and cyber-illusion...
