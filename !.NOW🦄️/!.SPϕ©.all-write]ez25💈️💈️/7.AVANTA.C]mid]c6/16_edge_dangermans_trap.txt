INT. AVANTA.C - ELDRADOR RUINS - HIDDEN CHAMBER - DAY

Following the cryptic clues from the expedition's notes, Lucky, Astra, and Kael delve deeper into the Eldrador ruins. They navigate through crumbling passages, their footsteps echoing in the oppressive silence. Astra uses her magic to illuminate dark corners, revealing ancient carvings and faded murals depicting strange, celestial events.

Finally, they arrive at a hidden chamber, concealed behind a cleverly disguised rock face. The air inside is still, heavy with an ancient, almost palpable energy. In the center of the chamber, bathed in a single, ethereal beam of light filtering from a crack in the ceiling, sits a pedestal.

Upon the pedestal rests a chest, not of wood or metal, but of what appears to be solidified starlight, shimmering with an inner luminescence. It's intricately carved with symbols that seem to shift and dance before their eyes.

LUCKY
(Whispering)
Is that... the artifact?

Kael's eyes are wide, a rare, genuine awe replacing his usual calculating demeanor. He approaches the chest, his movements slow and deliberate.

KAEL
(Voice hushed)
This is beyond anything I've ever seen. The craftsmanship... it's not of this world.

He reaches out and, with a soft click, the chest opens. Inside, nestled on a bed of shimmering, ethereal dust, lies the **ELDRIDIAN AMULET**. It's a palm-sized, multifaceted crystal, pulsing with a soft, rhythmic light that seems to draw the very air around it into its depths. Strange, ancient symbols glow faintly within its core.

The amulet emits a powerful, almost hypnotic energy. Lucky feels an irresistible pull towards it, a sense of destiny. He reaches out, his fingers brushing against its cool, smooth surface.

As his fingers make contact, a low, resonant HUM fills the chamber. The ethereal light from the amulet intensifies, casting dancing shadows across the walls. The ground beneath their feet begins to TREMBLE, subtly at first, then with increasing intensity.

ASTRA
(Alarmed)
Something's happening! This isn't just a treasure, it's a trigger!

CRACKS spiderweb across the ancient stone walls of the chamber. Dust and small pebbles rain down from the ceiling. The single beam of light from above flickers, then vanishes, plunging the chamber into near darkness, save for the amulet's growing glow.

LUCKY
(Stumbling as the ground lurches)
What's going on?!

KAEL
(His voice tight with urgency)
It's a trap! Eldrador's Maze! The ruins are shifting! We need to move, now!

Massive stone blocks, previously part of the chamber walls, begin to grind and slide, reconfiguring the space. New passages open, while others slam shut with deafening CRUNCHES. The very layout of the chamber is transforming, becoming a labyrinth.

The amulet, still clutched in Lucky's hand, pulses wildly, its light now a frantic, strobing beacon in the chaos. The walls are closing in, threatening to crush them, or trap them forever within the shifting confines of the maze.

They are caught. The treasure is theirs, but the price of its retrieval is their freedom, perhaps even their lives.

FADE OUT.
