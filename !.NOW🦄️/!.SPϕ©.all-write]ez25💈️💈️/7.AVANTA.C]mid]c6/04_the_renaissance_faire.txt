INT. AVANTA.C - BUSTLING CITY SQUARE - DAY (CONT.)

Lucky, still observing the lively gathering around the pedestal, feels a subtle shift in the air. His gaze is drawn to the edge of the crowd, where a figure stands apart. It's ASTRA (20s, mysterious, mage), her dark robes blending with the shadows, her eyes fixed intently on him.

Her gaze is piercing, seeming to bore into his very soul, as if sizing him up for some unknown purpose. For a moment, their eyes lock across the bustling square. A strange, almost electric current passes between them, and Lucky senses a deep, undeniable connection, a recognition he can't quite place.

Then, Astra's intense expression softens, shifting from scrutiny to a flicker of contrition. She begins to move, her steps measured and deliberate, cutting through the periphery of the dancing crowd, heading directly towards Lucky.

ASTRA
(Voice, low and husky, a hint of regret)
Lucky, I'm sorry. I didn't mean for things to go so far out of control back on Earth. I promise to make it right, somehow.

Lucky is taken aback. He had expected anger, perhaps indifference, from anyone who knew his past. But an apology? A glimmer of empathy? It's disarming.

LUCKY
(Confused)
What do you mean?

Astra's eyes, now filled with a quiet determination, sparkle.

ASTRA
I'll help you get what you want from Avanta.C. We can work together to—

Suddenly, the lively music and dancing around the pedestal cease. Aria, the bard, her face painted with a mixture of concern and excitement, approaches them, her lute still clutched in her hand.

ARIA
(Voice laced with intrigue, a wide smile)
Lucky! You're the one who's been making waves in the virtual world, aren't you? The new arrival everyone's whispering about?

Lucky nods, unsure where this conversation is headed, his attention split between Astra's earnest apology and Aria's sudden appearance.

Astra steps forward, her eyes locking onto Lucky's once more, a silent plea and a challenge in their depths.

ASTRA
I think we can help each other out. What do you say? Want to team up and take on Avanta.C together?

The possibility of forming an alliance with Astra tantalizes Lucky. He senses that their partnership could be the key to unlocking a brighter future in this vast, unpredictable virtual world. He looks from Astra's determined face to Aria's curious one, a new path opening before him.
