**Synopsis:**

In a dystopian future, a desperate refugee named Lucky escapes the oppressive EMPRIE on Earth and is forced into indentured servitude in the sprawling virtual world of Avanta.C. He teams up with a mysterious and powerful mage named Astra, and together they navigate the vibrant and dangerous digital landscape. As they level up and uncover the secrets of Avanta.C, they run afoul of the ruthless Metal Mafia guild and its enigmatic leader, Edge Dangerman. Their journey takes an unexpected turn when they ally with Kael, a secret agent of the Nova Spetsnaz, who reveals a deeper conspiracy involving the enslavement of human consciousness. Lucky and Astra must choose a side in a war for the very soul of the virtual world.
