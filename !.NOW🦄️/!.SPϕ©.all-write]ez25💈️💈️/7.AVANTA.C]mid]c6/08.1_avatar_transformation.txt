INT. AVANTA.C - ILLEGAL FURRY SERVER - AVATAR CUSTOMIZATION PARLOR - NIGHT

Inside the Illegal Furry Server, Astra leads Lucky through a maze of pulsating virtual rooms until they reach an exclusive AVATAR CUSTOMIZATION PARLOR. The space is cramped, filled with holographic displays, virtual mirrors, and floating interface panels controlled by a grizzled, multi-armed spider-furred hacker known only as 'PIXEL'.

PIXEL
(Six arms gesturing at various avatar options floating in the air)
Welcome to my shop, travelers. I can give you anything you desire. Duck, swan, cat, wolf... whatever your heart desires. The question is: what do you want to become?

LUCKY
(Studying the options)
I've been thinking about it since we got here. I want... something that seems unassuming but has hidden depths. A duck, maybe?

ASTRA
(Smiling knowingly)
Fitting. You do have that ability to appear calm on the surface while paddling furiously underneath. I think I'll go with... a swan. Something elegant that glides with grace but can be fierce when needed.

PIXEL
(Arms moving over interface panels)
Ah, interesting choices. Duck and swan, got it. Now, for the conversion process, we'll need to access your base avatar code directly. This is illegal on the main servers, but here... we can do it.

Pixel's multiple arms work furiously, opening up Lucky's avatar code. The process is visualized as Lucky's human form begins to shimmer and morph.

LUCKY
(Feeling the transformation)
This... this is incredible! I can feel every change!

Lucky's skin begins to sprout feathers that shimmer with an iridescent blue and gold pattern. His face elongates into a duck bill, and webbed feet replace his toes. His clothes shift and adapt, becoming more form-fitting for his new form.

For Astra, the transformation is equally dramatic. Her human features soften as a coat of soft, white feathers grows across her body. Her arms become elegant swan wings, and a long, graceful neck extends from her shoulders. Her legs become powerful with webbed feet, and a flowing white tail feathers behind her.

ASTRA
(Amazed at her reflection)
I... I look magnificent! This is unlike anything I've ever experienced.

PIXEL
(Charging them through the interface)
Custom furry avatars, full-body conversion. That'll be 500 gold coins each. But I've got a tip for you - there are some underground gaming circuits in the server. Beat Saber hacking battles are all the rage. You could earn that back in no time.

LUCKY
(Duck form, voice quacking but still recognizable)
Beat Saber hacking battles? What's that?

PIXEL
Grins with sharp, digital teeth)
You'll see. The stakes are real, and so are the rewards. And the dangers.

Lucky paddles around, testing his new webbed feet and streamlined body. Astra glides gracefully, getting used to her new swan form and its elegant movement.

LUCKY
(Cont.)
Well, this is a new beginning. How do I look?

ASTRA
(Gently preening him with her beak)
Like a proper waterfowl. And I feel more graceful than ever. I think we're going to fit in perfectly here.

The two of them exit the parlor, now fully transformed and better suited to the wild underground of Avanta.C.

FADE OUT.
