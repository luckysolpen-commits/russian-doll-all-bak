INT. AVANTA.C - FAIRY CITY - DAY

From the industrial grit of Troll City, Lucky and Astra ascend into a realm of ethereal beauty: Fairy City. It's a breathtaking metropolis built amidst the colossal, ancient trees of Avanta.C's deepest forests. The city isn't built *on* the trees, but *within* and *around* them, with structures woven from living wood and glowing flora.

The air here is impossibly clean, carrying the sweet scent of nectar and damp earth. Sunlight filters through the dense canopy in dappled patterns, illuminating shimmering dust motes that dance like tiny stars. Soft, melodic CHIMES and the gentle rustle of leaves replace the clang and roar of Troll City.

They walk along winding pathways made of polished, iridescent moss, which glow faintly underfoot. Sparkling WATERFALLS cascade from impossible heights, their waters catching the light and scattering rainbows across the forest floor. These waterfalls often feed into crystal-clear pools where luminous fish swim.

RAINBOW-COLORED BRIDGES, seemingly spun from pure light and dew, arch gracefully between the massive tree trunks, connecting different sections of the city. Some bridges are so delicate they appear to be made of spider silk, yet they hold their weight effortlessly.

The buildings are intricately carved wooden structures, some resembling giant, hollowed-out gourds, others like delicate, multi-tiered lanterns. They are adorned with glowing crystals, intricate vine patterns, and blossoms that pulse with soft light.

Fairies, small and graceful beings with translucent, insect-like wings, flit through the air like living jewels. They wear garments woven from leaves and petals, and their voices are like the tinkling of tiny bells.

Astra, her demeanor softening considerably in this serene environment, introduces Lucky to a group of friendly Fairies who are tending to a garden of glowing mushrooms.

ASTRA
(Voice hushed, respectful)
Greetings, guardians of the glade. This is Lucky, a new traveler to Avanta.C.

The Fairies, their eyes wide and curious, greet Lucky with soft, musical chirps. One, with particularly vibrant blue wings, offers him a small, glowing berry.

FAIRY (O.S.)
(A melodic whisper)
Welcome, wanderer. May your path be filled with light.

Lucky takes the berry. It tastes like pure sunlight and honey, leaving a warm, pleasant sensation.

They stroll further, and Astra shares tales of ancient magic, of how the Fairies are the keepers of the forest's deepest secrets, and how their magic is intrinsically linked to the health of Avanta.C itself.

ASTRA
(Cont.)
They say the oldest trees here remember the very first lines of code that formed Avanta.C. And the Fairies are their living memory.

Lucky looks around, feeling a profound sense of peace and wonder. After the oppressive atmosphere of Earth and the raw energy of Troll City, Fairy City is a balm to his soul. It's a reminder of the beauty and magic that still exists, even in a virtual world.

He realizes that each new place in Avanta.C is not just a location, but an entire culture, a different way of life. And he's only just begun to scratch the surface.
