**Screenplay Outline (20 Scenes)**

**SEQUENCE 1: SURVIVAL**

1.  **EXT. RUINED CITY - DAY**
    LUCKY, ASTRA, and GEM navigate a landscape of urban decay. They find shelter in a collapsed office building, the threat of CARCAZOIDS ever-present.

2.  **EXT. CITY STREETS - DAY**
    A tense scavenging run. The trio narrowly escapes a pack of hunting Carcazoids, showcasing their survival skills and teamwork.

3.  **INT. ABANDONED GROCERY STORE - DAY**
    They hit a jackpot of canned goods and medical supplies. A brief moment of relief and hope.

4.  **EXT. SALVAGE YARD - DAY**
    MONTAGE: The trio finds an armored truck and works together to repair and modify it, creating a rugged survival vehicle.

5.  **EXT. HIGHWAY - NIGHT**
    The trio makes camp. They are suddenly AMBUSHED by a gang of rabid, violent CLOWN THIEVES. A brutal, close-quarters fight ensues.

**SEQUENCE 2: THE MYSTERY**

6.  **INT. ABANDONED HOUSE - DAY**
    While studying a map, Astra notices strange, non-random patterns in the Carcazoid movements, suggesting they are being controlled.

7.  **EXT. WOODS - DAY**
    Seeking a safer location, the trio drives their armored truck through a dense, dangerous forest.

8.  **EXT. ABANDONED MALL - DUSK**
    They discover a massive, deserted shopping mall and decide to use it as a fortified base.

9.  **INT. MALL / SECURITY OFFICE - NIGHT**
    Gem finds old security logs and cryptic notes from previous survivors that mention "The Architect" and a safe haven called "The Nexus."

10. **EXT. CITY OUTSKIRTS - DAY**
    The trio arrives at a massive, decaying metropolis, following the clues toward The Nexus.

**SEQUENCE 3: CONFRONTATION**

11. **EXT. COURTHOUSE (THE NEXUS) - DAY**
    They find the courthouse—impossibly clean and guarded by heavily armed, emotionless CLONE GUARDS.

12. **EXT. COURTHOUSE GATES - DAY**
    The guards deny them entry. Gem steps forward, using her charisma and wit in a tense negotiation to talk their way inside.

13. **INT. THE NEXUS - DAY**
    They are escorted through sterile, white corridors, a stark contrast to the world outside. The facility is a high-tech fortress.

14. **INT. ARCHITECT'S OFFICE - DAY**
    They meet THE ARCHITECT, a man radiating intelligence and malice. He gleefully confesses to creating the apocalypse to rebuild the world in his image.

15. **INT. NEXUS / ATRIUM - DAY**
    The Architect unleashes his army. The trio must fight for their lives against a swarm of MUTANT CLOWNS and CARCAZOIDS in the main atrium.

16. **INT. ARCHITECT'S LAB - DAY**
    The trio fights their way to the lab for the final showdown with The Architect, who pilots a massive bio-mechanical monstrosity.

17. **INT. ARCHITECT'S LAB - CONTINUOUS**
    They defeat The Architect. Beaten, he reveals the secret: a vial of "MUTAGEN" that can create a cure, but it must be processed on Mars.

18. **EXT. NEXUS ROOFTOP - DAY**
    The trio fights their way to the rooftop helipad, where The Architect's sleek, private SPACECRAFT awaits.

19. **INT. SPACECRAFT - CONTINUOUS**
    They board the ship and initiate the launch sequence as the Nexus begins to self-destruct below them.

20. **EXT. SPACE - DAY**
    The spacecraft breaks through the atmosphere, leaving the scarred Earth behind. Their faces are grim but determined. They are heading to Mars, into another unknown.
