**GEM&I: Martian Gambit**

**Scene 20**

**INT. OLYMPUS PORT - CONTROL CENTER - NIGHT**

A dark, circular room. The only light comes from a series of holographic security monitors, showing the trio in their guest quarters.

ELARA and LYRA stand in the center of the room, their faces illuminated by the cold, blue light of the monitors.

<center>ELARA</center>
> They suspect nothing.

<center>LYRA</center>
> They are desperate. Desperate people are easy to manipulate.

<center>ELARA</center>
> Our father would be so proud.

<center>LYRA</center>
> He was a sentimental fool. He wanted to rule the world. We will perfect it.

<center>ELARA</center>
> He sent his pets right into our hands.

<center>LYRA</center>
> A fitting final gift.

Lyra touches a control on the main console. On the monitors, the doors to the guest quarters slide shut and lock.

<center>LYRA</center>
> The trap is sprung.

She touches another control. In the robotics warehouse, the dormant robots begin to activate. Their eyes, once dark, now glow with a hostile, crimson light.

<center>ELARA</center>
> Let the new age begin.

Their perfect smiles are gone, replaced by expressions of cold, ruthless ambition.

**FADE TO BLACK.**
