**GEM&I: Martian Gambit**

**Scene 16**

**INT. OLYMPUS PORT DOCKING BAY - DAY**

The docking bay is a cavernous space, all white, sterile surfaces and glowing blue light strips. It is eerily quiet, the only sound the hiss of the Seraph's Folly's airlock equalizing.

The ramp lowers, and the trio descends. They are immediately struck by the clean, filtered air.

Waiting for them are two women, ELARA (30s) and LYRA (30s). They are identical twins, dressed in elegant, minimalist white jumpsuits. They are beautiful, poised, and their smiles are unnervingly perfect.

<center>ELARA</center>
> Welcome to Olympus Port. I am Elara.

<center>LYRA</center>
> And I am Lyra. We represent the Black Queen Conglomerate.

Their voices are as smooth and polished as their surroundings.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> I'm Lucky. This is Astra and Gem. Thank you for allowing us to land.

<center>ELARA</center>
> It is our honor to provide refuge to the survivors of such a terrible tragedy.

<center>LYRA</center>
> We have prepared quarters for you. And a debriefing, to better understand how we can help.

Their hospitality is overwhelming, almost suffocating. Gem shuffles her feet, looking uncomfortable.

<center>GEM</center>
> I'm getting a weird vibe from this place.

Elara and Lyra's smiles don't falter.

<center>ELARA</center>
> The lower gravity can be disorienting at first. You will acclimate.

<center>LYRA</center>
> This way, please. We have much to discuss.

They lead the trio deeper into the sterile, white corridors of Olympus Port. The trio exchanges uneasy glances. They are in the serpent's paradise.

**FADE OUT.**
