**Title:** GEM&I: Martian Gambit

**Logline:** Three survivors of a world-ending plague of clowns and monsters must journey to a seemingly utopian Mars to acquire the technology to save Earth, only to discover the red planet is a carefully laid trap run by powerful and sinister forces.

---
**OUTLINE**
---

**ACT I: THE ESCAPE FROM EARTH**

**SCENE 1:** INT. NEXUS CONTROL ROOM - DAY
- The trio (LUCKY, ASTRA, GEM) assesses the post-Architect world. The virus is working, but it's too slow. They need a better delivery system.

**SCENE 2:** INT. NEXUS CONTROL ROOM - DAY
- Astra finds the solution in the Architect's logs: An army of pest-control robots on Mars and the Architect's private spacecraft, the "Seraph's Folly," at the nearby Ares Launch Facility. The mission is defined.

**SCENE 3:** EXT. SALVAGE YARD - DAY
- The trio scavenges for a vehicle suitable for the cross-country journey. They find a dilapidated but sturdy armored transport truck.

**SCENE 4:** INT. ARMORED TRUCK - MONTAGE
- A montage of the trio reinforcing the truck, adding scavenged armor, a battering ram, and weapon mounts. A "Cataclysm: DDA" style crafting sequence.

**SCENE 5:** EXT. BROKEN HIGHWAY - DAY
- The journey begins. The armored truck rumbles through a desolate, ruined landscape.

**SCENE 6:** EXT. HIGHWAY - NIGHT
- The truck is ambushed by a pack of feral, rabid CLOWN THIEVES. A short, violent battle ensues, testing the truck's new defenses.

**SCENE 7:** INT. ARMORED TRUCK - NIGHT
- The aftermath. The trio tends to wounds and repairs the truck. The constant threat of the world is reinforced.

**SCENE 8:** EXT. ABANDONED TOWN - DAY
- A necessary supply run for fuel and parts. The town is eerily quiet.

**SCENE 9:** INT. GENERAL STORE - DAY
- The quiet is a trap. They are attacked by dormant CARCAZOIDS. A tense, close-quarters fight.

**SCENE 10:** EXT. ARES LAUNCH FACILITY - DAY
- The trio arrives at the massive, surprisingly intact launch facility. The Seraph's Folly sits on the launchpad, a beacon of hope.

**SCENE 11:** INT. LAUNCH CONTROL - DAY
- Astra races to hack the launch systems while Lucky and Gem stand guard, fighting off the facility's automated security drones.

**SCENE 12:** INT. SERAPH'S FOLLY (COCKPIT) - CONTINUOUS
- They make it to the ship just as the launch sequence becomes irreversible. Liftoff.

**ACT II: THE SERPENT'S PARADISE**

**SCENE 13:** EXT. SPACE
- The ship ascends, revealing the scarred Earth below. A moment of awe and sadness.

**SCENE 14:** INT. SERAPH'S FOLLY (MESS HALL) - SPACE
- Mid-journey. The trio discusses their plan for Mars. They are wary of the "Black Queen Conglomerate" that runs the colony.

**SCENE 15:** EXT. MARS ORBIT
- Mars, the red planet. They receive a deceptively warm and professional welcome from OLYMPUS PORT control.

**SCENE 16:** INT. OLYMPUS PORT DOCKING BAY - DAY
- The bay is sterile and hyper-modern. They are greeted by ELARA and LYRA, the elegant and unnervingly charming daughters of the Black Queen.

**SCENE 17:** INT. OLYMPUS PORT CONFERENCE ROOM - DAY
- The trio makes their case. Elara and Lyra listen with practiced sympathy and agree to their request for the robot army with suspicious ease.

**SCENE 18:** INT. GUEST QUARTERS - NIGHT
- The trio is given luxurious quarters. They are immediately suspicious of how perfect everything is. Gem senses a "bad vibe."

**ACT III: THE TRAP SPRINGS**

**SCENE 19:** INT. OLYMPUS PORT - ROBOTICS WAREHOUSE - DAY
- Elara and Lyra give them a tour of the massive warehouse, filled with dormant robots. They explain the transfer process will take one cycle.

**SCENE 20:** INT. OLYMPUS PORT - CONTROL CENTER - NIGHT
- THE REVEAL. Elara and Lyra watch the trio on security monitors. They discuss how their "father," the Architect, sent his "pets" right into their hands. The trap is sprung. The doors lock. The robots begin to activate, but their eyes glow a hostile red.
