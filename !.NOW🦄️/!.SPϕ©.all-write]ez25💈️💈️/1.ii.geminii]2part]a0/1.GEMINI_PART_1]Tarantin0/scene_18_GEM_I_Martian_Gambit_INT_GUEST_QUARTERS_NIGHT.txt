**GEM&I: Martian Gambit**

**Scene 18**

**INT. GUEST QUARTERS - NIGHT**

The guest quarters are luxurious. Soft beds, ambient lighting, a viewport showing the stark beauty of the Martian landscape.

The trio is gathered in the main room, their faces a mixture of exhaustion and suspicion.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> I don't trust them.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Neither do I. They agreed too easily. There was no negotiation, no discussion of payment or terms.

<center>GEM</center>
> And they're creepy. It's like they're not real people. They're like... robots. But with better hair.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> We need to be careful. We'll get the robots, and then we get out of here. No delays, no side trips.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> I'll see if I can hack into their systems. Find out what they're really after.

<center>GEM</center>
> I'm going to take a shower. A real one. With water that isn't recycled from my own... well, you know.

She shudders.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Don't get too comfortable. This is a gilded cage.

<center>GEM</center>
> I know. But it's a really, really nice cage.

She heads to the bathroom. Lucky and Astra are left alone with their thoughts. The silence of the room is heavy with unspoken fears.

**FADE OUT.**
