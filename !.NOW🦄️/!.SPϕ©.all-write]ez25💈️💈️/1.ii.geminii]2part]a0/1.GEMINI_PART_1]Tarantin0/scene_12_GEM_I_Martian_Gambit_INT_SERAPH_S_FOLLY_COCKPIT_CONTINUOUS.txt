**GEM&I: Martian Gambit**

**Scene 12**

**INT. SERAPH'S FOLLY (COCKPIT) - CONTINUOUS**

The cockpit is a marvel of advanced technology. Sleek, minimalist controls and holographic displays. LUCKY and GEM are in the pilot and co-pilot seats, looking overwhelmed.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Astra, we're in. But I have no idea how to fly this thing.

<center>ASTRA (V.O.)</center>
> You don't have to. The launch is automated. Just strap yourselves in.

Through the cockpit window, they can see the launch tower retracting. The sound of the engines spooling up is a deafening roar.

INT. LAUNCH CONTROL - CONTINUOUS

The blast door is beginning to buckle under the assault from the drones outside. Sparks fly from the damaged frame.

ASTRA is still at the console, monitoring the launch sequence.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> T-minus thirty seconds.

EXT. LAUNCHPAD - CONTINUOUS

The Seraph's Folly is bathed in smoke and fire. The engines ignite, shaking the very ground.

INT. COCKPIT - CONTINUOUS

LUCKY and GEM are strapped into their seats, their faces pressed back by the G-forces.

<center>GEM</center>
> This is way better than the truck!

INT. LAUNCH CONTROL - CONTINUOUS

The blast door is breached. A security drone flies into the room, its weapons pointed at Astra.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> T-minus five... four... three... two... one...

She looks up at the drone, a small, defiant smile on her face.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> See you on the other side.

EXT. LAUNCHPAD - CONTINUOUS

The Seraph's Folly lifts off, a pillar of fire and smoke. It ascends into the sky, leaving the ruined world behind.

INT. LAUNCH CONTROL - CONTINUOUS

The drone fires. But before the blast can hit Astra, the force of the launch shakes the entire facility. The drone is thrown off balance, its shot going wide. The ceiling collapses, burying the drone and sealing the control room.

Astra is gone.

**FADE OUT.**
