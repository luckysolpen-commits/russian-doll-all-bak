**GEM&I: Martian Gambit**

**Scene 8**

**EXT. ABANDONED TOWN - DAY**

The town is a ghost town in the truest sense of the word. A single main street, lined with dilapidated buildings. A faded welcome sign reads "Welcome to Harmony Creek." The harmony is long gone.

The armored truck rolls slowly down the street, its engine a low growl in the oppressive silence. The trio is on high alert, their eyes scanning the empty windows and doorways.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> I don't like it. It's too clean.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> What do you mean?

<center>LUCKY</center>
> No bodies. No blood. No signs of a fight. It's like everyone just... vanished.

<center>GEM</center>
> Maybe they all went on vacation?

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Unlikely.

Lucky stops the truck in front of a building with a faded sign that reads "Harmony Creek General Store."

<center>LUCKY</center>
> This is it. We go in, we get what we need, and we get out. No detours, no sightseeing.

<center>GEM</center>
> You're no fun.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> I'm not trying to be fun. I'm trying to keep us alive.

He grabs a shotgun from the rack. Astra takes a pistol. Gem, a crowbar.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Stay close. Stay quiet. And watch your backs.

The trio exits the truck, the silence of the town swallowing them whole.

**FADE OUT.**
