**EXT. ARES LAUNCH FACILITY - DAY**

The landscape has changed. The rolling hills and forests have given way to a flat, desolate desert. And in the center of it, a monument to a forgotten age of ambition: the Ares Launch Facility.

It is massive. A sprawling complex of concrete and steel, surprisingly intact. The centerpiece is the launch tower, a colossal structure that seems to scrape the sky. And on the launchpad, gleaming in the harsh sunlight, is the SERAPH'S FOLLY.

It is a sleek, beautiful spacecraft, a stark contrast to the ruined world around it. It is a beacon of hope.

The armored truck is parked on a ridge overlooking the facility. The trio is looking at it through binoculars.
