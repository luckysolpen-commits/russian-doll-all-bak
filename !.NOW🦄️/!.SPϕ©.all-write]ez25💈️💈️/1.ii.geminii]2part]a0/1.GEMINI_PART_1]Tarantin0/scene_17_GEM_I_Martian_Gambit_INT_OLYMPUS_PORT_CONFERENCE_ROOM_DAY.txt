**GEM&I: Martian Gambit**

**Scene 17**

**INT. OLYMPUS PORT CONFERENCE ROOM - DAY**

A sleek, minimalist conference room. A long, white table. The trio sits on one side, Elara and Lyra on the other.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> ...and that is why we need the robots. With them, we can distribute the mutagen and reclaim our world.

Astra has laid out their entire story, from the Architect to the escape from Earth. Elara and Lyra have listened with practiced, unwavering sympathy.

<center>ELARA</center>
> Your story is... remarkable. You have endured so much.

<center>LYRA</center>
> And your courage is an inspiration to us all.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> So you'll help us?

Elara and Lyra look at each other, a silent, inscrutable communication passing between them.

<center>ELARA</center>
> The Black Queen Conglomerate is dedicated to the preservation of human life.

<center>LYRA</center>
> And we believe that your mission aligns with our core values.

<center>ELARA</center>
> Therefore, we will grant you full access to the terraforming units.

<center>LYRA</center>
> We will help you save your world.

Their agreement is immediate, unconditional, and deeply suspicious.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Just like that?

<center>ELARA</center>
> Just like that.

<center>LYRA</center>
> We are all human, after all. We must stand together.

Their smiles are as bright and cold as the Martian sun.

**FADE OUT.**
