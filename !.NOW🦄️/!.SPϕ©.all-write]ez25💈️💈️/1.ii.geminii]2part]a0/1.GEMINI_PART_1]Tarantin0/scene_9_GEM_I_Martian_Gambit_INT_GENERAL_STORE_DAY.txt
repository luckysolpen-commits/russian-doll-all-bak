**GEM&I: Martian Gambit**

**Scene 9**

**INT. GENERAL STORE - DAY**

The air inside is thick with the smell of dust and decay. Sunlight streams through grimy windows, illuminating floating dust motes. The shelves are mostly bare, but there are still some canned goods, tools, and other supplies scattered about.

The trio moves through the aisles, their footsteps echoing in the silence.

LUCKY keeps his shotgun at the ready. ASTRA scans the shelves, looking for specific items. GEM pokes at a can of expired beans with her crowbar.

<center>GEM</center>
> I dare you to eat it.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> No.

Lucky motions for them to be quiet. He points to the floor. There are strange, web-like structures in the corners of the room, made of a dried, chitinous material.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (Whispering)
> What is that?

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (Whispering)
> It looks like... a nest.

Suddenly, there is a sound from the back of the store. A soft, wet, clicking sound.

The trio freezes.

LUCKY gestures for them to move towards the front of the store. But it's too late.

From behind a row of shelves, a CARCAZOID emerges. It is a grotesque creature, a vaguely humanoid shape made of chitin and exposed muscle. It was dormant, camouflaged against the grime, but now it is awake.

It lets out a piercing shriek, and two more CARCAZOIDS drop from the ceiling.

The fight is sudden and brutal. Lucky fires his shotgun, the blast echoing like a thunderclap in the enclosed space. The Carcazoid stumbles back, but isn't killed.

Astra fires her pistol, aiming for the creature's head. Gem swings her crowbar, smashing one of the creatures in its spindly legs.

The fight is a chaotic dance of violence in the dusty aisles of the general store. The Carcazoids are fast and relentless, their claws slashing through the air.

LUCKY manages to kill one with a point-blank shotgun blast. ASTRA takes down another with a well-aimed shot to its glowing eye-like organ.

The last Carcazoid lunges at Gem. She screams, but before the creature can reach her, Lucky impales it with a metal rod he pulls from a broken shelf.

The creature collapses, its death shriek echoing through the store.

The trio stands in the sudden silence, breathing heavily. They are covered in dust and ichor.

<center>GEM</center>
> Well... that was fun.

**FADE OUT.**
