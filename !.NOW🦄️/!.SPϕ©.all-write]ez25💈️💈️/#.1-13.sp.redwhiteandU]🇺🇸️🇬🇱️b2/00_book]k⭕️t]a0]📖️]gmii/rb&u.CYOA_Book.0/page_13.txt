You ask Jax about the Red Queen. His face darkens. "The Red Queen is a plague on this galaxy," he says. "She and her army of clones have conquered dozens of worlds. They are ruthless, efficient, and they never stop. I've lost more friends to the Red Queen than I can count."

He looks at you, his eyes full of a strange mixture of pity and respect. "You're lucky to be free, kid," he says. "Don't ever take it for granted."
