You are a clone. You were made for more than this. A life of smuggling and petty crime is not your destiny.

You start searching for a purpose. You hear whispers of the Underground Xircus, a rebellion of clones who fight for freedom. You also hear rumors of a more radical faction, led by a legendary figure known as "The Prophet," who preaches a gospel of total war against the Empire, even if it means sacrificing everything.

You have a choice to make. Do you seek out the established rebellion, with its more measured approach? Or do you seek out The Prophet, hoping to find a cause worthy of your abilities?

*   If you seek out The Prophet, turn to page 135.
