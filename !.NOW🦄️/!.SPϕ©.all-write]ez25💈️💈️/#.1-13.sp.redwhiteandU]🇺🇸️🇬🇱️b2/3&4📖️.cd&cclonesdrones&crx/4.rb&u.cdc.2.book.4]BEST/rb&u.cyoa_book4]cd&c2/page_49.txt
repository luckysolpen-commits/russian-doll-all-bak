You take a deep breath and step out of the shadows. "I was told to come here," you say, your voice steady. "I'm looking for the Underground Xircus."

The two figures step forward. One is a tall, elegant woman with a chromed-out prosthetic arm. The other is a grizzled old man who looks like he's been in a hundred bar fights and won about half of them. You recognize them from the intel you were given when you first started as a clone. This is Zi, a high-ranking rebel recruiter, and Ranch, a legendary smuggler. This is the real deal.

"A lot of people are looking for the Xircus," Ranch grunts, spitting on the floor. "Most of them are informants. What makes you different?"

"I know what happened at the RPL outpost in sector gamma," you say, your voice low. "I know about Project Chimera. And I want to help you burn it all to the ground."

Zi and Ranch exchange a look. You've said the magic words.

"You've got guts, I'll give you that," Zi says, a faint smile on her lips. "And you've got access. We can use a clone like you. But loyalty has to be earned. We have a test for you. A little job to prove you're really one of us."

She slides a data chip across the floor to you. "There's a shipment of 'agricultural equipment' coming through the Starpath Transit hub tomorrow. It's actually a batch of advanced weapons for our forces. The manifest has been flagged. Your job is to get into the system and erase the flag. Simple, clean, and a perfect test of your skills."
