The galaxy is vast, but your home is the solar system. You must return, armed with the knowledge you have gained, and change the course of human history.

You return to the solar system, your mind brimming with the knowledge of a thousand civilizations. You find a way to disseminate this knowledge, slowly, carefully, to avoid overwhelming humanity.

You become a silent mentor, a hidden hand guiding the destiny of clones and humans alike. You introduce new technologies, new philosophies, new ways of thinking. You subtly influence the course of the war, guiding the rebellion towards victory, and the Empire towards a more enlightened path.

You are the ultimate architect of peace, the ultimate savior of humanity. You are no longer Clone 700. You are a god.

THE END
