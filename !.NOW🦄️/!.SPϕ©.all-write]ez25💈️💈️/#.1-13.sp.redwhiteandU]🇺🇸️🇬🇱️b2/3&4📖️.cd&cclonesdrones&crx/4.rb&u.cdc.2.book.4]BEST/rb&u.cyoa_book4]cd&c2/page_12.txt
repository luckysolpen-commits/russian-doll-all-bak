Every part of your training screams that this is a mistake. But your gut, that strange new voice inside you, says otherwise. You power down your weapon systems. The red targeting lights on your console blink out one by one.

"Alright," you broadcast. "Go. Get out of here."

The clown in the decorated helmet gives a slight bow. "A clone with sense. I'm impressed. The name's qynx, by the way. Maybe we'll see you around."

The second clown ship, the one that was flanking you, slowly powers down its weapons as well. The two vessels lift off, their engines surprisingly quiet, and disappear into the darkness of the tunnel system. You are left alone with the smoking ruins of the Redplanet Labs outpost.

You have a choice to make. You can report that the clowns ambushed you and escaped, a simple lie that will likely be accepted. Or you can go inside the ruined lab and see for yourself what they were so keen on "liberating." What were they doing in there that a clown would risk their life for? Your curiosity is a dangerous, burning thing.
