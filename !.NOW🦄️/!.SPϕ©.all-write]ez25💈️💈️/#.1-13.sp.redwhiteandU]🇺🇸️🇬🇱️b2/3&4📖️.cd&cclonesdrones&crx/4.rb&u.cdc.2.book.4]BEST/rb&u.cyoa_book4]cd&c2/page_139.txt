You are a loyal clone. You will not betray your oath.

"I will not cooperate," you say, your voice firm. "I will not betray the Empire."

The leader sighs. "A pity," she says. "You could have been a valuable asset. But loyalty, misplaced or otherwise, is still loyalty."

You are thrown into a holding cell. You are not tortured. You are not interrogated. You are simply left there, to rot.

Days turn into weeks. Weeks turn into months. You are forgotten. You are irrelevant.

You are a loyal clone. And you will die a loyal clone. In a forgotten cell, in a forgotten corner of a forgotten base.

THE END
