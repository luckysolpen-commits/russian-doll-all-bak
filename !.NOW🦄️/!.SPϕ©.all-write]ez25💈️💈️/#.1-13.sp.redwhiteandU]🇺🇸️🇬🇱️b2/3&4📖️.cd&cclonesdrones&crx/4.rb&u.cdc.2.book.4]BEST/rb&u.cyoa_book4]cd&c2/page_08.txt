A surge of ambition, hot and sharp, cuts through your disciplined mind. Power. This is power. The ability to transcend the fragile clone body and become a weapon of legend. This is the honor you have been trained to crave.

"I am ready to serve," you say, your voice unwavering. "In any form the Empire requires."

The Proctor smiles, a thin, predatory expression. "Excellent. Not all candidates are so... enlightened."

You are taken to a secret medical facility deep in the Lunar core, a place that exists on no official map. Here, under the watchful eyes of the Blood Queen Monarchy's own bio-engineers, the process begins. It is agony. Your body is flooded with mutagens, your DNA rewritten, your bones broken and reshaped. You spend weeks in a fluid-filled tank, conscious and aware as your flesh twists and your mind is assaulted by primal, predatory instincts. They teach you to control the hunger, to harness the rage. To become a scalpel, not a sledgehammer.

You emerge... different. Taller, stronger, with a chitinous layer just beneath your skin. Your senses are heightened to a terrifying degree. You can smell the fear-sweat of a technician from fifty meters away. This is just the first stage. The full transformation can be triggered by a specific neural impulse, turning you from clone to monster in seconds.

Your first mission is simple. A test. A "clown sympathizer" has been identified within the Solar Empire's diplomatic corps on Mars. You are to be sent back to the Red Planet, inserted into the diplomat's security detail, and at the designated moment, you will eliminate the target and anyone else in the room. There must be no witnesses. Only a monster.
