The truth is a bitter pill. You are a tool, a weapon, designed and unleashed by an Empire that cares nothing for its own. You cannot continue to serve such a corrupt system.

"I refuse," you say, your voice firm. "I will not hunt my own kind. I will not be a pawn in your games."

Your handler's smile vanishes. Her eyes turn to ice. "You forget your place, Clone 700. You are ours. To refuse is to break your conditioning. And we have very effective ways of dealing with broken tools."

She signals to the guards. Two hulking figures enter the room. They are not clones. They are larger, stronger, clearly enhanced. These are the "effective ways."

You know you cannot fight them. But you can run. You use your Carcazod abilities, transforming instantly. The guards are surprised, but they quickly recover. You burst through the door, leaving them scrambling.

You are a rogue Carcazod, hunted by the Empire, feared by the clowns. You are alone. But you are free.
