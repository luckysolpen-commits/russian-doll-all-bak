You are a social creature, a product of a collective. Solitude is not in your nature. You need to find people.

You travel for days, following the faint, almost invisible trails that crisscross the Martian desert. Finally, you find what you are looking for: a small, dusty settlement huddled in the shadow of a massive canyon.

The town is called "Last Chance," and it is well-named. It is a haven for smugglers, criminals, and deserters. It is a place where no one asks questions, as long as you have credits to spend.

You find work as a cargo hauler, using your piloting skills to transport illicit goods from one end of the Farland region to the other. It is a dangerous, thankless job, but it pays the bills.

You have disappeared. You are no longer Clone 700. You are just another face in the crowd, another cog in the Martian underworld. You have found a new life, of a sort. But it is a life on the fringes, a life of constant danger and paranoia.
