You land your battered ship in the docking bay. The air is warm and smells of soil and growing things. It's the first time you've smelled real plants since the training vats.

A group of clowns is there to meet you. They are a motley crew, their armor a patchwork of different styles and colors. They are led by a tall, imposing figure with a chrome-plated helmet and a long, flowing coat. This is not a soldier. This is a showman.

"Welcome, brother," the showman says, his voice a theatrical boom. "Welcome to The Big Top! We are the Underground Xircus, and you are our newest star!"

You have found the heart of the rebellion. It's not a military base. It's a circus. A chaotic, vibrant, and fiercely independent community of artists, engineers, soldiers, and dreamers.

You are taken on a tour of the asteroid. You see vast hydroponic farms, ingenious recycling plants, and workshops where clowns build and repair their own equipment. You see a school, a hospital, and a theater. This is not just a rebellion. This is a civilization.

You are a hero here. The story of your escape, of your journey across the solar system, has already spread. You are a symbol of hope, a testament to the fact that any clone can break free.
