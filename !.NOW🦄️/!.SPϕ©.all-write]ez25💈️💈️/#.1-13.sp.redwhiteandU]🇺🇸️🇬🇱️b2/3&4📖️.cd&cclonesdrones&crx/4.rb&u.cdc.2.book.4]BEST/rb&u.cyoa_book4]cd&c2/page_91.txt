"I'll do it," you say. "Give me a team and a target."

The hawk-faced clown, whose name is Jex, gives you a grim smile. "You're the team," he says. "We're not risking our own on a rookie. You'll go in alone."

It's a suicide mission. But you have no choice.

The next day, you are perched on a ridge overlooking a narrow canyon. The CHEMMARS INDUSTRIES convoy is due any minute. You have a detonator, a handful of stolen explosives, and a plan.

You have planted the explosives on the canyon walls, creating a bottleneck. Your job is to trigger a rockslide, trapping the convoy, and then... what? You can't take on a full convoy escort by yourself.

The convoy arrives. Two heavily armed escort vehicles and three large cargo haulers. You trigger the explosion. The rockslide is perfect. The convoy is trapped.

The escort vehicles' gunners open fire on your position. You are pinned down. You have completed the first part of your mission. But you have no way to complete the second. You are a sitting duck.
