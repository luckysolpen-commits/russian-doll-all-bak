"You're right," you say, trying to sound scared and desperate. "I was captured by the clowns. They destroyed my ship and took me prisoner. They tortured me for information. I just escaped."

The intelligence officer raises an eyebrow. "Escaped? How?"

"They got careless," you lie, thinking fast. "I managed to overpower a guard and steal his weapon. I fought my way out. I was trying to get back to friendly territory when your patrol found me."

It's a flimsy story, full of holes. But it's better than the truth. The officer listens patiently. When you are finished, he smiles, a cold, humorless expression.

"A heroic tale," he says. "Unfortunately for you, we recovered the flight recorder from your Digger. It shows no signs of an ambush. It shows you disobeying orders, flying to the RPL outpost, and then... nothing. The recorder was fried in an explosion that originated from inside your own ship."

He leans in close. "You are a very bad liar, 700. And we have very little tolerance for bad liars."

He snaps his fingers. Two large, heavily armed guards enter the room. They are not clones. They are hulking, gene-spliced monstrosities, the personal bodyguards of the Blood Queen.

"Take him to the re-education center," the officer says. "Let's see if we can't improve his honesty."

You are dragged away to a place from which no one ever returns.
