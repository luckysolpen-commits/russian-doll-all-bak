You are a general. You will lead your people into battle.

You prepare Scraptown for war. You fortify its defenses, you rally your troops, you prepare for the inevitable clash.

The Imperial forces arrive, led by a Carcazod general. It's a terrifying sight. The Carcazod transforms, its monstrous form leading the charge.

But Scraptown is ready. Your troops fight with a ferocity born of desperation and loyalty. You lead from the front, your own Digger-class patrol craft (salvaged and modified) a blur of plasma fire and evasive maneuvers.

The battle is long and bloody. The Carcazod general is a formidable opponent, but you are a better commander. You use the terrain, you use your tactics, you use your knowledge of Imperial weaknesses.

Finally, after hours of brutal combat, the Imperial forces retreat. The Carcazod general is dead. Scraptown has won.

You are the hero of Scraptown, the liberator of the Mutangia region. Your legend grows.
