You have proven yourself. Whether through brutal efficiency as a monster or through unwavering integrity as a clone, you have caught the eye of the powers that be.

You are now a member of a secret, elite unit, operating directly under the command of the Blood Queen's most trusted advisors. Your new handler is the same woman who recruited you, the official with the voice of silk and steel. She informs you that your unit's purpose is to deal with threats to the Empire that are too sensitive for the regular military. You are an off-the-books solution for off-the-books problems.

"Your first mission is a delicate one," she tells you, her face projected on a holographic display. "We have a problem within the Mutangia region on Mars. A charismatic leader has emerged, uniting several of the smaller clans, and preaching a doctrine of open rebellion. Our intelligence suggests he is more than just a rabble-rouser. We believe he is an uncontrolled Carcazod, a rogue monster."

She continues, "Your job is to infiltrate his organization, determine the extent of his power, and, if necessary, eliminate him. You will have no backup. You will be disavowed if captured. The fate of the entire region rests on your shoulders."

This is a new level of the game. You are no longer a simple soldier. You are a spy, an assassin, a monster-hunter, all in the name of the Empire.
