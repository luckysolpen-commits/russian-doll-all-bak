"I'll do it," you say, a strange sense of calm settling over you. "Send me the coordinates for the rendezvous."

"You're a brave clone, 700," qynx says, her voice tinged with respect. "Or a very foolish one. Good luck." The channel goes dead.

The coordinates appear on your console. You have your meeting point. Now you just have to survive to get there.

You turn your Digger towards the direction of the incoming patrol and you fly like a madman. You fire your weapons into the air, you bounce off the tunnel walls, you do everything you can to make a scene. It works. Three Imperial patrol craft appear on your scanner, closing fast.

You lead them on a chase through the labyrinthine tunnels of the Valles Marineris. They are good pilots, but you are better. You know the tunnels, you know the ship. You push the Digger to its absolute limit, your knuckles white on the controls.

You manage to stay ahead of them, but your ship is taking a beating. A stray shot takes out your rear shields. Another damages your port engine. You are losing speed. They are gaining on you. You need to lose them, and fast. You see a narrow, unmapped service tunnel coming up on your right. It's a tight squeeze, and it could be a dead end. But it's your only chance.
