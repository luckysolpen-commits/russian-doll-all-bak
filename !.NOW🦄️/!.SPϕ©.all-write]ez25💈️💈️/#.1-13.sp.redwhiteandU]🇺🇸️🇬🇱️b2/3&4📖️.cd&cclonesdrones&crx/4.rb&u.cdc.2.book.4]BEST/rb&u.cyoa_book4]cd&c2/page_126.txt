You are a spy. You are a clown. You are the ultimate double agent.

You maintain your cover within the Empire, rising through the ranks, gaining access to even more sensitive information. You become a trusted advisor to high-ranking officials, a silent partner in their schemes.

But every piece of information you gather, every secret you uncover, is immediately relayed to the rebellion. You become their eyes and ears in the heart of the enemy. You save countless lives, prevent countless atrocities.

You are a hero, but no one knows it. You live a life of constant deception, a tightrope walk between loyalty and betrayal. Your soul is a battlefield, constantly warring between the person you pretend to be and the person you truly are.

One day, you get a message from Zi. "It's time," she says. "The final push. We need you to initiate a system-wide shutdown from the inside. It will cripple their defenses, giving us the opening we need to strike at the Nexus."

This is it. The moment you have been waiting for. The ultimate act of betrayal.
