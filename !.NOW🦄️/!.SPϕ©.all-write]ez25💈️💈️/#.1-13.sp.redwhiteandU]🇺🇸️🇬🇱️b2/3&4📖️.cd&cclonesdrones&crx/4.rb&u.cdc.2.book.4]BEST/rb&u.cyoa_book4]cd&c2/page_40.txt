The beast is in control.

Your disciplined mind, the centuries of clone training, all of it evaporates in a red mist of pure, primal hunger. These are not people. They are prey. The screams are a symphony. The fear is a delicious spice in the air.

You are a whirlwind of claws and teeth. The two officials are dispatched in seconds, their bodies torn asunder. Cassian, the traitor, tries to fight back, grabbing a heavy statuette from his desk. The object, which would have been a dangerous weapon against a normal clone, glances harmlessly off your chitinous hide. You savor his terror for a moment before you end it.

You stand in the silence, surrounded by carnage, dripping with the blood of your victims. Your mission was to eliminate the target and any witnesses. Mission accomplished. But as the bloodlust begins to recede, a cold horror takes its place. This was too easy. You enjoyed it too much.

You trigger the de-transformation. The process is painful, your body shrinking back to its clone form, the monstrous shell receding beneath your skin. You are Clone 700 again, standing in a room that looks like a butcher's shop. You have to clean this up. Make it look like a clown attack, as you were instructed. But you know the truth. This was not a clown attack. This was the work of a monster. And the monster is you.
