You are tired of compromise. You are tired of half-measures. You want a revolution.

You seek out The Prophet, a legendary figure whispered about in the darkest corners of the Martian underworld. He is a former clone, an engineer who designed many of the Empire's most brutal weapons. He saw the truth, and he broke free. Now, he preaches a gospel of total war, a complete annihilation of the Empire, even if it means sacrificing everything.

You find him in a hidden bunker, surrounded by a small, fanatical following. He is old, his face scarred, his eyes burning with a righteous fury.

"You seek purpose, Clone 700?" he asks, his voice raspy. "You seek to burn the Empire to ash? Then you have come to the right place."

He offers you a choice. You can join his crusade, a bloody, uncompromising war against the Empire. Or you can turn away, returning to the relative peace of the Underground Xircus.
