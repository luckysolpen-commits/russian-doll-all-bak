You are bound and thrown into the back of the patrol craft. The journey is short. You are taken to a small, forward operating base carved out of the rock. There, you are thrown into a holding cell.

Hours later, an intelligence officer arrives. He is not a clone. He has the cold, calculating eyes of a man who has seen too much and enjoyed all of it. He questions you for hours. You stick to your story: you were ambushed, you survived, you were trying to get back to friendly territory. He doesn't seem to believe you.

"You are a long way from home, 700," he says, circling your chair. "And we have reports of a... disturbance... in your sector. A Redplanet Labs facility was hit. Two clown ships were destroyed. And one Imperial Digger was lost. You wouldn't know anything about that, would you?"

He knows. Maybe not everything, but enough. He is trying to get you to confess. Your life hangs in the balance.
