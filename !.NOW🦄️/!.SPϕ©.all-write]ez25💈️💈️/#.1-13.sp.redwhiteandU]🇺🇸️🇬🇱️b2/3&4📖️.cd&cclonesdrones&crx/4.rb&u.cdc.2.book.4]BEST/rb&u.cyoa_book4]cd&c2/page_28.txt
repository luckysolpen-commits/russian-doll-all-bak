The clown's last words echo in your mind. "Underground Xircus." A safe haven. A place for people like him... and maybe, now, people like you.

You climb into the cockpit of the wrecked clown ship. The systems are mostly dead, but the emergency power is still flowing to the nav-computer. You access the system. It's password-protected, but the dying clown must have anticipated this. A sticky note is attached to the corner of the screen. On it is a single, hand-written word: "Ranch."

You enter the password. The system unlocks, revealing a starmap. A single destination is plotted: a hidden asteroid base in the Kuiper Belt, far from the prying eyes of the Empire. The base is codenamed "The Big Top." This is the heart of the Underground Xircus.

You can't get there in this wreck. But you can transfer the data to a portable drive. You now hold a map to the heart of the rebellion. Your own ship is a wreck, but maybe, just maybe, you can get it space-worthy enough for one last trip. Or you could try to get your comms online and call for help, but that would mean explaining what you were doing in a clown's nav-computer.
