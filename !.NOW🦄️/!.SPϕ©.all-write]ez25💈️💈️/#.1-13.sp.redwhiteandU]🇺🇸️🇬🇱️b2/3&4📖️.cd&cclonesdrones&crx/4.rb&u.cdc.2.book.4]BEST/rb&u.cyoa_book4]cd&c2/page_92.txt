"I'm a soldier, not a bandit," you say, your voice firm. "I kill for a cause, not for profit. Give me a real mission, and I will prove my loyalty. But I will not be your hired thug."

Jex, the hawk-faced clown, scoffs. "A clone with principles. How quaint." He turns to the other guards. "We have no use for a moralist. Throw him out."

You are stripped of your weapon and marched to the edge of the settlement. "Don't come back," Jex warns.

You are alone in the desert again, with no weapon, no supplies, and no allies. You have failed to join the rebellion. Your principles have left you with nothing.

Your only hope is to find another settlement, another chance. Or to give up, and let the desert claim you.
