You've had enough of war. You keep your head down, do your work, and enjoy the simple pleasures of a life without conflict. You tinker with machines, you share drinks with Gus at the bar, you listen to Sal's stories of the old days.

It is a good life, a peaceful life. But it is a life lived on borrowed time.

One day, it runs out. An Imperial patrol, larger and more heavily armed than any you have seen before, descends on Scraptown. They surround the settlement, their weapons aimed at the terrified, untrained clowns.

A voice booms over a loudspeaker. "Surrender and you will be granted a merciful death. Resist, and you will be annihilated."

There is no hope of victory. The clowns are outnumbered, outgunned, and outmaneuvered. Gus looks at you, a desperate hope in his eyes. "What do we do?" he asks.

You are just one clone. You cannot save them. But you can die with them.

THE END
