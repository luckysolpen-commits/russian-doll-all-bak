You stay in place, your every move calculated, every word chosen with care. You watch The Proctor's hunt for the mole with a detached fascination, noting his tactics, his methods. You are a master of deception, and you use every trick in the book to cover your tracks.

The Proctor never finds you. His hunt eventually leads him to a dead end, a low-ranking clone who took the fall for your actions. You are safe.

You are in a unique position, a ghost within the machine, with access to both sides of the conflict. You could use this to become a kingmaker, to play both sides against each other for your own benefit. Or you could become the ultimate double agent, feeding critical intelligence to the rebellion while maintaining your cover.
