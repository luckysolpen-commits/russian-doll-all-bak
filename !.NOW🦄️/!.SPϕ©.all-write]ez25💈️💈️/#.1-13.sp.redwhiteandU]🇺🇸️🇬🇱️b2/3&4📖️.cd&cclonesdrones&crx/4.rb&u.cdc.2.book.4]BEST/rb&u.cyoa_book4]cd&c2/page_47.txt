You didn't survive this long by being reckless. You find a spot in the shadows overlooking the settlement and you watch.

You see a clear hierarchy. There's a group of heavily armed clowns who seem to be the law. There's a shrewd-looking female clown running the main bar and trading post; she seems to be the one with the real power. You see mechanics, farmers, and gamblers. It's a society in miniature, a microcosm of rebellion.

You also see that they are wary. A newcomer who caused a disturbance was quickly and brutally thrown out. But another, who offered a piece of salvaged military tech, was welcomed with open arms. It seems that your value here is determined by what you can offer.

You have nothing but the suit on your back and the pistol on your hip. Your sidearm is Imperial-issue, a valuable piece of tech. But offering it would also clearly identify you as a recent deserter. You could also offer your services as a trained pilot or soldier, but that might arouse suspicion.
