Page 113: Pursuing the Blood Trophy Collectors

The Blood Trophy Collectors operate by gathering intelligence through the collection of trophies from defeated opponents. This elite group believes that information is most valuable when it's literally part of a defeated enemy, and they've developed sophisticated methods for extracting and preserving intelligence-bearing materials.

Three trophy collection methods are practiced: The Memory Extraction Technique, where neural tissue containing specific knowledge is preserved and studied; The Capability Sampling System, where biological or technological components that granted special abilities are collected and analyzed; and The Honor Trophy Protocol, where symbolic items representing defeats are collected to enhance the collectors' standing within the Monarchy hierarchy.

As you observe their operations, you realize the Blood Trophy Collectors have developed an alternative approach to intelligence that's more direct and immediate than other methods. Their collections contain actual biological and technological samples that provide concrete insights into enemy capabilities.

Your Solar Empire background emphasized information as abstract data, but the Trophy Collectors treat it as a physical resource to be harvested, preserved, and utilized. This approach provides unique insights into the actual capabilities of transformed beings rather than just theoretical knowledge.

The collectors' methods are brutal but effective, and their collections represent the most comprehensive database of transformation-related materials in the Monarchy. Access to their resources could provide unprecedented understanding of carcazod capabilities.
