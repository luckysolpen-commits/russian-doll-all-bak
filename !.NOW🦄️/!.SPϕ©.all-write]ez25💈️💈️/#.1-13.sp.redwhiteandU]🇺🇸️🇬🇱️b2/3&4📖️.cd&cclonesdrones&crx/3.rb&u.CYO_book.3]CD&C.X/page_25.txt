Page 25: Infiltrating the High-Level Meeting

Your stealth drone brings you to a location that shouldn't exist in the official tunnel maps – a neutral chamber where representatives from all four major factions are meeting in secret. The architectural elements in this area show influences from each faction's aesthetic, creating a bizarre but functional meeting space that exists outside the normal political boundaries.

Through your sensors, you identify the three most important elements of the meeting: the main conference table where the faction leaders are discussing something called "Project Carcazod Supremacy," a side chamber where what appear to be carcazod specimens are being displayed as if they're weapons, and a quantum communication array that suggests the meeting is being monitored or even controlled from a distant location.

The discussion focuses on a proposal that would end the current factional conflicts by creating a unified carcazod army that would serve all governments jointly. The representatives seem to be close to an agreement, suggesting this secret collaboration has been in development for a long time.

Your presence here represents a major intelligence coup – you may be witnessing the formation of a new power structure that would fundamentally change the political landscape of Mars. However, your stealth systems detect other surveillance devices that suggest you're not the only one monitoring this meeting.

An emergency session is being called in the side chamber, where the representatives are examining what appears to be the corpse of a failed carcazod transformation – a warning about the dangers of the process they're discussing.
