Page 41: The Deep Thermal Vents

"The thermal vents are where Mars' internal heat creates unique conditions," explains your guide. "The extreme temperatures and chemical compositions have created environments where the boundary between biological and technological systems breaks down. Many believe the original carcazod transformation processes were discovered here."

As you descend toward the thermal vents, your drone's heat shielding is tested to its limits. Three distinct thermal zones present different opportunities: the Lower Heat Chambers, where consistent high temperatures may have preserved ancient transformation equipment; the Chemical Convergence Pools, where unique Martian compounds have formed in concentrations impossible elsewhere; and the Pressure Integration Wells, where the combination of heat and pressure creates conditions that enable unique biological-mechanical fusion.

The air around the vents carries suspended particles that seem to respond to electromagnetic fields, creating patterns that suggest a form of primitive intelligence. Your sensors detect evidence of artificial structures that have been partially dissolved and reformed by the extreme conditions - possibly the remains of ancient laboratories.

As you approach the main vent complex, you realize that the heat and chemical environment here could accelerate or even trigger carcazod transformation processes. The boundary between different types of matter becomes fluid in these conditions, which may be why the original transformation research began here.

Your drone detects three areas of particular interest that may hold the secrets of ancient Martian transformation technology: a laboratory that appears to have been preserved by the extreme conditions; a chamber where thermal and chemical processes continue to operate after millennia; and a fusion zone where the distinction between different types of matter has completely broken down.
