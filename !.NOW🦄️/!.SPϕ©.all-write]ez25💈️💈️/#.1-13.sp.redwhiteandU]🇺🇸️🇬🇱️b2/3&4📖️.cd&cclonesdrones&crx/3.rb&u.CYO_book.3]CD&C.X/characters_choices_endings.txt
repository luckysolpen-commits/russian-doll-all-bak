CYOD#3: Clones, Drones and Carcazods - Characters, Choices & Endings

**KEY CHARACTERS:**

**Player Character (PC):**
- Clone Designation: Various model types with different capabilities
- Drone Operator: Specialized in Martian tunnel navigation
- Background: Created in vats, trained for specific faction, or self-made operator

**Major NPCs:**
1. The Red Czar - Leader of the clone factions on Mars, mysterious figure who controls carcazod transformation access
2. Neon Clown - Legendary rogue clown who hunts both clowns and clones, possible mentor/enemy
3. Prime Circuit - AI leader of the Robot State, seeks to understand biological transformation
4. Blood Duchess - Ruler of the Blood Queen Monarchy, obsessed with the honor of SS. SOL service
5. Tunnel Whisper - Anonymous information broker in the underground, knows everyone's secrets
6. The Carcazod - Former clone who achieved transformation, now something beyond human understanding
7. Moon Handler - Contact person for the lunar training/trade base, keeper of ancient secrets

**CRITICAL CHOICES:**

**Choice 1 (Early): Identity Declaration**
- Choose to remain loyal to your assigned faction
- Declare independence and become a rogue clown hunter
- Accept an offer to become a spy for another faction
- Seek the path to carcazod transformation

**Choice 2 (Mid): Alliance Formation**
- Ally with other clones against the clown underground
- Form a network of fellow rogue operators
- Infiltrate the highest levels of government as a spy
- Begin dangerous experiments to achieve transformation

**Choice 3 (Advance): Moral Crossroads**
- Report back to your original faction as a loyal agent
- Turn against your original faction and work to destroy it
- Abandon humanity entirely for the carcazod transformation
- Seek to unite the factions against a common threat

**Choice 4 (Late): The Ultimate Decision**
- Sacrifice yourself for the SS. SOL to achieve spiritual ascension
- Destroy the carcazod transformation process for everyone
- Become the supreme carcazod and rule the Martian underground
- Bridge the gap between all factions and create a new society

**POSSIBLE ENDINGS:**

**Loyalist Endings:**
A) The Honored Clone - Serve your faction so well you're selected for SS. SOL service, dying with honor as your soul fuels the great ship
B) The Faction Hero - Rise to high rank in your government, establishing new policies that reshape Mars
C) The Martyred Agent - Give your life in service to your faction, becoming a legend among your people

**Rogue Clown Endings:**
A) The Final Clown - Successfully hunt down the last of the old clown generation, becoming the sole ruler of the underground
B) The Hunted - Become the most wanted being on Mars, living in constant danger but ultimate freedom
C) The Clown King - Unite the scattered clown communities into a new underground nation

**Spy Endings:**
A) The Double Agent - Successfully manipulate all factions until they are too weak to oppose you
B) The Leaker - Reveal all secrets of the factions, creating chaos that allows for a new world order
C) The Burned Spy - Your cover is blown, but you've gathered enough information to blackmail the governments

**Transformation Endings:**
A) The First Carcazod - Successfully undergo the mutation process, becoming the first true carcazod
B) The Failed Experiment - Partially transform, becoming something neither clone nor human
C) The Carcazod Sage - Achieve transformation but retain your humanity, becoming a guide to others
D) The Metamorphosis Master - Master the transformation process and control who can become carcazods

**Additional Endings:**
A) The Underground Navigator - Master the tunnel systems so completely that you become the ultimate guide between surface and depth
B) The Martian Unifier - Bring all four factions together, ending conflict through diplomacy or conquest
C) The Moonward Bound - Complete your journey to the lunar base, discovering secrets that change everything
D) The Eternal Drifter - Reject all factions and paths, becoming a nomad of the Martian tunnels forever
