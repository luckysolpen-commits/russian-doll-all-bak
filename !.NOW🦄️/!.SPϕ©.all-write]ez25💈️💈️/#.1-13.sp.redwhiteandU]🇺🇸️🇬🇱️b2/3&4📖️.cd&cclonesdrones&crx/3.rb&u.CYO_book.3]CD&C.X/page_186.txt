Page 186: Attempting the Universal Synthesis

As you attempt the Universal Synthesis in the final integration chamber, your consciousness begins to unify all aspects of transformation - biological, technological, and quantum - into a single cohesive form. As the universal synthesis proceeds, you experience the sensation of becoming something that transcends the distinction between different types of existence. Your form, consciousness, and capabilities integrate into a unified whole that operates beyond normal limitations.

Your synthesis attempt reveals the ultimate goal of carcazod development - the creation of beings who have unified all aspects of their existence into a single, perfected form that operates at maximum efficiency and capability.

The universal synthesis provides the pinnacle of transformation achievement, but also risks losing connection to normal existence as you become something that transcends conventional categories of being.
