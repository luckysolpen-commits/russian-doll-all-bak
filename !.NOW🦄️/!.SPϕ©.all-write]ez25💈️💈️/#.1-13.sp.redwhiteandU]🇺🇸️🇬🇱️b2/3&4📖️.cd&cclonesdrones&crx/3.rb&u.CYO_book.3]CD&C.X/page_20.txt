Page 20: Following the Northern Heat Signature

The steady heat signature leads your drone Scout-X7 through a series of increasingly warm passages that seem to descend deeper into Mars' geological structure than any of the mapped tunnels. Your thermal sensors indicate that the heat source is not volcanic in nature, but something else entirely – possibly artificial, or perhaps biological on a massive scale.

As you progress, you begin to notice that the tunnel walls are lined with what appear to be organic containment units, each holding a different stage of some kind of transformation process. The heat signature is strongest from these units, and your sensors detect various forms of life in different states of development.

Captain Vex's voice cuts through on the comm: "Scout-X7, your heat readings don't match any known geological patterns. What have you found?"

You've discovered what appears to be an ancient Martian facility for large-scale biological transformation – a massive installation that predates all of the current factions' attempts to create carcazods. Three areas draw your attention immediately: a vast cloning chamber where entities are being grown rather than born, a mutation acceleration zone where beings are being transformed at an accelerated rate, and a final integration chamber where transformed beings appear to be reaching a higher state of consciousness.

The implications are staggering – this facility could hold the original secrets of the transformation process, knowledge that the current factions have only been able to piece together fragmentarily.
