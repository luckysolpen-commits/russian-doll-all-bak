Page 7: The Eastern Escort

You choose the Eastern Escort mission, which involves protecting a high-value transport carrying precious resources and personnel through the contested Eastern Tunnels. Captain Vex briefs you on the details: "The transport contains both rare Martian minerals and several high-ranking officials from the Robot State. The route passes through three contested zones where rogue clowns and competing factions have increased their activities."

As your escort drone powers up, you review the transport manifest and security protocols. The convoy consists of one heavily armored transport vehicle and two additional escort drones. Your pilot designation CL7X-914 grants you command of the escort flight.

The Eastern Tunnels wind through some of the oldest sections of the Martian underground, where ancient geological formations create natural chokepoints and ambush opportunities. Your sensors detect the transport beginning its journey, and you move into formation as the convoy moves deeper into the tunnel system.

Halfway through the route, your warning systems sound. Three different threats appear on your scanner: an ambush by unknown forces ahead, a tunnel collapse blocking your path behind you, and a possible drone swarm approaching from the side passages. The transport officials' lives – and the valuable cargo – depend on your decisions.

Through your comm, you hear the transport commander: "Escort leader CL7X-914, we're counting on you. The implications of this cargo's safe delivery extend beyond the Robot State."
