Page 27: Leading the Strike Force

Colonel Argent's briefing is stark: "The Blood Queen Monarchy has established a fortified outpost in the Crimson Depths, threatening our supply lines and harboring rogue carcazod researchers. Your mission is to lead a strike force to neutralize the outpost and capture any transformation research materials." 

You're assigned a team of six elite Solar Guard clones, each with specialized capabilities. As you approach the outpost, you identify three potential attack vectors: a direct assault through the main entrance that would showcase Solar Empire military might, an infiltration through the ventilation system that would allow for precision elimination of key targets, or a coordinated strike that splits your forces to attack multiple points simultaneously.

The outpost itself is a marvel of Blood Queen architecture – crimson walls adorned with golden geometric patterns that shift and change as you watch, suggesting active security systems. Your intelligence indicates that the facility houses not only military assets but also specimens from various stages of carcazod transformation.

Your team looks to you for leadership, their augmented eyes reflecting the crimson light of the outpost walls. The choice of attack strategy will determine not only the mission's success but also your reputation within the Solar Empire hierarchy.

Communication intercepts suggest that the Blood Queen forces are expecting some kind of assault, but they don't know the exact timing or approach. The element of surprise remains your greatest asset, but it must be used wisely.
