Page 8: The Central Zone

Your selection of the Central Zone mission marks you as a pilot willing to take extreme risks. Captain Vex hands you a classified briefing: "This area is contested by all four factions, but it's also where the most dangerous elements operate – clowns who hunt other clowns, spy networks that have infiltrated every government, and rumors of the first successful carcazod transformations."

You're assigned a stealth drone with advanced cloaking capabilities and tasked with gathering intelligence on three potential targets: a suspected clown hunting operation, a high-level meeting between faction representatives, or a research facility that may be connected to carcazod transformation.

The Central Zone is a maze of tunnels where the architectural styles of different factions clash and merge. One moment you're flying through the geometric precision of the Robot State, the next through the organic curves favored by the Blood Queen Monarchy. The very air seems thick with competing loyalties and hidden agendas.

Your drone's sensors detect unusual activity in three locations: movement that suggests a large-scale clown hunting operation is underway, energy signatures indicating a high-level meeting, and bio-luminescent readings that might indicate carcazod research. The choice of where to focus your surveillance will determine what intelligence you can gather.

Captain Vex's final words echo in your mind: "Remember, CL7X-914, in the Central Zone, the greatest threat may not be the obvious enemies but the friends who become enemies and the enemies who become something else entirely."
