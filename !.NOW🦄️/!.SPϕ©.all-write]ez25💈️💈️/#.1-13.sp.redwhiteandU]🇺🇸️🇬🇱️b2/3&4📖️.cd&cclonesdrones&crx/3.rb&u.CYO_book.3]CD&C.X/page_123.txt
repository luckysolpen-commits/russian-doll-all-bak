Page 123: Investigating the Preserved Laboratory

The preserved laboratory has been maintained by the extreme conditions of the thermal vents, creating an environment where ancient Martian research equipment remains intact and operational. Your exploration drone reveals three distinct research areas: The Transformation Process Archive, where the original methods for achieving carcazod status are documented and preserved; The Consciousness Transfer Protocols, which detail methods for moving awareness between different forms; and The Environmental Integration Techniques, showing how beings can merge with the Martian environment itself.

As you explore the laboratory, you realize that the research conducted here predates all current faction methods and represents the original understanding of transformation processes. The equipment shows signs of having been designed specifically for beings with clone-like characteristics.

Your sensors detect that the laboratory's preservation is not accidental but the result of active maintenance systems that have operated for centuries. The extreme heat and chemical environment that preserved the equipment also continues to power the ancient systems.

The research materials include detailed information about the carcazod transformation process that exceeds all current faction knowledge. The original Martian researchers achieved a comprehensive understanding of the transformation that subsequent civilizations have only partially recovered.

The laboratory's systems respond to your presence with activation sequences, suggesting they recognize your clone nature and transformation potential. The ancient equipment may be designed to work specifically with beings of your type.
