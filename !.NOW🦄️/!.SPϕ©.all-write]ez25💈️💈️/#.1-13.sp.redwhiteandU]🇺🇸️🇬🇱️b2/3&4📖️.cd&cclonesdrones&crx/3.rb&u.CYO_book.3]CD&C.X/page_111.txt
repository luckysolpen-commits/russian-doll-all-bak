Page 111: Joining the Honor Guard Intelligence Corps

The Honor Guard Intelligence Corps operates through formal dueling challenges and honor-based information exchanges. Your integration requires that you prove your worthiness through ritual combat before being granted access to the Monarchy's most sensitive intelligence operations.

Three aspects of honor-based intelligence work emerge: The Challenge Network, where information is earned through formal victories over other agents; The Honor Debt System, where intelligence is exchanged through complex relationships of obligation and repayment; and The Valor Recognition Program, where successful intelligence operations enhance your standing and grant access to higher-level information.

As you participate in your first challenge, you realize that the Monarchy's intelligence system is as much about personal development and honor as it is about information gathering. Your success depends on demonstrating the Monarchy's valued qualities of courage, skill, and commitment to their principles.

Your Solar Empire training emphasized efficiency and discretion, but Monarchy intelligence work requires public demonstration of capability and adherence to formal codes of conduct. Each intelligence gathering operation is also a test of your worthiness and loyalty.

The challenges you face reveal the depth of Monarchy intelligence operations, which extend beyond mere information gathering to include the development of superior beings through the rigorous application of their honor codes.
