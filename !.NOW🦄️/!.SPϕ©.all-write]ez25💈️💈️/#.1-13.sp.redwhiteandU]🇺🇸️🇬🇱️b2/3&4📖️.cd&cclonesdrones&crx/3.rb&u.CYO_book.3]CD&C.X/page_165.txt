Page 165: Studying the Original Network

As you focus on the bioluminescent maps showing the Original Network, your drone reveals tunnel systems that were carved by ancient Martian rivers and later modified by pre-human intelligence. These passages form the foundation of all current tunnel systems and contain information about Mars' original environmental conditions and the first attempts to modify the planet's biosphere.

Three network aspects emerge: Geological Foundation Mapping, showing the original river systems and geological formations that underlie all current tunnels; Pre-Human Modification Layers, revealing how ancient intelligence modified the natural tunnels for unknown purposes; and Transformation Integration Points, indicating where the original network was designed to support consciousness and biological transformation.

As you study the original network, you realize it was designed as more than just transportation - it's a system for managing planetary consciousness and facilitating the evolution of awareness. The ancient Martians created a living system that spans the entire planet.

Your understanding of the original network reveals that all current faction territories and conflicts exist within a much larger system designed for planetary-scale consciousness evolution. The original network connects to areas that don't appear on any current maps and facilitates processes that affect Mars as a living system.

The original network knowledge provides insight into the true purpose of the tunnel system, but also reveals that your current activities may be part of a much larger process that extends far beyond the immediate faction conflicts.
