Page 69: Targeting the Command Drone

You lock onto the command drone that appears to be coordinating the entire swarm. Your targeting systems indicate this is no ordinary control unit but a sophisticated command center that's been transformed using carcazod-level bio-mechanical integration, making it both more powerful and more dangerous than standard units.

As you approach firing range, three distinct command systems become apparent: The Neural Command Interface, where the drone processes tactical information using organic computing elements; The Swarm Coordination Matrix, which manages the complex relationships between individual swarm units; and The Adaptive Strategy Core, where the command drone can modify its tactics in real-time based on battlefield conditions.

Your weapons systems target the command drone, but it begins to move with enhanced agility, its bio-mechanical systems providing capabilities far beyond those of normal drones. The swarm's behavior shifts as it anticipates your attack on its command center.

You realize the command drone is not just controlling the swarm but is partially integrated with it - destroying it may cause the other units to scatter or, conversely, may trigger defensive protocols that make them more dangerous. The carcazod-level modifications to this unit suggest it may be operated by a partially transformed being.

The Robot State officials in the transport below depend on your success, but the sophistication of the command drone indicates you're facing technology that exceeds the current faction capabilities.
