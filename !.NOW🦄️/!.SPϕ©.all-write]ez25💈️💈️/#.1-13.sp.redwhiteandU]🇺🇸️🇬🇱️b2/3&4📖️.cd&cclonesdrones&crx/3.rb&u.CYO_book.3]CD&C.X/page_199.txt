Page 199: Disrupting the Navigation Disruption Array

As you disrupt the Navigation Disruption Array generating electromagnetic fields that confuse navigation systems, your equipment targets the systems that are specifically designed to trap targets in the collapsed area. These fields create false readings and interfere with tunnel navigation, making escape nearly impossible for vehicles without specialized equipment.

Three disruption outcomes emerge: Navigation System Restoration, where eliminating the disruption allows the transport's systems to properly identify escape routes; Electromagnetic Signature Exposure, revealing how the disruption array was masking other important signals and information in the tunnel area; and Guidance System Enhancement, demonstrating how clearing the disruption creates opportunities to provide accurate guidance to the transport.

As the navigation disruption array is disrupted, you observe its sophisticated interference patterns - designed to create false readings, redirect navigation systems toward dead ends, and maintain the disorientation of trapped targets.

Your array disruption reveals the precision of the electromagnetic interference technology, while also showing how the disruption was integrated with other aspects of the ambush to ensure complete navigation failure.

The navigation disruption elimination provides clear pathfinding for the transport, but also exposes the convoy to other detection systems that were masked by the navigation interference and may reveal the convoy's exact position to other threats.
