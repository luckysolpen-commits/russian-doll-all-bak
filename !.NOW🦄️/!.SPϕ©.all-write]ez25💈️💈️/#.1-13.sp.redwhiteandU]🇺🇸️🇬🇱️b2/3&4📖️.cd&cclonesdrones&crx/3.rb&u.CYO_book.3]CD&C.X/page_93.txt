Page 93: Targeting the Solar Empire Clone Tracking Agent

The Solar Empire clone tracking agent operates from a hidden chamber in the Web of Whispers, using sophisticated biometric and behavioral analysis systems to monitor clone activities throughout the Martian tunnel system. Your Crimson Handler has identified this as a critical target that could undermine the Empire's control over their clone forces.

Three aspects of the agent's operation become apparent: The Biometric Surveillance Network, where the agent tracks clones through unique biological signatures that are supposed to be undetectable; The Behavioral Pattern Analysis System, which predicts clone actions based on subtle deviations from standard programming; and The Loyalty Monitoring Matrix, which identifies clones who may be developing independent thought or transformation potential.

As you observe the agent's work, you realize this individual has achieved a level of understanding about clone psychology and physiology that exceeds the official Empire research programs. The tracking methods are so sophisticated that they could identify you as an infiltrator despite your modified identity.

Your mission is complicated by the fact that the agent may be working against Empire interests in their own way - the tracking data reveals evidence of Empire clone modification experiments that even your handlers weren't aware of. The agent might be a rogue element within the Empire system.

The Web of Whispers environment allows for multiple simultaneous operations, and you detect that the clone tracking agent is not just monitoring Empire clones but gathering intelligence about transformation research across all factions. This information could be invaluable to your true mission.
