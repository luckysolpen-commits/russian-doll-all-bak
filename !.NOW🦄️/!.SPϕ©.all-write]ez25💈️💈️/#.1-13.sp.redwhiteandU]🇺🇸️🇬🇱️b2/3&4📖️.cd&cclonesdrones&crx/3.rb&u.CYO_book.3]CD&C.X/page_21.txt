Page 21: Engaging the Ambush

"Transport, hold formation," you command as you engage the ambush head-on. Your drone's weapons systems power up as you assess the attackers – they're not random raiders but well-organized fighters using advanced equipment from all four factions. Some units show modifications that suggest they may be carcazod-enhanced beings, while others appear to be rogue clones or drone operators.

As combat erupts, your tactical display shows three critical targets that could break the ambush if eliminated: a command unit coordinating the attack from a high vantage point, a weapons cache supplying the ambushers with advanced ordnance, and a rear guard that's blocking any retreat path for the transport.

Your drone's capabilities are tested as enemy fire rains down from multiple directions. The transport officials' lives and the precious cargo depend on your success, but the ambush appears to have been planned by someone with intimate knowledge of Solar Empire escort protocols.

Through the chaos, you recognize tactical formations used by all four factions – this is not a simple attack but a coordinated effort involving elements from each of Mars' major powers. The question becomes whether they're working together or if this is a complex double-cross.

Your weapons systems are locked and loaded, waiting for your decision on which target to prioritize.
