Page 65: Eliminating the Rear Guard

You decide to eliminate the rear guard blocking the transport's retreat path. Your drone's sensors show this is not just a simple blocking position but a sophisticated tactical formation designed to prevent exactly this kind of escape route.

As you engage the rear guard, three distinct defensive elements become apparent: The Forward Observation Post, where the retreat path is monitored and any movement toward escape is tracked; The Flanking Position, where ambushers can attack the transport from the sides as it attempts to retreat; and The Anchor Point, where the retreat route is physically blocked by mobile tunnel sections that can be sealed or opened as needed.

Your attack reveals that the rear guard is not just a defensive position but part of a living tunnel system - the walls themselves can shift and change to create new defensive positions or block escape routes. The technology is more advanced than anything used by the current factions.

You realize that the ambush was designed by someone who understands the tunnel system as a unified organism rather than just a static environment. This level of integration suggests involvement from beings with carcazod-level understanding of biological-mechanical fusion.

The transport officials depend on your success in clearing the retreat path, but the sophistication of the rear guard's defenses indicates you're facing an opponent of extraordinary capability.
