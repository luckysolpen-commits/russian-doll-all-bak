Page 22: Clearing the Tunnel Collapse

Instead of engaging the ambush, you decide to clear the tunnel collapse behind the transport to create a retreat option. Your drone Scout-X7 is equipped with demolition charges that could clear the path, but the collapse appears to be part of the ambush – strategically positioned to trap the convoy.

As you approach the collapse, your sensors reveal three important details: the debris is held in place by sophisticated support beams that suggest the collapse was artificially created, thermal readings indicate there may be a safe passage buried within the rubble, and the electromagnetic signature of the support system matches technology used in carcazod transformation facilities.

Your mission priorities conflict: you're supposed to protect the transport and its valuable cargo, but clearing a retreat path might be the only way to ensure their survival. The ambushers ahead are likely expecting you to engage them directly.

The officials in the transport are watching your actions carefully. This decision could determine not only the success of the mission but also your standing with the Robot State officials on board.

As you begin work on clearing the collapse, you realize that whoever planned this ambush has intimate knowledge of both the tunnel system and the convoy's planned route. The sophistication of the operation suggests involvement from multiple factions or perhaps someone with access to classified intelligence from all sides.
