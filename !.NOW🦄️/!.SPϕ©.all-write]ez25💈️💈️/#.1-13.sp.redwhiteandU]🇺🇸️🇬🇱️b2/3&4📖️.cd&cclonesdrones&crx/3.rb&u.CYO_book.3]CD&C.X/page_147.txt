Page 147: Choosing Environmental Immunity

The Cockroach Shark Mutation chamber completes the Survival Metamorphosis through Environmental Immunity, making you capable of surviving in any atmospheric, temperature, or radiation condition found on Mars. Your biology adapts to neutralize any environmental threat while maintaining your essential capabilities.

Three immunity capabilities emerge: Atmospheric Adaptation Systems, where your respiratory and circulatory systems automatically adjust to function in any atmospheric composition; Thermal Regulation Mastery, allowing your body to maintain optimal temperature in any environmental conditions from absolute cold to molten heat; and Radiation Immunity Control, enabling you to operate safely in any level of radiation without biological damage.

As the environmental immunity completes, you experience the sensation of being able to exist in any environment without risk or limitation. Your biological systems automatically adjust to neutralize any environmental challenges while maintaining your full cognitive and physical capabilities.

Your enhanced immunity allows you to operate in the most hostile environments on Mars, explore areas that are lethal to normal beings, and function effectively in any conditions across the entire Martian system.

The environmental immunity provides unprecedented operational flexibility, but also requires constant biological adaptation that consumes energy and may make you dependent on environmental challenges to maintain optimal function.
