Page 213: Attacking the Quantum Communication Hub

This hub represents the quantum-level communication technology that makes the coordinated swarm operation possible.

As the quantum communication hub is attacked, you observe its sophisticated quantum communication processes - the entanglement maintenance systems, the instantaneous information transfer mechanisms, and the quantum state preservation that enables communication without time delays.

Your hub attack reveals the critical role of quantum communication in the swarm operation, while also showing how the failure of quantum systems can have effects that extend beyond the immediate target area.

The communication hub destruction provides the potential for complete swarm coordination breakdown, but also risks creating quantum instabilities that could affect other systems and may trigger emergency communication protocols using classical systems. Your destruction creates quantum ripples that connect to the central tunnel system's neural network.
