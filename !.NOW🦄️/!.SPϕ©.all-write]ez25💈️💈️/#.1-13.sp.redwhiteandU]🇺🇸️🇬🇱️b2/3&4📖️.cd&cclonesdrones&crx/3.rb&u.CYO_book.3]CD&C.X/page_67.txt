Page 67: Finding and Clearing the Safe Passage Within the Rubble

Your thermal sensors detect a potential safe passage buried within the rubble, suggesting the collapse was designed to conceal rather than completely block this route. Your drone begins the delicate operation of clearing this hidden path while avoiding triggering any additional cave-ins.

Three distinct passage types emerge from your analysis: The Maintenance Access, which follows old tunnel infrastructure that predates the current faction systems; The Bio-Organic Corridor, where the passage appears to be formed by organic growth that can shift and change as needed; and The Quantum Tunnel, which exists partially outside normal space-time and requires special navigation to use safely.

As you clear the passage, you realize it's not just a random route but part of an ancient network of hidden corridors that were built by the original Martian colonists. These passages were designed to remain accessible even during major tunnel system disruptions.

Your drone's sensors detect that the passage has been actively maintained - the bio-organic sections show signs of recent growth, and the quantum tunnel sections have been calibrated to current dimensional conditions. Someone has been using this route regularly.

The hidden passage system may be connected to the carcazod research facilities, as some of the organic sections show signs of the bio-mechanical integration that's associated with successful transformations. This could be the route that transformed beings have been using to move through the tunnel system undetected.
