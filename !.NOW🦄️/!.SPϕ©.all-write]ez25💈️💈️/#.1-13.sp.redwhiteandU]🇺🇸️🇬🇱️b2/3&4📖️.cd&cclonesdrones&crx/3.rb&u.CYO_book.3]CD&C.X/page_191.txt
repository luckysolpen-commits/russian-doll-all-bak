Page 191: Eliminating the Personal Security Detail

As you target the Personal Security Detail protecting the command unit's commander, your weapons focus on the elite carcazod-enhanced guards. These beings represent the highest level of transformation achievement, making them dangerous opponents but their elimination could remove the command unit's most capable protection.

Three elimination outcomes emerge: Commander Vulnerability Creation, where eliminating the security detail exposes the command unit's leader to direct attack; Security System Overload, revealing how the enhanced guards have connections to other security systems throughout the tunnel network; and Carcazod Capability Assessment, demonstrating the true capabilities of the most successful transformation subjects.

As the personal security detail is eliminated, you observe the advanced capabilities of the carcazod-enhanced guards - their integration of biological and technological systems, their enhanced reflexes and perception, and their ability to adapt to changing combat conditions.

Your security elimination reveals the true capabilities of successful carcazod development, while also showing how the most advanced transformed beings are utilized as elite protection units for high-value targets.

The personal security elimination provides access to the command unit's leader, but also demonstrates the advanced capabilities of the most successful transformations and may alert other advanced subjects to your presence and capabilities.
