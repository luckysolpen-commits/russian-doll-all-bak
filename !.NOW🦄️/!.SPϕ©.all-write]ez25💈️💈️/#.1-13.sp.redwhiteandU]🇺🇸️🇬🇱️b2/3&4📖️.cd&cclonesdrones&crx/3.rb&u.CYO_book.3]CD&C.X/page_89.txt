Page 89: Assaulting the Creatures' Hideout

The hideout you've targeted is not a simple refuge but a sophisticated living environment that has been modified to accommodate the transformed beings' unique needs. As you prepare for assault, you identify three distinct defensive elements: The Bio-Mechanical Perimeter, where the hideout's defenses are integrated into living systems that can adapt and respond to threats; The Consciousness Network Barrier, where the collective awareness of the inhabitants creates a form of psychic defense system; and The Transformation Adaptation Zone, where the environment itself can change to neutralize threats or enhance the defenders' capabilities.

As you move toward the assault, your sensors detect that the hideout spans multiple levels of the tunnel system and connects to areas that weren't on any of your maps. The transformed beings have been expanding their territory through careful cultivation of the tunnel environment.

Your equipment reveals that the hideout contains not just the "rogue" creatures but what appears to be a functioning society of transformed beings who have developed their own culture and social systems. The term "creatures" may be a misnomer - these may be the most successful examples of carcazod transformation.

The assault you were ordered to conduct is not against monsters but against beings who have achieved a form of existence that transcends the limitations of the faction system. Your role in this mission is suddenly much more complex than originally briefed.

Your targeting systems struggle to acquire clear shots, as the bio-mechanical defenses shift and change in response to your approach, creating new defensive positions and obstacles as needed.
