Page 91: Joining the Golden Gauntlet Team-Based Combat

The Golden Gauntlet tournament takes place in a massive arena complex where teams of three compete in challenges that test not only individual skill but collective coordination and trust. You must form a team with two other combatants, and your choices will determine not only your success but the internal dynamics of your group.

Three team composition strategies are available: The Balanced Team, combining different types of combat expertise and transformation modifications; The Specialized Unit, where all members share similar capabilities but operate with enhanced coordination; and The Complementary Pairing, where team members' abilities fill each other's weaknesses while amplifying their combined strengths.

As you evaluate potential teammates, you realize that the Monarchy's tournament system is designed to build lasting relationships that extend beyond the arena. The bonds formed in Golden Gauntlet combat often persist as military units, research partnerships, or transformation collaboratives.

Your Solar Empire training emphasized individual excellence and hierarchical command, but Monarchy team combat requires a different approach based on mutual respect and shared honor. Success demands adapting your leadership style to the Monarchy's collaborative combat protocols.

The arena challenges ahead will test your ability to work with others while maintaining your cover identity. Your true mission to subvert Monarchy leadership could be compromised if your teammates become too close to you.
