Page 16: The Cockroach Shark Mutation

"Ah," says the transformed being, "the path of primal adaptation. The cockroach shark was one of Mars' most successful early colonists – a bio-engineered organism designed to survive in the harshest conditions. The mutation ritual attempts to merge that adaptability with the consciousness of a drone operator or clone."

You enter a bio-lab filled with tanks containing hybrid specimens in various stages of development. Some tanks show successful fusions, creating beings with enhanced survival abilities, while others contain failed experiments that serve as warnings about the process. The air is thick with organic vapors and the scent of growth stimulants.

"Three aspects of the cockroach shark can be integrated into your being," explains a researcher whose own form shows subtle shark-like features. "The Stealth Adaptation allows you to become nearly invisible in the tunnel environment. The Survival Metamorphosis grants the ability to live in any condition, no matter how hostile. The Predatory Integration provides enhanced hunting and combat abilities for the pursuit of other beings – including clowns, drones, or even other clones."

You see yourself reflected in the bio-tanks – still recognizably human, but with the potential to become something that exists between human and the most successful Martian organism. The researcher continues: "Each aspect grants significant advantages for survival in the Martian depths, but the integration process is physically and mentally traumatic. You will lose aspects of your original identity."

The transformation chamber awaits, its walls lined with bio-mechanical interfaces that will begin the integration process. You can already see your future form beginning to take shape in the chamber's holographic display.
