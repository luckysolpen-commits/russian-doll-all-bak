Page 64: Destroying the Weapons Cache

You target the weapons cache that's supplying the ambushers with advanced ordnance. Your drone's sensors indicate this cache contains weapons that exceed the standard issue for any single faction - possibly a joint operation or stolen technology from multiple sources.

As you approach the cache location, three distinct storage areas become apparent: The Standard Ordnance Depot, containing conventional weapons from all four factions; The Carcazod Enhancement Arsenal, where transformation-based weapons that can adapt to their targets are stored; and The Quantum Munitions Vault, with explosives that exist in multiple quantum states until triggered.

Your weapons lock onto the cache, but as you fire, you realize that the storage system is designed to be mobile as well. The cache begins to shift and relocate, indicating it's not just a simple storage unit but a transformed organism that can move through the tunnel system.

The weapons cache's movement reveals it to be part of a larger network of mobile supply units that support coordinated operations across the Martian tunnel network. This is not just an ambush but part of a systematic campaign using advanced logistics.

Your attack on the cache alerts the ambushers to your tactical priorities, and they begin to shift their strategy. The transport you're protecting remains in danger, but destroying the cache could significantly reduce the ambushers' combat effectiveness.
