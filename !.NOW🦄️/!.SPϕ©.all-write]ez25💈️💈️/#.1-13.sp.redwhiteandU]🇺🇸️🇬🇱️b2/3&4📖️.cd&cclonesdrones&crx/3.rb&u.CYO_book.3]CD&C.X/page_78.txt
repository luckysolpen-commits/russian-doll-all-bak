Page 78: Investigating the Hybrid Cloning Bay

The hybrid cloning bay represents the ultimate fusion of human and alien genetic material, combined with carcazod transformation techniques. Your drone's sensors reveal three distinct cloning operations: The Multi-Species Integration Line, where human and Martian genetic material are combined with other, unknown genetic sources; The Transformation-Ready Clones, beings designed from birth to undergo carcazod transformation with minimal risk; and The Consciousness-Compatible Clones, created specifically to serve as hosts for transferred awareness.

As you observe the cloning process, you realize the facility is creating beings specifically designed to serve as perfect vessels for the joint transformation program. These aren't ordinary clones but hybrid organisms that combine the best traits of multiple species with the adaptability of carcazod-level transformation.

Your analysis shows that the genetic source material includes not just human and Martian life but samples from other worlds, possibly obtained through off-world operations that predate the current political structure. The cloning process itself incorporates quantum-level genetic manipulation that allows for real-time adaptation of the genetic code.

The clones in development show characteristics that suggest they're being optimized for specific roles in the planned supremacy army. Some are designed for combat, others for surveillance, and still others for integration with the tunnel system infrastructure itself.

Your drone detects that some of the developing clones show signs of consciousness development that exceeds normal parameters - they may be emerging as fully sentient beings with their own awareness rather than just enhanced soldiers.
