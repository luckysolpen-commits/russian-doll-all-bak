Page 12: Hunting the Spies

The patchwork-clad figure nods approvingly as you choose to join the hunt against spy networks. "Good," they say. "The infiltrators have become bolder, with agents from all four factions operating in the neutral spaces we claim. You'll be working with the Shadow Cell, identifying and neutralizing threats before they can compromise our operations."

They lead you to a hidden command center where displays track the movements of various agents throughout the tunnel system. A map shows the complex web of loyalties and betrayals that characterize the underground. "Three major spy rings have been detected: a Solar Empire network specializing in clone tracking, a Blood Queen operation focused on honor-duel intelligence, and a Robot State collective that monitors all other activities. Each presents different challenges for a hunter."

You're given basic equipment and a new identity that allows you to move through different faction territories. Your mentor, the same patchwork figure, continues: "Remember, in the hunt, you become what you pursue. The deeper you go into their methods, the more you risk becoming like them. Stay true to the rogue identity."

Your first target is identified on the system: a suspected spy who's been gathering information about the carcazod research facilities. The intelligence suggests this individual is operating under deep cover and has access to high-level information about the transformation process.
