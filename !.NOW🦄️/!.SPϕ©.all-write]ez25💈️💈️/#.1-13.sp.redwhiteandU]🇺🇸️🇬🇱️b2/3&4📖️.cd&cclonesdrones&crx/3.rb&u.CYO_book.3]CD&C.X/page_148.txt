Page 148: Undergoing Adaptive Metabolism

The Cockroach Shark Mutation chamber completes the Survival Metamorphosis through Adaptive Metabolism, allowing you to subsist on any available matter, including the minerals and energy sources found in the tunnel system. Your digestive and energy conversion systems adapt to process any available resources.

Three metabolic capabilities emerge: Resource Conversion Efficiency, where you can extract energy and nutrients from materials that would be indigestible to normal beings; Waste Elimination Optimization, allowing you to process any consumed material with maximum efficiency and minimal waste; and Energy Storage Adaptation, enabling you to store energy from various sources and convert it to the forms needed by your biological systems.

As the adaptive metabolism completes, you experience the sensation of being able to sustain yourself from the environment itself - your body automatically identifies and processes any available resources to maintain your existence and capabilities.

Your enhanced metabolism allows you to survive indefinitely in any environment without external food sources, convert environmental materials into the resources you need, and maintain peak performance in resource-scarce conditions.

The adaptive metabolism provides unprecedented survival capabilities, but also creates a dependency on environmental resources and may make you vulnerable if environmental conditions become too sterile or uniform.
