The tracking senses provide unprecedented hunting and pursuit capabilities, but also create an obsession with following and identifying targets that may override other objectives and make you overly focused on pursuit rather than other mission requirements. Your tracking senses detect quantum signatures that lead to a central convergence point in the tunnel system.
