Page 73: Studying the Neural Net Capture Methods

The neural net capture methods used by the clown hunters are far more sophisticated than simple restraint devices. These are bio-mechanical systems that can interface directly with the nervous systems of their targets, three distinct approaches are evident: The Consciousness Containment Web, which captures not just physical beings but their mental patterns and memories; The Transformation Inhibition Field, which prevents captured beings from undergoing carcazod-level changes while in custody; and The Neural Mapping Interface, which extracts knowledge and experiences from captured subjects without necessarily harming them.

As you observe the capture process, your sensors detect that the neural nets are made from organic materials similar to those found in successful carcazod specimens. The hunters have learned to use transformed biological matter as a tool to control other transformed beings.

Your analysis reveals that the neural net technology is actually a form of controlled carcazod-level biological integration - the hunters have found a way to weaponize the transformation process itself. This explains their success in capturing other transformed beings.

The capture methods show evidence of research from all four factions, suggesting the hunters have access to classified transformation data from across the Martian political spectrum. The sophistication of their techniques indicates high-level support from multiple governments.

You begin to understand that the hunters aren't just capturing random rogues but targeting specific transformation pathways that might threaten the established power structure.
