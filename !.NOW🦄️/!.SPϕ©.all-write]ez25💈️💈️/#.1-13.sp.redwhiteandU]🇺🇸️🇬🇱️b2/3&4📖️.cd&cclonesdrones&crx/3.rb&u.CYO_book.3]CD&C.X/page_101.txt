Page 101: Attempting Consciousness Multiplexing

The Neural Expansion Chamber initiates Consciousness Multiplexing, allowing your awareness to exist simultaneously across multiple channels and locations. Your consciousness begins to branch into parallel streams of awareness while maintaining connection to a core identity.

Three forms of multiplexed consciousness become possible: Distributed Awareness, where you can experience multiple locations and situations simultaneously; Parallel Processing Consciousness, allowing you to consider multiple complex problems while maintaining normal awareness; and Networked Consciousness Sharing, where you can temporarily merge awareness with other beings while retaining your individual identity.

As the multiplexing process unfolds, you experience the strange sensation of being in multiple places at once. You can observe your own body from multiple perspectives and conduct multiple complex tasks simultaneously. However, you also feel the constant challenge of maintaining a unified sense of self across all awareness streams.

Your multiplexed consciousness allows you to infiltrate multiple faction facilities simultaneously, gather intelligence from various sources while maintaining cover, and even attempt to influence the joint transformation research program from multiple angles at once.

The multiplexing process is partially reversible, but each branch of consciousness gains its own experiences and memories. Managing these parallel streams of experience without fragmenting your core identity becomes a complex mental exercise that requires constant attention.
