Page 15: The Brain Sniffing Ritual

The transformed being nods as you choose the Brain Sniffing Ritual. "Courage or folly, I cannot say, but you have chosen one of the most feared paths to carcazod transformation. The ritual requires you to literally inhale the essence of consciousness from others – to experience their memories, their knowledge, and their deepest fears as if they were your own."

You are led to a circular chamber where several beings in various states of transformation wait. Some are clearly in the early stages of change, with neural pathways visibly extending from their skulls like gossamer threads. Others are so transformed that it's difficult to identify what they once were.

"The ritual has three phases," explains your guide. "First, you must choose the type of consciousness to absorb: military strategies from a former Solar Empire commander, honor-bound knowledge from a Blood Queen duelist, or computational awareness from a Robot State AI construct. Each offers different advantages but comes with its own psychological risks."

Before you are three containment vessels, each holding the essence of a different type of consciousness. The air around them shimmers with stored knowledge and memory. You can feel the power radiating from each one, and your own consciousness seems to reach out toward them involuntarily.

"The first step is always the most difficult," whispers a nearby transformed being, their voice a mixture of human and electronic tones. "To truly become a carcazod, you must accept that your original self will be changed beyond recognition."
