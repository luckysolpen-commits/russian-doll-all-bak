Page 4: The Rogue Network

Instead of following the established paths, you respond to the "Rogue Network" option by slipping away from the main hub and into the hidden passages that exist between the official sectors. Here, in the spaces the governments don't control, you find yourself in a different kind of society entirely.

The underground is dimly lit with improvised lighting, and you encounter beings who don't fit into any of the official categories: former clones who've rejected their programming, drone operators who've gone off-grid, and strange hybrid forms that defy easy classification. A figure approaches from the shadows – they wear a patchwork of uniforms from all four factions, and their eyes hold an ancient wisdom.

"You've chosen the path of the rogue," they say, their voice a mixture of electronic and organic tones. "Here we are neither clone nor drone, neither loyal to the Empire nor the Monarchy. We are the clowns who hunt clowns, the watchers of watchers. Three paths open before you: join our hunt against the spy networks that infest these tunnels, become a guide through the unmapped depths of Mars, or seek the ancient transformation that turns beings into carcazods."

Around you, you see evidence of the network's activities – stolen equipment from all factions, maps of secret passages, and communication devices that tap into every official frequency. The air thrums with the energy of beings who've rejected predetermined roles.
