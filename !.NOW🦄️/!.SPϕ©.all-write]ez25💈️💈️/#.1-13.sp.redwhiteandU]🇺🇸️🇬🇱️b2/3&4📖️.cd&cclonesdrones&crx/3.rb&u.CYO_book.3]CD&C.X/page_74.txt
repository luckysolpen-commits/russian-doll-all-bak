Page 74: Infiltrating the Processing Facility

The processing facility is where captured clowns undergo what appears to be a reversal of the carcazod transformation process. Your stealth drone reveals three distinct processing areas: The De-Integration Chamber, where bio-mechanical modifications are systematically removed from captured beings; The Memory Wipe Laboratory, where transformation-related knowledge and experiences are extracted; and The Re-Conditioning Center, where captured beings are prepared for reintegration into normal faction society.

As you observe the facility's operations, you realize that the "clowns" being processed aren't random rogues but targeted individuals - beings who have achieved specific transformation milestones that the factions consider threatening. The facility is designed to reverse successful transformations without causing permanent harm to the subjects.

Your sensors detect that the facility uses a combination of ancient Martian technology and modern faction research to achieve transformation reversal. The process appears to be successful but leaves subjects with gaps in their memories and reduced capabilities.

The captured beings in the facility show signs of having achieved remarkable transformation levels - some display characteristics that suggest they were approaching the highest levels of carcazod development before capture. The facility is essentially a prison for the most successful transformed beings.

You begin to understand that the entire clown hunting operation is designed to prevent any single being from achieving transformation levels that might threaten the power balance between the four factions.
