Page 11: The Carcazod Research Division

The Clone Commander's expression shifts subtly as you request assignment to the carcazod research division. "Few clones choose this path, CL7X-914. The transformation is not guaranteed, and the process... changes more than just your physical form. You will be assigned to Dr. Vessel's team in the Deep Research Facility. Three programs are currently active: the Neural Expansion Project, the Biological Metamorphosis Trials, or the Consciousness Transfer Experiments."

You're transported to a section of the facility unlike any other – the walls seem to pulse with organic life, and the air itself feels thicker, almost viscous. Other researchers move through the corridors, and you notice that some of them have subtle modifications that mark them as partially transformed.

Dr. Vessel meets you personally – a figure who appears mostly human but whose eyes shift between organic and technological aspects. "Welcome to the frontier of evolution," they say. "Here we explore what it means to transcend the clone condition. Each program offers different possibilities: expand your consciousness beyond normal limits, transform your body into something beyond current understanding, or transfer your awareness into a form that can exist independently of physical constraints."

The laboratory you're shown contains equipment that defies easy categorization – some clearly scientific, others that seem almost organic in nature. In one corner, you glimpse what appears to be a partially transformed being suspended in a bio-gel, their form shifting between human and something else entirely.
