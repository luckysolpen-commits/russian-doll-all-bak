Page 37: The Blood Queen Honor-Duel Intelligence Operation

"The Blood Queen's network operates on honor and ritual," explains your patchwork guide. "Information is exchanged through challenges, victories, and the accumulation of prestige. To infiltrate their intelligence operations, you'll need to master their system of honor-based espionage."

You're assigned to the Crimson Web, where the Blood Queen Monarchy's intelligence agents operate under a complex system of dueling challenges and honor debts. The facility itself is a marvel of shifting crimson architecture where the walls rearrange themselves based on the outcomes of various challenges.

Three distinct intelligence operations offer opportunities for infiltration: the Honor Guard Intelligence Corps, where information is gathered through formal dueling challenges; the Crimson Shadows, who operate through informal honor networks based on personal loyalty; and the Blood Trophy Collectors, who gather intelligence through the collection of trophies from defeated opponents.

Your cover identifies you as a former Solar Empire officer who's come seeking the more honor-bound life of the Monarchy. To maintain this cover, you'll need to participate in their ritual intelligence gathering - a complex system where information is won through challenges and bound by honor codes.

The Blood Queen's agents are particularly focused on tracking successful carcazod transformations, viewing them as potential threats to the traditional honor systems that govern their society. This makes your mission especially delicate - you're investigating carcazod-related intelligence for the rogue network while surrounded by those who see carcazods as dishonorable.

Your first challenge is to establish your credibility within their honor-based system while gathering information without revealing your true loyalties.
