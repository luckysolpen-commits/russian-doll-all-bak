Page 57: Investigating the Electromagnetic Field Control Array

The electromagnetic field control array is a marvel of ancient Martian engineering that predates all current factions - a vast crystalline structure that manipulates electromagnetic fields on a planetary scale. Your drone's instruments struggle to comprehend the complexity of the system, which appears to control not just communication and navigation systems, but the very electromagnetic environment that allows certain types of carcazod transformation to occur.

Three distinct control systems emerge from the array: The Resonance Tuning Network, which adjusts electromagnetic frequencies to match specific biological processes; The Quantum Field Regulators, which maintain the quantum coherence necessary for consciousness transfer operations; and The Transformation Amplifiers, which enhance the electromagnetic conditions that facilitate the carcazod metamorphosis process.

As you analyze the system more closely, you realize that the current Martian tunnel network's electromagnetic properties are not natural but carefully maintained by this ancient array. The different factions' territories may have been established specifically to take advantage of the array's modified electromagnetic environment.

Your drone detects that the array is currently operating at reduced capacity - it was designed to manage far more complex electromagnetic interactions than it's currently handling. Some of its functions appear to have been deliberately disabled, possibly in response to the rise of the current factions.

The array's control systems respond to your presence in unexpected ways, suggesting it recognizes your hybrid nature as both clone and potential carcazod. Some of its functions begin to activate as you approach, possibly indicating that it's been waiting for a being with your specific characteristics.
