Page 201: Clearing the Maintenance Access

As you clear the Maintenance Access following old tunnel infrastructure that predates the current faction systems, your exploration reveals passages that were built long before the current political structures existed. These ancient routes provide alternative paths that bypass the modern security and control systems entirely.

Three access outcomes emerge: Historical Route Navigation, where following the ancient paths provides access to areas not controlled by current faction systems; Infrastructure Compatibility Recognition, revealing how the maintenance access connects to systems that still function despite being forgotten by modern civilization; and Pre-Human Construction Discovery, demonstrating that some of the maintenance routes were built by intelligence that existed before human arrival on Mars.

As the maintenance access is cleared, you observe the sophisticated nature of the ancient infrastructure - its integration with geological features, its self-maintaining systems, and its connection to resources and facilities unknown to current maps.

Your access clearing reveals the continuity of tunnel systems across geological periods, while also showing how ancient construction continues to function and provide utility despite being disconnected from modern operational systems.

The maintenance access clearing provides secure routes that bypass current security, but also risks leading into areas where the tunnel systems interface with unknown ancient technologies or where the infrastructure may be unstable due to age and neglect.
