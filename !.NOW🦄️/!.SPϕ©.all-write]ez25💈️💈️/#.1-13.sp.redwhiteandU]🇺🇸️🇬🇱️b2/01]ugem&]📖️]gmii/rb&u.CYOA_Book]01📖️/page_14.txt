You decide to seek out the rebels and learn more about the spirit stones. You use the faint glow of the stone to guide you. It leads you to a hidden cave system deep in the wilderness. There, you find a small community of rebels and native people, living together in secret. They welcome you, and they teach you about the spirit stones. They tell you that the stones are a connection to the planet's life force, and that the Empire is afraid of them because they represent a power that they cannot control.
