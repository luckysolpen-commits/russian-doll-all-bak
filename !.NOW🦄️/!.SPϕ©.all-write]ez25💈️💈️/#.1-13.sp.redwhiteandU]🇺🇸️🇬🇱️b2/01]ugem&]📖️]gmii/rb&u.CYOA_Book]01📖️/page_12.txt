You kick open the door and burst into the room, your hands raised in the air. "I surrender," you say.

The rebels are startled. They raise their weapons, their faces a mixture of fear and suspicion. The woman from the alley steps forward.

"Who are you?" she asks, her voice steady.

"I am a clone," you say. "But I am not your enemy."

You tell them your story. They listen in silence. When you are finished, the woman lowers her weapon.

"I am Anya," she says. "And we believe you."

You have found the rebellion. But can you trust them?
