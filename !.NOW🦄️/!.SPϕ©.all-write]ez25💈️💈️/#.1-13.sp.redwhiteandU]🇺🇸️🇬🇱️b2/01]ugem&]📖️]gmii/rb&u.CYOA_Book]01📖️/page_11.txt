As you clean the latrines, you listen to the gossip of the other clones. You learn about the patrol schedules, the security weaknesses, the secret supply caches. You are a ghost in the machine, invisible and ignored.

You start to formulate a plan. You will escape from this place, and you will take as many supplies as you can carry.

One night, you put your plan into action. You slip out of the barracks and make your way to the supply depot. You fill a bag with food, water, and weapons. You are about to leave when you hear a noise.

It's your commander. He has been watching you.
