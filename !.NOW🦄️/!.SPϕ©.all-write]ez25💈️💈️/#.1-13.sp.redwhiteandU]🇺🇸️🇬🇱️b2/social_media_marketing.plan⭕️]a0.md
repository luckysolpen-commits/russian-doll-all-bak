# Social Media Marketing Plan: CYOA Books

## 1. Overall Strategy

The goal is to create intrigue and give potential readers a taste of the interactive experience. We'll use a multi-platform approach, with tailored content for each. The core of the strategy is the "mini CYOA," a short, self-contained story that's easy to engage with in a social media feed.

## 2. Mini CYOA Examples

Here are a few examples you can post on forums and social media. You can post the initial setup, and then reply to people's choices with the outcome.

**Mini CYOA 1: The Red Queen's Test**

> You are a clone, designation 734. The Red Queen inspects you. Her chrome mask reflects your identical face. "You will be my fist," she says. But a seed of doubt is in your mind. What do you do?
> 
> A. Shout "Yes, your Majesty!" with the others.
> B. Remain silent.
> 
> ---
> 
> **If you chose A:** You are assigned to a desolate outpost. You have chosen obedience. For now. [Link to full story]
> **If you chose B:** The Red Queen's gaze lingers on you. "A defective clone?" she whispers. You are dragged to a dark cell. Your adventure has just begun. [Link to full story]

**Mini CYOA 2: The Child in the Street**

> You are Meg, a clone soldier. Your commander yells "Shoot!" at the child in the street. Your training screams "decoy," but your heart screams "innocent." What do you do?
> 
> A. Follow orders. Shoot the child.
> B. Hesitate. Defy your commander.
> 
> ---
> 
> **If you chose A:** The child falls. You feel like a monster. You have chosen obedience, but at what cost? [Link to full story]
> **If you chose B:** The child is saved by her mother. Your commander is furious. You have chosen defiance, but where will it lead? [Link to full story]

## 3. Copywriting Examples

Here are a few different post styles for different platforms.

**For Twitter:**

> You are a clone. Do you obey or rebel? Your choice. Your story. #CYOA #ChooseYourOwnAdventure #SciFi #InteractiveFiction [Link to mini CYOA or full book]

> The Red Queen's Empire is vast. But you are a flaw in her design. A clone with free will. What will you do with it? #Dystopian #SciFiBooks #Storytelling [Link to mini CYOA or full book]

**For Facebook/Instagram:**

> (With a cool image, maybe of a clone or a futuristic city)
> 
> Your designation is 735. Your mission is to quell a rebellion. But when you see a child in the line of fire, you have a choice to make. A choice that will change everything.
> 
> Our new Choose Your Own Adventure story, "Meg's Choice," is now available. Click the link in our bio to start your adventure.
> 
> #CYOA #InteractiveStory #NewBook #SciFiLovers #Bookstagram

**For Reddit (e.g., r/writing, r/scifi, r/interactivefiction):**

> **Title:** I wrote a 100-page Choose Your Own Adventure story about a rebellious clone. Here's a mini-adventure to give you a taste.
> 
> **Body:** (Paste one of the mini CYOAs here)
> 
> I've been working on a couple of CYOA stories and I'm really excited to share them. If you enjoyed this little adventure, you can find the full 100-page story [here](link). I'd love to hear what you think!

## 5. Call to Action (CTA)

The CTA should be clear and direct people to where they can read the full books. Since the books are currently local files, you will need to host them somewhere (like a personal website, a blog, or a platform like itch.io or Wattpad) and then replace the "[Link to full story]" placeholders with the actual links.
