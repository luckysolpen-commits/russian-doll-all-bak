Worldbuilding Sheet

## Settings

*   **Aethel:** The planet where the Red Queen's cloning facility is located.
*   **Xylos:** A planet with a strong Red Queen presence, where the smugglers go for a job.
*   **The Red Queen's Cloning Facility:** A sterile and oppressive environment where clones are created and trained.
*   **Hangar Bay:** A location within the cloning facility where ships are parked.
*   **Secret Tunnels:** A network of hidden passages within the cloning facility.
*   **Rebel Base:** A hidden asteroid that serves as a base for the rebellion.
*   **The Land of Time:** A place where time is currency.
*   **The Centroid:** A place for learning about the Soul Pen and Tear It Cards.
*   **Planet X:** A planet with Extremophile slaves.
*   **The Metal Sun:** A giant structure built by Emperor Sol.
*   **Glitch World:** A digital realm.
*   **Avanta.C:** An MMORPG.
*   **Underwater Cities:** Built by Lucky to save Nuhumans.
*   **Luna's Moonbase:** A location on the moon.
*   **New Atlantis:** An underwater city.

## Items

*   **Soul Pen:** A mystical pen.
*   **Tear It Cards:** Mystical cards.
*   **Soul Bank:** A place to store souls.
*   **Data Chip:** Contains the location of every Red Queen's cloning facility.
*   **Advanced Weapons:** A shipment of weapons that the smugglers acquire.

## Lore

*   **The Red Empire:** A vast and powerful empire ruled by the Red Queen.
*   **Clones:** Biologically enhanced beings created to serve the Red Queen.
*   **Clowns:** Rebel clones who fight against the Red Empire.
*   **The Rebellion:** A secret organization dedicated to overthrowing the Red Queen.
*   **The Nexus:** The central core where the Red Queen's consciousness is stored.
*   **The Soul Pen Franchise:** The overarching story with 13 books.
*   **DUSTOPEDIA:** The lore bible for the franchise.
*   **Underground Xircus:** A social network for fans.
*   **Carcazods:** Dinosaur-like creatures.
*   **Nuhumans:** A group of people saved by Lucky.
*   **Extremophiles:** An alien species.
*   **Time Fu:** A martial art based on time manipulation.
