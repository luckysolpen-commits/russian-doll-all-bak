Character Sheet

## Main Characters (from our story)

*   **Clone 734 (The Player):** A biologically enhanced clone who can choose to be loyal to the Red Queen or become a "clown" and rebel. Has the potential to be a skilled pilot, spy, saboteur, or leader.
*   **The Red Queen:** The tyrannical ruler of the Red Empire. A powerful being who exists as a hive mind, a collective consciousness of all the clones who have ever served her. Her consciousness is stored in a central core called the nexus.
*   **Jax:** A grizzled old space dog and the captain of a smuggling crew. He is a member of the rebellion and a mentor figure to the clone.
*   **Anya:** A mysterious and elegant woman who is a member of the rebellion. She recruits the clone to the cause.
*   **Mr. Silk:** A wealthy and powerful businessman who is the leader of a criminal syndicate. He tries to recruit the clone to his organization.
*   **Nyx:** The leader of a small but powerful group of rebels. She is a skilled warrior and a potential mentor or partner to the clone.
