# Character Sheet

## Elara Veyn (Quill)
*   **Age:** Barely nineteen.
*   **Appearance:** Not explicitly described, but implied to be a dreamer, perhaps less practical than others.
*   **Personality:** Curious, determined, a bit reckless (like her mother), loyal (to her mother's memory), initially fearful but quickly turns to anger when challenged. She's a dreamer, fond of stories.
*   **Skills:** Lockpicking (hairpin and thin blade), knowledge of Quillhaven's alleys and hidden paths.
*   **Possessions:** Worn leather satchel, the Soulpen, a hairpin, a thin blade, a small lantern.
*   **Goals:** Find answers about her mother's disappearance, understand and learn to use the Soulpen, eventually open a potions bar for supernatural creatures.
*   **Relationships:**
    *   **Lirien (Mother):** Vanished five years ago, was the last keeper of the Scribe's Hollow, used the Soulpen to cross the veil. Elara is deeply connected to her memory.
    *   **Torren:** Initially distrustful, but sees him as a source of information about the Soulpen and her mother.
    *   **Villagers:** See her as a dreamer, impractical.
*   **Arc:** From naive dreamer to someone who understands the dangerous power of the Soulpen and is willing to confront its truths.

## Torren
*   **Appearance:** Cloaked in gray, sharp features, eyes like chipped obsidian.
*   **Personality:** Mysterious, knowledgeable about the Soulpen, cynical, cautious, has a dry sense of humor (laughs like breaking twigs). Seems to have a protective or warning stance towards Elara.
*   **Skills:** Tracking (found Elara in the Hollow), knowledge of hidden paths, understanding of the Soulpen's mechanics and dangers.
*   **Goals:** Wants the Soulpen "gone" or "destroyed" to prevent it from waking what it shouldn't. Wants to prevent Elara from suffering the same fate as her mother.
*   **Relationships:**
    *   **Lirien (Elara's Mother):** Knew her, describes her as "reckless."
    *   **Elara:** Acts as a reluctant guide/mentor, warning her about the Soulpen's dangers.
*   **Motivation:** Has lost people to the Soulpen, implying a personal history with its destructive power.

## Lirien Veyn (Elara's Mother)
*   **Role:** Vanished five years ago, last keeper of the Scribe's Hollow.
*   **Connection to Soulpen:** Used it to cross the veil, wrote something "too big" that "broke the veil."
*   **Personality (implied):** Reckless, guarded the Soulpen.
*   **Status:** "Beyond the veil, where ink meets bone."
