EXT. MAGICAL MARKET - DAY / MONSTER LAIR

SOUND of a bustling crowd, exotic music, strange animal calls

Elara navigates a vibrant, chaotic MAGICAL MARKET. Her INKLING, perched on her shoulder, shimmers with fascination, its form mimicking the various magical lights. Stalls overflow with shimmering fabrics, glowing crystals, strange herbs, and enchanted artifacts. Vendors hawk their wares in a cacophony of languages, both human and otherworldly. Elara, now more confident, haggles for prices, her potions proving popular.

She sells a batch of "Dream Weaver" potions to a group of giggling PIXIES, and exchanges a "Truth Serum" with a shifty-eyed GOBLIN for a rare, glowing mushroom.

Suddenly, a collective GASP ripples through the market. A terrified SCREAM echoes from a nearby alley. A SHADOW BEAST, a hulking, amorphous creature of pure darkness with glowing red eyes, emerges, its form rippling like smoke. It snatches a terrified MERCHANT and begins to drag him away.

Panic erupts. People scatter, stalls are overturned. Elara, without hesitation, grabs her satchel.

ELARA
(to Kai, who has appeared at her side)
We have to help him!

KAI
(drawing his throwing knives)
Looks like our monster hunt just came to us!

His MISCHIEF GREMLIN, usually playful, is now wide-eyed with fear, clinging to Kai's leg.

LORCAN appears, his staff glowing faintly. His miniature ARCANE GOLEM hovers beside him, its light pulsing with alarm. He looks at the Shadow Beast with a grim expression.

LORCAN
Another tear. The veil thins faster than I anticipated.

DUNGEON DELVE 3 (MONSTER HUNT): Elara, Kai, and Lorcan track the Shadow Beast through the panicked market, then into a dark, abandoned warehouse that serves as its temporary lair. The air is cold, filled with an oppressive sense of dread. The Inkling shimmers, its form almost invisible in the gloom. The Mischief Gremlin whimpers softly. The Arcane Golem pulses with a faint, steady light.

Inside, the Shadow Beast has cornered the merchant. It snarls, its form flickering.

Kai throws a volley of knives, which pass harmlessly through the creature's smoky form. Lorcan unleashes a blast of arcane energy, which momentarily disrupts the beast but doesn't stop it.

Elara pulls out a vial of her "Lightbringer" potion, a shimmering golden liquid. She uncorks it and splashes it onto the beast. The potion sizzles, and parts of the creature's form solidify, making it vulnerable. The Inkling shimmers with approval.

Elara then pulls out the Soulpen. She writes furiously on a scrap of parchment:

CLOSE UP - The words appear:
"Bind the shadows."

Golden chains of light erupt from the parchment, weaving themselves into the chaotic rift. The Inkling pulses with an intense light, amplifying the magic. The creature roars in pain, its red eyes flaring. Elara feels a massive drain, a sharp, blinding pain behind her eyes, and a brief, terrifying vision of being pulled into a swirling void.

As the beast struggles, a powerful, stoic WARRIOR, RHYS (30s, broad-shouldered, with a scarred face and a massive, enchanted axe), bursts through a wall. Trotting faithfully beside him is a GRYPHOUND, a sturdy, loyal dog-like creature with feathered wings, its eyes fixed on the Shadow Beast. Rhys takes in the scene, his eyes initially dismissive of Elara, but then widening in surprise at the glowing chains.

Rhys, seeing his opportunity, brings his axe down with a mighty swing, cleaving through the now-solidified parts of the Shadow Beast. The Gryphound lets out a triumphant bark. The creature dissipates into black smoke, leaving behind a faint, acrid smell.

Rhys turns to Elara, his expression a mix of awe and suspicion. He nods curtly to Lorcan and Kai. The Gryphound nudges Rhys's hand, as if urging him to investigate Elara.

RHYS
(gruffly)
Impressive. Never seen magic like that. What... what was that?

Elara, still recovering from the Soulpen's cost, can only manage a weak smile. Kai grins, ever ready to take credit.

KAI
Just Elara's special brew. And a little help from her... unique pen.

Rhys's gaze lingers on Elara, a new interest sparked in his eyes. He's clearly intrigued by her power and her unconventional methods. The Gryphound lets out a soft whine, as if sensing Elara's exhaustion.
