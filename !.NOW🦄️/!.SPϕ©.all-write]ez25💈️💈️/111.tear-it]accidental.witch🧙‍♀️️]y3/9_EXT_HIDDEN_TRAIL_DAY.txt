EXT. HIDDEN TRAIL - DAY

Torren pulls his hood up, his face obscured. His SHADOW SPRITE, a tiny, bat-like creature, flits silently ahead, scouting the path. He moves along the cliff path with the ease of someone who has walked it countless times before. Elara follows, staying low, her eyes scanning their surroundings. Her INKLING, perched on her shoulder, shimmers, its form mimicking the rocky terrain.

They reach a hidden trail, one Elara had used as a child to sneak out at night. It winds down the cliffs, narrow and treacherous, barely more than a goat path. Loose scree crumbles underfoot, and the roar of the sea grows louder as they descend.

Elara struggles to keep her footing, her boots slipping on the damp rocks. She has to use her hands to steady herself against the cliff face. Torren, however, moves with an almost unnatural grace, never missing a step, never looking back to check on her. He is a shadow, a silent guide.

Elara grits her teeth, determined not to fall behind. She pushes through the fatigue, her gaze fixed on Torren's retreating form. The Soulpen in her satchel feels like a heavy anchor, but also a promise. The Inkling pulses faintly, as if encouraging her.
