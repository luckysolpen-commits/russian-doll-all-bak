EXT. QUILLHAVEN - DAY

SOUND of a distant bell, sharp and urgent

Elara glares at Torren, her fear turning to anger. Her INKLING shimmers on her shoulder, agitated.

ELARA
If you know so much, tell me where she is!

Torren's gaze softens, just for a moment. His SHADOW SPRITE flits nervously around his head.

TORREN
I don't know. But I know she used the Soulpen to cross the veil. And I know it's calling you to do the same.

The words Elara wrote earlier – *beyond the veil, where ink meets bone* – echo in her mind.

Before she can respond, the bell tolls again, louder this time. The village alarm. Torren curses under his breath.

TORREN
Wardens. They must've seen the light.

Elara grabs the Soulpen and the parchment, stuffing them into her satchel. Her Inkling dives into the satchel, hiding amongst the folds. She doesn't wait for Torren's answer. She bolts down the spiral stairs, Torren's footsteps heavy behind her, his Shadow Sprite a blur beside him.

They burst out of a side door of the Scribe's Hollow just as lantern beams sweep the tower's main entrance. VOICES shout, BOOTS pound the gravel. Elara, fueled by adrenaline, leads Torren through the labyrinthine alleys of Quillhaven, her intimate knowledge of the village's twists and turns giving them an edge.

They duck into a narrow gap between two cottages, crouching in the deep shadows. Elara's heart hammers against her ribs as the WARDENS pass, their lanterns casting long, searching fingers of light. Their voices and footsteps fade into the distance. The Inkling peeks out from the satchel, its shimmering eyes wide.
