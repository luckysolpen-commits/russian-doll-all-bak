**Dramatis Personae**

*   **LUCKY SHEPHERD:** (50s) A brilliant, world-weary tech billionaire, seeking peace but drawn back into chaos. Resourceful, quick-thinking, but initially reluctant. He is a master strategist and a quick study, rapidly mastering the SolPen. He is the story's protagonist.

*   **ASTRA:** (40s) Lucky's long-time friend and genius inventor. Practical, loyal, and adaptable. She is a skilled scientist and engineer, and her technical expertise is invaluable to Lucky. She is also a former corporate spy.

*   **SOL:** (Ageless, masked) The enigmatic leader of a lunar society, seemingly benevolent but with a hidden agenda. Possesses advanced technology, including the SolPen. He is a master manipulator and a long-term thinker, and his ultimate goals are unknown.

*   **LOONA TWILIGHT:** (30s) Leader of the "moon crew," initially mysterious, later a key figure in Sol's organization. She is a skilled warrior and a loyal follower of Sol.

*   **GEM:** (Appears 30s) Initially presented as a friend, later revealed as a disguised clone and spy for SOL. She is a skilled operative and a devoted follower of Sol.

*   **AGENT THOMPSON:** (40s) A ruthless CIA agent who blackmails Lucky. She is a cunning and ambitious operative who will stop at nothing to achieve her goals.

*   **KAITO:** (40s) Leader of the Shadow Syndicate, a dangerous criminal. He is a powerful and influential figure in the criminal underworld.

*   **BLACK QUEEN:** (Ageless) The leader of the clone rebels on Mars. She is a powerful and charismatic figure, and she is a formidable opponent for Lucky.

*   **PRINCESS MEGAN8R:** (Ageless) The Black Queen's second-in-command. She is a skilled warrior and a loyal follower of the Black Queen.
