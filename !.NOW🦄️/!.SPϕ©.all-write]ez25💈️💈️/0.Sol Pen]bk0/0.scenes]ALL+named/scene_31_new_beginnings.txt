INT. SSOL - OBSERVATION DECK - DAY

SOUND of the SSOL's quiet hum, the vast silence of space

The SSOL hangs in orbit above Mars. Below, the colony is quiet, the rebellion quelled. Lucky and Astra stand at the observation window, looking down at the red planet.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> We did it. We actually did it.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> We did. But it doesn't feel like a victory.

He thinks of the Black Queen's words, the clones in the Genesis Chamber, the nagging feeling that he's still missing a piece of the puzzle.

Gem enters the room. She has a data pad in her hand.

<center>GEM</center>
> Sol sends his congratulations. The Black Queen and Princess Megan8r are being transported to a secure facility. He says you've proven yourself a valuable asset.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Is that what I am? An asset?

<center>GEM</center>
> You are a part of something much larger than yourself, Lucky. We all are.

She hands him the data pad. On the screen is a star chart, but it's unlike any he's ever seen. It shows a vast, dark region of space, labeled "The Clean Zone." And on the edge of that zone, a swarm of red dots, labeled "Carcazods."

<center>GEM</center>
> This is what we're fighting for, Lucky. A future free from chaos. A future Sol is building, one system at a time.

Lucky looks at the chart, then at Astra. He knows he can't go back to his old life. He's seen too much, done too much. He has a new purpose now, a new role to play in this cosmic game.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> So what's our next move?

Gem smiles, a genuine smile this time.

<center>GEM</center>
> We prepare. The real war is just beginning.

Lucky looks out the window again, at the endless expanse of space. He feels a sense of trepidation, but also a flicker of excitement. The reluctant hero has finally accepted his destiny.

FADE TO BLACK.
