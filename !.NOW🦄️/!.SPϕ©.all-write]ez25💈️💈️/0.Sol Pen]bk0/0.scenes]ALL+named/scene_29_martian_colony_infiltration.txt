EXT. MARTIAN COLONY - DAY

SOUND of wind whistling through the canyons, distant explosions

The Martian landscape is a desolate red, the colony a cluster of metallic domes nestled in a valley. The air is thin, the gravity low.

Lucky, Astra, and Gem, clad in their Light Exo Suits, stand on a ridge overlooking the colony. The diversion is underway, a series of explosions rocking the far side of the valley, drawing the rebels' attention.

<center>GEM</center>
> The diversion is working. Their forces are moving to investigate.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> That's our cue. Let's go.

They leap from the ridge, their suits absorbing the impact of the long fall. They move quickly and silently, using the canyons for cover.

They reach the perimeter of the colony. The main gate is heavily guarded, but Lucky has a plan. He uses the SolPen to create a swarm of small, metallic drones. The drones buzz towards the gate, creating a distraction.

While the guards are occupied, Astra uses her bypass key to unlock a service hatch. They slip inside, unnoticed.

The interior of the colony is a maze of corridors and tunnels. They can hear the sounds of fighting in the distance, the rebels battling the phantom attackers from the diversion.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (Whispering)
> So far, so good.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Don't get cocky. The hard part is just beginning.

They move deeper into the colony, their destination the command center, where the Black Queen is located.

FADE OUT.
