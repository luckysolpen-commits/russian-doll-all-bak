INT. LUCKY'S RANCH - NIGHT

SOUND of crickets, distant coyote howl

The ranch house is bathed in the soft glow of interior lights. Cozy, lived-in. Lucky and Astra are in the living room, perhaps reading or playing a board game, enjoying the quiet evening.

Suddenly, a soft THUD from the front porch.

Lucky and Astra exchange a look.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> What was that?

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Probably just a squirrel. Or a very large, clumsy owl.

But there's a hint of unease in her voice. Lucky rises, cautiously approaches the front door. He opens it slowly, peering out into the darkness.

On the porch, nestled against the doorframe, is a MYSTERIOUS PACKAGE. It's wrapped in plain brown paper, tied with twine, no discernible markings or address.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Well, that's new.

Astra joins him, her curiosity piqued. She kneels, examining the package.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> No sender. No return address. And definitely not from Amazon.

GEM (30s), a seemingly friendly and unassuming individual, emerges from the kitchen, wiping her hands on a dish towel. She's been staying with Lucky and Astra for a few days, a mutual friend visiting.

<center>GEM</center>
> What's all the fuss about? Oh, a package! Are we expecting something?

She eyes the bundle with a hint of skepticism, a subtle, almost imperceptible shift in her demeanor.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Not that I know of.

Just then, a figure emerges from the shadows at the edge of the porch. LOONA TWILIGHT (30s), striking and self-assured, steps into the faint light. She carries herself with an air of quiet power.

<center>LOONA</center>
> I believe that's for you, Lucky.

Lucky and Astra turn, surprised. Gem's eyes narrow almost imperceptibly.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Loona? What are you doing here? And what is this?

Loona offers a small, enigmatic smile. She holds up a sleek, futuristic DEVICE. It glows with a soft, internal light.

<center>LOONA</center>
> A delivery. And a warning.

She presses a button on the device. The mysterious package on the porch begins to GLOW with an ethereal, pulsing light. The air around it seems to hum.

Lucky and Astra stare, a mixture of awe and apprehension on their faces. Gem watches Loona intently, a flicker of something unreadable in her eyes.

FADE OUT.
