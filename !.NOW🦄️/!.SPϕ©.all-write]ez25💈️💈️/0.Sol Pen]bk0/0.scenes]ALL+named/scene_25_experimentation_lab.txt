INT. MARTIAN COLONY - MEDICAL BAY/EXPERIMENTATION LAB - NIGHT

SOUND of low, guttural moans, the hiss of medical equipment, dripping liquid

Astra, her face pale with disgust, stares into a series of transparent containment tubes. This is the Black Queen's experimentation lab, a horrifying mockery of a medical bay. The tubes contain grotesque, half-formed creatures – human and clone bodies in various stages of forced mutation, their limbs distended, their skin mottled, their eyes wide with silent agony.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (Voice trembling)
> Oh, my god... She's not just creating Carcazods. She's torturing them into existence.

Lucky, his SolPen clutched tightly, feels a wave of nausea. The stench of chemicals and something far worse hangs heavy in the air. This is pure depravity.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> This is beyond anything Sol described. This isn't evolution; it's a nightmare.

Gem, ever stoic, approaches a terminal, her expression grim. She accesses the data logs.

<center>GEM</center>
> The Black Queen is attempting to accelerate the mutation process, bypassing natural selection. She believes she can create a perfect, unstoppable army in weeks, not centuries.

On a nearby operating table, a partially mutated human writhes, strapped down, its eyes pleading. A series of needles are embedded in its flesh, pumping a viscous, glowing liquid into its veins.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> We have to stop this. We have to put them out of their misery.

Astra nods, tears welling in her eyes. She raises her energy weapon, aiming at the containment tubes.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> I can overload the life support. It would be quick.

Suddenly, the lab doors hiss open. Two heavily armed REBEL SCIENTISTS, their faces covered by masks, enter, accompanied by a hulking, newly formed CARCAZOD, its body still glistening with amniotic fluid.

<center>REBEL SCIENTIST 1</center>
> Intruders! Don't let them interfere with the Queen's work!

The Carcazod charges. Lucky intercepts it, creating a powerful energy blast with the SolPen, sending the creature reeling. He then uses the SolPen to rapidly construct a series of containment fields around the remaining tubes, preventing Astra from carrying out her mercy.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Lucky, what are you doing?! We can't let them suffer!

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (Voice strained)
> We can't destroy the evidence, Astra! Sol needs to see this. He needs to understand what she's truly capable of!

He engages the Rebel Scientists, using the SolPen to disarm them, creating temporary restraints. Gem provides cover, her movements precise and efficient.

As the fight continues, Lucky glances back at the tubes, at the suffering within. The moral dilemma weighs heavily on him. He knows he needs to expose the Black Queen's true nature, but at what cost to these tortured souls?

FADE OUT.
