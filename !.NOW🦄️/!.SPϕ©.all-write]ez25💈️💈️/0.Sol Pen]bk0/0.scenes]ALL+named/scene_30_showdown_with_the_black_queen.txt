INT. MARTIAN COLONY - COMMAND CENTER - CONTINUOUS

SOUND of alarms blaring, muffled gunfire

The command center is a large, circular room, dominated by a holographic map of the solar system. The BLACK QUEEN, a tall, imposing figure in a dark, ornate exo-suit, stands before it. PRINCESS MEGAN8R, younger and more agile, stands beside her.

Lucky, Astra, and Gem burst into the room, weapons raised. The Black Queen turns, her expression a mixture of surprise and amusement.

<center>BLACK QUEEN</center>
> So, the puppets have arrived.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> It's over. Your rebellion is finished.

<center>PRINCESS MEGAN8R</center>
> Is it? Or is it just beginning?

She lunges forward, a pair of energy blades extending from her wrists. Gem meets her charge, their battle a blur of motion.

The Black Queen raises a hand, and the room's gravity plating shifts. Lucky and Astra are thrown off balance. The Black Queen advances on Lucky, her eyes glowing with a faint red light.

<center>BLACK QUEEN</center>
> You have no idea what you're fighting for, do you? You're just a pawn in Sol's game.

She unleashes a wave of energy, but Lucky is ready. He uses the SolPen to create a shield, deflecting the blast. He retaliates, not with a weapon, but with a creation. He materializes a swarm of nanobots that engulf the Black Queen's exo-suit, disabling it.

She stumbles, her power cut off. At the same time, Gem manages to disarm Princess Megan8r, holding her at bay.

Astra, meanwhile, has made her way to the command console. She quickly overrides the colony's systems, restoring normal gravity and locking down the command center.

Lucky approaches the now-powerless Black Queen.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Maybe I am a pawn. But right now, I'm the one who just took your queen.

He looks at the holographic map, at the vastness of space. He knows this is far from over. But for now, they have won.

FADE OUT.
