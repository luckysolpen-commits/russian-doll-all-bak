INT. MARTIAN COLONY - BLACK QUEEN'S INNER SANCTUM - DAY

SOUND of a low, ethereal hum, faint, unsettling whispers

Lucky, Astra, and Gem finally breach the last layer of security, the heavy, ornate doors to the BLACK QUEEN'S INNER SANCTUM hissing open. The room is vast, circular, and bathed in an eerie, shifting purple light. Holographic projections of swirling galaxies and ancient Martian landscapes dance across the walls.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (Into comms)
> We're in. No sign of the Queen.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (Scanning with her device)
> Readings are... strange. High energy fluctuations. And something else. A psionic signature.

Suddenly, the holographic projections solidify. Ancient Martian WARRIORS, their eyes glowing with malevolent energy, emerge from the walls, their weapons crackling. They are not real, but their attacks feel terrifyingly so.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Illusions! But they're projecting force!

Lucky extends the SolPen, creating a rapidly spinning ENERGY SHIELD that deflects the warriors' spectral blades. He realizes this isn't a physical fight, but a battle of wills, of perception.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (To Astra and Gem)
> Don't engage them directly! They're not real! Find the source of the projection!

Astra, using her scientific acumen, begins to scan the room, looking for the focal point of the illusions. Gem, with her enhanced senses, tries to pinpoint the psionic signature.

The illusions multiply, overwhelming them. Giant, mutated CARCAZODS, their roars deafening, materialize from the floor, their claws tearing at the air. The room itself seems to twist and distort, disorienting them.

<center>GEM</center>
> (Strained)
> The psionic energy is radiating from the central dais! It's a mental attack!

Lucky realizes the Black Queen isn't just using technology; she's using her mind. He needs to fight fire with fire. He focuses the SolPen, not on creating physical objects, but on disrupting the illusions. He projects a counter-frequency, a wave of pure, unadulterated reality.

The illusory warriors flicker, their forms wavering. The Carcazods shriek, their roars dissolving into static. The room begins to stabilize.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (Gritting his teeth)
> She's trying to break us mentally! Don't let her in!

As Lucky battles the mental assault, Astra finally locates the source of the projections – a hidden crystal embedded in the central dais. She fires her energy weapon, shattering the crystal.

The illusions vanish instantly. The room returns to its normal, albeit still eerie, state. The silence is deafening after the cacophony.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Got it! The crystal was amplifying her psionic abilities.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (Wiping sweat from his brow)
> Good work. She's playing dirty.

Suddenly, a low, mocking LAUGH echoes through the chamber. The Black Queen herself materializes on the central dais, not as an illusion, but in the flesh. She is regal, imposing, and utterly unfazed.

<center>BLACK QUEEN</center>
> Impressive, Lucky Shepherd. You are more resilient than I anticipated. But this was merely a test. The true confrontation... begins now.

She raises her hands, and the very air around her crackles with dark energy. This is no illusion. This is raw power.

FADE OUT.
