INT. LUXURY CASINO RESORT - DAY (FLASHBACK)

SOUND of slot machines, distant laughter, upbeat lounge music

The air hums with a vibrant, almost electric energy. Sunlight streams through massive, arched windows, glinting off polished marble floors and shimmering chandeliers. This is a temple of opulence, a monument to ambition.

LUCKY SHEPHERD (30s), sharp-suited and radiating an almost boyish charm, moves through the bustling casino floor. He’s not playing; he’s observing. His eyes, bright and intelligent, take in every detail – the flow of guests, the efficiency of the dealers, the subtle tells of the high rollers.

He exchanges a quick, confident nod with a PIT BOSS, then pauses to watch a group of excited tourists at a roulette table. A genuine, almost mischievous smile plays on his lips. This is his world, and he’s at the top of it.

A MONTAGE begins, quick cuts illustrating Lucky’s meteoric rise:

*   He’s in a boardroom, commanding attention, a stack of blueprints before him.
*   He’s shaking hands with a beaming dignitary at a ribbon-cutting ceremony for another grand resort.
*   He’s on the phone, a fierce intensity in his eyes, closing a multi-million dollar deal.
*   He’s surrounded by flashing cameras, a celebrity in his own right, the face of a burgeoning empire.
*   A fleeting, almost imperceptible shot of him looking tired, a flicker of something darker in his eyes, quickly masked by a practiced smile. The cost of this empire.

The montage ends back on the casino floor. Lucky claps a friendly hand on the shoulder of a passing MANAGER, a gesture of camaraderie and ownership. He surveys his domain one last time, a sense of profound accomplishment, but also a subtle weariness, settling over him.

He turns, walking towards a private elevator, leaving the noise and glamour behind. The doors slide shut, a silent, definitive close to this chapter of his life.

FADE TO BLACK.
