INT. MOON BASE - GENESIS CHAMBER - CONTINUOUS

SOUND of humming machinery, the gentle bubbling of liquid

The heavy doors to the Genesis Chamber slide open with a hiss, revealing a room bathed in a soft, blue light. Lucky and Astra step inside, Astra's bypass key still in hand.

The chamber is filled with rows upon rows of translucent pods, each containing a developing clone in fetal position. The air is thick with the smell of amniotic fluid and sterilization chemicals.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> My god... It's a clone factory.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (Muttering)
> More than a factory. It's a nursery.

He points to a console at the far end of the room. It's the source of the hidden data stream. As they approach, the screen flickers to life, displaying a message:

"NURTURE SEQUENCE INITIATED. SUBJECTS: BLACK QUEEN, MEGAN8R. STATUS: ACCELERATED GROWTH."

Suddenly, Sol's voice echoes through the chamber, calm and collected.

<center>SOL (O.S.)</center>
> I see you've discovered my little secret.

Sol enters the chamber, flanked by two guards. He doesn't seem angry, merely... disappointed.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> You're growing them here. The Black Queen. Megan8r.

<center>SOL</center>
> I am. A necessary precaution. A way to study our enemy, to understand their weaknesses.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> You're creating them!

<center>SOL</center>
> A replica. A controlled experiment. The real Black Queen and her rebels are already on Mars, threatening to destabilize the entire system. I had to understand what we are up against.

Sol gestures to the pods.

<center>SOL</center>
> This is not a betrayal, Lucky. It is a necessity. The message you received was a trick, a ploy by the rebels to sow discord. And it almost worked.

Lucky looks from Sol to the clones, then to Astra. He's still not sure what to believe, but Sol's explanation is plausible.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> So what now?

<center>SOL</center>
> Now, we proceed as planned. The mission to Mars is more critical than ever. You've seen what they are capable of. Now, go and stop them.

Sol turns and leaves the chamber. The guards remain, their presence a silent order. Lucky and Astra exchange a look. The mystery of the Genesis Chamber is solved, but a new, more complex one has taken its place.

FADE OUT.
