INT. MOON BASE - LUNARIA - DAY

SOUND of a low, constant hum, distant activity, a sense of contained vastness

The SSOL descends into a massive, cavernous hangar within the Moon. The space is immense, filled with countless CLONE SOLDIERS, all identical, moving with precise, synchronized movements. They are performing various tasks – maintaining machinery, transporting equipment, training.

Lucky and Astra are led by Sol through the bustling hangar. Lucky is overwhelmed, his mind struggling to comprehend the scale of this hidden lunar society. Astra, while still observant, seems less surprised, almost as if she's been here before, or has knowledge of such places.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (Whispering to Astra)
> Clones? All of them?

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (Quietly)
> It appears so. A highly organized, self-sufficient society.

They pass by sections of the base dedicated to research, development, and even what looks like agricultural zones, all under the artificial light of the moon base. It's a fully functioning city, hidden within the lunar crust.

As they walk deeper, they see LOONA TWILIGHT, overseeing a team of technicians. She glances up, a knowing smile for Lucky, a subtle nod for Astra. She is clearly a high-ranking member of this society.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (To Sol)
> Loona? She's part of this?

<center>SOL</center>
> Loona Twilight is a vital asset. She understands the delicate balance required to maintain order.

They continue through a bustling marketplace, where clones are exchanging goods and services. It's a vibrant, almost surreal scene – a hidden economy thriving beneath the lunar surface.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> An entire city... on the Moon. How is this possible?

<center>SOL</center>
> Necessity. And the application of advanced principles. We have been here for centuries, observing, preparing.

They arrive at a vast training room. Here, clones are engaged in various exercises, some with what appear to be rudimentary versions of the SolPen, creating simple constructs.

Sol gestures to the room.

<center>SOL</center>
> This is where your true training begins, Lucky Shepherd. You will learn to master the SolPen. To harness the power of creation.

Lucky looks at the clones, then at the SolPen in Sol's hand. A new kind of awe, mixed with a growing sense of purpose, begins to replace his initial fear.

FADE OUT.
