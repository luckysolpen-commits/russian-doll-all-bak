Lucky’s eyes locked onto the palace gates, his mind racing with plans to breach the fortress. He knew the Black Queen wouldn’t take kindly to his sudden arrival, but he was ready for whatever came next. “Time to make some noise,” he whispered to himself, a wicked grin spreading across his face.

The hovercraft plummeted towards the gates, its engines screaming in protest as it prepared to kamikaze into the palace. Lucky gripped the edge of the craft, his heart pounding like a drumline… “Hold on, folks! This is gonna be a wild ride!”

The hovercraft smashed through the gates, sending shards of metal flying everywhere. Lucky tumbled out of the wreckage, scrambling to his feet as he took in the chaos. Guards rushed towards him, blasters blazing, but Lucky was ready for them. He summoned up a wave of distractions – holographic projections of himself, swirling around the palace like a swarm of angry bees. The guards hesitated, unsure which target to aim for… “Ha! You can’t catch me!” Lucky cackled, dashing off towards the palace’s main entrance.

Inside, he found himself face-to-face with Princess Megan8r – aka “Megan”, the Black Queen’s daughter. Her eyes gleamed with a mischievous spark as she sized him up… “Well, well, well! Look what we got here! The infamous Lucky’s in town!”

Lucky grinned, unfazed by her icy tone. “Just passing through, Princess Megan8r. I heard you had some… interesting parties going on.”

Megan snorted. “You’re just in time for the after-party. Follow me, and you might just learn something about these ‘clowns’ everyone’s been raving about…”

She led him through winding corridors, flanked by guards with faces as impassive as stone. Lucky trailed behind her, his eyes scanning the walls… “Hmm… Looks like someone’s been busy digging tunnels under the palace.”

Megan halted before a discreetly hidden door. “You’re really curious, aren’t you? Well, let me show you something…”

The door slid open, revealing an extensive network of secret underground tunnels. Lucky’s eyes widened as he took in the sheer scale of the operation… “Whoa… The clowns were using this to smuggle blood? No wonder they were so busy!”

Megan’s grin grew even wider. “You’re really starting to get it now, aren’t you? The Black Queen might just have a thing or two up her sleeve…”

Lucky’s mind reeled as he took in the implications. He knew he had to move fast – before Megan8r decided to make him an honorary guest at one of her infamous parties… “What’s the plan, Princess Megan8r? You’re not going to let me leave here alive, are you?”

Megan’s eyes sparkled with amusement. “Oh, Lucky… I think you’ll find that we have a little surprise waiting for you back in your room…”

Lucky’s grin faltered as he realized the extent of his predicament… “Uh-oh… Time to put on my thinking face again…”
