"I knew I made the right decision by bringing you all on board," Lucky said, clapping Maria on the back. "Now let's get this party started!"

As the sun dipped below the horizon, the resort erupted into a whirlwind of activity. The sounds of laughter, music, and the clinking of glasses filled the air as guests mingled and explored the vast array of games, attractions, and amenities.

Lucky watched it all unfold with a sense of satisfaction, knowing that this was just the beginning of his next chapter. He had built an empire from scratch, and now it was time to reap the rewards.

"I'm retiring," he declared to Maria as they stood on the rooftop, taking in the breathtaking view of the city skyline. "Time for me to live off the profits and enjoy the fruits of my labor."

Maria raised an eyebrow. "You're done with the casino business?"

Lucky nodded. "For now. But don't worry, I'll still be around to keep an eye on things. And who knows? Maybe someday I'll come back, but for now...it's time to put my feet up and enjoy the ride."

As they gazed out at the glittering lights of the city, Lucky couldn't help but feel a sense of gratitude for the connections he had made along the way – connections that would soon prove to be crucial in his next adventure.
