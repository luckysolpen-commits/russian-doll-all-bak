The Black Queen, weakened but not defeated, triggered a self-destruct sequence for the entire colony, a last-ditch effort to ensure their defeat. The ground trembled, alarms blared, and sections of the chamber began to collapse. “We need to go!” Astra shouted, her voice urgent. Lucky, with a final, powerful blast from the SolPen, created an escape route, a shimmering tunnel of energy that led out of the collapsing chamber. They raced through the crumbling corridors, dodging falling debris, the sounds of destruction echoing behind them. Gem, having finally subdued Princess Megan8R, dragged her unconscious form through the tunnel, a grim trophy of their victory.

They made it back to the SSOL, battered but alive. The colony below was a ruin, a testament to the brutal cost of their victory. In the SSOL’s briefing room, a holographic map of the Martian colony hung in the air, now showing a desolate, scarred landscape. Gem, no longer hiding her role as a SOL agent, was all business, her face impassive as she debriefed Sol on the mission’s success. Lucky and Astra stood beside her, exhausted but resolute. The rebellion was quelled, the Black Queen defeated, but the victory felt hollow, tainted by the horrors they had witnessed.
