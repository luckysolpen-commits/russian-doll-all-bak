Days later, the SSOL hung in orbit above Mars, a silent guardian watching over the red planet. Below, the colony was quiet, the rebellion quelled, the scars of battle slowly fading. Lucky and Astra stood at the observation window, looking down at the red planet, a new beginning stretching before them. The SolPen, now a symbol of both creation and destruction, rested in Lucky’s hand, its power a constant reminder of the choices they had made, and the choices that still lay ahead. The universe was vast, filled with unknown threats and unimaginable wonders, and Lucky Shepherd, the reluctant hero, was finally ready to face it, with Astra by his side, and the SolPen as his guide.
