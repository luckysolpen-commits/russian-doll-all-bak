Weeks turned into months. Lucky’s mastery of the SolPen was undeniable. He was no longer just a tech mogul; he was a force of creation, a living weapon, a man who could shape reality itself. But with this power came a growing unease, a nagging suspicion that Sol’s intentions were not as benevolent as they seemed. The price of this power, he realized, might be far greater than he could ever imagine. The whispers of the clones’ “souls” fueling the SolPen, the subtle manipulations, the hidden agendas – all contributed to a growing sense of dread. He was a master of a powerful tool, but was he truly in control, or merely a more sophisticated pawn in Sol’s grand, inscrutable game?
