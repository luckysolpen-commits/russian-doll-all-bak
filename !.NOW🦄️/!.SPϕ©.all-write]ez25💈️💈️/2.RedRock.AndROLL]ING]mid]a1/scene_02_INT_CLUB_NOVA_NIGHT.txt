**SCENE 2**

**INT. CLUB NOVA - NIGHT**

SOUND of pulsating synth music, chatter of a crowded bar

The club is a riot of neon and chrome, a stark contrast to the sterile world of Atlantis Prime. Clones, mercs, and other off-worlders pack the space.

GEM (30s, sharp, athletic, with a cybernetic arm that she keeps covered) sits at the bar, nursing a drink. She scans the crowd, her eyes missing nothing.

A figure slides onto the stool next to her. It's RATCHET (30s, a clone like Gem, but with a more relaxed, streetwise demeanor).

<center>RATCHET</center>
> Been a long time, Gem.

<center>GEM</center>
> Not long enough.

Ratchet grins, unfazed.

<center>RATCHET</center>
> Still charming as ever. Heard you went corporate. Security consultant? Doesn't sound like you.

<center>GEM</center>
> It pays the bills. Better than the alternative.

She takes a sip of her drink, her gaze distant.

<center>GEM</center>
> What do you hear from Mars?

Ratchet's grin fades. He leans closer, his voice low.

<center>RATCHET</center>
> It's not good. The climate is still shot. And the... problem... is getting worse.

<center>GEM</center>
> The 'Vampires'?

Ratchet nods.

<center>RATCHET</center>
> The blood shipments are sporadic. The Queen can't control the supply lines. Zara's faction hits them every chance they get. The clones... they're getting desperate. Doing things... the old way.

<center>GEM</center>
> The Queen's losing control.

<center>RATCHET</center>
> She's losing more than that. She's losing her army. A guard dog that's starving is more dangerous to its master than the wolf at the door.

Gem finishes her drink, a grim look on her face.

<center>GEM</center>
> This peace... it's not going to last.

<center>RATCHET</center>
> Peace is just the intermission between wars.

Gem stands, putting a few credits on the bar.

<center>GEM</center>
> Keep your ear to the ground, Ratchet. Let me know if you hear anything... actionable.

<center>RATCHET</center>
> You know I will.

Gem gives him a curt nod and disappears into the crowd.

**FADE OUT.**
