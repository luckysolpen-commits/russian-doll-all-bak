**SCENE 13**

**EXT. SPACE - DAY**

SOUND of muffled explosions, laser fire, straining metal

The space above Mars is a chaotic ballet of destruction. The QUEEN's small, outdated fleet is engaged in a desperate battle with ZARA's larger, more advanced armada.

It's a one-sided fight. The Queen's ships are being torn apart.

**INT. QUEEN'S FLAGSHIP - BRIDGE - DAY**

The bridge is shaking from explosions. Alarms are blaring.

<center>QUEEN'S CAPTAIN</center>
> We've lost the 'Valiant'! Shields are failing on the 'Defiant'!

GEM is at a console, her face tense with concentration.

<center>GEM</center>
> (into comms)
> All ships, concentrate your fire on their lead cruiser! Create a breach! We need to give the Serenade a window!

**EXT. SPACE - DAY**

The Queen's remaining ships converge on a single point, a suicidal, desperate charge. They pour all their firepower into one of Zara's cruisers.

The cruiser explodes, a massive, silent fireball.

For a moment, there is a gap in Zara's blockade.

**INT. ZARA'S FLAGSHIP - BRIDGE - DAY**

<center>COMMANDER 1</center>
> We have an unauthorized launch from the surface! A courier ship!

Zara looks at the tactical display. She sees the Starlight Serenade streaking through the gap.

<center>ZARA</center>
> A gnat. Swat it down.

**EXT. SPACE - DAY**

Several of Zara's fighters break off and pursue the Starlight Serenade.

The Queen's flagship, heavily damaged, moves to intercept them, sacrificing itself to protect the courier.

**INT. STARLIGHT SERENADE - COCKPIT - DAY**

LUCKY is in the pilot's seat, his face a mask of concentration. ASTRA is in the co-pilot's seat, her hands gripping the console.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Fighters on our tail!

<center>LUCKY</center>
> I see them! This thing isn't built for this!

He pushes the engines to their limit. The small ship shudders.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> They're gaining!

<center>LUCKY</center>
> I know!

He flies the ship with a reckless abandon, a skill learned in a different life.

They are a tiny ship in a vast, deadly expanse, with the fate of two worlds in their hands.

**FADE OUT.**
