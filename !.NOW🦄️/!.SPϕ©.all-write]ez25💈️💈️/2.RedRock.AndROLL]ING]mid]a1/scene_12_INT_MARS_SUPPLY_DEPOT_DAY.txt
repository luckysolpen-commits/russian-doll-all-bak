**SCENE 12**

**INT. MARS SUPPLY DEPOT - DAY**

SOUND of explosions, gunfire, shouting

The supply depot is a battlefield. The red dust of Mars is kicked up by explosions and running feet.

ASTRA and her mercs are pinned down behind a barricade of crates. They are exchanging fire with ZARA's forces.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (into comms)
> We're taking heavy fire! We can't get to the lab!

<center>GEM (V.O.)</center>
> The fleet can't hold them off for long! You need to move, now!

Astra looks at her team.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Cover me!

She breaks from cover, running towards the lab building. Her mercs provide covering fire.

She makes it to the door, planting a breaching charge.

**INT. MARS LAB - DAY**

The lab is pristine, untouched by the battle outside. A single, glowing container sits on a pedestal in the center of the room.

Astra grabs the container.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (into comms)
> I have the package! Repeat, I have the package!

**EXT. MARS SUPPLY DEPOT - DAY**

Astra runs out of the lab, the container in her hand. The battle is still raging.

She sees the STARLIGHT SERENADE descending, its landing ramp already down. LUCKY is at the top of the ramp, waving her on.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> A little faster, if you don't mind! We have a schedule to keep!

Astra sprints for the ship, her mercs covering her retreat.

She makes it up the ramp just as a massive explosion rocks the depot.

The ramp closes, and the Starlight Serenade lifts off, leaving the battle behind.

**FADE OUT.**
