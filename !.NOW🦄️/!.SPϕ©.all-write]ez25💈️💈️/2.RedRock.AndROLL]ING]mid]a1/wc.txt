Word Count per Page and Chapter:

For a 75k-100k word novel with 15-20 chapters:

*   **Words per chapter:** Approximately 4,000 to 5,000 words.
*   **Pages per chapter:** Approximately 10 to 15 pages.
*   **Words per page:** Approximately 400 to 500 words.

To ensure lines are not too long, I will aim for a newline every 10 words or so.
