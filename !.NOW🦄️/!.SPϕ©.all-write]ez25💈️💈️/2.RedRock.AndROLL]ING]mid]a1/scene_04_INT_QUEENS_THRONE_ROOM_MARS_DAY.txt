**SCENE 4**

**INT. QUEEN'S THRONE ROOM (MARS) - DAY**

SOUND of wind howling outside

The throne room is a grand, cavernous space, but it feels cold and empty. The once-pristine chamber is now showing signs of neglect. A fine layer of red dust covers everything.

The RED QUEEN (40s, regal, but with a weary, haunted look in her eyes) sits on her throne. Her guards, pale and gaunt with glowing eyes, stand at attention. They are the 'Vampires.'

A life-sized hologram of ZARA (40s, sharp, ruthless, with a military bearing) stands before the throne.

<center>ZARA</center>
> ...another one of your supply convoys was 'lost' in the storm. Unfortunate.

<center>QUEEN</center>
> You and I both know the storm had nothing to do with it, Zara.

<center>ZARA</center>
> I can't be held responsible for the... internal difficulties you seem to be having. Your guards are looking unwell. Perhaps you should feed them.

The Queen's hands tighten on the arms of her throne.

<center>QUEEN</center>
> My internal affairs are my own. Your aggression will not go unanswered.

<center>ZARA</center>
> Aggression? I'm merely protecting my assets. The resources of Mars should be managed by a stable power. You, my dear, are anything but.

Zara's hologram flickers.

<center>ZARA</center>
> The climate fails, your honor guard is a pack of starving ghouls, and you sit on a dusty throne, pretending you still have power. The game is over. It's time for a new queen.

The hologram vanishes, leaving the Queen alone in the echoing silence.

She looks at her guards, a mixture of pity and fear in her eyes. One of them shifts, its eyes fixed on her, a flicker of hunger in its gaze.

The Queen looks away, her regal facade crumbling for a moment, revealing the desperate woman beneath.

**FADE OUT.**
