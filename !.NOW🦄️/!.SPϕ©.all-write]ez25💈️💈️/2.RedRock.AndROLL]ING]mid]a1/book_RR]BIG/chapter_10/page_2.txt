The main viewscreen flickered, resolving into the stern,
-unyielding face of UN Commander Eva Rostova. Her eyes, sharp
and suspicious, immediately fixed on Lucky. “This is *Diplomat
One*. Identify yourselves. You are operating without clearance
in a restricted zone.”

“Commander Rostova, this is Lucky Starr,” he began, his voice
-smooth, practiced. “We are on an urgent mission, authorized
by the Red Queen of Mars. We carry critical intelligence
-regarding Zara’s true intentions and the mutagen she plans
to unleash.”

Rostova’s expression remained impassive. “We have received
-intelligence regarding a Martian bio-weapon, Mr. Starr. And
it implicates the Red Queen.” Her gaze darted to the container
in his hands. “Is that it? The plague?”

“It is *a* mutagen, Commander,” Lucky corrected, his voice
-firm. “But not the one Zara intends to deliver. This is the
-unweaponized sample, extracted from her own labs. It proves
her deception. The Queen has also sent a peace agreement, a
-genuine offer of alliance against Zara’s aggression.”
