The council members, their faces pale with shock, exchanged
-horrified glances. Zara, stripped of her composure, stood
-rigid, her eyes wide with a dawning horror that mirrored
their own. Her ambition, her ruthless pursuit of power, had
-unleashed a nightmare she could not control.

“This… this is the Architect’s influence,” Lucky stated, his
-voice cutting through the stunned silence. “The one who
-manipulated Zara, who fed her the technology, the knowledge.
The one who has been pulling the strings from the shadows,
-waiting for the perfect moment to unleash this… this new
-form of Carcazod.”

The Red Queen, her earlier indignation replaced by a chilling
-understanding, nodded grimly. “The ancient texts spoke of a
-force that sought to ‘re-sculpt’ life, to ‘perfect’ it
-through forced evolution. We dismissed them as myths. But
Zara’s mutagen… it fits the description.”
