“You don’t have a choice,” Lucky replied, his voice leaving

And so, the most unlikely of alliances was forged. A renegade

They worked together, their minds a whirlwind of activity.

And they found one.

The Carcazod, for all its power, for all its intelligence,
had a weakness. It was a biological creature. It had a
