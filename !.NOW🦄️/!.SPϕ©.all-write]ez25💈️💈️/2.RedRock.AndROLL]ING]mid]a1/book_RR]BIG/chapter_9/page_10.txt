As they approached the UN envoy ship, a pristine white
-vessel that was a symbol of peace and diplomacy, they knew
that the hardest part of their journey was still ahead.

They had to convince the UN to listen to them. They had to
-convince them that the Queen was not their enemy, that Zara
was the true threat.

It would be a battle of words, of wits, of diplomacy. A
-battle they were not accustomed to fighting.

But they were ready. They had the truth on their side. And
they had each other.

They were a team. A family. And they were about to change
the course of history.
