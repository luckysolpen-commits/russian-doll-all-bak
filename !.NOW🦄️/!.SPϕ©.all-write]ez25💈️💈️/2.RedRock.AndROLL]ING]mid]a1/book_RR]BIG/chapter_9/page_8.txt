On the *Starlight Serenade*, the mood was tense, but hopeful.
They had made it through the blockade. They had a clear shot
to the UN envoy ship.

But they were not out of the woods yet. Zara’s fleet was
-still out there. And they would be looking for them.

“They’ll be expecting us to make a run for it,” Lucky said,
his mind racing, calculating the odds. “We need to do the
-unexpected.”

“What do you have in mind?” Astra asked, her eyes fixed on
the navigation console.

“We’re not going to run,” Lucky replied, a cunning smile
-on his face. “We’re going to hide.”

He pointed to a cluster of asteroids on the tactical display.
“We’ll power down the ship, drift into the asteroid field,
and wait for them to pass us by.”

It was a risky move. But it was also a brilliant one.

They were a ghost in the machine, a whisper in the darkness.
And they were about to disappear.
