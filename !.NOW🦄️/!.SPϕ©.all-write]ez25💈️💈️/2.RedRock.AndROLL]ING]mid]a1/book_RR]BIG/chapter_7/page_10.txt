The freighter’s engines hummed to life, a low, powerful
-thrum that vibrated through the deck plates. The ship
-lifted off, a ghost in the night, leaving the glittering
-city of Atlantis Prime behind.

They were on their way to Mars. They were on their way to
-war.

And as they flew into the darkness, into the unknown, Lucky
-felt a sense of peace he had not felt in years. He was a
-warrior again. He was a leader again. He was a part of a
-family again.

He was Lucky Starr. And he was ready for whatever the
-universe threw at him.
