The plan was set. The team was ready. The stakes were
-higher than ever before.

They would leave for Mars under the cover of darkness, on
a stolen, unmarked freighter. They would be ghosts, shadows
in the night. They would be a whisper in the wind, a rumor
in the darkness.

They were the last, best hope for a universe on the brink
of destruction. They were a team of misfits, of outcasts,
of survivors. They were a family.

And they were ready to go to war.
