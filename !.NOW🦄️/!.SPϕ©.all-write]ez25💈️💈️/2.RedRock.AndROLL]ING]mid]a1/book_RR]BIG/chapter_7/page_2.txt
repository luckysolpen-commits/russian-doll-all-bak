Reluctantly, Astra agreed. The plan began to take shape.
The Queen, desperate for any advantage, granted them
-unprecedented access to her intelligence network and
-resources. It was a risky gamble, but the alternative was
-unthinkable.

Gem, with Ratchet’s invaluable assistance, became the eyes
and ears of their operation. Ratchet, with his deep
-connections in the Martian underworld and his uncanny
-ability to blend into any crowd, was a ghost. He moved
-through the shadows of Martian cities, gathering whispers,
-intercepting encrypted communications, and piecing together
the fragmented puzzle of Zara’s movements. Gem, from the
-relative safety of Atlantis Prime, used her advanced
-cybernetic implants to process the raw data, cross-
-referencing it with UN intelligence, identifying patterns,
and pinpointing key targets.

Lucky, leveraging his legitimate business empire, began to
-track Zara’s supply lines. He delved into the intricate web
of corporate shell companies, offshore accounts, and black
-market dealings that fueled Zara’s operations. He discovered
-shipments of rare chemicals, specialized equipment, and even
-human resources being funneled to clandestine labs on Mars.
Each transaction, each anomaly, was a breadcrumb leading
them closer to the heart of Zara’s mutagen project. He used
his charm, his connections, and a healthy dose of corporate
-espionage to extract information from unsuspecting
-executives and disgruntled employees.
