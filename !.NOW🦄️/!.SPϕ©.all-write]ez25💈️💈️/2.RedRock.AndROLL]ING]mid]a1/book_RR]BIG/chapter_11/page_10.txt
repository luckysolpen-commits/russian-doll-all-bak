As they worked, Lucky looked at the faces of his new allies.
He saw the fear, the determination, the unwavering resolve.
He saw the best of humanity, the spirit that had allowed them
to survive against all odds.

He was not just fighting for himself anymore. He was fighting
for them. For all of them.

He was a part of something bigger than himself. He was a part
of a team. A family. A species.

He was Lucky Starr. And he was ready to save the world.
