**SCENE 1**

**INT. TRITON TOWER OFFICE - DAY**

SOUND of a deep, resonant HUM

The office is the pinnacle of corporate luxury, minimalist and clean. The only thing betraying the sterile environment is a half-empty glass of amber liquid on the desk.

Through a massive panoramic window, the city of ATLANTIS PRIME sprawls. A breathtaking network of interconnected biodomes, crystalline towers scraping the seabed, and transit tubes pulsing with maglev shuttles. The water outside is a deep, clear blue, populated by schools of exotic, bio-luminescent fish.

LUCKY STARR (40s, sharp, wearing a tailored suit of sharkskin synth-leather) stands by the window, looking out at his kingdom. The grit of a mercenary is still visible in his eyes, but it's buried under a layer of polished charm.

A holographic display shimmers into existence on his desk. The face of DIRECTOR VANCE (50s, stern, wearing the crisp uniform of the UN Reconstruction Authority) appears.

<center>VANCE</center>
> Mr. Starr.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Director. To what do I owe the pleasure?

<center>VANCE</center>
> Your grant proposal for the 'Kelp-Carbon Sequestration Project' has passed preliminary review.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Fantastic news.

<center>VANCE</center>
> However, the appropriations committee has some... concerns about the projected ROI.

Lucky turns from the window, a practiced, disarming smile on his face.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Director, the ROI isn't just in currency. It's in breathable air. My new biotech venture, 'Abyssal Bio-Tech,' isn't just a business, it's a lifeline.

He walks to his desk, leaning forward, his voice dropping to a conspiratorial, persuasive murmur.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> We're not just building a city; we're building a new Eden. Besides, the grant is a drop in the ocean compared to the goodwill it will generate. The people need to see progress, to believe in this underwater dream. My project is good PR, Director. Very good PR.

Vance's expression remains impassive, but Lucky sees a flicker in her eyes. He's won.

<center>VANCE</center>
> I'll... pass your sentiments along to the committee. Vance out.

The hologram vanishes.

Lucky picks up his glass, swirling the liquid. He looks back out the window. A school of massive, bio-luminescent squid glide past, their light painting shifting, ethereal patterns across his face.

He takes a sip, the picture of success.

**FADE OUT.**
