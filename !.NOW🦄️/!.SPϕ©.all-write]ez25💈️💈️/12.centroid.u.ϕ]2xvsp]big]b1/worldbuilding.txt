Settings:
- The Centroid: School of the Multiverse: Harry Potter-esque institution, centered around the Golden Ratio. Main hub for video games (connects TSOTS ONLINE & UNDERGROUND CIRCUS PPL INTO 1 PLAY PLACE). Part of a cluster of asteroids that revolve around a shooting star.
- Diagonal Alley: Labyrinthine quarter filled with eccentric shops, mysterious artifacts, and enigmatic characters. Contains "Mystic's Muse" (Zephyr's shop) and "The Celestial Cartographers" (Lyra's shop).
- The Fountain of Eternal Youth: A café in Diagonal Alley where ancient beings appear eternally young.
- Dustopia: Mysterious realm where creativity and destruction coexist. Contains DUSTOPEDIA, the biggest library in all of creation, full of the rarest, most impossible books. Has Keepers of Balance.
- Metal Sun: City from which Lucky's family and citizens are brought back.

Items:
- Soul Pen: Mystical writing instrument imbued with the power of creation. Allows users to tap into the collective unconscious and manifest ideas into reality. Attuned to the user's soul frequency.
- Tear It Cards: Seemingly ordinary cards that hold the power to manifest reality. By writing a desire or intention and tearing it apart, energy is released to make the dream a reality.
- Soul Bank: Ancient repository of knowledge and wisdom, a reservoir of creative potential within each soul.
- Soul Oracle: Crystal orb that reveals unique soul frequencies and connects to the Soul Bank.
- Angler Seeds: Powerful artifacts created by ancient Celestial Scribes. Amplify the power of creation, allowing those who possess them to shape reality itself.
- SS. VINIX: Magical bird/spaceship/shooting star powered by the "Holy Spirit". Contains ancient artifacts and scrolls of the Celestial Scribes.

Concepts:
- Golden Ratio: Fundamental principle governing the universe, harmony between creation and destruction, chaos and order. An energy signature that resonates through all existence.
- Pure Creations: Designs made with the power of the Soul Pen and Tear It Cards.
- The Nothing: Ancient threat, a void seeking to consume all reality. Nullification of all existence.
- Singularity: A realm where the full potential of the Golden Ratio can be harnessed.
