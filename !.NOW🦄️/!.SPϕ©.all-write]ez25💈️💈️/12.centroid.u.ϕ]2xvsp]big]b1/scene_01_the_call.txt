Lucky and Astra had lived forever. Literally. As ancient beings, they'd witnessed civilizations rise and fall, stars born and die. Their existence was a mystery even to themselves. But one thing was certain – their eternal youth was about to take a drastic turn.

A package arrived at their doorstep, adorned with the symbol of The Centroid: School of the Multiverse. A small note attached read:

"Congratulations, Lucky and Astra! You've been selected for admission to The Centroid, where you'll discover the secrets of creation and harness the power of the Golden Ratio. Report to Diagonal Alley for orientation and further instructions.

P.S. Leave your mortal concerns behind; they won't be needed here."

The pair exchanged a puzzled glance. "What's this about?" Lucky asked, eyeing the package skeptically.

Astra shrugged. "Maybe it's some kind of prank?"

Their curiosity piqued, they decided to investigate further. Inside the package, they found a small, intricately carved wooden box with a note that read:

"The Centroid awaits. Your journey begins here."

With that, the mysteries began to unfold...
