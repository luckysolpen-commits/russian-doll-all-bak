center of each canvas was a perfect

of the student who had created it.

made of what looked like polished obsidian

any storm, a sphere that could anchor
a galaxy. Astra's sphere, on the other

of spun moonlight and captured starlight. It

of infinite possibility. They looked at each
other's creations, a sense of wonder

see the essence of their friend in

nothing could ever tear them apart. Professor
