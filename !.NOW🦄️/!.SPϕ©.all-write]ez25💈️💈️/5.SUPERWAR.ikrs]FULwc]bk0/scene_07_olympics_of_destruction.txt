INT. ARCHITECT'S GRAND ARENA - AFRICA - DAY

SOUND of clashing metal, explosions, cheering crowds

The Equestrian Games are in full swing. The arena is a maelstrom of controlled chaos. The UN's MECHS, sleek and agile, with their blue and silver plating, dart and weave, firing energy blasts and deploying force fields. China's MECHS, hulking, red and gold behemoths, move with brute force, smashing through obstacles and unleashing powerful kinetic attacks.

The Architect watches from his elevated platform, a tablet in hand, his eyes darting between the various skirmishes. He occasionally taps on the screen, making notes, a faint, almost imperceptible smile playing on his lips.

On one side of the arena, a UN SCOUT MECH, nimble and fast, attempts to outmaneuver a CHINESE SIEGE MECH. The Siege Mech, slow but incredibly powerful, unleashes a volley of heavy projectiles, forcing the Scout Mech to perform acrobatic evasive maneuvers. The crowd roars with every near miss and direct hit.

In another section, a UN DEFENDER MECH, equipped with a massive energy shield, holds its ground against two CHINESE ASSAULT MECHS. The Assault Mechs hammer at the shield, their blows echoing like thunder, but the Defender Mech stands firm, its shield shimmering with absorbed energy.

The Architect's Metal Horses and Metal Dragon are not yet participating, but they stand at attention on the sidelines, their glowing eyes following the action, an aura of silent menace emanating from them.

LUCKY, GEM, and ASTRA, still observing from the crowd, analyze the combat. Gem is furiously typing on a small, portable device, recording data.

GEM
(Muttering to herself)
The UN's energy distribution is efficient, but their chassis integrity is… concerning. China's armor is superior, but their targeting systems are sluggish.

ASTRA
(Her gaze fixed on the Architect)
He's not just watching the fight. He's learning. Improving. Every clash, every weakness, he's cataloging it.

LUCKY
(His jaw tight)
He's treating this like a live-fire exercise for his own designs. He wants to see how humanity's best stacks up, so he can build something even better.

Suddenly, a particularly powerful explosion rocks the arena as a Chinese mech lands a critical blow on a UN counterpart. The UN mech crumples, sparks flying, and the crowd erupts in a mix of cheers and groans.

ARCHITECT
(A satisfied nod, making another note on his tablet)
Fascinating. The kinetic transfer was… impressive.

The games continue, a brutal, beautiful dance of destruction, all under the calculating gaze of the Architect.

FADE OUT.
