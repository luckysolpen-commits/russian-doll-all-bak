INT. ARCHITECT'S UNDERGROUND MINE CAMP - AFRICA - DAY

SOUND of distant, rhythmic drilling

LUCKY, GEM, ASTRA, and the KOREANS stand in a cavernous, dimly lit chamber. The air is thick with the smell of damp earth and ozone. Strange, glowing conduits snake across the rough-hewn walls.

LUCKY
(To the group, holding a small, glowing device)
Another cryptic summons from the Architect. "Earth security," it says. Vague as ever.

GEM
(Adjusting her glasses, scanning the device)
The coordinates are precise, though. Deep in the African interior. He certainly doesn't make it easy to find him.

ASTRA
(Looking around, a hint of unease in her voice)
I still don't trust him. Not after everything with Loona.

KOREAN 1
(Nodding in agreement)
His interest in antimatter technology is… concerning.

LUCKY
(A grim set to his jaw)
Which is precisely why we're here. To understand his true intentions. He wants to meet us without me, apparently. That alone raises a red flag.

The group exchanges uneasy glances. The distant drilling intensifies, a low thrumming vibration through the ground.

FADE OUT.
