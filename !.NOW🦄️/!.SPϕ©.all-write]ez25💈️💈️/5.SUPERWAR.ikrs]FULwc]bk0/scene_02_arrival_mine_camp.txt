INT. ARCHITECT'S UNDERGROUND MINE CAMP - AFRICA - DAY

Lucky, Gem, Astra, and the Koreans follow a narrow, winding tunnel. The air grows warmer, and a faint hum vibrates through the rock.

They emerge into a colossal, brightly lit cavern. At its center stands a monstrous, intricate structure, easily hundreds of feet tall, pulsating with soft, internal light. It resembles a colossal 3D printer, its various arms and nozzles moving with silent, precise grace.

LUCKY
(Whispering, awestruck)
The Soul Ray.

GEM
(Her eyes wide, adjusting her glasses)
It's even larger than the schematics suggested. And the energy readings… unprecedented.

ASTRA
(A shiver running down her spine)
What is he printing with this thing?

Suddenly, a voice, calm and resonant, echoes through the cavern. The ARCHITECT, a figure of imposing stature and serene demeanor, steps out from behind a console, his gaze sweeping over them.

ARCHITECT
(A slight smile playing on his lips)
Welcome, my esteemed guests. I trust your journey was… enlightening.

He gestures to the colossal printer.

ARCHITECT (CONT'D)
This, my friends, is the genesis of a new era. The Metal Sun. A Dyson Sphere, designed to harness the very essence of the cosmos. And the Soul Ray is its heart, its forge.

KOREAN 2
(Stepping forward, a tremor in his voice)
A Dyson Sphere? But… for what purpose?

ARCHITECT
(His smile widening, a glint in his eye)
To transcend. To evolve. To merge with the universe itself. And for that, we will require… considerable energy. The antimatter technology that claimed your friend, Loona, will be instrumental.

Lucky's eyes narrow. Gem and Astra exchange worried glances. The hum of the Soul Ray seems to intensify, a silent, ominous promise.

FADE OUT.
