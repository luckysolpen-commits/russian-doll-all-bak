EXT. EARTH'S ATMOSPHERE / VARIOUS CITIES - DAY

SOUND of a chilling, mechanical WHIRRING, growing louder, then the CRACKLE of energy weapons

The sky above Earth darkens, not with clouds, but with a swarm of countless, sleek, metallic shapes descending from orbit. These are the WORLD POLICE BOTS, launched from the Icarus, their forms a terrifying blend of efficiency and menace. They are supported by smaller, faster craft bearing the insignia of Mars and the Lunar Colony, acting as escorts and tactical support.

On the ground, in major cities across the globe, panic erupts. Citizens scatter, their screams swallowed by the growing mechanical din. The bots land with heavy THUDS, their multi-jointed limbs unfolding, their optical sensors glowing with an cold, unwavering light.

INT. CITY STREETS - DAY

CHAOS. People run, but there's nowhere to hide. The bots move with ruthless efficiency, herding civilians like cattle. Any attempt at resistance is met with swift, non-lethal but incapacitating energy blasts. Buildings are not destroyed, but their entrances are sealed, their windows shattered by sonic pulses.

VOICEOVER (V.O.) - ARCHITECT (calm, authoritative, broadcast globally)
Citizens of Earth. Your resistance is futile. Embrace the new order. Embrace compliance. Embrace the Mark.

Close on a terrified civilian, forced to extend their hand. A small, glowing device on a bot's arm projects a beam of light onto their skin, leaving a faint, almost invisible MARK. The civilian flinches, then their eyes glaze over, a subtle shift in their demeanor.

INT. UNDERGROUND CAMP ENTRANCE - DAY

A long, grim line of humans, their faces etched with despair, are being funneled into a massive, reinforced underground entrance. Armed bots stand guard, their presence absolute.

One bot holds a scanner. As each human passes, the scanner emits a soft GREEN GLOW if they have the Mark, and a harsh RED FLASH if they don't. Those with the red flash are immediately separated, pushed roughly into a different, even more desolate line.

INT. UNDERGROUND CAMP - DAY

The camp is a vast, dimly lit cavern, filled with rows of makeshift shelters. The air is stale, heavy with the scent of fear and desperation. Hundreds, perhaps thousands, of humans are already here, their faces blank, defeated.

LUCKY, his face streaked with dirt, a faint, almost imperceptible MARK on his wrist, is pushed into the camp by a bot. He stumbles, looking around at the defeated faces. He sees familiar faces, friends, colleagues, all stripped of their dignity.

He tries to move towards a group, but a bot's energy baton extends, blocking his path. He is directed to an empty section of the camp. He looks up, his eyes burning with a quiet fury, but he knows resistance here is pointless.

EXT. CITY RUINS - DAY

The once vibrant cityscapes are now desolate. Buildings stand, but they are empty husks, monuments to a lost era. The only movement is the patrolling World Police Bots, their metallic forms glinting in the harsh sunlight, symbols of absolute control. The sky is clear, but the air feels heavy, oppressive.

FADE OUT.
