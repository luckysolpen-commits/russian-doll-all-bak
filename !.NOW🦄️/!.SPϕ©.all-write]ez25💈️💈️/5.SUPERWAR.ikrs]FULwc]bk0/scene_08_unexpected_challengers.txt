INT. ARCHITECT'S GRAND ARENA - AFRICA - DAY

SOUND of a sudden, unexpected ROAR, crowd murmurs turning to gasps

The mecha olympics are momentarily paused, a lull in the metal-on-metal chaos. The Architect is still engrossed in his tablet, reviewing data from the previous rounds.

Suddenly, a new, powerful ROAR echoes through the arena, distinct from the mechanical sounds of the UN and Chinese mechs. All eyes turn to a previously unnoticed entrance on the far side of the arena.

From the tunnel, a massive, DRAGON-LIKE MECH emerges. It's not as sleek as the UN mechs, nor as brutish as China's. Instead, it possesses an organic, almost primal design, its metallic scales shimmering with an iridescent sheen. Its eyes glow with a familiar, fierce intelligence. This is LUCKY'S DRAGON BOT CLONE, but clearly manned, its cockpit glowing faintly.

The crowd erupts in a mixture of confusion and excitement. The Architect looks up from his tablet, his serene expression replaced by a flicker of surprise, then a calculating gaze.

Close behind Lucky's mech, two more figures emerge. These are not bipedal mechs, but rather elegant, equine-shaped robots, clearly designed for speed and agility. They are the MANNED RIDING HORSE ROBOTS, "STARLIGHT" (piloted by GEM) and "MOONBEAM" (piloted by ASTRA). Their metallic hides are polished to a mirror sheen, reflecting the arena lights, and their movements are fluid and graceful, like living horses.

Gem and Astra, visible in their respective cockpits, wave to the stunned crowd, a defiant grin on their faces.

ANNOUNCER (V.O.)
(Stuttering, clearly caught off guard)
Ladies and gentlemen, it appears we have… unexpected challengers! A rogue entry! From… from the Earth Alliance, it seems!

The Architect's eyes narrow. He recognizes the design of Lucky's mech, a clear homage to his own Dragon Bot, but with a crucial difference – it's piloted. And the horse-like mechs… he hadn't anticipated this.

ARCHITECT
(Into a hidden comm, a hint of annoyance in his voice)
Security! Explain this breach!

A security officer's voice crackles back, apologetic.

SECURITY OFFICER (O.S.)
(V.O.)
Supreme Emperor, they… they bypassed all our protocols. Their energy signatures were… masked. We didn't detect them until they were in the arena!

The Architect dismisses the officer with a wave of his hand, his gaze fixed on Lucky's team. A slow, predatory smile spreads across his face. This was unexpected, yes, but perhaps… entertaining.

Lucky's Dragon Bot Clone lets out another, challenging ROAR, its head turning to face the Architect's own Metal Dragon, which stands motionless on the sidelines, its red eyes now glowing brighter, as if in recognition of a rival.

FADE OUT.
