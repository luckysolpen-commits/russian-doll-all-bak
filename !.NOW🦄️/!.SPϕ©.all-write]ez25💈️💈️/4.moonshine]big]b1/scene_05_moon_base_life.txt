SCENE 5

INT. LUNAR BASE - VARIOUS LOCATIONS - DAY

SOUND of general activity, distant hum of machinery

The hollow moon base is a marvel of engineering and comfort. We see various members of The Chaebol exploring and settling in.

In a state-of-the-art GYM, ASTRA is strapped into a "Lunar Luge" simulator, her face a mask of concentration as she navigates a virtual, gravity-defying track. She laughs as she narrowly avoids a simulated crater.

ASTRA
(To herself)
This is incredible!

In a vast, multi-level LIBRARY, GEM is surrounded by holographic books, his fingers flying across a console as he devours information on astrophysics and lunar geology. He occasionally mutters to himself, lost in thought.

GEM
(Muttering)
...quantum entanglement of silicate structures... fascinating...

In a vibrant GAMING ROOM, JIN, MIN, and YOON are immersed in a virtual reality game, their movements synchronized as they battle unseen enemies. Cheers and shouts erupt as Jin scores a critical hit.

JIN
(Shouting)
Boom! Headshot!

Meanwhile, LUCKY and EDGE are in a bustling COMMERCIAL AREA. Lucky, ever the entrepreneur, is sketching designs on a tablet, while Edge, surprisingly, is listening intently.

LUCKY
(Enthusiastically)
Think about it, Edge. A lunar-themed cafe. "Moonshine Cafe." Space-grown coffee, moon-made pastries. We cater to the base personnel, the visiting dignitaries... it's a goldmine!

EDGE
(Grinning)
"Moonshine Cafe," I like it! And I know a guy who can get us some... *special* ingredients. For the pastries, of course.

Lucky eyes him suspiciously, but a small smile plays on his lips.

LUCKY
(Warningly)
Just stick to the pastries, Edge.

Edge winks, then claps Lucky on the back.

EDGE
Don't worry, boss. I'm a professional. Mostly.

FADE OUT.
