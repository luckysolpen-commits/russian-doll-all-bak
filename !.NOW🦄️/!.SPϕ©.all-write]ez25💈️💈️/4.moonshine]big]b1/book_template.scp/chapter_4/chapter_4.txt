Chapter 4: Edge Danger Man

The lunar shuttle settled onto the vast landing pad with a soft hiss of powering down engines, the faint whisper of lunar wind carrying through the comms. The Chaebol team disembarked, their space suits gleaming under the harsh, unfiltered lunar sun. The landscape stretched out before them, a desolate expanse of craters and dust, yet possessing a stark, alien beauty.

Suddenly, a figure detached himself from a group of lunar base personnel, moving with an exaggerated swagger that defied the low gravity. This was Edge Danger Man, a man in his forties, lean and athletic, with a mischievous glint in his eyes that was almost palpable even through his visor. He wore a custom black jumpsuit, a silver "EDM" logo emblazoned proudly on his chest.

"Well, well, well!" Edge's voice boomed, slightly distorted by the comms, yet filled with an undeniable theatricality. "Look what the space cat dragged in! The Chaebol, eh? I'm Edge Danger Man, and I'll be your guide to the wild, wild world of asteroid exploration!"

He extended a hand, first to Astra, then to Gem, and finally to the Koreans, shaking each hand with an almost theatrical flourish. He winked at Astra, who, despite herself, couldn't help but crack a small smile.

"He's... enthusiastic," Astra murmured quietly to Lucky.

Lucky watched Edge, a flicker of annoyance in his eyes. He sensed a rival, especially as Edge's charm seemed to be working its magic on Astra and Gem.

"Alright, team!" Edge announced, his voice cutting through the lunar silence. "No time for moon-gazing! We've got asteroids to conquer, riches to claim! And I'm here to teach you how to wrangle these cosmic beasts with the latest in drone technology!"

Edge gestured towards a fleet of sleek, advanced exploration and drilling drones parked nearby. With effortless grace, he leaped onto the back of one, striking a pose that was clearly meant for an audience.

"These babies are the key!" he declared. "And I'm the master key-holder!"

Lucky stepped forward, a competitive glint in his eye. "I've had some experience with drones myself, Edge. Perhaps we can compare notes."

Edge hopped off the drone, a challenging smirk on his face. "Oh, a challenger! I like it! But let's see if your 'experience' can keep up with pure, unadulterated Danger!"

Jin, Min, and Yoon exchanged excited glances. Jin, ever the bold one, stepped forward. "Can you... do a stunt? With the drone?"

Edge's eyes lit up, a true showman's grin spreading across his face. "Can I do a stunt? My friend, I *am* the stunt! Watch and learn, Chaebol!"

With a whoop, Edge leaped back onto the drone, revved its engines, and sent it soaring into the lunar sky, performing an impressive series of aerial maneuvers. Astra and Gem laughed, while the Koreans cheered, pulling out their comms to record the spectacle.

Lucky shook his head, a mixture of exasperation and grudging admiration on his face. This was going to be an interesting partnership, indeed.
