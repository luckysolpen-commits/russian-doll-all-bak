SCENE 4

EXT. LUNAR SURFACE - MOON BASE LANDING PAD - DAY

SOUND of shuttle engines powering down, lunar wind

The lunar shuttle settles onto a vast landing pad. The Chaebol team disembarks, their space suits gleaming under the harsh lunar sun. The landscape is desolate, yet strangely beautiful.

Suddenly, a figure detaches from a group of lunar base personnel. He moves with an exaggerated swagger, even in the low gravity. This is EDGE DANGER MAN (40s, lean, athletic, with a mischievous glint in his eyes). He wears a custom black jumpsuit with a silver "EDM" logo emblazoned on the chest.

EDGE
(Voice booming, slightly distorted by comms)
Well, well, well! Look what the space cat dragged in! The Chaebol, eh? I'm Edge Danger Man, and I'll be your guide to the wild, wild world of asteroid exploration!

Edge extends a hand to Astra, then Gem, then the Koreans, shaking each hand with an almost theatrical flourish. He winks at Astra, who can't help but crack a small smile.

ASTRA
(To Lucky, quietly)
He's... enthusiastic.

Lucky watches Edge, a flicker of annoyance in his eyes. He senses a rival, especially as Edge's charm seems to be working on Astra and Gem.

EDGE
(To the group)
Alright, team! No time for moon-gazing! We've got asteroids to conquer, riches to claim! And I'm here to teach you how to wrangle these cosmic beasts with the latest in drone technology!

Edge gestures towards a fleet of sleek, advanced EXPLORATION AND DRILLING DRONES parked nearby. He effortlessly leaps onto the back of one, striking a pose.

EDGE (CONT'D)
These babies are the key! And I'm the master key-holder!

Lucky steps forward, a competitive glint in his eye.

LUCKY
(Through comms)
I've had some experience with drones myself, Edge. Perhaps we can compare notes.

Edge hops off the drone, a challenging smirk on his face.

EDGE
Oh, a challenger! I like it! But let's see if your 'experience' can keep up with pure, unadulterated Danger!

Jin, Min, and Yoon exchange excited glances. Jin steps forward.

JIN
(To Edge)
Can you... do a stunt? With the drone?

Edge's eyes light up. He grins, a true showman.

EDGE
Can I do a stunt? My friend, I *am* the stunt! Watch and learn, Chaebol!

Edge leaps back onto the drone, revs its engines, and with a whoop, sends it soaring into the lunar sky, performing an impressive series of aerial maneuvers. Astra and Gem laugh, while the Koreans cheer, pulling out their comms to record the spectacle.

Lucky shakes his head, a mixture of exasperation and grudging admiration on his face. This was going to be an interesting partnership.

FADE OUT.
