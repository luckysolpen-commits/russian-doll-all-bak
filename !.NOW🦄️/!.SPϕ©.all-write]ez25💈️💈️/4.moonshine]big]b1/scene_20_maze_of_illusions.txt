SCENE 20

INT. THE DIABOLUS - MAZE OF ILLUSIONS - CONTINUOUS

SOUND of disorienting whispers, shifting sounds

Lucky's team steps into the inky blackness. Immediately, the path behind them vanishes. The air is thick with disorienting whispers, some seductive, some terrifying, all designed to sow doubt and fear.

GEM
(Voice strained)
I can't see anything! And what are those voices?

ASTRA
(Closing her eyes, focusing)
Illusions. They're trying to confuse us. To make us turn on each other.

Suddenly, a shimmering image of a vast, untouched diamond mine appears before Lucky. It's a vision of unimaginable wealth, enough to solve all their financial woes, to fund any venture. A voice, smooth and persuasive, whispers in his ear.

WHISPERING VOICE (V.O.)
(Seductive)
Take it, Lucky. It's all yours. No more struggles. No more risks. Just endless prosperity.

Lucky hesitates, his eyes drawn to the glittering vision. He can almost feel the weight of the diamonds in his hand.

LOONA (Hologram, appearing beside him, her form solid and clear)
(Firmly)
An illusion, Lucky. A temptation. Remember your purpose.

Lucky blinks, and the vision flickers, revealing a grotesque, skeletal hand reaching out from behind the diamonds. He recoils.

Meanwhile, Astra is confronted by an illusion of her greatest fear: the complete destruction of Earth, a barren wasteland where nothing remains. The whispers are filled with despair.

WHISPERING VOICE (V.O.)
(Despairing)
It's inevitable, Astra. All your efforts are futile. The end is coming. You cannot stop it.

Astra clutches her head, tears welling in her eyes. The despair is overwhelming.

LUCKY
(Calling out)
Astra! It's not real! Focus on my voice!

Astra forces herself to open her eyes, to look at Lucky. The illusion wavers.

Jin, Min, and Yoon are bombarded with visions of their families, safe and happy, but with a condition: they must abandon the mission and return to Earth immediately. The whispers promise comfort and security.

JIN
(Torn)
My family... they need me...

MIN
(Shaking her head)
It's a trick, Jin! We have to stay!

They push through the illusions, relying on each other, on the anchors of their shared purpose. The whispers grow louder, more aggressive, but they press on, a united front against the psychological assault.

Finally, the darkness begins to recede, revealing a narrow, winding path. The whispers fade, replaced by a low, resonant hum.

DEMON (V.O.)
(A low rumble, almost a sigh)
First trial... passed. But the path ahead... is far more perilous.

FADE OUT.
