# Screenplay Outline

## Logline:
After a galactic war ends in an uneasy truce, a team of unlikely heroes embarks on an asteroid mining venture, only to discover a demonic entity within a celestial body, forcing them to make ultimate sacrifices and confront a shadowy figure seeking to exploit their discoveries for his own sinister technological advancements.

## Synopsis:
Following a sudden ceasefire in a galactic conflict, Lucky and his allies, including the NewKorean forces, are drawn into a lucrative black market asteroid mining operation by Luna. They establish a base on the hollow moon and begin extracting valuable resources, celebrating their initial success. However, their triumph is short-lived when Loona Twilight reveals that one of their asteroids, dubbed "The Diabolus," is a hollow, fast-moving, dark-energy-emitting entity heading for Earth. As Earth descends into panic, Edge Danger Man, a reckless stunt devil who joined their team, attempts to cut a deal with the UN to protect his past, leading to an over-budget and ill-conceived mission. Meanwhile, Lucky's secret team discovers The Diabolus is a literal demon that cannot be destroyed by force. Both teams enter the demon realm within the asteroid, where Edge's hubris leads to disaster. Lucky's team then attempts to appease the demon through trials, culminating in Loona's ultimate sacrifice to make The Diabolus disappear. In the aftermath, as they mourn Loona, Lucky and his remaining team are cryptically summoned by The Architect, a mysterious venture capitalist who seeks to exploit Loona's sacrifice and their discoveries for his own research into dangerous warp drive and antimatter technology, isolating Lucky upon their arrival at his hidden African mine camp.

## Characters:
### Protagonist: Lucky
[Leader of the team, initially an entrepreneur, becomes a reluctant hero. Driven by a sense of responsibility and loyalty to his friends.]

### Antagonist: The Architect
[A mysterious, wealthy venture capitalist with a hidden agenda. Seeks to exploit advanced technology, including antimatter and warp drives, regardless of the cost.]

### Supporting Characters:
- **Astra:** Lucky's girlfriend, perceptive, empathetic, thrill-seeker, scientist.
- **Gem:** Lucky's friend, pragmatic, excited by financial prospects.
- **Luna:** Initiator of the asteroid mining venture, confident, visionary.
- **Mr. Kim:** Korean representative, investor, cautious but interested in profit.
- **Jin, Min, Yoon:** Korean tear-it users, skilled, enjoy gaming and stunts.
- **Edge Danger Man:** Charismatic, reckless stunt devil, initially a guide, later self-serving and impulsive.
- **Loona Twilight:** Hologram queen, provides crucial information about The Diabolus, makes the ultimate sacrifice.
- **Dr. Patel:** Stern UN representative, initially skeptical of Edge, later willing to work with him for results.
- **Kofi:** Emissary of The Architect, African warrior, leads the Architect's forces.

## Act I: Setup
### Inciting Incident:
News of UN/USA treaty ends galactic war, leading to Luna's black market asteroid mining venture.

### Key Scenes:
- Post-war regrouping of Lucky's team and NewKorea forces.
- Luna presents the lucrative asteroid mining opportunity to Lucky's team and Mr. Kim.
- The team agrees to invest, Mr. Kim demands due diligence.
- The Chaebol (Lucky's team and Koreans) travels to the hollow moon.
- Introduction of Edge Danger Man, who clashes with Lucky but charms the others.
- Exploration of life and amenities inside the hollow moon base.

## Act II: Confrontation
### Plot Point 1 (End of Act I):
The Chaebol begins asteroid exploration, with Edge showboating his skills.

### Midpoint:
The team brings asteroids to the moon for quantum and nuclear analysis, extracts valuable resources, and sends a positive report to Mr. Kim, leading to a celebration party.

### Plot Point 2 (End of Act II):
Loona Twilight's holographic visit reveals Asteroid-6 ("The Diabolus") is a hollow, fast-moving, dark-energy-emitting object heading for Earth. Edge tries to prevent communication with Earth to protect his past. Earth panics, labels it "The Diabolus," and plans an investigation. Edge attempts to strike a deal with the UN behind everyone's back.

### Key Scenes:
- First asteroid exploration, Edge's daring stunts and Lucky's frustration.
- Scientific analysis of asteroids on the moon, extraction of rare elements.
- Celebration party for their findings and funding, with relationship development.
- Loona Twilight's secret visit to Astra, revealing the true nature of Asteroid-6.
- Edge's attempts to control information and prevent Lucky from contacting Earth.
- Earth's widespread panic and the UN's declaration of "The Diabolus" as an imminent threat.
- Edge's meeting with Dr. Patel and the UN, where he secures a deal to lead the mission.
- Edge presents an over-the-top, over-budget mission plan to the UN.
- Lucky's secret team (Loona, Gem, Astra) develops a backup plan involving harnessing Tear-It power, potentially sacrificing clones.
- Lucky's secret team uses SoulPens to discover The Diabolus is a literal demon that will feed on violence.
- Edge and the UN team, backed by The Architect, also plan to enter The Diabolus for commercial exploitation.

## Act III: Resolution
### Climax:
- The Architect sends advanced ships and African soldiers (led by Kofi) to assist the UN team in entering the demon realm within The Diabolus.
- Edge's team, driven by hubris, agitates the demon, leading to the destruction of Kofi's soldiers and robots, forcing Edge and the UN to flee.
- Lucky's secret team steps in, attempting to appease The Diabolus through a series of trials.
- Loona makes the ultimate sacrifice, entering the demon realm to make The Diabolus disappear completely.

### Resolution:
- The team returns to the moon, devastated by Loona's sacrifice.
- A grand mourning party and funeral are held for Loona on the Hollow Moon, honoring her as their queen.
- Lucky, Gem, Astra, and the Koreans receive a cryptic summons from The Architect to his African mine camp, under the guise of "Earth security."
- The Architect's true, sinister agenda is revealed: to exploit Loona's sacrifice and the antimatter technology for warp drives.
- Upon arrival, Lucky is isolated, and the rest of the team is led to a briefing room, confirming The Architect's manipulative intentions.

### Key Scenes:
- Entry into the demon realm, encounter with Kofi and The Architect's forces.
- Confrontation with the demon in its lair, Edge's team's foolish aggression.
- Edge and UN team's cowardly retreat.
- Lucky's team's trials of appeasement.
- Loona's ultimate sacrifice and disappearance of The Diabolus.
- The team's grief and return to the moon.
- Loona's funeral and legacy.
- The Architect's cryptic summons.
- The team's journey to the African mine camp.
- Lucky's isolation and the revelation of The Architect's true motives to the rest of the team.
