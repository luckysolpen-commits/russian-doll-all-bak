SCENE 13

INT. LUNAR BASE - UN CONFERENCE ROOM - DAY

SOUND of projector humming, Edge's booming voice

Edge Danger Man stands before a holographic projector, a massive, intricate diagram of his mission plan swirling behind him. Dr. Patel and the UN officials sit, looking increasingly bewildered.

EDGE
(Gesturing grandly)
...and so, our multi-layered defense system begins with the deployment of retro-fitted Cold War-era satellites! We're talking vintage tech, repurposed for cutting-edge trajectory tracking! Think of it as a cosmic tripwire!

He clicks a remote, and a diagram of ancient-looking satellites appears, surrounded by flashing arrows.

UN OFFICIAL 2
(Whispering to Dr. Patel)
Cold War-era? Is he serious?

DR. PATEL
(Whispering back)
Apparently.

EDGE
(Undeterred)
Next, our advanced AI-powered drones! These aren't your garden-variety quadcopters, folks! These are orbital disruptors! They'll create a... a 'kinky' path for The Diabolus, making it impossible for it to hit Earth directly!

The diagram shifts to show drones zipping around The Diabolus, creating a chaotic, swirling path.

UN OFFICIAL 1
(Clearing throat)
Mr. Danger Man, the term 'kinky path' is not standard scientific terminology.

EDGE
(Waving a hand dismissively)
Details, details! It's about the *effect*! And the effect will be glorious! Then, for the main event: an arsenal of vintage laser cannons and particle beams! We'll blast that rock into oblivion!

The holographic display shows a barrage of colorful energy beams striking The Diabolus, which explodes in a shower of sparks.

DR. PATEL
(Sighing)
And the budget for this... arsenal?

EDGE
(Beaming)
Ah, the numbers! Our team has prepared an extensive report! Suffice it to say, it's a significant investment in planetary safety! And finally, if all else fails, our secret weapon: the 'Asteroid-Smashing' robot! Code-named: DOOM-BOT 3000!

A massive, menacing robot, bristling with weapons, appears on the display, its eyes glowing red. It smashes The Diabolus with a giant fist.

UN OFFICIAL 2
(Eyes wide)
Doom-Bot 3000?

EDGE
(Puffing out his chest)
Only the best for Earth's last line of defense! The cost-benefit analysis is overwhelmingly in our favor!

Dr. Patel pinches the bridge of her nose. The plan is absurd, bloated, and likely to fail, but Edge's confidence is almost infectious. And they are desperate.

DR. PATEL
(Wearily)
Mr. Danger Man, can you provide a more detailed breakdown of the budget and timeline? Within the hour, as you promised?

EDGE
(Grinning)
Consider it done, Doctor! The future of Earth is in good hands!

FADE OUT.
