**SCENE 10**

**EXT. OCEAN FLOOR - NIGHT**

The ocean floor is a vast, dark expanse, illuminated only by the powerful lights of a specialized Sub-Korea deployment vessel. The current manipulator device, now much larger and more complex than the crystalline model seen earlier, is being carefully lowered into position by robotic arms.

LUCKY, DORCHESTER, GEM, SPIKE, and ASTRA are on the bridge of the vessel, overseeing the operation. The tension is palpable.

LUCKY
(to a Sub-Korea technician)
Status on the power conduits?

TECHNICIAN
All green, sir. Full power transfer initiated.

The device hums to life, a low thrumming sound that vibrates through the vessel.

ASTRA
(at a console, fingers flying)
Security systems bypassed. All UN surveillance in this sector is blind. They won't see us coming.

GEM
(watching the sonar screen)
UN naval exercise. Three destroyers, two frigates, and a carrier group. Right on schedule.

SPIKE
(cracking her knuckles)
Time to give them a rude awakening.

Lucky approaches a large console, his Soul Pen device glowing faintly in his hand. He connects it to the console, and a complex holographic interface springs to life around him. He begins to input a series of precise sonic frequencies.

LUCKY
(focused)
Initiating primary sequence. Targeting UN fleet coordinates.

The ocean outside begins to churn. Small ripples turn into powerful currents. The vessel rocks gently.

DORCHESTER
(a grim smile)
It's working.

On the main viewscreen, the UN fleet, previously a neat formation, begins to be buffeted by unseen forces. Their ships are tossed and turned, their lights flickering.

UN COMMANDER (V.O.)
(from a crackling comms channel)
What in the blazes is going on?! We're losing control! All hands, brace for impact!

The currents intensify, forming massive, swirling vortexes. The destroyers are pulled downwards, their hulls groaning under the immense pressure. The frigates are slammed into each other. The carrier, a behemoth of steel, is caught in a colossal whirlpool, slowly being dragged into the abyss.

LUCKY
(sweat beading on his brow)
Secondary sequence initiated. Creating a localized maelstrom. They won't know what hit them.

The viewscreen shows the UN fleet disappearing, one by one, into the churning depths. The ocean roars with the power of the manipulated currents.

GEM
(a gasp of awe)
They're gone. All of them.

SPIKE
(a low whistle)
Impressive, Lucky. Very impressive.

Suddenly, the device on the ocean floor begins to glow with an unnatural, blinding intensity. It pulses erratically, the hum turning into a high-pitched whine.

LUCKY
(alarmed)
What the...? It's overloading! The energy output is spiking!

DORCHESTER
(her face pale)
It's not just overloading. It's... changing. Look!

On the viewscreen, the device is no longer just glowing. It's expanding, morphing, its crystalline structure twisting and growing. A strange, dark energy emanates from it, spreading through the water.

LUCKY
(frantic)
I'm losing control! It's not responding to my commands!

The vessel is violently thrown sideways. Alarms blare on the bridge.

ASTRA
(at her console)
Energy readings off the charts! It's creating a localized singularity! It's going to tear the ocean apart!

DORCHESTER
(eyes wide with horror)
We've made a mistake. A terrible mistake.

**FADE OUT.**
