**SCENE 5**

**EXT. NEW ATLANTIS - DAY**

NEW ATLANTIS is a stunning underwater city, a mix of ancient Greek and modern Asian architecture. Towering buildings made of coral and shell rise from the ocean floor. The city is bustling with activity, with mer-people and humans coexisting.

LUCKY, SPY-K, DORCHESTER, and RAVEN arrive in a sleek, unmarked submarine. They dock at a secluded port and enter the city.

DORCHESTER
(as they walk through the city)
New Atlantis. A city of dreams and schemes. The perfect place to disappear... or to find someone who doesn't want to be found.

They move through the crowded streets, taking in the sights and sounds. The city is a vibrant, multicultural hub.

ASTRA
So, where do we start?

DORCHESTER
We start by finding the source of the problem. The faction that's pulling the strings.

She leads them to a less glamorous part of the city, a district filled with industrial buildings and warehouses. She stops in front of a large, imposing building with a sign that reads: "Sub-Korea Research Institute."

LUCKY
Sub-Korea? I've heard of them. A powerful tech conglomerate.

DORCHESTER
They're more than that. They're a front. They're using their technology to take control of the city, one sub-robot at a time.

They find a vantage point overlooking the institute. They see sub-robots, small, spider-like machines, patrolling the perimeter. The security is tight.

GEM
How do we get in?

DORCHESTER
We don't. Not yet. First, we need to know what we're walking into. Raven, you're up.

Raven grins and pulls out a small, sophisticated-looking drone. She launches it, and it flies towards the institute, its camera feeding them a live video stream.

**INT. SUB-KOREA RESEARCH INSTITUTE - CONTINUOUS (VIA DRONE)**

The drone flies through an open window, giving them a view of the institute's interior. They see rows of workstations, where scientists are assembling and programming the sub-robots. The scale of the operation is massive.

LUCKY
They're building an army.

Suddenly, the drone's camera whips around. A large, shadowy figure is moving in the background. It's massive, much larger than a human.

SPIKE
What was that?

The drone tries to get a closer look, but an alarm blares. The drone is shot down.

DORCHESTER
So, they have a pet. This just got more interesting.

LUCKY
We need to get inside. Find out what that thing is.

DORCHESTER
(smiling)
I thought you'd never ask.

**FADE OUT.**
