**SCENE 13**

**INT. UN COMMAND CENTER - GENEVA - NIGHT**

The UN Command Center is a sprawling, multi-level complex, a hive of activity even at night. Holographic displays project global data, troop movements, and economic forecasts. UN soldiers, crisp in their uniforms, patrol the corridors.

LUCKY, DORCHESTER, JI-HYUN, SOO-JIN, HYE-MI, and MI-SUN move through the complex like ghosts. Dorchester, in a UN officer's uniform, leads the way, her face a mask of professional calm. Lucky and the others are in stealth suits, their movements silent.

DORCHESTER
(whispering into her comms)
Almost there. Sáoirse's personal guard is concentrated on the main summit hall. This is our window.

They reach a reinforced door, marked with a red warning light. This is the entrance to Sáoirse's private command center.

LUCKY
(to Dorchester)
Ready?

DORCHESTER
(a dangerous glint in her eye)
Born ready.

Ji-Hyun steps forward, her hands glowing faintly. She channels her elemental energy, and a powerful blast of focused wind slams into the door, tearing it from its hinges with a deafening CRASH. Alarms blare.

Inside, SAOIRSE (50s), elegant and composed, sits at a large console, surrounded by her personal guard – a dozen AUGMENTED SUPER-SOLDIERS, their bodies rippling with enhanced musculature, their eyes glowing faintly.

SÁOIRSE
(a cold, amused smile)
Well, well, well. Look what the tide dragged in. Dorchester. And her little friends. I knew you couldn't be trusted.

DORCHESTER
(drawing a sleek, energy-bladed dagger)
And I knew you were a monster, Sáoirse. Time to end this.

The Super-Soldiers charge. The command center erupts into a maelstrom of combat.

Lucky draws his Soul Pen Device. It pulses with a vibrant blue light as he channels his energy, firing concentrated blasts that send Super-Soldiers flying. He moves with fluid grace, a whirlwind of precision strikes.

Ji-Hyun unleashes torrents of water, creating slippery surfaces that send soldiers tumbling, then follows with bursts of fire that force them back. Her elemental control is absolute.

Soo-Jin, nimble and quick, uses her hacking skills to disrupt the Super-Soldiers' comms and targeting systems, causing confusion in their ranks. She throws Tear-It cards, which explode with blinding flashes of light or disorienting sonic bursts.

Hye-Mi, despite her gentle nature, is a formidable force. She uses her Tear-It cards to create shimmering energy shields that deflect incoming fire, and her touch, usually for healing, now delivers stunning electrical shocks.

Mi-Sun, a blur of motion, engages the Super-Soldiers in brutal hand-to-hand combat. Her movements are precise and powerful, each strike designed to incapacitate. She uses Tear-It cards to enhance her physical strength, delivering blows that crack armor.

Dorchester, a whirlwind of lethal grace, dances through the fray. Her energy dagger slices through armor, and she uses her knowledge of the complex's layout to her advantage, leading the Super-Soldiers into traps and ambushes.

Sáoirse watches the battle with a detached amusement, occasionally issuing commands to her remaining guards. She is confident, almost arrogant, in her defenses.

One of the Super-Soldiers, larger and more heavily armored than the rest, lunges at Lucky. It's a brutal, enhanced version of the UN agents from the Soul Pen.

LUCKY
(gritting his teeth)
Still sending your puppets, Sáoirse?

He unleashes a powerful blast from his Soul Pen, augmented by a Tear-It card, sending the Super-Soldier crashing into a console, sparks flying.

The team systematically dismantles Sáoirse's defenses. The command center is a wreck, consoles shattered, holographic displays flickering erratically. Only Sáoirse and a few of her elite guards remain.

SÁOIRSE
(her composure finally cracking, a snarl on her face)
You fools! You think this changes anything? I am the future! I am inevitable!

She presses a hidden button on her console. A section of the floor slides open, revealing a sleek, personal SUBMARINE. It's small, designed for rapid escape.

SÁOIRSE (CONT'D)
You may have won this battle, but the war is far from over! You have no idea what I am capable of!

She leaps into the submarine. The hatch hisses shut. The submarine begins to descend rapidly into a hidden shaft.

LUCKY
(running to the shaft)
She's getting away!

DORCHESTER
(a grim determination)
Not if I can help it. Ji-Hyun, Soo-Jin, with me! We'll take the pursuit sub! Gem, Spike, Astra, secure this place! Make sure no one else escapes!

**FADE OUT.**
