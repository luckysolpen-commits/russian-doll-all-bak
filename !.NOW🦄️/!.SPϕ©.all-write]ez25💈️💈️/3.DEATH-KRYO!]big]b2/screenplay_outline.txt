**Title:** DEATH-KRYO!

**Logline:** A newly rich tech genius, framed for murder by a shadowy organization, must uncover a vast conspiracy involving underwater cities, cloned mermaids, and a planet-threatening superweapon, all while battling his own inner demons and his evil twin brother.

**Characters:**

*   **Lucky:** A brilliant and rebellious tech genius, now super-rich. He's the creator of the "Soul Pen" and has just cured the "Carcazod" plague. He is framed for murder and hunted by the UN.
*   **Spy-K:** A group of highly skilled female operatives, friends with Lucky.
    *   **Gem:** A member of Spy-K.
    *   **Astra:** A member of Spy-K with bio-enhancements.
    *   **Spike:** A member of Spy-K.
*   **Dorchester:** A mysterious and deadly female pirate/assassin with a hidden agenda. She is training her younger sister, Raven.
*   **Raven:** Dorchester's younger sister, learning the art of deception.
*   **The Architect:** Lucky's evil twin brother, trying to control him from the shadows.
*   **Queen Sophia:** A queen whose daughter is murdered.
*   **Seasha:** A UN agent and femme fatale.
*   **Sáoirse:** The ruthless leader of the UN.
*   **New-Korea and Sub-Korea:** Asian factions controlling underwater cities and using sub-robots. They have an alliance with the clone-merpeople.
*   **Clone-Merpeople:** A peaceful faction of mutant clones that the UN wants to eliminate.
*   **Ji-Hyun, Soo-Jin, Hye-Mi, Mi-Sun:** Women of the New Korean Resistance who become Lucky's allies.

**Outline:**

**Act I: The Setup**

1.  **Scene 1: The Party.**
    *   **Setting:** The Soul Pen, a lavish underwater nightclub.
    *   **Event:** A massive party celebrating Lucky's success in curing the Carcazod plague and selling his company, Cerberus Inc., to the UN for a fortune.
    *   **Inciting Incident:** UN counter-force agents crash the party, murder one of Queen Sophia's daughters, and frame Lucky for the crime.

2.  **Scene 2: The Escape.**
    *   **Setting:** The Soul Pen and the surrounding underwater tunnels.
    *   **Event:** Lucky and Spy-K fight off the UN agents and escape the Soul Pen.

3.  **Scene 3: The Cryptic Clue.**
    *   **Setting:** An abandoned underwater ruin.
    *   **Event:** They discover a cryptic message: "DORCHESTER'S GAME AFOOT," leading them to the infamous pirate.

**Act II: The Confrontation**

4.  **Scene 4: Dorchester's Lair.**
    *   **Setting:** Dorchester's hidden underwater lair.
    *   **Event:** They meet Dorchester and her sister, Raven. Dorchester reveals she's been hired to find out who is behind the agents hunting Lucky and that they are connected to a faction in the underwater city of New Atlantis. She proposes a game of cat and mouse.

5.  **Scene 5: New Atlantis.**
    *   **Setting:** The underwater city of New Atlantis.
    *   **Event:** They travel to New Atlantis and discover the "Sub-Korea Research Institute," where New-Korea and Sub-Korea are using sub-robots to take control.

6.  **Scene 6: Carc-Zilla.**
    *   **Setting:** New Atlantis.
    *   **Event:** They are attacked by "Carc-Zilla," the UN's secret weapon. They escape into a hidden cave system.

7.  **Scene 7: Sub-Korea.**
    *   **Setting:** The underwater city of Sub-Korea.
    *   **Event:** They learn about the alliance between the Korean factions and the clone-merpeople, and the UN's plot to commit genocide. Dorchester reveals her past with the UN's genetic engineering program.

8.  **Scene 8: The Alliance.**
    *   **Setting:** A council chamber in Sub-Korea.
    *   **Event:** The Korean factions propose an alliance with Lucky's group to take down the UN. They have a device that can manipulate ocean currents.

9.  **Scene 9: The Deal.**
    *   **Setting:** Sub-Korea.
    *   **Event:** Lucky's group agrees to the deal, but on their own terms.

10. **Scene 10: The Deployment.**
    *   **Setting:** The ocean floor near Sub-Korea.
    *   **Event:** They deploy the device, creating a massive storm that sucks in the UN's naval forces. The device malfunctions, threatening a catastrophe.

11. **Scene 11: Calm Before the Storm.**
    *   **Setting:** A Korean-style villa in Seoul.
    *   **Event:** The group bonds with the women of the New Korean Resistance (Ji-Hyun, Soo-Jin, Hye-Mi, Mi-Sun). They learn about "Tear-It" cards and train for the upcoming battle.

12. **Scene 12: The Assassination Plot.**
    *   **Setting:** Seoul.
    *   **Event:** Dorchester reveals her plan to assassinate Sáoirse, the leader of the UN. They learn that Sáoirse has her own plot to kidnap Dorchester's sister.

**Act III: The Resolution**

13. **Scene 13: The Battle for the Command Center.**
    *   **Setting:** Sáoirse's command center.
    *   **Event:** The team infiltrates the command center and battles Sáoirse and her forces.

14. **Scene 14: The Chase.**
    *   **Setting:** The ocean.
    *   **Event:** Sáoirse escapes in a submarine, and the team gives chase into the deep, dark part of the ocean.

15. **Scene 15: The War for the Deep.**
    *   **Setting:** The deep ocean.
    *   **Event:** Sáoirse summons millions of Carc-Zillas, leading to a massive battle between the Carc-Zillas, the merpeople, and the New Korean forces.

16. **Scene 16: The Unexpected Truce.**
    *   **Setting:** The deep ocean.
    *   **Event:** The battle ends in a stalemate as a truce is declared between the UN, the USA, and New Korea, due to the threat of China's involvement.

17. **Scene 17: A New Venture.**
    *   **Setting:** The surface.
    *   **Event:** The world returns to an uneasy peace. Lucky, Astra, and Gem receive a message from a contact named Luna about a secret black market asteroid mining venture.

18. **Scene 18: The Architect's Shadow.**
    *   **Setting:** Unknown.
    *   **Event:** A final scene reveals Lucky's evil twin brother, "the architect," monitoring Lucky's activities, hinting at a future conflict.
