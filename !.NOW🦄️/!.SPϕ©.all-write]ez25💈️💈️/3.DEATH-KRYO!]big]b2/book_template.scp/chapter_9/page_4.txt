"They're almost as good as the real thing. They learn, they adapt. It's impressive, and terrifying."

Her words were a venomous lie, a thinly veiled attempt to control the narrative, to twist the truth to her own nefarious ends. Dorchester's eyes narrowed, a dangerous glint in their depths, a cold, simmering fury. "Justice. She wouldn't know justice if it bit her in the face. It's time we paid her a visit. A personal visit.
