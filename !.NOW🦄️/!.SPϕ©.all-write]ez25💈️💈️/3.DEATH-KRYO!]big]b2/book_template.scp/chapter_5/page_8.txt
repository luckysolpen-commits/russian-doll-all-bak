**INT. SUB-KOREA NEGOTIATION CHAMBER - DAY**

The negotiation chamber was a study in minimalist efficiency, a stark contrast to the ornate, traditional council hall. Sleek, polished surfaces reflected the soft, ambient light, and holographic displays projected data and agreements with silent, precise movements. Lucky, Dorchester, Gem, Spike, and Astra sat across from the New-Korea General and a Mer-People Diplomat, the air thick with the unspoken weight of their decisions, of the future hanging in the balance. Raven, ever the quiet observer, sat in a corner, occasionally tapping notes onto a tablet, her bright blue hair a splash of vibrant color in the otherwise subdued room. She was absorbing everything, her young mind processing the complex dance of power and diplomacy.

"Our proposal is simple: an alliance," the New-Korea General stated, projecting a holographic document onto the table, its words glowing with a soft, blue light. "We provide the resources, the technology, and the strategic support. You provide the unique expertise to operate the current manipulator and, of course, your... unconventional methods. Your ability to adapt, to improvise, to think outside the box." His gaze lingered on Lucky, a silent acknowledgment of his unique genius.

Lucky leaned forward, his gaze direct, unwavering. "And our terms are equally simple. We deploy the device. We control its activation. And we retain a piece of the technology. A full schematic, and access to its core programming. For research purposes. For the advancement of science, for the betterment of all." It was a bold demand, a calculated risk, but he knew his worth, his irreplaceable value to their cause.

The General and the Diplomat exchanged a glance, a silent communication passing between them, a flicker of hesitation, then a nod of agreement. The Mer-People Diplomat, his voice calm and measured, finally spoke. "Such a powerful device... its secrets are closely guarded. It is a sacred trust."
