The team systematically dismantled Sáoirse's defenses. The command center, once a pristine hub of control, was now a wreck, consoles shattered, holographic displays flickering erratically, the air thick with the smell of ozone and burned metal. Only Sáoirse and a few of her elite guards remained, cornered and desperate, their faces grim, their eyes filled with a dawning fear.

Sáoirse's composure finally cracked, a snarl twisting her elegant features, her mask of indifference shattering. "You fools!" she shrieked, her voice raw with fury, with a desperate, animalistic rage. "You think this changes anything? I am the future! I am inevitable! You cannot stop me!" Her eyes, wild and desperate, scanned the ruined room, searching for an escape, for a way out of the trap she had so confidently walked into.

With a sudden, desperate movement, she pressed a hidden button on her console. A section of the floor slid open, revealing a sleek, personal submarine, its form designed for rapid escape, a final, desperate gambit. It was small, compact, and built for speed, a silent, deadly torpedo designed for one purpose: escape.

"You may have won this battle, but the war is far from over!" Sáoirse screamed, her voice echoing through the ruined chamber, a promise of future retribution. "You have no idea what I am capable of! You have no idea what I will unleash!" She leaped into the submarine, her movements surprisingly agile, a desperate, final act of defiance. The hatch hissed shut, sealing her inside, a metallic clang that echoed through the chamber. The submarine began to descend rapidly into a hidden shaft, disappearing into the depths of the complex, a dark, silent shadow.

"She's getting away!" Lucky yelled, running to the shaft, his hand outstretched, as if to snatch her from the air, to pull her back from the abyss. His heart pounded in his chest, a frantic drumbeat of frustration.

Dorchester's face was grim, a determined glint in her eyes, a cold, calculating resolve. "Not if I can help it. Ji-Hyun, Soo-Jin, with me! We'll take the pursuit sub! Gem, Spike, Astra, secure this place! Make sure no one else escapes!" The battle was far from over. The chase had just begun, a desperate race against time and a ruthless enemy.
