**SCENE 18**

**INT. UNKNOWN LOCATION - NIGHT**

The room is dark, minimalist, and cold. A single, large holographic screen dominates one wall, displaying a complex web of data streams, financial transactions, and news feeds. On one of the feeds, a small blip represents Lucky's new asteroid mining venture, heading towards the 'Void's Embrace' cluster.

THE ARCHITECT (O.S.), a voice chillingly similar to Lucky's, but colder, more calculating, speaks.

THE ARCHITECT
(a low, satisfied hum)
So, my dear brother. You escape one cage, only to fly into another. Always seeking, always striving. Always so predictable.

He steps into the faint light cast by the holographic screen. He is the spitting image of Lucky, but his eyes are devoid of warmth, filled instead with a predatory intelligence. He wears a sleek, dark suit, perfectly tailored.

He gestures, and the holographic screen shifts, showing a detailed analysis of the Soul Pen device, its energy signatures, its potential.

THE ARCHITECT (CONT'D)
Your little toy. So much potential, wasted on petty squabbles. But soon, it will be mine. And with it, the true power of the 'blood and souls' will be unleashed.

He brings up another feed, showing a shadowy figure, barely discernible, moving through a crowded market on a distant, alien world. It's a figure that resembles Sáoirse, but subtly different, perhaps a clone, or an echo.

THE ARCHITECT (CONT'D)
Sáoirse was a useful pawn. A necessary distraction. But her ambition was... limited. She thought small. I think... cosmically.

He clenches his fist, and the holographic screen ripples, then shows a vast, swirling nebula, within which countless stars are being born and dying. It's a vision of immense power, of creation and destruction.

THE ARCHITECT (CONT'D)
This universe is a canvas. And I am the artist. Soon, brother, you will understand. Soon, you will join me. Or you will be erased.

He turns away from the screen, his face once again shrouded in shadow. His laughter, cold and devoid of mirth, echoes in the silent room.

**FADE TO BLACK.**

**THE END.**
