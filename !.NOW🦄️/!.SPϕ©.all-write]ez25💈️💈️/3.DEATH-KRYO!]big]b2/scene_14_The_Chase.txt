**SCENE 14**

**INT. UN COMMAND CENTER - CONTINUOUS**

Chaos reigns. GEM, SPIKE, and ASTRA move through the wreckage of Sáoirse's command center, securing the area. Disoriented Super-Soldiers are being rounded up by New-Korea forces who have now entered the complex.

GEM
(into her comms)
Perimeter secured. No other escape routes detected. Sáoirse is definitely in that sub.

SPIKE
(kicking a downed Super-Soldier's helmet)
Good riddance. But I have a bad feeling about this. Sáoirse doesn't run without a plan.

ASTRA
(at a console, rapidly typing)
She's heading for the deep ocean. Beyond the continental shelf. Where the pressure is immense, and light doesn't penetrate.

**INT. PURSUIT SUBMARINE - CONTINUOUS**

LUCKY, DORCHESTER, JI-HYUN, and SOO-JIN are in a sleek, high-speed pursuit submarine, provided by the New-Korea forces. The interior is minimalist, dominated by a large panoramic viewscreen showing the dark blue depths.

DORCHESTER
(at the helm, her hands flying over the controls)
She's fast. Too fast for a standard escape pod. This sub must be heavily modified.

JI-HYUN
(at a tactical console)
Her energy signature is fluctuating. She's pushing her engines to their limit.

LUCKY
(eyes glued to the viewscreen)
She's not just running. She's leading us somewhere. She always has a purpose.

SOO-JIN
(at a comms station, trying to establish a link)
I'm trying to hack into her sub's comms, but her encryption is military-grade. It's like trying to crack a diamond with a toothpick.

As they descend deeper, the light outside fades, replaced by the eerie glow of the submarine's headlights. Strange, bioluminescent creatures drift past the viewscreen.

LUCKY
(a thought striking him)
Carc-Zillas. She commanded them before. What if she's going to a place where she can control them on a massive scale?

DORCHESTER
(grimly)
The deep black. That's where the largest, most ancient Carcazod strains are rumored to reside. Untouched by human intervention. Until now.

JI-HYUN
(pointing to a blip on her screen)
Energy spike! Massive. Multiple signatures. Directly ahead!

The viewscreen flickers, then resolves. Hundreds, then thousands, of glowing red eyes appear in the darkness. They belong to CARC-ZILLAS, their monstrous forms emerging from the abyssal plains. They are larger, more grotesque than the one they encountered in Sub-Korea.

LUCKY
(a chill running down his spine)
She's calling them. She's going to unleash them.

Sáoirse's submarine, a tiny speck amidst the growing horde, weaves through the Carc-Zillas, seemingly unmolested. It's clear they are under her command.

SOO-JIN
(her voice tight)
There are millions of them. We can't fight them all.

DORCHESTER
We don't have to. We just have to get to Sáoirse. She's the key.

The pursuit sub is suddenly buffeted by a massive Carc-Zilla, its grotesque face pressing against the viewscreen. The sub's hull groans under the pressure.

LUCKY
(activating his Soul Pen, its energy flaring)
Ji-Hyun, Soo-Jin, cover us! Dorchester, get us through this!

Ji-Hyun unleashes a torrent of superheated water, forcing the Carc-Zilla back. Soo-Jin throws a Tear-It card, creating a blinding flash that disorients another.

Dorchester expertly maneuvers the sub, dodging the monstrous creatures, weaving through the chaotic swarm. The chase is on, a desperate race against time and an army of nightmares.

**FADE OUT.**
