**Character Sheet: DEATH-KRYO!**

**1. Lucky**
*   **Role:** Protagonist, tech genius, reluctant hero.
*   **Appearance:** Handsome, roguishly charming, often seen in sharp, tailored suits.
*   **Personality:** Brilliant, resourceful, quick-witted, but can be impulsive and prone to getting into trouble. Deeply loyal to his friends. Initially driven by a desire for justice and clearing his name, later by a sense of responsibility for the Soul Pen's power.
*   **Abilities/Skills:** Master of sonic technology (Soul Pen device), exceptional hacker, quick thinker, surprisingly capable in combat when pushed.
*   **Key Relationships:** Friends with Spy-K (Gem, Astra, Spike), complex alliance with Dorchester, target of the UN, unknowingly manipulated by his twin brother, The Architect.
*   **Arc:** From a celebrated genius framed for murder to a leader fighting for the fate of the world, eventually venturing into space for new challenges and resources.

**2. Spy-K (Female Operative Group)**
*   **Role:** Lucky's loyal friends, highly skilled operatives, his primary support system.
*   **Shared Traits:** Fiercely loyal, highly trained, adaptable, and possess unique individual skills.

    *   **Gem**
        *   **Appearance:** Striking, emotionally expressive.
        *   **Personality:** Intuitive, empathetic, strong emotional intelligence. Takes to Tear-It cards naturally due to her emotional intensity.
        *   **Abilities/Skills:** Expert in close-quarters combat, highly skilled with Tear-It cards (especially emotional manipulation and elemental channeling), good at reconnaissance.

    *   **Astra**
        *   **Appearance:** Bio-enhanced, sleek, perhaps with subtle cybernetic implants.
        *   **Personality:** Analytical, focused, pragmatic. Less outwardly emotional than Gem, but deeply committed.
        *   **Abilities/Skills:** Bio-enhancements (enhanced senses, strength, agility), master hacker, excellent at surveillance and data analysis. Finds synergy between her tech and Tear-It magic.

    *   **Spike**
        *   **Appearance:** Tough, no-nonsense, physically imposing.
        *   **Personality:** Direct, protective, pragmatic, and a bit cynical. Always ready for a fight.
        *   **Abilities/Skills:** Master of hand-to-hand combat and various weaponry, highly effective in direct confrontation. Adapts magic to enhance physical prowess.

**3. Dorchester**
*   **Role:** Mysterious pirate/assassin, anti-hero, former UN genetic engineer.
*   **Appearance:** Late 30s, sharp, intelligent eyes, confident smirk, often wears practical but stylish attire.
*   **Personality:** Cunning, ruthless, pragmatic, but with a hidden moral compass. Deeply protective of her sister, Raven. Holds a grudge against the UN for their atrocities.
*   **Abilities/Skills:** Master strategist, expert infiltrator, skilled combatant (energy-bladed dagger), deep knowledge of UN operations and genetic engineering. Develops a unique combat magic style using the Soul Pen.
*   **Key Relationships:** Sister to Raven, complex alliance with Lucky, former UN operative, enemy of Sáoirse.
*   **Arc:** From a self-serving mercenary to a key ally in the fight against the UN, driven by a desire for justice and protecting her sister.

**4. Raven**
*   **Role:** Dorchester's younger sister, apprentice in deception.
*   **Appearance:** 16 years old, petite, bright blue hair, eager grin.
*   **Personality:** Mischievous, curious, quick learner, enthusiastic. Naive but observant.
*   **Abilities/Skills:** Developing skills in deception, infiltration (drone operation), and observation. Has unique, undefined abilities that interest Sáoirse.
*   **Key Relationships:** Sister to Dorchester, protected by the team.

**5. The Architect**
*   **Role:** Antagonist, Lucky's evil twin brother, mastermind.
*   **Appearance:** Identical to Lucky, but with cold, predatory eyes. Wears sleek, dark suits.
*   **Personality:** Calculating, manipulative, arrogant, nihilistic. Believes in cosmic control and sees the universe as his canvas. Views Lucky as a pawn.
*   **Abilities/Skills:** Master manipulator, vast resources, advanced technological knowledge (possibly beyond Lucky's), capable of operating from the shadows.
*   **Key Relationships:** Lucky's twin brother, unseen puppet master behind various conflicts.

**6. Queen Sophia**
*   **Role:** Mer-people royalty, victim.
*   **Appearance:** Regal, graceful mer-person.
*   **Personality:** Grieving, dignified.
*   **Key Relationships:** Mother of the murdered daughter.

**7. Seasha**
*   **Role:** UN agent, femme fatale.
*   **Appearance:** Icy demeanor, opportunistic.
*   **Personality:** Ruthless, self-serving.

**8. Sáoirse**
*   **Role:** Primary Antagonist, ruthless leader of the UN.
*   **Appearance:** Elegant, composed, but with a cold, calculating demeanor.
*   **Personality:** Ambitious, tyrannical, believes in absolute control, sees herself as inevitable. Unscrupulous in her methods.
*   **Abilities/Skills:** Master manipulator, commands Carc-Zillas, vast resources of the UN.
*   **Key Relationships:** Leader of the UN, enemy of Lucky and his allies, target of Dorchester's assassination plot.

**9. New-Korea and Sub-Korea Officials**
*   **Role:** Leaders of the Korean factions, allies to Lucky.
*   **Appearance:** Stern, unyielding, dignified.
*   **Personality:** Pragmatic, protective of their people and the ocean, willing to form alliances for survival.
*   **Key Relationships:** Allies with the clone-merpeople, oppose the UN.

**10. Clone-Merpeople**
*   **Role:** Peaceful faction, victims of UN aggression, allies to New-Korea.
*   **Appearance:** Shimmering scales, flowing hair, elegant, bioluminescent armor.
*   **Personality:** Peaceful, advanced civilization, but capable of fighting for their survival.

**11. New Korean Resistance Women (Lucky's New Friends)**
*   **Role:** Allies, provide sanctuary and training.
*   **Shared Traits:** Loyal, supportive, skilled in various areas, embrace the blend of technology and magic.

    *   **Ji-Hyun**
        *   **Appearance:** Long black hair, calm demeanor.
        *   **Personality:** Wise, strategic, a master of elemental manipulation.
        *   **Abilities/Skills:** Powerful elemental magic (wind, fire, water), strategic thinker.

    *   **Soo-Jin**
        *   **Appearance:** Petite, mischievous grin.
        *   **Personality:** Quick-witted, playful, but highly intelligent.
        *   **Abilities/Skills:** Master hacker, skilled with Tear-It cards (disorientation, system disruption), musician.

    *   **Hye-Mi**
        *   **Appearance:** Gentle soul.
        *   **Personality:** Nurturing, empathetic, calming presence.
        *   **Abilities/Skills:** Healing touch, uses Tear-It cards for defensive shields and stunning electrical shocks.

    *   **Mi-Sun**
        *   **Appearance:** Fiery redhead.
        *   **Personality:** Energetic, passionate, sharp-tongued.
        *   **Abilities/Skills:** Excellent cook, formidable hand-to-hand combatant, uses Tear-It cards to enhance physical strength.

**12. Luna**
*   **Role:** Black market contact, information broker.
*   **Appearance:** Striking, short silver hair, piercing blue eyes.
*   **Personality:** Confident, resourceful, opportunistic, has a knack for finding hidden opportunities.
*   **Abilities/Skills:** Extensive network of contacts, master of information and logistics, privy to secret ventures.
