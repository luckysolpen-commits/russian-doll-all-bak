**SCENE 15**

**EXT. THE DEEP OCEAN - CONTINUOUS**

The pursuit submarine bursts through a swirling mass of Carc-Zillas, emerging into a vast, abyssal plain. The sight that greets them is apocalyptic.

Millions of CARC-ZILLAS, of all shapes and sizes, swarm across the ocean floor and through the water column. Their glowing red eyes create an eerie, malevolent constellation in the darkness. They are a living, breathing tide of destruction.

Against this overwhelming force, the combined might of the MERPEOPLE and NEW-KOREA forces are fighting a desperate, losing battle. The merpeople, with their elegant, bioluminescent armor and trident-like weapons, move with grace and agility, but they are being swamped. New-Korea's advanced sub-fighters, sleek and fast, weave through the chaos, firing energy torpedoes, but their numbers are dwindling.

On a massive, grotesque Carc-Zilla, larger than any other, SAOIRSE stands, a dark, triumphant figure. Her hands are raised, and a pulsating, dark energy emanates from her, controlling the horde. She is a conductor of chaos.

LUCKY
(his voice filled with dread)
She's using them as a living weapon. This isn't just a battle; it's a genocide.

DORCHESTER
(gritting her teeth)
She's trying to wipe out any opposition to her global dominion. The merpeople and New-Korea are the last bastions of resistance in the deep.

JI-HYUN
(at her console, her face grim)
Casualties are mounting. The UN is politically stymying any surface intervention. They're letting this happen.

SOO-JIN
(frantically trying to hack into the Carc-Zilla network)
Their neural links are too complex! Sáoirse has integrated them into a hive mind! I can't break through!

The pursuit sub is rocked by a near miss from a massive Carc-Zilla. Alarms blare.

LUCKY
(activating his Soul Pen, its energy flaring)
We have to get to Sáoirse! If we can cut off the head, the body will fall!

They plunge into the thick of the battle. Lucky fires concentrated blasts from his Soul Pen, augmented by Tear-It cards, creating temporary clearings in the Carc-Zilla swarm. Ji-Hyun unleashes powerful elemental attacks – torrents of water that sweep away dozens of creatures, bursts of superheated steam that melt their grotesque flesh.

Dorchester, with her intimate knowledge of Sáoirse's tactics, guides the sub, dodging attacks, finding the narrowest gaps in the enemy lines. She fires torpedoes with deadly accuracy, targeting the larger Carc-Zillas that act as command nodes.

SOO-JIN
(a breakthrough)
I've found a weakness! A resonant frequency in their neural network! It's unstable!

LUCKY
(a plan forming)
Can you amplify it? Channel it through the sub's sonar?

SOO-JIN
It'll be risky! It could fry our systems!

DORCHESTER
Do it! We don't have a choice!

Soo-Jin works furiously, rerouting power, pushing the sub's systems to their breaking point. A high-pitched, agonizing whine fills the sub, then blasts outwards.

The Carc-Zillas react violently. Their movements become erratic, uncoordinated. Some crash into each other. Others writhe in apparent agony. Sáoirse, on her command Carc-Zilla, clutches her head, her dark energy flickering.

SÁOIRSE
(a scream of rage, distorted)
No! You will not defy me!

She redoubles her efforts, forcing the Carc-Zillas back into formation, but the disruption has bought the merpeople and New-Korea forces precious time. They rally, launching a counter-attack.

LUCKY
(to Dorchester)
Now! Full speed! Straight for Sáoirse!

The pursuit sub, battered but determined, surges forward, a tiny spearhead aimed at the heart of the monstrous horde.

**FADE OUT.**
