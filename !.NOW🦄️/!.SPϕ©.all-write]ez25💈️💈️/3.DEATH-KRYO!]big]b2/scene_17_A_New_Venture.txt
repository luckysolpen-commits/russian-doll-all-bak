**SCENE 17**

**INT. KOREAN-STYLE VILLA - DAY (SEVERAL WEEKS LATER)**

The villa is still a sanctuary, but the mood is lighter, more forward-looking. The initial disappointment of the anticlimactic truce has faded, replaced by a new sense of purpose. LUCKY, GEM, SPIKE, ASTRA, DORCHESTER, and RAVEN are gathered around a holographic projection of a distant asteroid field.

LUNA (O.S.)
(a crisp, confident voice from the projection)
...and that's the long and short of it. The 'Void's Embrace' asteroid cluster. Untapped resources, minimal oversight, and a very, very lucrative black market for rare earth elements and exotic minerals.

LUNA's avatar, a striking woman with short, silver hair and piercing blue eyes, flickers into full view. She's a known figure in the intergalactic black market, a master of information and logistics.

LUCKY
(leaning closer to the projection)
Untapped? That's a bold claim, Luna. Every rock in this system has been picked clean.

LUNA
(a knowing smile)
Not every rock, Lucky. Not when you know where to look. My sources on the Moon, deep within the old lunar mining colonies, have been tracking anomalies. Gravitational distortions, unusual energy signatures. They point to something... hidden.

GEM
(eyes wide)
Hidden? Like, a secret stash of unobtanium?

LUNA
(chuckles)
Something like that. But far more valuable. These aren't just minerals, Gem. These are elements with unique properties. Properties that could revolutionize energy, propulsion, even bio-engineering.

ASTRA
(her bio-enhancements humming faintly)
Revolutionize... or weaponize?

LUNA
(shrugs)
That, my dear Astra, is entirely up to the buyer. My job is to get it out. Your job, if you choose to accept it, is to get it in the first place.

SPIKE
(cracking her knuckles)
Sounds like a challenge. What's the catch?

LUNA
(her smile fading slightly)
The usual. Corporate rivals, space pirates, and the occasional rogue AI. And the UN, of course. They've been trying to regulate the asteroid belts for years. They won't be happy about a new player.

DORCHESTER
(a glint in her eye)
Sounds like old times. But with better scenery.

RAVEN
(bouncing on the balls of her feet)
Can I come? I can help find the hidden stuff! I'm really good at that!

Luna looks at Raven, a flicker of surprise in her eyes, then a knowing smile.

LUNA
(to Dorchester)
She's got spirit. And a good eye, I hear. Very well. The more, the merrier. Or, in this case, the richer.

Lucky looks at his team. The weariness from the UN conflict is gone, replaced by a familiar spark of adventure. This is what they do. They find the impossible, they challenge the powerful, and they make their own rules.

LUCKY
(a determined grin)
Alright, Luna. You've got our attention. What's the first step?

LUNA
(her avatar expanding, showing a detailed star map)
First, we acquire a suitable vessel. Something fast, something discreet. And then, we head for the stars. The Void's Embrace awaits.

The holographic map zooms out, showing their small group, a tiny speck against the vastness of space, heading towards a distant, glittering asteroid field.

**FADE OUT.**
