**SCENE 6**

**INT. SUB-KOREA RESEARCH INSTITUTE - NIGHT**

The team infiltrates the research institute under the cover of darkness. The corridors are eerily silent, the only sound the hum of machinery.

They move with stealth and precision, disabling security cameras and avoiding patrols. Lucky uses his technical skills to bypass electronic locks, while Spy-K handles the physical security.

They reach the main laboratory, the same one they saw through the drone's camera. It's even more impressive up close. Thousands of sub-robots are in various stages of assembly.

DORCHESTER
(whispering)
This is bigger than I thought.

Suddenly, they hear a loud, metallic thud from a large, sealed chamber at the far end of the lab. The chamber door is reinforced, with warning signs plastered all over it.

LUCKY
Whatever's in there, they don't want it getting out.

He starts working on the electronic lock, his fingers flying across the keypad.

GEM
Are you sure this is a good idea?

LUCKY
We need to know what we're up against.

The lock clicks open. The heavy door swings open with a groan, revealing a dark, cavernous space.

They step inside, their flashlights cutting through the darkness. And then they see it.

CARC-ZILLA. A monstrous creature, a grotesque fusion of metal and flesh. It's a bio-mechanical weapon, a perversion of nature. It's dormant, but even in its sleep, it's terrifying.

ASTRA
(in awe and horror)
What is it?

LUCKY
It's a Carcazod. They weaponized it.

Suddenly, the creature's eyes snap open, glowing with a malevolent red light. An alarm blares throughout the facility.

DORCHESTER
We've overstayed our welcome!

Carc-Zilla lets out a deafening roar and lunges at them. They scatter, dodging its massive claws. The creature is incredibly fast and powerful.

SPIKE
We can't fight that thing!

DORCHESTER
We don't have to! This way!

She leads them towards a maintenance tunnel, but their path is blocked by a squad of Sub-Korea soldiers.

The soldiers open fire. The team is pinned down, caught between Carc-Zilla and the soldiers.

RAVEN, who had been staying back, throws a smoke bomb, creating a diversion. In the confusion, Dorchester pulls open a floor grate, revealing a dark, watery tunnel.

DORCHESTER
Go! Now!

They jump into the tunnel, one by one, as Carc-Zilla's massive form closes in on them. Lucky is the last to go. He takes one last look at the monster, a look of grim determination on his face.

**INT. CAVE SYSTEM - CONTINUOUS**

They are swept away by a strong current, tumbling through a network of dark, underwater caves. The sound of the battle fades behind them.

They finally come to a rest in a large, quiet cavern. They are battered and bruised, but alive.

GEM
Did we lose them?

DORCHESTER
For now.

LUCKY
(to Dorchester)
You knew about this. You knew about Carc-Zilla.

DORCHESTER
I had my suspicions. Now we know for sure. The UN isn't just trying to control the underwater world. They're trying to create a new plague.

**FADE OUT.**
