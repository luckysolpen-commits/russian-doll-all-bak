**SCENE 2**

**INT. THE SOUL PEN - CONTINUOUS**

The initial shock gives way to chaos. The guests, a mix of fear and anger in their eyes, are closing in on LUCKY. Queen Sophia is wailing, cradling her daughter's body.

GEM
(to Lucky, low and urgent)
We need to go. Now.

Lucky is still reeling, his mind struggling to process what just happened. Spike and Astra create a perimeter around him, pushing back the encroaching crowd.

SPIKE
Move! Get back!

ASTRA
(into her comms)
Emergency evac. Rendezvous point Delta.

Lucky snaps out of his stupor. He knows they're right. Staying here means a UN holding cell, or worse.

LUCKY
This way. Service tunnels.

He leads Spy-K towards a discreet panel in the wall, hidden behind a large, exotic-looking plant. He presses a hidden switch, and the panel slides open, revealing a dark, narrow passageway.

They slip inside just as the club's security forces breach the main entrance.

**INT. SERVICE TUNNELS - CONTINUOUS**

The four of them race through the cramped, dimly lit service tunnels. The sound of alarms blares behind them.

GEM
They'll have this whole sector locked down in minutes.

LUCKY
I know a way out. An old construction access point. They won't have it on their schematics.

They navigate the labyrinthine tunnels, Lucky leading the way. Suddenly, they hear the distinct sound of armored boots echoing from ahead.

SPIKE
Contact!

Two UN agents round the corner, their weapons raised. Without hesitation, Spike and Gem engage them. The fight is brutal and efficient. Spike, a master of close-quarters combat, disarms one agent while Gem uses a taser to incapacitate the other.

They don't stop to celebrate. They keep moving.

ASTRA
(looking at a datapad)
They're rerouting power, trying to seal the exits. We're running out of time.

LUCKY
Almost there.

He stops at a junction, looking at the seemingly identical tunnels. He closes his eyes for a moment, concentrating. He then points to a tunnel on the right.

LUCKY (CONT'D)
This one.

They follow him, and soon the tunnel ends in a solid wall.

GEM
Dead end?

LUCKY
(smiling faintly)
Not quite.

He runs his hand along the wall, feeling for something. He finds a small indentation and presses it. A section of the wall slides open, revealing the dark, open ocean.

LUCKY (CONT'D)
After you.

One by one, they swim out into the vast, silent darkness of the ocean, leaving the chaos of the Soul Pen behind.

**FADE OUT.**
