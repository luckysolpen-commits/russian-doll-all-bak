INT. XERXES' DOMAIN - TEMPORARY XVS BASE - DAY

The Anglers have established a makeshift base in a relatively stable section of the Xerxes’ Domain. The air still hums with discordant energy, and the sounds of chaotic Xerxes echo in the distance. Dr. Luna is hunched over a portable console, the data chip from Kato inserted, her horn glowing intensely as she processes information.

Around her, the rest of the team attempts to manage the chaos. Hana and Jack are setting up a temporary energy barrier to keep agitated Xerxes at bay. Zara tries to soothe a particularly distressed Xerxes with calming frequencies, but it only twitches erratically.

LUNA
(Muttering to herself, equations and waveforms flashing across her console)
…a complex harmonic. Not a simple frequency, but a layered one. Designed to bypass their neural filters… to induce cognitive dissonance.

JACK
(Wrestling with a power conduit)
Anything, Doc? This barrier won’t hold forever. These guys are getting… enthusiastic.

HANA
(Her shield flaring as a Xerxes slams into it)
They’re not thinking, Jack. They’re just reacting. Like a broken circuit.

Kael observes Luna, his strategic mind already anticipating her findings.

KAEL
If it’s a frequency, can we broadcast a counter-frequency? Or a nullifier?

LUNA
(Eyes widening, a breakthrough)
A nullifier… yes! But it needs to be precise. A specific inverse wave. And it needs to be broadcast across their entire network. Their communication towers… they’re still active, but corrupted.

JAX
(Joining them, his face grim)
Can we do it? Can we purify their network?

LUNA
(Tapping furiously at her console)
I can synthesize the counter-frequency. But we need a stable, high-power broadcast point. And we need to override their existing, corrupted signals.

Jack snaps his fingers.

JACK
Their central comms spire! It’s the tallest structure on the planet. If we can get a clean signal into its primary relay, I can re-route the power. But it’s heavily guarded… and the Xerxes around it are particularly violent.

Kael steps forward.

KAEL
Then we create a diversion. Hana, you and Jack secure the spire. Zara, can you amplify Luna’s counter-frequency once it’s ready? Use your horn as a localized beacon.

ZARA
(Nodding, determined)
I can try. It’ll be a massive strain, but I can do it.

Jax looks at Kato, who has been silently observing the chaotic Xerxes, his horn almost invisible.

JAX
Kato. Clear a path. And ensure no one interferes with Luna’s broadcast.

Kato gives a subtle nod and vanishes into the swirling chaos.

Minutes later, a series of controlled explosions rock the distant comms spire – Jack and Hana’s diversion. The chaotic sounds of the Xerxes intensify, drawing their attention.

INT. XERXES' DOMAIN - COMMS SPIRE - DAY

Hana and Jack fight their way through a horde of maddened Xerxes. Hana’s spright-saber cuts through the air, deflecting attacks, while Jack uses his tools to disable automated defenses.

JACK
(Grinning, as he bypasses a security panel)
Almost there! Luna, you ready?

INT. XERXES' DOMAIN - TEMPORARY XVS BASE - DAY

Luna connects her console to a portable amplifier. Zara stands beside her, her horn glowing with immense power, ready to act as a conduit.

LUNA
(Sweat beading on her brow)
Ready! Zara, prepare for the surge!

Zara closes her eyes, focusing, her horn vibrating with raw energy.

INT. XERXES' DOMAIN - COMMS SPIRE - DAY

Jack connects Luna’s amplifier to the spire’s main relay. A high-pitched whine fills the air.

JACK
(Shouting over the noise)
It’s broadcasting! Now, let’s see if it works!

INT. XERXES' DOMAIN - VARIOUS LOCATIONS - DAY

Across the planet, the discordant hum begins to falter. The erratic movements of the Xerxes slow. Their wide, unfocused eyes begin to clear. The babbling ceases. The violent lashing out stops.

One Xerxes, who was repeatedly slamming its head against a wall, slowly lowers its head, looking confused. Another, who was babbling nonsense, blinks, then looks at its hands, as if seeing them for the first time.

INT. XERXES' DOMAIN - TEMPORARY XVS BASE - DAY

Luna collapses, exhausted, but a triumphant smile spreads across her face. Zara’s horn dims, but a soft, steady light now emanates from it.

LUNA
(Weakly)
It worked. The frequency… it’s neutralized.

Jax looks out at the now-calm Xerxes, their movements regaining their former grace. The chaotic energy in the air has dissipated, replaced by a quiet hum of restored order.

JAX
(A sigh of relief)
Balance restored. For now.

FADE OUT.
