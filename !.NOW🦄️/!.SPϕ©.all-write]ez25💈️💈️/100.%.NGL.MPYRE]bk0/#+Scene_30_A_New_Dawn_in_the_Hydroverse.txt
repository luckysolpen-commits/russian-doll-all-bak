INT. HYDROVERSE - VARIOUS LOCATIONS - DAY

The Hydroverse is a tapestry of light and shadow, order and chaos. The Angler Empire still exists, its grand structures gleaming, its patrols maintaining a semblance of order. But it is no longer the monolithic, unquestioned power it once was. Its light, though still strong, is now one among many.

INT. IMPERIAL OBSERVATORY - DAY

A new, younger Vorith (perhaps a descendant, or a new officer who has studied the original Vorith’s failures) stands before the holographic displays. The data streams still show the Unlit Tribe’s energy signature, vibrant and growing. But this Vorith’s horn glows with a more nuanced light, a hint of understanding in his eyes.

NEW VORITH
(To a junior officer)
The Unlit Tribe. They are not a disease to be eradicated. They are a force of nature. A necessary counterpoint.

The display shows the Unlit Tribe’s influence spreading, not through conquest, but through connection. Worlds once under Imperial control now engage in open dialogue with the Unlit, their own horns glowing with a newfound individuality.

INT. UNLIT TRIBE CAMP - DAY

The Unlit Tribe camp is a beacon of vibrant, diverse light. Naeth, now an elder, guides a new generation of Anglers, their horns glowing with a spectrum of colors, reflecting their unique talents and perspectives.

NAETH
(To a group of young Anglers)
The Empire will always seek to define. To control. But the Hydroverse is infinite. And so is our potential.

She gestures to the rift, which pulses with a steady, powerful energy.

NAETH (CONT'D)
The unlit void is not a place of emptiness. It is a place of possibility. Of constant evolution.

EXT. HYDROVERSE - SPACE - DAY

A small, sleek XVS shuttle, bearing the old Angler insignia, drifts through space. Inside, an aged JAX, his horn now a deep, resonant glow, sits beside an equally aged KAEL, his horn a steady, calculating pulse. They are no longer bound by Imperial protocol, but by a shared understanding.

JAX
(A faint smile)
Who would have thought? The Hydroverse… changed.

KAEL
(Nodding)
Evolution, Commander. It is inevitable. Even for empires.

LUNA, her scientific horn glowing with a serene wisdom, observes a distant world.

LUNA
The balance is precarious. But it is a living balance. Not a stagnant one.

JACK, his horn a steady, reliable light, tinkers with a new, hybrid device – a blend of Imperial and Unlit technology.

JACK
Still plenty of things to fix. Plenty of things to build.

HANA, her security horn a vigilant, protective glow, watches over them.

HANA
And plenty of things to protect.

ZARA, her horn a vibrant, ever-changing light, communicates with a distant, newly awakened world.

ZARA
The conversations are just beginning. The Hydroverse is finally learning to speak its own truth.

KATO, his horn almost invisible, yet radiating a profound sense of peace, observes the vastness of space.

KATO
The unlit void… it is vast. And it is beautiful.

The camera pulls back, showing the Hydroverse, a swirling canvas of light and shadow, order and chaos. The conflict between the Empire and the Unlit Tribe continues, not as a war of annihilation, but as a dynamic interplay of opposing forces, each pushing the other towards a new, evolving future. The original Anglers, or their descendants, are now part of this new paradigm, their individual lights contributing to the ever-changing, ever-evolving symphony of the Hydroverse.

FADE TO BLACK.
