INT. UNLIT TRIBE CAMP - COUNCIL CHAMBER - DAY

Jureth, though healed, is visibly weaker. His horn glows with a deep, steady light, but his movements are slow, deliberate. He sits at the head of a council table, surrounded by the core members of the Unlit Tribe: Gadreth, Lirith, Naeth, Zorath, and Mirah. The chamber is filled with other tribe members, a mix of original Anglers and new recruits, their faces expectant.

JURETH
(His voice soft, but clear, resonating with authority)
The unlit path is long. And my journey… it nears its end. But the tribe must endure. Its wisdom must be carried forward.

A murmur goes through the crowd. Grief, but also understanding.

JURETH (CONT'D)
I have chosen not one, but many. A council of diverse voices, each embodying a different aspect of the unlit philosophy. For true strength lies not in a single light, but in the constellation of many.

He gestures to the first.

JURETH (CONT'D)
First, NAETH. My daughter, by spirit and by choice. Her voice, a song of storm and freedom, will guide our communication. Her ability to weave truth from chaos will be our shield against deception.

Naeth, now a confident young woman, steps forward, her horn glowing with a vibrant, multi-hued light.

NAETH
I will honor the unlit path. And ensure our song is heard throughout the Hydroverse.

JURETH
Next, ESHAR. A scout who joined us from the river’s banks. His keen eye for detail, his unwavering loyalty, and his ability to navigate the unseen currents of the Hydroverse will be our eyes and ears.

ESHAR (20s), a lean, agile figure, steps forward, his horn a steady, observant glow.

ESHAR
I will watch. And I will report. The Empire will not surprise us.

JURETH
Then, KAI. A young scientist, drawn to our philosophy from a distant, Imperial-controlled world. Her insatiable curiosity, her ability to unravel the mysteries of the quantum, will be our scientific compass.

KAI (20s), a brilliant, inquisitive Angler, steps forward, her horn pulsing with analytical energy.

KAI
The unlit void holds infinite secrets. I will seek them. And share them.

JURETH
And finally, LEO. A former Imperial mechanic, disillusioned by the Empire’s rigid control. His practical ingenuity, his ability to transform and adapt, will be our hands.

LEO (30s), a sturdy, grounded Angler, steps forward, his horn a steady, practical glow.

LEO
I will build. And I will mend. For the tribe.

Jureth looks at his chosen heirs, then at the assembled tribe.

JURETH
These are but a few. The unlit path is open to all who seek truth, who embrace freedom, who dare to question. Let each generation find its own way, guided by the principles of art, leadership, and the unlit way. Evolution is our destiny, and it shall carry us beyond the Empire’s reach.

Zorath and Mirah stand proudly, their horns glowing with a quiet satisfaction. Gadreth and Lirith nod in approval. The tribe murmurs, a mix of awe and acceptance.

Jureth closes his eyes, a faint smile on his face. His horn dims slightly, but the light within the chamber, reflected in the horns of his heirs and his tribe, burns brighter than ever. His legacy is secure.

FADE OUT.
