INT. XVS BRIEFING ROOM - DAY

The seven Anglers stand at attention in a sleek, minimalist briefing room. Holographic displays flicker with star charts and planetary data. At the head of a long, polished table stands VORITH PRIME (60s), a high-ranking official with an imposing presence and a horn that glows with a cold, authoritative light. He is an ancestor of the later Vorith.

VORITH PRIME
(Voice resonating with authority)
Anglers. You are the chosen few. The Multiversal Police, or XVS, as we are known. Our mandate is simple: maintain order across all realities. The Hydroverse, in its infinite complexity, is prone to… deviations.

He gestures to a holographic projection of a vibrant, blue-green planet, swirling with aquatic life.

VORITH PRIME (CONT'D)
Our first assignment takes you to Neutrino Prime. A distant, aquatic world. Recent energy signatures indicate… anomalies. Disturbances in the natural flow. We suspect a localized corruption.

Jax steps forward slightly.

JAX
Corruption, sir? Of what nature?

VORITH PRIME
(Eyes narrowing)
That is for you to discover, Commander. What we do know is that the Neutrinos, the indigenous population, are exhibiting unusual behavior. Their bioluminescence, normally a beacon of harmony, is erratic. Fading in places, flaring violently in others.

Kael studies the projection.

KAEL
Any known threats? Localized resistance?

VORITH PRIME
Our intelligence is limited. Which is precisely why we dispatch you. You are not merely enforcers, Anglers. You are diagnosticians. Healers of cosmic ailments. Your unique abilities, your angler features, are designed to sense and rectify these imbalances.

He looks at each of them, his gaze lingering on Zara.

VORITH PRIME (CONT'D)
Ensign Zara, your communication skills will be paramount. Dr. Luna, your scientific acumen will be tested. Chief Hana, ensure their safety. Private Jack, prepare for any mechanical contingencies. Agent Kato, observe everything. And Commander Jax, lead them with the unwavering hand of order.

The holographic planet pulses, a subtle warning.

VORITH PRIME (CONT'D)
Remember this above all: the fabric of reality is delicate. One tear, left unaddressed, can unravel entire sectors. Your mission is to mend. To restore. To ensure the Hydroverse remains… orderly.

The Anglers exchange glances, the weight of the mission settling upon them.

FADE OUT.
