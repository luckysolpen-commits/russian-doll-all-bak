INT. GRAND COUNCIL CHAMBER - DAY

A vast, circular chamber, bathed in the soft, pulsating light of a thousand holographic displays. Figures in ornate, flowing robes move with quiet purpose. At the center, a raised dais.

A stern, ancient voice, amplified, echoes through the chamber.

VOICE (O.S.)
The Hydroverse trembles. Order falters.
We require those attuned to the deepest currents.

A spotlight illuminates the first figure.

COMMANDER JAX (50s)
A seasoned leader, his face etched with experience. A subtle, steady glow emanates from the tip of his elongated, angler-like horn, which curves gracefully from his forehead.

VOICE (O.S.)
Commander Jax. Your unwavering resolve.

Jax nods, his gaze firm. The light shifts.

LIEUTENANT KAEL (30s)
Sharp, analytical eyes. His horn is shorter, more angular, its tip emitting a rapid, calculating flicker.

VOICE (O.S.)
Lieutenant Kael. Your strategic brilliance.

Kael gives a curt, almost imperceptible bow.

ENSIGN ZARA (20s)
Bright, eager. Her horn is slender, almost delicate, with a vibrant, fluctuating glow that seems to respond to her thoughts.

VOICE (O.S.)
Ensign Zara. Your ambitious communication.

Zara stands tall, a hint of excitement in her posture.

PRIVATE JACK (20s)
Muscular, practical. His horn is thick, robust, with a strong, consistent pulse of light, like a well-oiled machine.

VOICE (O.S.)
Private Jack. Your mechanical ingenuity.

Jack shifts, a restless energy about him.

DR. LUNA (40s)
Calm, intellectual. Her horn is smooth, almost translucent, with a soft, ethereal glow that seems to absorb and refract light.

VOICE (O.S.)
Dr. Luna. Your brilliant scientific mind.

Luna’s expression is one of quiet contemplation.

CHIEF HANA (40s)
Vigilant, protective. Her horn is broad, almost shield-like, its light a steady, unwavering beam.

VOICE (O.S.)
Chief Hana. Your unyielding security.

Hana’s stance is defensive, ready.

AGENT KATO (30s)
Subtle, observant. His horn is almost imperceptible, its light a faint, almost invisible shimmer.

VOICE (O.S.)
Agent Kato. Your keen espionage.

Kato remains still, a shadow in the light.

The seven Anglers stand before the dais, their unique features subtly glowing, a spectrum of purpose.

VOICE (O.S.)
You are conscripted. A critical mission awaits.
The fate of the Hydroverse rests upon your angler's light.

The chamber darkens, leaving only the seven, their horns casting long, individual shadows. They look at each other, a silent understanding passing between them.

FADE OUT.
