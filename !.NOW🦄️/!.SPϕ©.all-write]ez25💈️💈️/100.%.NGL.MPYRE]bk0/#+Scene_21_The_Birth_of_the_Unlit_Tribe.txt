INT. THE RIFT - DAY

The rift, a wound in the cosmos, pulses with raw, untamed energy. It is a place of swirling shadows and unpredictable light, far from the rigid order of the Angler Empire.

JURETH (20s), his horn blazing with the vibrant, chaotic light of the unlit void, stands at the edge of the rift. His face is resolute, no longer the hesitant youth, but a nascent leader.

Around him gather the core members of what will become the UNLIT TRIBE:

GADRETH (30s), the wanderer, his cloak of vapor shimmering, his brow bearing the mark of the Shimmering Hosts. He stands with a quiet strength, his gaze fixed on Jureth.

LIRITH (30s), her yarn symbiotes woven with threads of resilience, her spright-saber humming softly. She holds the hand of NAETH (10s), her daughter, whose young horn glows with a curious, untamed light.

Jureth turns to them, his voice resonating with conviction.

JURETH
The Empire seeks to control. To define. To limit. But the Hydroverse is vast. And the unlit void… it is boundless. We have seen its truth. We have felt its freedom.

GADRETH
The Shimmering Hosts showed me a path beyond the lanterns’ glow. A path of true freedom.

LIRITH
The shadow of the sphere taught me that even in perfect order, there can be decay. We seek a different kind of strength.

NAETH
(Her voice clear)
A strength that sings.

Jureth nods, a profound understanding passing between them.

JURETH
Then let us forge a new path. Here, by the rift, where the Empire’s light cannot reach. Let us be the Unlit Tribe. Bound not by chains, but by choice.

Suddenly, ZORATH (50s), Jureth’s father, and MIRAH (50s), his mother, emerge from the swirling shadows near the rift. Their faces are etched with weariness, but their eyes hold a newfound determination. Zorath’s horn, once a beacon of Imperial order, now flickers with a hesitant, yet hopeful light.

ZORATH
(Voice hoarse)
My son. We heard your call. The Empire… it has become a cage. We seek the truth you speak of.

Mirah embraces Jureth, tears in her eyes.

MIRAH
We will follow you, my son. To the unlit. To freedom.

Jureth embraces his parents, a rare, tender moment amidst the cosmic struggle.

JURETH
Welcome, Mother. Father. Your strength, your wisdom… it will guide us.

Gadreth, Lirith, and Naeth nod in welcome. The core of the Unlit Tribe is now complete.

JURETH (CONT'D)
(Turning back to the rift, his voice ringing with purpose)
We are the Unlit Tribe! And our light shall be our own!

The rift pulses in response, a silent affirmation. The shadows seem to deepen, yet within them, a new, vibrant energy begins to coalesce.

FADE OUT.
