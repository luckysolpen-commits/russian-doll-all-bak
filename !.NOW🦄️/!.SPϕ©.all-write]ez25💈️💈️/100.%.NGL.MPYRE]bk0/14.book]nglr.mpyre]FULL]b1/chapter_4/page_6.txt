The throne room was a monument to ego. It was a vast, cavernous space, its walls and floor carved from a single, flawless crystal that pulsed with the golden light of the ‘Power.’ At its center, on a throne of solidified energy, sat the emperor. He was a Nexus, but transfigured, his form elongated and ethereal, his eyes burning with the light of a captive star. He was less a person and more a living embodiment of the golden energy, a god-king in a temple of his own making. The air was thick with his power, a crushing, arrogant presence that sought to dominate, to overwhelm, to force submission.

The emperor’s royal guard, a dozen Nexus warriors similarly transfigured, turned as one, their movements perfectly synchronized, their golden eyes fixing on the three Anglers who had dared to defile their sacred space. There was no surprise on their faces, only a cold, divine anger. They had been expecting them. The offer of surrender had been a transparent ploy, but the emperor, in his infinite arrogance, had allowed it. He wanted to make an example of them, to show the galaxy the futility of resisting his glorious light.

“So, the insects have scuttled into the heart of the sun,” the emperor’s voice boomed, not from his mouth, but from the very air around them. It was a voice of pure power, a sound that vibrated in their bones. “You have come to extinguish the light, little Anglers. But you are nothing. Less than nothing. You are a fleeting shadow, and I am eternity.”

Jax, Hana, and Kato didn’t waste time with words. They knew that every second counted. As the royal guard advanced, their golden forms gliding effortlessly through the air, the three Anglers sprang into action. Hana laid down a suppressing fire with her pulse rifle, the energy bolts dissipating harmlessly against the guards’ golden shields. Jax charged forward, his energy sword a streak of defiant blue against the overwhelming gold. Kato, as was his way, vanished, melting into the geometric shadows cast by the pulsating crystal walls. The battle for the soul of Nexus had begun.
