The psychic siphon was not a weapon in the traditional sense. It did not destroy; it drained. It was designed to sever the connection between the emperor and the golden ‘Power,’ to create a vacuum that would collapse the hive mind. But the emperor’s connection was stronger than Luna had anticipated. He was not just a conduit for the ‘Power’; he *was* the ‘Power.’ The siphon, instead of severing the connection, created a feedback loop, a tug-of-war between the black, anti-energy of the device and the golden, absolute power of the emperor.

The throne room began to shake, the crystal walls groaning under the strain of the two opposing forces. The emperor, his face now a mask of divine rage, focused his will on the siphon, trying to overwhelm it, to crush it. The black cylinder in Jax’s hand grew hot, the device straining under the immense pressure. A web of cracks appeared on its surface, and a high-pitched whine filled the air. It was going to overload. “It’s not working!” Hana shouted, her voice filled with a desperate urgency. “Jax, get out of there!”

But Jax held his ground. He could feel the siphon straining, but he could also feel something else. The emperor’s power, for the first time, was not absolute. It was being challenged, and the challenge was creating a flaw, a weakness. The golden aura around the emperor began to flicker, to pulse erratically. The guards, their connection to the hive mind wavering, faltered in their attacks. Kael’s voice crackled in their comms, a lifeline of hope in the chaos. “The energy field around the planet is destabilizing! Whatever you’re doing, it’s working! Keep it up!”

Jax poured the last of his own will, his own energy, into the siphon. He was no longer just holding the device; he was a part of it, his own defiant spirit a third force in the cosmic tug-of-war. The whine of the siphon grew to a fever pitch, the cracks in its surface spreading, a network of black lightning against the golden light. The emperor, his face contorted in a silent scream of rage and disbelief, focused all of his immense power on the defiant mortal at his feet. The siphon exploded.
