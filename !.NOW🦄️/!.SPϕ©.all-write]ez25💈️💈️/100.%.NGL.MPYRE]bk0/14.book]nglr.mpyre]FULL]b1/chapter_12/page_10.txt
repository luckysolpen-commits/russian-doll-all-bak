The war would be long, and it would be hard. There would be victories, and there would be defeats. There would be moments of hope, and moments of despair. But the new Anglers would not falter. They would not surrender. They would fight, not for glory, or for power, or for a single, absolute truth, but for the simple, profound, and inalienable right to be messy, to be beautiful, to be free.

They were the children of the revolution, and they would not let the ghosts of the past destroy the future they were all fighting so hard to create. They were the Straylight, and their story was just beginning. The end of the beginning. The beginning of the end. The universe is a story that is always being written, a song that is always being sung. And the most beautiful, most interesting, and most necessary notes are the ones that are yet to come.
