INT. UNLIT TRIBE CAMP - DAY

The Unlit Tribe’s camp by the rift, once a vibrant hub of new ideas, now shows signs of strain. The energy signature of the rift, usually a source of strength, seems to flicker with uncertainty.

Jureth, Gadreth, Lirith, Naeth, Zorath, and Mirah gather around a flickering holographic map of their sector. The map, usually showing trade routes and resource flows, now displays a series of red lines, indicating blockades and diversions.

ZORATH
(His horn dim, a worried frown on his face)
The Empire’s first strike. Not with spright-sabers, but with tariffs. Our supply lines are being choked. The trade routes we established with the independent worlds… they’re drying up.

MIRAH
(Her voice soft, but firm)
Our food stores are dwindling. The water purifiers are struggling. They’re trying to starve us out.

GADRETH
(His vapor cloak shimmering with frustration)
They’re calling it “economic restructuring.” “Fair trade practices.” Lies. All lies.

LIRITH
(Her yarn symbiotes tightening around her arms)
And the whispers. They’re everywhere. Rumors of our “heresy.” Of Jureth’s “madness.” They’re trying to turn our allies against us.

NAETH
(Her young horn flickering with anger)
They say we’re selfish. That we hoard the unlit truth. That we seek to destroy the Hydroverse.

Jureth listens, his horn glowing with a steady, defiant light. He sees the doubt creeping into the eyes of some of the newer tribe members, who stand at a distance, listening to the conversation.

JURETH
(To his family and core allies)
This is Vorith’s “Art of Subtle Dominion.” He seeks to demoralize us. To make us question our own path. To sterilize us – financially, intellectually, even spiritually.

ZORATH
(Sighing)
It’s working, son. Some of the newer recruits… they’re starting to grumble. They miss the stability of the Empire. The easy access to resources.

MIRAH
They don’t understand the cost of that stability. The cost of that “easy access.”

Jureth walks to the edge of the rift, gazing into its swirling depths. He feels the immense, untamed power of the unlit void.

JURETH
(Voice strong, resonating with conviction)
The Empire seeks to hide the Shell. To deny us its pearls of wisdom. But the Shell is not an object. It is a state of being. And its pearls are within us. In our minds. In our spirits.

He turns back to his tribe, his horn blazing.

JURETH (CONT'D)
They want to divide us. To splinter our reality. But unity is our shield! Our strength! We will not be starved! We will not be silenced! We will not be broken!

Gadreth, Lirith, Naeth, Zorath, and Mirah stand with him, their horns glowing in solidarity. But the faces of the other tribe members remain uncertain. The subtle attack has taken its toll.

JURETH (CONT'D)
(To himself, a new resolve in his eyes)
I must write. I must give them a voice. A rebuttal to Vorith’s insidious lies. A path to the unlit truth.

FADE OUT.
