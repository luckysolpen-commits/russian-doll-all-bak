INT. AVANTA.C - ELDRADOR'S MAZE - DAY

The chamber has transformed into a shifting, grinding labyrinth. Walls slide, floors tilt, and new passages appear and vanish with disorienting speed. Dust and debris rain down constantly. The Eldridian Amulet in Lucky's hand pulses erratically, its light now a frantic, strobing beacon that seems to react to the maze's movements.

LUCKY
(Shouting over the grinding)
Which way?! It keeps changing!

Astra, despite the chaos, maintains a surprising composure. She projects a small, glowing orb of light from her wand, trying to map their surroundings, but the layout is too fluid.

ASTRA
(Voice strained)
It's a living maze! We need to find a pattern, a weakness!

Kael, however, seems strangely calm. His eyes dart around, not with panic, but with a calculating intensity. He points down a newly formed corridor.

KAEL
This way! I sense a momentary stabilization in this section!

They follow him, scrambling over newly formed rubble. As they round a sharp corner, the passage opens into a wider, circular chamber. But it's not empty.

Standing before them, blocking their path, is a group of figures. They are clad in dark, sleek armor, their faces obscured by menacing, angular masks. In the center, a tall, imposing figure steps forward. His armor is more ornate, his mask bearing a stylized, jagged symbol. This is EDGE DANGERMAN (30s, ruthless, charismatic), leader of the Metal Mafia guild.

EDGE DANGERMAN
(Voice, a low, resonant growl, amplified by his helmet)
Well, well, well. Look what the maze dragged in. Lucky. Astra. I've been expecting you.

Lucky's grip on the amulet tightens. Astra's hand instinctively goes to her Blade-Shield of Valor.

LUCKY
(Defiant)
Edge Dangerman. What do you want?

Edge chuckles, a chilling sound that echoes in the confined space. His masked gaze sweeps over them, lingering for a moment on Kael.

EDGE DANGERMAN
(Cont.)
Want? I want what's mine. And what's yours, apparently. I've been tracking your little adventures. Your rapid leveling, your acquisition of... interesting artifacts. You're quite the pair of rising stars. But naive. And weak, in the grand scheme of things.

Astra's eyes flash with anger.

ASTRA
So, this was an ambush. You set this trap.

EDGE DANGERMAN
(A shrug, almost casual)
Not entirely. The maze is Eldrador's own defense. But I merely... encouraged its activation. And ensured I was here to greet the lucky (or unlucky) souls who triggered it. You see, I have a particular interest in powerful artifacts. And in eliminating... competition.

His gaze shifts to Kael, a subtle, knowing glint in his masked eyes. Kael's expression remains neutral, unreadable, but Lucky notices a flicker of something – perhaps a fleeting tension – in his posture.

EDGE DANGERMAN
(Cont., a hint of amusement)
And Kael. You're a bit of a surprise. Though, I suppose, not entirely unexpected. The Metal Mafia always has its eyes everywhere. Even on its... more ambitious operatives.

Lucky's head snaps to Kael. The pieces begin to click into place. The constant surveillance, Kael's uncanny knowledge of their movements, his sudden appearance.

LUCKY
(To Kael, a dawning realization)
You... you're with him?

Kael's eyes meet Lucky's for a brief moment. There's a complex mix of calculation and something else – perhaps a flicker of regret, or a deeper game at play – before his face becomes a mask once more.

KAEL
(Voice calm, almost detached)
My allegiances are... complicated. But for now, let's just say I have a vested interest in how this plays out.

Edge Dangerman laughs, a triumphant, chilling sound.

EDGE DANGERMAN
(To his guild members)
Surround them! They're trapped. And soon, their power, and their precious artifact, will be ours.

The masked figures begin to advance, their weapons drawn. The walls of the maze continue to shift and groan around them, making escape seem impossible. Lucky, Astra, and Kael are cornered, facing not just the maze, but a formidable, well-organized enemy.

FADE OUT.
