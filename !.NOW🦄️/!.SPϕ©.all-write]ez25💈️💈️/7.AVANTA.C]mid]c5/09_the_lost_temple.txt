INT. AVANTA.C - PLAYER HUB - DAY

After their whirlwind tour of Avanta.C's diverse landscapes, Lucky and Astra find themselves in a quiet corner of a bustling PLAYER HUB, a central meeting point for adventurers. Holographic billboards advertise quests, guilds, and rare items. The air hums with the low thrum of countless data streams.

They decide it's time to get serious about their progression. Astra taps a series of invisible commands in the air, and a translucent, glowing INTERFACE materializes before them. It's the CHARACTER MENU, displaying their current stats and inventory.

LUCKY'S STATS (Holographic Display)

LEVEL: 5
HP: 120/150 (Health Points)
MP: 80/100 (Mana Points)
STRENGTH: 10 (Physical power, melee damage)
AGILITY: 15 (Movement speed, dodge chance)
INTELLIGENCE: 8 (Magic power, spell effectiveness)

Lucky studies his numbers. He's a decent all-rounder, leaning slightly towards agility.

LUCKY
(Muttering)
Level five. Still a long way to go.

Astra's stats appear next to his, slightly higher.

ASTRA'S STATS (Holographic Display)

LEVEL: 6
HP: 150/180
MP: 100/120
STRENGTH: 12
AGILITY: 18
INTELLIGENCE: 10

ASTRA
(A slight smirk)
We're still low-level. Too low for any serious guild to consider us. We need to grind.

She navigates to the INVENTORY tab. A grid of glowing icons appears, representing their current gear.

LUCKY'S INVENTORY (Holographic Display)

- QUICK SWORD (Basic attack weapon. +2 Strength)
- SHIELD OF COURAGE (Basic shield. +5 Defense)
- HEALING POTION (Restores 20 HP. Quantity: 3)
- AVANTA.C STARTER PACK (Contains: 50 Gold Coins, Basic Map, Beginner's Guide)

ASTRA'S INVENTORY (Holographic Display)

- WAND OF THE ELEMENTS (Basic magic staff. +3 Intelligence)
- SPELLBOOK OF SHADOWS (Contains: Fireball, Healing Hands. +5 MP)
- MANA CRYSTAL (Restores 20 MP. Quantity: 2)
- AVANTA.C STARTER PACK (Contains: 50 Gold Coins, Basic Map, Beginner's Guide)

LUCKY
(Sighs)
Not exactly decked out for a dragon hunt, are we?

As Lucky and Astra are reviewing their gear, Astra's avatar interface suddenly chimes with an incoming message. She opens it, her expression changing as she reads.

ASTRA
(Reading the message, surprised)
Lucky... I think our escape route just arrived.

LUCKY
(Interested)
What do you mean?

ASTRA
It's from Pixel, the avatar customizer from the illegal furry server. He's offering us a way to convert our illegal accounts to legal citizenship on Icarus - one of the most prestigious worlds in Avanta.C.

LUCKY
(Eyes widening)
How is that possible?

ASTRA
(Reading further)
He says there's an underground economy where we can convert regular gold into 'Furry Coins' - a special currency that can be exchanged for citizenship accounts. But we need to build up our reputation and gold reserves first. The process goes through a group called 'Anonymous' - they specialize in account conversions.

LUCKY
(Studying the message)
So we need to do quests, earn gold, level up, get better gear... all to fund our account conversion?

ASTRA
(Nodding)
Exactly. Pixel says if we can prove ourselves capable in the legal server quests, he can help us with the conversion. But we have to be careful - the Metal Mafia has been tracking anyone trying to make the move from illegal to legal servers. They control a lot of that underground currency exchange.

LUCKY
(Determined)
Then we'd better start proving ourselves. Time to get our reputation up legitimately before we attempt that.

She closes the menu and looks at him, a determined glint in her eye.

ASTRA
Ready to get your hands dirty, Lucky? Our future depends on it.

Lucky grins, a spark of excitement in his eyes. He may be low-level, but with Astra by his side and a clear goal in mind, he feels ready for anything Avanta.C can throw at them.

LUCKY
Let's do this. For real citizenship on Icarus.

FADE OUT.
