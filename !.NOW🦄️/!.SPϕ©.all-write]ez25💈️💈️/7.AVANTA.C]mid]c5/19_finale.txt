INT. METAL MAFIA MOTHERSHIP - AVANTACY SERVER ROOM - NIGHT

Kael, now fully in his Nova Spetsnaz persona, moves with a chilling precision. He's no longer the charming merchant; he's a ghost in the machine, a digital phantom. He stands before a complex holographic map of the Metal Mafia Mothership, projected from a device on his wrist. Lines of code, security protocols, and structural schematics scroll rapidly across the display.

He points to a section of the map, a heavily fortified core.

KAEL
(Voice, a low, almost synthesized murmur)
This is it. The Avantacy Server Room. The heart of their operation. It's a fortress, both physically and digitally. Every server, every data stream, every access point is monitored. They process the stolen consciousness here, control their robotic legions, manage their entire virtual empire.

He zooms in on the room's layout. Holographic representations of robotic GUARDS patrol designated routes. Laser grids crisscross corridors. Data conduits glow with a faint, dangerous energy.

KAEL
(Cont.)
Physical defenses are formidable. But the true challenge lies in their digital countermeasures. Firewalls, intrusion detection systems, AI sentinels... they're designed to detect even the slightest anomaly. One wrong move, one misplaced packet, and the entire Mothership will be on high alert.

He begins to type rapidly on a holographic keyboard, his fingers a blur. Lines of complex code, algorithms, and network bypasses flash across the display. He's not just hacking; he's orchestrating a symphony of digital infiltration.

KAEL
(Cont.)
I've spent years studying their systems, finding the cracks, the backdoors. But even with my knowledge, it's a razor's edge. The teleportation matrix... it's deep within the core. Heavily encrypted. I'll need to be inside, physically, to activate it.

He looks at his reflection in the holographic map. His avatar shifts, becoming sleeker, more streamlined. He's donned his "Halo body," a specialized infiltration suit that allows for enhanced stealth and digital interfacing.

KAEL
(Cont.)
This is it. The moment we've been waiting for. For years, my comrades have been trapped, enslaved. This is their chance at freedom. At revenge.

He takes a deep breath, a rare moment of human emotion flickering across his otherwise stoic face. He then moves with a renewed sense of purpose, a man on a mission.

INT. METAL MAFIA MOTHERSHIP - CORRIDORS - NIGHT

Kael navigates the labyrinthine corridors of the Mothership. The walls are sleek, metallic, glowing with a cold, blue light. Robotic GUARDS, silent and imposing, patrol the hallways. Kael moves like a shadow, his Halo body rendering him almost invisible to their optical sensors. He uses subtle digital pulses to disrupt their patrol patterns, creating momentary blind spots.

He encounters LASER GRIDS, shimmering walls of red light. He doesn't try to disable them. Instead, he uses his advanced agility and knowledge of their timing to slip through the gaps, his movements precise and economical. He's a dancer in a deadly ballet.

He reaches a heavily reinforced door, marked with the Metal Mafia's jagged symbol. A complex biometric scanner glows beside it. Kael doesn't bother with brute force. He extends a finger, and a thin, almost invisible tendril of light extends from it, probing the scanner. Lines of code flow from his finger into the device. The scanner flickers, then glows green. The heavy door slides open with a soft HISS.

INT. METAL MAFIA MOTHERSHIP - AVANTACY SERVER ROOM - NIGHT

Kael steps into the Avantacy Server Room. It's a vast, cavernous space, filled with row upon row of towering servers and mainframes. Thousands of data cables, thick as pythons, snake across the floor and up the walls, glowing with the constant flow of information. The air hums with the low thrum of processors, a symphony of digital power.

More robotic GUARDS patrol the perimeter, their optical sensors sweeping the room. Laser beams crisscross the space, forming an intricate, deadly web. Holographic displays flicker on massive screens, showing complex data visualizations, network traffic, and what appear to be abstract representations of human minds, flickering and dim.

Kael moves swiftly but silently, a phantom amidst the machines. He avoids the patrolling guards, his movements fluid and precise. He navigates the laser grid, his body contorting in impossible ways, slipping through the narrow gaps.

He reaches a central console, a massive, glowing interface at the heart of the room. This is the teleportation device. It's heavily protected, surrounded by a final, impenetrable-looking energy shield.

Kael extends both hands, his fingers splayed. A torrent of code, a digital storm, erupts from his fingertips, slamming against the energy shield. The shield shimmers, resisting, but Kael pushes harder, his face a mask of intense concentration. The air around him crackles with energy.

He types a final, complex command into the console, his voice a strained whisper.

KAEL (V.O.)
/activate teleportation_matrix NovaSpetsnaz_Protocol_Omega

A brilliant, blinding LIGHT erupts from the console, engulfing Kael. The entire server room is bathed in an intense, white glow. The robotic guards freeze, their systems overloading. The laser grids flicker and die. The hum of the servers rises to a deafening SCREAM.

Kael's form begins to shimmer, to distort, to dissolve into pure light. He is activating the teleportation. He is bringing his people home. He is fulfilling his vengeance.

But then, a new sound. A high-pitched, discordant WAIL, like a corrupted data stream. The brilliant light from the console flickers, then turns a sickly, pulsating green. The energy shield, instead of collapsing, seems to solidify, trapping the light within.

Kael's dissolving form solidifies, then distorts, his features twisting in a silent scream. The light around him doesn't dissipate; instead, it pulls him, violently, into the console itself. The room shudders, not with the controlled energy of a teleportation, but with the violent, unpredictable force of a system crash.

The last thing Lucky sees, a fleeting image burned into his mind, is Kael's face, contorted in agony and confusion, as he is sucked into the glowing green vortex of the console. Then, a blinding flash, and a sensation of being ripped apart, of falling through an endless void.

FADE TO BLACK.
