INT. IKARTUS - ABANDONED TERMINAL - NIGHT

Lucky stands before a grimy, abandoned terminal in a forgotten corner of Ikarus. The air is thick with the smell of ozone and stale data. He clutches the data crystal BB gave him, its faint glow illuminating his determined face.

He inserts the crystal into a dusty port. The terminal flickers to life, displaying a crude, black-market bot account interface. Lines of code scroll rapidly across the screen, then a prompt appears:

"INITIATING AVANTA.C CONNECTION... UNAUTHORIZED ACCESS DETECTED. PROCEED? (Y/N)"

Lucky hesitates for a moment, then types 'Y'. The screen flashes, and a new interface appears, sleek and vibrant, a stark contrast to the grimy terminal. It's the Avanta.C login screen.

VOICEOVER (FEMALE, SYNTHESIZED)
Welcome, new soul. Prepare to transcend.

The screen shifts, presenting a dizzying array of AVATAR OPTIONS. Holographic projections of fantastical beings, from towering warriors to ethereal mages, spin before him. Each one is rendered in exquisite detail, a testament to Avanta.C's immersive power.

Lucky scrolls through the options, his fingers hovering over various choices. He sees avatars with glowing eyes, intricate armor, and flowing robes. He considers a hulking, armored figure, then a nimble, cloaked rogue. He pauses on a figure that seems to embody both strength and agility, a balanced warrior with a hint of mystery.

He selects it. The chosen avatar glows, then solidifies, its features becoming clearer. It's a lean, athletic figure, clad in practical, yet stylish, light armor. Its face is obscured by a cowl, hinting at a hidden identity.

VOICEOVER (FEMALE, SYNTHESIZED)
Avatar selected. Identity confirmed. Preparing for immersion.

The terminal hums, a low, resonant thrum that vibrates through the floor. The holographic projections of Avanta.C's world begin to swirl around him, forming a vortex of color and light. Lucky closes his eyes, a sense of anticipation and trepidation washing over him.

He takes a deep breath, ready to step into his new life, his new reality.

FADE TO BLACK.
