INT. AVANTA.C - ILLEGAL FURRY SERVER - AVATAR CUSTOMIZATION PARLOR - NIGHT

Inside the Illegal Furry Server, Astra leads Lucky through a maze of pulsating virtual rooms until they reach an exclusive AVATAR CUSTOMIZATION PARLOR. The space is cramped, filled with holographic displays, virtual mirrors, and floating interface panels controlled by a grizzled, multi-armed spider-furred hacker known only as 'PIXEL'.

PIXEL
(Six arms gesturing at various avatar options floating in the air)
Welcome to my shop, travelers. I can give you anything you desire. Dragon, pony, cat, wolf... whatever your heart desires. The question is: what do you want to become?

LUCKY
(Studying the options)
I've been thinking about it since we got here. I want... something powerful. Something that matches what I'm going through. A dragon, maybe?

ASTRA
(Smiling knowingly)
Fitting. You do have that stubborn streak. I think I'll go with... a pony. Something that can move fast and maneuver well.

PIXEL
(Arms moving over interface panels)
Ah, classic choices. Dragon and pony, got it. Now, for the conversion process, we'll need to access your base avatar code directly. This is illegal on the main servers, but here... we can do it.

Pixel's multiple arms work furiously, opening up Lucky's avatar code. The process is visualized as Lucky's human form begins to shimmer and morph.

LUCKY
(Feeling the transformation)
This... this is incredible! I can feel every change!

Lucky's skin begins to sprout scales that shimmer with an iridescent blue and gold pattern. His limbs grow more powerful, his face elongates into a dragon snout, and magnificent wings unfurl from his back. His clothes shift and adapt, becoming more form-fitting for his new form.

For Astra, the transformation is equally dramatic. Her human features soften as a coat of soft, purple fur grows across her body. Her legs become sturdy, powerful pony legs, and a flowing mane cascades down her neck. Her ears become pointed and mobile, and a matching purple tail swishes behind her.

ASTRA
(Amazed at her reflection)
I... I look magnificent! This is unlike anything I've ever experienced.

PIXEL
(Charging them through the interface)
Custom furry avatars, full-body conversion. That'll be 500 gold coins each. But I've got a tip for you - there are some underground gaming circuits in the server. Beat Saber hacking battles are all the rage. You could earn that back in no time.

LUCKY
(Dragon form, voice deeper but still recognizable)
Beat Saber hacking battles? What's that?

PIXEL
Grins with sharp, digital teeth)
You'll see. The stakes are real, and so are the rewards. And the dangers.

Lucky flexes his new dragon wings, testing his balance on all fours before shifting to a bipedal stance. Astra prances around, getting used to her new pony form and its enhanced agility.

LUCKY
(Cont.)
Well, this is a new beginning. How do I look?

ASTRA
(Playfully nuzzling him with her snout)
Like a proper dragon lord. And I feel faster than ever. I think we're going to fit in perfectly here.

The two of them exit the parlor, now fully transformed and better suited to the wild underground of Avanta.C.

FADE OUT.
