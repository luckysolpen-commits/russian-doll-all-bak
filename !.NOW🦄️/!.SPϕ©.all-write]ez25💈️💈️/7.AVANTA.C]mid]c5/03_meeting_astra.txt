INT. AVANTA.C - BUSTLING CITY SQUARE - DAY

Lucky opens his eyes. The grimy terminal is gone. He stands in the heart of a vibrant, fantastical city square. Towering spires of iridescent crystal pierce a sky of impossible blues and greens. Shimmering waterfalls cascade down the sides of colossal, moss-covered structures, feeding into crystal-clear rivers that wind through the city.

The air is alive with the melodic chirping of unseen creatures, the distant clang of a blacksmith's hammer, and the murmur of countless voices. Lush, bioluminescent flora glows softly from planters and hanging gardens, casting a gentle, otherworldly light.

Lucky takes a deep breath, the scent of exotic blossoms and freshly baked bread filling his senses. This is Avanta.C. It's more real, more vivid, than anything he could have imagined.

He looks down at his hands. They are no longer gaunt and trembling, but strong and capable, clad in the light armor of his chosen avatar. He feels a surge of energy, a lightness he hasn't known in years.

His thoughts drift back to Earth – the EMPRIE's relentless pursuit, the desperate struggle for survival, the Blue Bird Bounty Hunter's unexpected rescue. Those memories are still there, a faint echo in the back of his mind, but they feel distant, almost like a dream. Avanta.C offers a fresh start, a chance to be someone new, to build a life free from the shadows of his past.

As he wanders through the square, he marvels at the diversity of the inhabitants. Elves with graceful, pointed ears haggle with burly dwarves over enchanted trinkets. Gnomish engineers tinker with intricate clockwork devices. Human adventurers, clad in gleaming armor, stride purposefully through the crowds.

He spots a group gathered around a large, ornate stone pedestal in the center of the square. A woman with fiery red hair, ARIA (20s, bard, charismatic), strums a lively tune on a lute, her fingers dancing across the strings. Her music is infectious, a blend of ancient melodies and modern beats. Others dance and cheer in unison, their laughter echoing through the square.

Lucky finds himself drawn to the energy, a faint smile touching his lips. He feels an inexplicable urge to join in, to shed the weight of his past and simply exist in this vibrant, new world.
