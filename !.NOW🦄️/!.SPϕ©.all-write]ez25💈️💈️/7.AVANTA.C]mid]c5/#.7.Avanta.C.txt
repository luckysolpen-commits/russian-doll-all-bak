him, and Lucky felt himself being pulled in as well.

When their vision cleared, they found themselves standing in 
the midst of a bustling cityscape. The streets were filled 
with people going about their daily business, completely 
unaware of the true nature of this world.

"Where are we?" Lucky asked, looking around in confusion.

Kael's expression was grim. "We're in the Glitch World."

Lucky's eyes narrowed. "What do you mean?"

"It's a reality created by the Metal Mafia," Kael explained. 
"They use it to imprison their enemies and try to brainwash 
them into serving their interests."

The city around them seemed normal, but Lucky could sense that
something was off. The people walking by seemed... glitchy.

"What's going on?" Lucky asked, his voice low.

Kael's gaze locked onto his. "We're trapped, Lucky. We need to
find a way out of here before it's too late."

But as they looked around, they realized that escape wouldn't 
be easy. The streets were filled with Metal Mafia robots, 
patrolling the area and keeping watch for any signs of 
trouble.
