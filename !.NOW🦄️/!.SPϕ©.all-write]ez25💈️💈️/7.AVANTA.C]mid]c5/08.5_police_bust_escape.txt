INT. AVANTA.C - ILLEGAL FURRY SERVER - PARTY ZONE - CONTINUOUS

The party comes to an abrupt end as EMPEROR'S BORDER POLICE AVATARS materialize throughout the space. They're imposing, military-grade characters with stark black armor, red accents, and glowing visors. Their presence is overwhelming and authoritative.

EMPEROR'S BORDER POLICE CAPTAIN
(Voice booming with authority)
This is an illegal server raid! All participants must submit for identity verification! No exceptions!

PANICKED PARTY-GOERS
(Shouting, running in all directions)
Everyone's shifting back to their standard humanoid forms as the inebriant effects fade. The digital fog clears, revealing the chaos as party-goers scatter in all directions.

LUCKY
(Dragon form immediately alert)
Astra! We need to get out of here, now!

ASTRA
(Pony form already moving)
The mounted section! They can't all fit through the narrow passages!

In the corner of the server, there's a section dedicated to virtual mounts - exotic creatures for rapid transportation through the digital landscape. Lucky and Astra race toward this area.

LUCKY
(Dragon form calling out)
How do we mount up while staying in these forms?

ASTRA
(Finding a pair of cybernetic PEGASUS MOUNTS)
These will work! The system will adapt!

They quickly mount two of the cybernetic pegasus creatures, which are part horse, part dragon, with metallic wings and glowing circuits. The mounts are designed for speed and agility within the virtual space.

EMPEROR'S BORDER POLICE CAPTAIN
(To his forces)
Don't let anyone escape! And especially don't let Lucky get away - the EMPRIE has a special interest in capturing him!

LUCKY
(Surprised)
They're looking for me specifically?

ASTRA
(Piloting her mount)
We'll figure that out later! Right now, hold on tight!

The mounted chase begins as Lucky and Astra burst through the party area on their cybernetic mounts. The police try to follow, but the narrow passages and shifting digital landscape of the illegal server create natural obstacles.

LUCKY
(Leaning low on his dragon mount)
These things are fast, but I can feel them gaining!

ASTRA
(Navigating through a maze of virtual architecture)
There! The exit tunnel! If we can make it to the legal servers, they can't follow!

Behind them, multiple police avatars pursue them through a maze of data streams and code structures. The chase is intense, with Lucky and Astra weaving between floating platforms and leaping across gaps in the virtual terrain.

EMPEROR'S BORDER POLICE CAPTAIN
(Over radio)
Target is heading toward the server boundary! Block all escape routes!

The cybernetic mounts prove their worth as Lucky and Astra utilize both the dragon and pony avatars' natural abilities - Lucky's dragon form providing additional power to urge the mount faster, while Astra's pony agility helps her navigate the treacherous path.

LUCKY
(Calling to Astra as they race through a narrow canyon of code)
We're almost there! Just a little further!

ASTRA
(Deftly avoiding a police attack that shoots past them)
The boundary is right ahead! Go, go, go!

They burst through the digital boundary between the illegal furry server and the main Avanta.C network just as the police forces hit an access barrier - they can't cross without legal warrants that they don't have time to process.

LUCKY
(Dragon form breathing heavily as they slow down)
That was... too close.

ASTRA
(Pony form looking back at the barrier)
They were definitely after you, Lucky. The EMPRIE connection must be deeper than we thought.

LUCKY
(Dismounting from his cybernetic pegasus)
At least we're safe for now. And we've got better gear, new avatars, and a reputation in the underground.

ASTRA
(Smiling despite the danger)
And we know how to work together. The Metal Mafia, the hackers, even the Emperor's Police... they all know who we are now.

They return their mounts to the virtual stables, their hearts still racing from the escape.

FADE OUT.
