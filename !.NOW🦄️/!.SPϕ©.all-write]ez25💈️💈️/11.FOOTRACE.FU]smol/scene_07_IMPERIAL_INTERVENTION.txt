SCENE 7: IMPERIAL INTERVENTION

[INT. TIME HOTEL - LUXURY SUITE - DAY]

The uneasy calm is shattered. The ornate doors to the suite burst open with a resounding CRASH. Standing in the doorway are six IMPERIAL GUARDS, their armor gleaming, their faces grim. They are armed with temporal staves that crackle with contained energy.

Their leader, CAPTAIN JIN (40s, stern, unyielding), steps forward, his gaze fixed on Mei-Ling.

CAPTAIN JIN
Mei-Ling, by order of the Time Emperor, you are under arrest. You are to return to the Imperial Palace immediately.

Lucky, Ling-Li, and Xi-Xi instinctively move to shield Mei-Ling. Lucky's mind races, trying to comprehend what's happening. Arrest? Mei-Ling? The Emperor's daughter?

LUCKY
(Confused)
What's going on? Arrest for what?

Mei-Ling steps forward, her serene demeanor replaced by a fierce determination. Her eyes flash with defiance.

MEI-LING
(Voice firm, resonant)
I will not return. Not until I have completed my mission.

CAPTAIN JIN
Your mission is irrelevant. Your father, the Emperor, demands your presence. Your continued defiance is an affront to the Imperial family and the stability of the Land of Time.

LING-LI
(Stepping forward, her playful nature replaced by anger)
She has done nothing wrong! She is merely seeking balance, something your Emperor has long forgotten!

XI-XI
(Her voice trembling slightly, but resolute)
We will not let you take her!

Captain Jin scoffs, raising his temporal staff. The other guards follow suit, their staves humming ominously.

CAPTAIN JIN
Then you leave us no choice. Guards, apprehend the Princess. And secure the Fuzzball wielder.

The guards advance, their movements precise and synchronized. Lucky feels a surge of adrenaline. Zick and Zack, sensing the danger, begin to glow faintly, their fur bristling.

Lucky looks at Mei-Ling, then at Ling-Li and Xi-Xi. Their faces are set, ready for a fight. He realizes the gravity of the situation. This wasn't just a misunderstanding; this was a direct confrontation with the ruling power of the Land of Time. And he was caught right in the middle of it.
