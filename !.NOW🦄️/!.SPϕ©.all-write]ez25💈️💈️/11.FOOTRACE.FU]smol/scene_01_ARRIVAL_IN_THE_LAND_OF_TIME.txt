SCENE 1: ARRIVAL IN THE LAND OF TIME

[EXT. TIME DOJO - DAY]

The air shimmers with an ethereal glow as the TIEM TRAIN, a sleek, jade-green locomotive wreathed in temporal energy, hisses to a stop. Steam, smelling faintly of cherry blossoms and ozone, billows around its ornate wheels.

LUCKY (17, earnest, a little overwhelmed, but with an underlying spark of determination) steps off the train, his worn backpack slung over one shoulder. Clinging to his shoulders, like furry, sentient epaulets, are ZICK and ZACK, his FUZZBALL companions. Zick, a vibrant sapphire, vibrates with nervous energy, while Zack, a calm emerald, observes with wide, curious eyes.

The Land of Time unfolds before him. Towering pagodas with roofs that curl like dragon scales pierce a sky painted in hues of lavender and gold. Cherry blossom trees, their petals drifting like pink snow, line cobblestone paths. Everywhere, people move with an unusual grace, some seemingly in fast-forward, others in slow-motion, their movements a testament to the pervasive "Time Fu" energy.

A wizened, ancient TRAINER, his robes embroidered with intricate clockwork patterns, glides past, his hand making a subtle gesture that seems to ripple the very fabric of time around him. He glances at Lucky, a knowing smile playing on his lips.

Lucky feels a strange pull, a subtle hum in the air. He notices a digital counter on a nearby lamppost, flickering with numbers – "TIME DEBT: 00:00:00:05." He blinks, and it changes to "00:00:00:04." A chill runs down his spine.

Zick lets out a high-pitched squeak, pointing a tiny, fuzzy paw at a group of trainers practicing intricate martial arts forms, their movements blurring and snapping back into focus. Zack nudges Lucky’s ear, a soft, comforting presence.

Lucky takes a deep breath, the scent of incense and blooming flowers filling his lungs. This was it. The Land of Time. And he was ready, or at least, he hoped he was. He clutches a small, ornate ticket in his hand – his passage to a new destiny. The Time Dojo, a grand structure of polished dark wood and gleaming brass, stands majestically before him, its doors invitingly ajar. He steps forward, Zick and Zack chattering softly, into the heart of this time-warped world.
