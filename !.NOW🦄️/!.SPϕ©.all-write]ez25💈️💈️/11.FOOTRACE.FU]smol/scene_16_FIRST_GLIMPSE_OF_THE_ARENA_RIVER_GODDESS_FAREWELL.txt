SCENE 16: FIRST GLIMPSE OF THE ARENA / RIVER GODDESS'S FAREWELL

[INT. TIME FU TOURNAMENT ARENA - DAY]

Lucky, Mei-Ling, Ling-Li, and Xi-Xi emerge from the shimmering portal, blinking as their eyes adjust to the dazzling light of the Time Fu Tournament Arena. The air crackles with raw temporal energy, a palpable hum that vibrates through their very bones.

The arena is colossal, a marvel of temporal architecture. Tiered seating, carved from what appears to be solidified time, rises steeply around a vast, circular battleground. Holographic projections of past legendary Time Fu masters flicker across the walls, their movements fluid and awe-inspiring. The roar of the crowd, a cacophony of cheers and temporal echoes, washes over them.

Lucky looks around, his jaw slightly agape. This was beyond anything he could have imagined. Zick and Zack, perched on his shoulders, are vibrating with excitement, their colors flashing like tiny temporal beacons.

LING-LI
(Shouting over the din, her eyes wide with wonder)
This is incredible!

MEI-LING
(A rare smile gracing her lips)
Indeed. The Grand Time Fu Tournament. A spectacle unlike any other.

As they take in the grandeur, the shimmering portal behind them begins to close. The majestic DRAGON-BIRD form of the RIVER GODDESS, her scales still shimmering with all the colors of the rainbow, hovers for a moment, her ancient eyes meeting Lucky's.

RIVER GODDESS
(V.O., her voice a melodic chorus of rushing water and wind chimes, now softer, more intimate)
Remember, Lucky. The true power lies not in the manipulation of time, but in the understanding of its flow. Trust your instincts. Trust your heart. And trust those who stand by your side.

She gives a final, majestic beat of her wings, sending a gentle temporal breeze through the arena. Then, with a final, knowing glance, she dissolves into a cascade of shimmering light, disappearing completely as the portal winks out of existence.

Lucky feels a sense of profound gratitude and a renewed determination. The River Goddess's words echo in his mind. He looks at Mei-Ling, Ling-Li, and Xi-Xi, their faces reflecting a mixture of awe and excitement. Their bond, forged through shared experiences and growing affection, felt stronger than ever.

LUCKY
(Turning to the sisters, a confident smile on his face)
Alright. Let's do this.

Mei-Ling nods, her eyes filled with pride. Ling-Li pumps her fist in the air, a playful challenge in her eyes. Xi-Xi offers a gentle, encouraging smile. The roar of the crowd intensifies, beckoning them forward. The tournament awaited, and Lucky, with his Fuzzballs and his newfound family, was ready to face whatever temporal challenges lay ahead.
