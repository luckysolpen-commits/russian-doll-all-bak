SCENE 19: RED WAX TIME POLICE INTERVENTION

[INT. TIME FU TOURNAMENT ARENA - BACKSTAGE CORRIDOR - DAY]

Lucky, flushed with victory from a particularly challenging match, is walking through a backstage corridor, his Time Twin shimmering brightly beside him. He's been pushing his temporal twinning abilities to their limits, creating multiple, increasingly stable duplicates to overwhelm his opponents. Zick and Zack are buzzing with amplified energy.

Suddenly, two figures in distinctive, crimson-colored armor step out from a shadowed alcove, blocking his path. Their helmets are sleek, featureless, and their movements are precise, almost robotic. These are the RED WAX TIME POLICE, the enforcers of temporal law.

RED WAX OFFICER 1
(Voice synthesized, devoid of emotion)
Lucky. You are exceeding your temporal energy allocation. Your rapid advancement in temporal twinning has been noted. You are in violation of Temporal Code 7.3, subsection B: Unauthorized Proliferation of Temporal Duplicates.

Lucky stares, bewildered. He had no idea there were rules about this.

LUCKY
Unauthorized? I'm just trying to win the tournament!

RED WAX OFFICER 2
(Equally monotone)
Temporal manipulation, particularly the creation of multiple, stable temporal duplicates, places undue strain on the fabric of time. It creates ripples. Unforeseen consequences. You are coming with us.

Before Lucky can protest, the officers move with startling speed. They grab him, their grip surprisingly strong. His Time Twin flickers, then dissipates, unable to maintain its form under their temporal suppression fields. Zick and Zack let out distressed squeaks, their colors dimming.

[INT. SOUL PRIVY HEAD STORAGE DIMENSION - CONTINUOUS]

Lucky finds himself in a bizarre, disorienting dimension. It's a vast, cavernous space, filled with countless, glowing, disembodied heads floating in mid-air. Each head seems to be whispering, murmuring, a cacophony of forgotten thoughts and temporal echoes. The air is thick with the scent of ozone and something vaguely metallic.

RED WAX OFFICER 1
(His voice echoing strangely)
This is the Soul Privy Head Storage Dimension. A temporary holding facility for those who disregard temporal regulations. It allows for... introspection.

Lucky feels a strange pressure on his mind, a sense of being observed by a thousand unseen eyes. He tries to move, but he's held firmly in place by an invisible force.

RED WAX OFFICER 2
(Holding up a glowing temporal tablet)
Your temporal signature has been registered. Your twinning abilities have been logged. Further unauthorized proliferation will result in more severe penalties, including temporal re-education or, in extreme cases, temporal dissolution.

Lucky shivers at the last phrase. Temporal dissolution sounded permanent.

RED WAX OFFICER 1
(A faint, almost imperceptible shift in his synthesized voice, perhaps a hint of something akin to warning)
Be mindful, Lucky. The tournament is not what it seems. There are forces at play far beyond your current comprehension. Your abilities, while impressive, make you a target. Proceed with caution.

Suddenly, the pressure lifts. The glowing heads recede, and the strange dimension dissolves around him.

[INT. TIME FU TOURNAMENT ARENA - BACKSTAGE CORRIDOR - CONTINUOUS]

Lucky is back in the corridor, standing exactly where he was. The Red Wax Time Police are gone. He looks around, disoriented, wondering if it was all a dream. But Zick and Zack are still agitated, and a small, glowing temporal registration chip is now embedded in his wrist.

He touches the chip, a cold, metallic sensation. The warning from the Red Wax Officer echoes in his mind. The tournament is not what it seems. A new layer of unease settles over him. He was no longer just a competitor; he was now a registered temporal anomaly, under the watchful eye of the Red Wax Time Police.
