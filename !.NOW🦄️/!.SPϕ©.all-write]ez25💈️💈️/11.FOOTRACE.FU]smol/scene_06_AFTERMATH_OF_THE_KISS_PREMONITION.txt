SCENE 6: AFTERMATH OF THE KISS / PREMONITION

[INT. TIME HOTEL - LUXURY SUITE - MORNING]

Sunlight streams through the ornate windows, painting golden stripes across the polished floor. Lucky stirs, a soft smile on his face as he recalls Mei-Ling's kiss. He glances over to see Mei-Ling already awake, meditating gracefully by the window, her silhouette framed by the rising sun. Ling-Li is still asleep, a playful tangle of limbs, and Xi-Xi is meticulously arranging a small bouquet of temporal lilies on a bedside table.

Lucky feels a warmth in his chest, a sense of peace he hasn't known before. He watches Mei-Ling, her movements fluid and serene, and a new understanding dawns on him. This wasn't just a casual affection; there was a deeper connection, a silent promise in her gesture.

LUCKY
(Softly)
Good morning.

Mei-Ling opens her eyes, her gaze meeting his. A faint, knowing smile touches her lips.

MEI-LING
Good morning, Lucky. Did you sleep well?

LUCKY
Better than I have in a long time.

He hesitates, then asks, his voice a little shy.

LUCKY (CONT'D)
About last night... the kiss...

Mei-Ling rises and walks towards him, her movements unhurried. She sits beside him, her hand gently resting on his arm.

MEI-LING
It was a gesture of acceptance, Lucky. Into our family. Into our lives. You are important to us.

Her words are sincere, and Lucky feels a blush creep up his neck. He looks at Ling-Li and Xi-Xi, who are now stirring, and sees no judgment, only warmth.

LING-LI
(Stretching, yawning)
What's all the serious talk so early? Did you two finally admit you like each other?

Lucky and Mei-Ling exchange a glance, a shared, unspoken understanding passing between them. Xi-Xi giggles softly.

Suddenly, a faint, almost imperceptible tremor runs through the room. The temporal lilies on the table shiver, and the light from the window flickers for a brief moment. Mei-Ling's serene expression tightens, her eyes narrowing slightly.

MEI-LING
(Voice low, a hint of concern)
That's... unusual. A disturbance in the temporal flow.

Lucky looks around, confused. He didn't feel anything. Zick and Zack, however, are agitated, zipping around the room, their colors flashing erratically.

XI-XI
(Her voice barely a whisper)
It feels like... a storm is coming.

Ling-Li, her playful demeanor gone, looks out the window, her brow furrowed. The vibrant colors of the Land of Time outside seem to dim, a subtle shift in the atmosphere. A sense of unease settles over the group. The peaceful morning has been subtly, yet undeniably, disrupted. A premonition of impending trouble hangs heavy in the air.
