
CHARACTER SHEET: FOOTRACE FU

---

**LUCKY**
*   **Age:** 17
*   **Appearance:** Average build, earnest eyes, often looks a little overwhelmed but possesses an underlying spark of determination. Wears simple, comfortable clothes.
*   **Personality:** Kind-hearted, initially naive and a bit awkward, but quick to learn and adapt. Possesses an innate connection to temporal currents. Loyal to his friends.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Fuzzball Wielder:** Can amplify the temporal abilities of Zick and Zack.
    *   **Temporal Shield:** Instinctively created a shield to deflect attacks.
    *   **Time Twin Mastery:** Learns to create multiple, increasingly stable temporal duplicates of himself, capable of independent action and amplifying his abilities.
    *   **Enhanced Temporal Perception:** Develops the ability to anticipate movements and temporal shifts.
*   **Motivation:** Initially seeking adventure and a new life, later driven by a desire to protect his new friends and restore balance to the Land of Time.

---

**MEI-LING**
*   **Age:** 19
*   **Appearance:** Elegant, serene, with long black hair often adorned with delicate jade pins. Her eyes are warm honey-colored.
*   **Personality:** Calm, wise, commanding presence. Deeply caring for her sisters and Lucky. Possesses vast knowledge of Time Fu and the Land of Time.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Advanced Time Fu:** Master of localized time distortion, precise temporal manipulation, and creating intricate temporal constructs.
    *   **Strategic Mind:** Excellent tactician and planner.
*   **Role:** The eldest sister, leader of the harem, and eventually revealed as the Time Emperor's daughter. A mentor and protector for Lucky.

---

**LING-LI**
*   **Age:** 18
*   **Appearance:** Vivacious and playful, with a cascade of chestnut hair and eyes that sparkle with mischief.
*   **Personality:** Energetic, outgoing, and quick-witted. Can be mischievous but is fiercely loyal and protective.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Agile Time Fu:** Master of accelerated self-speed, weaving through temporal currents, and creating playful temporal nudges.
    *   **Social Charmer:** Able to extract information and build rapport easily.
*   **Role:** The energetic and fun-loving sister, often providing comic relief but also a formidable fighter.

---

**XI-XI**
*   **Age:** 17
*   **Appearance:** Shy and gentle, with dark braids interwoven with silk ribbons. Quiet and observant.
*   **Personality:** Introverted, kind, and possesses a quiet strength. Highly empathetic and intuitive.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Subtle Time Fu:** Master of localized temporal eddies, subtle temporal shifts, and creating temporal illusions.
    *   **Keen Observer:** Notices details others miss.
    *   **Meditative Focus:** Aids in guiding others to connect with temporal energy.
*   **Role:** The youngest sister, providing emotional support and subtle but effective temporal assistance.

---

**ZICK & ZACK (The Fuzzballs)**
*   **Appearance:** Zick (vibrant sapphire), Zack (calm emerald). Small, furry, sentient beings that resemble furballs.
*   **Personality:** Zick is nervous and energetic; Zack is calm and observant. Both are loyal and affectionate.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Pure Temporal Energy:** They are conduits and amplifiers of temporal energy.
    *   **Temporal Manipulation:** Can create small temporal bubbles, counter-currents, and bursts of temporal static.
    *   **Emotional Resonance:** Their colors and energy levels reflect Lucky's emotional state and the temporal environment.
*   **Role:** Lucky's companions, guides, and power amplifiers.

---

**ARIANNA**
*   **Age:** 20s
*   **Appearance:** Striking, with long silver hair and eyes like chips of ice. Wears a sleek, dark uniform.
*   **Personality:** Cold, authoritative, enigmatic, and seemingly devoid of emotion. Possesses a strange, indirect way of teaching and guiding.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Advanced Temporal Manipulation:** Can halt conflicts, induce unconsciousness with a touch, and create subtle temporal pushes.
    *   **Temporal Resonance:** Her voice carries temporal resonance.
*   **Role:** A mysterious figure who guides Lucky, but with her own hidden agenda. She is a catalyst for Lucky's growth.

---

**THE KEEPER**
*   **Age:** Ageless
*   **Appearance:** Wizened, ancient, with eyes that seem to hold the wisdom of millennia. Skin like ancient parchment.
*   **Personality:** Wise, patient, and speaks in resonant whispers.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Profound Temporal Knowledge:** Possesses vast knowledge of the Land of Time's history, artifacts, and temporal balance.
    *   **Temporal Scrutiny:** Can perceive and analyze temporal signatures.
*   **Role:** The ancient guardian of temporal balance, who tasks Lucky with retrieving the artifacts.

---

**CAPTAIN JIN**
*   **Age:** 40s
*   **Appearance:** Stern, unyielding, wears gleaming Imperial Guard armor.
*   **Personality:** Loyal to the Emperor, rigid, and by-the-book.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Imperial Guard Training:** Skilled in martial arts and wielding temporal staves.
*   **Role:** Leader of the Imperial Guards, tasked with apprehending Mei-Ling.

---

**THE RIVER GODDESS**
*   **Appearance:** Fluid, constantly shifting between a serene woman with water-like hair and a majestic Dragon-Bird with rainbow-colored scales.
*   **Personality:** Majestic, wise, and benevolent.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Elemental Temporal Control:** Can manipulate water and create temporal portals.
    *   **Guidance:** Offers cryptic but profound advice.
*   **Role:** Guardian of the first artifact, and a spiritual guide for Lucky.

---

**KAITO**
*   **Age:** 20s
*   **Appearance:** Lean, intense, with eyes that seem to track multiple timelines.
*   **Abilities:** Master of temporal displacement (rapid-fire temporal blinks, leaving afterimages).
*   **Role:** A competitor in the Time Fu Tournament.

---

**LADY LUNA**
*   **Age:** 20s
*   **Appearance:** Ethereal, graceful, with long silver hair shimmering with temporal energy.
*   **Abilities:** Manipulates ambient light, creates illusions, specializes in temporal perception and illusion.
*   **Role:** A competitor in the Time Fu Tournament, later an ally in the rebellion.

---

**MASTER WONG**
*   **Age:** 60s
*   **Appearance:** Jovial, with a booming laugh and surprisingly agile frame.
*   **Abilities:** Master of temporal reversal and flow (reversing water flow, etc.).
*   **Role:** A competitor in the Time Fu Tournament.

---

**ZHRA'GHA**
*   **Appearance:** Hulking, reptilian creature, scales shifting colors with temporal fluctuations.
*   **Abilities:** Creates small, localized temporal tremors, wields raw temporal force.
*   **Role:** A competitor in the Time Fu Tournament.

---

**GORTHOK**
*   **Appearance:** Towering, armored warrior, movements slow and deliberate.
*   **Abilities:** Absorbs temporal energy, making others feel sluggish; master of temporal inertia.
*   **Role:** A competitor in the Time Fu Tournament.

---

**LYSANDER**
*   **Age:** 20s
*   **Appearance:** Aristocratic, with an air of detached superiority, piercing blue eyes.
*   **Personality:** Calculating, cold, and possesses a dangerous mastery over time.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Temporal Acceleration/Deceleration:** Can rapidly age/de-age objects.
    *   **Temporal Causality Manipulation:** Manipulates the very fabric of cause and effect.
    *   **Architect of the Time Factory:** Designed and built the Time Factory.
*   **Role:** The Emperor's son, a temporal prodigy, and the primary antagonist.

---

**AKIRA**
*   **Age:** 18
*   **Appearance:** Fiery, with a competitive glint in her eyes, short black hair spiked with temporal energy.
*   **Abilities:** Creates miniature temporal vortexes, master of offensive temporal attacks.
*   **Role:** A competitor in the Time Fu Tournament, later an ally in the rebellion.

---

**MYSTA**
*   **Age:** 19
*   **Appearance:** Enigmatic, face partially obscured by a veil, movements fluid and unpredictable.
*   **Abilities:** Phases in and out of existence, leaves faint temporal echoes, specializes in temporal phasing and stealth.
*   **Role:** A competitor in the Time Fu Tournament, later an ally in the rebellion.

---

**RED WAX TIME POLICE**
*   **Appearance:** Figures in distinctive, crimson-colored armor, sleek, featureless helmets.
*   **Personality:** Initially robotic and by-the-book, later show hints of urgency and concern.
*   **Abilities:** Temporal suppression fields, ability to transport to the Soul Privy Head Storage Dimension.
*   **Role:** Enforcers of temporal law, who warn Lucky about the true nature of the tournament.

---

**THE EMPEROR**
*   **Appearance:** Ancient, regal, but with a cruel glint in his eyes.
*   **Personality:** Tyrannical, power-hungry, believes in absolute control over time.
*   **Abilities:** Amplified temporal abilities through the Time Factory.
*   **Role:** The tyrannical ruler of the Land of Time, and the ultimate antagonist.
