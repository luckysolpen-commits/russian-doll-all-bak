SCENE 9: ARIANNA'S ARRIVAL AND MEI-LING'S CAPTURE

[INT. TIME HOTEL - LUXURY SUITE - DAY]

The chaotic clash between the sisters and the Imperial Guards reaches a fever pitch. Just as Mei-Ling prepares a powerful temporal surge to incapacitate Captain Jin, a new presence enters the fray. The air in the suite suddenly grows heavy, a chilling stillness descending upon the temporal distortions.

A figure materializes in the doorway, seemingly out of thin air. ARIANNA (20s, striking, with an aura of cold authority, her long silver hair cascading down her back, eyes like chips of ice) surveys the scene with an unnerving calm. She wears a sleek, dark uniform that seems to absorb the light around her.

Her voice, though soft, cuts through the din of battle like a razor.

ARIANNA
Enough.

The single word, imbued with an unexpected temporal resonance, causes everyone to freeze. The guards, the sisters, even Lucky and the Fuzzballs, are momentarily paralyzed. The temporal distortions around them dissipate, leaving the suite in an eerie silence.

Arianna walks with deliberate, unhurried steps towards Mei-Ling. Captain Jin and the guards part for her, their expressions a mixture of fear and deference. Mei-Ling, though momentarily stunned, recovers quickly, her eyes narrowing in suspicion.

MEI-LING
Arianna. I should have known you would be involved.

Arianna offers no reply, her gaze unwavering. She reaches out a hand, her fingers, adorned with intricate temporal rings, glowing faintly. She gently touches Mei-Ling's forehead. There's no struggle, no resistance from Mei-Ling. Instead, a wave of profound exhaustion washes over her. Her eyes flutter, her body sways, and she slumps forward, unconscious, into Arianna's arms.

LING-LI
Mei-Ling!

Ling-Li and Xi-Xi rush forward, but Arianna, with a subtle temporal push, sends them stumbling back, unharmed but unable to reach their sister. The Imperial Guards immediately move in, carefully taking Mei-Ling from Arianna and securing her.

Arianna turns her icy gaze towards Lucky. He feels a prickle of fear, a sense of being utterly exposed. Her eyes seem to pierce through him, analyzing, assessing. Zick and Zack, usually so bold, shrink back, their colors dimming.

ARIANNA
(To Lucky, her voice devoid of emotion)
So, you are the Fuzzball wielder. The one the Keeper has been waiting for.

Lucky tries to speak, but no words come out. He watches helplessly as the guards, under Arianna's silent command, carry the unconscious Mei-Ling out of the suite. The doors close behind them, leaving Lucky, Ling-Li, and Xi-Xi in stunned silence, the echoes of the battle fading, replaced by a chilling sense of loss and foreboding.
