SCENE 15: THE RIVER GODDESS AND TOURNAMENT INVITATION

[EXT. LAKE OF ECHOES - DAY]

Lucky, holding the pulsating artifact, breaks the surface of the Lake of Echoes, gasping for air. The sunlight, previously filtered and ethereal, now feels warm and real on his skin. Mei-Ling, Ling-Li, and Xi-Xi rush to the water's edge, their faces alight with relief and pride.

MEI-LING
Lucky! You did it!

LING-LI
I knew you could!

XI-XI
(Smiling shyly)
You were amazing.

As Lucky swims towards them, a shimmering, iridescent light begins to emanate from the center of the lake. The water swirls, forming a vortex of pure energy. From its depths, a magnificent creature emerges. It is the RIVER GODDESS, a being of breathtaking beauty and power. Her form is fluid, constantly shifting between that of a serene woman with flowing, water-like hair, and a majestic DRAGON-BIRD, its scales shimmering with all the colors of the rainbow, its wings spanning the width of the lake.

Her voice, a melodic chorus of rushing water and wind chimes, echoes across the landscape.

RIVER GODDESS
(V.O.)
Well done, young one. You have faced your inner demons and claimed the first key to balance. Your mastery of the temporal currents is truly remarkable.

She extends a graceful, clawed hand towards Lucky. The artifact in his hand floats gently from his grasp and settles into her palm. It pulses brighter, merging with her essence.

RIVER GODDESS (CONT'D)
But this is merely the beginning. The true test of your abilities awaits. The Land of Time is on the precipice of a great shift, and only those with true temporal mastery can guide its destiny.

Her form shifts, becoming more distinctly the Dragon-Bird. She lowers her head, her eyes, like ancient pools, gazing directly into Lucky's.

RIVER GODDESS (CONT'D)
I invite you, Lucky, and your companions, to the Grand Time Fu Tournament. It is a gathering of the most skilled temporal practitioners from across all realms. There, you will be tested, challenged, and perhaps, find the answers you seek.

With a powerful beat of her wings, the River Goddess creates a shimmering portal of pure temporal energy. It swirls, revealing glimpses of a vast, ornate arena, filled with cheering crowds and crackling temporal energy.

RIVER GODDESS (CONT'D)
Step through, if you dare. Your journey continues.

Lucky looks at the portal, then back at the sisters. Mei-Ling nods, her eyes filled with encouragement. Ling-Li grins, her excitement palpable. Xi-Xi offers a gentle, reassuring smile. Zick and Zack, sensing the adventure, chirp excitedly.

Lucky takes a deep breath, the scent of the lake and the faint ozone from the portal filling his senses. He takes Mei-Ling's hand, then Ling-Li's, and Xi-Xi's. Together, they step into the shimmering portal, leaving the tranquil Lake of Echoes behind, and entering a new, exhilarating chapter of their journey.

[FADE TO WHITE]
