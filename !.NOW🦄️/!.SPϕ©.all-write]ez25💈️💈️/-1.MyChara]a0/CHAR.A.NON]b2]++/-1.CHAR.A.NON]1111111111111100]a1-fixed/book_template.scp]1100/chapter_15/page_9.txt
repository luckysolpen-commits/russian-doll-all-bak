a stark contrast to the raw, untamed wilderness outside. Chara felt a profound sense of
betrayal, not just by the Agency, but by the very system she had dedicated her life to protecting.

was a shifting sand and truth a fleeting illusion. The sun, now a fiery orb on the horizon,

about to be revealed in Chara’s own world. The truth, she knew, was often a bitter pill
to swallow, but Chara was ready to face it, to embrace the unknown, to carve her path through
the darkness. The desert was her ally, her confidante, her silent witness to the unfolding
drama.
