SCENE 1

INT. BRIEFING ROOM - DAY

SOUND of hushed murmurs, projector hum

DIMLY LIT. A long, polished mahogany table dominates the room. CHARA (30s, sharp, observant, with a quiet intensity), sits at one end, her posture impeccable. Across from her, GIANA NGUYEN (40s, Chara's superior, stern but with a hint of weary wisdom), stands beside a large holographic display.

GIANA
(Voice low, serious)
Alright, Chara. This isn't a milk run. Junior mission, yes, but the stakes are higher than your clearance suggests.

Chara nods, her gaze fixed on the shifting data on the display: cryptic symbols, blurred satellite images, and a single, stark phrase: "NEGATIVE ONE. NEGATIVE FOUR] LITTLE ENDIANS <>."

GIANA (CONT'D)
Our intel comes from ANON. Trusted source, but… unconventional. He’s flagged something big. Something that could destabilize the entire region if it gets out.

Chara raises an eyebrow, a silent question.

GIANA (CONT'D)
Your primary objective: transport a classified asset. From point A to point B. Simple on paper. In reality? You’ll be moving through Afrotta. Hostile territory. Subsistence-level stealth is required. No fireworks, Chara. No collateral.

Chara’s eyes narrow slightly. She understands the implications of "subsistence-level stealth" – minimal support, maximum self-reliance.

GIANA (CONT'D)
Insertion and extraction will be via helicopter, directly from Mothra Base. You’ll be back here, same helipad, when it’s done. Or… not.

Giana’s gaze holds Chara’s for a beat, a silent acknowledgment of the danger. Chara’s jaw tightens almost imperceptibly. She glances at the display again, a new image appearing: a desolate, sun-baked landscape, and a faint, almost imperceptible outline of an oil strut – Mothra Base.

FADE OUT.
