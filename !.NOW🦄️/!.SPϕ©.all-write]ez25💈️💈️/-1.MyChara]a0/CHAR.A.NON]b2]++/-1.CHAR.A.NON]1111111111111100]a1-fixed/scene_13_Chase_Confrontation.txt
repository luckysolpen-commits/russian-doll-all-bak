SCENE 13

EXT. AFROTTA - CHASE/CONFRONTATION - DAY

SOUND of engines roaring, gunfire, tires screeching on sand

The vast, open expanse of the Afrotta desert. A convoy of REBEL VEHICLES – battered, modified pickups and SUVs – speeds across the sand, kicking up massive plumes of dust. Inside one of the lead vehicles, CHARA, ANON, and ZARA are hunched over a tactical map, Zara pointing out routes.

ZARA
(Over the engine noise)
They're tightening the net. Government patrols, mercenary checkpoints. They know we're moving.

ANON
They're desperate. We've hit them where it hurts – their supply lines, their intel. They're reacting.

Suddenly, a series of EXPLOSIONS rock the ground behind them. Chara looks back. A line of heavily armed, sleek BLACK SUVS, clearly military-grade, are gaining on them, firing heavy machine guns.

CHARA
(Grabbing her rifle)
Company! Looks like our old friends from the village.

ZARA
(To her driver)
Faster! Head for the canyons! We can lose them in the terrain!

The rebel vehicles swerve, narrowly avoiding incoming fire. Dust and sand explode around them. Chara leans out the window, returning fire, targeting the lead mercenary vehicle's tires. One of the SUVs swerves wildly, then flips, tumbling in a cloud of dust and debris.

But more vehicles appear, seemingly out of nowhere, flanking them. The mercenaries are well-coordinated, relentless. They're not just chasing; they're herding.

ANON
(Pointing to a distant, jagged rock formation)
There! The Serpent's Tooth! A narrow pass. If we can make it through, we might shake them!

The rebel convoy pushes harder, engines screaming. The mercenaries are closing in, their machine gun fire tearing into the rebel vehicles. One of the rebel pickups takes a direct hit, spinning out of control.

Chara, seeing the danger, makes a snap decision. She grabs a grenade.

CHARA
(To Zara)
Cover me! I'm going to try and create a diversion!

Before Zara can protest, Chara opens the door and, with a practiced roll, drops out of the moving vehicle. She hits the sand, tucking and rolling, coming up in a crouch behind a small dune. The mercenary vehicles roar past, focused on the main convoy.

Chara quickly primes the grenade, then hurls it with surprising force towards a rocky overhang above the mercenary convoy. The EXPLOSION sends a shower of rocks and dust raining down, momentarily disorienting the mercenaries and forcing them to slow.

This buys the rebel convoy precious seconds. They surge forward, disappearing into the narrow, winding entrance of the Serpent's Tooth canyon.

Chara, now alone, is exposed. The mercenaries, realizing they've been outmaneuvered, turn their attention to her. The lead SUV screeches to a halt, MERCENARIES pouring out, weapons raised.

Chara doesn't run. She takes cover behind a large boulder, her rifle ready. She knows she can't take them all, but she can buy time. Time for Zara and Anon to escape.

She fires, picking off the closest threats. The mercenaries return fire, their bullets chipping away at her cover. She's pinned down, outnumbered.

Suddenly, a familiar voice over her comms.

GIANA (V.O.)
Chara! Do you read me? We have a window. Extraction inbound. Give us a target.

Chara smiles grimly. Giana. She knew she wouldn't be left behind.

CHARA
(Into comms, panting)
Negative extraction. I have a target for you, Giana. The lead mercenary vehicle. It's a black SUV, heavily armored. Take it out. And the rest will scatter.

GIANA (V.O.)
Are you sure, Chara? That's a high-risk shot. And you'll be exposed.

CHARA
(Looking at the advancing mercenaries, a fierce determination in her eyes)
Do it, Giana. For Klondike. For everything.

She lays down a final burst of suppressive fire, drawing the mercenaries' attention. Then, she drops her rifle, pulls out her sidearm, and charges directly towards the lead SUV, drawing their fire.

From the sky, a CIA DRONE, sleek and silent, streaks overhead. A targeting laser locks onto the mercenary SUV. A MISSILE launches, a fiery streak across the sky.

The SUV EXPLODES in a fiery inferno, sending debris and mercenaries flying. The remaining mercenary vehicles screech to a halt, their occupants stunned, disoriented.

Chara, thrown back by the blast, lands hard. She looks up, seeing the remaining mercenaries hesitate, then turn and flee, their morale broken. The desert is silent once more, save for the crackling of the burning wreckage.

She pushes herself up, bruised but alive. The Serpent's Tooth canyon yawns before her, a path to safety. She glances back at the burning wreckage, a grim satisfaction settling over her. One less piece of the conspiracy.

FADE OUT.
