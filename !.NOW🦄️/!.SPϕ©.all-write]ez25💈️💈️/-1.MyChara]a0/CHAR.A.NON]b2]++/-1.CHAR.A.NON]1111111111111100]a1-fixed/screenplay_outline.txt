Title: The Shadow of the Crow: CHARANON
Logline: A skilled CIA operative, Chara, embarks on a junior mission to transport a classified asset across a treacherous African desert, only to uncover a deep-seated conspiracy, the shocking return and death of a legendary agent, and a personal betrayal that forces her to confront her past and fight for the truth.

Characters:
- Chara: Skilled CIA operative, protagonist.
- Giana Nguyen: Chara's superior.
- ANON: Trusted CIA informant, mysterious figure.
- Agent Klondike: Legendary agent, presumed dead.

Setting:
- Mothra Base: Oil strut, CIA outpost.
- Afrotta: Remote desert region in Africa.

--- SCREENPLAY OUTLINE ---

ACT I: THE MISSION

Scene 1: INT. BRIEFING ROOM - DAY
- Chara is briefed by Giana Nguyen on a high-priority junior CIA mission: "Negative One. Negative Four] LITTLE ENDIANS <>".
- The mission involves an investigation from a trusted CIA informant, ANON.
- Chara learns she must transport a valuable, classified asset across Afrotta, with subsistence-level stealth required.
- The mission starts and ends at Mothra Base via helicopter.

Scene 2: EXT. MOTHRA BASE - HELIPAD - DAY
- Chara boards a helicopter, preparing for insertion into Afrotta.
- The journey is tense, highlighting Chara's skills as they navigate treacherous terrain.

Scene 3: EXT. AFROTTA - VILLAGE OUTSKIRTS - DAY
- Helicopter insertion into Afrotta, near a small, dusty village.
- Chara and her team meet a grizzled local contact.
- The contact reveals a mercenary group is attempting to intercept the asset.

Scene 4: EXT. AFROTTA - MERCENARY AMBUSH - DAY
- Chara engages the mercenaries, showcasing her combat and tactical skills.
- She successfully neutralizes the threat, but the encounter leaves her unsettled.
- A fleeting thought of a mysterious figure from her past crosses her mind.

ACT II: THE REVELATION AND THE FALL

Scene 5: EXT. AFROTTA - ISOLATED VILLAGE - DAY
- Chara and her team reach a remote village, the final checkpoint.
- The villagers are unusually quiet, huddled together, creating an eerie atmosphere.
- A mysterious woman emerges from the shadows – ANON.
- ANON reveals the shocking truth: the "asset" is Agent Klondike, long presumed dead.

Scene 6: EXT. AFROTTA - VILLAGE BATTLE - DAY
- A massive mercenary force attacks, overwhelming Chara and her team.
- Klondike is fatally wounded during the chaotic battle.
- Chara fights desperately, but is wounded and defeated, witnessing Klondike's death.
- The mission collapses into a catastrophic failure.

Scene 7: EXT. AFROTTA - RETREAT/EXTRACTION - DAY/NIGHT
- Chara, wounded and grieving, makes a desperate escape or is extracted from the battlefield.
- The journey back to Mothra Base is somber and reflective.

ACT III: THE AFTERMATH AND THE TRUTH

Scene 8: INT. HELICOPTER/PLANE - NIGHT
- Chara begins writing her debriefing report, struggling with the emotional weight of Klondike's death and the mission's failure.
- She feels detached but also deeply affected by the events.

Scene 9: INT. CIA HEADQUARTERS - CHARA'S OFFICE - DAY
- Weeks later, Chara is assigned desk work, struggling with grief and regret.
- She feels a profound emptiness but is driven by a need to uncover the truth.

Scene 10: INT. CIA HEADQUARTERS - ARCHIVES/SECURE ROOM - DAY
- Chara discreetly begins her own investigation into Klondike's death and the mission's true nature.
- She uncovers inconsistencies or cryptic intel, hinting at a deeper conspiracy.

Scene 11: INT./EXT. CLANDESTINE MEETING LOCATION - NIGHT
- ANON reappears, providing more direct clues or involving Chara in a new, clandestine agenda.
- This encounter forces Chara to confront a past betrayal or a hidden truth about her own history.

Scene 12: EXT. AFROTTA - REBEL BASE - DAY
- Chara, following leads, makes contact with a rebel group in Afrotta.
- She forms an uneasy alliance, realizing they possess crucial information or resources.

Scene 13: EXT. AFROTTA - CHASE/CONFRONTATION - DAY
- The government's pursuit intensifies, forcing Chara and the rebels into a desperate evasion.
- They clash with the rival mercenary group, gaining key intel about the conspiracy.

Scene 14: INT. CONSPIRACY HEADQUARTERS - DAY/NIGHT
- Chara uncovers the true purpose of the "asset" and its connection to a larger global conflict.
- She confronts the mastermind behind the conspiracy, leading to a climactic battle.

Scene 15: EXT. MOTHRA BASE - HELIPAD - DAY
- Chara returns to Mothra Base, having achieved a form of resolution.
- She reflects on her journey, the sacrifices, and her transformed understanding of loyalty and truth.
- Chara looks towards a new mission, a more seasoned operative.
