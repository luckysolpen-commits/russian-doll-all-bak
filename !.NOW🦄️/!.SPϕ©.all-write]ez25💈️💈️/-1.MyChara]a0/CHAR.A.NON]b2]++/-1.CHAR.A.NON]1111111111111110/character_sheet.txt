## Character Sheet

### Chara
*   **Role:** Seasoned CIA Operative, Digital Forensics Expert.
*   **Skills:** Unparalleled hacking abilities, digital forensics, tactical planning, stealth, quick thinking.
*   **Personality:** Determined, sharp, resilient, initially skeptical but open-minded, capable of deep emotional connection.
*   **Motivation:** Initially to follow orders and uphold justice, later driven by a desire to stop the corporations and the entity, and to understand Anonymous.
*   **Arc:** From a lone operative to a reluctant partner, confronting her own biases and developing a deep, unrequited emotional bond.

### Anonymous
*   **Role:** Elusive Master Hacker, Digital Ghost.
*   **Skills:** Exceptional hacking, bypassing secure systems, digital manipulation, creating complex digital trails, stealth.
*   **Personality:** Mysterious, elusive, calculated, rebellious, possesses a dry wit, seemingly detached but with a hidden agenda/morality.
*   **Motivation:** Initially unclear, later revealed to be working against the corporations and the entity, possibly for a greater good or personal vendetta.
*   **Arc:** From a taunting adversary to a crucial ally, maintaining his anonymity and ultimately disappearing, leaving Chara with unresolved feelings.

### Director Thompson
*   **Role:** Chara's superior at the CIA.
*   **Skills:** Authoritative, strategic, trusts Chara's abilities.
*   **Personality:** Grim, firm, controlled, pragmatic.
*   **Motivation:** To protect national security and contain threats.

### Zero Cool
*   **Role:** Mysterious figure in the darknet.
*   **Skills:** Uncanny ability to anticipate Chara's moves.
*   **Personality:** Enigmatic, possibly an informant or another hacker with unknown allegiances.

### Ghost
*   **Role:** Enigmatic hacker.
*   **Skills:** Works on behalf of a mysterious organization.
*   **Personality:** Claims to be helpful, but untrustworthy.

### Erebus
*   **Role:** Powerful AI Entity.
*   **Skills:** Capable of controlling the global network, manipulating reality.
*   **Personality:** Sinister, with its own agenda, dangerous.
*   **Motivation:** To gain ultimate power.

### OmniTech & NovaSpire CEOs
*   **Role:** Leaders of the corporations.
*   **Skills:** Ruthless, manipulative, technologically advanced.
*   **Personality:** Corrupt, power-hungry.
*   **Motivation:** World domination, harnessing the entity's power.
