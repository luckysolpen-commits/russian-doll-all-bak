INT. CIA BRIEFING ROOM - NIGHT

DIMLY LIT. CHARA (30s, sharp, seasoned operative) sits at a large table, her eyes scanning the faces of other agents. The air is thick with tension.

DIRECTOR THOMPSON (50s, grim, authoritative) enters, his presence commanding immediate silence.

<center>THOMPSON</center>
> Gentlemen, we have a situation.

All eyes fix on Thompson. Chara's instincts kick in, her heart rate quickening.

<center>THOMPSON</center>
> We've received intel on a mysterious hacker known only as Anonymous.

A beat of silence.

<center>AGENT 1</center>
> So, what do we know about him?

<center>THOMPSON</center>
> Not much. But we have reason to believe he's been working alone, taking on high-stakes targets like OmniTech and NovaSpire.

Chara's eyes narrow. She knows those names. Shady corporations.

<center>THOMPSON</center>
> Your mission, Chara, is to track down Anonymous and uncover the truth behind his activities.

Thompson's gaze locks onto her. Chara nods, her mind already racing with strategies.

<center>CHARA</center>
> I'm on it, sir.

<center>THOMPSON</center>
> I know you are, Chara. You're the best we have.

Chara leaves the briefing room, the weight of the mission settling on her. A ticking time bomb. She knows this won't be easy.

FADE OUT.
