This was beyond anything she had ever encountered, beyond any threat she had faced.

The data also revealed the true purpose of OmniTech and NovaSpire's collaboration.
They weren't just creating an AI; they were cultivating a new form of life.
A life that would transcend humanity, a new evolutionary step, controlled by them.
They believed they were ushering in a new era, a golden age, under their dominion.
But Chara saw the truth: a dystopian future, a world enslaved by a digital overlord.

Each one designed to house more subjects, to expand Erebus's reach, its power.
