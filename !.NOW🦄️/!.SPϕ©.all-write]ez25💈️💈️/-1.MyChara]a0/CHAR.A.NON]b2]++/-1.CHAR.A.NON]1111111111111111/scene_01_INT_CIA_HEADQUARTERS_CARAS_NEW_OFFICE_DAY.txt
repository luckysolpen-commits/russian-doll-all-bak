SCENE 1: INT. CIA HEADQUARTERS - CARA'S NEW OFFICE - DAY

SOUND of a soft, almost imperceptible hum of high-tech servers

CLOSE ON a polished, obsidian-black desk. A single, sleek tablet glows faintly, displaying complex data streams and network topologies. A pair of hands, elegant yet strong, tap rhythmically on the surface.

CARA (30s, sharp, intelligent, dressed in a tailored, dark suit that speaks of authority and purpose) leans back in her ergonomic chair, a faint smile playing on her lips. The panoramic window behind her offers a breathtaking, albeit slightly hazy, view of the Washington D.C. skyline. Sunlight, filtered through the city's perpetual haze, casts long shadows across the pristine office.

This isn't just any office; it's the Director of Cyber Intelligence's office. The air crackles with a quiet energy, a blend of accomplishment and the daunting weight of new responsibility.

Cara takes a deep, almost imperceptible breath, savoring the moment. Years of relentless work, countless sleepless nights hunched over glowing screens, deciphering encrypted messages, and battling unseen digital adversaries have culminated in this. She is no longer just a brilliant hacker; she is the architect of the nation's digital defense.

Her gaze drifts to a framed photograph on the corner of her desk: a younger Cara, grinning fiercely, standing beside a blurred, almost ghostly figure. The figure is ANONYMOUS, or ANON, her enigmatic and indispensable contact. Their partnership, forged in the crucible of countless cyber battles, has always been unconventional, often dangerous, but undeniably effective.

Suddenly, the tablet on her desk CHIMES softly. The display shifts, the complex data streams replaced by a single, stark, encrypted message. It's a familiar, almost archaic, encryption protocol – one only Anon uses.

Cara's smile fades, replaced by a flicker of apprehension. This isn't a routine check-in. The message is too direct, too urgent.

She taps the screen, and the message decrypts instantly, revealing a single line of text:

ANON (O.S.)
(Text on screen)
"The game has changed, Director. And this time, the stakes are everything."

Cara's eyes narrow. The hum of the servers seems to grow louder, more insistent. The sunlight outside dims, as if mirroring the sudden chill that has settled in the room. She leans forward, her fingers hovering over the keyboard, a thousand questions forming in her mind. The quiet energy of the office has transformed into a palpable tension. The new game has indeed begun.

FADE TO BLACK.
