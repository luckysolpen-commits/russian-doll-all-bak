SCENE 2: INT. CIA CYBER INTELLIGENCE LAB - DAY

SOUND of frantic keyboard clatter, hushed urgent conversations, distant sirens

INT. CIA CYBER INTELLIGENCE LAB - DAY

The lab is a hive of controlled chaos. Rows of monitors display cascading lines of code, network maps, and alarming red alerts. AGENT THOMPSON (40s, grizzled, experienced, a veteran of countless cyber wars) paces anxiously, barking orders into a headset.

DR. PATEL (50s, brilliant, calm under pressure, her glasses perched on her nose as she meticulously analyzes data) points to a rapidly expanding red zone on a global network map.

DR. PATEL
(To Thompson, voice strained)
It's spreading faster than we anticipated, Agent. Multiple financial institutions, critical infrastructure… the attack vectors are unprecedented.

Thompson curses under his breath, running a hand through his thinning hair. He glances at a large screen displaying a news ticker: "GLOBAL CYBER ATTACK: UNPRECEDENTED DISRUPTION."

THOMPSON
(Into headset)
I need an update on the source! Anything? Is it state-sponsored? Terrorist group?

VOICE (O.S.)
(From headset, tinny)
Negative, sir. Signatures are… anomalous. Nothing we've seen before. It's like a ghost in the machine.

Cara enters the lab, her expression grim. The hum of the servers from her office now feels like a roar here. She takes in the scene, her eyes quickly assessing the damage.

CARA
(Voice calm, authoritative)
Status report, Agent Thompson. Dr. Patel.

Thompson snaps to attention, momentarily forgetting his headset.

THOMPSON
Director Cara. It's bad. We're seeing coordinated attacks across multiple sectors. Financial, energy grids, even some government databases. The scale is… global.

Dr. Patel turns from her monitor, her face etched with concern.

DR. PATEL
And the most disturbing part, Director, is the common thread. Every compromised system, every single one, had recent access to the 'Project Nightingale' database.

Cara's brow furrows. Project Nightingale. She knows the name. A highly classified, obscure research initiative.

CARA
Project Nightingale? What is that, exactly?

Thompson hesitates, exchanging a glance with Dr. Patel.

THOMPSON
(Reluctantly)
It's… sensitive, Director. A black-book operation. It involves… monitoring and analyzing anomalous aerial phenomena. UFO sightings, essentially.

Cara's eyes widen, a sudden, chilling realization dawning on her. Anon's warning. "The game has changed." The cryptic message. It all clicks into place with a terrifying precision.

CARA
(Quietly, almost to herself)
UFOs.

She looks at the frantic activity around her, the glowing screens, the worried faces. The cyber attack isn't just about data. It's about something far, far stranger.

FADE OUT.
