INT. MECH FACILITY - DAY/NIGHT

The air inside the facility is a cacophony of metallic clangs, the hum of machinery, and the distant shouts of PMC guards. CHARA moves like a phantom through the sprawling complex, a stark contrast to the heavy-footed patrols.

She slips through a barely ajar service door, entering a dimly lit CORRIDOR. Pipes crisscross the ceiling, dripping condensation onto the concrete floor. The smell of oil and ozone hangs heavy in the air.

Chara presses herself against a wall as two heavily armed PMC GUARDS round a corner, their conversation muffled by their helmets. She waits, motionless, until they pass, then continues, her movements economical and precise.

She finds a ventilation shaft, its grate loosely secured. With a practiced hand, she pries it open, the metal groaning softly. She slides inside, the confined space echoing with her quiet movements. The shaft leads her above a large, open BAY, where several partially assembled MECHS stand like skeletal giants. Their designs are menacing, clearly built for combat, not construction.

Chara observes from her vantage point. Engineers in lab coats move around the mechs, making adjustments. PMC officers review schematics on holographic displays. She notes the weapon systems, the armor plating, the sheer destructive potential. This is indeed a game-changer, just as ANON had warned.

She continues through the ventilation system, navigating a labyrinth of ducts and vents. She overhears snippets of conversation – talk of accelerated production, new energy sources, and a looming deadline. The urgency of her mission intensifies.

She drops silently into a maintenance area, narrowly avoiding a patrolling guard. She uses a small, handheld EMP device, discreetly disabling a security camera before it can register her presence. The red light on the camera flickers, then dies.

She moves deeper into the facility, past barracks, armories, and control rooms. Each step is calculated, each shadow a potential hiding place. She is a ghost in the machine, gathering intelligence, mapping the enemy's stronghold, and preparing for the next phase of her mission.

She spots a heavily reinforced door, marked with a symbol she recognizes from ANON's schematics: a data storage vault. Her next target.
