# Worldbuilding: My Chara: CHARANON

## Locations:

*   **Mothra Base:** An isolated oil strut in the African jungle, serving as a clandestine CIA forward operating base. It's functional, metallic, and exposed to the elements, a stark contrast to the lush jungle surrounding it. It features a helipad, living quarters, a debriefing room, and training facilities. It's a place of both intense work and unexpected camaraderie.

*   **African Jungle:** Dense, humid, and treacherous. It's a character in itself, providing cover, obstacles, and a sense of isolation. It's home to hidden facilities and serves as a challenging environment for infiltration and escape.

*   **Rogue Mech Facility:** A sprawling, heavily guarded complex hidden deep within the jungle. It's a manufacturing and research hub for advanced combat mechs, characterized by metallic clangs, humming machinery, and a sterile, industrial atmosphere. It contains server rooms, assembly bays, and various security measures.

*   **Langley, CIA Headquarters:** The central hub of the CIA, a stark, bureaucratic complex of concrete and glass in Virginia. It represents the administrative and analytical side of intelligence work, a stark contrast to the field operations.

*   **Small Airfield:** A remote, dusty airstrip serving as a transfer point between field operations and larger transport.

## Technology & Equipment:

*   **Advanced Combat Mechs:** The primary threat, these are large, heavily armed robotic war machines. The "BEHEMOTH" is a particularly advanced and terrifying model, indicating a significant leap in military technology.

*   **Suppressed Firearms:** Standard issue for stealth operations, allowing for silent eliminations.

*   **Anti-Materiel Rifle:** A powerful sniper rifle capable of penetrating heavy armor, used by Agent Klondike.

*   **Rocket Launcher:** A destructive weapon used for breaching and eliminating fortified targets.

*   **Portable Data Drive:** A secure device for downloading and storing critical intelligence.

*   **Encrypted Tablet:** Used for mapping, communication, and accessing secure data in the field.

*   **Comms Device (Earpiece):** Standard communication tool for field agents.

*   **Specialized EMP Device:** A compact electronic device capable of temporarily disabling electronic systems like security cameras and laser grids.

*   **Stealth Suit (Advanced):** Implied to be part of Chara's standard gear, allowing for exceptional stealth capabilities.

*   **Holographic Displays:** Used in the mech facility and debriefing room for displaying schematics and data.

## Organizations:

*   **CIA (Central Intelligence Agency):** The primary intelligence agency, responsible for gathering, processing, and analyzing national security information from around the world.

*   **PMC (Private Military Company):** Mercenary forces employed by the rogue organization to guard their facilities and operations.

*   **Cerberus:** A shadowy, global organization operating behind the scenes, funding insurgencies and destabilizing regions to create a market for their advanced weaponry, particularly the mechs. They are the overarching antagonist.

## Other Elements:

*   **Green Cigars:** A recurring motif, symbolizing moments of relaxation and camaraderie among the agents.

*   **Alcohol:** Used as a means of unwinding and fostering bonding among the team.

*   **Sniper Fire:** A key plot device that initiates the compromise of the stealth mission and leads to Giana's injury and Chara's solo mission.

*   **Oil Strut Environment:** Provides a unique and isolated setting for the base, emphasizing the clandestine nature of their operations.
