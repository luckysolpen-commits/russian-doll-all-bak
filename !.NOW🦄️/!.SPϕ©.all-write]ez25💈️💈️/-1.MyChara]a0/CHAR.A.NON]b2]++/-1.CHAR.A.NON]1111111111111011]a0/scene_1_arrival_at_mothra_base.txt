INT. HELICOPTER - DAY

The rhythmic THUMP-THUMP-THUMP of rotor blades fills the small cabin. Below, a vibrant tapestry of AFRICAN JUNGLE stretches to the horizon, an endless sea of green.

CHARA (30s, sharp, focused, dressed in tactical gear) sits opposite GIANA NGUYEN (40s, calm, experienced, Chara's superior). Giana sips from a canteen, her eyes scanning the dense canopy.

<center>GIANA</center>
> Another day, another jungle. You ever get tired of the humidity, Chara?

Chara offers a faint, almost imperceptible smile.

<center>CHARA</center>
> It's better than a desk job, Giana. And the air conditioning at Langley is always on the fritz.

Giana chuckles softly.

<center>GIANA</center>
> Can't argue with that. Still, this one feels... different. ANON's usually not this cagey.

<center>CHARA</center>
> Top-priority intel, he said. And a "game-changer." Sounds like he's got something big.

<center>GIANA</center>
> Let's hope it's not another wild goose chase. My feet are starting to ache just thinking about it.

Chara checks the action on her suppressed pistol, then holsters it. Her gaze drifts to the window, a flicker of anticipation in her eyes.

<center>CHARA</center>
> We'll find out soon enough.

EXT. MOTHRA BASE - DAY

The helicopter descends, a powerful gust of wind rippling through the jungle trees. Below, a sprawling OIL STRUT, "MOTHRA BASE," rises from the ocean, a metallic island amidst the vast green. It's a functional, no-frills outpost, a collection of platforms, cranes, and a helipad.

The helicopter touches down with a gentle thud. The rotors begin to slow, their thumping fading to a low hum.

Chara and Giana disembark, their boots clanking on the metal grating of the helipad. Chara's eyes immediately sweep the perimeter, taking in the sparse security, the distant glint of the ocean, the oppressive heat. Giana takes a deep breath, the scent of salt and damp earth filling her lungs.

A figure emerges from a nearby structure, moving with a quiet confidence. This is ANON (50s, lean, dressed in nondescript clothing, his face obscured by dark sunglasses). He carries a worn leather folder.

<center>ANON</center>
> Chara. Giana. Good to see you both.

His voice is low, gravelly, and carries an undeniable authority. Chara's hand instinctively hovers near her holstered weapon. Giana nods, her expression serious.

<center>CHARA</center>
> ANON. What's the situation?

ANON opens the folder, revealing a few grainy satellite images and some cryptic schematics.

<center>ANON</center>
> Rogue mech facility. Hidden deep in the jungle, about fifty klicks inland. Intel suggests they're developing new tech. Something that could give them a significant advantage on the battlefield.

Chara's eyes narrow, studying the images.

<center>CHARA</center>
> What makes you think this is legit?

ANON's lips curl into a faint, almost imperceptible smile behind his sunglasses.

<center>ANON</center>
> I have it on good authority. From within the enemy ranks. This isn't a drill, Chara. This is a game-changer.

Giana's gaze is fixed on the schematics.

<center>GIANA</center>
> We need to get in, gather intel, and take out that facility. Can we count on your team for support?

ANON closes the folder, his eyes, though hidden, seem to bore into Chara.

<center>ANON</center>
> You'll have what you need. But I want something from you, Chara.

<center>CHARA</center>
> What do you want?

<center>ANON</center>
> Stealthy insertion. No loud noises, no alarms. We need to get in and out without being detected.

Chara holds his gaze, a silent understanding passing between them. She nods, her mind already racing, formulating a plan. The mission has begun.
