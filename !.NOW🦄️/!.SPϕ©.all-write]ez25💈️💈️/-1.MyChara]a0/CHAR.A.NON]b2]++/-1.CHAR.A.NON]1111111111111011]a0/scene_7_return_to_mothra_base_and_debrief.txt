EXT. MOTHRA BASE - DAY

The familiar silhouette of the oil strut, Mothra Base, rises from the shimmering ocean. A helicopter, smaller and more agile than the one that brought her here, descends towards the helipad. Inside, CHARA, looking weary but resolute, gazes out at the approaching platform.

She disembarks, her movements stiff but purposeful. Waiting for her are GIANA NGUYEN, her leg bandaged but her stance firm, and AGENT KLONDIKE, his usual stoic demeanor softened by a hint of relief. ANON stands slightly apart, his sunglasses still obscuring his eyes, but a subtle nod acknowledges her arrival.

<center>GIANA</center>
> Chara! You made it. We were starting to worry.

<center>CHARA</center>
> I had a job to do.

Klondike offers a rare, almost imperceptible smile.

<center>KLONDIKE</center>
> You certainly did. Heard the fireworks from here.

INT. DEBRIEFING ROOM - MOTHRA BASE - DAY

The debriefing room is spartan: a large table, a few chairs, and a holographic projector. Chara, Giana, Klondike, and ANON are gathered. The air is thick with anticipation.

Chara plugs her data drive into the projector. Images and schematics flash across the screen: detailed blueprints of the "BEHEMOTH" mech, financial ledgers detailing illicit arms deals, communication intercepts revealing a vast, interconnected network of rogue elements.

<center>CHARA</center>
> The facility was a front. A manufacturing hub for a new generation of combat mechs. And this... (she gestures to the image of the Behemoth) ...is their crown jewel. Far more advanced than anything we've seen.

<center>ANON</center>
> (His voice grave)
> As I suspected. Cerberus. They're escalating. This data confirms their global reach.

<center>GIANA</center>
> Global reach? What are we looking at, ANON?

<center>ANON</center>
> A shadow organization, Giana. Funding insurgencies, destabilizing regions, all to create a market for their weapons. The mech facility was just one piece of a much larger puzzle.

Klondike, usually silent, speaks up.

<center>KLONDIKE</center>
> So, the sniper, the ambush... they knew you were coming.

<center>CHARA</center>
> They were waiting. But they didn't get the data.

ANON nods slowly, a flicker of satisfaction crossing his face.

<center>ANON</center>
> No, they didn't. This intelligence... it changes everything. It gives us a clear picture of their operations, their key players.

<center>GIANA</center>
> What's the next step?

ANON looks at Chara, a silent acknowledgment of her success.

<center>ANON</center>
> We analyze this data. We identify their weaknesses. And then, we hit them where it hurts. Hard.

Chara feels a surge of purpose. The exhaustion is still there, but it's overshadowed by the weight of the new mission. The war against Cerberus has just begun, and she's on the front lines.
