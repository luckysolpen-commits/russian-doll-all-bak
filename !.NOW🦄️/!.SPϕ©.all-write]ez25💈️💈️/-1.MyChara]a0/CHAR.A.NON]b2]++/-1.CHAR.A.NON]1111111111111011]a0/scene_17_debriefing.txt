INT. MOTHRA BASE - DEBRIEFING ROOM - DAY

The debriefing room at Mothra Base. The atmosphere is tense, but there's an underlying current of relief. CHARA, though still bearing the marks of her ordeal, sits upright, her eyes sharp. GIANA NGUYEN, fully recovered, sits beside her, a supportive presence. AGENT KLONDIKE, having piloted the extraction, stands by the holographic projector. ANON is present, his sunglasses still in place, but his posture suggests deep engagement.

Chara plugs in her data drive and the encrypted chip from the lead scientist. Images and data flood the holographic display: the new energy source, the global automated defense network schematics, and the chilling details of Cerberus's ultimate plan for total global dominance.

<center>CHARA</center>
> The Eastern European facility wasn't just a research lab. It was their command center for the global defense network. And the lead scientist... he was developing a new energy source to power it. Enough to bring the entire system online simultaneously.

<center>ANON</center>
> (His voice low, almost a whisper)
> Total global dominance. They weren't just selling weapons. They were building an empire.

<center>GIANA</center>
> And the Behemoth mechs were just the enforcers. This network... it's a digital iron curtain.

<center>KLONDIKE</center>
> (Pointing to a schematic of the prototype mech)
> This little beauty almost took us down. Fast, agile, and that energy cannon packs a punch.

<center>CHARA</center>
> It was the lead scientist's personal project. Designed for pursuit. I managed to get the data chip from him before I left.

ANON leans forward, a rare gesture of intensity.

<center>ANON</center>
> This intelligence, Chara, is beyond invaluable. It gives us the full scope of Cerberus's ambition. Their infrastructure, their technology, their timeline. We can finally dismantle them, piece by piece.

Suddenly, a COMM VOICE crackles over the speaker, cutting through the room's tension.

<center>COMM VOICE (O.S.)</center>
> Mission Control to Mothra Base. Urgent update regarding global situation. Satellite intelligence indicates increased Cerberus activity in multiple key regions. We're seeing coordinated movements, likely in response to the Eastern European facility's compromise.

ANON's head snaps up. He looks at Chara, a silent understanding passing between them. The fight is far from over. In fact, it's just beginning.

<center>ANON</center>
> (To Mission Control)
> Understood. We have new intelligence that will be critical to your assessment. Prepare for immediate data transfer.

He turns back to the team, his hidden eyes conveying a grim determination.

<center>ANON</center>
> This data gives us the advantage. But Cerberus is reacting. We need to move fast. The world is counting on us.

Chara nods, her exhaustion momentarily forgotten. The stakes have never been higher. The debriefing is over, but the real work has just begun.
