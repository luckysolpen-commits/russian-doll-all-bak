INT./EXT. EASTERN EUROPEAN FACILITY - NIGHT

Alarms blare, a piercing, insistent WAIL echoing through the metallic corridors. CHARA moves with a desperate urgency, the data chip secured, her own data drive hot in her hand. The facility, once a maze of shadows, is now a hostile, brightly lit trap.

She sprints through a series of interconnected labs, dodging laser fire from automated turrets that have now activated. She uses her EMP device, but the turrets are more resilient than the cameras. She manages to disable one, but the others continue to track her.

She bursts into a large, open hangar bay. Several of the new, smaller mechs are being prepped for deployment. PMC SOLDIERS are scrambling, shouting orders, their weapons raised. Chara is outnumbered, outgunned, but not outmatched.

She fires a series of precise shots, targeting the mechs' optical sensors, temporarily blinding them. She then uses the chaos to her advantage, weaving through the melee, engaging in brutal, close-quarters combat when necessary. She disarms a soldier, uses his weapon against another, then discards it, preferring her own suppressed pistol.

She spots an emergency exit, a heavy blast door leading to the outside. It's heavily guarded. She throws a flashbang, momentarily stunning the guards, then charges, a whirlwind of motion. She takes them down with swift, incapacitating strikes, then slams her hand on the emergency release.

The blast door GRINDS open, revealing the frigid, snow-swept mountain night. She bursts out, the cold air a shock to her lungs.

EXT. EASTERN EUROPEAN MOUNTAINS - NIGHT

Chara runs, her boots crunching on the snow. Searchlights from the facility sweep the mountainside. The WHIRR of approaching helicopters fills the air. They're sending everything after her.

She scrambles up a steep, icy incline, her specialized climbing gear digging into the frozen earth. She reaches a precarious ledge, looking down at the vast, dark chasm below. No way out.

Suddenly, a familiar THUMP-THUMP-THUMP. A helicopter, larger than the facility's, appears from behind a peak. It's a friendly extraction. AGENT KLONDIKE is at the controls, his face grimly determined.

But before she can reach it, a new, smaller MECH, a prototype, leaps from the facility, its powerful legs propelling it across the snow. It's fast, agile, and armed with a devastating energy cannon. It's the lead scientist's personal project, designed for pursuit.

> Chara! Get to the rope! I'll handle this!

Chara sees a grappling hook shoot from the helicopter, landing near her. She grabs it, securing it to her harness. As she begins to ascend, the prototype mech fires its energy cannon. The blast narrowly misses the helicopter, sending a shower of ice and rock.

Klondike maneuvers the helicopter with incredible skill, dodging the mech's attacks. He fires a volley of missiles, but the mech is too fast, too agile. It leaps onto a nearby peak, preparing another attack.

Chara reaches the helicopter, pulling herself inside. She looks back at Klondike, then at the pursuing mech. This isn't over.
