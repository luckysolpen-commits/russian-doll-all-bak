EXT. EASTERN EUROPEAN MOUNTAINS - DAY

The landscape is stark, a dramatic contrast to the lush African jungle. Jagged, snow-capped peaks pierce a grey sky. The wind howls, carrying the bite of ice. CHARA, now clad in specialized cold-weather gear, moves through the treacherous terrain, her breath misting in the frigid air.

She scales a sheer rock face, her specialized climbing gear biting into the ice. Below, a vast, desolate valley stretches out, dotted with the occasional, ominous-looking structure. This is Cerberus's new stronghold.

She reaches a high ridge, pulling out her binoculars. Below, nestled within a natural rock formation, is the hidden facility. It's a formidable fortress, camouflaged against the mountainside, with reinforced walls, watchtowers, and a network of underground tunnels. Heavy vehicles move in and out of a large, blast-proof entrance.

Chara studies the facility, noting patrol routes, blind spots, and potential entry points. The intel from ANON was precise. This place is a hive of activity.

INT. EASTERN EUROPEAN FACILITY - NIGHT

Chara has infiltrated the facility. The interior is cold, metallic, and highly organized. The air hums with the sound of machinery and the low murmur of voices. She moves through a network of corridors, her stealth suit blending seamlessly with the shadows.

She encounters ELITE MERCENARIES, their gear more advanced, their movements more disciplined than the PMC forces she faced in Africa. They are Cerberus's best. Chara uses her exceptional stealth, slipping past them, sometimes inches away, her presence undetected.

She observes a research lab, where SCIENTISTS in sterile suits work on what appear to be advanced weapon systems. Holographic displays show complex energy signatures and schematics for new, devastating weaponry. This is not just about mechs anymore.

She navigates a series of laser grids and motion sensors, using her EMP device with surgical precision. The facility is a maze of security, but Chara is a master of infiltration.

She hears the distinct WHIRR of a mech. She presses herself against a wall, peering around a corner. A new type of mech, smaller and faster than the Behemoth, patrols the corridor. It's armed with energy weapons, its optical sensors glowing an ominous red.

Chara waits for the mech to pass, then moves quickly, her objective clear: find the lead scientist, disrupt their operations, and get out with any additional intel she can find.
