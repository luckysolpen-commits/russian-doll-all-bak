INT. CIA SECURE LAB - NIGHT

SOUND of computer fans whirring, Chara's focused typing

Chara sits in a sterile, secure lab, surrounded by monitors displaying lines of code and data streams. The hard drives recovered from the monastery's server room are connected to a high-powered analysis rig. Agent Miller stands nearby, observing.

MILLER
Anything, Chara? They wiped those drives clean. It's a miracle we got anything at all.

CHARA
(Eyes glued to the screen)
They thought they did. But they were sloppy. Or overconfident. There's a ghost in the machine. A residual signature. It's faint, but it's there.

Hours pass. Chara sifts through layers of encrypted data, fragmented files, and corrupted logs. It's like piecing together a shattered mirror. Finally, a breakthrough. A hidden URL, buried deep within a system log.

CHARA
Got it. A darknet forum. Looks like their primary communication hub.

She navigates to the site, bypassing its initial security protocols with ease. The forum is a chaotic mess of cryptic posts, coded messages, and disturbing imagery. She filters through the noise, searching for anything related to the monastery, to Omnicorp, to the "Order of Providence."

She finds a thread titled "Our Rituals." Her gut clenches. She clicks on it. The first post is a link to a video file. She downloads it, her heart pounding.

CHARA
(To Miller)
You're going to want to see this.

The video begins to play. Grainy, shaky footage. A dimly lit BASEMENT. College-aged kids, maybe five or six of them, gathered around a makeshift altar. Candles flicker, casting dancing shadows. They're chanting, their voices low and rhythmic. A sense of unease fills the lab.

MILLER
What the hell is this?

CHARA
(Voice tight)
A seance. But not like any I've ever seen.

The video distorts. The chanting grows louder, more frenzied. The candles flare, unnaturally bright. The faces of the participants contort, their eyes wide with a mixture of terror and ecstasy. Then, a sudden, violent FLASH of light. The video cuts out.

Silence in the lab. Chara and Miller stare at the blank screen.

MILLER
(Shaken)
What was that?

CHARA
(Her voice barely a whisper)
I don't know. But I have a bad feeling about it.

She scrolls through the forum thread. More posts. Descriptions of "successful rituals." Mentions of "offerings." And then, a series of links to news articles. Local news. Unsolved murders. All with a chilling commonality.

CHARA
(Reading aloud, her voice growing colder)
"Five bodies discovered in abandoned warehouse… heads missing." "Ritualistic killings suspected in suburban home… victims decapitated." "College students found dead in basement… no heads."

Miller's face pales. The connection is undeniable. The seance video. The headless bodies. The monastery. It's all linked.

CHARA
(Looking at Miller, her eyes grim)
These aren't just hackers, Miller. They're murderers. And they're performing some kind of dark ritual.

MILLER
(Running a hand through his hair)
My God. We need to get to those crime scenes. Now.

FADE OUT.
