EXT. OLD CLOCK TOWER - NIGHT (MIDNIGHT)

SOUND of wind whistling, distant city hum

Chara stands alone in the desolate square before the old clock tower. Its gothic architecture looms against the moonlit sky, a silent sentinel. The air is thick with anticipation, a palpable tension that prickles her skin. She checks her watch: exactly midnight.

She scans the surroundings, her eyes darting from shadow to shadow. No movement. No sound beyond the wind and the distant city hum. The square is empty, save for her.

CHARA
(Whispering to the darkness)
Anonymous? Are you here?

Silence. Only the creaking of the old clock tower's mechanism, a rhythmic, metallic groan, answers her. She feels a prickle of unease. Is this a trick? A test?

Suddenly, a low, MODULATED VOICE echoes from seemingly nowhere. It’s disembodied, reverberating off the ancient stone walls, making it impossible to pinpoint its origin.

MODULATED VOICE (O.S.)
(Calm, almost amused)
I am always here, Agent Chara. Watching. Listening.

Chara spins around, trying to locate the source of the voice. Her hand instinctively goes to her concealed weapon, but she doesn't draw it. She knows a physical confrontation isn't the point here.

CHARA
(Voice firm)
Show yourself. What do you want?

MODULATED VOICE (O.S.)
Truth. The same as you, I believe. Though our methods may differ.

Chara looks up at the clock tower. Above the main clock face, she notices a faint, almost imperceptible GLOW. It’s coming from a stylized symbol etched into the stone – an EYE, surrounded by intricate patterns. It’s the same symbol she saw in the "shadow net" message.

CHARA
(Realizing)
The eyes are watching me.

MODULATED VOICE (O.S.)
(A hint of satisfaction)
Indeed. And they will continue to watch you until you uncover the truth. The game has only just begun, Agent Chara.

The glow from the eye symbol intensifies for a moment, then fades. The modulated voice seems to dissipate into the night air, leaving Chara alone once more in the silent square.

Chara stands her ground, her gaze fixed on the now-darkened eye symbol. A mix of frustration and fierce determination hardens her expression. She knows she's been played, but she also knows she's closer than ever.

FADE OUT.
