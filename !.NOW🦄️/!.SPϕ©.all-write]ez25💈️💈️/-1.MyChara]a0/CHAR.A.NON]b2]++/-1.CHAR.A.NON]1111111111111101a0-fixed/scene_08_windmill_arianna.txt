EXT. OLD WINDMILL - NIGHT (MIDNIGHT)

SOUND of wind, creaking windmill blades

Chara stands before the ancient windmill, its silhouette stark against the moonlit sky. The air is colder here, carrying the scent of damp earth and old wood. The blades of the windmill turn slowly, rhythmically, like a giant, watchful eye.

She scans the area, her senses on high alert. No sign of Anonymous. No sign of anyone. Just the vast, empty fields stretching out around her.

CHARA
(Whispering)
Another test?

Suddenly, a figure emerges from the deep shadows cast by the windmill. It’s a WOMAN (30s, striking, with piercing green eyes and long, dark, curly hair). She wears a black cloak that seems to absorb the moonlight, making her almost ethereal. She moves with a quiet confidence, her gaze fixed on Chara.

Chara’s hand instinctively goes to her weapon, but the woman makes no threatening move. She simply stops a few feet away, a faint, knowing smile playing on her lips.

WOMAN
You are Chara, the hacker.

Chara’s eyes narrow. The woman’s voice is low, husky, and carries an ancient resonance. It’s not modulated like Anonymous’s, but it has a similar disquieting quality.

CHARA
(Voice firm)
Who are you? And how do you know my name?

WOMAN
My name is Arianna. And I know many things. I have been waiting for you.

Arianna gestures to the windmill. Above its entrance, Chara notices a familiar symbol etched into the stone – the stylized EYE, identical to the one on the clock tower, but larger, more prominent.

ARIANNA
The eyes of providence are watching you, Chara. And they will continue to watch until you uncover the truth.

Chara feels a strange pull, a sense of recognition, despite the unsettling nature of the encounter. Arianna’s eyes seem to bore into her, seeing beyond her facade.

CHARA
(Hesitantly)
What truth?

ARIANNA
The secrets of the hacker. The secrets that will change everything. Come. There is much to show you.

Arianna turns and begins to walk away, not towards the windmill, but into the darkness of the fields, towards something unseen. Chara hesitates for a moment, her instincts warring with her insatiable curiosity. The risk is immense, but the promise of answers is too strong to ignore.

She follows Arianna, her gaze fixed on the retreating figure, wondering what ancient secrets lie hidden in the shadows.

FADE OUT.
