INT. CHARA'S APARTMENT - CONTINUOUS

SOUND of Chara typing furiously

Chara is still at her workstation, the blank screen from the video transmission still burned into her mind. She’s running every possible algorithm, every decryption key, trying to find a trace of Anonymous, but he’s vanished without a ripple.

CHARA
(Frustrated, to herself)
Nothing. He’s a ghost. How does he do it?

Just as she’s about to give up, her computer BEEPS. A new message. From an unknown sender. It’s a simple text file, no video, no audio. Just words.

"Follow the whispers, Chara."

Chara’s eyes narrow. It’s the same cryptic tone as the video. She immediately tries to trace the sender, but again, the trail goes cold. It’s like the message appeared out of thin air.

She leans back in her chair, rubbing her temples. This hacker isn't just good; he's playing a game. And he's inviting her to play along.

She opens a new browser window, navigating to a series of obscure forums and darknet marketplaces. She starts searching for any mention of "Whispering Walls" or similar phrases. It’s a long shot, but she has a feeling this isn't just a random phrase.

Hours pass. The sun is fully up now, casting long shadows across her apartment. She finds nothing concrete, just fragmented rumors and urban legends about a hidden network, a "shadow net," where information flows freely, unmonitored by governments or corporations.

CHARA
(Muttering)
Shadow net…

Suddenly, another BEEP. This time, it’s a pop-up on her screen, bypassing all her firewalls. A single line of text, stark against the black background:

"The shadow net is watching you, Chara."

Chara stares at the screen, a chill running down her spine. He knows what she’s thinking. He’s not just leaving breadcrumbs; he’s actively monitoring her. This isn't a game anymore. This is a direct challenge.

FADE OUT.
