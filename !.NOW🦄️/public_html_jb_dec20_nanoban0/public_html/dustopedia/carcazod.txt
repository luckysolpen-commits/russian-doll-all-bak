# CARCAZOD (Homo sapiens carcharias-arthropoda)
### Bio-Weapon Classification: Omega-Class Metamorphic Combatant

**SECURITY CLEARANCE: LUNAR COMMAND LEVEL 7 REQUIRED**

---

## Overview

The Carcazod represents the apex of the Blood Queen Monarchy's bio-engineering program: a controlled metamorphic weapon system built upon a human clone chassis. The designation "Carcazod" derives from *Carcharodon* (great white shark) and the suffix "-zod," denoting artificial genesis.

---

## Genetic Architecture

### Base Genome Modification

The Carcazod transformation involves radical resequencing of human DNA through introduction of xenogenic material:

**Primary Genetic Sources:**
- **Arthropod Integration (47% genetic material):** Primarily *Periplaneta americana* (American cockroach) and *Limulus polyphemus* (horseshoe crab)
  - Provides chitinous exoskeleton substrate
  - Enables rapid cellular regeneration
  - Introduces decentralized nervous system architecture
  
- **Chondrichthyes Sequences (31%):** *Carcharodon carcharias* (great white shark) and *Isistius brasiliensis* (cookiecutter shark)
  - Electroreception organs (ampullae of Lorenzini analogs)
  - Cartilaginous skeletal reinforcement
  - Predatory neural pathway enhancement
  - Continuous tooth regeneration in jaw structures

- **Retained Human Sequences (22%):** Primarily cerebral cortex, prefrontal regions
  - Maintains higher cognitive function
  - Preserves tactical reasoning and language centers
  - Allows voluntary transformation control (with extensive neural conditioning)

### Chromosomal Modifications

The transformation introduces 6 additional chromosomal pairs (total: 29 pairs/58 chromosomes), encoding:
- **Pair 24:** Chitin synthesis enzymes
- **Pair 25:** Metamorphic trigger sequences
- **Pair 26:** Enhanced muscle fiber density protocols
- **Pair 27:** Sensory amplification genes
- **Pair 28:** Rage-state neurochemical regulators
- **Pair 29:** Accelerated healing factors

## Transformation Process

### Stage One: Mutagen Introduction (Weeks 1-3)

Subjects undergo continuous immersion in oxygenated bio-gel containing:
- **CRISPR-Cas9 variant delivery vectors** (atmospheric pressure: 2.1 ATM)
- **Retroviral gene therapy agents** targeting osteoblasts, myocytes, and dermal cells
- **Growth hormone cascades** (500x normal human levels)
- **Pain management insufficient by design** - consciousness maintained to prevent psychological fracture during identity reformation

**Physiological Changes:**
- Bone marrow conversion to cartilage-generating tissue
- Epidermis replaced by sub-dermal chitinous layer (2-4mm thick)
- Muscle fiber density increases 340%
- Metabolic rate increases 600%

### Stage Two: Neural Conditioning (Weeks 4-8)

Subjects undergo invasive neural mapping and conditioning:
- **Predatory instinct cultivation** via hypothalamic stimulation
- **Rage-state management** through amygdala reprogramming
- **Transformation control training** via voluntary neurochemical release

**Survival Rate:** 61% (39% mortality from neurological rejection or uncontrolled mutation)

### Stage Three: Combat Conditioning (Weeks 9-12)

Surviving subjects trained in:
- Voluntary transformation triggering
- Predatory combat techniques
- Target discrimination under rage influence
- Metamorphic partial transformations for infiltration

## Physiological Specifications

### Dormant State (Human-Passing Form)

**Appearance:** Near-human, with notable differences under close inspection
- Height: +15-20cm above baseline clone height
- Weight: +40-60kg (dense muscle and sub-dermal armor)
- Skin: Slightly gray-tinted, hardened texture
- Eyes: Nictitating membrane visible in bright light
- Teeth: Slightly pointed canines, continuous growth

**Sensory Capabilities:**
- Olfactory sensitivity: 10,000x human baseline (can detect adrenaline in sweat at 50+ meters)
- Electroreception: Can sense bioelectric fields within 5-meter radius
- Low-light vision: Tapetum lucidum layer in retinas
- Auditory range: 10 Hz - 80 kHz

**Physical Performance:**
- Lifting capacity: 800-1000kg
- Sprint speed: 45-50 km/h sustained
- Reaction time: 80ms (vs. 250ms human average)

### Combat State (Full Transformation)

**Transformation Time:** 4-7 seconds (voluntary neural trigger)

**Appearance:** 
- Height: 2.8-3.2 meters
- Weight: 400-500kg
- Chitinous plates (6-12mm thick) erupt from sub-dermal layer
- Four primary scythe-like appendages extend from forearm/shoulder structures
- Jaw extends, reveals multiple rows of regenerating teeth
- Spinal ridge protrudes with serrated plates

**Combat Capabilities:**
- **Scythe Appendages:** Chitin-carbide composite, can shear through 50mm durasteel
- **Bite Force:** 18,000 PSI (comparable to *Dunkleosteus*)
- **Regeneration:** Non-fatal wounds close within minutes; severed limbs regrow in 72-96 hours
- **Speed:** 80+ km/h sprint, wall-climbing via chitin claw integration
- **Durability:** Resistant to small arms fire, vulnerable to anti-tank weaponry

**Neurochemical State:**
- Adrenaline levels: 40x baseline
- Dopamine flood in predatory centers
- Serotonin suppression (reduces impulse control)
- Norepinephrine saturation (hyperawareness state)

**Psychological Profile:** Subjects report experiencing the transformation as "controlled fury" - awareness maintained but impulse control significantly diminished. Extended periods in combat form (>20 minutes) risk permanent psychological damage and loss of voluntary control.

## Metabolic Requirements

### Caloric Intake
- **Dormant State:** 8,000-12,000 calories/day
- **Post-Transformation:** 20,000-30,000 calories required within 6 hours (primarily protein and calcium)

### Weaknesses
- **Thermal Regulation:** Poor heat dissipation in combat form (maximum combat duration: 45 minutes before heat exhaustion)
- **Nutritional Dependency:** Requires specialized diet including chitin precursors
- **Neural Fatigue:** Each transformation causes 8-12 hour recovery period
- **Electromagnetic Sensitivity:** Strong EM fields disrupt electroreception, cause disorientation
- **Psychological Instability:** 23% of subjects experience permanent psychosis after 10+ transformations
