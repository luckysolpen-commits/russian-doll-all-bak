
Biology investigates life, from cells to ecosystems. Cells are life’s units, with DNA storing genetic instructions for proteins. Evolution drives diversity through natural selection—species adapt over generations. Plants photosynthesize, converting sunlight into energy. Animals consume food for fuel. Ecosystems balance predators, prey, and resources. Genetics explains inheritance, like eye color passing from parents. Microbes, tiny yet vital, decompose waste or cause disease.
