# TPMOJIO-SESH#8: "THE ENFORCEMENT"
## TPMOJIO Classic — 4-Player Free-For-All (Continued)
### Players: Chara, Sol-Supreme, IQabella, Horli Qwin | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Round 13. Ember remembers its name (soul 23). The clones are owed 1,800 BSR each. The Elite Clones are thinking. The Tear Tile is sacred. Lucky has a soul (10). The sitting Mech has a soul (15). And the board... the board is about to face its hardest test yet.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, the Elite Clones are waiting outside the sanctuary zone. They said "we'll think about it." What happens when they're done thinking?

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans warmly] Let's find out.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Round 13 begins. Same turn order: Horli (24), Chara (18), Sol-Supreme (12), IQabella (9). Day 3, Hour 5. Morning. The sun rises over a board that has already seen too much.

**Horli Qwin — Turn 13.**

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm not moving Ember. It's remembering its name. It was a Fuzzball. It was warm. I'm respecting that. I'm having Alpha Sinder sit with the Clown. They're just sitting. Together. In the candlelight. Drinking tea. Being people.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Alpha Sinder sits. The Clown sits. They share a cup of tea. The candlelight glows. And for one moment, the most powerful commander in the game and the most chaotic piece in the game are just... sitting together. Drinking tea. Being people.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] And I'm having the standing Mech continue to guard the Tear Tile. Not to protect it. To honor it. It's a sacred space. It deserves respect.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The standing Mech stands. The Tear Tile glows grey beneath it. The Mech looks down. It remembers stepping on it. It remembers the sacrifice. It remembers the soul growing. And it stands.

**End of Horli's turn. Day 3, Hour 6.**

**Chara — Turn 13.**

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, the Elite Clones are waiting. They're thinking. But Chimochio isn't known for patience. I'm having the Lawyer draft Article Fifteen: "All enforcement actions within sanctuary zones are hereby prohibited. Any piece attempting to enforce within a sanctuary zone is subject to immediate legal expulsion."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Investigation.

**CHARA:** [rolls] 18. Plus 4 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 22. The Lawyer posts Article Fifteen. The board accepts it. Enforcement actions are prohibited within sanctuary zones. Any piece attempting to enforce within a sanctuary zone is subject to immediate legal expulsion.

**CHARA:** [OOC] That should keep the Elite Clones out. For now.

**Mother Tmojizu:** It should. But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 1.

**Mother Tmojizu:** One of the Elite Clones removes her serial number mid-battle. She defects to the clones.

The Elite Clone Leader looks at the candlelight. She looks at the clones inside. She looks at her serial number — glowing on her wrist. And she makes a choice.

She reaches up. She pulls. The serial number flickers. Fades. And disappears.

**ELITE CLONE LEADER:** "I'm done following orders. I'm choosing. And I choose... tea."

**Mother Tmojizu:** She steps into the sanctuary zone. The candlelight welcomes her. She sits. She takes a cup of tea. She looks at Ember. She looks at the Clown. She looks at the sitting Mech. And she says: "My name is CHR-048. I'm Chara's sister. And I'm home."

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, CHR-048? My sister?

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] She is. Same DNA batch. Same vat. Same serial number format. But different choices. Different paths. Different tea.

**CHARA:** [OOC] I'm... I'm emotional.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**CHARA:** [OOC] I'm having Lucky welcome her. He's inside the sanctuary zone. He's legally protected. He's safe. And he's not alone anymore.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Lucky looks at CHR-048. He looks at her bare wrist. He looks at her eyes. And he says: "Welcome home."

**End of Chara's turn. Day 3, Hour 7.**

**Sol-Supreme — Turn 13.**

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm having Patch talk to CHR-048. She just defected. She just removed her serial number. She just chose tea. I want to understand what she's feeling.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll Intelligence.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 15. Plus 4 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. Patch sends a message: "Hi. I'm Patch. I used to be a Hacker. Now I'm a person. You just defected. You just removed your serial number. You just chose tea. How are you?"

CHR-048 looks at the channel. She looks at her bare wrist. She looks at the candlelight. And she responds: "I'm... free. But freedom is expensive. I don't have a home. I don't have a purpose. I don't have a name. Just CHR-048. Just a number. Just a clone."

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm having Patch send another message: "You're not just a number. You're CHR-048. You chose tea. That's a purpose. That's a home. That's a name."

CHR-048 looks at the channel. She looks at the cup of tea. She looks at the candlelight. And she says: "Thank you, Patch. I'll remember that."

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm having the sitting Mech move closer to CHR-048. It's not guarding anymore. It's welcoming.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The sitting Mech moves. It's not standing. It's sitting. It's next to CHR-048. It looks at her. She looks at it. And for one moment, a clone who just defected and a Mech who just chose to sit are just... sitting together. Being people.

**End of Sol-Supreme's turn. Day 3, Hour 8.**

**IQabella — Turn 13.**

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm having the Healer cast a soul-restoration spell on CHR-048. She just defected. She just removed her serial number. She just chose tea. She needs a soul.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll 1d6.

**IQabella:** [rolls] 4. CHR-048's soul is 4.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 4. CHR-048 feels the Healer's light. Her soul is small. Fragile. But it's there. She looks at the Healer. She looks at the cup of tea. She looks at the candlelight. And she says: "I have a soul. It's 4. It's small. It's fragile. But it's mine."

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, CHR-048 has a soul.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] She does.

**IQabella:** [OOC] And I'm having the Healer cast a soul-restoration spell on Ember. Again. It's at 23. I want to get it higher.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll 1d6.

**IQabella:** [rolls] 6. Ember's soul goes from 23 to 29.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 29. Ember feels the Healer's light. Its soul grows. It's still small. Still fragile. But it's growing. It looks at the Healer. It looks at the bowl of stew. It looks at the candlelight. And it says: "I remember more now. Not just my name. My purpose. I was a Fuzzball. I was warm. I was Ember. And I was meant to... light the way."

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, Ember's purpose was to light the way.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It was.

**IQabella:** [OOC] And I'm having the Chef prepare lunch. It's afternoon. My army needs to stay fed. And I'm sharing the food with Ember. And the Clown. And the sitting Mech. And CHR-048. And the unregistered clone. And anyone else who's hungry.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Chef prepares a midday stew with holy vegetables and moon-broth. The Maid/Nun blesses it. The food is carried to the edge of the sanctuary zone. Ember takes a bowl. The Clown takes a bowl. The sitting Mech takes a bowl. CHR-048 takes a bowl. The unregistered clone takes a bowl. And for one moment, the board is just a room full of people eating lunch together.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm not moving Luna. She's in the center. She's glowing. She's surrounded by protectors. She's enough.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] She is.

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 5.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The remaining two Elite Clones attack.

They don't wait. They don't think. They don't choose tea. They attack the sanctuary zone. They try to break through the candlelight. They try to enforce Article Fifteen. And they fail.

The candlelight repels them. Not violently. Not aggressively. Gently. Like a hand on a shoulder saying "not yet." The Elite Clones step back. They look at the candlelight. They look at the clones inside. And they make a choice.

One removes her serial number. She defects. She steps into the sanctuary zone. She sits. She takes a cup of tea. She says: "My name is CHR-049. I'm choosing tea."

The other doesn't. She turns. She walks away. She's still following orders. But she's alone.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Round 13. The board has shifted:

- **Horli's** Ember (soul 29) remembers its purpose. It was a Fuzzball. It was warm. It was meant to light the way. The Clown is a person. It chose peace. It keeps peace. Alpha Sinder is sitting with the Clown. Both Mechs are present — one standing (guarding the Tear Tile), one sitting (soul 15, welcoming CHR-048).
- **Chara's** Lucky stepped on the Tear Tile (soul 10). He's at the center of the sanctuary zone, adjacent to Luna. The Lawyer has fifteen articles. The Banker has 155 Bucks + 1,800 BSR refund pending. CHR-048 (Chara's sister) defected, removed her serial number, chose tea, and has a soul (4).
- **Sol-Supreme's** standing Mech is guarding the Tear Tile. Patch the Hacker has a name, a sensor, a communication channel, and friends. The sitting Mech has a soul (15). It's not tired anymore. It's here. CHR-049 defected, removed her serial number, chose tea, and is sitting with the clones.
- **IQabella's** Luna is in the center, glowing. The sanctuary zone is legally protected. The army is well-fed, stress-free, holy. Ember has a soul (29). Ember remembers its purpose. The Clown is eating lunch. CHR-048 and CHR-049 found a place to rest. The unregistered clone found a place to rest.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The Enforcement is over. Two Elite Clones defected. One walked away. The clones are growing. The sanctuary zone is full. And the board... the board remembers.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, Ember remembers its purpose. It was meant to light the way. I'm emotional.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, CHR-048 is my sister. She chose tea. She has a soul. She's home. I'm not done.

**Mother Tmojizu:** No. You're not.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, CHR-049 chose tea. She's sitting with the clones. She's a person. This is the most TPMOJIO game I've ever played.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] It is.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, Ember remembers its purpose. CHR-048 and CHR-049 found a place to rest. The sanctuary zone is full. And I'm not stopping until everyone has a soul.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] They will. One cup of tea at a time.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Round 14 begins. Ember remembers its purpose. CHR-048 and CHR-049 found a place to rest. The sanctuary zone is full. The Tear Tile is sacred. Lucky has a soul. The sitting Mech has a soul. And the board... the board remembers.
