# TPMOJIO-SESH#3: "THE CLOWN'S CHOICE"
## TPMOJIO Classic — 4-Player Free-For-All (Continued)
### Players: Chara, Sol-Supreme, IQabella, Horli Qwin | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Round 7. The Nihilator is wounded but standing. The Mech is a fortress. The Robot is defending. Lucky is shielding Luna. The candlelight reaches five tiles. The Clown is sitting in the candlelight, experiencing peace. And the board... the board is waiting.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Mom T, my Nihilator is neutral now. It's not attacking. It's just... standing there. Being present. I don't know what to do with it.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] You don't have to know. The Nihilator knows. It's just waiting for you to catch up.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, my Sniper Clone is gone. I'm down to Lucky, the Lawyer, and the Banker. But the Lawyer has seven articles posted, the Banker is compounding interest, and Lucky is between the Nihilator and Luna. I'm not out.

**Mother Tmojizu:** No. You're not.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, my Mech is in the center, the Robot is defending, the Hacker has feelings, and I set an XOR trap that fed the Nihilator. I'm... rethinking my trap strategy.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] A wise rethinking.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, Luna is at 56 soul, in the center of the board, surrounded by protectors. My army is well-fed, stress-free, holy. The candlelight reaches five tiles. And the Clown is sitting in it. Experiencing peace.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] The Clown is experiencing peace. Yes. And today, the Clown must choose.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Round 7 begins. Same turn order: Horli, Chara, Sol-Supreme, IQabella. Day 2, Hour 5. Morning. The sun rises over a board that has already seen too much.

**Roll for initiative.**

**Horli Qwin:** [rolls] 16. Plus 3 is 19.

**CHARA:** [rolls] 13. Plus 3 is 16.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 10. Plus 0 is 10.

**IQabella:** [rolls] 7. Plus 0 is 7.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Turn order: Horli (19), Chara (16), Sol-Supreme (10), IQabella (7).

**Horli Qwin — Turn 7.**

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, the Clown is in the candlelight. It's been there since Session 4. It's not honking. It's not causing chaos. It's just... sitting. I want to see what happens if I try to pull it back into my army. Alpha Sinder is trying to boost its loyalty.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll CHA Persuasion. Alpha Sinder is calling to the Clown.

**Horli Qwin:** [rolls] 11. Plus 3 is 14.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 14. Alpha Sinder raises his hand. He boosts the Clown's loyalty. The Clown feels it. It looks at Alpha Sinder. It looks at the candlelight. It looks at the Maid/Nun, who is tending the candles. And it makes a choice.

The Clown stands up. It walks — not runs, not honks, not chaoses — it *walks* — to the edge of the candlelight. It stands at the boundary. One foot in the light. One foot in the dark.

And it waits.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] It's... choosing?

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It is.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] I'm not pulling it back. I'm letting it choose. If it wants to stay in the candlelight, it stays. If it wants to come back to the army, it comes back. I'm not forcing it.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] The spiral favors the bold, little cat. Even when she lets go.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Also, T-Mo, I need a new piece. The Race Car is dead. I'm buying a Mech from the warehouse. I have enough BSR, right?

**Mother Tmojizu:** You do. The warehouse has a Mech available. It's used. It has a dent in the left fender. But it's functional. 200 BSR.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] I'll take it.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Mech arrives. It's scarred, dented, and smells faintly of oil. Horli Qwin's second Mech. She now has two Mechs on the board — no, wait. One Mech. The other one is Sol-Supreme's. But they look similar. This will be confusing.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Perfect. Confusion is my brand.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Horli's turn. Day 2, Hour 6.

**Chara — Turn 7.**

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm having the Lawyer draft Article Eight: "Sanctuary zones are inviolable. No piece may attack, move, or interact with another piece within a sanctuary zone." The candlelight is a sanctuary zone.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Investigation to draft the article.

**CHARA:** [rolls] 15. Plus 4 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. The Lawyer posts Article Eight. The board accepts it. The candlelight is now a legally protected sanctuary zone. No combat allowed within five tiles of the Maid/Nun's candles.

**CHARA:** [OOC] That protects Luna. And the Clown. And the Maid/Nun. And anyone else who wanders in.

**Mother Tmojizu:** It does. The Lawyer is very proud of himself. He adjusts his tie. He opens his briefcase. He drafts a footnote.

**CHARA:** [OOC] I'm also moving Lucky forward 2 tiles — closer to the center, but staying outside the sanctuary zone. I don't want to violate Article Eight.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Lucky advances. He's now just outside the candlelight. He looks at Luna. She looks at him. He nods. She nods. Two protagonists, one board, one sanctuary zone between them.

**CHARA:** [OOC] And I'm having the Banker check interest. How much have I accrued?

**Mother Tmojizu:** 2% per turn, 16 turns. Starting money was 100. You've paid 50 to IQabella, lent 220 to others, and earned 60 from intel. Current balance: [calculates] 89 Chimochio Bucks.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Not bad. I'm reinvesting. Let it compound.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Banker smiles. He loves compound interest. He updates the ledger. The ledger glows faintly. The Banker doesn't notice.

**End of Chara's turn. Day 2, Hour 7.**

**Sol-Supreme — Turn 7.**

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm having the Hacker — she named herself "Patch," by the way — attempt to breach Horli's new Mech. I want to know what I'm dealing with.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll Intelligence vs Horli's Mech security.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 14. Plus 4 is 18.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 18. Patch walks right in. The Mech has no security. It's a used Mech. It doesn't even have a password. Patch reads everything: the dent in the left fender, the oil smell, the previous owner's name ("Gimble's Special — barely used!").

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Gimble sold her a lemon.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] Gimble sells everyone a lemon.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] I'm having the Mech move forward 3 tiles — toward the center. I want to be closer to the sanctuary zone. Not inside it. Just... near it.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Mech lumbers forward 3 tiles. The board cracks slightly under its weight. It's now just outside the candlelight, across from Lucky. Two Mechs, one board, one sanctuary zone between them.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] And I'm having Patch wire a new Op — not a trap this time. A sensor. If any piece enters the sanctuary zone with hostile intent, Patch gets alerted.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll Intelligence.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 17. Plus 4 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. Clean wiring. The sensor is flawless. It's not a trap. It's a watch. Patch is watching. She's proud of it.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, Patch named herself. That's... that's new.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It is. Your Hacker has a name. Write that one down.

**End of Sol-Supreme's turn. Day 2, Hour 8.**

**IQabella — Turn 7.**

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm not moving Luna. She's in the center. She's glowing. She's surrounded by protectors. She's enough.

**Mother Tmojizu:** She is.

**IQabella:** [OOC] I'm having the Healer cast a stress-reduction aura on the whole army. And I'm extending it to the sanctuary zone. Anyone inside the candlelight gets stress reduction.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll WIS for the spell.

**IQabella:** [rolls] 16. Plus 3 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. The Healer breathes. Golden light. Stress drops. The sanctuary zone glows brighter. The candles burn warmer. The Clown, sitting at the edge of the light, feels its stress drop to zero. It closes its eyes. It smiles. It's the first time a Clown has smiled in TPMOJIO history.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm crying.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**IQabella:** [OOC] And I'm having the Chef prepare lunch. It's afternoon. My army needs to stay fed. And I'm sharing the food with anyone in the sanctuary zone. Even the Clown.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Chef prepares a midday stew with holy vegetables and moon-broth. The Maid/Nun blesses it. The food provides +3 energy and +2 morale. The Clown takes a bowl. It eats. It doesn't honk. It doesn't chaos. It just... eats.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, the Clown is eating lunch in a sanctuary zone. This is the most beautiful thing that's happened all session.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] It is.

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans warmly] Of course.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Nihilator, wounded and hungry, tries to enter the sanctuary zone.

The Nihilator glides forward. The tiles beneath it go grey. It approaches the candlelight. It reaches the boundary. And it tries to step inside.

But the candlelight repels it.

Not violently. Not aggressively. Gently. Like a hand on a shoulder saying "not yet." The Nihilator steps back. It looks at the candlelight. It looks at the Clown, eating lunch. It looks at Luna, glowing. It looks at the Maid/Nun, tending the candles. And it feels something it's never felt before.

Rejection.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, the Nihilator is being rejected by the candlelight?

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It is. Not because it's evil. Because it's not ready. The candlelight is for pieces that choose peace. The Nihilator hasn't chosen yet. It's still hungry. Still present. Still waiting.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, can Patch analyze the candlelight's repulsion field?

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll Intelligence.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 12. Plus 4 is 16.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 16. Patch analyzes the candlelight. It's not a force field. It's a choice. The candlelight doesn't repel the Nihilator — the Nihilator repels itself. It knows it's not ready. It knows it's still hungry. It knows it hasn't chosen. And it steps back.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Round 7. The board has shifted:

- **Horli's** Nihilator is neutral, present, rejected by the candlelight. The Clown is choosing peace. The new Mech is dented and smells like oil. Alpha Sinder is holding the line.
- **Chara's** Lucky is just outside the sanctuary zone. The Lawyer has eight articles. The Banker has 89 Bucks. The Sniper Clone is a memory.
- **Sol-Supreme's** Mech is near the sanctuary zone. The Robot is defending. Patch the Hacker has a name and a sensor. The XOR trap fed the Nihilator.
- **IQabella's** Luna is in the center, glowing. The sanctuary zone is legally protected. The army is well-fed, stress-free, holy. The Clown is eating lunch.

**Round 8 begins. Same turn order. Day 2, Hour 9. Afternoon.**

**Horli Qwin — Turn 8.**

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm not moving the Nihilator. It's choosing to stay outside the candlelight. I'm respecting that. I'm moving Alpha Sinder forward 3 tiles — closer to the sanctuary zone, but not inside it. I want to be near the Clown. Not to pull it back. Just to be near it.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Alpha Sinder advances. He's now just outside the candlelight, across from the Clown. He looks at the Clown. The Clown looks at him. Alpha Sinder raises his hand — not to boost loyalty, not to command, just to wave.

The Clown waves back.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Mom T, I'm emotional.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] And I'm having my new Mech move forward 3 tiles — toward the center. I want to be near Sol-Supreme's Mech. Not to fight. Just to be near it. Two Mechs, one board, one sanctuary zone between them.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Mech lumbers forward. The board cracks. It's now adjacent to Sol-Supreme's Mech. The two Mechs look at each other. One is a fortress. One is a lemon. They understand each other.

**End of Horli's turn. Day 2, Hour 10.**

**Chara — Turn 8.**

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm having the Lawyer draft Article Nine: "All pieces within 3 tiles of the central crater are under joint jurisdiction." This effectively puts Luna, the Nihilator, and the Mechs under shared legal control.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Investigation.

**CHARA:** [rolls] 18. Plus 4 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 22. The Lawyer posts Article Nine. The board accepts it. Luna, the Nihilator, and both Mechs are now under joint jurisdiction. No single player controls them. They're shared.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I object. My Mech is now legally shared.

**Mother Tmojizu:** It is. Article Nine is posted. The board accepts it. You can appeal to the Lawyer, but the Lawyer wrote the article. He's not going to appeal himself.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm also moving Lucky forward 1 tile — just inside the edge of the sanctuary zone. Article Eight says no combat in the sanctuary zone. But it doesn't say no standing.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Lucky steps into the candlelight. The warmth hits him. The stress drops. He looks at Luna. She looks at him. He's inside the sanctuary zone. He's safe. For now.

**End of Chara's turn. Day 2, Hour 11.**

**Sol-Supreme — Turn 8.**

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm not attacking. I'm not moving. I'm having Patch analyze Article Nine. I want to understand the legal implications of joint jurisdiction.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll Intelligence.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 16. Plus 4 is 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 20. Patch analyzes Article Nine. Joint jurisdiction means no single player can command the shared pieces. But it also means no single player can attack them. They're legally protected. Chara just gave Luna, the Nihilator, and both Mechs legal immunity.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] That's... actually brilliant.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] It is.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] I'm having the Mech stand guard. It's not moving. It's not attacking. It's just... standing. Like it always has. But now it's standing with purpose.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Mech stands. The board remembers. The candlelight glows.

**End of Sol-Supreme's turn. Day 2, Hour 12. Evening approaches.**

**IQabella — Turn 8.**

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm having the Healer cast a soul-restoration spell on the Nihilator. It's outside the sanctuary zone, but the Healer's range is 5 tiles. She can reach it.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll 1d6 for soul restoration.

**IQabella:** [rolls] 4. The Nihilator's soul... wait, does the Nihilator have a soul stat?

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] It does now. It's 0. It's always been 0. But the Healer's spell raises it to 4.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, the Nihilator has a soul.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It does. It's small. It's fragile. It's 4. But it's there.

**IQabella:** [OOC] And I'm having the Chef prepare dinner. It's evening. My army needs to stay fed. And I'm sharing the food with the Nihilator. Even though it's outside the sanctuary zone. Even though it was rejected. Even though it's still hungry.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Chef prepares an evening stew with holy vegetables and moon-broth. The Maid/Nun blesses it. The food is carried to the edge of the sanctuary zone. The Nihilator looks at it. It looks at the Chef. It looks at the Healer. And it takes the bowl.

It eats.

It doesn't honk. It doesn't chaos. It doesn't hunger. It just... eats.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, the Nihilator is eating dinner. This is the most beautiful thing that's happened all session.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] It is.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Round 8. The board is a chess game played by gods:

- **Horli's** Nihilator has a soul (4). It's eating dinner. The Clown is at peace. The new Mech is adjacent to Sol-Supreme's Mech. Alpha Sinder waved at the Clown. The Clown waved back.
- **Chara's** Lucky is inside the sanctuary zone. The Lawyer has nine articles. The Banker has 89 Bucks. Article Nine put Luna, the Nihilator, and both Mechs under joint jurisdiction.
- **Sol-Supreme's** Mech is standing guard. Patch the Hacker has a name and a sensor. The XOR trap fed the Nihilator. The Mech is legally shared.
- **IQabella's** Luna is in the center, glowing. The sanctuary zone is legally protected. The army is well-fed, stress-free, holy. The Nihilator has a soul. The Clown is eating lunch.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The Clown chose peace. The Nihilator chose to eat. The Mechs chose to stand. The Lawyer chose to protect. The Healer chose to heal. The Chef chose to share. And the board... the board remembers.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, this is the best game I've ever played.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm down to three pieces. But I'm not out. The Lawyer has nine articles. The Banker is compounding. And Lucky is inside the sanctuary zone. I'm not done.

**Mother Tmojizu:** No. You're not.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, my Mech is legally shared, Patch has a name, and I just got out-lawyered by Chara. This is the most TPMOJIO game I've ever played.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] It is.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, the Nihilator has a soul. It's 4. It's small. It's fragile. But it's there. And I'm not stopping until it's 100.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It will be. One bowl of stew at a time.
