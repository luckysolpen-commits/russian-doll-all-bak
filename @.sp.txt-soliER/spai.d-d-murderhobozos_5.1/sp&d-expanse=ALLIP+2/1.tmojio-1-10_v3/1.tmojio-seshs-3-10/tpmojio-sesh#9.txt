Not just moonlight. Not just a hand reaching out. Not just a voice saying "you're not alone." It remembers its purpose. It was a Fuzzball. It was warm. It was meant to light the way. And it realizes: it's been lighting the way all along. The candlelight. The sanctuary zone. The tea. The sitting. The choosing. It's all been Ember's light.

**EMBER:** [in character, for the second time] "I'm ready. Not to leave. To shine."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Ember stands. It steps onto the Tear Tile. The grey cold hits it. The moonlight washes over it. It feels the sacrifice — 10 HP lost. But it feels something else too — +10 soul gained. Its soul goes from 29 to 39.

And then it glows.

Not the grey cold of the Tear Tile. Not the warm gold of the candlelight. A new light. Orange. Warm. Alive. The light of a Fuzzball. The light of Ember. And it spreads.

The sanctuary zone glows brighter. The candles burn warmer. The tea steams hotter. The clones inside feel it — the warmth, the light, the purpose. And they look at Ember. And they see it.

A Fuzzball. Glowing. Shining. Lighting the way.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, Ember is glowing. It's a Fuzzball. It's lighting the way. I'm crying.
