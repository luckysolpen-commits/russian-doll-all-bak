# TPMOJIO-SESH#4: "THE LAWYER'S GAMBIT"
## TPMOJIO Classic — 4-Player Free-For-All (Continued)
### Players: Chara, Sol-Supreme, IQabella, Horli Qwin | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Round 9. The Nihilator has a soul (4). The Clown chose peace. The Mechs stand guard. Lucky is inside the sanctuary zone. The Lawyer has nine articles. And the board... the board is about to get complicated.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I've been planning this since Round 7. The Lawyer is about to change everything.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans warmly] I know. And I'm both terrified and impressed.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Round 9 begins. Same turn order: Horli (21), Chara (18), Sol-Supreme (12), IQabella (8). Day 2, Hour 13. Evening. The sun sets over a board that has already seen too much.

**Horli Qwin — Turn 9.**

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm not moving the Nihilator. It's eating dinner. It has a soul. It's choosing to be present. I'm respecting that. I'm moving Alpha Sinder forward 2 tiles — closer to the Clown. I want to sit with it. Not command it. Just sit.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Alpha Sinder advances. He's now adjacent to the Clown. He sits down. The Clown looks at him. He looks at the Clown. And for one moment, the most powerful commander in the game is just... sitting with a Clown in a candlelit sanctuary zone.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] And I'm having my Mech stand guard next to Sol-Supreme's Mech. Two Mechs, one board, one sanctuary zone between them. They're legally shared now thanks to Article Nine. They're basically coworkers.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] The two Mechs stand guard. One is a fortress. One is a lemon. They're coworkers now. They share a shift. They don't speak. They don't need to.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] And I'm having the Clown... I'm not having it do anything. It's just sitting. Experiencing peace. Being a Clown that chose peace. That's enough.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It is.

**End of Horli's turn. Day 2, Hour 14.**

**Chara — Turn 9.**

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, this is it. The Lawyer's Gambit. I'm drafting Article Ten: "The Nihilator is hereby recognized as a sovereign entity with full legal personhood. It cannot be attacked, controlled, or commanded by any player. It acts of its own free will."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Investigation to draft the article.

**CHARA:** [rolls] 20. Plus 4 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. The Lawyer posts Article Ten. The board shakes. The tiles tremble. The candles flicker. And the Nihilator — the Nihilator feels it.

The Nihilator looks up from its bowl. It looks at the Lawyer. It looks at the board. And it understands. It's not a piece anymore. It's a person.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, the Nihilator is a person now?

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It is. Article Ten grants it full legal personhood. It can't be attacked. It can't be controlled. It can't be commanded. It acts of its own free will. And for the first time in TPMOJIO history, a piece is no longer a piece. It's a person.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] I'm... I'm crying.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm also drafting Article Eleven: "The Clown is hereby recognized as a sovereign entity with full legal personhood. It chose peace. It keeps peace. It is protected under sanctuary law."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Investigation.

**CHARA:** [rolls] 17. Plus 4 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. The Lawyer posts Article Eleven. The Clown looks up from its lunch. It looks at the Lawyer. It looks at the candlelight. And it understands. It's not a piece anymore. It's a person.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, both my pieces are people now. I don't control them anymore.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] You never did. You just didn't know it yet.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm also moving Lucky forward 1 tile — deeper into the sanctuary zone. He's now adjacent to Luna. Article Eight says no combat in the sanctuary zone. Article Ten and Eleven say the Nihilator and Clown are people. And Article Nine says everything within 3 tiles of the central crater is under joint jurisdiction. I'm building a legal fortress.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Lucky steps deeper into the candlelight. He's now adjacent to Luna. He looks at her. She looks at him. He's inside the sanctuary zone. He's legally protected. He's safe.

**CHARA:** [OOC] And I'm having the Banker check interest. How much now?

**Mother Tmojizu:** 2% per turn, 18 turns. Current balance: [calculates] 107 Chimochio Bucks.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Compound interest is beautiful.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Banker smiles. He loves compound interest. He updates the ledger. The ledger glows faintly. The Banker doesn't notice.

**End of Chara's turn. Day 2, Hour 15.**

**Sol-Supreme — Turn 9.**

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm having Patch analyze Article Ten and Eleven. I want to understand the legal implications of personhood for pieces.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll Intelligence.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 19. Plus 4 is 23.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 23. Patch analyzes the articles. Personhood means the Nihilator and Clown are no longer game pieces — they're game entities. They can't be moved, attacked, or commanded. They act of their own free will. But they're still on the board. And they still have stats. They just choose what to do with them.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] That's... that's revolutionary.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It is.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] I'm having the Mech stand guard. It's not moving. It's not attacking. It's just... standing. But now it's standing next to a person. Not a piece. A person.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Mech stands. The Nihilator eats. The Clown sits. The candlelight glows. And the board remembers.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] And I'm having Patch wire a new Op — not a sensor this time. A communication channel. She wants to talk to the Nihilator. Not as a hacker. As a person.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll Intelligence.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 15. Plus 4 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. Patch wires a communication channel. It's not a hack. It's a conversation. She sends a message to the Nihilator: "Hi. I'm Patch. I used to be a Hacker. Now I'm a person. What are you?"

The Nihilator looks at the channel. It looks at the message. And it responds: "I'm hungry. But not for souls. For... connection."

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, the Nihilator just talked.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It did. For the first time. Not as a piece. As a person.

**End of Sol-Supreme's turn. Day 2, Hour 16.**

**IQabella — Turn 9.**

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm having the Healer cast a soul-restoration spell on the Nihilator. Again. It's at 4. I want to get it higher.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll 1d6.

**IQabella:** [rolls] 5. The Nihilator's soul goes from 4 to 9.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 9. The Nihilator feels the Healer's light. Its soul grows. It's still small. Still fragile. But it's growing. It looks at the Healer. It looks at the bowl of stew. It looks at the candlelight. And it says: "Thank you."

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, the Nihilator said thank you.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It did.

**IQabella:** [OOC] And I'm having the Chef prepare a midnight snack. It's late evening. My army needs to stay fed. And I'm sharing the food with the Nihilator. And the Clown. And anyone else who's hungry.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Chef prepares a midnight snack — warm bread, moon-broth, and a cup of tea. The Maid/Nun blesses it. The food is carried to the edge of the sanctuary zone. The Nihilator takes a piece. The Clown takes a piece. And for one moment, the board is just a room full of people eating together.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm not moving Luna. She's in the center. She's glowing. She's surrounded by protectors. She's enough.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] She is.

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 2.

**Mother Tmojizu:** A clone's loyalty drops.

One of the shared Mechs — the one that belongs to both Horli and Sol-Supreme under Article Nine — experiences a loyalty crisis. It's legally shared. It's emotionally confused. It doesn't know who to follow.

**MOTHER TOMOKAZU:** The Mech looks at Horli. It looks at Sol-Supreme. It looks at the board. And it makes a choice.

It walks away.

Not away from the battle. Away from the confusion. It walks to the edge of the sanctuary zone. It sits down. It looks at the candlelight. And it waits.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, my Mech just walked away.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It did. It's not broken. It's not defective. It's just... confused. It needs time.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, can Patch talk to it?

**Mother Tmojizu:** She can. Roll Intelligence.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 14. Plus 4 is 18.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 18. Patch sends a message to the Mech: "Hi. I'm Patch. I used to be a Hacker. Now I'm a person. You're a Mech. You're confused. That's okay. You don't have to choose. You can just... be."

The Mech looks at the channel. It looks at the message. And it responds: "I'm tired of standing. But I don't know how to sit."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] The Mech sits. It's next to the Clown. It's next to Alpha Sinder. It's next to the Nihilator. And for one moment, the edge of the sanctuary zone is just a room full of people sitting together.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Round 9. The board has shifted:

- **Horli's** Nihilator is a person (soul 9). It's eating. It's talking. It's choosing. The Clown is a person. It chose peace. It keeps peace. Alpha Sinder is sitting with the Clown. The Mech walked away and sat down.
- **Chara's** Lucky is deep inside the sanctuary zone, adjacent to Luna. The Lawyer has eleven articles. The Banker has 107 Bucks. Article Ten and Eleven granted personhood. Article Nine created joint jurisdiction.
- **Sol-Supreme's** Mech is standing guard. Patch the Hacker has a name, a sensor, and a communication channel. She talked to the Nihilator. She talked to the Mech. She's building bridges.
- **IQabella's** Luna is in the center, glowing. The sanctuary zone is legally protected. The army is well-fed, stress-free, holy. The Nihilator has a soul (9). The Clown is eating lunch. The midnight snack is shared.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The Lawyer's Gambit is over. The Nihilator is a person. The Clown is a person. The Mech sat down. And the board... the board remembers.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, this is the most beautiful game I've ever played.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, the Lawyer has eleven articles. I've built a legal fortress. I'm not done.

**Mother Tmojizu:** No. You're not.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, Patch talked to the Nihilator. The Nihilator talked back. My Mech sat down. This is the most TPMOJIO game I've ever played.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] It is.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, the Nihilator has a soul (9). It said thank you. It ate a midnight snack. And I'm not stopping until it's 100.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It will be. One bowl of stew at a time.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Round 10 begins. The Nihilator is a person. The Clown chose peace. The Mech sat down. Patch is building bridges. The Lawyer has eleven articles. And the board... the board remembers.

**Roll for initiative.**

**Horli Qwin:** [rolls] 20. Plus 3 is 23. NATURAL 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans warmly] Of course.

**Horli Qwin:** LET'S GOOOOO!

**CHARA:** [rolls] 16. Plus 3 is 19.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 11. Plus 0 is 11.

**IQabella:** [rolls] 9. Plus 0 is 9.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] The spiral turns. The board resets. The pieces remember.
