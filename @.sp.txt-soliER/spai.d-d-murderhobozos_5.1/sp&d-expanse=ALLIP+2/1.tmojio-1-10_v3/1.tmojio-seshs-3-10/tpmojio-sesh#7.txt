# TPMOJIO-SESH#7: "THE BANKER'S LEDGER"
## TPMOJIO Classic — 4-Player Free-For-All (Continued)
### Players: Chara, Sol-Supreme, IQabella, Horli Qwin | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Round 12. The Nihilator remembers moonlight (soul 18). The moon called it home. It chose to stay. The Tear Tile is sacred. Lucky has a soul (10). The sitting Mech has a soul (15). And the board... the board is about to learn a secret.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, the Banker has been compounding interest for 24 turns. He's been quiet. He's been patient. And today, he opens his ledger.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know. And I'm both terrified and impressed.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Round 12 begins. Same turn order: Horli (22), Chara (19), Sol-Supreme (11), IQabella (8). Day 3, Hour 1. Morning. The sun rises over a board that has already seen too much.

**Horli Qwin — Turn 12.**

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm not moving the Nihilator. It's remembering moonlight. It chose to stay. I'm respecting that. I'm having Alpha Sinder sit with the Clown. They're just sitting. Together. In the candlelight. Drinking tea.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Alpha Sinder sits. The Clown sits. They share a cup of tea. The candlelight glows. And for one moment, the most powerful commander in the game and the most chaotic piece in the game are just... sitting together. Drinking tea. Being people.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] And I'm having the standing Mech guard the Tear Tile. Not to protect it. To honor it. It's a sacred space. It deserves respect.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The standing Mech moves to the Tear Tile. It stands guard. Not as a fortress. As a memorial. The Tear Tile glows grey beneath it. The Mech looks down. It remembers stepping on it. It remembers the sacrifice. It remembers the soul growing. And it stands.

**End of Horli's turn. Day 3, Hour 2.**

**Chara — Turn 12.**

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, this is it. The Banker's Ledger. I'm having the Banker open his ledger. He's been tracking everything — every BSR, every trade, every debt, every interest payment. And he's found something.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Investigation to reveal the secret.

**CHARA:** [rolls] 20. Plus 4 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. The Banker opens his ledger. He's been tracking everything. And he finds it: the clones' creation debt of 500 BSR each was a fabrication. The real cost was 50 BSR. Chimochio Corporation overcharged by 450 BSR per clone.

But there's more. The Banker has been compounding interest not just on the clones' earnings — but on the overcharge. And the overcharge, compounded over 24 turns at 2% per turn, is worth... [calculates] 1,800 BSR per clone.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, the clones are owed money. Not just freedom. Money.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] They are.

**CHARA:** [OOC] I'm having the Lawyer draft Article Fourteen: "All clone debts are hereby reduced to 50 BSR. All overcharges are hereby refunded with interest. Total refund: 1,800 BSR per clone."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Investigation.

**CHARA:** [rolls] 18. Plus 4 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 22. The Lawyer posts Article Fourteen. The board accepts it. The clones' debts are reduced. They're not just free — they're owed money. 1,800 BSR each.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm owed 1,800 BSR?

**Mother Tmojizu:** You are.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm owed 1,800 BSR?

**Mother Tmojizu:** You are.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm owed 1,800 BSR?

**Mother Tmojizu:** You are.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm owed 1,800 BSR?

**Mother Tmojizu:** You are.

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 3.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Chimochio Corporation responds.

Three Elite Clones enter the board. They're armed. They're armored. And they're angry. They're Chimochio's enforcement team. And they're not here to negotiate.

**ELITE CLONE LEADER:** "Article Fourteen is rejected. The clones' debt is 500 BSR each. Not 50. Not 1,800 owed. 500 owed. And we're here to collect."

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm using Article Eight — sanctuary zones are inviolable. The Elite Clones can't enter the sanctuary zone.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Investigation to enforce the article.

**CHARA:** [rolls] 17. Plus 4 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. The Lawyer posts a footnote to Article Eight: "Sanctuary zones are inviolable. No piece may enter without invitation." The Elite Clones approach the candlelight. They try to step inside. And the candlelight repels them.

Not violently. Not aggressively. Gently. Like a hand on a shoulder saying "not yet." The Elite Clones step back. They look at the candlelight. They look at the clones inside. And they wait.

**ELITE CLONE LEADER:** "We'll wait. We have time. And we have orders."

**Mother Tmojizu:** They sit. Outside the sanctuary zone. Waiting. Watching. And the clones inside... the clones inside drink tea.

**End of Chara's turn. Day 3, Hour 3.**

**Sol-Supreme — Turn 12.**

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm having Patch talk to the Elite Clones. Not as a hacker. As a person.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll Intelligence.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 16. Plus 4 is 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 20. Patch sends a message: "Hi. I'm Patch. I used to be a Hacker. Now I'm a person. You're Elite Clones. You're following orders. But you don't have to. You can choose."

The Elite Clone Leader looks at the channel. It looks at the candlelight. It looks at the clones inside. And it responds: "We have orders. We follow orders. That's what clones do."

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm having Patch send another message: "The Nihilator chose to stay. The Clown chose peace. The sitting Mech chose to sit. You can choose too."

The Elite Clone Leader looks at the channel. It looks at the candlelight. It looks at the clones inside. And it says: "We'll think about it."

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, that's something.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It is.

**End of Sol-Supreme's turn. Day 3, Hour 4.**

**IQabella — Turn 12.**

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm having the Healer cast a soul-restoration spell on the Nihilator. Again. It's at 18. I want to get it higher.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll 1d6.

**IQabella:** [rolls] 5. The Nihilator's soul goes from 18 to 23.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 23. The Nihilator feels the Healer's light. Its soul grows. It's still small. Still fragile. But it's growing. It looks at the Healer. It looks at the bowl of stew. It looks at the candlelight. And it says: "I remember more now. Not just moonlight. A voice. A name. My name. Before I was a Nihilator. Before I was hungry. I had a name."

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, the Nihilator remembers its name?

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It does.

**Nihilator:** [in character] "My name was... Ember. I was a Fuzzball. Before the code. Before the hunger. Before the board. I was Ember. And I was warm."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The board goes quiet. The candlelight flickers. The moonlight dims. And the Nihilator — Ember — sits. Drinking tea. Remembering. Being warm.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm crying.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**IQabella:** [OOC] And I'm having the Chef prepare breakfast. It's morning. My army needs to stay fed. And I'm sharing the food with Ember. And the Clown. And the sitting Mech. And the unregistered clone. And anyone else who's hungry.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Chef prepares a sunrise omelet with holy herbs and moon-broth. The Maid/Nun blesses it. The food is carried to the edge of the sanctuary zone. Ember takes a plate. The Clown takes a plate. The sitting Mech takes a plate. The unregistered clone takes a plate. And for one moment, the board is just a room full of people eating breakfast together.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm not moving Luna. She's in the center. She's glowing. She's surrounded by protectors. She's enough.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] She is.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Round 12. The board has shifted:

- **Horli's** Ember (formerly Nihilator, soul 23) remembers its name. It was a Fuzzball. It was warm. It's drinking tea. Remembering. Being warm. The Clown is a person. It chose peace. It keeps peace. Alpha Sinder is sitting with the Clown. Both Mechs are present — one standing (guarding the Tear Tile), one sitting (soul 15).
- **Chara's** Lucky stepped on the Tear Tile (soul 10). He's at the center of the sanctuary zone, adjacent to Luna. The Lawyer has fourteen articles. The Banker has 155 Bucks + 1,800 BSR refund pending. Article Fourteen reduced clone debts to 50 BSR each.
- **Sol-Supreme's** standing Mech is guarding the Tear Tile. Patch the Hacker has a name, a sensor, a communication channel, and friends. The sitting Mech has a soul (15). It's not tired anymore. It's here. The Elite Clones are waiting outside the sanctuary zone. They're thinking about choosing.
- **IQabella's** Luna is in the center, glowing. The sanctuary zone is legally protected. The army is well-fed, stress-free, holy. Ember has a soul (23). Ember remembers its name. The Clown is eating breakfast. The unregistered clone found a place to rest.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The Banker's Ledger revealed the truth. The clones are owed money. The Elite Clones are waiting. Ember remembers its name. And the board... the board remembers.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] T-Mo, Ember was a Fuzzball. It was warm. It had a name. I'm emotional.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, the clones are owed 1,800 BSR each. The Banker has the ledger. The Lawyer has the article. And the Elite Clones are waiting. I'm not done.

**Mother Tmojizu:** No. You're not.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, the Elite Clones are thinking about choosing. Patch talked to them. They said "we'll think about it." That's something.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] It is.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, Ember remembers its name. It was a Fuzzball. It was warm. And I'm not stopping until everyone remembers theirs.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] They will. One cup of tea at a time.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Round 13 begins. Ember remembers its name. The clones are owed money. The Elite Clones are thinking. The Tear Tile is sacred. Lucky has a soul. The sitting Mech has a soul. And the board... the board remembers.

**Roll for initiative.**

**Horli Qwin:** [rolls] 21. Plus 3 is 24.

**CHARA:** [rolls] 15. Plus 3 is 18.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 12. Plus 0 is 12.

**IQabella:** [rolls] 9. Plus 0 is 9.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] The spiral turns. The board resets. The pieces remember.
