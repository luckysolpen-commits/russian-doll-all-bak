# CLONE CHRONICLES — SESSION 3: "THE QI EXCHANGE"
## Part 1: The Blood Market — Session 3 of 10
### Players: CHR-047 (Chara), SOL-001 (Sol-Supreme), IQA-112 (IQabella), HQW-006 (Horli Qwin) | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Day 3. You've earned 695 BSR total. You need 1,305 more. The morning bell rings. The market wakes up. And today, the Qi Exchange opens.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm already plotting my next hustle.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] I know. But first — the room. The cramped room. The four beds. The locked door. The serial numbers. The tea set.

**IQA-112:** [in character] I'm brewing tea. Mint again. I found another tea bag under the bed. I don't know how it got there. I don't want to know. I'm placing a cup by each bed.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] The tea is warm. The clones wake up. SOL-001 opens the door with his architect codes — the same flicker, the same click, the same "that shouldn't have worked" energy. You descend into the Blood Market.

It's different today. Quieter. The brokers are still calling prices, but there's a new energy — a hum, a vibration, like the market is holding its breath.

At the center of the street, a new building has appeared. It wasn't there yesterday. It's a temple — or something like one. Stone steps, incense smoke, a sign that reads: **THE QI EXCHANGE — "Qi Flows Where the Heart Leads."**

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, a building that wasn't there yesterday? Is that normal?

**Mother Tmojizu:** In the Blood Market? Nothing is normal. But this is Master Feng's doing. He's a monk. He doesn't build buildings. He manifests them.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm assessing the building before we go in. INT Investigation.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 15. Plus 4 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. You circle the building. Stone steps. Incense smoke. No visible doors — just an archway. Inside, you can see a large room with a central pool of water. The water is still. The surface reflects the ceiling. And around the pool, four banners hang — each one a different color, a different symbol.

Red banner: a crab claw. **Crustacean Bastion — Siege.**
Orange banner: an ape fist. **Primate Swarm — Horde.**
Blue banner: a centaur hoof. **Thunderhoof Legion — Mobile.**
White banner: an owl wing. **Aether Wings — Air.**

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Four factions. Siege beats Horde. Horde beats Mobile. Mobile beats Air. Air beats Siege. Rock-paper-scissors with armies."

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, is this the TEAR-IT faction system?

**Mother Tmojizu:** It is. The Qi Exchange is where clones learn about the factions, trade Qi Energy, and eventually — when they have enough power — choose a faction and build an army. But you're not there yet. Today, you're just learning.

**Mother Tmojizu:** You step through the archway.

The room is vast — larger than the building should allow. The central pool is still. The incense smoke curls upward in ϕ spirals. And sitting cross-legged beside the pool is Master Feng.

He's a monk — calm, ageless, wearing simple robes. His eyes are closed. His hands rest on his knees. He doesn't open them when you enter. He doesn't need to.

**MASTER FENG:** "CHR-047. SOL-001. IQA-112. HQW-006. Red-headed. Same batch. Different hearts. Welcome to the Qi Exchange."

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, does everyone in this market know our names?

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] Everyone who matters.

**MASTER FENG:** "Qi Energy is the lifeblood of faction warfare. It powers abilities, fuels armies, and tips the balance between victory and defeat. You can trade it. You can hold it. You can spend it. But you cannot create it. Qi flows where the heart leads."

**MASTER FENG:** "Today, I'm accepting new traders. The price of Qi fluctuates based on faction warfare news. Right now, the market is stable. Qi is 10 BSR per unit. But that can change."

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I want to trade Qi. I want to understand the market mechanics. INT Arcana to predict price movements.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 17. Plus 3 is 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 20. You close your eyes. You reach into your architect codes — the encrypted patterns, the hidden systems. And you see it: the Qi market is about to shift. The Primate Swarm faction is gaining ground in the underground skirmishes. Their Qi demand is rising. The price will go up — from 10 BSR to 15 BSR — within the next few hours.

**SOL-001:** [in character] "I'm buying Qi now. At 10 BSR. I'm buying 10 units."

**Mother Tmojizu:** That costs 100 BSR. You have 200. You'll have 100 left.

**SOL-001:** [in character] "I know. I'm investing."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Master Feng nods. He dips a ladle into the pool. The water glows — blue, electric, alive. He pours 10 units into a vial and hands it to SOL-001.

**MASTER FENG:** "Qi flows where the heart leads. Your heart leads to patterns. Be careful. Patterns can become prisons."

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm holding the Qi. I'll sell when the price goes up.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Noted.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I want to ask Master Feng about the factions. Which one aligns with peace?

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll WIS Insight.

**IQA-112:** [rolls] 18. Plus 3 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. You look at the four banners. The Crustacean Bastion is defensive — protective, but rigid. The Primate Swarm is aggressive — numerous, but chaotic. The Thunderhoof Legion is mobile — fast, but fragile. The Aether Wings are visionary — far-seeing, but detached.

None of them are peaceful. But the Aether Wings come closest — they see the whole board, not just the battle.

**IQA-112:** [in character] "Master Feng, I'm drawn to the Aether Wings. They see the whole board."

**MASTER FENG:** [opens his eyes] "The Aether Wings see far. But seeing far doesn't mean seeing clearly. Be careful what you look at, duckling."

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm buying 5 Qi units. At 10 BSR each. 50 BSR.

**Mother Tmojizu:** You have 75. You'll have 25 left.

**IQA-112:** [in character] "I know. I'm investing in vision."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Master Feng ladles 5 units into a vial. The water glows white — soft, warm, like moonlight. He hands it to IQA-112.

**MASTER FENG:** "Vision without warmth is just distance. Don't lose your warmth, duckling."

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I want to trade Qi too. But I want to understand the intelligence angle. INT Investigation to analyze the faction warfare patterns.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 14. Plus 4 is 18.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 18. You analyze the faction warfare patterns. The Crustacean Bastion is losing ground — their defensive posture is too slow. The Primate Swarm is gaining — their numbers are overwhelming. The Thunderhoof Legion is holding — their speed keeps them alive. The Aether Wings are watching — their range gives them information, but not power.

You see a pattern: the Primate Swarm will peak in 3 days. Then they'll overextend. Then the Thunderhoof Legion will counter-attack. Then the Crustacean Bastion will fortify. Then the Aether Wings will strike from above.

It's a cycle. And you know when to buy and sell.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "I'm buying 8 Qi units. At 10 BSR each. 80 BSR."

**Mother Tmojizu:** You have 180. You'll have 100 left.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "I know. I'm investing in information."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Master Feng ladles 8 units into a vial. The water glows grey — cold, precise, like a surveillance feed. He hands it to CHR-047.

**MASTER FENG:** "Information without action is just noise. Don't just watch, duckling. Act."

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm not buying Qi. I'm performing. I'm rapping in the Qi Exchange. CHA Performance.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans warmly] Of course you are.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 16. Plus 5 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. Horli-Six steps into the center of the Qi Exchange. She draws her rapier — "The Mic" — and she starts rapping.

**HQW-006:** [in character, rapping] "Yo, I'm HQW-006, red-headed and free / I don't need your Qi, I got Qi in me / Four factions, four banners, four ways to fight / But I'm the fifth faction — I'm the chaos tonight / Qi flows where the heart leads, and my heart leads to beats / And if you don't tip me, I'm stealing your seats!"

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Qi Exchange stops. Master Feng opens his eyes. The incense smoke pauses. And then — a coin. A BSR coin. It lands at Horli's feet. Then another. Then another. By the end of the performance, she's collected 60 BSR.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] SIXTY BSR! T-Mo, I'm the queen of this market!

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] The spiral favors the bold, little cat.

**MASTER FENG:** [quietly] "Chaos is a faction too. It just doesn't have a banner."

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Day 3. Let's count earnings:

- **SOL-001:** Clone labor (50) + Qi investment (holding 10 units at 10 BSR each) = **100 BSR liquid + 100 BSR in Qi**
- **HQW-006:** Busking (40) + Qi Exchange performance (60) = **100 BSR**
- **IQA-112:** Tea stand tips (25) + Qi investment (holding 5 units at 10 BSR each) = **25 BSR liquid + 50 BSR in Qi**
- **CHR-047:** Info brokering (60) + Qi investment (holding 8 units at 10 BSR each) = **100 BSR liquid + 80 BSR in Qi**

**Mother Tmojizu:** But wait — the Qi price just went up. SOL-001's prediction was right. Qi is now 15 BSR per unit.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] I'm selling my 10 units at 15 BSR each. That's 150 BSR.

**Mother Tmojizu:** You bought at 100. You sell at 150. Profit: 50 BSR. Your total liquid: 150 BSR.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] I'm selling my 8 units at 15 BSR each. That's 120 BSR. Profit: 40 BSR. My total liquid: 220 BSR.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm keeping mine. I like the way it glows.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] You keep yours. The white Qi glows softly in your pocket. It's warm. It's yours.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm not buying or selling Qi. I'm just vibing. And counting my 100 BSR.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] Fair enough.

**Mother Tomakazu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 3.

**Mother Tmojizu:** A clone's faction allegiance is discovered.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Whose?

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll WIS Insight to figure out who.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 12. Plus 3 is 15.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 15. You notice a clone — CHR-033, the one who bought your intel yesterday — being confronted by two Primate Swarm enforcers. They've discovered he's been secretly trading with the Thunderhoof Legion. In the faction world, that's betrayal.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "That's the guy who bought my intel. He's in trouble."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Should we help him?"

**CHR-047:** [in character] "I don't know. Should we?"

**SOL-001:** [in character] "He paid us. He's a customer. We protect our customers."

**IQA-112:** [in character] "He's a person. We help people."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Fine. But if this goes sideways, I'm rapping us out of it."

**Mother Tmojizu:** You approach the enforcers. They're large — Primate Swarm, orange banners, ape fists on their armor. They look at you. They look at CHR-033. They look at your serial numbers.

**ENFORCER:** "Clones. This doesn't concern you. Walk away."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "It concerns us. He's our customer. You touch him, you touch our business."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll CHA Intimidation.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 10. Plus 0 is 10.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 10. The enforcer laughs. "You're clones. You're property. You don't have customers. You have owners."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Actually, we have 400 BSR between us and a rapier. That makes us entrepreneurs."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll CHA Persuasion.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 19. Plus 5 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 24. The enforcer pauses. He looks at Horli's rapier. He looks at her serial number. He looks at the other clones. And he makes a calculation.

**ENFORCER:** "Fine. He's yours. But the Thunderhoof Legion will hear about this. And they don't forget."

**Mother Tmojizu:** They leave. CHR-033 looks at you. He's shaking.

**CHR-033:** "Thank you. I owe you. Here." He hands you a small vial. "It's a memory. A real one. It's worth 100 BSR. Take it."

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, do I take it?

**Mother Tmojizu:** You can. Or you can refuse. Or you can negotiate.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Keep it. Use it. And next time, don't get caught."

**Mother Tmojizu:** CHR-033 nods. He leaves. And you've made an enemy of the Primate Swarm enforcers — but an ally in CHR-033.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The sun sets. The market quiets. The clones return to their cramped room. The door locks from the outside. The serial numbers glow. And four red-headed clones lie on four cheap beds, counting their earnings.

150. 100. 25. 220.

Plus Qi holdings: 0 + 0 + 50 + 0 = 50 BSR in Qi.

Total liquid: 495 BSR. Total with Qi: 545 BSR. 1,455 to go.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The Qi Exchange closes. Master Feng sits by the pool. He looks at the four banners. He smiles. "Four red-headed clones," he says. "Four different hearts. Qi flows where the heart leads. Let's see where theirs leads."

Tomorrow, Scratch's Serial Shop is open. He's removing serial numbers today. But the police are watching.

**IQA-112:** [in character] I'm brewing one last cup of tea. And I'm saying: "Tomorrow, we face a choice. Keep our numbers or lose them. Stay property or become free."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I sell more intel."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "No."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Maybe."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] Sleep, ducklings. The market will be here in the morning. The spiral turns. The pieces remember.
