## SESSION OVERVIEW

The Qi Exchange opens. Master Feng offers Qi Energy trades — the per-turn resource that powers faction abilities. The clones learn about the TEAR-IT faction system (Siege > Horde > Mobile > Air) and start positioning themselves for the future empire game.

## KEY BEATS

1. **The Qi Exchange Opens** — Master Feng introduces Qi Energy. "Qi flows where the heart leads."
2. **Faction Introductions** — Clones learn about the four factions. Each is drawn to a different one.
3. **Qi Trading** — Buy, sell, and trade Qi Energy. Market volatility creates opportunities and risks.
4. **First Faction Allegiance** — Each clone tentatively aligns with a faction (not committed yet).
5. **End of Day 3** — Earnings: 200-500 BSR each. Hook for Session 4.

## QI TRADING MECHANICS

- Qi Energy price fluctuates based on faction warfare news
- Clones can buy Qi cheap and sell high, or hold it for future faction abilities
- INT (Arcana) to predict market movements
- CHA (Persuasion) to negotiate better prices with Master Feng
- Natural 20 = predict a market swing. Natural 1 = buy at the peak.
