# CLONE CHRONICLES — SESSION 6: "THE MARKET CRASH"
## Part 1: The Blood Market — Session 6 of 10
### Players: CHR-047 (Chara), SOL-001 (Sol-Supreme), IQA-112 (IQabella), HQW-006 (Horli Qwin) | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Day 6. You have 5,450 BSR total. You've paid off your 500 BSR debts. You're free. But freedom is expensive — because today, the market crashes.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm already panicking.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans warmly] Good. You should be.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The morning bell rings. But it's different today — lower, slower, like the bell itself is depressed. The market wakes up. And the first thing you hear is screaming.

Not pain screaming. Financial screaming.

**BROKER:** "BSR IS DOWN! DOWN 50%! EVERYTHING IS WORTH HALF!"

**Mother Tmojizu:** Your 5,450 BSR is now worth 2,725. Broker Vex's 1,000 BSR investment per clone is now worth 500. The House's cut is halved. The Memory Bar's prices are halved. The Qi Exchange's rates are halved. Everything is halved.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm assessing the crash. INT Investigation.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 19. Plus 4 is 23.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 23. You analyze the crash. It's not natural. Someone manipulated the BSR market — a massive sell-off, triggered by a single entity. You trace the transaction logs. The sell-off came from Chimochio Corporation itself. They're deflating the currency — making clones' earnings worth less, keeping them in debt.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Chimochio crashed the market on purpose. They're deflating the currency to keep clones in debt. This isn't a crash. It's a control mechanism."

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, can I use my architect codes to access Chimochio's transaction logs?

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Arcana.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 18. Plus 3 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. Your architect codes flicker. A door opens — not a physical door, a digital one. You see Chimochio's internal ledger. The crash was authorized by Executive Director Voss — a human, not a clone, with access to the BSR mint.

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Executive Director Voss authorized the crash. I have the transaction logs. I can prove it."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "That's worth more than BSR. That's leverage."

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I want to help clones who are panicking. I'm setting up a tea stand — free tea, emotional support. CHA Persuasion.

**Mother Tomakazu:** Roll it.

**IQA-112:** [rolls] 20. Plus 4 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. IQA-112 sets up her tea stand in the middle of the chaos. The tea is warm. The clones come — panicked, crying, desperate. She serves tea. She listens. She holds hands. She says: "It's going to be okay. We'll rebuild. We always do."

And then — a tip. A clone drops 50 BSR (now worth 25). Then another. Then another. By the end of the day, she's collected 200 BSR in tips (now worth 100).

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, that's the most beautiful thing that's happened all session.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] It is.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm buying. If BSR is down 50%, I'm buying everything. I'm going to the Memory Bar, the Qi Exchange, the Clone Exchange — I'm buying low.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Investigation to find the best deals.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 11. Plus 0 is 11.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 11. You buy — but you buy blindly. You purchase 10 memory vials (worthless in a crash), 5 Qi units (volatile), and a "premium" clone labor contract (which turns out to be a scam). You spend 500 BSR (now worth 250). You lost money.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I got scammed.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] You bought blindly in a crash. You got scammed.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] Fair.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm using the transaction logs to negotiate with Broker Vex. I'm showing him that Chimochio crashed the market on purpose. INT Persuasion.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 15. Plus 1 is 16.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 16. Broker Vex looks at the logs. He looks at you. He looks at the market. And he makes a decision.

**BROKER VEX:** "You're right. Chimochio crashed the market. And I'm tired of being their puppet. Here's what I'll do: I'll match your investment. You put in 1,000 BSR, I'll put in 1,000 BSR. We'll buy the crash. We'll hold. And when the market recovers, we split the profit."

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm in. 1,000 BSR.

**Mother Tmojizu:** You and Broker Vex pool 2,000 BSR (now worth 1,000). You buy crash assets — undervalued Qi, discounted memory vials, and cheap clone labor contracts. You hold.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm selling the intel about Chimochio's crash to the highest bidder. Who's buying?

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll CHA Persuasion to find a buyer.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 17. Plus 2 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. You find three buyers:
- **The House** offers 300 BSR (now 150).
- **Master Feng** offers a favor instead of BSR — "Qi flows where the heart leads."
- **Officer Krell** offers legal protection — "I'll look the other way for 30 days."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "I'll take Krell's protection. 30 days of looking the other way is worth more than BSR."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Officer Krell nods. "30 days. Don't waste them."

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 5.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Original appears.

Not all four originals. Just one. CHR-047's original — the real Chara, the ex-CIA operative, the woman whose DNA was used to make CHR-047. She walks into the Blood Market. She sees CHR-047. She stops.

**THE ORIGINAL CHARA:** "You're me. But you're not me. You're red-headed. I'm not. You're a clone. I'm not. But you have my memories. My instincts. My paranoia."

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm... I'm looking at myself. But not myself.

**Mother Tmojizu:** You are. She's taller. Older. Harder. But the eyes are the same. The scan is the same. The paranoia is the same.

**THE ORIGINAL CHARA:** "I came to warn you. Chimochio didn't just crash the market. They're planning something bigger. A clone roundup. They're going to collect all clones with removed serial numbers and re-register them. Force them back into the system."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "When?"

**THE ORIGINAL CHARA:** "Soon. You need to be ready."

**Mother Tmojizu:** She turns. She walks away. And CHR-047 is left standing in the Blood Market, staring at her own face — older, harder, gone.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm emotional.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Day 6. Let's count earnings:

- **SOL-001:** 1,200 - 1,000 (investment) = **200 BSR liquid** + 1,000 BSR in crash assets (now worth 500)
- **HQW-006:** 2,000 - 500 (scammed purchases) = **1,500 BSR liquid** (now worth 750)
- **IQA-112:** 1,100 + 200 (tea tips) = **1,300 BSR liquid** (now worth 650)
- **CHR-047:** 1,150 + 0 (took protection instead of BSR) = **1,150 BSR liquid** (now worth 575)

**Total: 4,150 BSR liquid (now worth 2,075) + 500 BSR in crash assets.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The market crashed. The clones lost half their money. But they gained something more valuable: information. Chimochio is planning a clone roundup. The Original warned them. And 30 days of Officer Krell's protection is ticking.

**IQA-112:** [in character] I'm brewing one last cup of tea. And I'm saying: "Tomorrow, the Clone Exchange opens. We need to buy clones. We need to build a workforce. And we need to prepare for the roundup."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I open a door I don't understand. Again."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I prepare for war."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I rap for more coins. And definitely steal something."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "No."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Maybe."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] Sleep, ducklings. The market will be here in the morning. The spiral turns. The pieces remember.
