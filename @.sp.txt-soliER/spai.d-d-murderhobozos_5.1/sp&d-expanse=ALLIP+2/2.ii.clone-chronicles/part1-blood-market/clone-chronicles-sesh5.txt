# CLONE CHRONICLES — SESSION 5: "THE UNDERGROUND XIRQUS"
## Part 1: The Blood Market — Session 5 of 10
### Players: CHR-047 (Chara), SOL-001 (Sol-Supreme), IQA-112 (IQabella), HQW-006 (Horli Qwin) | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Day 5. You're broke. Three of you have bare wrists. One has a glowing serial number. You owe each other money. And today, you receive invitations.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm already rapping in my head.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] I know. But first — the room. The cramped room. The four beds. The locked door. The serial numbers. The tea set.

**IQA-112:** [in character] I'm brewing tea. Mint again. I found another tea bag. I don't ask questions anymore. I'm placing a cup by each bed. And I'm placing four envelopes on the pillows.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] The tea is warm. The clones wake up. SOL-001 opens the door with his architect codes — the same flicker, the same click. You descend into the Blood Market.

But before you reach the street, IQA-112 notices the envelopes. She opens hers. The others open theirs.

Each envelope is black, sealed with a golden ϕ spiral. Inside, a single card:

*"You are invited to the Underground Xirqus Social Event. Tonight. The Velvet Room. Reputation is currency. Dress accordingly. — The House"*

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm assessing the invitation. INT Investigation.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 18. Plus 4 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 22. The invitation is real. The ϕ spiral seal is authentic — it matches the Chimochio Corporation's official mark. But the Underground Xirqus isn't run by Chimochio. It's run by The House — an anonymous, omnipresent operator who takes a cut of everything in the Blood Market.

The Velvet Room is a private club — exclusive, lavish, dangerous. Clones are rarely invited. When they are, it means The House has noticed them. And The House doesn't notice clones unless they're useful.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "The House invited us. That means we're on their radar. This is either an opportunity or a trap."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "It's both. It's always both."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "We're going. We need the connections. And we need the money."

**IQA-112:** [in character] "We need to look presentable. We don't have clothes."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] You don't. You're wearing cheap market clothes. But the Blood Market has a tailor — a clone named TLR-089 who makes clothes for clones who can't afford clothes. He works on credit.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I want to visit the tailor. I'm negotiating clothes on credit. CHA Persuasion.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**IQA-112:** [rolls] 17. Plus 4 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. IQA-112 finds TLR-089 — a nervous clone with a measuring tape around his neck and a sewing machine in a closet. She negotiates: four outfits, on credit, payable after the event. He agrees — because IQA-112 is warm, and warmth is rare in the Blood Market.

The outfits are simple but clean. Dark colors. No serial numbers visible (for the three bare wrists). CHR-047's outfit has a high collar — hiding nothing, but looking sharp.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The sun sets. The Velvet Room opens.

It's not a room. It's a cathedral — repurposed, renovated, glowing with golden light. Chandeliers hang from the ceiling. Music plays — soft, ambient, expensive. Clones and non-clones mingle in designer clothes, drinking drinks you can't name, talking deals you can't afford.

And at the center of it all — The House. You don't see them. You feel them. A presence. A voice in the walls.

**THE HOUSE:** "CHR-047. SOL-001. IQA-112. HQW-006. Red-headed. Same batch. Three bare wrists. One glowing serial number. Welcome to the Underground Xirqus."

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, does The House know everything?

**Mother Tmojizu:** The House knows everything that matters.

**THE HOUSE:** "Reputation is currency. Tonight, you'll earn both. Network. Deal. Hustle. And remember — The House always collects."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Okay. You're in the Velvet Room. Each clone chooses a networking target. Roll CHA Persuasion to build reputation.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] I'm approaching Broker Vex. He's at the Clone Exchange, but he's here tonight. I want to negotiate a better labor rate. CHA Persuasion.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 15. Plus 2 is 17.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 17. You approach Broker Vex. He's drinking something dark, reading a ledger, and ignoring everyone. You sit down. You don't speak. You wait.

**BROKER VEX:** "CHR-047. You're the one who discovered the debt inflation. 200 BSR real cost, 500 BSR charged. You sold that intel for 60 BSR. You could have sold it for 600."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "I sold it for what the buyer could afford. Not what it was worth."

**BROKER VEX:** [pauses] "Interesting. I'll raise your labor rate to 75 BSR a day. And I'll give you access to premium job listings. But you owe me a favor. One favor. I'll call it when I need it."

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, do I accept?

**Mother Tmojizu:** You can. Or you can negotiate. Or you can refuse.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "I accept. But the favor has limits. I won't hurt other clones."

**BROKER VEX:** "Noted. The favor is logged."

**Mother Tmojizu:** CHR-047 earns 150 BSR in reputation value — a better labor rate, premium job listings, and a favor owed to Broker Vex.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm approaching Master Feng. He's here too. I want to discuss Qi trading strategies. INT Arcana.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 20. Plus 3 is 23.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. You approach Master Feng. He's sitting in a corner, eyes closed, hands on his knees. You sit across from him. You don't speak. You wait.

**MASTER FENG:** "SOL-001. Encrypted architect codes. You opened a door you shouldn't have been able to open. You traded Qi at the right time. You predicted the market. And now you're here — at the Velvet Room — because The House noticed you."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "I want to understand the Qi market. Not just trade it. Understand it."

**MASTER FENG:** [opens his eyes] "The Qi market is a reflection of faction warfare. When the Primate Swarm gains ground, Qi demand rises. When the Crustacean Bastion fortifies, Qi demand falls. When the Thunderhoof Legion counters, Qi demand spikes. When the Aether Wings strike, Qi demand crashes."

**MASTER FENG:** "But there's a deeper pattern. The cycle repeats every 12 days. You're on Day 5. The Primate Swarm peaks on Day 8. The Thunderhoof Legion counters on Day 10. The Crustacean Bastion fortifies on Day 12. The Aether Wings strike on Day 14. And then it starts again."

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, that's incredible intel. I'm memorizing this.

**Mother Tmojizu:** You earn 200 BSR in reputation value — Master Feng's trust, Qi market insights, and a 12-day cycle prediction.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm approaching Mnemosyne. She's here too. I want to visit my memory — the one I traded. The warm tea memory.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll WIS Insight.

**IQA-112:** [rolls] 19. Plus 3 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 22. You find Mnemosyne at the bar. She's serving drinks to clones and non-clones alike. You sit down. You don't order. You just look at her.

**MNEMOSYNE:** "IQA-112. You want to visit your memory. The warm tea. The chipped cups. The way SOL-001 said 'thank you.'"

**IQA-112:** [in character] "Yes."

**MNEMOSYNE:** She reaches behind the bar. She pulls out a vial — warm, golden, glowing. She places it in front of you. "Look. But don't touch. It's not yours anymore."

**Mother Tmojizu:** You look. The memory is there — warm, golden, alive. You see yourself brewing tea in the cramped room. You see the chipped cups. You see SOL-001 saying "thank you." And you feel it — the warmth, the kindness, the choice.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm crying.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**MNEMOSYNE:** "A memory shared is a memory doubled. It's still here. It's still warm. And it's still yours — just... shared."

**Mother Tmojizu:** IQA-112 earns 100 BSR in reputation value — Mnemosyne's respect, a standing invitation to visit her traded memories, and a reputation for warmth in a cold market.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm approaching The House. I don't know where they are, but I'm rapping at the center of the room. CHA Performance.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans warmly] Of course you are.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 20. Plus 5 is 25.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. Horli-Six steps into the center of the Velvet Room. She draws her rapier — "The Mic" — and she raps.

**HQW-006:** [in character, rapping] "Yo, I'm HQW-006, red-headed and free / Three bare wrists and a debt to pay / But I'm rapping so loud, The House gonna pay / Reputation is currency, and I'm rich in beats / And if you don't tip me, I'm stealing your seats!"

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Velvet Room stops. The chandeliers flicker. The music pauses. And then — a coin. A BSR coin. It lands at Horli's feet. Then another. Then another. Then a platinum coin — worth 100 BSR. Then another. Then another.

By the end of the performance, she's collected 500 BSR.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] FIVE HUNDRED BSR?! T-MO!

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] The spiral favors the bold, little cat. Even in a cathedral.

**THE HOUSE:** [from the walls] "HQW-006. You're entertaining. You're chaotic. You're useful. I have a deal for you."

**HQW-006:** [OOC] I'm listening.

**THE HOUSE:** "Work for me. I'll make you rich. But you'll owe me. A favor. One favor. I'll call it when I need it."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "What's the favor?"

**THE HOUSE:** "I'll tell you when I need it. But it won't hurt other clones. I promise."

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, do I accept?

**Mother Tmojizu:** You can. Or you can negotiate. Or you can refuse.

**HQW-006:** [in character] "I accept. But if the favor hurts other clones, I walk."

**THE HOUSE:** "Noted. The favor is logged."

**Mother Tmojizu:** HQW-006 earns 500 BSR in reputation value — The House's attention, a standing invitation to the Velvet Room, and a favor owed to The House.

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 4.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Broker Vex attends the event — and he wants to negotiate.

**BROKER VEX:** "Clones. I've been watching you. Day 1: 175 BSR. Day 2: 695. Day 3: 545. Day 4: broke. Day 5: whatever you just earned. You're volatile. But you're profitable. I want to invest in you."

**BROKER VEX:** "Here's the deal: I'll give each of you 1,000 BSR. In exchange, I get 10% of your future earnings. Forever."

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, that's a predatory deal. 10% forever?

**Mother Tmojizu:** It is. But 1,000 BSR each would put you at 1,000+ BSR liquid. You could pay off your debts faster. You could invest. You could grow.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I want to negotiate. INT Investigation to find a better deal.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 16. Plus 3 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. You analyze the deal. 10% forever is predatory. But 5% for 30 days is fair. You counter-offer.

**SOL-001:** [in character] "5% for 30 days. Then the deal expires."

**BROKER VEX:** [pauses] "7% for 20 days."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "5% for 15 days. Final offer."

**BROKER VEX:** [smiles] "5% for 15 days. Done."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Each clone receives 1,000 BSR from Broker Vex. In exchange, he gets 5% of their earnings for the next 15 days.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Day 5. Let's count earnings:

- **SOL-001:** 1,000 BSR (Vex investment) + 200 BSR (reputation) = **1,200 BSR**
- **HQW-006:** 500 BSR (performance) + 500 BSR (reputation) + 1,000 BSR (Vex investment) = **2,000 BSR**
- **IQA-112:** 100 BSR (reputation) + 1,000 BSR (Vex investment) = **1,100 BSR**
- **CHR-047:** 150 BSR (reputation) + 1,000 BSR (Vex investment) = **1,150 BSR**

**Total: 5,450 BSR.**

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm a millionaire.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] You're a clone with 2,000 BSR. But yes. You're a millionaire in spirit.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The sun sets. The Velvet Room closes. The clones return to their cramped room. The door locks from the outside. Three bare wrists. One glowing serial number. And four red-headed clones lie on four cheap beds, counting their earnings.

1,150. 1,200. 1,100. 2,000.

5,450 BSR total. They're no longer broke. They're no longer hustling for scraps. They're market players.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] But tomorrow, the market crashes. BSR value drops 50%. Panic. Opportunity. Chaos. And the clones will have to decide: hold, sell, or buy.

**IQA-112:** [in character] I'm brewing one last cup of tea. And I'm saying: "Tomorrow, the market crashes. Tomorrow, we lose half our money. But tomorrow, we also learn what we're made of."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I open a door I don't understand. Again."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I buy low and sell high."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I rap for more coins. And definitely steal something."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "No."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Maybe."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] Sleep, ducklings. The market will be here in the morning. The spiral turns. The pieces remember.
