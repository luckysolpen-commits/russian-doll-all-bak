## SESSION OVERVIEW

The clones wake up in a cramped room in the Blood Market. They have nothing. Each owes 500 BSR. Their serial numbers glow on their wrists. They're red-headed, same DNA batch. The morning bell rings. The market wakes up. Five days to start earning.

## OPENING SCENE

> Mother Tmojizu: "The morning bell rings. The Blood Market wakes up. You hear brokers calling prices — 'Clone labor, 50 BSR a day!' 'Memory fragments, fresh batch!' 'Qi Energy, buy now, sell later!' You're in your room. Four clones. Four red heads. Four serial numbers. Four debts. What do you do?"

## KEY BEATS

1. **Wake Up & Assess** — Clones discover their situation: 500 BSR debt each, serial numbers, red hair, cramped room
2. **Explore the Market** — First walk through the Blood Market. Meet Broker Vex, see the Clone Exchange, hear the prices
3. **Choose First Hustles** — Each clone picks their opening move
4. **First Hustle Attempts** — Skill checks, roleplay, small wins and losses
5. **End of Day 1** — Count earnings. Set goals for Day 2.
