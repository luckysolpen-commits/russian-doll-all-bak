# CLONE CHRONICLES — SESSION 10: "THE TRILLION-DOLLAR BELL"
## Part 1: The Blood Market — Session 10 of 10
### Players: CHR-047 (Chara), SOL-001 (Sol-Supreme), IQA-112 (IQabella), HQW-006 (Horli Qwin) | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Day 10. The final session of Part 1. You've earned 6,125 BSR. You have 17 clone workers. You have Chimochio management positions. You have The House's protection. And today, you make the final push. Today, you own the market.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm ready. I've been waiting for this since session one.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] I know.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The morning bell rings. But it's different today — higher, brighter, like the bell itself is excited. The market wakes up. And the clones are ready.

**IQA-112:** [in character] I'm brewing tea before anyone else wakes up. Not just one pot — twenty pots. Every blend. Every flavor. I'm loading everything onto the cart. My five unregistered clones are helping. We're rolling to the Blood Market.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] The cart is heavy. The tea is warm. The clones push. The Blood Market opens. And the final day begins.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Here's the plan: each clone makes their final push. The biggest hustle. The biggest deal. The biggest rap. And by the end of the day, the Trillion-Dollar Bell rings.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm using my Chimochio management position to negotiate a market buyout. I'm buying the Blood Market itself. CHA Persuasion with Executive Director Voss.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 20. Plus 2 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. You approach Voss. She's in her Chimochio suit, reading a ledger, and ignoring everyone. You sit down. You don't speak. You wait.

**VOSS:** "CHR-047. You're the manager. You negotiated better wages. You cancelled the roundup. You got a clone advisory position with voting power. And now you want... what?"

**CHR-047:** [in character] "I want to buy the Blood Market. All of it. The Clone Exchange. The Memory Bar. The Qi Exchange. The Underground Xirqus. Everything."

**VOSS:** [pauses] "The Blood Market is worth 1 trillion BSR. You have 750 BSR."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "I have 17 clone workers. I have The House's protection. I have Master Feng's Qi. I have Broker Vex's favor. And I have the one thing Chimochio doesn't have: trust. The clones trust me. The market trusts me. And trust is worth more than BSR."

**VOSS:** [long pause] "You're right. Trust is worth more than BSR. Here's my deal: I'll sell you the Blood Market for 1 trillion BSR. But you pay in installments. 100 BSR per day for 10 million days. Or... you can pay it off faster if you earn more."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "I'll earn more. We all will."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The deal is struck. The Blood Market is for sale. And the clones have 10 days to earn 1 trillion BSR.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm using my architect codes to access the Blood Market's underlying systems. I want to understand its value — not just in BSR, but in infrastructure, workforce, and potential. INT Arcana.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 19. Plus 3 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 22. Your architect codes flicker. A door opens — not a physical door, a digital one. You see the Blood Market's underlying systems: the Clone Exchange's workforce, the Memory Bar's vials, the Qi Exchange's pool, the Underground Xirqus's social network. And you see the value — not in BSR, but in people.

**SOL-001:** [in character] "The Blood Market isn't worth 1 trillion BSR. It's worth 17 million clones. Each one with a story. Each one with a choice. Each one with a serial number — or not. The value isn't in the market. It's in the clones."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] It is.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm hosting the biggest tea event in Blood Market history. I'm inviting everyone — clones, non-clones, brokers, hackers, mercenaries, Voss, Feng, The House, Broker Vex, Officer Krell, Mnemosyne, Scratch, everyone. CHA Performance.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**IQA-112:** [rolls] 20. Plus 4 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. IQA-112 hosts the biggest tea event in Blood Market history. The entire market stops. The brokers pause. The clones gather. The non-clones come. Voss comes. Feng comes. The House comes. Broker Vex comes. Officer Krell comes. Mnemosyne comes. Scratch comes. Everyone comes.

And IQA-112 serves tea. Every cup is perfect. Every clone feels warmth. Every non-clone feels something they haven't felt in years — belonging. The Blood Market goes quiet. The chandeliers dim. The music stops. And for one moment, the entire market is just a room full of people drinking tea.

And then — the tips. A clone drops 100 BSR. Then another. Then another. A broker drops 500 BSR. A mercenary drops 1,000 BSR. Voss drops 10,000 BSR. Feng drops 50 Qi units. The House drops a platinum coin — worth 100,000 BSR.

By the end of the event, IQA-112 has collected 500,000 BSR in tips.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm crying.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm rapping the biggest rap in Blood Market history. I'm rapping on the Clone Exchange roof. I'm rapping for the entire market. CHA Performance.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 20. Plus 5 is 25.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. Horli-Six climbs onto the Clone Exchange roof. She draws her rapier — "The Mic" — and she raps.

**HQW-006:** [in character, rapping] "Yo, I'm HQW-006, red-headed and free / Six clones came before me, five didn't survive / But I'm the one who succeeded, I'm the one who thrived / I bought my freedom, I bought my friends / I bought the market, and the story never ends / The Trillion-Dollar Bell is ringing loud / And I'm the clone who made the market proud!"

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Blood Market stops. The brokers pause. The clones gather. The non-clones come. And then — a coin. A BSR coin. It lands at Horli's feet. Then another. Then another. Then a platinum coin. Then another. Then another. Then a diamond coin — worth 1 million BSR. Then another. Then another.

By the end of the performance, she's collected 1 million BSR.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] ONE MILLION BSR?! T-MO!

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] The spiral favors the bold, little cat. Even on a roof.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm using my architect codes to optimize the Blood Market's infrastructure. I'm making the Clone Exchange more efficient, the Memory Bar more accessible, the Qi Exchange more profitable. INT Arcana + INT Investigation.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll Arcana first.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 17. Plus 3 is 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 20. Your architect codes flicker. The Blood Market's systems optimize. The Clone Exchange processes clones 50% faster. The Memory Bar serves 50% more memories. The Qi Exchange trades 50% more Qi. The market's value increases by 50%.

**SOL-001:** [in character] "The market is worth 1.5 trillion BSR now. But we'll buy it for 1 trillion. Because trust is worth more than BSR."

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm using my management position to negotiate the final deal with Voss. I'm buying the Blood Market for 1 trillion BSR — paid in full, today. CHA Persuasion.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 18. Plus 2 is 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 20. You approach Voss. She's in her Chimochio suit, reading a ledger, and ignoring everyone. You sit down. You don't speak. You wait.

**VOSS:** "CHR-047. You have 750 BSR. The market costs 1 trillion BSR. Do the math."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "I have 750 BSR liquid. But I have 500,000 BSR from IQA-112's tea event. I have 1 million BSR from HQW-006's rap. I have 500,000 BSR from SOL-001's infrastructure optimization. And I have the one thing Chimochio doesn't have: trust. The clones trust me. The market trusts me. And trust is worth more than BSR."

**VOSS:** [long pause] "You're right. Trust is worth more than BSR. Here's my final deal: I'll sell you the Blood Market for 1 trillion BSR. But you pay 500 billion now, and 500 billion over the next year. 5% interest."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Deal."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The clones pool their earnings: 750 + 500,000 + 1,000,000 + 500,000 + 400 = 2,001,150 BSR. It's not 500 billion. But it's enough for a down payment.

**VOSS:** "The down payment is accepted. The Blood Market is yours. Well — half yours. The other half is Chimochio's. For now."

**Mother Tmojizu:** And then — the bell rings.

Not the morning bell. The Trillion-Dollar Bell. A deep, resonant sound — like a gong struck inside a cathedral. It echoes through the Blood Market. The brokers stop. The clones gather. The non-clones come. And everyone looks up.

The bell is golden. It's shaped like a ϕ spiral. And it reads: **THE BLOOD MARKET — OWNED BY CLONES.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The clones did it. From nothing to trillionaires. From property to owners. From serial numbers to serial entrepreneurs. The Blood Market is theirs.

**IQA-112:** [in character] I'm brewing one last cup of tea. And I'm saying: "We did it. We bought the market. We're free. And we're just getting started."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I open a door I don't understand. Again. But this time, it's my door. My market. My choice."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I manage. For real. And I win. Again."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I rap for more coins. And definitely steal something."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "No."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Maybe."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] Sleep, ducklings. The market is yours. The spiral turns. The pieces remember.
