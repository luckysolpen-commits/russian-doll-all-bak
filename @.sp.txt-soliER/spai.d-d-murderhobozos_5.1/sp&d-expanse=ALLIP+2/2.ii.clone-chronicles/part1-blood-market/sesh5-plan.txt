# PART 1 — SESSION 5: "THE UNDERGROUND XIRQUS"
## Clone Chronicles — Session 5 of 10
### BSR: 1K → 5K | Status: Social layer, reputation building

---

## SESSION OVERVIEW

The Underground Xirqus hosts a social event. Reputation is currency. The clones attend, network, and make their first big deals. This is where they transition from hustlers to market players.

## KEY BEATS

1. **The Invitation** — The clones receive invitations to the Underground Xirqus social event.
2. **The Event** — Lavish, exclusive, dangerous. Clones are rarely invited. They stand out.
3. **Networking** — CHA (Persuasion) checks to build reputation. INT (Investigation) to find opportunities.
4. **First Big Deals** — Clones negotiate their first major contracts: info brokering, Qi trading, clone labor management.
5. **End of Day 5** — Earnings: 1K-5K BSR each. Hook for Session 6.

## REPUTATION MECHANICS

- Each clone has a **Reputation Score** (starts at 0)
- CHA (Persuasion) increases reputation
- WIS (Insight) detects opportunities and threats
- Natural 20 = legendary reputation boost. Natural 1 = scandal.
- Reputation affects future deals: higher rep = better prices, more trust.

**The House's Interest:** The House notices the clones. It offers them a deal: "Work for us. We'll make you rich. But you'll owe us."
