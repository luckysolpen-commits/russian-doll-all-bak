# PART 1 — SESSION 4: "THE SERIAL SHOP"
## Clone Chronicles — Session 4 of 10
### BSR: 500 → 1,000 | Status: Illegal dealings, police risk, moral choices

---

## SESSION OVERVIEW

Scratch's Serial Shop is open. He removes serial numbers — the mark of clone ownership. It's illegal. The police are watching. The clones must decide: pay the premium and risk arrest, or keep their numbers and remain property.

## KEY BEATS

1. **Discover Scratch's Shop** — Hidden alley, paranoid gnome, premium prices. "I don't ask questions. I don't answer them either."
2. **The Decision** — Each clone decides: remove the serial number or keep it?
3. **The Procedure** — DEX (Sleight of Hand) to avoid police detection during the removal.
4. **Police Risk** — Officer Krell patrols the area. Clones must hide or bluff their way through.
5. **End of Day 4** — Earnings: 500-1,000 BSR each. Hook for Session 5.

## SERIAL REMOVAL MECHANICS

- **Cost:** 300 BSR per removal
- **Risk:** Police detection (DEX save or arrested)
- **Reward:** No serial number = no tracking, no ownership, but also no clone network access
- **Moral Choice:** Some clones may choose to keep their numbers — a statement of identity

**Officer Krell's Patrol:** If caught, clones can:
- **Bluff** (CHA Deception): "We're just shopping."
- **Bribe** (pay 100 BSR): Krell looks the other way.
- **Run** (DEX Acrobatics): Escape through the market.
- **Fight** (combat): Dangerous. Krell has backup.
