# PART 1: "THE BLOOD MARKET" — Overview
## Clone Chronicles Arc 1 — From Nothing to Trillions
### 10 Sessions | BSR: 0 → 1 Trillion

---

## THE ARC

The clones wake up in a cramped room in the Blood Market of Troll City. They have nothing — no money, no connections, no freedom. Each owes 500 BSR (their creation debt). Their serial numbers glow on their wrists. They're red-headed, same DNA batch, same vat. But they're not the same person.

Over 10 sessions, they hustle, trade, scheme, and build their way from zero to one trillion BSR. They don't just buy their freedom — they buy the market itself.

## KEY LOCATIONS

1. **The Clone Exchange** — Where clones are bought, sold, traded. Run by Broker Vex.
2. **The Memory Bar** — Trade memory fragments for BSR. Run by Mnemosyne (ancient spirit).
3. **The Serial Shop** — Illegal serial number removal. Run by Scratch (paranoid gnome).
4. **The Qi Exchange** — Trade Qi Energy for faction abilities. Run by Master Feng (calm monk).
5. **The Underground Xirqus** — Social layer, deals, reputation. Run by The House (anonymous).
6. **The Blood Market Streets** — Where the hustle happens — street vendors, informants, police, desperate clones.

## CORE MECHANICS

### Hustle System
Each session, clones choose hustles. Each hustle involves skill checks, roleplay, and risk/reward:

| Hustle | Skills | Risk | Reward |
|--------|--------|------|--------|
| Memory Trading | CHA (Deception), WIS (Insight) | Low — might lose real memory | 50-150 BSR |
| Clone Labor | STR/DEX, CON saves | Medium — exhaustion | 50 BSR/day |
| Info Brokering | INT (Investigation), CHA (Persuasion) | High — dangerous intel | 100-300 BSR |
| Qi Trading | INT (Arcana), CHA (Persuasion) | Medium — volatility | 75-200 BSR |
| Serial Removal | DEX (Sleight of Hand), INT | Very High — illegal | 200-500 BSR |
| Tear-It Hustle | CHA (Performance), DEX | Low — gambling | 0-400 BSR |

### Complication Table (d6, rolled mid-session)
| Roll | Complication |
|------|-------------|
| 1 | Police Raid — hide or fight |
| 2 | Memory Plague — corrupted memories spread |
| 3 | Market Crash — BSR drops 50% |
| 4 | Clone Uprising — rogue clones attack |
| 5 | The Original Appears — confrontation |
| 6 | Chimochio Audit — debts forgiven or doubled |

### Loyalty System
- Starts at 50% (neutral)
- Drops when clones are exploited, betrayed, or treated as property
- Rises when clones make choices, earn respect, or help each other
- 0% = goes rogue (becomes a Clown piece)
- 100% = fully sovereign (serial number fades naturally)
