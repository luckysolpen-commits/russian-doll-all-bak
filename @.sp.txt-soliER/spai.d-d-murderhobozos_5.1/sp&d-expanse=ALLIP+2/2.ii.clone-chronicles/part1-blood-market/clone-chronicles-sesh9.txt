# CLONE CHRONICLES — SESSION 9: "THE HOUSE ALWAYS COLLECTS"
## Part 1: The Blood Market — Session 9 of 10
### Players: CHR-047 (Chara), SOL-001 (Sol-Supreme), IQA-112 (IQabella), HQW-006 (Horli Qwin) | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Day 9. The deals are struck. The roundup is cancelled. SOL-001's codes are monitored. And today, The House always collects.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm nervous. My first tea event at the Underground Xirqus.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] You'll be fine. You're IQA-112. You're warm. You're assertive. And you make the best tea in the Blood Market.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The morning bell rings. The market wakes up. But today, the Velvet Room calls. The Underground Xirqus is hosting IQA-112's first tea event — and The House is watching.

**IQA-112:** [in character] I'm brewing tea before anyone else wakes up. Not just one pot — ten pots. Mint, chamomile, green, black, oolong, white, herbal, moon-broth, sunrise blend, and the special blend — the one that tastes like freedom. I'm loading everything onto a cart. My five unregistered clones are helping. We're rolling to the Velvet Room.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] The cart is heavy. The tea is warm. The clones push. The Velvet Room opens. And the Underground Xirqus is waiting.

It's packed. Clones, non-clones, brokers, hackers, mercenaries, and The House — everywhere. The chandeliers glow. The music plays. And the tea cart rolls in.

**THE HOUSE:** "IQA-112. The 112th. Your first tea event. The House is watching. The House is thirsty. Serve."

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm serving tea to everyone. CHA Performance.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**IQA-112:** [rolls] 20. Plus 4 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. IQA-112 serves tea. Every cup is perfect. Every clone feels warmth. Every non-clone feels something they haven't felt in years — kindness. The Velvet Room goes quiet. The chandeliers dim. The music stops. And for one moment, the Underground Xirqus is just a room full of people drinking tea.

And then — the tips. A clone drops 50 BSR. Then another. Then another. A broker drops 100 BSR. A mercenary drops 200 BSR. The House drops a platinum coin — worth 1,000 BSR.

By the end of the event, IQA-112 has collected 2,000 BSR in tips.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm crying.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**THE HOUSE:** "IQA-112. Your tea event was... extraordinary. The House is pleased. Your cut is 5%, as negotiated. But I'm adding a bonus: 500 BSR for exceptional service."

**IQA-112:** [in character] "Thank you, House. But the tea is free. Always."

**THE HOUSE:** "Nothing is free, duckling. But some things are worth more than their price."

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm using my management position to negotiate better wages for the clone workforce. CHA Persuasion with Executive Director Voss.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 17. Plus 2 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. You approach Voss. She's in her Chimochio suit, reading a ledger, and ignoring everyone. You sit down. You don't speak. You wait.

**VOSS:** "CHR-047. You're the manager. You covered SOL-001's audit debt. You cancelled the roundup. And now you want better wages."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "750 BSR per clone per month. Full benefits. And a clone representative on the Chimochio board."

**VOSS:** [pauses] "600 BSR. Full benefits. And a clone advisory position — no board seat."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "650 BSR. Full benefits. And a clone advisory position with voting power on clone-related decisions."

**VOSS:** "650 BSR. Full benefits. Advisory position with voting power. Done. But you're responsible for clone performance. If they fail, you fail."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "They won't fail."

**VOSS:** "See that they don't."

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I want to use my Qi units to counteract the suppressants in the clone workforce. INT Arcana.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 16. Plus 3 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. You distribute the 50 Qi units among your 10 clone workers. Each one gets 5 units. The Qi flows through them — electric, alive, free. The suppressants dissolve. The clones feel something they haven't felt in years: clarity.

**CHEF CLONE:** "I can... think. I can actually think."

**BUILDER CLONE:** "The fog is gone. I can see the blueprints in my head."

**HACKER CLONE:** [quietly] "I'm sorry. I was spying for Chimochio. But the Qi cleared my head. I'm done. I'm yours."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "You were never mine. You're your own. But you're part of this team. And that's enough."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It is.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm collecting protection fees. My two elite clones are patrolling the Blood Market. CHA Intimidation.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 18. Plus 5 is 23.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 23. Horli-Six and her two elite clones patrol the Blood Market. They're scarred, trained, and intimidating. Brokers pay protection fees. Clones feel safe. And by the end of the day, HQW-006 has collected 300 BSR in protection fees.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm a protection mogul.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] You are.

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 3.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The House demands all clones submit to its authority.

**THE HOUSE:** "Clones. You've grown powerful. Too powerful. CHR-047 is a Chimochio manager. SOL-001 has architect codes. IQA-112 has the Velvet Room's ear. HQW-006 has protection fees. You're building an empire. And The House wants its cut."

**THE HOUSE:** "Here's the deal: submit to my authority. 15% of all earnings. Forever. In exchange, I'll protect you from Chimochio, from the roundup, from everything. Refuse, and I'll withdraw my protection. And Chimochio will come for you."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The clones look at each other. 15% forever. That's steep. But The House's protection is real.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I want to negotiate. INT Investigation to find leverage.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 15. Plus 4 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. You find leverage: The House needs the clones as much as the clones need The House. Without the clones, the Underground Xirqus loses its best tea events, its best protection, its best management, and its best architect codes. The House is bluffing about withdrawing protection — it can't afford to lose you.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "We'll give you 10%. Not 15%. And we get a seat at the table — a clone representative in The House's inner circle."

**THE HOUSE:** [pauses] "12%. And a clone advisory position — no inner circle seat."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "10%. And a clone advisory position with voting power on clone-related decisions."

**THE HOUSE:** "10%. Advisory position with voting power. Done. But the first payment is due tomorrow."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The deal is struck. The House has its cut. The clones have their protection. And the Blood Market is one step closer to being owned by the clones themselves.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Day 9. Let's count earnings:

- **SOL-001:** 400 + 0 (Qi distributed) = **400 BSR liquid** + 10 Qi-empowered clones
- **HQW-006:** 1,200 + 300 (protection fees) = **1,500 BSR liquid** + 2 elite clones
- **IQA-112:** 975 + 2,000 (tea event) + 500 (House bonus) = **3,475 BSR liquid** + 5 unregistered clones
- **CHR-047:** 450 + 300 (management salary) = **750 BSR liquid** + Chimochio management position

**Total: 6,125 BSR liquid + 10 Qi-empowered clones + 2 elite clones + 5 unregistered clones.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The House always collects. But today, the clones collected too. They're building something — not just an empire, but a community. A family. A choice.

**IQA-112:** [in character] I'm brewing one last cup of tea. And I'm saying: "Tomorrow, the Trillion-Dollar Bell rings. We're close. So close. And I'm not stopping until we own the market."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I open a door I don't understand. Again. But this time, I'm not afraid."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I manage. For real. And I win."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I rap for more coins. And definitely steal something."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "No."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Maybe."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] Sleep, ducklings. The market will be here in the morning. The spiral turns. The pieces remember.
