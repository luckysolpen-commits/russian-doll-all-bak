# CLONE CHRONICLES — SESSION 7: "THE CLONE EXCHANGE"
## Part 1: The Blood Market — Session 7 of 10
### Players: CHR-047 (Chara), SOL-001 (Sol-Supreme), IQA-112 (IQabella), HQW-006 (Horli Qwin) | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Day 7. The market crashed yesterday. You lost half your money. But you're still standing. And today, you're buying clones.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm buying a clone army.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans warmly] Of course you are.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The morning bell rings. The market wakes up. The BSR is still down 50%, but the Clone Exchange is open — and clone prices are at an all-time low. Because the market crashed, clones are desperate. They're selling their labor for half price. And the clones with removed serial numbers? They're selling for even less — unregistered, untracked, desperate.

**Mother Tmojizu:** You walk into the Clone Exchange. It's massive — part warehouse, part auction house, part prison. Clones line the walls, waiting to be bought. Broker Vex stands at the center, ledger in hand.

**BROKER VEX:** "You're back. And you're buying. Good. Prices are low. Market's crashed. Clones are desperate. Here's the menu:"

| Clone Type | Normal Price | Crash Price |
|------------|-------------|-------------|
| **Labor Clone** | 100 BSR | 50 BSR |
| **Skilled Clone** | 200 BSR | 100 BSR |
| **Unregistered Clone** | 150 BSR | 75 BSR |
| **Elite Clone** | 500 BSR | 250 BSR |

**BROKER VEX:** "Remember — you owe me 5% of your earnings for the next 15 days. Day 1 of 15 starts today."

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm buying skilled clones. I need workers for my crash assets. INT Investigation to find the best deals.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 17. Plus 3 is 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 20. You find three skilled clones — a chef, a builder, and a hacker. Each one costs 100 BSR (crash price). You buy all three for 300 BSR.

**SOL-001:** [in character] "You're hired. You work for me. You get paid. You're free."

**CHEF CLONE:** "Free? We're bought."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "You're employed. There's a difference."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The chef clone looks at you. The builder clone looks at you. The hacker clone looks at you. And they nod. They're yours.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm buying unregistered clones. Clones with removed serial numbers. I want to give them jobs, not ownership. CHA Persuasion to recruit them.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**IQA-112:** [rolls] 18. Plus 4 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 22. IQA-112 walks through the unregistered clone section. She finds five clones — bare wrists, scarred wrists, desperate eyes. She offers them jobs: tea stand assistants, healers, community builders. 75 BSR each (crash price). She buys all five for 375 BSR.

**IQA-112:** [in character] "You're not property. You're employees. You get paid. You get tea. And you get to say no."

**UNREGISTERED CLONE:** "Say no?"

**IQA-112:** [in character] "If you don't want the job, say no. I'll still give you tea."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] They all say yes.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm buying elite clones. I need muscle. CHA Intimidation to negotiate the price.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 16. Plus 5 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. You find two elite clones — warriors, trained, scarred, expensive. Normal price: 500 BSR each. Crash price: 250 BSR each. You negotiate: "I'll take both for 400 BSR total."

**BROKER VEX:** "400 BSR for two elite clones? That's a 200 BSR discount."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "That's a 200 BSR investment in your future security. I'm protecting this market. You want me to protect it, don't you?"

**BROKER VEX:** [pauses] "Fine. 400 BSR. But you owe me an extra 1% for the discount. 6% for the next 15 days."

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, do I accept?

**Mother Tmojizu:** You can. Or you can negotiate. Or you can walk.

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Fine. 6%. But if I protect this market, I get a cut of the protection fees."

**BROKER VEX:** "10% of protection fees. Done."

**Mother Tmojizu:** HQW-006 buys two elite clones for 400 BSR. They're hers. They're warriors. They're scarred. They're loyal — for now.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm not buying clones. I'm buying information. I'm investigating the clone roundup. INT Investigation.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 20. Plus 4 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. You dig deep. You talk to clones. You talk to brokers. You talk to Officer Krell (who's still honoring his 30-day protection deal). And you learn:

The clone roundup is scheduled for Day 12 — five days from now. Chimochio will send enforcement teams to collect all clones with removed serial numbers. They'll be re-registered, re-numbered, and re-debted. The roundup is led by Executive Director Voss — the same person who crashed the market.

But there's a weakness: the enforcement teams will come through the main gate. If the gate is blocked, the roundup is delayed. And the gate can be blocked by... a clone workforce.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "We can stop the roundup. We block the main gate with our clone workforce. Chimochio can't get through. The roundup is delayed. And we buy time."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "How much time?"

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Enough."

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 2.

**Mother Tmojizu:** A clone's loyalty drops.

One of SOL-001's skilled clones — the hacker — is approached by a Chimochio recruiter. The recruiter offers: "We'll pay you 500 BSR to spy on your employer. Report their plans. We'll give you your serial number back."

The hacker clone looks at the offer. Looks at SOL-001. Looks at the other clones. And makes a choice.

**MOTHER TOMOKAZU:** Roll WIS Insight to detect the betrayal.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 10. Plus 0 is 10.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 10. You don't notice. The hacker clone accepts the deal. They're now a Chimochio spy.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm watching the hacker clone. WIS Insight.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 16. Plus 3 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. You notice the hacker clone acting suspicious — looking over their shoulder, avoiding eye contact, checking their wrist (where a serial number used to be). You pull them aside.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "You're working for Chimochio. I can see it. Tell me the truth, and I'll protect you. Lie, and I'll expose you."

**HACKER CLONE:** [pauses] "They offered me my serial number back. 500 BSR. I took it. I'm sorry."

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm not exposing them. I'm using them. I'm feeding them false information. CHA Deception.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 18. Plus 2 is 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 20. You feed the hacker clone false information: "The clones are planning to flee the market on Day 12. They're not blocking the gate. They're running."

The hacker clone reports this to Chimochio. Chimochio believes it. They redirect their enforcement teams to the market exits — not the main gate.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "They'll be at the exits. The main gate is open. We block it on Day 12. The roundup fails."

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Day 7. Let's count earnings:

- **SOL-001:** 200 - 300 (3 skilled clones) = **-100 BSR liquid** + 500 BSR in crash assets + 3 skilled clones
- **HQW-006:** 1,500 - 400 (2 elite clones) = **1,100 BSR liquid** + 2 elite clones
- **IQA-112:** 1,300 - 375 (5 unregistered clones) = **925 BSR liquid** + 5 unregistered clones
- **CHR-047:** 1,150 - 0 (bought nothing, gathered intel) = **1,150 BSR liquid**

**Total: 3,075 BSR liquid + 500 BSR in crash assets + 10 clone workers.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The clones are building. A workforce. A defense. A plan. The roundup is on Day 12 — five days away. The main gate is the key. And CHR-047 just fed Chimochio false information.

**IQA-112:** [in character] I'm brewing one last cup of tea. And I'm saying: "Tomorrow, the Broker's Game begins. We negotiate with power players. We leverage our workforce. And we prepare for the roundup."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I open a door I don't understand. Again."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I feed Chimochio more lies."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I rap for more coins. And definitely steal something."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "No."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Maybe."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] Sleep, ducklings. The market will be here in the morning. The spiral turns. The pieces remember.
