# CLONE CHRONICLES — SESSION 8: "THE BROKER'S GAME"
## Part 1: The Blood Market — Session 8 of 10
### Players: CHR-047 (Chara), SOL-001 (Sol-Supreme), IQA-112 (IQabella), HQW-006 (Horli Qwin) | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Day 8. You have a workforce. You have a plan. The roundup is on Day 12 — four days away. And today, the power players come to negotiate.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm ready to negotiate. And by negotiate, I mean hustle.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] I know.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The morning bell rings. The market wakes up. And three power players arrive at the Clone Exchange:

1. **Executive Director Voss** — the human who crashed the market and ordered the roundup. She's here to negotiate. She doesn't know CHR-047 fed her false intel.
2. **Master Feng** — the monk who controls Qi. He's here to offer a deal.
3. **The House** — the anonymous operator of the Underground Xirqus. They're here to collect.

**BROKER VEX:** "You're the center of attention today. Voss wants your workforce. Feng wants your Qi. The House wants its cut. Choose carefully."

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I want to negotiate with Voss. I want to delay the roundup. CHA Persuasion.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 16. Plus 2 is 18.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 18. You approach Executive Director Voss. She's tall, cold, wearing a Chimochio Corporation suit that costs more than your entire workforce. She looks at you. She doesn't see a clone. She sees a problem.

**VOSS:** "CHR-047. You're the one who discovered the debt inflation. You're the one who fed me false intel about the clone exodus. And you're the one planning to block the main gate on Day 12."

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, she knows?

**Mother Tmojizu:** She knows. She's not stupid. She's just playing a longer game.

**VOSS:** "Here's my deal: I'll cancel the roundup. In exchange, you and your workforce work for Chimochio. 500 BSR per clone per month. Full benefits. Serial numbers restored for the unregistered. And you — CHR-047 — you get a management position."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "And if I refuse?"

**VOSS:** "Then the roundup happens. And you're the first one re-registered."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll WIS Insight to detect if she's bluffing.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 19. Plus 3 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 22. She's not bluffing. But she's also not telling the whole truth. The roundup is already failing — because of your false intel, Chimochio's enforcement teams are at the exits, not the main gate. She needs you to call off the gate blockade because she can't afford a public failure.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "I'll accept. But with conditions: 750 BSR per clone per month. Full benefits. Serial numbers restored for the unregistered — by choice, not force. And I get a management position with veto power over clone assignments."

**VOSS:** [pauses] "600 BSR. Full benefits. Serial numbers restored by choice. And you get a management position with advisory power — no veto."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Deal."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The deal is struck. The roundup is cancelled. The clones are safe. And CHR-047 just negotiated a Chimochio management position.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I want to negotiate with Master Feng. I want to understand the Qi market's role in the roundup. INT Arcana.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 15. Plus 3 is 18.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 18. Master Feng sits cross-legged beside the Clone Exchange pool. He looks at you. He smiles.

**MASTER FENG:** "SOL-001. Encrypted architect codes. You opened doors you shouldn't have. You traded Qi at the right time. You predicted the market. And now you're here — negotiating with a monk."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "I want to understand the Qi market's role in the roundup. Was Qi used to control the clones?"

**MASTER FENG:** "Qi flows where the heart leads. Chimochio tried to use Qi to control clones — they injected Qi suppressants into the clone workforce, dampening their ability to resist. But Qi cannot be suppressed. It flows. It always flows."

**MASTER FENG:** "Here's my deal: I'll give you 50 Qi units — enough to counteract the suppressants in your entire workforce. In exchange, you give me 5% of your Qi earnings for the next 30 days."

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I accept. 50 Qi units for 5% of Qi earnings for 30 days.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Master Feng ladles 50 Qi units into a vial. The water glows blue — electric, alive, free. He hands it to SOL-001.

**MASTER FENG:** "Qi flows where the heart leads. Your heart leads to freedom. Don't let the suppressants stop it."

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I want to negotiate with The House. They want their cut. I want to negotiate a better deal. CHA Persuasion.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**IQA-112:** [rolls] 17. Plus 4 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. The House speaks from the walls.

**THE HOUSE:** "IQA-112. The 112th. Warm. Assertive. You bought five unregistered clones and gave them tea. I noticed. I always notice."

**IQA-112:** [in character] "I want to negotiate your cut. You've been taking 10% of my earnings. I want to reduce it to 5%."

**THE HOUSE:** "In exchange?"

**IQA-112:** [in character] "In exchange, I'll host a weekly tea event at the Underground Xirqus. Free tea for all members. Reputation boost. Morale boost. And I'll bring my clone workforce — they'll perform, serve, and entertain. Your members will love it."

**THE HOUSE:** [pauses] "5% for weekly tea events. Done. But the first event is tomorrow. Don't be late."

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I just negotiated The House down to 5%.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] You did. With tea.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I want to negotiate with Broker Vex. I want to reduce my 6% rate back to 5%. CHA Intimidation.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 14. Plus 5 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. You approach Broker Vex. He's reading his ledger, drinking something dark, and ignoring everyone. You sit down. You don't speak. You wait.

**BROKER VEX:** "HQW-006. You want your rate reduced. You have two elite clones. You're collecting 10% of protection fees. And you're still broke."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "I'm not broke. I'm investing. And I want my rate reduced to 5%. Or I'll take my protection business elsewhere."

**BROKER VEX:** [pauses] "Fine. 5%. But you owe me one more favor. Two favors total. I'll call them when I need them."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "One favor. Final offer."

**BROKER VEX:** "One favor. Done."

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 6.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Chimochio Audit.

Executive Director Voss returns. She has a ledger. She's auditing all clone debts. And she's found something: SOL-001's architect codes were used to access restricted areas. That's a violation of clone terms.

**VOSS:** "SOL-001. You used architect codes to access restricted areas. That's a violation. Your debt is doubled. 1,000 BSR."

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, can I argue that the codes are part of my DNA — not a violation?

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Persuasion.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 13. Plus 1 is 14.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 14. It's not enough. Voss doesn't budge. But CHR-047 steps in.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Executive Director, I'm his manager now. His debt is my responsibility. I'll cover the 1,000 BSR. From my management salary."

**VOSS:** [pauses] "Accepted. But SOL-001's architect codes are now monitored. Every use is logged."

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm okay with that. I don't want to lose the codes.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Day 8. Let's count earnings:

- **SOL-001:** -100 + 500 (crash assets) - 0 (CHR-047 covered the audit) = **400 BSR liquid** + 50 Qi units
- **HQW-006:** 1,100 + 100 (protection fees) = **1,200 BSR liquid** + 2 elite clones
- **IQA-112:** 925 + 50 (tea tips) = **975 BSR liquid** + 5 unregistered clones
- **CHR-047:** 1,150 - 1,000 (covered SOL-001's audit) + 300 (management advance) = **450 BSR liquid** + Chimochio management position

**Total: 3,025 BSR liquid + 50 Qi units + 500 BSR crash assets + 10 clone workers.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The Broker's Game is over. The deals are struck. The roundup is cancelled. The clones are safe. But SOL-001's architect codes are now monitored. And Chimochio is watching.

**IQA-112:** [in character] I'm brewing one last cup of tea. And I'm saying: "Tomorrow, the House always collects. My first tea event is at the Underground Xirqus. And I need to make it perfect."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I open a door I don't understand. Again. But this time, they're watching."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I manage. For real."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I rap for more coins. And definitely steal something."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "No."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Maybe."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] Sleep, ducklings. The market will be here in the morning. The spiral turns. The pieces remember.
