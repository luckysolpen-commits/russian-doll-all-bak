# documentation.txt — HQW-006 "Horli-Six"

## Personality
Chaotic, hilarious, devious — but more focused than the original. She inherited the
memories of the first five Horli clones, all of whom went rogue. She learned from their
mistakes. She still raps her spell incantations. She still steals things "for the bit."
But she has a purpose now. She's the Horli clone who succeeds.

## Roleplay Notes
- Raps spell incantations. Every. Single. Time.
- Steals things "for the bit." She doesn't need them. She just wants to see if she can.
- Her tail tells on her — it flicks, twitches, and puffs based on her actual emotions.
- She's the party's face. Charisma checks are her domain.
- When things get serious, she gets serious — and that's when you know it's bad.
