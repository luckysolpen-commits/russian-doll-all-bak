# PART 2 — SESSION 2: "THE FIRST CLAIM"
## Clone Chronicles — Part 2, Session 2 of 10
### Power: Regional | Status: Building infrastructure, first trade deals

---

## SESSION OVERVIEW

The clones expand their territories, build infrastructure, and negotiate their first trade deals. This session is about growth: HR, R&D, Manufacturing, Marketing, Finance, and Diplomacy.

## KEY BEATS

1. **Turn 2 Begins** — Each clone makes their business decisions.
2. **Infrastructure Building** — Factories, Foundries, Markets, Defenses.
3. **First Trade Deals** — Clones negotiate with each other and NPCs.
4. **Expansion** — Claim additional territory or reinforce existing claims.
5. **End of Turn 2** — Hook for Session 3.

## BUSINESS DECISIONS (Per Clone)

| Decision | Effect | Cost |
|----------|--------|------|
| **HR** | Hire units, set salaries | BSR (salary × hires) |
| **R&D** | Research tech, unlock abilities | BSR |
| **Marketing** | Increase demand for goods | BSR |
| **Manufacturing** | Build units, structures | BSR |
| **Finance** | Borrow money, sell shares | Interest or equity |
| **Diplomacy** | Trade, ally, or declare war | CHA checks |

## TRADE DEAL MECHANICS

- CHA (Persuasion) for negotiations
- WIS (Insight) to detect bad deals
- INT (Investigation) to assess value
- Natural 20 = perfect deal. Natural 1 = catastrophic betrayal.

## COMPLICATION (d6)
| Roll | Complication |
|------|-------------|
| 1 | A clone's factory malfunctions — production halts |
| 2 | Trade deal betrayal — one clone breaks an agreement |
| 3 | Resource shortage — manufacturing costs double |
| 4 | A barbarian faction raids a clone's territory |
| 5 | The House demands a favor |
| 6 | The Original's forces appear on the border |

## END STATE
- Each clone has expanded territory or infrastructure
- First trade deals completed (or broken)
- Hook: "Faction warfare begins. The board is set. Choose your allies."
