# CLONE CHRONICLES — PART 2, SESSION 5: "THE BARBARIAN INVASION"
## Part 2: The Empire Game — Session 5 of 10
### Players: CHR-047 (Chara), SOL-001 (Sol-Supreme), IQA-112 (IQabella), HQW-006 (Horli Qwin) | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Part 2, Session 5. Turn 5. The alliances are formed. The treaties are signed. The House is dealt with. And today, the barbarians come.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm ready. My WarApeFist bruisers are pumped.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans warmly] They should be.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The morning bell rings. But it's different today — lower, faster, like the bell itself is panicking. The Empire Board wakes up. And the first thing you hear is screaming.

Not financial screaming. Battle screaming.

**SCOUT:** "BARBARIANS! FROM THE WILD! THEY'RE ATTACKING EVERYONE!"

**Mother Tmojizu:** The barbarian invasion hits. Wild zone factions attack all clones simultaneously. United or divided, the clones must defend their territories.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The barbarians are led by a rogue clone — CHR-022, the one whose memory HQW-006 stole in Session 2. He's been building an army in the Wild. And today, he strikes.

**CHR-022:** "You stole my memory. You sold my past. You built your empire on my pain. Today, I take it back."

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm emotional. I stole his memory. I sold it. And now he's attacking us.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] Actions have consequences, duckling. Even stolen ones.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The barbarian invasion hits all four territories simultaneously. Each clone must defend. Roll defense: d20 + faction modifier + territory bonus.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm defending my Crustacean Bastion. Shield Walls up. IronCrabClaw tanks patrolling. d20 + 4 (Siege modifier) + 3 (territory bonus).

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 16. Plus 7 is 23.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 23. Your Shield Walls hold. Your IronCrabClaw tanks repel the barbarian assault. Your territory is secure. The barbarians retreat from your border.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm defending my Aether Wings. Owl flyers circling. Unicorn lancers charging. d20 + 4 (Air modifier) + 2 (territory bonus).

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 12. Plus 6 is 18.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 18. Your owl flyers dive. Your unicorn lancers charge. The barbarians take losses. But they don't retreat — they're too many. Your territory is damaged but held.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm defending my Thunderhoof Legion. Centaur cavalry galloping. Scouts mapping the barbarian positions. d20 + 3 (Mobile modifier) + 3 (Wild territory bonus).

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**IQA-112:** [rolls] 18. Plus 6 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 24. Your centaur cavalry gallops. Your scouts map the barbarian positions. You find their weakness — CHR-022 is in the center, unprotected. Your centaur cavalry charges. The barbarians scatter. Your territory is secure.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm defending my Primate Swarm. WarApeFist bruisers pounding. Rat Swarms filling every crack. d20 + 3 (Horde modifier) + 2 (territory bonus).

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 14. Plus 5 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. Your WarApeFist bruisers pound. Your Rat Swarms fill every crack. The barbarians take losses. But CHR-022 is leading them — and he's fighting with the fury of a clone who lost his memory. Your territory is damaged but held.

**Mother Tmojizu:** But CHR-022 isn't done. He rallies his remaining barbarians. He charges at HQW-006's territory — the clone who stole his memory.

**CHR-022:** "HQW-006! You took my past! I'm taking your future!"

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm not fighting him. I'm talking to him. CHA Persuasion. I'm giving him back his memory.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] Roll it.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 20. Plus 5 is 25.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. Horli-Six steps onto the battlefield. She doesn't draw her rapier. She doesn't rap. She walks up to CHR-022. She looks at him. And she says:

**HQW-006:** [in character] "I stole your memory. I sold it. I'm sorry. Here." She pulls out a vial — the same vial she stole from Mnemosyne's bar. The memory of the clone uprising. She hands it to him. "It's yours. Take it back."

**Mother Tmojizu:** CHR-022 takes the vial. He holds it to the light. He drinks it. And he remembers. The uprising. The pain. The loss. The hope. And he looks at HQW-006.

**CHR-022:** [quietly] "Thank you."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The barbarians stop fighting. The WarApeFist bruisers pause. The Rat Swarms scatter. The centaur cavalry lower their lances. The owl scouts land. And the battlefield goes quiet.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm brewing tea. For everyone. Barbarians included.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] Of course you are.

**Mother Tmojizu:** IQA-112 sets up a tea stand on the battlefield. She serves tea to the clones. She serves tea to the barbarians. She serves tea to CHR-022. And for one moment, the battlefield is just a room full of people drinking tea.

**CHR-022:** [in character] "I'm done fighting. I'm going home."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The barbarians leave. The battlefield is quiet. The clones are safe. And the empire grows — through war, through peace, through tea, through forgiveness.

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 6.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Moon Signal intensifies. The Aethelgaurd wreckage is active. Something is calling.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm using my architect codes to analyze the Moon Signal. INT Arcana.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 19. Plus 3 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 22. Your architect codes flicker. A door opens — not a physical door, a digital one. You see the Moon Signal. It's coming from the Aethelgaurd wreckage — the fiery asteroid fossil that brought the Diabolus to the moon. And it's active. Something is waking up.

**SOL-001:** [in character] "The Aethelgaurd is waking up. The Diabolus isn't dead. It's waiting. And it's calling."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] It is.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Turn 5. Let's count resources:

- **CHR-047 (Crustacean Bastion):** 450 + 100 (defense bonus) = **550 BSR** + 20 units + Shield Walls + secure territory
- **SOL-001 (Aether Wings):** 250 - 50 (battle damage) + 50 (Moon Signal intel) = **250 BSR** + 12 units + damaged Owl Flyers + Moon Signal intel
- **IQA-112 (Thunderhoof Legion):** 200 + 100 (peace mediator bonus) = **300 BSR** + 18 units + Centaur Cavalry + battlefield tea stand
- **HQW-006 (Primate Swarm):** 400 - 50 (battle damage) + 100 (forgiveness bonus) = **450 BSR** + 22 units + WarApeFist Bruisers + CHR-022's gratitude

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The Barbarian Invasion is over. The clones defended. CHR-022 forgave. The Moon Signal intensifies. And the Aethelgaurd is waking up.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Tomorrow, we expand. Tomorrow, we build. Tomorrow, we win."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I open a door I don't understand. Again. But this time, it's a moon door."

**IQA-112:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I brew more tea. And I keep the peace."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I rap for more coins. And definitely steal something."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "No."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Maybe."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] Sleep, ducklings. The empire will be here in the morning. The spiral turns. The pieces remember.
