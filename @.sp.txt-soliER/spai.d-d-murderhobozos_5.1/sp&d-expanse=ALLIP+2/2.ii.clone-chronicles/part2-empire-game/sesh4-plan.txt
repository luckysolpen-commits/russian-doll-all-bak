# PART 2 — SESSION 4: "THE DIPLOMACY"
## Clone Chronicles — Part 2, Session 4 of 10
### Power: Global | Status: Alliances, treaties, betrayals

---

## SESSION OVERVIEW

The clones negotiate. Alliances form. Treaties are signed. Betrayals lurk. This session is about diplomacy — the art of getting what you want without fighting (or setting up the fight you want).

## KEY BEATS

1. **Diplomatic Summit** — All clones gather. Negotiations begin.
2. **Alliance Offers** — Clones propose alliances, trade deals, vassalage.
3. **Treaty Signing** — Agreements are formalized. Trust is tested.
4. **Betrayal** — Someone breaks an agreement. Or doesn't.
5. **End of Turn 4** — Hook for Session 5.

## DIPLOMACY MECHANICS

- **Alliance:** Two clones combine forces. Shared resources, shared victory.
- **Trade Deal:** Exchange resources, Qi Energy, or territory.
- **Betrayal:** Break an alliance. Gain short-term advantage, lose long-term trust.
- **Submission:** One clone submits to another. Becomes a vassal. Gains protection, loses autonomy.

**Checks:**
- CHA (Persuasion) for negotiations
- WIS (Insight) to detect betrayal
- INT (Investigation) to uncover hidden agendas
- Natural 20 = perfect deal. Natural 1 = catastrophic betrayal.

| Roll | Complication |

## END STATE
- Alliances formed (or broken)
- Trust levels established between clones
- Hook: "The barbarians are coming. United or divided, you'll face them."
