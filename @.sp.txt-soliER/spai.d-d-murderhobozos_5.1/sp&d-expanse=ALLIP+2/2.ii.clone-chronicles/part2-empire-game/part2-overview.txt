# PART 2: "THE EMPIRE GAME" — Overview
## Clone Chronicles Arc 2 — From Trillions to Empires
### 10 Sessions | Power: Local → Eternal

---

## THE ARC

The clones own the Blood Market. They're trillionaires. They're free. But freedom is expensive — and empires don't build themselves.

Over 10 sessions, the clones transition from market owners to empire builders. They choose factions, claim territories, wage wars, negotiate treaties, and build civilizations. By the end, they're not just rich — they're sovereign. The board doesn't measure them anymore. They measure the board.

## KEY SYSTEMS

### Faction System (TEAR-IT Inspired)
Rock-Paper-Scissors warfare:

| Faction | Type | Strength | Weakness |
|---------|------|----------|----------|
| **Crustacean Bastion** | Siege | Defensive, armored | Slow, expensive |
| **Primate Swarm** | Horde | Numbers, aggression | Fragile, undisciplined |
| **Thunderhoof Legion** | Mobile | Speed, hit-and-run | Light armor |
| **Aether Wings** | Air | Range, recon | Fragile, weather-dependent |

### Territory System (LSR Inspired)
| Zone | Type | Features |
|------|------|----------|
| **Government Land** | Safe, taxed | Clone Exchange, Memory Bar, Qi Exchange |
| **Company Claims** | Competitive | Factories, Foundries, Markets |
| **Unclaimed/Wild** | Dangerous, free | Barbarian zones, resource nodes |
| **The Moon** | Endgame | Aethelgaurd wreckage, Diabolus remnants |

### Business Simulation Mechanics
Each turn, clones make decisions:
1. **HR** — Hire units, set salaries
2. **R&D** — Research tech, unlock abilities
3. **Marketing** — Increase demand
4. **Manufacturing** — Build units, structures
5. **Finance** — Borrow, sell shares, manage debt
6. **Diplomacy** — Trade, ally, or declare war

## CORE MECHANICS

### Combat Resolution
- Faction warfare uses simplified TEAR-IT skirmish rules
- Rock-Paper-Scissors determines advantage
- 2-3 rounds with dice rolls and tactical decisions
- Losers lose units and territory. Winners gain resources.

### Diplomacy Mechanics
- CHA (Persuasion) for negotiations
- WIS (Insight) to detect betrayal
- INT (Investigation) to uncover hidden agendas
- Natural 20 = perfect deal. Natural 1 = catastrophic betrayal.
