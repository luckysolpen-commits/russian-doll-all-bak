# PART 2 — SESSION 5: "THE BARBARIAN INVASION"
## Clone Chronicles — Part 2, Session 5 of 10
### Power: Planetary | Status: United defense, survival

---

## SESSION OVERVIEW

The barbarian invasion hits. Wild zone factions attack all clones simultaneously. United or divided, the clones must defend their territories. This session is about survival — and whether the clones can work together when it matters most.

## KEY BEATS

1. **The Invasion Begins** — Barbarian forces attack from multiple directions.
2. **Defense Planning** — Clones coordinate (or don't) their defenses.
3. **Battle Rounds** — 2-3 rounds of defensive warfare.
4. **Aftermath** — Territories damaged but held (or lost).
5. **End of Turn 5** — Hook for Session 6.

## INVASION MECHANICS

- **Barbarian Strength:** Scales with clone power level
- **Defense Roll:** d20 + faction modifier + territory bonus
- **Alliance Bonus:** Allied clones get +2 to defense
- **Victory:** Hold all territories. **Defeat:** Lose territory, pay reparations.

## END STATE
- Barbarian invasion repelled (or partially successful)
- Clone relationships tested
