# CLONE CHRONICLES — PART 2, SESSION 8: "THE BETRAYAL"
## Part 2: The Empire Game — Session 8 of 10
### Players: CHR-047 (Chara), SOL-001 (Sol-Supreme), IQA-112 (IQabella), HQW-006 (Horli Qwin) | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Part 2, Session 8. Turn 8. The Originals have returned and left. The clones are more themselves than ever. The Moon Signal intensifies. The Aethelgaurd is rising. And today, trust breaks.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I can feel it. Something's about to go wrong.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans warmly] Yes. It is.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The morning bell rings. But it's different today — sharper, colder, like the bell itself is betraying you. The Empire Board wakes up. And the first thing you notice is that the peace treaty is broken.

Not by war. By betrayal.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 3.

**Mother Tmojizu:** A clone betrays the others during the invasion.

The hacker clone — the one SOL-001 hired in Session 7, the one CHR-047 turned into a double agent in Session 7 — has been playing both sides. And today, she plays her own.

**HACKER CLONE:** "I'm done working for Chimochio. I'm done working for you. I'm working for myself. And I'm taking the Blood Market's treasury with me."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Blood Market's treasury — 500,000 BSR — is gone. The hacker clone has stolen it. And she's running.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm tracking her. My architect codes can trace digital transactions. INT Arcana.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 17. Plus 3 is 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 20. Your architect codes flicker. A door opens — not a physical door, a digital one. You trace the hacker clone's transactions through the Blood Market's financial network, through the Chimochio Corporation's satellite array, through the lunar relay station, and into the Wild zone.

She's hiding in the Wild zone. In IQA-112's territory.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I didn't know she was there. I swear.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know you didn't. But the others don't know that.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm investigating IQA-112's territory. INT Investigation.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 14. Plus 4 is 18.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 18. You find evidence — not of IQA-112's complicity, but of the hacker clone's cleverness. She planted false evidence to make it look like IQA-112 was involved. But you see through it. The evidence is too perfect. Too clean. Too fabricated.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "IQA-112 didn't do this. The hacker clone planted evidence. She's trying to turn us against each other."

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm not convinced. I'm investigating IQA-112's territory myself. INT Investigation.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 8. Plus 0 is 8.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 8. You find the planted evidence. And you believe it.

**HQW-006:** [in character] "IQA-112, you're involved. I can see it. The evidence is right here."

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm not involved. I'm brewing tea. CHA Persuasion to prove my innocence.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**IQA-112:** [rolls] 19. Plus 4 is 23.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 23. IQA-112 brews tea. She serves it to HQW-006. She looks at her. And she says:

**IQA-112:** [in character] "I'm not involved. I would never betray you. I'm the one who kept the peace. I'm the one who mediated the border wars. I'm the one who served tea to the barbarians. And I'm the one who reminded you who you are when the Originals came back. I'm not your enemy. I'm your friend."

**Mother Tmojizu:** HQW-006 looks at the tea. She looks at IQA-112. She looks at the evidence. And she makes a choice.

**HQW-006:** [in character, quietly] "I believe you."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] She does.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm tracking the hacker clone to her hideout. I'm going after her. INT Arcana + DEX Stealth.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll Arcana first.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 16. Plus 3 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. You find her hideout — a cave in the Wild zone, filled with BSR, Qi units, and stolen memories. The hacker clone is there. She's counting her loot. And she doesn't see you coming.

**SOL-001:** [in character] "It's over. Give back the treasury."

**HACKER CLONE:** "Make me."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll attack.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 15. Plus 4 (Air modifier) is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. Your owl flyers dive. Your unicorn lancers charge. The hacker clone's defenses crumble. And the treasury is recovered.

**SOL-001:** [in character] "The treasury is back. The betrayal is over. But the trust is broken."

**Mother Tmojizu:** It is.

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 6.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Moon Signal intensifies — and the Aethelgaurd begins its descent.

**THE MOON SIGNAL:** "Clones. You've betrayed each other. You've broken trust. You've shaken empires. And now the Aethelgaurd lands. Not tomorrow. Not next week. Today."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The sky darkens. The Moon Signal becomes a roar. And the Aethelgaurd — the fiery asteroid fossil, the Diabolus's mothership — begins its descent from the moon.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Shield Walls up. IronCrabClaw tanks patrolling. Defensive perimeter activated. The Aethelgaurd hits my walls first."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Owl flyers circling. Unicorn lancers charging. Lunar relay station broadcasting. I'm trying to communicate with the Diabolus."

**IQA-112:** [in character] "Centaur cavalry galloping. Scouts mapping the impact zone. Tea stand ready. I'm preparing for the aftermath."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "WarApeFist bruisers pounding. Rat Swarms filling every crack. Rapier drawn. I'm ready to fight."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The betrayal is over. The trust is broken. The Aethelgaurd is landing. And the clones prepare — with shields, with tea, with words, and with Rat Swarms.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Turn 8. Let's count resources:

- **CHR-047 (Crustacean Bastion):** 450 - 100 (defensive activation) = **350 BSR** + 22 units + activated Shield Walls
- **SOL-001 (Aether Wings):** 250 - 50 (treasury recovery) = **200 BSR** + 12 units + treasury recovered
- **IQA-112 (Thunderhoof Legion):** 350 - 50 (tea preparation) = **300 BSR** + 18 units + aftermath preparation
- **HQW-006 (Primate Swarm):** 700 - 100 (battle readiness) = **600 BSR** + 30 units + ready to fight

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The Betrayal is over. The treasury is recovered. The trust is broken. The Aethelgaurd is landing. And the clones prepare for the endgame.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Tomorrow, the endgame begins. Tomorrow, we fight the Diabolus. Tomorrow, we win."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I open a door I don't understand. Again. But this time, it's the endgame door."

**IQA-112:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I brew more tea. And I keep the peace."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I rap for more coins. And definitely steal something."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "No."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Maybe."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] Sleep, ducklings. The empire will be here in the morning. The spiral turns. The pieces remember.
