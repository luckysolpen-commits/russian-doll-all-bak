## Clone Chronicles — Part 2, Session 3 of 10
### Power: Continental | Status: Warfare, conquest, defense

---

## SESSION OVERVIEW

Faction warfare erupts. The clones' armies clash. Rock-Paper-Scissors determines advantage. Combat is resolved in 2-3 rounds with dice rolls and tactical decisions. This session is about war.

## KEY BEATS

1. **War Declared** — Two or more clones are at war.
2. **Battle Planning** — Each clone positions units, chooses tactics.
3. **Combat Rounds** — 2-3 rounds of faction warfare.
4. **Aftermath** — Losers lose units and territory. Winners gain resources.
5. **End of Turn 3** — Hook for Session 4.

## COMBAT MECHANICS

- **Faction Advantage:** Siege > Horde > Mobile > Air > Siege
- **Attack Roll:** d20 + faction modifier vs. defender's AC
- **Damage:** Based on unit type and faction strength
- **Victory:** Eliminate enemy leader or control the territory
- **Defeat:** Lose units, lose territory, pay reparations

## END STATE

- Territory changes hands
