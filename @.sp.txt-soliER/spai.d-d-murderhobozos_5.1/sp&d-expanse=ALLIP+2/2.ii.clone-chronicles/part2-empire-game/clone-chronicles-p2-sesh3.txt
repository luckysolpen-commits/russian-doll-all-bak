# CLONE CHRONICLES — PART 2, SESSION 3: "THE FACTION WARS"
## Part 2: The Empire Game — Session 3 of 10
### Players: CHR-047 (Chara), SOL-001 (Sol-Supreme), IQA-112 (IQabella), HQW-006 (Horli Qwin) | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Part 2, Session 3. Turn 3. The trade deals are struck. The border tensions are mediated. The resource shortages are weathered. And today, warfare erupts.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I've been waiting for this since I chose the Primate Swarm.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans warmly] I know.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The morning bell rings. The Empire Board wakes up. Four factions. Four territories. And today, the first war begins.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen for the Conflict Table.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 1.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Border War. Two clones' territories share a border. Skirmish breaks out.

HQW-006's Primate Swarm (Horde) shares a border with SOL-001's Aether Wings (Air). The Rat Swarms have been creeping back into SOL-001's territory. The owl scouts have been watching too closely. And today, the tension snaps.

**MOTHER TOMOKAZU:** A Rat Swarm crosses the border. An owl scout attacks it. The Rat Swarm bites back. The owl scout calls for reinforcements. The Rat Swarm calls for more rats. And within minutes, the border is a war zone.

**SOL-001:** [in character] "HQW-006, your rats are attacking my scouts. This is war."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "My rats are exploring. Your scouts are aggressive. This is natural selection."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll CHA Persuasion to de-escalate.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 8. Plus 1 is 9.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 9. It's not enough. The war begins.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Faction warfare uses Rock-Paper-Scissors: **Siege > Horde > Mobile > Air > Siege.**

HQW-006's Primate Swarm (Horde) vs. SOL-001's Aether Wings (Air). **Air beats Horde.** SOL-001 has the advantage.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm attacking. Owl flyers first, then unicorn lancers.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll attack. d20 + faction modifier vs. defender's AC.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 16. Plus 4 (Air advantage) is 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 20. The owl flyers dive. The unicorn lancers charge. The Rat Swarms scatter. The WarApeFist bruisers swing wildly. And the Aether Wings strike.

**SOL-001:** Damage: 2d8 + 4 (Air modifier). [rolls] 6, 4 + 4. That's 14 damage.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 14 damage. The Rat Swarms take heavy losses. HQW-006's territory is breached.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm counter-attacking. WarApeFist bruisers, all of them. CHA Intimidation.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 19. Plus 5 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 24. The WarApeFist bruisers pound the ground. They charge the owl flyers. They swing their massive fists. And they connect.

**HQW-006:** Damage: 3d6 + 5 (Horde modifier). [rolls] 4, 6, 3 + 5. That's 18 damage.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 18 damage. The owl flyers take heavy losses. The unicorn lancers retreat. SOL-001's territory is breached.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm using my Qi units to boost my owl flyers' range. 5 Qi units.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Noted. Your owl flyers regain range. They circle higher. They strike from above.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 14. Plus 4 is 18. Damage: 2d8 + 4. [rolls] 7, 5 + 4. That's 16 damage.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 16 damage. The WarApeFist bruisers fall. The Rat Swarms scatter. HQW-006's territory is heavily damaged.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm rapping a battle rap. CHA Performance. If I win, I get a morale boost.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] Roll it.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 20. Plus 5 is 25.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. Horli-Six steps onto the battlefield. She draws her rapier — "The Mic" — and she raps.

**HQW-006:** [in character, rapping] "Yo, I'm HQW-006, red-headed and fierce / My Rat Swarms are many, your owl flyers are pierce-d / Wait, that doesn't rhyme / But my army's gonna win every time!"

**Mother Tmojizu:** The battlefield stops. The WarApeFist bruisers pause. The owl scouts pause. Even the unicorn lancers pause. And then — a cheer. The Rat Swarms cheer. The WarApeFist bruisers cheer. Even some owl scouts cheer. Morale boost: +2 to all Horde attacks for the rest of the battle.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 17. Plus 5 + 2 (morale) is 24. Damage: 3d6 + 5 + 2. [rolls] 5, 4, 6 + 7. That's 22 damage.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 22 damage. The owl flyers fall. The unicorn lancers retreat. SOL-001's territory is heavily damaged.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm mediating. I'm riding my centaur cavalry to the battlefield. I'm offering tea to both sides. CHA Persuasion.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**IQA-112:** [rolls] 16. Plus 4 is 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 20. IQA-112 rides her centaur cavalry onto the battlefield. She sets up a tea stand. She offers tea to the WarApeFist bruisers and the owl flyers. They drink. They pause. They look at each other. And for one moment, the battlefield is peaceful.

**IQA-112:** [in character] "There's enough land for everyone. Share it."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] There is. For now.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm using the distraction to expand my territory. I'm building Shield Walls along my border. INT Investigation to find the best defensive positions.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 18. Plus 4 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 22. Your Crustacean Bastion expands. Shield Walls rise. IronCrabClaw tanks patrol. Your territory is an impenetrable fortress. And you've gained territory while the others fought.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Turn 3. Let's count resources:

- **CHR-047 (Crustacean Bastion):** 250 + 100 (territory expansion) = **350 BSR** + 20 units + Shield Walls + expanded territory
- **SOL-001 (Aether Wings):** 200 - 50 (battle losses) = **150 BSR** + 10 units + damaged Owl Flyers
- **IQA-112 (Thunderhoof Legion):** 50 + 50 (trade deal revenue) = **100 BSR** + 18 units + Centaur Cavalry + peace mediator reputation
- **HQW-006 (Primate Swarm):** 400 - 100 (battle losses) = **300 BSR** + 20 units + damaged WarApeFist Bruisers + battle rap morale

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The Faction Wars have begun. SOL-001 and HQW-006 clashed. IQA-112 mediated. CHR-047 expanded. And the empire grows — through war, through peace, through tea.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Tomorrow, we expand. Tomorrow, we build. Tomorrow, we win."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I open a door I don't understand. Again. But this time, it's a war door."

**IQA-112:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I brew more tea. And I keep the peace."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I rap for more coins. And definitely steal territory."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "No."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Maybe."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] Sleep, ducklings. The empire will be here in the morning. The spiral turns. The pieces remember.
