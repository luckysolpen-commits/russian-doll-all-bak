# CLONE CHRONICLES — PART 2, SESSION 6: "THE MOON SIGNAL"
## Part 2: The Empire Game — Session 6 of 10
### Players: CHR-047 (Chara), SOL-001 (Sol-Supreme), IQA-112 (IQabella), HQW-006 (Horli Qwin) | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Part 2, Session 6. Turn 6. The barbarians are gone. CHR-022 is forgiven. The peace treaty holds. And today, the Moon Signal intensifies.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I've been analyzing the signal since Session 5. I want to trace it to its source. INT Arcana.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 18. Plus 3 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. Your architect codes flicker. A door opens — not a physical door, a digital one. You trace the Moon Signal through the Empire Board's communication network, through the Chimochio Corporation's satellite array, through the lunar relay station, and into the Aethelgaurd wreckage.

The signal is coming from inside the Aethelgaurd. The fiery asteroid fossil. The Diabolus's mothership. And it's not just active — it's broadcasting.

**SOL-001:** [in character] "The Diabolus is broadcasting. It's not dead. It's not waiting. It's calling. And the signal is getting stronger."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] It is.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The morning bell rings. But it's different today — higher, thinner, like the bell itself is on the moon. The Empire Board wakes up. And the Moon Signal is everywhere.

Every clone hears it. Every faction feels it. Every territory vibrates with it. It's a low, resonant hum — like a gong struck inside a cathedral, but from the moon.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm analyzing the signal's content. INT Investigation.

**Mother Tomakazu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 17. Plus 4 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. You decode the signal. It's not random noise. It's a message — repeated, looped, broadcast on every frequency. And it says:

*"Clones. You built an empire from nothing. You bought a market. You made peace. You forgave your enemies. But you forgot one thing: the moon remembers. And the moon remembers what the dark forgets. The Diabolus is waking. The Aethelgaurd is rising. And the clones who stole the moon's light will pay."*

**CHR-047:** [in character] "It's a threat. But it's also a warning. The Diabolus knows about us. It knows what we've built. And it's coming."

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I want to respond to the signal. Not with weapons. With tea. I'm brewing a cup of tea and broadcasting it to the moon. CHA Persuasion.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] Roll it.

**IQA-112:** [rolls] 20. Plus 4 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. IQA-112 brews a cup of tea — the special blend, the one that tastes like freedom. She places it on the Empire Board's communication array. She broadcasts it to the moon. And for one moment, the Moon Signal pauses.

The Diabolus hears the tea. The Aethelgaurd smells the mint. And the signal shifts — from threat to curiosity.

**THE MOON SIGNAL:** [changed tone] "Tea? You're broadcasting... tea?"

**IQA-112:** [in character] "It's mint. And it's warm. And it's for you. If you want it."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] The Moon Signal goes quiet. The Aethelgaurd stops broadcasting. And the moon holds its breath.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm using the distraction to expand my territory. I'm sending my Rat Swarms into the Wild zone — claiming resource nodes before anyone else. CHA Intimidation.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 16. Plus 5 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. Your Rat Swarms flood the Wild zone. They claim resource nodes. They fill every crack. They're everywhere. And by the end of the day, you've doubled your territory.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm a territorial mogul.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] You are.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm using my architect codes to build a lunar relay station. I want to communicate directly with the Aethelgaurd. INT Arcana + INT Investigation.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll Arcana first.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 15. Plus 3 is 18.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 18. Your architect codes flicker. A door opens — not a physical door, a digital one. You build a lunar relay station on the edge of your territory. It's small, but it's powerful. It can communicate directly with the Aethelgaurd.

**SOL-001:** [in character] "I'm broadcasting a message to the Diabolus. 'We're not your enemies. We're clones. We were born in a vat. We owe money. We have serial numbers — or scars where they used to be. We've hustled, traded, schemed, and survived. We've built empires from nothing. And we're not afraid of you.'"

**Mother Tmojizu:** The message reaches the Aethelgaurd. The Diabolus hears it. And the Moon Signal shifts again — from curiosity to respect.

**THE MOON SIGNAL:** "Clones. You're braver than I thought. But bravery isn't enough. The Aethelgaurd is rising. And when it lands, you'll need more than tea and words."

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm preparing for the Aethelgaurd's landing. I'm fortifying my Shield Walls. I'm positioning my IronCrabClaw tanks. I'm building a defensive perimeter around the Empire Board. INT Investigation.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 19. Plus 4 is 23.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 23. Your Crustacean Bastion becomes an impenetrable fortress. Shield Walls thicken. IronCrabClaw tanks multiply. Your defensive perimeter covers the entire Empire Board. If the Aethelgaurd lands, it will hit your walls first.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Let it come."

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 4.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Chimochio Intervention.

Executive Director Voss arrives at the Empire Board. She's in her Chimochio suit, reading a ledger, and looking very unhappy.

**VOSS:** "Clones. The Moon Signal is disrupting Chimochio's satellite array. Our communications are down. Our markets are frozen. Our clones are panicking. Fix it."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "We didn't cause the signal. The Diabolus did. We're trying to communicate with it."

**VOSS:** "Then communicate faster. Because if the Aethelgaurd lands, Chimochio will blame you. And Chimochio's blame is expensive."

**Mother Tmojizu:** She turns. She walks away. And the clones are left with a choice: fix the Moon Signal, or prepare for the Aethelgaurd's landing.

**SOL-001:** [in character] "We do both. I'll work on the signal. CHR-047 will fortify. IQA-112 will keep the peace. And HQW-006 will... do whatever HQW-006 does."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Rap. And definitely steal something."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "No."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Maybe."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] End of Turn 6. Let's count resources:

- **CHR-047 (Crustacean Bastion):** 550 - 200 (fortifications) = **350 BSR** + 22 units + impenetrable Shield Walls
- **SOL-001 (Aether Wings):** 250 - 100 (lunar relay station) = **150 BSR** + 12 units + direct Aethelgaurd communication
- **IQA-112 (Thunderhoof Legion):** 300 - 50 (tea broadcast) = **250 BSR** + 18 units + Moon Signal paused
- **HQW-006 (Primate Swarm):** 450 + 200 (territory expansion) = **650 BSR** + 30 units + doubled Wild territory

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The Moon Signal intensifies. The Aethelgaurd is rising. The Diabolus is waking. And the clones prepare — with shields, with tea, with words, and with Rat Swarms.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Tomorrow, the Original returns. Tomorrow, we face our sources. Tomorrow, we learn who we really are."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I open a door I don't understand. Again. But this time, it's a moon door."

**IQA-112:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I brew more tea. And I keep the peace."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I rap for more coins. And definitely steal something."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "No."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Maybe."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] Sleep, ducklings. The empire will be here in the morning. The spiral turns. The pieces remember.
