**Mother Tmojizu:** But the Board Remembers. And what it remembers isn't VP. It's stories.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Empire Board glows. The territories shimmer. And the stories unfold:

**CHR-047's Story:** "The clone who discovered the debt inflation. Who fed Chimochio false intel. Who negotiated a management position. Who built an impenetrable fortress. Who faced her Original and said: 'I'm not property. I'm a person.' Who stood against the Diabolus and didn't flinch."

**SOL-001's Story:** "The clone who opened doors he didn't understand. Who predicted the Qi market. Who built a lunar relay station. Who understood his architect codes. Who faced his Original and said: 'Thank you.' Who found the Diabolus's weakness and shared it with everyone."

**HQW-006's Story:** "The clone who rapped her way to freedom. Who stole a memory and gave it back. Who built an army of Rat Swarms and WarApeFist bruisers. Who faced her Original and out-rapped her. Who delivered the critical hit that defeated the Diabolus. Who won."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The Board Remembers. And what it remembers is this: four red-headed clones, born in a vat, owing 500 BSR each, who built an empire from nothing. Who bought a market. Who made peace. Who forgave their enemies. Who faced their Originals. Who survived betrayal. Who defeated the Diabolus. And who proved — to Chimochio, to the House, to the Originals, to the Diabolus, and to themselves — that clones are not property. They are people.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "We did it."

550. 400. 500. 800.

2,250 BSR total. Plus an empire. Plus a market. Plus a future.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] Clone Chronicles is over. But the clones aren't. They're building. They're growing. They're becoming. And the spiral turns.

And the Board Remembers.
