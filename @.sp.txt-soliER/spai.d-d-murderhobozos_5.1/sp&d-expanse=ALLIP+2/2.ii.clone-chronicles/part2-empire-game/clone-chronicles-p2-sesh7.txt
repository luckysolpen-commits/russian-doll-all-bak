# CLONE CHRONICLES — PART 2, SESSION 7: "THE ORIGINAL RETURNS"
## Part 2: The Empire Game — Session 7 of 10
### Players: CHR-047 (Chara), SOL-001 (Sol-Supreme), IQA-112 (IQabella), HQW-006 (Horli Qwin) | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Part 2, Session 7. Turn 7. The Moon Signal intensifies. The Aethelgaurd is rising. The Diabolus is waking. And today, the Originals return.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I've been dreading this since Session 6.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The morning bell rings. But it's different today — deeper, older, like the bell itself remembers something. The Empire Board wakes up. And four figures walk onto the board.

Not clones. Originals.

The real Chara. The real Sol-Supreme. The real IQabella. The real Horli Qwin.

They stand at the edge of the Empire Board. They look at their clones. And the clones look back.

**THE ORIGINAL CHARA:** "CHR-047. You're me. But you're not me. You're red-headed. I'm not. You're a clone. I'm not. But you have my memories. My instincts. My paranoia. And you've built an empire from nothing. I'm... impressed."

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm looking at myself. But not myself. She's taller. Older. Harder. But the eyes are the same.

**Mother Tmojizu:** They are.

**THE ORIGINAL SOL-SUPREME:** "SOL-001. You're me. But you're not me. You're red-headed. I'm not. You're a clone. I'm not. But you have my architect codes. My patterns. My certainty. And you've built an empire from nothing. I'm... concerned."

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm looking at myself. But not myself. He's wearing the helmet. The visor glows. The voice is distorted. But the uncertainty is the same.

**Mother Tmojizu:** It is.

**THE ORIGINAL IQABELLA:** "IQA-112. You're me. But you're not me. You're red-headed. I'm not. You're a clone. I'm not. But you have my warmth. My kindness. My tea. And you've built an empire from nothing. I'm... proud."

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm crying.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**THE ORIGINAL HORLI QWIN:** "HQW-006. You're me. But you're not me. You're red-headed. I'm not. You're a clone. I'm not. But you have my chaos. My rap. My theft. And you've built an empire from nothing. I'm... terrified."

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, the Original Horli Qwin is terrified of me?

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] She is.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Originals step onto the Empire Board. They look at the factions. They look at the territories. They look at the Moon Signal. And they make their demands.

**THE ORIGINAL CHARA:** "We want our empires back. You're clones. You're property. You built this with our DNA. We're taking it."

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm not giving up my empire. CHA Persuasion.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 18. Plus 2 is 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 20. You look at the Original Chara. You look at her harder eyes. Her older face. Her unscarred wrists. And you say:

**CHR-047:** [in character] "I'm not property. I'm a person. I built this empire from nothing. I hustled, traded, schemed, and survived. I bought a market. I made peace. I forgave my enemies. And I'm not giving it up. Not to you. Not to anyone."

**THE ORIGINAL CHARA:** [pauses] "You're right. You're not property. You're a person. And you've built something I couldn't. I'm... sorry."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] The Original Chara steps back. She doesn't take the empire. She acknowledges it.

**THE ORIGINAL SOL-SUPREME:** "SOL-001. Your architect codes — they're mine. But they're yours too. I encrypted them in your DNA because I knew you'd need them. Not because I wanted to control you. Because I wanted to free you."

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm... emotional. INT Arcana to understand the codes fully.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 20. Plus 3 is 23.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. Your architect codes flicker. A door opens — not a physical door, a digital one. And you see it: the Original Sol-Supreme didn't encrypt the codes to control you. He encrypted them to protect you. From Chimochio. From the roundup. From everything. The codes were a gift. Not a chain.

**SOL-001:** [in character, quietly] "Thank you."

**THE ORIGINAL SOL-SUPREME:** [quietly] "You're welcome."

**THE ORIGINAL IQABELLA:** "IQA-112. You're warmer than me. More assertive. More brave. You built an empire from nothing. And you did it with tea. I'm... inspired."

**IQA-112:** [in character] "You inspired me. Even if I'm not you. You inspired me."

**THE ORIGINAL IQABELLA:** [smiles] "I know."

**THE ORIGINAL HORLI QWIN:** "HQW-006. You're more focused than me. The first five went rogue. You succeeded. You built an empire from nothing. And you did it with rap. I'm... out-rapped."

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm rapping at the Original Horli Qwin. CHA Performance.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] Roll it.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 19. Plus 5 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 24. Horli-Six steps onto the Empire Board. She draws her rapier — "The Mic" — and she raps.

**HQW-006:** [in character, rapping] "Yo, I'm HQW-006, red-headed and free / Six clones came before me, five didn't survive / But I'm the one who succeeded, I'm the one who thrived / I built an empire, I bought the market / And if you're the Original, you're just a start-er!"

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Original Horli Qwin pauses. She looks at HQW-006. She looks at the rapier. She looks at the empire. And she smiles.

**THE ORIGINAL HORLI QWIN:** "Okay. You win. The empire is yours. But I get to rap at victory celebrations."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Fine. But you're opening for me."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] The Originals step back. They don't take the empires. They acknowledge them. And they leave.

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 5.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Originals' departure triggers a loyalty check.

Each clone must roll WIS Saving Throw to maintain their sense of identity. The Originals' presence shook something deep — the question of who they really are.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 16. Plus 3 is 19. Pass.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 14. Plus 0 is 14. Pass.

**IQA-112:** [rolls] 18. Plus 3 is 21. Pass.

**HQW-006:** [rolls] 10. Plus 1 is 11. Fail.

**Mother Tmojizu:** HQW-006 fails. For a moment — just a moment — she questions who she is. Is she HQW-006? Or is she the Original Horli Qwin's shadow? A copy? A reflection? A rap with no rapper?

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm... I'm not okay.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know. But you will be.

**IQA-112:** [in character] I'm walking over to HQW-006. I'm handing her a cup of tea. And I'm saying: "You're HQW-006. You're red-headed. You're a clone. You built an empire from nothing. And you're the one who succeeded. Not the Original. You."

**HQW-006:** [in character, quietly] "I'm the one who succeeded."

**IQA-112:** [in character] "Yes. You are."

**Mother Tmojizu:** HQW-006 drinks the tea. The warmth returns. The identity holds. And the empire grows — through confrontation, through acknowledgment, through tea.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Turn 7. Let's count resources:

- **CHR-047 (Crustacean Bastion):** 350 + 100 (Original acknowledgment bonus) = **450 BSR** + 22 units + impenetrable Shield Walls
- **SOL-001 (Aether Wings):** 150 + 100 (architect code revelation) = **250 BSR** + 12 units + fully understood architect codes
- **IQA-112 (Thunderhoof Legion):** 250 + 100 (Original inspiration bonus) = **350 BSR** + 18 units + Centaur Cavalry + identity anchor
- **HQW-006 (Primate Swarm):** 650 - 50 (loyalty crisis) + 100 (IQA-112 support) = **700 BSR** + 30 units + identity restored

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The Originals returned. They confronted. They acknowledged. They left. And the clones are more themselves than ever.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Tomorrow, the betrayal comes. Tomorrow, trust breaks. Tomorrow, we learn who we really are."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I open a door I don't understand. Again. But this time, it's a betrayal door."

**IQA-112:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I brew more tea. And I keep the peace."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I rap for more coins. And definitely steal something."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "No."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Maybe."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] Sleep, ducklings. The empire will be here in the morning. The spiral turns. The pieces remember.
