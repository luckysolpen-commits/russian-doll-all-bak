# CLONE CHRONICLES — PART 2, SESSION 1: "THE FOUNDATION"
## Part 2: The Empire Game — Session 1 of 10
### Players: CHR-047 (Chara), SOL-001 (Sol-Supreme), IQA-112 (IQabella), HQW-006 (Horli Qwin) | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Part 2. You're trillionaires. You own the Blood Market. You have 17 clone workers. You have Chimochio management positions. You have The House's protection. But freedom is expensive — and empires don't build themselves.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm ready to build an empire. And by build, I mean hustle on a continental scale.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] I know.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The morning bell rings. But it's different today — deeper, wider, like the bell itself is building something. The Blood Market wakes up. But today, you're not in the market. You're on the map.

A massive territory map spreads before you — the Empire Board. It shows four zones:

- **Government Land** — safe, taxed, controlled by Chimochio
- **Company Claims** — competitive, profitable, contested
- **Unclaimed/Wild** — dangerous, free, barbarian zones
- **The Moon** — endgame, Aethelgaurd wreckage, Diabolus remnants

**Mother Tmojizu:** Each clone chooses a faction and claims a starting territory. Four factions. Rock-Paper-Scissors warfare:

**Crustacean Bastion** (Siege) — Defensive, armored. For the patient builder.
**Primate Swarm** (Horde) — Numbers, aggression. For the chaotic hustler.
**Thunderhoof Legion** (Mobile) — Speed, hit-and-run. For the strategic mover.
**Aether Wings** (Air) — Range, recon. For the visionary.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm choosing the Crustacean Bastion. Siege. Defensive. I'm building a fortress. I'm not losing what I've earned.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Crustacean Bastion. IronCrabClaw tanks, Shield Walls, defensive fortifications. You claim a territory on Government Land — safe, taxed, but secure. Your starting resources: 1,000 BSR, 1 Factory, 10 units.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm choosing the Aether Wings. Air. Range, recon. I want to see the whole board. I claim a territory on Company Claims — competitive, but with visibility.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Aether Wings. Owl flyers, Unicorn lancers, snipers. You claim a territory on Company Claims. Your starting resources: 1,000 BSR, 1 Factory, 10 units.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm choosing the Thunderhoof Legion. Mobile. Speed, hit-and-run. I claim a territory on Unclaimed/Wild — dangerous, but free. I want to build something warm in a cold place.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] The Thunderhoof Legion. Centaur cavalry, Scouts, hit-and-run tactics. You claim a territory on Unclaimed/Wild. Your starting resources: 1,000 BSR, 1 Factory, 10 units. But the Wild is dangerous — barbarian zones, resource nodes, hidden treasures.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm choosing the Primate Swarm. Horde. Numbers, aggression. I claim a territory on Company Claims — right next to SOL-001. I want to be close to the action.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans warmly] Of course you do. The Primate Swarm. WarApeFist bruisers, Rat Swarms, overwhelming numbers. You claim a territory on Company Claims — adjacent to SOL-001's Aether Wings. Your starting resources: 1,000 BSR, 1 Factory, 10 units.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The board is set. Four factions. Four territories. Four empires. Turn 1 begins.

Each clone makes their business decisions: HR, R&D, Marketing, Manufacturing, Finance.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] I'm hiring 5 units (HR), spending 200 BSR on R&D for shield technology, spending 100 BSR on Marketing, building 3 defensive structures (Manufacturing), and borrowing 200 BSR (Finance).

**Mother Tmojizu:** Noted. Your Crustacean Bastion fortifies. Shield Walls rise. IronCrabClaw tanks patrol. Your territory is a fortress.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] I'm hiring 3 units (HR), spending 300 BSR on R&D for owl reconnaissance technology, spending 200 BSR on Marketing, building 2 owl flyers (Manufacturing), and selling shares for 300 BSR (Finance).

**Mother Tmojizu:** Noted. Your Aether Wings take flight. Owl scouts circle your territory. Unicorn lancers patrol the borders. Your territory sees everything.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm hiring 5 units (HR), spending 100 BSR on R&D for centaur medical technology, spending 300 BSR on Marketing (warmth advertising), building 3 centaur cavalry (Manufacturing), and borrowing 100 BSR (Finance).

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] Noted. Your Thunderhoof Legion gallops. Centaur cavalry patrol the Wild. Scouts map the barbarian zones. Your territory is fast, warm, and free.

**HQW-006:** [OOC] T-Mo, I'm hiring 10 units (HR), spending 50 BSR on R&D (bare minimum), spending 50 BSR on Marketing (rap advertising), building 5 WarApeFist bruisers (Manufacturing), and borrowing 500 BSR (Finance). I'm going all-in on numbers.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans] I knew you were going to say that. Your Primate Swarm swarms. WarApeFist bruisers pound the ground. Rat Swarms fill the cracks. Your territory is loud, aggressive, and everywhere.

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 1.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Two clones claim adjacent territories — border tension.

HQW-006's Primate Swarm is adjacent to SOL-001's Aether Wings. The border is contested. The WarApeFist bruisers are pounding too close to the owl scouts. The owl scouts are watching too closely. Tension rises.

**SOL-001:** [in character] "HQW-006, your bruisers are too close to my border. Pull them back."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Make me."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll CHA Persuasion to de-escalate.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 12. Plus 1 is 13.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 13. It's not enough. The border tension remains. But it doesn't escalate — not yet.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I want to mediate. I'm riding my centaur cavalry to the border. I'm offering tea to both sides. CHA Persuasion.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**IQA-112:** [rolls] 19. Plus 4 is 23.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 23. IQA-112 rides her centaur cavalry to the border. She sets up a tea stand. She offers tea to the WarApeFist bruisers and the owl scouts. They drink. They pause. They look at each other. And for one moment, the border is peaceful.

**IQA-112:** [in character] "There's enough land for everyone. Share it."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] There is. For now.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Turn 1. Let's count resources:

- **CHR-047 (Crustacean Bastion):** 1,000 - 200 (R&D) - 100 (Marketing) - 300 (Manufacturing) + 200 (borrowed) = **600 BSR** + 15 units + Shield Walls
- **SOL-001 (Aether Wings):** 1,000 - 300 (R&D) - 200 (Marketing) - 200 (Manufacturing) + 300 (shares) = **600 BSR** + 13 units + Owl Flyers
- **IQA-112 (Thunderhoof Legion):** 1,000 - 100 (R&D) - 300 (Marketing) - 300 (Manufacturing) + 100 (borrowed) = **400 BSR** + 15 units + Centaur Cavalry
- **HQW-006 (Primate Swarm):** 1,000 - 50 (R&D) - 50 (Marketing) - 500 (Manufacturing) + 500 (borrowed) = **900 BSR** + 20 units + WarApeFist Bruisers

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The foundation is laid. Four factions. Four territories. Four empires. The board is set. The pieces remember.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Tomorrow, we expand. Tomorrow, we build. Tomorrow, we win."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I open a door I don't understand. Again. But this time, it's an empire door."

**IQA-112:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I brew more tea. And I keep the peace."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I rap for more coins. And definitely steal territory."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "No."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Maybe."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] Sleep, ducklings. The empire will be here in the morning. The spiral turns. The pieces remember.
