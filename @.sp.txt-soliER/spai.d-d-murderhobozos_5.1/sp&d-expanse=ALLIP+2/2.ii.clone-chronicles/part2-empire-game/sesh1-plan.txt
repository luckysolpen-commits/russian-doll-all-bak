### Power: Local | Status: Choosing factions, claiming territory

## SESSION OVERVIEW

The clones are trillionaires. They own the Blood Market. Now they choose factions, claim territories, and begin building empires. This session is about foundation-laying: faction choice, territory claims, initial infrastructure.

## KEY BEATS

1. **The Transition** — Mother Tmojizu opens: "You're free. You're rich. You own the market. But freedom is expensive. What do you build?"
2. **Faction Choice** — Each clone chooses a faction (Crustacean Bastion, Primate Swarm, Thunderhoof Legion, Aether Wings).
3. **Territory Claim** — Each clone claims their starting territory on the map.
4. **Initial Build** — HR, R&D, Manufacturing, Finance decisions for Turn 1.
5. **End of Turn 1** — Hook for Session 2.

## FACTION CHOICE MECHANICS

Each clone chooses based on personality and strategy:
- **Crustacean Bastion** (Siege) — Defensive, armored. For the patient builder.
- **Primate Swarm** (Horde) — Numbers, aggression. For the chaotic hustler.
- **Thunderhoof Legion** (Mobile) — Speed, hit-and-run. For the strategic mover.
- **Aether Wings** (Air) — Range, recon. For the visionary.

**Rock-Paper-Scissors:** Siege > Horde > Mobile > Air > Siege

## TERRITORY CLAIM MECHANICS

- Each clone claims 1 territory (starting claim)
- Territory types: Government Land (safe, taxed), Company Claims (competitive), Unclaimed/Wild (dangerous, free)
- WIS (Insight) to choose the best territory
- INT (Investigation) to assess resource potential
