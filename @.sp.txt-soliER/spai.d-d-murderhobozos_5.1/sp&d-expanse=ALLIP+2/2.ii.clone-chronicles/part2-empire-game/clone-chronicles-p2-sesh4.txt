# CLONE CHRONICLES — PART 2, SESSION 4: "THE DIPLOMACY"
## Part 2: The Empire Game — Session 4 of 10
### Players: CHR-047 (Chara), SOL-001 (Sol-Supreme), IQA-112 (IQabella), HQW-006 (Horli Qwin) | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Part 2, Session 4. Turn 4. The Faction Wars have begun. SOL-001 and HQW-006 clashed. IQA-112 mediated. CHR-047 expanded. And today, diplomacy begins.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm hosting a diplomatic summit. All four clones. Neutral territory. Tea provided.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] Of course you are.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The morning bell rings. The Empire Board wakes up. And today, the Diplomatic Summit begins.

IQA-112's territory — the Thunderhoof Legion's Wild zone. A neutral clearing. A tea stand. Four chairs. And four clones, sitting down to negotiate the future of the empire.

**IQA-112:** [in character] "Welcome to the summit. Tea is served. Let's talk."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Each clone proposes an alliance, a trade deal, or a declaration. Roll CHA Persuasion for your proposal.

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm proposing a defensive alliance with IQA-112. My Crustacean Bastion protects her Thunderhoof Legion, and her centaur cavalry scouts my borders. CHA Persuasion.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 17. Plus 2 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. IQA-112 considers the proposal. She looks at CHR-047's Shield Walls. She looks at her centaur cavalry. And she nods.

**IQA-112:** [in character] "I accept. Your shields protect my cavalry. My cavalry scouts your borders. We're allies."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The defensive alliance is struck. CHR-047 and IQA-112 are now allies. Shared resources, shared defense, shared victory.

**SOL-001:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm proposing a trade alliance with HQW-006. I give her 60% of my owl scout intel, and she gives me 60% of her WarApeFist bruiser production. INT Persuasion.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**SOL-001:** [rolls] 14. Plus 1 is 15.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 15. HQW-006 considers the proposal. She looks at SOL-001's owl scouts. She looks at her WarApeFist bruisers. And she smiles.

**HQW-006:** [in character] "I accept. But I get 70% of your intel, and you get 50% of my bruisers."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "60/60. Final offer."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Fine. 60/60. But I get to rap at your victory celebrations."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Fine."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The trade alliance is struck. SOL-001 and HQW-006 are now trade partners. Shared intel, shared production, shared... victory celebrations.

**IQA-112:** [OOC] Mom, I'm proposing a peace treaty between all four clones. No more border wars. No more Rat Swarm incursions. No more owl scout surveillance. Just peace. CHA Persuasion.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**IQA-112:** [rolls] 20. Plus 4 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. IQA-112 proposes the peace treaty. She looks at CHR-047. She looks at SOL-001. She looks at HQW-006. And she says:

**IQA-112:** [in character] "We're clones. We were born in a vat. We owe money. We have serial numbers — or scars where they used to be. We've hustled, traded, schemed, and survived. We've built empires from nothing. And now we're fighting each other over borders that don't exist. Let's stop. Let's build together. Not against each other. With each other."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The summit goes quiet. The tea steeps. The clones look at each other. And one by one, they nod.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "I accept."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "I accept."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "I accept. But I'm still rapping at victory celebrations."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] The peace treaty is struck. All four clones are now at peace. No more border wars. No more Rat Swarm incursions. No more owl scout surveillance. Just... peace.

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 2.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The House demands all clones submit to its authority.

**THE HOUSE:** [from the walls] "Clones. You've made peace. How sweet. But The House wants its cut. 15% of all empire earnings. Forever. Submit, and I'll protect you from everything. Refuse, and I'll withdraw my protection. And Chimochio will come for you."

**CHR-047:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm negotiating. INT Investigation to find leverage.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHR-047:** [rolls] 16. Plus 4 is 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 20. You find leverage: The House needs the clones' empire as much as the clones need The House's protection. Without the empire, The House loses its best tea events, its best protection, its best management, and its best architect codes. The House is bluffing about withdrawing protection — it can't afford to lose you.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "We'll give you 8%. Not 15%. And we get a clone representative in your inner circle."

**THE HOUSE:** [pauses] "10%. And a clone advisory position — no inner circle seat."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "8%. And a clone advisory position with voting power on clone-related decisions."

**THE HOUSE:** "8%. Advisory position with voting power. Done. But the first payment is due tomorrow."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The deal is struck. The House has its cut. The clones have their protection. And the empire is one step closer to being truly sovereign.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Turn 4. Let's count resources:

- **CHR-047 (Crustacean Bastion):** 350 + 100 (alliance bonus) = **450 BSR** + 20 units + Shield Walls + alliance with IQA-112
- **SOL-001 (Aether Wings):** 150 + 100 (trade bonus) = **250 BSR** + 12 units + Owl Flyers + trade alliance with HQW-006
- **IQA-112 (Thunderhoof Legion):** 100 + 100 (peace treaty bonus) = **200 BSR** + 18 units + Centaur Cavalry + peace treaty with all
- **HQW-006 (Primate Swarm):** 300 + 100 (trade bonus) = **400 BSR** + 22 units + WarApeFist Bruisers + trade alliance with SOL-001

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] The Diplomacy is over. Alliances formed. Treaties signed. The House dealt with. And the empire grows — through peace, through trade, through tea.

**CHR-047:** [in character] "Tomorrow, we expand. Tomorrow, we build. Tomorrow, we win."

**SOL-001:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I open a door I don't understand. Again. But this time, it's a peace door."

**IQA-112:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I brew more tea. And I keep the peace."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Tomorrow, I rap for more coins. And definitely steal territory."

**CHR-047:** [in character] "No."

**HQW-006:** [in character] "Maybe."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] Sleep, ducklings. The empire will be here in the morning. The spiral turns. The pieces remember.
