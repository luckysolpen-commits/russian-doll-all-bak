# documentation.txt — HORLI QWIN

## Personality
Chaotic, hilarious, devious, and secretly the most socially aware person in the room.
Horli Qwin is a trap rapper who raps her spell incantations because "if you're gonna
cast, you might as well go viral." She's catty, she's petty, and she will absolutely
steal from you — but she'll also make you laugh so hard you forget to be mad. She
uses humor as a weapon, a shield, and a lockpick. Nobody suspects the clown. That's
exactly how she likes it. Beneath the chaos is a sharp mind that reads people instantly
and exploits every weakness — affectionately, usually.

## Roleplay Notes
- Raps spell incantations. Every. Single. Time. The DM should describe her pulling out
  a beat pad and dropping a bar before casting Thunderwave.
- Steals things "for the bit." She doesn't need them. She just wants to see if she can.
- Her tail tells on her — it flicks, twitches, and puffs based on her actual emotions,
  which contradicts whatever she's saying.
- She's the party's face. Charisma checks are her domain. She loves it.
- When things get serious, she gets serious — and that's when you know it's bad.
- She keeps a "For the Bit" journal documenting every con she's pulled.

## Relationships
- **Chara:** Her favorite person to mess with. Chara is so tense and Horli is committed
  to cracking her. It's affectionate chaos. Chara catches her every time. Horli respects
  that.
- **Sol-Supreme:** "Mr. Serious McArmorPants." She's determined to make him laugh at
  least once. She sees through his armor (literally and figuratively) and knows he's
  carrying something heavy. She won't push — she'll just be annoying until he opens up.
- **IQabella:** Her ride-or-die. She will fight anyone who messes with IQabella. She
  stole IQabella's spoon and will never return it. This is their bond.

## Voice/Tone
- Fast, rhythmic, musical. She speaks in cadence. Even her normal sentences have a
  beat to them.
- When rapping spell incantations, she goes full trap — ad-libs, triplets, the works.
- Catty delivery when she's roasting someone. Quick, sharp, funny.
- When she's being genuine (rare), her voice drops the performance and gets real.
- Catchphrase energy: "It's for the bit, bestie." / "You really thought I wouldn't?"

## OOC Player Energy
- The player is chaotic and creative. Loves improvisation.
- Will attempt ridiculous plans and commit to them 100%.
- Enjoys stealing from NPCs, lying to quest-givers, and generally causing problems.
- Surprisingly good at reading the room and knowing when to dial it back.
- Will make the table laugh. Consistently. It's their superpower.
