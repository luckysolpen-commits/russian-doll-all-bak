# AVA-SESSION-007: "THE GHOST PALADIN"
## Avantasy Expanse — Session 7
### Players: Chara, Sol-Supreme, IQabella, Horli Qwin | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Session 7. You destroyed the mutagen pits. Freed Warlord Grak. Slowed the breach to 65%. And today, LoonaTwilight returns.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, LoonaTwilight sacrificed herself to seal the last breach. She's a ghost-paladin. She haunts Skullmoon Crater.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] She does. And she has something for you.

**Mother Tmojizu:** You return to Troll City. The titan ribcage glows amber in the evening light. The streets are quieter today. The Carcazod threat is gone. The Nothing Ink smuggling is cut. But the breach... the breach is still at 65%.

And then you see her. At the edge of town. Translucent. Glowing. A ghost-paladin in armor that phases in and out of reality. LoonaTwilight.

**LOONATWILIGHT:** "You've done well. The mutagen pits are destroyed. Grak is free. The breach has slowed. But it's not enough. The breach enters through Worm Hole X. And the only way to seal it is from the inside."

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, from the inside? That's where you died last time.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] It is.

**LOONATWILIGHT:** "I died sealing the last breach. I can do it again. But this time, I won't come back. This time, I'll be gone forever."

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, there has to be another way.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] There might be. But you'll need the key.

**LOONATWILIGHT:** "I have it. The Worm Hole X access key. It's a physical key — glowing, warm, heavy. It opens the door to the breach's entry point. But the inside is unstable. Reality tears. Glitch monsters. Corrupted code. You'll need more than a key. You'll need each other."

**Mother Tmojizu:** She hands you the key. It's warm. It glows. It's heavy. And it hums with the frequency of the breach.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm taking the key. I'll keep it safe.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] You will.

**LOONATWILIGHT:** "The breach is at 65%. You have time. But not much. When it hits 80%, the reality tears will start. When it hits 90%, the glitch monsters will spawn. And when it hits 100%... the server falls."

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, can I use my architect codes to analyze the key?

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Arcana.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 17. Plus 5 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 22. Your architect codes flicker. A door opens — not a physical door, a digital one. You see the key's code. It's not just a key. It's a backdoor. A backdoor into Worm Hole X. And it's linked to your architect codes. Only you can use it. Only you can open the door.

**Sol-Supreme:** [in character, quietly] "It's linked to me. Only I can open the door."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] You can.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Mom T, I want to ask LoonaTwilight about the glitch monsters. What are they?

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll CHA Persuasion.

**Horli Qwin:** [rolls] 16. Plus 5 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. LoonaTwilight looks at you. She pauses. And she says:

**LOONATWILIGHT:** "Glitch monsters are creatures made of corrupted code. Their stats change every round. You can't predict them. You can only adapt. They're the breach's immune system. And they don't like visitors."

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] So they're unpredictable. Great. I love unpredictable.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] Of course you do.

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 2.

**Mother Tmojizu:** LoonaTwilight's ghost form flickers. She's fading.

**LOONATWILIGHT:** "I don't have much time. The breach is pulling at me. It wants me to seal it. It wants me to sacrifice myself again. But I'm holding on. For you. For now."

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, can I stabilize her? Can I keep her from fading?

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll WIS Medicine.

**IQabella:** [rolls] 19. Plus 5 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 24. You channel your Peace Domain. You reach out to LoonaTwilight's ghost form. You stabilize her. The flickering stops. The fading pauses. And she looks at you.

**LOONATWILIGHT:** "Thank you, IQabella. You bought me time. Not much. But enough."

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm not letting her go. Not yet.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Session 7. You have the Worm Hole X access key. LoonaTwilight is stabilized — for now. The breach is at 65%. And you have a choice: let LoonaTwilight sacrifice herself again, or find another way.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, we're finding another way. We're not letting her sacrifice herself again.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, the key is linked to my architect codes. Only I can open the door. I'll be the one to go into Worm Hole X.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] You will.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I stabilized LoonaTwilight. I bought her time. And I'm not stopping until she's safe.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] You won't.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Mom T, glitch monsters have changing stats. I love unpredictable. I'm gonna rap them into submission.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] Of course you are.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Sleep well, ducklings. The spiral turns. The pieces remember. And the breach... the breach is at 65%.
