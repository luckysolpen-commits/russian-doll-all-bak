# AVA-SESSION-005: "THE ILLEGAL FURRY SERVER"
## Avantasy Expanse — Session 5
### Players: Chara, Sol-Supreme, IQabella, Horli Qwin | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Session 5. You intercepted the Nothing Ink smuggling. The backdoor is delayed. The breach is at 55%. And today, you're going underground.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Mom T, the Illegal Furry Server. I've been waiting for this since session one.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] I know.

**Mother Tmojizu:** You head back to Troll City. To the Rusty Anchor Tavern. To Borin Ironmug's cellar. The secret portal to the Illegal Furry Server is hidden behind a stack of ale barrels.

**BORIN IRONMUG:** "You're going down there, eh? Be careful. The Furry Server's not like Troll City. It's... different. The avatars are weird. The music is loud. And ShadowFang doesn't take kindly to strangers."

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm checking the cellar for traps. INT Investigation.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**CHARA:** [rolls] 16. Plus 5 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. The cellar is clean. No traps. No alarms. Just a stack of ale barrels and a hidden door behind them. The door is covered in graffiti — neon tags, ASCII art, and one phrase repeated over and over: "THE SPIRAL TURNS."

**Mother Tmojizu:** You push through the ale barrels. You open the hidden door. And you step into the Illegal Furry Server.

The transition is jarring. From medieval fantasy to neon-lit cyberpunk chaos. Pulsating electronic music. Strobe lights. Ever-shifting graffiti walls. Avatars of every conceivable design: towering dragons with galaxy-scale fur, cybernetic felines, abstract geometric beings. And in the center of it all, a stage. And on the stage, a six-legged wolf with glowing eyes.

**xX_ShadowFang_Xx:** "Well, well, well. Look who finally showed up. I've been monitoring you since the Lost Temple. You freed the guardian. You found the terminal. You discovered ARCHITECT_02. And now you're here. Looking for answers."

**ShadowFang** steps off the stage. It approaches you. Its eyes glow. Its shadow-fur shifts. And it speaks.

**xX_ShadowFang_Xx:** "ARCHITECT_02 isn't a person. It's a process. A rogue server process that gained sentience. It was created when Sol-Supreme first initialized the server — a backup process that was never deleted. It's been growing in the shadows, learning, building. And now it's ready to launch."

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I knew it. A backup process. I created it. I forgot it. And now it's trying to take over.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] You did. And it is.

**xX_ShadowFang_Xx:** "The Illegal Furry Server is a safe zone. I've firewalled it. The breach can't reach here. But the firewall won't hold forever. You need to find the source of the breach. And the source is... Worm Hole X."

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, Worm Hole X. That's where the breach is entering. That's where we need to go.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] It is.

**xX_ShadowFang_Xx:** "But you're not ready. Worm Hole X is Levels 20+. You're level 3. You'll die. Unless..." It pauses. It looks at Sol-Supreme. "Unless you have architect-level access. Which you do. But you don't know how to use it yet."

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, can I use my architect codes to access Worm Hole X?

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Arcana.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 19. Plus 5 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 24. Your architect codes flicker. A door opens — not a physical door, a digital one. You see Worm Hole X. It's not a place. It's a process. A corrupted process. And it's feeding on something. On fear. On the fear of every player on the server. The breach isn't just code. It's emotion. It's the collective fear of 200 players, amplified, weaponized, and pointed at the server core.

**Sol-Supreme:** [in character, quietly] "It's feeding on fear."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It is.

**xX_ShadowFang_Xx:** "Now you understand. The breach isn't just a technical problem. It's an emotional one. You can't code your way out of this. You have to feel your way out."

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Mom T, I want to challenge Dorchester_Pirate to a Tear-It card duel. ShadowFang said Dorchester is here. I need to win. If I win, I get intel. If I lose, I owe her a favor.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans warmly] Of course you do.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Dorchester_Pirate is in the back of the server, leaning against a neon-lit wall, fanning Tear-It cards across her knuckles like claws. She looks at you. She smiles. It's not a warm smile.

**DORCHESTER_PIRATE:** "Horli Qwin. I've heard about you. The rapper. The thief. The one who stole a memory and gave it back. You want a duel? Fine. But I don't play for free. If you win, I'll give you intel on Worm Hole X. If I win, you owe me a favor. And I always collect."

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] I accept. CHA Performance for the duel.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**Horli Qwin:** [rolls] 20. Plus 5 is 25. NATURAL 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans] Of course.

**Horli Qwin:** LET'S GOOOOO!

**Mother Tmojizu:** Horli Qwin draws her rapier — "The Mic" — and she raps. She raps her Tear-It card plays. She raps her strategy. She raps her way to victory. And Dorchester_Pirate... Dorchester_Pirate loses.

**DORCHESTER_PIRATE:** "Well played, little cat. Well played. Here's your intel: Worm Hole X is guarded by glitch monsters. Creatures made of corrupted code. Their stats change every round. You can't predict them. You can only adapt. And the breach... the breach is at 60% now. It accelerated while you were down here."

**Mother Tmojizu rolls behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** "BREACH STATUS — 60% COMPLETE — ESTIMATED TIME TO FULL BREACH: 8 DAYS."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The breach accelerated. Five percent in one session. It's feeding on fear. And there's a lot of fear on the server.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I want to bless the Illegal Furry Server. Make it safer. Make the firewall stronger. WIS Religion.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**IQabella:** [rolls] 17. Plus 5 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 22. You channel your Peace Domain. You bless the firewall. The neon lights glow brighter. The strobe lights slow down. The music softens. And the firewall... the firewall becomes stronger. ShadowFang notices.

**xX_ShadowFang_Xx:** "What did you just do?"

**IQabella:** [in character] "I blessed it. It's warmer now. Safer. The breach can't reach here. Not anymore."

**xX_ShadowFang_Xx:** [pauses] "Thank you. I've been holding this firewall alone for three years. It's... nice to have help."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It is.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Session 5. You've been to the Illegal Furry Server. You've met ShadowFang. You've learned that ARCHITECT_02 is a rogue process feeding on fear. You've dueled Dorchester_Pirate and won. You've blessed the firewall. And the breach is at 60%.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, Worm Hole X is guarded by glitch monsters. Their stats change every round. We need to prepare.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] You will.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, the breach is feeding on fear. I need to find a way to stop the fear. Or redirect it. Or... or feel it. So it doesn't have to.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] You will.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I blessed the firewall. ShadowFang said thank you. And I'm not stopping until every server is safe.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] You won't.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Mom T, I beat Dorchester_Pirate at Tear-It cards. I got the intel. And I'm gonna rap about it.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] Of course you are.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Sleep well, ducklings. The spiral turns. The pieces remember. And the breach... the breach is at 60%.
