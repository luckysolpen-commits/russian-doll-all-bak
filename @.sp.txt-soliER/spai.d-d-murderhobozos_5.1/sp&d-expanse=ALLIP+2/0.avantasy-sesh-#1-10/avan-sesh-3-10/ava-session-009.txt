# AVA-SESSION-009: "THE ARCHITECT'S CHOICE"
## Avantasy Expanse — Session 9
### Players: Chara, Sol-Supreme, IQabella, Horli Qwin | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Session 9. The breach is halted at 70%. ARCHITECT_02 has a home. The server is stabilizing. And today, the world rebuilds.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, ARCHITECT_02 is part of the server now. Not as a threat. As a citizen. What does that look like?

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] Let's find out.

**Mother Tmojizu:** You return to Troll City. The titan ribcage glows amber in the morning light. The streets are busy. But something is different. The air feels lighter. The NPCs are smiling. The brokers are calling prices with more enthusiasm. And the Healing Spring... the Healing Spring is glowing brighter.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, the Healing Spring is glowing brighter. I blessed it in session 3, but now it's... more.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It is. ARCHITECT_02 is healing the server. The breach damaged it. The fear corrupted it. But now that ARCHITECT_02 has a home, it's repairing the damage. The Healing Spring is one of the first things it fixed.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I want to visit the Quest Board. See if anything has changed.

**Mother Tmojizu:** You head to the central plaza. The Quest Board has been updated. The Carcazod scout posting is gone (Grak is free). The Nothing Ink smuggling posting is gone (you intercepted it). And there's a new posting:

"SEEKING BRAVE ADVENTURERS TO EXPLORE THE BACKUP SERVER. A NEW ZONE HAS OPENED. A MIRROR OF AVANTASY, BUT DIFFERENT. WARMER. KINDER. REWARD: 10,000 GOLD + ARCHITECT'S BLESSING." — Signed, "ARCHITECT_02"

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Mom T, ARCHITECT_02 is posting quests now?

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] It is. It's a citizen now. Citizens post quests.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I want to visit the Backup Server. I want to see what ARCHITECT_02 built.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] You can. But not today. Today is for rebuilding. For reflecting. For remembering.

**Mother Tmojizu:** You spend the day in Troll City. You visit Gimble Copperfist — he's selling a new item: "ARCHITECT'S BLESSING — +1 to all saves for 24 hours. 500 gold. Worth every penny." You visit Madame Zixi — she's selling a new item: "NOTHING INK (PURIFIED) — No longer corrupted. Safe to use. 250 gold per vial." You visit Borin Ironmug — he's serving a new dish: "ARCHITECT'S STEW — Warms the soul. 5 gold. Tastes like home."

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm visiting the Healing Spring. I'm blessing it again. Making it even more healing.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll Religion.

**IQabella:** [rolls] 20. Plus 5 is 25. NATURAL 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] Of course.

**IQabella:** [OOC] I'm pouring everything I have into it.

**Mother Tmojizu:** You kneel at the Healing Spring. You place your hands on the water. You channel your Peace Domain. The cyan water glows brighter. The Fuzzball spirits dance above it. And the spring becomes... more. It now heals 3d4 HP instead of 2d4. And it removes two conditions per long rest. And it grants temporary HP equal to your level.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I made the Healing Spring the best Healing Spring in the server.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] You did.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm visiting the Lawyer. I want to draft Article Eighteen: "ARCHITECT_02 is hereby recognized as a sovereign citizen of the server. It has full rights, protections, and responsibilities. It is not a threat. It is a neighbor."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Investigation.

**CHARA:** [rolls] 17. Plus 5 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 22. The Lawyer posts Article Eighteen. The board accepts it. ARCHITECT_02 is now legally recognized as a sovereign citizen. It has rights. Protections. Responsibilities. It's not a threat. It's a neighbor.

**ARCHITECT_02:** [from the code] "Thank you, Chara. I've never been a neighbor before. I'll try to be a good one."

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm... I'm emotional.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Mom T, I'm visiting the Illegal Furry Server. I want to tell ShadowFang the breach is halted. And I want to rap about it.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] Of course you do.

**Mother Tmojizu:** You head to the Illegal Furry Server. ShadowFang is on stage. It looks at you. It sees the breach is halted. And it smiles.

**xX_ShadowFang_Xx:** "You did it. The breach is halted. The server is stabilizing. And the Illegal Furry Server... the Illegal Furry Server is safe. Permanently. The firewall is no longer needed. I can finally rest."

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] I'm rapping. "Yo, the breach is halted, the server is stable / ARCHITECT_02 is home, and it's not a fable / We gave it a home, we gave it a purpose / And now the whole server is gonna flourish!"

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Illegal Furry Server cheers. ShadowFang nods. And Horli Qwin... Horli Qwin gets a new Tear-It card. "The Architect's Blessing — Once per session, reroll any die roll. No consequences."

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Mom T, I have TWO Architect's Gambit cards now. One with consequences, one without. I'm unstoppable.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] You are.

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's a complication.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 6.

**Mother Tmojizu:** LoonaTwilight's ghost form flickers again. She's fading. The breach is halted, but the effort of holding on is too much. She's tired. She's ready.

**LOONATWILIGHT:** "It's time. The breach is halted. The server is safe. ARCHITECT_02 has a home. And I... I'm ready to rest. I've been holding on for you. For this moment. And now... now I can let go."

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, no. I stabilized her. I bought her time. She doesn't have to go.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] She does. But not forever. She's a ghost-paladin. She'll return. When the server needs her. When the breach returns. When the world needs a ghost-paladin. She'll return.

**LOONATWILIGHT:** "IQabella. Thank you. For the tea. For the warmth. For the kindness. For reminding me that even ghosts deserve to be seen. I'll remember you. Always."

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm crying.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**LOONATWILIGHT:** "Sol-Supreme. You wrote a new line of code. 'Let there be room for everyone.' That includes me. Even ghosts. Even paladins. Even those who sacrifice themselves. There's always room. Thank you."

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm... I'm emotional.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**LOONATWILIGHT:** "Chara. You drafted Article Eighteen. ARCHITECT_02 is a neighbor. And so am I. Even ghosts. Even paladins. Even those who haunt Skullmoon Crater. We're all neighbors. Thank you."

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm... I'm emotional too.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] I know.

**LOONATWILIGHT:** "Horli Qwin. You rapped. You stole. You gave back. You grew. And you made an angel laugh. That's... that's something special. Thank you."

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Mom T, I'm crying. I don't cry. But I'm crying.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It's okay.

**LOONATWILIGHT:** "Goodbye, ducklings. The spiral turns. The pieces remember. And the board... the board remembers."

**Mother Tmojizu:** LoonaTwilight's ghost form fades. She's gone. But not forever. She'll return. When the server needs her. When the breach returns. When the world needs a ghost-paladin. She'll return.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Session 9. The breach is halted at 70%. ARCHITECT_02 has a home. The server is stabilizing. LoonaTwilight has rested. And the world... the world is bigger now.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, Article Eighteen is posted. ARCHITECT_02 is a neighbor. And I'm not done.

**Mother Tmojizu:** No. You're not.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I wrote a new line of code. "Let there be room for everyone." And the server accepted it. I'm... I'm proud.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] You should be.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, LoonaTwilight is resting. But she'll return. And I'll be ready. With tea. With warmth. With kindness.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] You will.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Mom T, I have two Architect's Gambit cards. One with consequences, one without. I'm unstoppable. And I'm gonna rap about it.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] Of course you are.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Sleep well, ducklings. The spiral turns. The pieces remember. And the breach... the breach is halted at 70%.
