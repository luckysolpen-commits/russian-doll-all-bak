# AVA-SESSION-006: "THE WASTES"
## Avantasy Expanse — Session 6
### Players: Chara, Sol-Supreme, IQabella, Horli Qwin | DM (Mother Tmojizu)

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. Session 6. You've been to the Illegal Furry Server. You've learned ARCHITECT_02 is a rogue process feeding on fear. The breach is at 60%. And today, you're going to the Carcazod Wastes.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, the Wastes are Levels 8-12. We're level 3. This is dangerous.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It is. But the mutagen pits are there. The source of the Carcazod spawning. If you destroy them, you cut off ARCHITECT_02's army. No army, no fear. No fear, no breach.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm ready. AC 18. Extra Attack. INT 20. IKARUS-7 with 2d8 Repulsor Blast. Let's go.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] You are.

**Mother Tmojizu:** You leave Troll City through the east gate. The jungle gives way to badlands. The badlands give way to ruins. And the ruins give way to the Carcazod Wastes.

Post-apocalyptic. Twisted carnival rides. Faded big-top tents. The skeletal remains of giant joke machines. And everywhere — Carcazods. Monkey-cockroach hybrids with chitinous exoskeletons and glowing red eyes.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls d6 for a random encounter.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** [rolls] 3.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Carcazod Scout Pack x4. They spot you. They screech. They charge.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm casting Silence on the area. No sound. They can't call for backup.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll Spell Save DC.

**CHARA:** [rolls] 16. Plus 5 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. The Silence falls. The Carcazods screech — but no sound comes out. They're confused. They slow down. And that's when you strike.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] I'm attacking with Extra Attack. Two swings. Warhammer.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll both attacks.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 18 and 15. Plus 7 is 25 and 22. Both hit.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Damage?

**Sol-Supreme:** First swing: 1d8 + 5. [rolls] 7 + 5 is 12. Second swing: 1d8 + 5. [rolls] 4 + 5 is 9. Total 21 bludgeoning damage. One Carcazod goes down.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Carcazod falls. The other three scatter. But Horli Qwin is flanking.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] I'm using Slashing Flourish. Rapier. AoE.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll attack.

**Horli Qwin:** [rolls] 17. Plus 5 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Hits. Damage?

**Horli Qwin:** 1d8 + 5. [rolls] 6 + 5 is 11. Plus Slashing Flourish 1d6. [rolls] 4. Total 15 slashing damage. And the flourish hits adjacent enemies — both nearby Carcazods take 5 slashing damage each.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Two Carcazods go down. The last one tries to flee. But IQabella is there.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm casting Sacred Flame. DEX save.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The Carcazod rolls... [rolls] 8. It fails.

**IQabella:** Damage: 2d8 radiant. [rolls] 6, 4. That's 10 radiant damage.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The last Carcazod falls. The scout pack is defeated. The Silence lifts. And the Wastes are quiet again.

**Mother Tmojizu:** But there's more. Ahead, you see it: the Mutagen Pits. Glowing green pools where Carcazods spawn. Three of them. And guarding them — Warlord Grak.

**WARLORD GRAK:** "GRAK SMASH. GRAK TAKE. GRAK RULE."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Warlord Grak is massive. CR 8. Chitinous armor. Carcazod claw. And behind him, the mutagen pits — bubbling, glowing, spawning.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, we don't need to kill Grak. We need to destroy the mutagen pits. IQabella's Healer can purify them. Sol-Supreme's architect codes can shut them down.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] Correct. You don't need to kill Grak. You need to destroy the pits.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm using my architect codes to analyze the mutagen pits. INT Arcana.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll it.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 20. Plus 5 is 25. NATURAL 20.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [groans warmly] Of course.

**Sol-Supreme:** LET'S GO!

**Mother Tmojizu:** Your architect codes flicker. A door opens — not a physical door, a digital one. You see the mutagen pits. They're not natural. They're server processes. ARCHITECT_02's processes. Each pit is a spawning node. Destroy the node, stop the spawning. And the code is vulnerable. You can shut it down with a single command.

**Sol-Supreme:** [in character] "I can shut them down. But I need time. Three rounds. And I need protection."

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'll protect him. Sneak Attack on anything that gets close.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'll buff everyone. Emboldening Bond on Chara, Sol-Supreme, and Horli Qwin. And I'll purify the pits once Sol-Supreme shuts them down.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Mom T, I'll flank. Slashing Flourish. AoE. And I'll rap. Because if I'm gonna protect an architect, I'm gonna do it with style.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] The plan is set. Three rounds. Protect Sol-Supreme. Shut down the pits. Purify the mutagen.

**Round 1.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** Warlord Grak charges. He's not attacking Sol-Supreme. He's attacking the pits. He's protecting them.

**WARLORD GRAK:** "GRAK PROTECT PITS. GRAK SMASH ARCHITECT."

**Mother Tmojizu:** Grak swings at Sol-Supreme. [rolls] 19. Plus 8 is 27.

**Sol-Supreme:** AC 18. Hits.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Damage: 2d10 + 8. [rolls] 8, 6 + 8. That's 22 slashing damage.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] I'm down 22 HP. I'm at... [checks] 18 HP. I'm still up.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm casting Healing Word on Sol-Supreme. Bonus action.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll 2d4 + 3.

**IQabella:** [rolls] 3, 2 + 3. That's 8 healing.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Sol-Supreme is back to 26 HP. He's analyzing the pits. Round 1 of 3.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm attacking Grak. Sneak Attack. Shortsword.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll attack.

**CHARA:** [rolls] 17. Plus 5 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Hits. Damage?

**CHARA:** 1d6 + 5. [rolls] 3 + 5 is 8. Plus Sneak Attack 2d6. [rolls] 5, 4. Total 17 slashing damage.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Grak takes the hit. He roars. He swings at Chara. [rolls] 14. Plus 8 is 22.

**CHARA:** AC 16. Hits.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Damage: 2d10 + 8. [rolls] 7, 3 + 8. That's 18 slashing damage.

**CHARA:** [OOC] I'm down 18 HP. I'm at 14 HP. I'm still up.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] I'm using Slashing Flourish. Rapier. AoE.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll attack.

**Horli Qwin:** [rolls] 16. Plus 5 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Hits. Damage: 1d8 + 5 + 1d6 flourish. [rolls] 5 + 5 + 4. Total 14 slashing damage. And the flourish hits Grak again for 5.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Grak roars. He's hurt. But he's not down. Round 1 ends. Sol-Supreme is 1/3 done.

**Round 2.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** Grak charges Sol-Supreme again. [rolls] 16. Plus 8 is 24.

**Sol-Supreme:** AC 18. Hits.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Damage: 2d10 + 8. [rolls] 9, 4 + 8. That's 21 slashing damage.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] I'm down 21 HP. I'm at 5 HP. I'm barely up.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm casting Healing Word again. Bonus action.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll 2d4 + 3.

**IQabella:** [rolls] 4, 1 + 3. That's 8 healing.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Sol-Supreme is back to 13 HP. He's analyzing the pits. Round 2 of 3.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, I'm attacking Grak again. Sneak Attack.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll attack.

**CHARA:** [rolls] 19. Plus 5 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Hits. Damage: 1d6 + 5 + 2d6. [rolls] 4 + 5 + 6, 3. Total 18 slashing damage.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Grak roars. He's badly hurt. But he's still protecting the pits.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] I'm using Slashing Flourish again. Rapier. AoE.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll attack.

**Horli Qwin:** [rolls] 18. Plus 5 is 23.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Hits. Damage: 1d8 + 5 + 1d6. [rolls] 7 + 5 + 3. Total 15 slashing damage.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Grak falls. He's not dead. He's KO'd. But the pits are still active. Round 2 ends. Sol-Supreme is 2/3 done.

**Round 3.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** The pits are spawning. Carcazod Scout x2 emerge from the green glow. They charge.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm casting Sacred Flame on both. DEX saves.

**Mother Tmojizu:** They roll... [rolls] 6 and 9. Both fail.

**IQabella:** Damage: 2d8 each. [rolls] 5, 3 and 7, 2. That's 8 and 9 radiant damage. Both Carcazods go down.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The scouts are defeated. Sol-Supreme finishes his analysis. Round 3 of 3.

**Sol-Supreme:** [in character, deep distorted voice] "Shutdown command executed."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The mutagen pits flicker. The green glow fades. The spawning stops. The Carcazod Wastes... go quiet.

Warlord Grak stirs. He's KO'd, not dead. He looks up. He sees the pits. They're dark. He looks at you. And he says:

**WARLORD GRAK:** "Grak... free?"

**IQabella:** [in character] "Yes. You're free. The mutagen is gone. You're not controlled anymore."

**WARLORD GRAK:** [pauses] "Grak... thank you."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] Warlord Grak is free. The mutagen pits are destroyed. The Carcazod spawning has stopped. And the breach... the breach slows.

**Mother Tmojizu rolls behind the screen.**

**Mother Tmojizu:** "BREACH STATUS — 65% COMPLETE — ESTIMATED TIME TO FULL BREACH: 12 DAYS."

**Mother Tmojizu:** The breach decelerated. Five percent slower. The mutagen pits were feeding it. No more pits, no more fear. No more fear, slower breach.

**CHARA:** [OOC] Ma'am, we did it. We freed Grak. We destroyed the pits. We slowed the breach.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] You did.

**Sol-Supreme:** [OOC] Tomo, I'm at 13 HP. I need a rest.

**IQabella:** [OOC] Mom, I'm casting Healing Word on everyone. And then we're taking a long rest.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] You earned it.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Session 6. The mutagen pits are destroyed. Warlord Grak is freed. The breach slowed to 65%. And the Wastes... the Wastes are quiet.

**Horli Qwin:** [OOC] Mom T, I rapped while protecting an architect. That's a new record.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [chuckles] It is.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Sleep well, ducklings. The spiral turns. The pieces remember. And the breach... the breach is at 65%.
