**DM (Mother Tmojizu):** You find yourselves in the bustling city of Troll City, the largest starter hub in the Avantasy Expanse. Curved white bone pillars rise two hundred feet above you, and the ceiling is a patchwork of timber and enchanted glass. The smell of roasted meat and dwarven ale fills your nostrils. The place is packed — players in every kind of avatar, NPCs going about their business, and the sound of laughter, argument, and someone attempting to play a lute very badly fills the air.

Behind the bar, a gruff dwarf with a magnificent beard and a permanent scowl nods to you as you enter. That's Borin Ironmug, the tavern keeper. He motions for you to find a seat at one of the empty tables in the corner.

As you settle in, you notice a few things. At a nearby table, a massive half-orc in a chef's apron is yelling at Borin about the stew. In the corner, a hooded figure sits alone, scanning the room. And at the bar, a tiefling merchant in an elaborate hat is counting gold coins with unsettling precision.
