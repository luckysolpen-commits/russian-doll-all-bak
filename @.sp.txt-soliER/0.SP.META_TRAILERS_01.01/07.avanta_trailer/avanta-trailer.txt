# AVANTA.C — MOVIE TRAILER
# =========================
# Title: "Avanta.C: The Virtual Escape"
# Length: ~3 minutes
# Voices: Narrator (Ryan), Lucky (Guy), Astra (Dalia), Edge (William), Kael (Christopher)

**NARRATOR:** When the real world became a prison, Lucky escaped into a game. But the game had its own rules. Its own dangers. And its own Emperor.

*SOUND: Digital login chime, pixel-art fanfare*

**NARRATOR:** Avanta.C. A virtual MMORPG. A world of trolls and fairies. Of Beat Saber battles and lightsaber duels. And of one man's desperate bid for freedom.

**LUCKY:** *logging in, voice shifting from weary to alert* Escape from Earth. Into the game. If the Architect can't find me here, I can buy us time.

**NARRATOR:** He arrived in Troll City. Vibrant. Chaotic. Lush greens and bright purples. Pixel-art sprites everywhere. And at the center — the Magic Tree.

**ASTRA:** *already in the game, relieved* Lucky! You made it. I've been waiting. The Renaissance Faire district is safe — for now. But Edge is here too.

**NARRATOR:** Edge Dangerman. In the game, he was worse. A guild leader. A trap-setter. And he knew Lucky was coming.

**EDGE:** *in-game, voice amplified* Welcome to Avanta.C, Lucky. I've been leveling up while you've been running. Let's see how your real-world skills translate to pixels.

**NARRATOR:** Lucky leveled up. Crafted spells at the Magic Tree. Sought the Halo Body — the ultimate avatar. The key to joining the elite.

**LUCKY:** *crafting at the Magic Tree, focused* If I can reach the Halo Body, I can access the server room. The admin controls. And from there... I can shut this whole thing down.

**NARRATOR:** But the game had secrets. An illegal furry server. A fairy city. And a lost temple holding the Eldridian Amulet.

**ASTRA:** *exploring the fairy city, awestruck* This place is incredible. Floating islands. Crystal bridges. And the amulet — it's real. It gives admin-level access.

**NARRATOR:** Then Lucky met Kael. A guide. An ally. Someone who knew the game's deepest secrets.

**KAEL:** *mysterious, helpful* The Lost Temple holds the amulet. But the path is guarded. You'll need everything you've learned. Every spell. Every level. Every friend.

**NARRATOR:** Together, they fought through Beat Saber battles. Lightsaber duels with Edge's Metal Mafia. A wild party that turned into a police bust. And an ambush in Eldrador.

**LUCKY:** *in combat, voice fierce* I didn't come this far to lose to a video game boss.

**NARRATOR:** But Kael had a secret. A betrayal. And a revelation that changed everything.

**KAEL:** *revealing his true nature, voice shifting* I'm not just a player, Lucky. I'm part of the game. Part of the code. And the Architect... he built me for you.

**NARRATOR:** The Avanta.C server room. The final level. Lucky faced Edge one more time. Lightsaber against lightsaber. Pixel against pixel.

**EDGE:** *in the server room, lightsaber ignited* This is MY game, Lucky. You're just a guest. And your subscription... is expired.

**NARRATOR:** Avanta.C. The Virtual Escape. Troll City. The Magic Tree. The Halo Body. And the game that was more real than reality.
