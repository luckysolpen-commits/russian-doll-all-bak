# SUPERWAR ICARUS — MOVIE TRAILER
# =================================
# Title: "Superwar Icarus: The Metal Sun"
# Length: ~3 minutes
# Voices: Narrator (Ryan), Lucky (Guy), Astra (Dalia), Gem (Ava), The Architect (Luke), UN Agent (Oliver)

**NARRATOR:** He built a mine camp in Africa. Colossal. Secret. And inside it, a machine that could print anything.

*SOUND: Distant drilling, glowing conduits humming*

**NARRATOR:** The Architect. Lucky's future self. The man behind every catastrophe. Every plague. Every war. And now... his grandest vision.

**LUCKY:** *holding a glowing device, voice grim* Another cryptic summons. "Earth security," it says. Vague as ever. But the coordinates are precise. Deep in the African interior.

**ASTRA:** *looking around the mine camp, uneasy* I still don't trust him. Not after everything with Loona.

**NARRATOR:** The Soul Ray. A 3D printer the size of a mountain. And its purpose: the Metal Sun. A Dyson Sphere. A shell around the sun itself.

**THE ARCHITECT:** *calm, chilling* I want to transcend human limitations. To merge with the universe through antimatter. The Metal Sun is not a weapon. It is evolution.

**GEM:** *confronting him, voice fierce* You're exploiting Loona's sacrifice. Her death means nothing to you, does it?

**THE ARCHITECT:** *cold* Her death means everything. It is the catalyst. The key to the warp drive. To antimatter. To infinity.

**NARRATOR:** The UN saw it differently. They called it a Death Star. And they sent a team to shut it down.

**UN AGENT:** *stern* The Metal Sun project is a threat to global security. We are shutting it down. Effective immediately.

**NARRATOR:** But the Architect had another plan. A spectacle. A distraction. A mecha Olympics.

*SOUND: Massive mechanical footsteps, metal clashing*

**NARRATOR:** The Equestrian Games. Metal Horses. A Black Eyes Metal Dragon. An arena transformed into a battlefield.

**LUCKY:** *watching the mechs, voice tense* Those things are weapons. He's testing them. Evaluating their performance for war.

**NARRATOR:** So Lucky did what Lucky does best. He crashed the party.

**GEM:** *grinning, climbing into her mech* Starlight and Moonbeam. My riding horse robots. Let's show them what real style looks like.

**ASTRA:** *in her own mech, focused* The equestrian skills competition. We can outmaneuver his Metal Dragon. If we stay synchronized.

**NARRATOR:** Lucky's Dragon Bot Clone. Gem and Astra's Starlight and Moonbeam. Against the Architect's entire mechanical army.

**THE ARCHITECT:** *watching from above, amused* Let them play. Every move they make teaches me something new.

**NARRATOR:** Agility. Brute force. Aerial acrobatics. The citizens watched. The world held its breath. And beneath the spectacle, the Metal Sun grew.

**LUCKY:** *in his mech, voice fierce* He's using this as a cover. While we fight in the arena, he's building the Dyson Sphere. We need to stop the Soul Ray.

**ASTRA:** *calculating* If the Metal Sun goes online with antimatter fuel, it could tear a hole in space-time itself. We're not fighting for a trophy. We're fighting for reality.

**NARRATOR:** Superwar Icarus. The Metal Sun. The Equestrian Games. The Architect's shadow. And the team that flew too close to the sun.
