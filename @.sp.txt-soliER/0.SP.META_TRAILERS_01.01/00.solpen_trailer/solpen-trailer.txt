# SOL PEN — MOVIE TRAILER
# ========================
# Length: ~3 minutes
# Voices: Narrator (Ryan), Lucky (Guy), Astra (Dalia), Gem (Ava), Sol (Luke), Loona (SunHi), Kaito (Keita), Black Queen (Ezinne), Agent Thompson (Michelle)

**NARRATOR:** Lucky Shepherd had everything. Billionaire. Casino empire. The world at his feet. But the cost of empire... is always the same.

**LUCKY:** *looking out over his casino floor, voice quiet* I built all of this. Every chip. Every deal. Every smile. And I'm... tired.

**NARRATOR:** Then came the package. No sender. No return address. And a woman from the shadows who knew his name.

**LOONA:** *emerging from shadows, voice cool* I believe that's for you, Lucky. And it will change everything.

**NARRATOR:** Within hours, the CIA was at his door. Fake evidence. Blackmail. A setup so convincing, even he almost believed it.

**AGENT THOMPSON:** *cold, professional* Mr. Shepherd. Your past has caught up with you. Cooperate, or we release this to the public.

**LUCKY:** *stunned* This is a setup.

**AGENT THOMPSON:** Maybe. But it's a convincing one.

**NARRATOR:** Hunted. Framed. With nowhere left to turn, Lucky made a deal with the last person he should have trusted.

**KAITO:** *calm, dangerous* I can get you out. But my price is... substantial. And my loyalty is not guaranteed.

**NARRATOR:** What followed was a battle in the Afghan desert. A double-cross. A rooftop surrounded by enemies. And then... the sky opened.

*SOUND: A deep, otherworldly hum — a ship descending*

**ASTRA:** *grabbing Lucky's arm, breathless* Lucky — LOOK UP.

**NARRATOR:** A silent craft. A flash of light. And Lucky Shepherd was gone.

**GEM:** *on the ship, beaming* Welcome to the SSOL, Lucky. You're not on Earth anymore.

**NARRATOR:** The moon. A base beneath the surface. And a man in a black helmet waiting for him.

**SOL:** *voice distorted through helmet modulator, deep and metallic* Draw the world you want to live in, Lucky. Or someone else will draw it for you.

**LUCKY:** *staring at the SoulPen* What is this?

**SOL:** It's a pen. It writes matter into existence. And you're going to need it. Because the Black Queen is coming. And she's not alone.

**NARRATOR:** The Black Queen. A rebel leader on Mars. An army of clones. And a secret that would shatter everything Lucky thought he knew.

**BLACK QUEEN:** *tall, imposing, voice commanding* You have no idea what you're fighting for, do you? You're just a pawn in Sol's game.

**NARRATOR:** In the Genesis Chamber, Lucky found the truth. Rows of pods. Clones growing. The Black Queen. Princess Megan8R. All of them... manufactured.

**ASTRA:** *horrified* My god... It's a clone factory.

**SOL:** *calm, collected* A necessary precaution. A way to study our enemy. This is not a betrayal, Lucky. It is a necessity.

**NARRATOR:** But the real enemy was already on Mars. And she was waiting.

**PRINCESS MEGAN8R:** *agile, fierce* Is it over? Or is it just beginning?

**NARRATOR:** Lucky Shepherd went to the moon a billionaire. He returns... something else. Something more. With a pen that writes reality. A team that trusts him. And a war he didn't ask for — but is the only one who can win.

**LUCKY:** *facing the Black Queen, voice steady* Maybe I am a pawn. But right now, I'm the one who just took your queen.

**NARRATOR:** Sol Pen. Draw the world you want to live in.
