# TEAR-IT ACCIDENTAL WITCH — MOVIE TRAILER
# ==========================================
# Title: "Tear-It Accidental Witch: The Angler Empyre"
# Length: ~3 minutes
# Voices: Narrator (Ryan), Lucky (Guy), Angler Captain (Roger), Astra (Dalia)

**NARRATOR:** They were the phosphorus fish-police of the Xpanse. Skeletal ships. Emerald energy beams. And a captain who hunted a Data-Kraken that was eating the Golden Ratio.

*SOUND: Deep space bioluminescence, fish-like ship hum*

**NARRATOR:** The Angler Empyre. Law enforcement of the Xpanse. And they needed Lucky's help.

**ANGLER CAPTAIN:** *gruff, fish-like voice* We've got a Data-Kraken eating the Golden Ratio. Without φ, reality unravels. We need someone who understands creation. We need you.

**NARRATOR:** Lucky boarded the Angler ship. Skeletal hull. Bioluminescent corridors. The smell of deep space and deep sea.

**LUCKY:** *walking the bioluminescent corridors, voice tense* This ship is alive. Or it was. And whatever's hunting the Golden Ratio... it's bigger than a kraken.

**NARRATOR:** Astra decoded the Kraken's signature. It wasn't natural. It was manufactured. A weapon. Designed to consume the mathematical foundation of reality.

**ASTRA:** *analyzing the data, voice urgent* The Kraken isn't eating the Golden Ratio randomly. It's targeting specific φ nodes. Someone built this thing to unmake reality at the mathematical level.

**NARRATOR:** The Sprite Swords. Phosphorus-green blades that cut through data itself. Lucky wielded one for the first time.

**LUCKY:** *igniting the Sprite Sword, voice awed* It's alive. The blade is alive. It cuts through data. Through code. Through the fabric of reality itself.

**NARRATOR:** The hunt was epic. Through bioluminescent nebulae. Through asteroid fields shaped like skeletal fish. Through the heart of the Data-Kraken itself.

**ANGLER CAPTAIN:** *in battle, voice fierce* Fire the emerald beams! Cut through its data-tentacles! And Lucky — use the Sprite Sword! Cut the φ node it's feeding on!

**NARRATOR:** They defeated the Kraken. But the revelation was worse. The Kraken was a symptom. Not the disease. Something older was consuming the Golden Ratio. Something that had been hungry since the beginning.

**NARRATOR:** Tear-It Accidental Witch. The Angler Empyre. The Sprite Swords. The Data-Kraken. And the hunt that saved the Golden Ratio.
