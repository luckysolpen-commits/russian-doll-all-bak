# DEATH-KRYO! — MOVIE TRAILER
# =============================
# Title: "Death-Kryo!: The Pirate Assassin"
# Length: ~3 minutes
# Voices: Narrator (Ryan), Lucky (Guy), Dorchester (Orla), Astra (Dalia), Gem (Ava)

**NARRATOR:** The ocean wasn't enough. Lucky Starr looked up. To the asteroids. To the void. And found something waiting in the dark.

*SOUND: Cold space silence, then a sharp metallic ring*

**NARRATOR:** Asteroid mining. The next frontier. The Tear-It Decks were born — cards that could manifest anything. Weapons. Monsters. Entire worlds. Draw. Tear. Reality.

**LUCKY:** *in the void, voice tense* We're not alone out here. Something's moving in the asteroid belt.

**NARRATOR:** Her name was Dorchester. Pirate. Assassin. And the most dangerous woman Lucky had ever met.

**DORCHESTER:** *cool, dangerous, Irish accent* You think your little cards make you powerful, Lucky? I've been tearing reality apart since before you knew what a Tear-It Deck was.

**NARRATOR:** They dueled in the void. Tear-It Cards against Tear-It Cards. Blood-monsters manifesting from torn pages. The cold of space against the heat of creation.

*SOUND: Cards tearing, monsters roaring in vacuum*

**ASTRA:** *watching the duel, breathless* They're manifesting blood-monsters. In the VOID. This shouldn't be possible.

**NARRATOR:** But Dorchester wasn't just a pirate. She was a player. And she had a game that went deeper than the asteroids.

**DORCHESTER:** *smiling, leaning back in her lair* I don't want your money, Lucky. I want your attention. Because there's something coming. Something bigger than asteroids. Bigger than your little underwater city.

**NARRATOR:** She led him back to New Atlantis. To secrets buried beneath the coral towers. To a creature that should not exist.

**GEM:** *staring at something massive in the deep, voice shaking* Lucky. That's... that's a KAIJU. A CARCAZOD KAIJU.

**NARRATOR:** Carc-Zilla. The Beast. An evolved Carcazod the size of a building. And it was heading for the city.

**LUCKY:** *voice urgent* We need an alliance. Now. Dorchester — can you fight?

**DORCHESTER:** *grinning* I don't just fight, Lucky. I play to win.

**NARRATOR:** Sub-Korea joined the fight. An unlikely alliance. Pirate and trillionaire. Scientist and artist. Against a monster from the deep.

**ASTRA:** *calculating* If we deploy the Tear-It Deck at the right frequency, we can manifest a counter-creature. But we need to be precise. One wrong tear, and we make it worse.

**NARRATOR:** But someone was plotting against them. An assassination attempt. A betrayal from within.

**GEM:** *discovering a plot, voice fierce* Lucky — they're planning to kill you. Tonight. In the command center.

**NARRATOR:** The battle for the command center. Cards torn. Monsters unleashed. Allies against traitors. And through it all, Carc-Zilla waited in the deep.

**LUCKY:** *fighting, voice fierce* I didn't build this city to watch it fall. Not to a monster. Not to a traitor. Not to ANYONE.

**DORCHESTER:** *drawing a Tear-It Card, voice deadly* Let's dance, Lucky. You and me. Against the world.

**NARRATOR:** The chase through the deep. The war for the ocean. And an unexpected truce that changed everything.

**NARRATOR:** Death-Kryo! The Pirate Assassin. The Beast. The Void. Draw the card. Tear the page. Face what comes next.
