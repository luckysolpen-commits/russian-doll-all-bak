# CENTROID UNIVERSITY — MOVIE TRAILER
# =====================================
# Title: "Centroid University: The Hogwarts of the Multiverse"
# Length: ~3 minutes
# Voices: Narrator (Ryan), Lucky (Guy), Astra (Dalia), Professor Void (Oliver)

**NARRATOR:** Five Houses. Floating marble islands. Libraries of glowing books. And a school that taught the art of creation itself.

*SOUND: Marble chimes, glowing pages turning, student chatter*

**NARRATOR:** Centroid University. The Hogwarts of the Multiverse. Where ancient beings in eternally young bodies learned to shape reality.

**LUCKY:** *arriving, voice awestruck* Floating marble islands. Libraries of glowing books. Five-colored banners. This isn't a school. It's a cathedral.

**NARRATOR:** Astra was already enrolled. Brilliant. Curious. The first to understand that creation was the highest form of love.

**ASTRA:** *excited, showing Lucky around* This is the Soul Bank. Every student gets Tear-It Cards. You draw what you want, tear the page out, and it becomes REAL. Matter generation. Elemental magic. Pure creation from thought.

**NARRATOR:** Professor Void taught the Empty Flexbox class. The most dangerous course in the multiverse.

**PROFESSOR VOID:** *calm, ancient* The Empty Flexbox is not empty. It is potential. Every container holds infinite possibility. Your job is not to fill it. Your job is to understand what it wants to become.

**NARRATOR:** Lucky crafted the SS VINIX in the hangar. A bird-spaceship. A vessel that could carry them beyond the Centroid. Beyond the known.

**LUCKY:** *in the hangar, working on the SS VINIX* She's beautiful. A bird made of creation. She'll carry us where no map has gone.

**NARRATOR:** But the Centroid had secrets. The Nihililator. An ancient chemical mistake. A void that consumed the rules of reality. And it was growing.

**ASTRA:** *reading in the Celestial Library, voice concerned* The Nihililator. It's been here since before the school. It consumes the rules that make reality possible. And it's getting stronger.

**NARRATOR:** Five Houses. One mission. To teach creation. To fight the void. To prepare the multiverse for what was coming.

**PROFESSOR VOID:** *addressing the students* You are not here to learn. You are here to CREATE. The Nihililator consumes. You must create. It is the only way.

**NARRATOR:** Centroid University. The Hogwarts of the Multiverse. The SS VINIX. The Empty Flexbox. And the education that was really a preparation for war.
