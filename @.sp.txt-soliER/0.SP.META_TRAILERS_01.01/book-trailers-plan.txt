## TRAILER STRUCTURE (each ~3 min, ~12-15 segments):
1. **Narrator opens** — cinematic hook, setting the world
2. **Protagonist intro** — who they are, what they want
3. **Inciting incident** — the event that changes everything
4. **Rising action** — complications, allies, enemies
5. **Midpoint twist** — revelation that changes the game
6. **Climax tease** — the big confrontation
7. **Narrator closes** — "Coming soon" style ending
