# XO FILES — MOVIE TRAILER
# =========================
# Title: "XO Files: Planet X"
# Length: ~3 minutes
# Voices: Narrator (Ryan), Lucky (Guy), Zick (Guy), Zack (Ava), Elder (Oliver)

**NARRATOR:** He crash-landed on a planet where the jungle glowed purple and the slavers were invisible.

*SOUND: Dense alien jungle, shimmering ship hulls*

**NARRATOR:** Planet X. Home of the Extremophiles. Ancient slavers who controlled the stars. And Lucky was their newest prisoner.

**LUCKY:** *crashing through the canopy, voice urgent* We're down. The ship's wrecked. But we're alive. Zick, Zack — status report.

**NARRATOR:** Zick and Zack. The Fuzzball brothers. Eight-colored sparks from the Big Bang. And Lucky's only allies on this godforsaken rock.

**ZICK:** *excited, bouncing* The jungle is AMAZING! Look at these plants! They're GLOWING! And the air is breathable! Mostly!

**ZACK:** *more cautious* Mostly isn't good enough, Zick. We need shelter. And we need to find the Afink Village before the Extremophiles find us.

**NARRATOR:** The Extremophiles. Invisible. Semi-transparent ships. Shimmering hulls that caught the purple light. They had been slaving for millennia. And they weren't about to stop.

**LUCKY:** *rebuilding his Unoman Suit into the Exo Suit, focused* If I can retrofit the suit with local materials, I can match their tech. But I need time. And I need the Afink elders to share their map data.

**NARRATOR:** The Afink Village. Ancient. Wise. Guardians of the oldest maps in the galaxy.

**ELDER:** *ancient, measured* You trade Nothing Ink for our maps, Lucky Starr. But know this: the maps show more than stars. They show the truth. And the truth is heavier than any currency.

**NARRATOR:** Lucky traded. He built. He negotiated. And he discovered something the Extremophiles had been hiding for millennia.

**LUCKY:** *examining the ancient maps, voice rising* These aren't just star charts. They're a blueprint. For the Ark. The ship that carries the seeds of everything.

**NARRATOR:** The XO Files. Planet X. The Exo Suit. The Afink maps. And the truth that was heavier than any currency.
