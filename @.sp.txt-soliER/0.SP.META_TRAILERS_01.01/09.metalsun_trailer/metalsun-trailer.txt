# METAL SUN — MOVIE TRAILER
# ==========================
# Title: "Metal Sun: The Dyson Sphere"
# Length: ~3 minutes
# Voices: Narrator (Ryan), Sol (Luke), Lucky (Guy), Astra (Dalia)

**NARRATOR:** He built a sun. Not a star. A MACHINE. A Dyson Sphere of gold and quantum-cooled white. And inside it, an empire of clones.

*SOUND: Massive solar hum, golden machinery*

**NARRATOR:** The Metal Sun. The Architect's masterpiece. A shell around the sun itself. Powered by antimatter. Guarded by legions of world-police bots.

**SOL:** *as Emperor, voice distorted through helmet* I am Supreme Emperor Sol by Divine Right. The Metal Sun is not a weapon. It is evolution. And evolution does not ask permission.

**NARRATOR:** Lucky was driven underground. Through magma. Through despair. Through months of running from the bots that crushed every resistance.

**LUCKY:** *in the tunnels, voice ragged* He built a sun to rule the world. And I'm hiding in a sewer. But I'm not done. Not yet.

**NARRATOR:** Astra joined the Architect on the Ikarus. She saw the truth. The clones. The executions. The Metal Sun's dark heart.

**ASTRA:** *watching the executions, horrified* He's killing his own clones. His own sons. Because they showed signs of anti-φ resonance. He chose cosmic harmony over blood. It broke him. But it kept the sun pure.

**NARRATOR:** The clones. Thousands of them. Each one a copy. Each one disposable. And each one a testament to the Architect's madness.

**SOL:** *cold, collected* They showed early signs of anti-φ resonance. Sin. I couldn't let it into the Metal Sun. I chose cosmic harmony over blood.

**NARRATOR:** But Lucky found the wormhole. Worm Hole X. The escape route. The path to freedom.

**LUCKY:** *at the wormhole, looking back at the Metal Sun* I'm leaving. But I'm coming back. And when I do, your sun goes dark.

**NARRATOR:** The Metal Sun. The Dyson Sphere. The Emperor who chose harmony over blood. And the man who crawled through the earth's core to fight him.
