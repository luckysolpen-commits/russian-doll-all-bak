# FOOTRACE.FU — MOVIE TRAILER
# =============================
# Title: "Footrace.FU: The Land of Time"
# Length: ~3 minutes
# Voices: Narrator (Ryan), Lucky (Guy), Horli (Denise), CHARA (Vivienne), IQabella (Ana)

**NARRATOR:** On the Land of Time, every second was a coin. Every minute was a fortune. And the Tiem Train was the only way out.

*SOUND: Clockwork gears, golden sand, train whistle*

**NARRATOR:** Lucky arrived in the Land of Time. Asian-steampunk. Floating pagodas. Brass clockwork gears. Trails of golden sand. And an Emperor who owned every second.

**LUCKY:** *arriving, voice tense* Time is currency here. Literally. Every tick is a coin. Every tock is a heartbeat. And we're broke.

**NARRATOR:** But Lucky learned Time Fu. The martial art of temporal manipulation. Acceleration. Deceleration. Phasing through moments.

**LUCKY:** *training, voice focused* I can feel the flow. The rhythm. Time isn't a river — it's a dance. And I'm learning the steps.

**NARRATOR:** With him: Horli. Trap rapper. Time Twin. The one who turned temporal combat into a beat.

**HORLI:** *dropping a clockwork beat* Time Fu? More like TIME FLOW. I'm rapping in 1.618 time, baby. The Golden Ratio of bars. *grinning* The Emperor's guards can't catch what they can't predict.

**NARRATOR:** And CHARA. Paranoid. Tactical. Already mapping the political structure of the Time Emperor's court.

**CHARA:** *scanning with datapad* The Emperor controls time flow through the Tiem Train. He accelerates it for his enemies, decelerates it for his allies. It's the ultimate weapon. And we need to hijack it.

**NARRATOR:** IQabella. The heart. The tea-bringer. The one who kept them human in a world of clockwork.

**IQABELLA:** *pouring tea, gentle* 175 degrees. For focus. And for courage. We're going to steal a train that controls time. We're going to need both.

**NARRATOR:** The heist was impossible. The Tiem Train was guarded by the Emperor's elite. But Lucky had something they didn't expect.

**LUCKY:** *on the train, voice fierce* You own the seconds. But I own the spaces BETWEEN them.

**NARRATOR:** Footrace.FU. The Land of Time. The Tiem Train. The Time Twin technique. And the race that was never about speed — it was about rhythm.
