# GEMINII PART 1 — MOVIE TRAILER
# ================================
# Title: "Gem&I: The Martian Gambit"
# Length: ~3 minutes
# Voices: Narrator (Ryan), Lucky (Guy), Astra (Dalia), Gem (Ava), The Architect (Luke), Elara (Maisie)

**NARRATOR:** The world ended. Not with a bang. With a plague. Monkey-cockroach hybrids. Clowns. And three people who refused to die.

*SOUND: Wind howling through ruins, distant screeching*

**NARRATOR:** Lucky. Former emergency worker. Now the only thing standing between his friends and extinction.

**LUCKY:** *panting, gripping a shotgun* Stay close. Watch the shadows. They're everywhere.

**NARRATOR:** Astra. Scientist. The only mind sharp enough to understand what killed the world.

**ASTRA:** *analytical, focused* The mutation pattern is accelerating. If we don't find a cure soon, there won't be anything left to save.

**NARRATOR:** And Gem. Artist. Pink hair. Crowbar. The heart that keeps them human.

**GEM:** *bright despite the darkness* Hey. We're still here. That counts for something. Right?

**NARRATOR:** They scavenged the ruins. Built an armored truck from scrap. Found a fortified neighborhood. Thought they were safe.

*SOUND: Night ambush — clowns laughing, metal clashing*

**GEM:** *swinging her crowbar* CLOWNS. Why did it have to be CLOWNS?!

**NARRATOR:** But the clowns were just the beginning. The Carcazoid attacks were too coordinated. Too precise. Someone was controlling them.

**ASTRA:** *reading cryptic messages on a wall* These aren't random. Someone left a trail. Messages. Logs. A name. *pauses* "The Architect."

**LUCKY:** The Architect. The man who built the apocalypse.

**NARRATOR:** The trail led to the largest city in the country. To a courthouse — pristine, untouched, patrolled by an army of identical clone guards.

*SOUND: Heavy boots marching in unison*

**NARRATOR:** Gem talked them past the guards. Her gift. Her curse.

**GEM:** *charming, confident* We're not here to fight. We're here to talk. And trust me — you want to hear what we have to say.

**NARRATOR:** Inside, they met him. The Architect. The man behind the curtain.

**THE ARCHITECT:** *calm, collected, terrifying* I orchestrated the entire apocalypse. The Carcazoids. The clowns. All of it. To seize control of the world. And to sell the cure.

**ASTRA:** *horrified* You created them. The plague. The monsters. You did this. On purpose.

**THE ARCHITECT:** Humanity was broken. I simply... accelerated the process. And now I offer the solution. For a price.

**NARRATOR:** Enraged, they fought. Through mutant clowns. Through Carcazoids. Through the Architect's entire army.

**LUCKY:** *fighting, voice fierce* We didn't come this far to die in your courthouse.

**NARRATOR:** They defeated him. Forced him to surrender the Mutagen — the substance that could wipe out the Carcazoids forever. But there was a catch.

**ASTRA:** *reading data, voice urgent* The Mutagen can only be processed in advanced labs. Labs that don't exist on Earth. *looks up* They're on Mars.

**NARRATOR:** Earth was too corrupted. Too infested. Too far gone. Their only hope was the Architect's private spacecraft.

**GEM:** *looking back at the ruined Earth, voice steady* We're leaving. And we're not coming back until we fix this.

**NARRATOR:** Three survivors. One ship. And a red planet where the daughters of the Black Queen hold the key to the future.

**ELARA:** *elegant, cold, over a transmission* Welcome to Olympus Port. My sister and I have been expecting you. But I'm afraid... we don't accept refugees.

**LUCKY:** *on the ship, looking at Mars growing larger in the viewport* We're not refugees. We're the cure.

**NARRATOR:** Gem&I. The Martian Gambit. Earth was just the beginning.
