# RED ROCK & ROLL — MOVIE TRAILER
# =================================
# Title: "Red Rock & Roll: The Trillionaire of the Depths"
# Length: ~3 minutes
# Voices: Narrator (Ryan), Lucky (Guy), Astra (Dalia), Gem (Ava), Zara (Ezinne), Dr. Thorne (Oliver), UN Director (Maisie)

**NARRATOR:** The Carcazods destroyed the surface. But they couldn't swim. So humanity went underwater. And one man built a city from coral.

*SOUND: Deep ocean hum, bioluminescent shimmer*

**NARRATOR:** Lucky Starr. Once a casino mogul. Now the architect of Atlantis Prime. A city of bioluminescent coral and iridescent alloys, a hundred meters beneath the Atlantic.

**LUCKY:** *in his coral office, voice smooth, confident* Innovation is the cornerstone of survival, Director. Especially when facing an existential threat like the Carcazods.

**NARRATOR:** He charmed the UN. Secured billions in grants. Built hydroponic farms that glowed in the deep. He became the Trillionaire of the Depths.

**UN DIRECTOR:** *skeptical* Mr. Starr, your proposal for the Abyssal Bloom initiative is... ambitious. The projected yields are quite high, even for your... innovative methods.

**LUCKY:** *smiling* Innovation is the cornerstone of survival.

**NARRATOR:** But beneath the polished coral and the bespoke suits, the silence was bought with blood, credits, and lies.

**ASTRA:** *in the lab, voice urgent* Lucky, I'm reading something in the water samples. The Carcazods. They're... changing.

**NARRATOR:** Astra discovered the truth. Zara's mutagen — designed to control populations — had done something far worse.

**ZARA:** *cold, calculating* I didn't create a weapon. I created a catalyst. And evolution does the rest.

**NARRATOR:** The Carcazods were evolving. Developing gills. Learning to swim. The one thing keeping Atlantis Prime safe... was disappearing.

**GEM:** *looking out the panoramic window, voice shaking* Lucky. There's something in the water. Something BIG.

**NARRATOR:** Dr. Thorne confirmed it. The bio-marker was unmistakable. An evolved strain. Older. More dangerous. And it was heading straight for the city.

**DR. THORNE:** *hoarse, exhausted* Zara's mutagen inadvertently accelerated the Carcazods' own evolution. She didn't create a weapon for herself. She created a catalyst for something far older. Far more dangerous.

**NARRATOR:** The UN Council Chambers — once a stage for petty squabbles — was now a scene of terrifying unity. The holographic display projected the monstrous image of the evolved Carcazod. Its eyes burning with unnatural intelligence.

**UN DIRECTOR:** *alarmed* How long do we have?

**ASTRA:** *checking calculations* Hours. Maybe less.

**NARRATOR:** Lucky had built the city. Now he had to defend it. With hydro-filters against teeth. With coral walls against claws. With everything he had against an enemy that was learning to swim.

**LUCKY:** *standing, voice fierce* We didn't come this far to drown. Activate the defenses. Seal the outer rings. And get me everything we have on Zara's mutagen.

**ZARA:** *smiling, over a transmission* You built a beautiful city, Lucky. But beauty doesn't stop evolution. Nothing does.

**NARRATOR:** Atlantis Prime. A miracle of human ingenuity. About to face the one thing it was never designed to fight.

**GEM:** *grabbing her crowbar, voice steady* We're not drowning today. Not on my watch.

**NARRATOR:** Red Rock & Roll. The Trillionaire of the Depths. The city that glowed in the dark. And the monsters that learned to swim.
