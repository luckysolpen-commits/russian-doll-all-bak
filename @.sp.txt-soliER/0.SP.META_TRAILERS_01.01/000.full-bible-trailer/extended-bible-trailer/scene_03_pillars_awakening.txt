# META TRAILER — SCENE 03
# ========================
# Title: "The Twenty Pillars — Part One: The Awakening"
# Voices: Mr. Know-It-All (Roger), Mother Tmojizu (Jenny), CHARA (Aria), Sol-Supreme (Guy), IQabella (Ana), Horli (Roger)

**MR. KNOW-IT-ALL:** Twenty Pillars hold up the sky. Each one a world. Each one a test. The journey begins with a detective in the rain and ends with a Metal Sun. Seven Pillars. From the streets of a digital city to the throne of an emperor who is also a ghost.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** *rolling the glowing d20* Pillar Zero. The Book of Charanon. Roll for perception.

**CHARA:** *rolling* 19. Plus 3 is 22. What do I see?

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Rain-slicked ASCII streets. Flickering neon screens. You are Agent Chara, and you are investigating a mysterious "Hacker" — a man called Anon. You find something at a crime scene. A pen. Sleek, dark, covered in Golden Ratio spirals. You touch it — and the file system of your world begins to change. You see the Mirror for the first time.

**CHARA:** *voice tense* The state.txt file. It's showing me... everything. Every Piece. Every container. The reality behind reality. *pauses* This pen. It's not a weapon. It's a key.

**MR. KNOW-IT-ALL:** Pillar Zero. The detective finds the key. And the key opens everything.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Pillar One. The Book of Sol Pen. Lucky Shepherd — billionaire mogul, mixed-race, colored mohawk, dressed in high-end Ph-F streetwear with heavy gold ϕ-chains — is whisked to the Hollow Moon. And there, he meets him. Sol. The future masked version of himself.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *voice dropping* That's me. The Architect. Full exo-armor. Matte black and gold. Helmet with the ϕ-visor. Voice distorted through heavy modulation. I'm his future. And I'm handing him the first Soul Pen.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Sol speaks: "Draw the world you want to live in, or someone else will draw it for you."

**IQABELLA:** *softly* That's... beautiful. And terrifying. He's giving himself the tool to change everything.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Pillar Two. Gem and I. Post-apocalypse. Lucky, Astra, and Gem battle the Carcazods — monkey-cockroach hybrids with chitinous exo-skeletons and glowing red eyes. Gem modifies an old school bus into a Ph-F armored tank. Astra uses the Soul Pen to 3D-print bug spray from Martian mutagens.

**HORLI:** Monkey-cockroaches. Of course. Because the apocalypse needed to be extra.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Pillar Three. Red Rock and Roll. Underwater biotech. Lucky builds New Atlantis to escape the non-swimming Carcazods. He strikes a deal with the UN, using government grants to build bioluminescent hydroponic farms. He becomes the Trillionaire of the Depths.

**IQABELLA:** *eyes wide* An underwater city. With neon coral and bubbles rendered as ASCII stars. It sounds... magical.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Pillar Four. Death and All Her Friends. Asteroid mining. The Tear-It Decks are born. Lucky meets Dorchester — the pirate assassin. They duel with Tear-It Cards, manifesting blood-monsters in the void.

**CHARA:** *tactical mind engaged* Tear-It Cards. You draw what you want, tear the page out, and it becomes real. Matter generation. Elemental magic. Pure creation from thought. *pauses* That's the most powerful weapon system I've ever heard of.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Pillar Five. Skullmoon. Loona Twilight's sacrifice. The moon is haunted. The Architect summons the team to his African diamond mine. He is the mirror of Lucky's greed.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *quiet* The Architect. That's me. Or will be. The mirror of Lucky's greed. I became what I fought against.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Pillar Six. Superwar Ikkarus. The Metal Sun rises. Emperor Sol builds a Dyson Sphere. Lucky refuses the Mark of the Beast — a digital chip. He is cast out. Astra betrays him to join Sol on the Ikarus.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *standing up, voice heavy* I executed my sons. Not out of hate. Out of preservation. They showed early signs of anti-φ resonance. Sin. I couldn't let it into the Metal Sun. I chose cosmic harmony over blood. It broke me. But it kept the sun pure.

**CHARA:** *looking at Sol, voice softer than usual* You killed your own children.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *sitting back down* I saved the Metal Sun. And I lost myself in the process.

**HORLI:** *quiet* That's... heavy. Like, really heavy.

**MR. KNOW-IT-ALL:** Seven Pillars. From a detective's rain-slicked street to the throne of an emperor who murdered his sons to save a sun. Lucky has been given the Soul Pen, built an underwater city, dueled with blood-monsters, and been betrayed by everyone he loves. And the worst is yet to come.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** *rolling the d20 again* The awakening is complete. The descent begins next. Through hell, through glitch, through time itself.
