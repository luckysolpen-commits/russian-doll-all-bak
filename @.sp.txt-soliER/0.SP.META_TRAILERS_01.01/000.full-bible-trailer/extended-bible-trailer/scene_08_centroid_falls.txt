# META TRAILER — SCENE 08
# ========================
# Title: "The Nihililator & The Centroid Falls"
# Voices: Mr. Know-It-All (Roger), Mother Tmojizu (Jenny), CHARA (Aria), Sol-Supreme (Guy), IQabella (Ana), Horli (Roger), Rahweh (Jenny)

**MR. KNOW-IT-ALL:** They made it. They don't remember making it. But it remembers them. And it grows at Golden Ratio speed — 1.618 times larger every second. The Nihililator. The Unmaking. The void where no rules apply.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** *the game board darkens — a rift appears at the edge of the Golden Ratio spiral* Flashback. FFU Session 13. The Infinitiator experiment. You combined all eight mutant labpet genes — glow, speed, telepathy, fire, size-shifting, invisibility, time-sensing, compound synthesis — plus all six element types, plus CRISPR-enhanced temporal DNA, plus quantum entangled consciousness.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *voice hollow* We made it. Session 13. The Labpet Empire. We combined everything. And it became nihilili. It ate the labpets' souls. It opened a wormhole using exotic matter and negative energy density. We stopped it — but it escaped into another dimension.

**HORLI:** *quiet for once* Professor Squeaks died because of that thing. His tiny lab coat — it's still in the warehouse. Empty. We promised we'd save him. *voice cracks* And now it's back.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Professor Pylorus's distress signal echoes from the Centroid: "If anyone can hear this — please. I am Professor Pylorus of the Centroid: School of the Multiverse. The Nihililator is returning. It's consuming the chemical foundations of our reality. We need creators who understand chemistry at the MOLECULAR level. You four — you've mastered both Time Fu AND chemical creation. You're the only ones who can help."

**CHARA:** *tactical mind racing* A distress signal from another dimension. Addressed specifically to US. Claiming that the Nihililator is consuming chemical foundations. *pauses* This has trap written all over it. But... the signal IS authentic. And if the Nihililator is real — if it's the same entity we created in Session 13 — then we owe it to Professor Squeaks. To all the labpets. We have to face what we made.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** The 4-party enters the flexbox. The dimensional container governed by CSS layout principles. Reality inside is structured by flex-direction, justify-content, align-items, flex-wrap.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *falling to his knees, overwhelmed* The main axis... it's a river of pure chemical potential. Flowing in one direction, carrying molecules that rearrange themselves according to flex-direction: row. The cross axis — perpendicular, creating resonance that generates NEW elements through justify-content: space-between. This is HYPER-ADVANCED CHEMISTRY. Molecular flex-wrapping. Proteins folding in real-time. We could restructure matter itself.

**HORLI:** *staring at the sentient molecules* The molecules are literally DANCING. They're rearranging themselves in patterns. It's like... choreography. Molecular choreography. *starts rapping quietly* Flex-direction, justify-content, align-items too, we're learning chemistry that we never knew we could do...

**MR. KNOW-IT-ALL:** But behind them, at the edge of the flexbox, something is appearing. A rift. A tear where no properties apply. No flex-direction. No justify-content. No align-items. No gap. No wrap. Nothing. Just... absence.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** The Nihililator. A void where flexbox rules don't exist. No chemistry can function there. No bonds can form. No reactions can occur. It's the UNMAKING of creation itself. And it's growing — exponentially. The rift expands by 0.618% every second. The Golden Ratio. Used against them.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *checking instruments, face pale* It's using the Golden Ratio against us. Every second, it's 1.618 times larger than the previous second. If we don't learn the chemistry that can fight it, the Nihililator will consume this flexbox, then the Centroid, then every dimension connected to it. Including the Land of Time. Including everything we've ever known.

**MR. KNOW-IT-ALL:** But before they can fight, before they can learn, before they can save anything — a golden star pyramid ship descends on the Centroid. The Necro-Star. Futuristic, high-tech Egyptian aesthetic. Gleaming pyramid structure. Hieroglyphic energy fields. And golden zombie clone armies pouring out of its hull like a river of molten metal.

**RAHWEH:** *stepping out of the Necro-Star, robes woven from stardust and death, eyes glowing* Well, well, well. Look at this place. Crystal bridges. Golden Ratio towers. Fuzzpet asteroids. *smirks* Cute. Real cute. *broadcasting to the entire Centroid* Listen up, crystal-bridge nerds and fuzzpet riders! I'm Rahweh the Necromantic, God of Life and Death, and I'm taking over this place. By submission or force.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *face going pale, temporal suit flickering* No. It can't be. I killed him. I killed him.

**HORLI:** Sol? Who's that? Who is he?

**SOL-SUPREME:** *barely audible* Rahweh. The Necromantic. My... son. From the Metal Sun. From Ikarus. I tried to kill him. I failed. And now he's here.

**RAHWEH:** *walking closer, golden zombie clones parting behind him* Nice suit, unk. What's the matter? Can't hold yourself together anymore? *laughs* Death is a suggestion, old man. I chose to ignore it. You really thought you could off me? Cute.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Sol attacked. He used everything — SoulPen, Time Fu, flexbox manipulation, temporal displacement. He wrote "fire" — a flame erupted. He wrote "shield" — a molecular barrier formed. He used advanced Time Fu techniques. He pushed hard, fast, relentless.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *fighting, voice strained* I won't let you take the Centroid. I won't let you destroy what we built.

**RAHWEH:** *parrying every attack, golden zombies absorbing the blows* Is that all you got, old man? I've been dead for millennia. I've had a lot of time to practice. *swings his sprite sword — it cuts through Sol's temporal shield* You really thought you could kill me? I'm the God of Life and Death. Death is my playground.

**MR. KNOW-IT-ALL:** Sol fought with everything he had. But Rahweh's golden zombie clones overwhelmed the Centroid's defenses. The fuzzpet asteroids fled, their breathing heavy, their surfaces tense. The dust pets scattered, chirping in terrified frequencies. The crystal bridges shattered under the weight of the golden zombie army. The Centroid was falling.

**PROFESSOR PYLORUS:** *appearing beside Sol, silver hair catching the firelight* Sol. You can't save the Centroid. But you can save yourself. Go back in time. Save Lucky. Fix the loop. The Centroid will fall, but the timeline can still be saved.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *breathing hard, temporal suit flickering* I can't leave. I can't let him win.

**PROFESSOR PYLORUS:** He's already won. The Centroid is gone. But Lucky, Astra, and CHARA can still be saved. The loop can still be fixed. You don't have much time. Literally. Go.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Sol, Horli, and IQabella launch the SSOL. The ship's engines roar with temporal energy. The ship shoots into the temporal stream, leaving the burning Centroid behind. Behind them, Rahweh's voice echoes across the ruins.

**RAHWEH:** *broadcasting* Run, old man! Run! But I'll find you. I always find you. Death is a suggestion, unk. And I'm the one who suggests it.

**MR. KNOW-IT-ALL:** The school is gone. The fuzzpet asteroids flee. The dust pets scatter. The crystal bridges shatter. And the loop... continues. Sol flickers. His temporal suit holds him together — barely. But he's going back. Back in time. To save Lucky. To fix the loop. To become the villain in his own story.

**IQABELLA:** *on the SSOL, watching the Centroid burn* We made the Nihililator. We lost the Centroid. And now we're running through time. *wiping tears, voice steady* But we're still here. We're still fighting. We're still... us.
