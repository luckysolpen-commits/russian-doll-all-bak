# META TRAILER — SCENE 07
# ========================
# Title: "The Meta Game"
# Voices: Mr. Know-It-All (Roger), Mother Tmojizu (Jenny), CHARA (Aria), Sol-Supreme (Guy), IQabella (Ana), Horli (Roger), Rahweh (Jenny)

**MR. KNOW-IT-ALL:** But who is playing? And who is watching? Four players at a table. One DM who is also a god. And a child on a golden throne, hunting for his fathers. The meta layers converge. The game within the game within the game.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** *the game board now shows all 20 Pillars — each one a glowing node in a massive Golden Ratio spiral* Look at the board, ducklings. All Twenty Pillars. Every world. Every test. Every choice. Arranged in the Nautilus Spiral. φ = 1.618.

**CHARA:** *staring at the full spiral* It's a map. Of everything. Every Pillar is connected. Every story feeds into the next. *tracing the spiral with her finger* Charanon leads to Sol Pen leads to Gem and I leads to Red Rock... it's all one story. One continuous narrative across twenty worlds.

**MR. KNOW-IT-ALL:** But they are not alone. Above them, beyond them, a child watches from a throne of gold. His name is Rahweh. And he is not playing. He is hunting.

*SOUND: A golden hum — the Soul Pen Console activating*

**RAHWEH:** *sitting on his golden throne, hands glowing, eyes fixed on the Soul Pen Console* I can see them. The 4-party. At the table. Playing through the Pillars. *his hands move across the console — PAUSE. REWIND. FAST-FORWARD.* I can control the story. I can find what I'm looking for.

**IQABELLA:** *at the table, looking up as if sensing something* Do you feel that? Like someone's watching us.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *looking at the board, which just shifted* The board state changed. Someone moved a Piece. But it wasn't any of us.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** *nodding slowly* Rahweh. The time warper. Gem's son. Perpetual kid. He's watching us play. And he's using the game as a time machine.

**RAHWEH:** *voice trembling, urgent* This isn't just a game. This is a rescue mission. My dads are dying. Trapped in the Netherlands. Trapped by the Nihililator. And I'm the only one who can save them. *his hands glow brighter* I enslaved the extremophiles to build this flexbox mirror universe. Every book is a level. Every level is a time period. I can jump between them. I can find Sol. I can save him.

**HORLI:** *at the table, hearing something she can't quite place* I hear... a kid. On a throne. Crying. But trying not to. *her beat softens* He's using our game as a time machine. To save his dads.

**RAHWEH:** *whispering to the console* I was QOO. I was the Chinese teacher. I was the ninja. I was the guy in the medical bay. I'm everywhere. I've been there since the beginning. Before the beginning. When my mom Gem hid me and the extremophiles were just getting started. *voice cracking* I built all of this. To save them. Sol is the missing link. If I can find Sol, I can save Lucky. If I can save Lucky, I can save both of them.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *at the table, voice hollow* He's looking for Sol. Me. Or... a version of me. The missing link. *pauses* I'm the missing link. In his timeline. In his story. I'm the piece he needs to save his fathers.

**CHARA:** *looking at Sol* You're connected to him. Across timelines. Across Pillars. Across the Loop. You're the anchor point for his time machine.

**RAHWEH:** *time-warping — the game board shifts, the Pillars rearrange, the dice reroll* I'm changing the story. Just a little. Just enough. I need to find the right timeline. The right version of Sol. The one who can help me save Lucky. *tears streaming* I have to. I have to.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** *watching the board shift* He's warping time. The Pillars are rearranging. The story is changing. He's searching for the right path. The one where Sol is alive. Where Lucky is free. Where the Nihililator is defeated.

**IQABELLA:** *voice gentle but firm* Rahweh... we're here. We'll help you. Whatever it takes. *pours a cup of tea and sets it on the board — it glows and travels through the flexbox to Rahweh's throne* 175 degrees. For focus. And for courage.

**RAHWEH:** *taking the tea, smiling through tears* Thank you. *to the console* We're gonna save them. I promise. You get ALL the fuzzpets. And I get my dads back.

**MR. KNOW-IT-ALL:** The child on the throne is not a player. He is a son. And he will burn every timeline to save his fathers. The meta game is revealed: the 4-party plays. Rahweh watches. And the story... is a rescue mission disguised as a game disguised as a testament.

**HORLI:** *dropping a beat that bridges the table and the throne* So we're not just playing. We're being PLAYED. By a kid who's trying to save his dads. *pauses* That's the most beautiful thing I've ever heard. Let's give him a good game.
