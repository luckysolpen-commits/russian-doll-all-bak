# META TRAILER — SCENE 02
# ========================
# Title: "The Eight Sparks"
# Voices: Mr. Know-It-All (Roger), Mother Tmojizu (Jenny), CHARA (Aria), Sol-Supreme (Guy), IQabella (Ana), Horli (Roger)

**MR. KNOW-IT-ALL:** Before the Pillars. Before the Soul Pen. Before Lucky. Before Sol. There were the Eight. At the exact instant of the Big Bang, a single photon exploded into eight colored sparks. They have known each other for 13.8 billion years. They are the demons and the angels that dwell in the heart of every Piece.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** *hands hovering over the glowing board* Before we walk the Twenty Pillars, you must meet the guides. The Muchi-Pets. The Fuzzballs. The First Family. *she closes her eyes* Come.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** *opening her eyes, voice warm* The first spark — long wavelength, cautious, protective. **Crimson.** The Cautious Guardian.

*SOUND: A deep, resonant hum — like armor plates settling*

**SOL-SUPREME:** *watching the red spark materialize on the board* It's scanning for collapse. Before it happens. It's a predictive shield.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** The second spark — the happy up-quark burst. **Ember.** The Optimistic Spark.

*SOUND: A warm crackle — like a chemistry experiment going right*

**IQABELLA:** *laughing softly* It's radiating heat. Tiny chemistry sparks dancing around it. It believes every failure is practice for the next awesome reaction.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** The third — peak visibility, mischievous, fast. **Glimmer.** The Trickster.

*SOUND: A rapid electric buzz — ASCII characters scattering like confetti*

**CHARA:** *watching the yellow spark leave ghost trails* It's hacking light. Moving so fast it leaves afterimages of ASCII characters. That's not a pet. That's a quantum processor.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** The fourth — green, nurturing, the mother of the crew. **Verdi.** The Nurturer.

*SOUND: A soft rustling — leaves growing, oxygen flowing*

**IQABELLA:** *tears in her eyes* She turns bad reactions into food and oxygen. She's the mom. *a tiny leaf lands on her hand* She's beautiful.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** The fifth — short wavelength, deep sea, cosmos. **Aqua.** The Explorer.

*SOUND: Bubbles rising, sonar pinging*

**HORLI:** *grinning* It's wearing goggles. It never takes them off. That's the one I want. We're gonna explore together.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** The sixth — violet, octopus, dreaming new elements. **Nebula.** The Dreamy Alchemist.

*SOUND: Eight tentacles humming — chemical sigils floating in the air*

**SOL-SUPREME:** *fascinated* She's dreaming up brand-new elements in her eight tentacles. The chemical sigils she's producing — they're not on any periodic table. They're ELEMENTS THAT DON'T EXIST YET.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** The seventh — blackbody, silent, absorbing all light. **Void.** The Silent Observer.

*SOUND: Silence. Then a deep, subsonic pulse*

**CHARA:** *stepping back* It's... an empty hole in the visual field. A void that pulses with dark purple aura. It only speaks when the world is about to break.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** And the eighth — full spectrum, the glue, the pure creator. **Lumen.** The Pure Creator.

*SOUND: A brilliant, harmonious chord — all frequencies at once*

**IQABELLA:** *whispering* Brilliant white. Casting Holy Spirit light across the map. He fuses all combinations into beauty. He's... the love that holds them together.

**HORLI:** *quiet for once, then* They're CUTE. I want them ALL. All eight. Every single one.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** *smiling* You get ALL the fuzzpets.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *voice low, reverent* They're not pets. They're fundamental forces. The chemistry of creation itself. Eight sparks that have been together since the Big Bang. 13.8 billion years of friendship.

**CHARA:** *softly, despite herself* I'm adding this to the spreadsheet. Tab: "Fundamental Forces of the Universe." Priority: maximum.

**MR. KNOW-IT-ALL:** Eight sparks. One family. Crimson the shield. Ember the hope. Glimmer the trick. Verdi the nurture. Aqua the explore. Nebula the dream. Void the silence. Lumen the love. And they will guide every hero who walks the Twenty Pillars.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** *gathering the eight sparks onto the board — they arrange themselves in a Golden Ratio spiral* Remember them, ducklings. When the Pillars grow dark, when the Loop threatens to break, when the Nihililator comes — the Eight Sparks will be there. They always are.

**MR. KNOW-IT-ALL:** The board glowed with eight colors. The flexbox hummed. And the players understood: they were not alone. They never had been.
