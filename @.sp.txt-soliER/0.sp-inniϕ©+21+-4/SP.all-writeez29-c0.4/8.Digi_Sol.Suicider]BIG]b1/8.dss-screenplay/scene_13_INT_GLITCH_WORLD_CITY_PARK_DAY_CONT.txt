INT. GLITCH WORLD - CITY PARK - DAY (CONT.)

As Lucky executes the `/glitch_transfer` command, a shimmering, green-blue energy, like liquid light, flows from the ancient fountain. It arcs through the air, connecting with the young woman's flickering form. Her violent digital static intensifies for a moment, then begins to stabilize. The garbled chirps and whirs from her mouth slowly coalesce into coherent, though still strained, gasps.

Her body, which was on the verge of dissolving into pixels, solidifies. The wild, fearful look in her eyes softens, replaced by a dawning comprehension. She takes a deep, shuddering breath, as if breathing for the first time in a long while.

YOUNG WOMAN
(Voice hoarse, weak)
What... what happened? I was... I was falling...

Anya, who had been watching with bated breath, rushes to her side, helping her sit up. The young woman's body still shimmers faintly, but the uncontrolled glitches are gone.

ANYA
(To Lucky, a mix of awe and relief)
You did it, Lucky! You stabilized her! I've never seen a direct energy transfer like that before. It's... incredible.

Lucky feels a surge of exhaustion, but also a profound sense of accomplishment. His own avatar flickers briefly, a minor visual distortion, as if the energy transfer took a toll.

YOUNG WOMAN
(Looking at Lucky, then Anya, her eyes clearing)
They were... they were trying to overwrite me. To make me forget. But then... a light. A connection. You... you pulled me back.

She tries to stand, but her legs are weak. Anya supports her. Lucky notices a faint, almost imperceptible GLOW around the young woman's hands. It's not the chaotic static from before, but a controlled, internal luminescence.

LUCKY
(Pointing)
Your hands... they're glowing.

The young woman looks at her hands, surprised. She flexes her fingers, and the glow intensifies. A small, perfectly formed, miniature holographic BUTTERFLY, made of pure light, flits from her palm, dances in the air for a moment, then vanishes.

YOUNG WOMAN
(A whisper of wonder)
I... I can create. I can manifest.

ANYA
(Her eyes wide)
She's a Manifestor! A rare type of Glitch power! They can pull data directly from the simulation's core and render it into physical form! The Metal Mafia tries to suppress them the most, because they can literally reshape reality within the Glitch World!

The young woman, still weak, looks at them, a new determination in her eyes.

YOUNG WOMAN
My name is Elara. And I remember. I remember everything. They tried to make me forget the coordinates. The location of the primary control node. The one that feeds the entire Glitch World.

Lucky and Anya exchange a look. This is huge. This is the key. Kael's disruption had created a window, and Lucky's quick thinking had saved a Manifestor, someone who could potentially turn the tide.

ELARA
(Cont., her voice gaining strength)
It's not in the city. It's hidden. Deep beneath the oldest part of the simulation. A place they call... the Core.

Suddenly, the idyllic park begins to distort. The trees stretch, the sky flickers, and the laughter of the children becomes a distorted, repeating loop. The Metal Mafia is reacting. They're trying to reassert control, to suppress the new anomaly.

ANYA
(Urgent)
They're trying to re-engage the brainwashing protocols! The system is fighting back! We need to move!

From the edges of the park, the subtle, disguised enforcers begin to converge, their eyes glowing red. Drones, previously distant, now descend rapidly, their optical sensors locked onto Elara.

ELARA
(Struggling to stand, but her resolve is clear)
I can show you the way. But I'll need more power. More stability. This place... it's trying to tear me apart.

Lucky looks at the approaching threats, then at Elara, the key to their escape. He knows what he has to do. He needs to find more power sources, more ways to stabilize Elara, to unleash her full potential. The fight for their minds, and their freedom, has just begun.

FADE OUT.
