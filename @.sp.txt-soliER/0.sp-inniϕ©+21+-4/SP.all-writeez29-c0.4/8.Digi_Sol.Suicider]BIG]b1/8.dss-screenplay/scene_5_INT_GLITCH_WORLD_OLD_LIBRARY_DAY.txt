INT. GLITCH WORLD - OLD LIBRARY - DAY

Lucky and Kael navigate the Glitch World's city, now with a clear destination: the old library. The journey is fraught with subtle dangers. They move through seemingly normal streets, but their senses are heightened, constantly scanning for the tell-tale signs of Metal Mafia enforcers – the momentary flicker of an eye, the subtle whir of hidden machinery, the too-perfect smile of a passerby.

They pass through a district that seems perpetually stuck in a time loop. A group of children endlessly play hopscotch, their laughter echoing, but never progressing. A street vendor perpetually offers the same apple, his arm frozen in mid-air. Kael uses his glitch perception to navigate the repeating patterns, guiding Lucky through the temporal distortions.

KAEL
(Voice low)
Temporal loops. A common tactic to disorient and exhaust prisoners. They're designed to make you question your sanity, to break your sense of linear time.

Lucky feels a wave of nausea as they step out of the loop, the world snapping back into fluid motion. He shakes his head, trying to clear the lingering disorientation.

They finally arrive at the OLD LIBRARY. It's a grand, classical building, made of what appears to be marble, but its facade is riddled with visual glitches. Sections of the wall flicker in and out of existence, revealing glimpses of raw code beneath. Columns occasionally stretch and compress, like breathing giants. The large, ornate doors are slightly ajar, revealing an interior shrouded in digital dust.

LUCKY
(Looking at the flickering walls)
This place is really struggling to maintain its illusion.

KAEL
(A grim smile)
Good. It means the information here is important. The system is actively trying to suppress it.

They step inside. The vast main hall is filled with towering bookshelves, stretching up into a digitally rendered dome ceiling. Thousands upon thousands of books line the shelves, but many are corrupted. Their titles are garbled, their pages filled with static or repeating patterns. The air is thick with the smell of old paper and ozone.

They begin to search, moving silently between the shelves. Kael uses his glitch powers to highlight anomalies, areas where the simulation is weakest. He runs his hand over a shelf, and for a moment, the books on it shimmer, revealing their true, underlying data structures.

KAEL
(Cont.)
We're looking for anything that doesn't fit. Any data that the Metal Mafia hasn't fully overwritten or suppressed.

Lucky, meanwhile, focuses on the physical books, hoping for a more tangible clue. He pulls out a book titled "The History of Avanta.C." Its pages are mostly blank, but a faint, almost invisible watermark appears on one page: a series of coordinates.

LUCKY
(Excited, showing Kael)
Look! Coordinates! What do you think they lead to?

Kael examines the coordinates, his brow furrowed. He pulls out a small, holographic map from his pocket and inputs the numbers. A red dot appears on the map, deep within a section of the Glitch World they haven't explored – a desolate, industrial zone.

KAEL
(Voice low)
This is... unexpected. These coordinates point to a forgotten data center. A very old one. It's been offline for decades, according to the public records of this simulation. But if the man was right, if the truth is hidden here...

Suddenly, a low, rhythmic HUM begins to vibrate through the library. The flickering glitches on the walls intensify, becoming more frequent, more violent. The air grows heavy, charged with a malevolent energy.

KAEL
(His eyes darting around)
They know we're here. The system is reacting. We've triggered an alarm.

From the far end of the hall, a group of figures emerges. They are not the subtle, disguised enforcers they've encountered before. These are full-blown METAL MAFIA ROBOTS, sleek and menacing, their optical sensors glowing with an aggressive red light. They move with a chilling, synchronized precision, their metallic footsteps echoing ominously in the vast hall.

LUCKY
(Drawing his Blade-Shield, a surge of adrenaline)
Looks like we found the truth. And now they're coming for us.

KAEL
(A grim determination on his face)
We have the coordinates. That's what matters. Now, we fight our way out. And then, we find out what's hidden in that data center.

FADE OUT.
