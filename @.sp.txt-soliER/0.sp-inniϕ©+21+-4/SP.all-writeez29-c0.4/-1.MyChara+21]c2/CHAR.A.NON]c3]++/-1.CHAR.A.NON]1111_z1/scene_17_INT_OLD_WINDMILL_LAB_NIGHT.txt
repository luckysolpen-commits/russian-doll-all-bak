SCENE 17: INT. OLD WINDMILL - LAB - NIGHT

SOUND of the rhythmic hum intensifying, a high-pitched whine, Cara's ragged breathing

[INT. OLD WINDMILL - LAB - NIGHT]

Cara stands transfixed before the altar, the iridescent black metal pulsating with a faint, internal light. Ariana watches her, a serene, almost melancholic expression on her face. The air in the chamber grows heavy, charged with an unseen energy. The rhythmic hum of the alien technology intensifies, a low thrum that vibrates through Cara's very bones.

CARA
(Voice barely a whisper)
This… this changes everything. The entire history we've been taught… it's a lie.

ARIANA
(Softly)
Truth is often a difficult burden, Director. But it is also liberation.

Suddenly, the holographic displays around them flicker wildly, the alien symbols distorting and reforming into chaotic patterns. A high-pitched WHINE emanates from the central console, growing louder, more piercing. The green light from the altar intensifies, bathing the room in an blinding, emerald glow.

CARA
(Alarmed)
What's happening?

Ariana's eyes widen, a flicker of genuine concern crossing her face for the first time.

ARIANA
(Urgent)
They know. They've found us! The energy signature… it's too strong!

Before Cara can react, a series of sharp CRACKS echo through the chamber. The crystalline structures embedded in the walls begin to fracture, spiderweb cracks spreading rapidly across their surfaces. Sparks fly from exposed conduits. The rhythmic hum transforms into a deafening ROAR.

Cara instinctively raises her arm to shield her eyes from the blinding light. The floor beneath her feet trembles violently. The air is thick with the smell of ozone and burning electronics.

ARIANA (CONT'D)
(Shouting over the din)
They're trying to suppress the signal! To prevent the truth from escaping!

The transparent containment unit in the corner, previously empty, suddenly glows with an intense, blinding white light. A faint, almost human-like SCREAM echoes from within, quickly cut short.

Cara tries to move, to reach Ariana, but the force of the energy is overwhelming. She feels a powerful, unseen hand push her back, a crushing pressure on her chest. Her vision blurs, the green light morphing into a swirling vortex of colors.

ARIANA (CONT'D)
(Her voice fading, distorted)
Remember, Cara! The truth… it will set you free…

The roar reaches a crescendo, a deafening, all-consuming sound that vibrates through every cell of Cara's body. The green light explodes into an unbearable white flash, consuming everything. Cara feels herself falling, tumbling into an abyss of pure, overwhelming energy.

And then, nothing. Absolute, profound blackness.
[FADE TO BLACK.]
