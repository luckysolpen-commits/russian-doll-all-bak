"But why is it on an altar?" Cara asks, circling the pedestal slowly,
her eyes never leaving the impossible metal. "Is this some kind
of cult? Do you believe it's an angel? A devil?" Ariana's expression
softens, a flicker of ancient sorrow crossing her features like
a shadow passing over the surface of a deep lake. "Humanity,
in its fear and its ignorance, often seeks to categorize the unknown.
To place it within familiar frameworks, to make it manageable,
to reduce the terrifying to the merely theological. For some,
it was indeed a deity, a source of divine power to be worshipped
and supplicated. For others, a demonic entity, a harbinger
of destruction to be feared and exorcised. And for the governments
who recovered it, it was simply a tool. A source of unimaginable
power and knowledge to be exploited, reverse-engineered, weaponized."
She gestures to the intricate symbols on the holographic displays,
which now seem to mirror the patterns on the metal fragment,
as if the room itself is trying to translate the alien into
something the human mind can begin to process. "But it is neither,
Director. It is simply technology. Advanced beyond your current
comprehension, yes. But technology nonetheless. And its placement
on this altar is a testament to the profound impact it had on those
who first encountered it. A symbol of their awe, their fear,
and their desperate attempt to control what they could not understand."
Cara circles the altar one more time, her mind racing, the implications
cascading through her consciousness like a dam breaking. This isn't just
a piece of a spaceship. It's a key. A key to understanding the true history
of humanity's interaction with extraterrestrial life. A key to unlocking
secrets that could shatter the very foundations of civilization,
religion, science, everything. "And you want to expose this?" she asks,
her voice firm with a new resolve that surprises even her. "To show
the world?" Ariana nods, her gaze unwavering, her eyes holding
a certainty that spans millennia. "It is time, Director. The lies
have gone on for too long. The manipulation, the control — it has
stifled humanity's true potential. This fragment, this truth,
must be revealed. Not as a weapon, but as a catalyst for evolution."
