Suddenly, the holographic displays around them flicker wildly,
the alien symbols distorting and reforming into chaotic patterns
that make Cara's eyes water and her stomach lurch. A high-pitched
whine emanates from the central console, growing louder, more piercing,
finding a frequency that feels like a needle pressed against
the inside of her skull. The green light from the altar intensifies,
bathing the room in a blinding, emerald glow that turns every surface
into a mirror, every shadow into a pool of impossible darkness.
"What's happening?" Cara shouts, her voice barely carrying over
the rising whine, her hand instinctively going to her pistol
though she has no idea what she would shoot. Ariana's eyes widen,
a flicker of genuine concern crossing her face for the first time,
and in that flicker Cara sees something terrifying — not fear
for herself, but fear for the truth, for the fragile vessel
of knowledge they are standing in, for the possibility that everything
they have built here is about to be undone. "They know," Ariana says,
and her voice carries a resonance that is no longer entirely human.
"They've found us. The energy signature from the fragment — it's too strong.
They can feel it being exposed. They can feel you seeing it."
Before Cara can process what Ariana is saying, before she can
ask who they are and how they can feel anything, a series of sharp
cracks echo through the chamber like gunshots. The crystalline
structures embedded in the walls begin to fracture, spiderweb cracks
spreading rapidly across their surfaces with audible pops,
each one a small death. Sparks fly from exposed conduits,
dancing through the air like electric fireflies. The rhythmic hum
transforms into a deafening roar, a sound that is less a sound
and more a physical force, pressing against her chest, her eardrums,
the walls of her mind. "They're trying to suppress the signal!"
Ariana shouts over the din, her voice barely human now, stretched
and distorted by the energy that fills the room. "To prevent
the truth from escaping!"
