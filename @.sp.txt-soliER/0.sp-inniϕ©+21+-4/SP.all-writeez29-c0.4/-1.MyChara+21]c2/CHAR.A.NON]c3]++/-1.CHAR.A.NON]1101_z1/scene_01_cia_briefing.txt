[INT. CIA BRIEFING ROOM - DAY]

SOUND of projector whirring

CHARA (30s, sharp, focused, dressed in a tailored suit) sits at a polished conference table. Her eyes are fixed on a holographic display showing a complex web of encrypted data. Across from her, AGENT MILLER (50s, stern, by-the-book) gestures to the display.

MILLER
(Gravely)
Agent Chara, we have a new priority. For months, an unknown entity has been leaving digital breadcrumbs across the darknet. Highly sophisticated. Untraceable. We're calling him... Anonymous.

Chara leans forward, her expression unreadable.

CHARA
What's his game? Political? Financial?

MILLER
That's what we need you to find out. He's been poking around in places he shouldn't. Highly sensitive corporate networks. Government servers. He's a ghost, Chara. Leaves no trace. But he's leaving a trail of chaos in his wake.

The holographic display shifts, showing a series of news headlines: "Global Markets Fluctuate After Unexplained Data Breach," "Energy Grid Briefly Compromised," "Classified Documents Leaked."

CHARA
(Quietly)
He's good.

MILLER
Too good. Your mission, Agent, is to track him down. Uncover his identity. And understand his motives. This isn't just about a rogue hacker. This feels... bigger.

Chara nods, her gaze returning to the intricate web of data on the display. A flicker of intrigue crosses her face.
[FADE OUT.]
