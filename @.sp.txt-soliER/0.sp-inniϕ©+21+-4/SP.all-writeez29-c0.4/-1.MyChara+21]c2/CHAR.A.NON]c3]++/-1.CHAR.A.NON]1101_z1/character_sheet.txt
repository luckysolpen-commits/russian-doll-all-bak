# Character Sheet

## Chara
*   **Role:** Seasoned CIA Agent, protagonist.
*   **Key Traits:** Sharp, focused, intelligent, determined, resilient, curious, analytical, resourceful, possesses strong instincts. Initially by-the-book but willing to bend rules for the truth.
*   **Skills:** Highly skilled in digital forensics, tracking, combat, and analysis. Adaptable and quick-thinking under pressure.
*   **Journey Arc:** Begins as a hunter of Anonymous, becomes an unwilling ally, and then a target of a vast conspiracy. Her understanding of the world is constantly challenged and expanded.
*   **Key Relationships:** Agent Miller (handler), Anonymous (enigmatic ally), Arianna (initial guide, later antagonist), Maya (informant/ally).

## Anonymous
*   **Role:** Enigmatic hacker, unseen ally to Chara.
*   **Key Traits:** Highly skilled, elusive, mysterious, intelligent, strategic, possesses a dry wit (implied through modulated voice). Operates from the shadows.
*   **Skills:** Master hacker, capable of infiltrating highly secure systems, manipulating digital environments, and leaving untraceable digital breadcrumbs. Uses drones and AI for physical interaction.
*   **Physicality:** Never physically seen by Chara or the reader. Communicates via modulated voice, drones, or text. Uses hacking and even paranormal means (Quinault prayer stick, manipulating ghosts/undead) for interaction.
*   **Motivations:** Seeks to expose truth and manipulate global events, seemingly against powerful corporations like OmniTech and NovaSpire.

## Agent Miller
*   **Role:** Chara's handler at the CIA.
*   **Key Traits:** Stern, by-the-book, professional, initially skeptical but trusts Chara's judgment.
*   **Function:** Provides Chara with missions and resources, represents the official agency perspective.

## Arianna
*   **Role:** Member of the Order of Providence, Chara's initial guide and later revealed as an antagonist.
*   **Key Traits:** Mysterious, calm, serene, possesses ancient wisdom, manipulative, zealous in her beliefs.
*   **Skills:** Deep knowledge of ancient lore, rituals, and advanced technology. Leads the Order of Providence.
*   **Motivations:** Believes in democratizing knowledge and neutralizing powerful entities like Omnicorp, even through morally ambiguous means.

## Maya
*   **Role:** Informant, potential ally, part of a larger resistance against Omicorp.
*   **Key Traits:** Resourceful, knowledgeable about the conspiracy, elusive, pragmatic.
*   **Skills:** Appears to have access to high-level information and resources, capable of orchestrating escapes.
*   **Motivations:** Seeks to expose Omicorp and the deeper corruption.
