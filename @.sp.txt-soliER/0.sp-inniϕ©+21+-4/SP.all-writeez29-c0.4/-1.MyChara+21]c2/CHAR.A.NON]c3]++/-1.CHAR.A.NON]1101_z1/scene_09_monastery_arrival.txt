[EXT. ANCIENT MONASTERY - NIGHT]

SOUND of distant owl hoot, Chara's footsteps on gravel

Arianna leads Chara through winding, overgrown paths. The air grows heavier, cooler, as they move deeper into the secluded countryside. The moonlight struggles to penetrate the thick canopy of ancient trees, casting long, dancing shadows that seem to shift and writhe.

Chara follows, her senses on high alert. She can feel an energy in the air, a subtle vibration that hums beneath the surface of reality. It’s unsettling, yet strangely compelling.

After what feels like an eternity, the trees begin to thin, revealing a clearing. And in the center of the clearing, looming against the dark sky, is an ANCIENT MONASTERY. Its stone walls are covered in thick ivy, its windows dark and empty, like the eyes of a forgotten giant.

The monastery is massive, its architecture a blend of gothic and something far older, almost alien. The air around it seems to vibrate with an otherworldly energy, a faint, almost imperceptible BLUE GLOW emanating from within its depths.

Chara stops, her breath catching in her throat. She’s seen ancient buildings before, but nothing like this. This place feels alive, imbued with centuries of secrets.

CHARA
(Whispering, awestruck)
What is this place?

ARIANNA
(Her voice hushed, reverent)
This is the Order's stronghold. Our sanctuary. Here, you will find the answers you seek.

Arianna begins to walk towards the monastery, her movements fluid and silent. Chara hesitates for a moment, a wave of trepidation washing over her. Every instinct screams danger, but the allure of the unknown, the promise of truth, pulls her forward.

She follows Arianna through a massive, ornate iron gate that creaks open silently as they approach. The air inside the monastery grounds is even colder, the blue glow more pronounced. The silence is absolute, broken only by their footsteps.

They approach a heavy, wooden door, bound with ancient iron. Arianna places her hand on the door, and without a sound, it swings inward, revealing a dark, cavernous interior.

ARIANNA
(Turning to Chara, her eyes glinting in the faint blue light)
But are you ready to face it?

Chara looks into the darkness beyond the threshold, then back at Arianna. Her jaw tightens. She’s come too far to turn back now.

CHARA
(Resolute)
I'm ready.
[FADE OUT.]
