[INT. ANONYMOUS'S APARTMENT - NIGHT]

SOUND of rapid keyboard clicks, multiple monitors humming

ANONYMOUS (unseen, only hands and a modulated voice heard) sits hunched over a desk, surrounded by a dizzying array of monitors displaying lines of code, network maps, and encrypted data streams. Empty coffee cups and energy drink cans litter the desk.

On one screen, a news report plays silently, showing a stern-faced CEO of OMNITECH denying recent data breaches. On another, a similar report features the CEO of NOVASPIRE, equally dismissive.

ANONYMOUS (V.O.)
(Modulated, calm, almost bored)
Deny all you want. The truth is in the code.

His fingers fly across the keyboard, a blur of motion. He navigates through layers of firewalls, bypassing security protocols with effortless grace. He’s deep inside OmniTech’s network, then NovaSpire’s. He finds what he’s looking for: encrypted files, hidden directories, and a series of highly unusual financial transactions.

ANONYMOUS (V.O.)
(A hint of surprise in his modulated voice)
Well, well, well. What have we here? Not just corporate espionage. Something far more… ambitious.

He uncovers schematics for advanced, experimental technology. Research papers on unconventional energy sources. And then, a series of communications between OmniTech and NovaSpire, discussing a joint venture, codenamed "Project Chimera."

ANONYMOUS (V.O.)
(A low chuckle)
Manipulating global events, indeed. You two aren't just playing with money. You're playing with fire.

He pauses, his fingers hovering over the keyboard. A new window pops up on one of his screens – a secure, encrypted channel. A single, cryptic message appears:

"The Whispering Walls are listening."

Anonymous’s head tilts slightly, a rare moment of curiosity. He types a rapid response.

ANONYMOUS (V.O.)
(Modulated, intrigued)
And what do the walls whisper?
[FADE OUT.]
