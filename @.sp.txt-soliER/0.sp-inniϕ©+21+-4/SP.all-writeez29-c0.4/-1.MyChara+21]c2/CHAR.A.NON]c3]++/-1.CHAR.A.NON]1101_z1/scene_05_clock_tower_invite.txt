CHARA

She looks at the clock on her wall. It’s already late. Midnight is only a few hours away. She considers her options. Inform Miller? Bring backup? Her instincts scream caution, but her curiosity, and the burning desire to uncover the truth, override it.

She knows the old clock tower. It’s an abandoned landmark on the edge of the city, a relic from a bygone era. Isolated. Perfect for a clandestine meeting. Too perfect.

CHARA

What are you planning, Anonymous?
