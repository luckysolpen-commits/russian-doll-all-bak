[INT. CHARA'S APARTMENT - NIGHT]

SOUND of keyboard clicks, soft hum of computer

CHARA sits at her home workstation, a stark contrast to the high-tech CIA setup. Multiple monitors display lines of code, network traffic, and a map of the darknet. Empty coffee mug and a half-eaten protein bar sit beside her.

She’s deep in the digital rabbit hole, tracing the breadcrumbs left by Anonymous. Each step is a complex puzzle, a series of encrypted servers and proxy chains that seem to lead nowhere and everywhere at once.

CHARA (V.O.)
(Muttering to herself)
He’s good. Too good. Like he knows I’m coming.

She navigates through a particularly convoluted series of redirects, her fingers flying across the keyboard. A small, almost imperceptible anomaly catches her eye – a faint, recurring signature embedded deep within the code. It’s like a digital fingerprint, unique and elusive.

She isolates the signature, running it through a series of decryption algorithms. The process is slow, painstaking. Hours pass. The city outside her window transitions from night to the first hint of dawn.

Finally, a small window pops up on her main monitor. It’s a video file. Encrypted. She hesitates for a moment, then initiates the decryption. The progress bar crawls.

CHARA (V.O.)
(Whispering)
Come on, Anonymous. Show me what you’re hiding.

The video begins to play. Grainy, dimly lit. It shows a room, sparsely furnished. In the center, on a small table, rests a QUINAULT PRAYER STICK. It begins to slowly rotate on its own axis. A low, almost imperceptible HUM fills the audio.

Chara leans closer, her brow furrowed. This isn't what she expected. The camera pans out, revealing a figure SHROUDED IN SHADOWS. Only a silhouette is visible. A MODULATED VOICE emanates from the figure.

MODULATED VOICE (O.S.)
(Deep, distorted)
The Whispering Walls are listening... I have a message for those who seek truth.

The transmission cuts out abruptly, leaving Chara staring at a blank screen. The hum of her computer is the only sound.

CHARA
(To herself, a mix of frustration and intrigue)
What do you want to tell me?

She immediately begins to trace the source of the video, but the trail is cold. Anonymous has vanished again, leaving only this bizarre, unsettling message.
[FADE OUT.]
