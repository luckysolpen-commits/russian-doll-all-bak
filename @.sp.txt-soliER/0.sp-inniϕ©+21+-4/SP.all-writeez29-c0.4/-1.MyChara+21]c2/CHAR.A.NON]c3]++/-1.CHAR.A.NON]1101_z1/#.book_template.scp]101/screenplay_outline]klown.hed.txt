**Logline:** A skilled CIA agent, Chara, hunts an enigmatic hacker, Anonymous, only to discover they are both entangled in a vast conspiracy involving powerful corporations, paranormal research, and a secret society, forcing them to unite against a common, terrifying enemy.

**Synopsis:**

**ACT I**
*   **Inciting Incident:** Chara, a seasoned CIA agent, is tasked with tracking down an elusive hacker, Anonymous, who leaves cryptic digital breadcrumbs. Simultaneously, Anonymous uncovers a sinister plot by OmniTech and NovaSpire to manipulate global events.
*   **Rising Action:**
    *   Chara follows Anonymous's digital trail, tracking him through encrypted servers.
    *   Anonymous continues to expose the corporations' manipulation of information and global events.
    *   Chara realizes Anonymous might be her only key to understanding the corporations' activities.
    *   Anonymous leaves a direct message for Chara: "The Whispering Walls are listening."
    *   Chara receives a video transmission from Anonymous, showing a Quinault prayer stick and a modulated voice.
    *   Anonymous continues to send cryptic messages, leading Chara deeper into the "shadow net."
    *   Anonymous invites Chara to meet at the old clock tower.
    *   Chara and Anonymous meet, with Anonymous's face obscured, and he warns her that "The eyes are watching you."

**ACT II**
*   **Rising Action:**
    *   Chara investigates the "eye" symbol, discovering a hidden message and a link to an ancient text about the "Order of Providence."
    *   Anonymous sends Chara to the old windmill, where she meets Arianna, a member of the Order of Providence.
    *   Arianna guides Chara to the Order's stronghold, an ancient monastery, promising answers.
    *   Chara undergoes three trials to prove her worthiness to the Order:
        *   **Test of Courage:** Chara faces and defeats a giant humanoid robot.
        *   **Test of Wisdom:** Chara navigates a labyrinth of knowledge, solving complex puzzles and riddles to retrieve ancient scrolls.
        *   **Test of Passion:** Chara explores a mystical garden, overcoming emotional challenges to find a hidden fountain.
    *   **Midpoint:** Chara passes the trials and is led to the server room in the basement of the monastery. She discovers the Order's project: copying Omnicorp's research.
    *   **Rising Action (continued):**
        *   Chara returns with reinforcements to the monastery, but the hacker cult (Order of Providence) has vanished, leaving behind only the disturbing evidence of their research.
        *   Chara analyzes hard drives, finding a URL to a darknet forum.
        *   She discovers a video of a seance gone wrong, leading to gruesome crime scenes with headless victims.
        *   Chara visits the crime scenes, confirming the ritualistic nature of the murders.
        *   She visits the sole survivor in the hospital, who refuses to talk. Omicorp agents also visit the survivor, raising Chara's suspicions about Omicorp's involvement.
        *   Chara researches Omicorp's shady past, including experimental medical procedures.
        *   Chara receives a mysterious text message, inviting her to a midnight meeting at an old warehouse.
        *   Chara goes to the warehouse, finds speedboats, and meets Maya, who reveals Omicorp is part of a larger, decades-long web of deceit involving government agencies and high-ranking officials.
        *   **Climax of Act II:** Chara returns to Langley and finds a severed head, adorned with clown makeup, on her desk. Anonymous, via drone, identifies the victim as a Langley analyst and the makeup's unique chemical signature points to OmniTech's R&D facility. This is a direct, personal threat.
