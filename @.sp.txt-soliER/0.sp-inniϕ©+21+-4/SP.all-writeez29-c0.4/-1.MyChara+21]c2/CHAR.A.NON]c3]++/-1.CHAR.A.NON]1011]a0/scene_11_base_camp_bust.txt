[EXT. BASE CAMP - NIGHT]

The makeshift base camp, nestled deep within the African jungle, is quiet. A small, camouflaged tent, a few supply crates, and a crackling fire. CHARA, having made her way back from the facility, is exhausted but alert. She's reviewing the data on her tablet, the glow illuminating her grim face.

Suddenly, the silence is shattered by the CRACK of gunfire and the THUD of heavy boots. PMC SOLDIERS, a large, well-armed force, burst into the clearing, their weapons blazing. They've tracked her.

Chara reacts instantly, rolling behind a fallen log, her suppressed pistol already in hand. Bullets tear through the air, shredding the tent and splintering the supply crates. She returns fire, precise and deadly, taking down two of the lead soldiers.

More soldiers pour in, fanning out to surround her. Their numbers are overwhelming. Chara moves with desperate agility, using the dense foliage and the few remaining pieces of cover to her advantage. She fires, reloads, and moves, a one-woman army against a relentless tide.

She hears the heavy THUMP-THUMP-THUMP of a helicopter approaching. Not a friendly one. Searchlights cut through the darkness, illuminating the clearing. She's exposed.

She spots a small, hidden path, barely visible through the undergrowth. It's a desperate gamble, but her only chance. She throws a smoke grenade, the thick, acrid smoke billowing out, providing a momentary screen.

Under the cover of the smoke, Chara sprints towards the path, bullets still whistling past her. She dives into the dense jungle, the sounds of the firefight slowly fading behind her as she disappears into the darkness.
