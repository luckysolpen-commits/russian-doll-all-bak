[INT./EXT. MOTHRA BASE - VARIOUS TIMES]

MONTAGE

SOUND of waves crashing against the oil strut

DAY

CHARA, GIANA, and KLONDIKE are engaged in mundane chores. Chara meticulously cleans her suppressed pistol, disassembling and reassembling it with practiced ease. Giana, her leg now fully healed, sorts through crates of supplies, her movements efficient. Klondike, surprisingly, is meticulously polishing a large, intricate piece of machinery, perhaps a part of the helipad's landing gear.

DAY

They are on the training deck. Chara and Giana spar, their movements a blur of controlled aggression. Chara demonstrates a new disarming technique, Giana counters with a swift kick. Klondike observes, offering a rare, concise piece of advice that immediately improves their form.

EVENING

On a small, open-air platform overlooking the ocean. The sun dips below the horizon, painting the sky in hues of orange and purple. Chara, Giana, and Klondike sit on overturned crates, each with a glass in hand. The clinking of ice, the low murmur of their voices. Chara takes a long drag from a green cigar, the smoke curling lazily into the twilight.

GIANA
You know, for a top-secret black site, this place has its moments.

KLONDIKE
Quiet. Efficient. Good for the soul.

Chara exhales slowly, a faint smile playing on her lips.

CHARA
And the company's not bad either.

They share a comfortable silence, the bond between them palpable. They talk about past missions, close calls, and the absurdity of their lives. Laughter, genuine and unrestrained, echoes across the water.

NIGHT

Chara and Giana are playing a game of darts in a dimly lit recreation room. Giana throws a perfect bullseye. Chara, feigning annoyance, throws her dart wildly, missing the board entirely.

GIANA
Still got it.

CHARA
Beginner's luck.

Klondike enters, carrying a tray with three more green cigars and a bottle of something amber. He sets it down, a silent invitation.

DAY

Chara is alone on the helipad, looking out at the vast expanse of the ocean. The wind whips her hair. She takes a deep breath, a sense of calm settling over her. This temporary peace, this camaraderie, has been a much-needed respite. But she knows it's fleeting. Langley awaits.
