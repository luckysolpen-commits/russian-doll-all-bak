[INT. HIDDEN CAVE - NIGHT]

CHARA sits in the damp, cool darkness of a hidden cave, her back against a rough rock wall. Her tactical gear is torn, her face smudged with dirt and exhaustion. She nurses a small wound on her arm, applying a makeshift bandage.

She pulls out her encrypted tablet, its screen a beacon in the gloom. She tries again to establish contact, and this time, a faint signal flickers to life. A secure channel opens.

ANON (V.O.)
Chara. Status report. We lost your signal after the base camp.

CHARA
(Voice hoarse)
Compromised. Base camp overrun. Giana extracted. I'm alone. But I have the data.

A beat of silence from ANON.

ANON (V.O.)
Understood. Good work, Chara. Your intel is invaluable. It confirms our suspicions. Cerberus is moving on a new target. A hidden facility, deep in the Eastern European mountains. They're planning something big. Something that could disrupt global stability on an unprecedented scale.

Chara listens, her eyes widening. The scale of the threat is even larger than she imagined.

CHARA
Eastern Europe? That's a long way from here.

ANON (V.O.)
Indeed. You have two options, Chara. Return to Mothra Base for debriefing and reassignment. Or... you can take on this new mission. Infiltrate the Eastern European facility. Stop them before they can execute their plan.

Chara looks at her injured arm, then at the tablet, the image of the "BEHEMOTH" mech still burned into her mind. She thinks of Giana, of Klondike, of the global implications of Cerberus's actions.

CHARA
What's the objective?

ANON (V.O.)
Infiltration. Disruption. And if possible, extraction of their lead scientist. He's key to their operation.

Another beat of silence. Chara closes her eyes, weighing the options. The exhaustion, the danger, the sheer impossibility of it all. But then, a flicker of determination. This is what she was trained for. This is what she does.

CHARA
Send me the coordinates. And any intel you have on the facility.

ANON (V.O.)
Understood. Extraction will be difficult. You'll be on your own for a significant portion of this mission.

CHARA
I'm used to it.

ANON sends a burst of data to her tablet: detailed maps, satellite images, and a profile of the lead scientist. Chara reviews them, her mind already formulating a new plan. The mission continues.
