[EXT. MOTHRA BASE - DAY]

The familiar THUMP-THUMP-THUMP of a helicopter approaches Mothra Base. It's the same helicopter that brought her here, and the same one that will take her away. CHARA sits inside, looking out at the vast expanse of the ocean, a sense of quiet reflection on her face.

She disembarks onto the helipad, her movements more fluid, more confident than when she first arrived. GIANA NGUYEN and AGENT KLONDIKE are waiting, their faces etched with a shared understanding of the battles fought and won. ANON stands nearby, a silent sentinel.

GIANA
Another one for the books, Chara. You really pulled it off.

CHARA
We pulled it off. Couldn't have done it without you two. Or Klondike's dramatic entrances.

Klondike offers a rare, genuine smile.

KLONDIKE
Just doing my job.

ANON steps forward, his voice losing some of its usual gravelly edge, a hint of genuine respect in his tone.

ANON
The data you brought back, Chara... it's a game-changer. Cerberus is reeling. We've initiated operations on multiple fronts. This is the beginning of the end for them.

Chara nods, a sense of quiet satisfaction settling over her. The exhaustion is still there, but it's tempered by the knowledge that she made a difference. The world is a little safer, thanks to their efforts.

CHARA
So, what's next?

ANON looks out at the horizon, a glint of determination in his hidden eyes.

ANON
More missions. Always more missions. But now, we fight on our terms. We have the intel, we have the advantage. And we have a team that can get the job done.

Giana places a hand on Chara's shoulder.

GIANA
You earned a break, Chara. But I have a feeling it won't last long.

Chara looks at her friends, her comrades. The bond forged in the crucible of danger, strengthened by shared purpose. She knows they'll be together again, facing new threats, fighting new battles.

She takes a deep breath, the salty air filling her lungs. The sun is setting, casting a golden glow over the oil strut. It's a beautiful, fleeting moment of peace before the next storm.
[FADE TO BLACK.]
