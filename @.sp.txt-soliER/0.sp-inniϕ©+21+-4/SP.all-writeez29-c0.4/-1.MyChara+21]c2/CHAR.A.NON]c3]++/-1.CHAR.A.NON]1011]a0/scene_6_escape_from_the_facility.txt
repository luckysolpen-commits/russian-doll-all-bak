[INT./EXT. MECH FACILITY - NIGHT]

The data storage room is now a chaotic scene. PMC GUARDS, alerted to the breach, swarm the area, their flashlights cutting through the dim light. CHARA, a shadow among shadows, moves with impossible speed and precision.

She uses the server racks as cover, darting from one to another, her suppressed pistol spitting silent death. A guard rounds a corner, his eyes wide with surprise as Chara appears, a swift, brutal strike to his neck silencing him before he can raise an alarm.

She navigates the labyrinthine corridors, the facility's layout now etched in her mind from her earlier reconnaissance. Alarms begin to blare, a piercing WAIL that echoes through the complex. The element of surprise is gone. Now it's about survival.

She encounters a squad of heavily armed PMC soldiers in a narrow hallway. No time for stealth. Chara unleashes a flurry of precise, incapacitating shots, dropping three of them before they can even react. The remaining two open fire, their bullets sparking against the metal walls. Chara rolls, firing from a prone position, taking them down with ruthless efficiency.

She reaches a service elevator, its doors stubbornly closed. She quickly hotwires the panel, sparks flying as she bypasses the security. The doors GRIND open, revealing a shaft leading upwards. She climbs, hand over hand, the sounds of pursuit growing fainter below.

She emerges onto a rooftop, the cool night air a welcome relief after the oppressive heat of the facility. Below, the jungle stretches out, a dark, undulating mass. Searchlights sweep across the perimeter, their beams cutting through the night.

Chara spots a makeshift zipline leading to a distant, overgrown hill. It's a risky escape route, but her only option. She secures her gear, takes a running leap, and slides down the line, the wind rushing past her ears.

Bullets WHIZZ past her, fired from the watchtowers below. She ignores them, her focus solely on the end of the line. She lands with a controlled roll, disappearing into the dense foliage of the hill.

She doesn't stop. She pushes through the undergrowth, the sounds of the facility fading behind her. She is injured, exhausted, but the data is secure. The mission, though messy, is a success. She makes her way towards a pre-determined safe zone, a small, hidden cave she had marked during her initial reconnaissance. The jungle, once her enemy, now offers her sanctuary.
