[EXT. JUNGLE - DAY]

The jungle, once a vibrant green, now feels oppressive. The sounds of distant explosions and gunfire have faded, leaving an unsettling silence. CHARA, now alone, moves with a renewed intensity. Her face is grim, but her eyes are sharp, constantly scanning, analyzing.

She moves through the dense undergrowth, her movements fluid and silent, a predator in her natural habitat. The loss of Giana, and the unexpected compromise of their mission, has only sharpened her resolve. She adapts, her mind already re-calculating, re-planning.

She pauses at a small clearing, pulling out a compact, encrypted tablet. She taps at the screen, bringing up a topographical map of the area. She marks potential enemy patrol routes, identifies choke points, and plots a new, more circuitous approach to the facility. Her finger traces a path through the dense jungle, avoiding the obvious, seeking the unseen.

She observes a patrol of PMC SOLDIERS in the distance, their heavy boots crunching on fallen leaves. She notes their patterns, their blind spots, the rhythm of their movements. She is a ghost, unseen, unheard, gathering every scrap of information.

She encounters a small, fast-flowing river. Instead of crossing at an obvious point, she scales a nearby tree, moving along its branches, using the natural canopy as cover. She drops silently to the other side, leaving no trace of her passage.

Hours pass. The sun begins its slow descent, casting long, distorted shadows through the trees. The air grows cooler, the sounds of the jungle shifting as nocturnal creatures begin to stir. Chara doesn't falter. Her focus is absolute.

She reaches a ridge overlooking the facility. It's a sprawling complex, a metallic scar on the verdant landscape. Lights begin to flicker on, illuminating watchtowers and reinforced perimeters. She pulls out a pair of high-powered binoculars, studying the defenses, identifying potential entry points, and noting the positions of heavy weaponry.

She sees the faint glow of a large, hangar-like structure, the likely location of the rogue mechs. Her target.

She takes a deep breath, the scent of damp earth and distant machinery filling her lungs. The mission has changed. The stakes are higher. But Chara is ready. She is a single, determined force, moving towards an unknown enemy, alone.
