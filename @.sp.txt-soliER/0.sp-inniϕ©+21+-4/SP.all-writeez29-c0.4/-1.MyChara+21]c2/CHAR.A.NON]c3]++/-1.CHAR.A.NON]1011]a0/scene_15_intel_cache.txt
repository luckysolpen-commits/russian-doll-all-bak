[INT. LEAD SCIENTIST'S OFFICE / LAB - NIGHT]

CHARA moves silently through a well-appointed office, clearly belonging to a high-ranking individual. Bookshelves filled with scientific texts, complex diagrams tacked to a corkboard, and a large, cluttered desk dominate the space. This must be the lead scientist's domain.

She quickly begins her search, her eyes scanning for anything that might contain valuable intel. She checks drawers, scans the desk for hidden compartments, and examines the computer terminal. The computer is locked, but Chara pulls out a small, specialized hacking device. With a few rapid keystrokes, the screen flickers, then grants her access.

She navigates through encrypted files, her mind processing information at an incredible speed. She finds research notes on a new energy source, far more potent than anything currently known. Schematics for a global network of automated defense systems, designed to be controlled by the Behemoth mechs. This is Cerberus's ultimate plan: total global dominance.

Suddenly, a faint CLICK. The sound of a door opening. Chara freezes, her hand instinctively going to her suppressed pistol. She ducks behind a large desk, becoming one with the shadows.

The LEAD SCIENTIST (50s, disheveled, brilliant but clearly under immense pressure) enters the office. He mutters to himself, rubbing his temples, then walks directly to a hidden wall safe behind a painting. He inputs a code, and the safe WHIRS open, revealing a single, encrypted data chip.

He takes the chip, then turns to the computer, intending to upload something. Chara sees her opportunity. As he turns his back, she moves, a blur of motion. Before he can react, she has him in a chokehold, her arm pressed firmly against his windpipe.

CHARA
Don't move. Don't speak. Just listen.

The scientist struggles for a moment, then goes limp, his eyes wide with fear. Chara quickly disarms him, taking the data chip from his hand. She then binds his hands and feet with zip ties, and gags him.

She glances at the computer screen. The download is almost complete. She quickly copies the remaining files to her data drive, then wipes the computer's logs, leaving no trace of her presence.

She hears distant FOOTSTEPS. A patrol. Her time is running out. She grabs the data chip, secures her own data drive, and slips out of the office, leaving the bound scientist behind. The intel is hers. Now, for the escape.
