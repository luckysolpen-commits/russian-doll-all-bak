# Screenplay Outline: My Chara: CHARANON

## ACT I: The Briefing & The Betrayal

### Scene 1: Arrival at Mothra Base
*   **INT. HELICOPTER - DAY**
    *   CHARA and GIANA NGUYEN in a helicopter, flying over lush African jungle.
    *   Their conversation establishes their mission and their relationship.
*   **EXT. MOTHRA BASE - DAY**
    *   Helicopter lands on the oil strut.
    *   Chara and Giana disembark, taking in the surroundings.
    *   They meet ANON, a mysterious CIA informant.
    *   ANON briefs them on a rogue mech facility hidden deep in the jungle.
    *   He emphasizes the critical need for stealth.

### Scene 2: Infiltration Gone Wrong
*   **EXT. JUNGLE / FACILITY PERIMETER - DAY**
    *   Chara and Giana are on approach to the facility, moving stealthily through dense jungle.
    *   Suddenly, they are pinned down by precise SNIPER FIRE.
    *   GIANA is hit and injured, unable to move.
    *   Chara calls for backup: AGENT KLONDIKE.
*   **EXT. JUNGLE / FACILITY PERIMETER - CONTINUOUS**
    *   Klondike arrives dramatically, taking out two snipers with expert shots.
    *   He fires a ROCKET LAUNCHER, striking a nearby penthouse, causing a massive explosion.
    *   Stealth is completely compromised.
    *   Klondike airlifts the injured Giana away, leaving Chara to continue the mission alone.

## ACT II: Solo Mission & Intel Retrieval

### Scene 3: Solo Reconnaissance & Approach
*   **EXT. JUNGLE - DAY**
    *   Chara, now alone, adapts her strategy.
    *   She continues reconnaissance, using her skills to gather intel on enemy positions and movements.
    *   She navigates the treacherous jungle terrain, approaching the facility perimeter with renewed focus.

### Scene 4: Facility Infiltration
*   **INT. MECH FACILITY - DAY/NIGHT**
    *   Chara infiltrates the heavily guarded facility, a sprawling complex filled with PMC forces.
    *   She uses her exceptional stealth abilities to evade detection, moving through ventilation shafts, shadows, and blind spots.
    *   She observes mech designs and enemy patrols, gathering more intel.

### Scene 5: Data Acquisition
*   **INT. DATA STORAGE ROOM - NIGHT**
    *   Chara locates a heavily guarded data storage room.
    *   She uses her hacking skills to bypass security systems and disable cameras.
    *   She downloads critical, valuable intel on the facility's operations and the rogue mechs.

### Scene 6: Escape from the Facility
*   **INT./EXT. MECH FACILITY - NIGHT**
    *   With the data acquired, Chara initiates her escape.
    *   She fends off pursuing PMC forces, engaging in brief, brutal combat when necessary.
    *   She makes her way to a pre-determined extraction point or a safe zone outside the facility.

## ACT III: Aftermath & Departure

### Scene 7: Return to Mothra Base & Debrief
*   **EXT. MOTHRA BASE - DAY**
    *   Chara is extracted and returns to Mothra Base.
    *   She debriefs with Giana (who is recovering or has recovered) and ANON/Klondike.
    *   They discuss the mission's success, the intel retrieved, and the implications for global stability.

### Scene 8: Downtime on the Oil Strut
*   **INT./EXT. MOTHRA BASE - VARIOUS TIMES**
    *   A montage of Chara, Giana, and Klondike spending time on the oil strut.
    *   They engage in chores, training drills, and moments of relaxation.
    *   Scenes of camaraderie, perhaps drinking alcohol or smoking green cigars, highlighting their bond.

### Scene 9: Emotional Goodbye
*   **EXT. MOTHRA BASE - NIGHT**
    *   The night before Chara's departure for Langley.
    *   Chara shares emotional goodbyes with Giana and Klondike.
    *   A bittersweet moment, acknowledging their bond and the necessity of her leaving.

### Scene 10: Journey to Langley
*   **EXT. HELICOPTER / INT. PLANE - DAY**
    *   Chara boards a helicopter from Mothra Base to a smaller airfield.
    *   She transfers to a larger plane bound for Langley.
    *   She reflects on her mission, the challenges faced, and the future.
*   **EXT. LANGLEY - DAY**
    *   The plane lands at Langley.
    *   Chara disembarks, a sense of quiet exhaustion and readiness for a new phase.
    *   The "boring Langley" provides a stark contrast to the field.
