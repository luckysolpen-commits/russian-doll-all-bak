[EXT. JUNGLE / FACILITY PERIMETER - DAY]

Dense, humid jungle. The air is thick with the scent of damp earth and exotic flora. CHARA and GIANA NGUYEN move with practiced stealth, their movements fluid and silent. They are deep within the African jungle, approaching the suspected rogue mech facility.

Chara, ever vigilant, scans the tree line, her suppressed rifle held ready. Giana, a few paces behind, checks her comms.

GIANA
Perimeter looks clear. No heat signatures. ANON's intel was solid.

CHARA
Too solid. This feels... quiet.

Suddenly, the air is ripped apart by the CRACK of a high-powered rifle. A bullet WHIZZES past Chara's head, embedding itself in a tree trunk with a THUD. Another shot rings out, and Giana cries out, stumbling. She clutches her leg, a dark stain rapidly spreading on her tactical pants.

GIANA
Sniper! Damn it! My leg!

Chara drops to a crouch, pulling Giana behind the meager cover of a fallen log. More shots pepper the surrounding foliage, splintering wood and kicking up dirt. They are pinned down.

CHARA
Stay down! Where's he coming from?

Giana grits her teeth, trying to move, but pain flashes across her face. She shakes her head.

GIANA
Can't tell. High ground... multiple angles...

Chara pulls out her comms device, her voice tight with urgency.

CHARA
Klondike, this is Chara! We're pinned down, Giana's hit! We need backup, now!

[EXT. JUNGLE / FACILITY PERIMETER - CONTINUOUS]

From a distant, unseen vantage point, a single, precise SHOT rings out. Then another. The sniper fire on Chara and Giana abruptly ceases.

AGENT KLONDIKE (40s, stoic, a man of few words, but his actions speak volumes) emerges from the dense jungle, moving with an almost supernatural quietness. He carries a massive anti-materiel rifle, its barrel still smoking faintly. He takes a quick glance at the penthouse building in the distance, then at Chara and Giana.

KLONDIKE
Two down. Third one's in the penthouse. Too much cover.

Without another word, Klondike unslings a ROCKET LAUNCHER from his back. He aims, a glint of determination in his eyes. The jungle is momentarily illuminated by a fiery WHOOSH as the rocket streaks towards the distant penthouse. A deafening EXPLOSION rocks the air, sending debris raining down. The building shudders, a gaping hole now visible in its upper floors.

Chara stares, momentarily stunned. Stealth is not just compromised; it's obliterated.

CHARA
So much for stealth.

Klondike is already moving towards Giana, assessing her injury with a practiced eye.

KLONDIKE
She needs extraction. Now.

He gently but firmly lifts Giana into his arms. Giana winces but manages a weak nod to Chara.

GIANA
Go, Chara. Finish it.

Klondike gives Chara a brief, reassuring nod. He turns and disappears back into the jungle, carrying Giana with surprising speed. Chara watches them go, then turns back to the facility, her expression hardening. She's alone now, but more determined than ever.
