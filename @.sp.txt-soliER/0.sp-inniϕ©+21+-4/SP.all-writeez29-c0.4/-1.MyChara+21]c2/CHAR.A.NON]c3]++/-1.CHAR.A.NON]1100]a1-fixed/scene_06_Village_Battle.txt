SCENE 6

[EXT. AFROTTA - VILLAGE BATTLE - DAY]

SOUND of distant gunfire, growing rapidly louder, shouts, chaos

The moment of stunned silence, the impossible reunion, shatters. From all directions – the rocky outcrops, the dry riverbed, even from within the village itself – a HAIL OF GUNFIRE erupts. Mercenaries, far more numerous and better equipped than the previous group, pour into the village square.

CHARA reacts instantly, her training kicking in. She shoves ANON behind a sturdy mud-brick wall, drawing her weapon.

CHARA
(Shouting over the din)
Take cover! They're everywhere!

Her two operatives, caught off guard by the sheer volume of the attack, scramble for cover, returning fire. The villagers scream, scattering in terror, adding to the chaotic symphony of war.

AGENT KLONDIKE, despite his age and apparent weariness, moves with a surprising agility. He pulls a hidden pistol from his belt, his eyes scanning the battlefield with a veteran's calm. He fires with deadly precision, dropping two mercenaries before they can even aim.

KLONDIKE
(Voice raspy, but firm)
They're not after us, Chara! They're after her! The asset!

He points to ANON, who is now trying to make sense of the unfolding nightmare. Chara understands. ANON isn't just an informant; she's the key, the target.

Chara lays down suppressing fire, trying to create a path for ANON to move to safer cover. But the mercenaries are relentless, their numbers seemingly endless. They advance with a brutal, coordinated efficiency.

One of Chara's operatives goes down, a sickening THUD as he hits the ground. The other is pinned down, returning fire desperately.

Klondike, seeing the overwhelming odds, makes a decision. He pushes ANON further behind cover, then steps out, drawing their fire. He becomes a shield, a diversion, a target.

KLONDIKE (CONT'D)
(To Chara, his voice strained)
Get her out of here, Chara! Now!

He unleashes a volley of shots, drawing the mercenaries' attention. But there are too many. A burst of automatic fire rips through the air. Klondike stumbles, a dark stain blooming on his chest. He falls to one knee, still firing, defiance in his eyes.

Chara screams his name, a raw, guttural sound. She tries to reach him, but a mercenary lunges at her, forcing her to defend herself. She disarms him with a brutal efficiency, but the distraction costs her precious seconds.

When she looks back, Klondike is on the ground, unmoving. His pistol lies beside him. His eyes are open, staring blankly at the sky. The legend, the mentor, the man presumed dead, is now truly gone. A profound, crushing grief washes over Chara, momentarily paralyzing her.

ANON, seeing Klondike fall, lets out a cry of anguish. She tries to run to him, but Chara grabs her, pulling her back.

CHARA
(Voice choked with emotion)
No! It's too late! We have to go!

But the mercenaries are closing in. Chara is wounded, a searing pain in her side. Her remaining operative is overwhelmed. The fight is lost. The mission is a catastrophic failure.

Chara, bleeding and broken, makes a desperate, last-ditch effort. She shoves ANON towards a narrow alleyway, away from the main assault.

CHARA (CONT'D)
Run! Get out of here! I'll buy you time!

She turns, facing the advancing mercenaries, her weapon raised, a fierce, desperate defiance in her eyes. But her movements are sluggish, her vision blurring. The odds are insurmountable.

She fires, but her shots lack their usual precision. A heavy blow to her head. Darkness encroaches. The last thing she sees is the triumphant, cruel faces of the mercenaries, and the fading image of Klondike's lifeless eyes.
[FADE TO BLACK.]
