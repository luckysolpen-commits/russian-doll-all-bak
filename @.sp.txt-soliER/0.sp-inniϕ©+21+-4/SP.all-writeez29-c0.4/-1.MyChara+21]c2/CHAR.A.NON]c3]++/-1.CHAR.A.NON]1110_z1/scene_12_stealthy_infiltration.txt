[INT. OMNITECH HEADQUARTERS - NIGHT]

ANONYMOUS (hooded, agile) crouches in the shadows outside OmniTech headquarters. He takes a deep breath, his movements silent as he blends into the darkness.

ANONYMOUS (V.O.)
I'm inside.

[INT. NOVASPIRE HEADQUARTERS - NIGHT]

CHARA (focused, determined) moves swiftly through the corridors of NovaSpire HQ, the hum of the air conditioning system barely audible.

CHARA (V.O.)
I'm in too. I've got access to their mainframe.

BACK TO OMNITECH HQ

Anonymous slips past security cameras and motion detectors, his skills honed from years of ghosting. He reaches a terminal and accesses the company's internal systems, downloading sensitive files and spreading disinformation.

BACK TO NOVASPIRE HQ

Chara, using her own set of skills, evades detection. She moves swiftly, accessing terminals and uploading malware that will cripple the corporation's operations.

BOTH SCENES INTERCUT

Chara and Anonymous keep each other updated on their progress via comms link. They know that if one is discovered, the other must be prepared to extract quickly.

CHARA (V.O.)
NovaSpire's mainframe is compromised. I'm spreading disinformation and deleting incriminating files.

ANONYMOUS (V.O.)
OmniTech is going haywire. Their systems are crashing left and right. I think we've got them in a bind.

They continue to work their magic, sowing chaos and confusion within the corporations. They know that once they've gathered enough evidence, they can use it to take down OmniTech and NovaSpire for good.

Suddenly, Anonymous's comms link beeps. He leans in closer to the terminal, his heart racing with excitement and fear.

CHARA (V.O.)
What is it?

Anonymous's eyes scan the data streaming across the screen.

ANONYMOUS (V.O.)
It looks like we've got a problem. The entity... it's getting restless.
