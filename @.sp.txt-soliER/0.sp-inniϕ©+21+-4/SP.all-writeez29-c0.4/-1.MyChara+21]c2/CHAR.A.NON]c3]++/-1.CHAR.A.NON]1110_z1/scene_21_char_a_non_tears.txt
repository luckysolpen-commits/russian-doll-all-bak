[INT. SECURE HACKING LAIR - DAY]

Chara and Anonymous stand amidst the quiet hum of their hacking lair. The screens, once alive with battle, now display a calm, almost serene, digital landscape. The victory is theirs, but a different kind of silence settles between them.

CHARA
So... this is it then?

Anonymous turns to her, his face still partially obscured by the shadows of his hood, but his posture conveys a sense of finality.

ANONYMOUS
Our work here is done. For now.

Chara's heart aches. She knows what this means. The unspoken connection, the shared intensity of their mission, is about to dissolve. She wants to reach out, to say something, anything, to keep him there.

CHARA
I... I don't know what to say.

Anonymous takes a step back, a subtle movement that feels like a chasm opening between them.

ANONYMOUS
There's nothing to say, Chara. We did what was necessary.

His voice is devoid of emotion, a stark contrast to the whirlwind of feelings within her. She realizes, with a painful clarity, that his path is one of solitude, a ghost in the machine, forever elusive.

CHARA
Will I... will I ever hear from you again?

Anonymous pauses, a flicker of something unreadable in his eyes before it vanishes. He doesn't answer directly.

ANONYMOUS
Stay safe, Chara.

And with that, he turns, a shadow melting into the deeper shadows of the lair. The hum of the machines seems to grow louder, filling the void he leaves behind.

Chara stands alone, tears welling in her eyes. She raises a hand, as if to grasp at the fading image of him, but it falls uselessly to her side. A single tear escapes, tracing a path down her cheek.

CHARA
(Whispering, to the empty space)
Wherever you are, Anon... I will always have you.

She closes her eyes, the image of his retreating form burned into her memory. The silence is deafening, broken only by the soft, rhythmic sound of her own quiet sobs.
[FADE TO BLACK.]
