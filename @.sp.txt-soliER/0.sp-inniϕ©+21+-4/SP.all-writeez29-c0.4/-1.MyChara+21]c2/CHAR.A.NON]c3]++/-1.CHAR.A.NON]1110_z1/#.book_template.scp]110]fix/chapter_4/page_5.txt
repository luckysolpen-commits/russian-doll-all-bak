Chara, Marcus, and Zero Cool were ready, anticipating his move, his desperate play.

Zero Cool, meanwhile, used Nexus to counter the sleeper agents' propaganda.

He exposed the Architect's manipulation, his lies, his desperate attempt to regain power.
The world, initially shaken, quickly rallied, their trust in The Weave unwavering.
Chara, meanwhile, focused on the physical threat, the Architect's last resort.
She knew he wouldn't just rely on digital attacks; he was a man of action, of violence.
Her intelligence indicated a direct assault on the Arctic facility, Nexus's physical core.
It was a desperate, suicidal mission, but the Architect was a fanatic, driven by obsession.
Chara mobilized her elite team, those who had faced Erebus, those who understood.

The Architect's forces, a ragtag group of mercenaries and disillusioned Purists, were already there.

Chara led the charge, her movements fluid, precise, a blur of controlled violence.

Her team fought with courage, their training kicking in, their resolve unwavering.
But the Architect's forces were relentless, pushing forward, their numbers seemingly endless.
Chara knew they couldn't hold them off forever; they needed a decisive move, a final strike.
She spotted the Architect, leading the charge, his eyes burning with a fanatical zeal.
