Your mission on Terra Nova begins. The world is a vibrant, yet volatile, planet teetering on the brink of civil war. Your observation post is a discreet orbital platform, allowing you to monitor communications and troop movements. You are tasked with identifying key factions and predicting potential outcomes, reporting back to your Angler superiors.

The political landscape is complex, with warring factions employing primitive but effective weaponry. You witness skirmishes and diplomatic failures, all while adhering to your directive of non-interference unless Imperial interests are directly threatened. This is your role: an unseen hand, shaping destinies from afar.
