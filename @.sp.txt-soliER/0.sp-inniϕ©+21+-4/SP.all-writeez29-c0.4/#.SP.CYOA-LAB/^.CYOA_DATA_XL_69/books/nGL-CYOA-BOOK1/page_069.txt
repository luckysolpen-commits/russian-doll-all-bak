You decide to search for resources or hidden paths indicated by the flora. Recalling how the crystalline lizards interacted with the environment, you examine the glowing mushrooms and the moss. You discover that some of the moss patches, when gently pressed, reveal faint, glowing symbols beneath them – similar to the ones the lizards used.

Following a trail of these symbols, you find a hidden opening in a large, crystalline rock formation. It appears to be a natural tunnel, leading into darkness. It might be a safe passage, or it might lead to unknown dangers.
