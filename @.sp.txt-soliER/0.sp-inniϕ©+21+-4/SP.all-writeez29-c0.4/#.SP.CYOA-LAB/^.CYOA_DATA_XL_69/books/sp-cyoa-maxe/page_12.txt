The Red Queen transports you not to a ship, but to a 'rift' in reality. You find yourself in a desolate, star-dusted void where strange, shadowy figures drift. These are the Veiled Ones, and their leader, Jureth, approaches.

"Welcome, awakened one," Jureth's voice whispers, seemingly from everywhere at once. "You have rejected the Empire's gilded cage. Here, in the Unlit Void, we seek truth beyond control. The Empire fears what it cannot comprehend. Your unique resonance is a threat to their order. Will you join our fight for true freedom?"
