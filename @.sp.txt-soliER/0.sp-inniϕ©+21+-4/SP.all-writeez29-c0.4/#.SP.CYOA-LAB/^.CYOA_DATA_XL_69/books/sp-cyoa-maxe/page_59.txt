You learn about the Noahites, aquatic beings with hydrogen fission antennae from Aqua Noah, and the Niveans, ice-dwelling tentacle people from Glacia Nephilim. They are locked in a "Hydro-Ice Schism" and cold wars over Shells and resources. You could choose to mediate their conflict, ally with one side, or simply gain knowledge of their unique technologies, adding to your understanding of the multiverse's intricate factions.
