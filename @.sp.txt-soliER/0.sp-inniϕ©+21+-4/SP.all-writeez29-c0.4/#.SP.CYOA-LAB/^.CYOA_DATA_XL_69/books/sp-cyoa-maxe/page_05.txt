You sit on the cold floor, awaiting your end. As the guards enter, you close your eyes. You expect pain, but instead, you feel a strange peace. The Red Queen herself enters the cell. She looks at you, not with anger, but with a flicker of something unreadable.

"You are... different," she says, her voice lacking its usual amplification. "You have seen what I have seen. You understand." She pauses. "Perhaps your fate is not execution. Perhaps it is something... more."

She extends her hand. Do you take it?
