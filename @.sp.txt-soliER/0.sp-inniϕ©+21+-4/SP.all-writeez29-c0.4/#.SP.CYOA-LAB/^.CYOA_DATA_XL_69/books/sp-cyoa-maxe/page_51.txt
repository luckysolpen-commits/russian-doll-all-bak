Commander Vorith is a figure of cold efficiency. You learn through hushed conversations and observation that Vorith is known for handling 'anomalies' – individuals or phenomena that defy the Empire's established order. They are said to be brilliant tacticians and utterly ruthless, prioritizing the Empire's mission above all else. Your training under Vorith promises to be a trial by fire.

*   If you embrace the training and seek to impress Vorith, turn to page 50.
*   If you see Vorith as a potential obstacle to your own freedom, turn to page 24 (considering rebellion).
