You look around your cell. The walls are made of smooth, seamless metal. There is no window, no door, only a small slot in the floor for food and water. You are about to give up hope when you see it. A small, almost invisible seam in the wall. You run your fingers over it and feel a slight vibration. It's a maintenance hatch.

You spend the next few hours working on the hatch. You use your fingernails to pry it open, and then you squeeze through the narrow opening. You find yourself in a series of dark, dusty tunnels. You don't know where you are going, but you know that you have to keep moving.

You are a clown now, a ghost in the machine. Your adventure has just begun.

You crawl through the tunnels for what feels like hours. You are hungry and thirsty, but you can't stop. You know that the Red Queen's guards are probably searching for you.

Finally, you see a faint light ahead. You crawl towards it and find yourself looking through a grate into a large, open space. It's a hangar bay. There are several ships parked in the bay, including a small, sleek freighter.

What do you do?
