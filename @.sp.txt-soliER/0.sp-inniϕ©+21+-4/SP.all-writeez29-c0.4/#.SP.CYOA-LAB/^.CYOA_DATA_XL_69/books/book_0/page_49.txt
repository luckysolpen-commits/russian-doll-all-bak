You train as a spy. You learn how to move in the shadows, how to gather information, and how to kill without being seen. You are a ghost, a whisper in the dark. You are sent on dozens of missions, from infiltrating Red Queen's bases to assassinating her top commanders. You are one of the rebellion's most valuable assets.

One day, you are given your most dangerous mission yet. You are to infiltrate the Red Queen's personal chambers and assassinate her. It is a suicide mission, but you accept it without hesitation.

You make your way to the Red Queen's chambers. You find her alone, unguarded. She looks at you, and for a moment, you see not a tyrant, but a lonely woman. You hesitate for a moment, then you remember all the people she has killed, all the worlds she has conquered. You raise your weapon and you fire.

The Red Queen is dead. The war is over. You have saved the galaxy.

THE END
