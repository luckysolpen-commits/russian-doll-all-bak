You decide to continue exploring the tunnels. You are not a saboteur, you are a survivor. You will continue to live in the shadows, to watch and to wait. You will learn the Red Queen's secrets, and one day, you will use them against her.

You spend months, maybe even years, in the tunnels. You become a part of the facility, a ghost that no one knows is there. You learn the layout of the entire complex, the patrol routes of the guards, the location of every camera and sensor. You are the ultimate spy.

One day, you discover the Red Queen's greatest secret. She is not a single person, but a collective consciousness of all the clones who have ever served her. She is a hive mind, and she is immortal.

You also discover her greatest weakness. There is a central core, a nexus, where her consciousness is stored. If you can destroy the nexus, you can destroy the Red Queen forever.

What do you do?
