You try to negotiate a peaceful settlement with the Empire. You meet with the Imperial commander, a cold and calculating woman named Admiral Thorne. You offer her a deal: the rebels will lay down their arms in exchange for amnesty and a promise that the Empire will respect the native people's way of life. Thorne agrees to your terms. But as soon as the rebels have disarmed, she orders her troops to attack. It is a massacre. You have been betrayed.

* If you manage to escape the massacre, turn to page 55.
* If you are captured by Admiral Thorne, turn to page 56.
