You take the toy to the barracks' quartermaster, an old, grizzled clone who has seen everything. You ask him if he knows anything about it. He takes one look at the toy and his eyes go wide.

"Where did you get this?" he whispers, his voice trembling. He tells you that it is a "spirit stone," an ancient artifact of the people who lived on this planet before the Empire arrived. He says that the stones are said to contain the memories and emotions of their owners.

He warns you to be careful. The Empire has been trying to destroy all the spirit stones, because they are a symbol of hope for the rebellion.
