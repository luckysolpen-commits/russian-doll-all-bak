You are a clone. Blending in is what you were made for. You pull the hood of your suit up to obscure the standard markings on your helmet and walk into the settlement.

The "town" is a chaotic, vibrant mess. Music blares from jury-rigged speakers. Clones with wildly painted armor and customized suits haggle over goods in a makeshift market. The air smells of ozone, cheap booze, and cooked food. Someone has managed to grow something that smells like onions.

You keep your head down and walk towards a stall selling roasted Martian grubs. You're starving. As you're about to order, a large, heavily built clone with a handlebar mustache painted on his helmet claps you on the shoulder.

"Haven't seen you around before, brother," he says, his voice a friendly boom. "Welcome to Scraptown! What's your story? Another deserter from the Red Queen's meat grinder?"

He's friendly, but everyone is watching. This is a test. Your answer will determine whether you are welcomed or thrown out into the wilderness.
