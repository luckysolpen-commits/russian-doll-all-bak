You're a trained soldier, not a scared scientist. You draw your sidearm and cautiously approach the dark corner of the lab from which the skittering sound emerged.

As you get closer, a smell hits you - ozone and rot. You peer behind a bank of overturned equipment. In the shadows, something moves. It's one of the scientists, or what's left of him. He is covered in the pulsating, glowing fungus, which seems to have integrated with his body. Wires from a nearby console are fused to his skull. His jaw hangs open, and his eyes are wide, unblinking pools of black.

He is not the source of the skittering. The sound is coming from his fingers, which are twitching and spasming at an unnatural speed, tapping against the metal floor. He seems to be trying to communicate, tapping out a message in some unknown code.

Then you see that the fungus around him is spreading. Tendrils of it are creeping across the floor towards you. The body of the scientist lurches, and a low moan escapes his lips. It sounds like a word. "Run."
