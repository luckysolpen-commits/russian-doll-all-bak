The Empire is eating itself. It's time to give the clowns a fork.

You use a series of dead drops and encrypted channels to leak the information about the impending assassination of The Proctor. You send it to a dozen different rebel cells, ensuring that the information will spread like wildfire.

The result is glorious chaos. The rebellion uses the information to sow discord and mistrust within the Imperial ranks. The Solar Empire and the Blood Queen Monarchy, already suspicious of each other, are pushed to the brink of open conflict. The Proctor, now aware of the plot against him, goes on the offensive, purging his own ranks and launching a series of devastating shadow attacks against his political rivals.

The Empire is so busy fighting itself that the rebellion is able to launch a series of successful offensives. They capture three key star systems and liberate a dozen worlds. You have single-handedly changed the course of the war.

But you have also made a powerful enemy. The Proctor is not a fool. He knows that someone on the inside leaked the information. He begins a ruthless, systematic hunt for the mole. Your position is more precarious than ever.
