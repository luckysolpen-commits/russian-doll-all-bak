The town scrambles into action. The defenses are meager, the clowns are disorganized. But they have spirit.

The Imperial scout car, seeing that it has been spotted, does not attack. It retreats, disappearing back into the desert.

You have saved the town from a surprise attack. But you have also confirmed its location to the Empire. A larger force will be coming. And the clowns know it.

Gus claps you on the shoulder. "Good work, kid," he says. "You may be a liar, but you've got good eyes."

You have earned a measure of trust. But you have also brought a death sentence upon Scraptown. You have a choice to make. You can stay and fight with them, or you can use the chaos to slip away and save yourself.
