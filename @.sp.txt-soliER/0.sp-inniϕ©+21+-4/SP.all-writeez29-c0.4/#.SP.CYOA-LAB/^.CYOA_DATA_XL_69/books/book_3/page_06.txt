"This is Clone Patrol 700," you broadcast on an open channel, your voice steady. "You are in a restricted zone and are confirmed to have engaged in hostile action against a corporate facility. Power down your weapons and surrender. Acknowledged military reinforcements are two minutes out."

It's a complete bluff. The only thing two minutes out is a long, lonely tunnel.

There's a burst of static, then a laugh. It's not a cruel laugh, but one of genuine amusement. "Military reinforcements? Honey, the only thing coming for you is us. But I like your style. Gutsy." The voice is female, with a wry, confident tone.

One of the clown ships powers down its weapon mount. A figure in a patched-up enviro-suit emerges, waving a hand in a gesture of non-aggression. The helmet is decorated with a crude, smiling face.

"Look, 'Clone Patrol 700'," the voice crackles again, "we're just... liberating some research materials. Redplanet Labs was doing things in there... things you wouldn't approve of, I guarantee. We've got what we came for. You can let us go, and we all live to fight another day. Or you can try to be a hero, and we'll see whose scrap heap is bigger."

From the corner of your eye, you see movement from the other ship. It's not powering down. It's maneuvering into a flanking position.
