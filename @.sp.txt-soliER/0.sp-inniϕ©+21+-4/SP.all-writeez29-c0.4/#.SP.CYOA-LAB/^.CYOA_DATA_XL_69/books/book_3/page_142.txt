You are a clown. You are a thief. You are a liberator.

You are part of an elite team of clown operatives, each a master of their craft. You infiltrate the Martian Trust Bank, a fortress of steel and fire, deep within a heavily fortified Imperial city.

Your mission is to extract financial data, not credits. You bypass security systems, neutralize guards, and delve into the bank's central servers. You find it: a vast network of corruption, bribery, and money laundering that implicates the highest levels of the Red Queen's government.

You extract the data, making sure to leave no trace of your presence. You escape into the Martian night, your mission accomplished.

The data you have stolen is a bombshell. The rebellion leaks it to the galactic media, exposing the Red Queen's corruption to the entire solar system. The Empire is rocked to its foundations. Protests erupt on every planet. The Red Queen's authority is shattered.

You have struck a critical blow against the Empire. You are a hero of the rebellion, a legend in the making.
