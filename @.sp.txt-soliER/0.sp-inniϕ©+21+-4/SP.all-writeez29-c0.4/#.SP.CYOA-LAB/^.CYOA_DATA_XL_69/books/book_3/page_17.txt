There is no hesitation. You are a weapon, and a weapon's purpose is to be used.

You are transported back to Mars in a black, unmarked ship that belongs to the Blood Queen's personal fleet. You are assigned a new identity: a low-level security officer in the Solar Empire's diplomatic corps. Your target is a man named Cassian, a trade envoy who has been leaking information to the Clown Anarchist Party.

The transformation is your secret weapon. You spend your days as a quiet, observant guard, mapping the layout of the diplomatic residence, learning Cassian's routines, and identifying the optimal time and place for the strike. The beast inside you is a coiled spring, waiting for the signal to be released.

The moment comes during a private reception. Cassian is in his penthouse office with two other officials. This is it. Your handler gives the signal through a sub-vocal comm.

You find a secluded spot, and you let go. The transformation is brutally fast. Your bones snap and reform, your skin splits and is replaced by hardened chitin. The world explodes into a symphony of new senses. You can smell the ozone from the office electronics, taste the faint tang of fear in the recycled air. You are the Carcazod.

You burst through the wall into the office, a monster of claws and teeth. The two officials scream. Cassian just stares, his face pale with the sudden, horrific understanding of what he's looking at.
