Curiosity kills the clone. But it also uncovers the truth.

You ignore the instruction to simply erase the flag. You delve deeper into the Starpath Transit network, bypassing more security protocols. You find the manifest for the "medical supplies" from Redplanet Labs. You cross-reference the shipment with internal RPL documents.

What you find makes your blood run cold. It's not medical supplies. It's a shipment of highly mutagenic bioweapons, specifically designed to accelerate Carcazod transformation in clones, and to induce violent, uncontrollable mutations in anyone else. It's Project Chimera, weaponized.

This is not a trap for the rebellion. It's a plague.

You immediately contact Zi. "It's a trap!" you broadcast, your voice urgent. "The RPL shipment is a bioweapon. It will turn anyone who touches it into a raging monster."

There's a moment of stunned silence from Zi. "You're sure?"

"I have the manifest," you reply, sending her the data. "They're trying to wipe out an entire sector."

Zi is quick to act. She reroutes the rebel forces, allowing the transport to pass unmolested. The plague shipment arrives at its intended destination: a heavily populated, neutral mining colony. The devastation is unimaginable.

You have saved the rebellion, but at a terrible cost. Thousands of innocent lives are lost.

"We owe you our lives, 700," Zi says, her voice grim. "But now... now we have a war to fight. A real war. One against bio-terrorists."

You have proven your loyalty to the rebellion in the most horrific way possible. You are no longer just a slicer. You are a hero.
