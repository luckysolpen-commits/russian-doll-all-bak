You are not a pawn. You are not a tool. You refuse.

"I am no one's personal agent," you say, your voice firm. "I serve the Empire, not a single individual."

The Proctor smiles, a cold, humorless expression. "A pity," he says. "I had hoped you would be more... flexible. But loyalty, misplaced or otherwise, is still loyalty."

You are dismissed. But you know this is not the end. The Proctor does not tolerate insubordination. You are now a loose end, and he will tie you up.

You immediately begin to plan your escape. You use your knowledge of Imperial protocols and security systems to create a diversion. You steal a small, unmarked courier ship and blast your way out of the Lunar base, leaving behind a trail of confused security forces.

You are a fugitive, once again. But this time, you are running from a much more dangerous enemy. The Proctor's reach is long, and his resources are vast. You will have to disappear completely.
