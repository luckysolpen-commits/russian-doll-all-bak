You are a clone. You were designed to be part of a collective. This is just a different kind of collective.

"I accept," you transmit, not with your voice, but with your thoughts.

The fungus surges forward. It covers you, envelops you. But it does not harm you. It merges with you. You feel a million new senses, a million new thoughts. You are no longer just Clone 700. You are a host, a partner, a symbiont.

The fungus, which calls itself "Mycel," shows you how to integrate it into your own biology. You can now communicate with it telepathically. You can draw on its vast processing power. You can even use it to interface with computer systems.

You are something new. Something powerful. Something the Empire would kill to control, and the clowns would kill out of fear.

You leave the lab, your new partner a silent, invisible presence within you. You have a choice to make. You can return to the Empire, hiding your new abilities and using them for your own advancement. Or you can seek out the rebellion, hoping that they will see you as an ally, not a monster.
