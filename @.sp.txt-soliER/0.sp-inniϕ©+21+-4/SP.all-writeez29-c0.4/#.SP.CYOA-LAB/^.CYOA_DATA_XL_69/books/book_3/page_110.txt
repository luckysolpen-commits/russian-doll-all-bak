Curiosity, dormant for so long, reawakens. You have to know what lies at the end of this ancient map.

You spend years meticulously repairing the ancient drone, reverse-engineering its long-dead technology. It is a slow, painstaking process, but you have nothing but time.

Finally, the drone is operational. You input the coordinates from the map. It is a long journey, taking you to a hidden cavern deep beneath the Martian polar ice caps. A place of unimaginable power.

You find it: a vast, crystalline chamber, filled with strange, glowing machinery. In the center, a massive conduit stretches up into the Martian crust. It is a terraforming engine, an ancient artifact capable of transforming Mars into a lush, green world.

You have a choice to make. You can activate the engine, transforming Mars and bringing life to the dead planet. Or you can destroy it, fearing the unknown consequences of such immense power.
