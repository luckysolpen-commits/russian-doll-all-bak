You are a ghost. You need to disappear. The asteroid belt is the perfect place for a ghost to haunt.

You push the shuttle's damaged engines to their limit, setting a course for the vast, empty expanse between Mars and Jupiter. You have enough fuel for one long burn. After that, you'll be drifting.

Life in the belt is hard. You learn to catch stray asteroids for their mineral and ice content. You become a scavenger, picking through the bones of ancient shipwrecks. You trade with the strange, insular communities of "rock rats" who have made the belt their home.

You are no longer a clone. You are a creature of the void, a silent, solitary figure navigating a universe of rock and ice. You have escaped the war, escaped the Empire, escaped yourself. You are free.

But one day, you stumble upon a discovery. A derelict ship, ancient and of unknown design. Inside, you find the remains of its crew, not human, not clone. And you find a starmap that shows a route to a place far beyond the known boundaries of the system. A place that could change everything.
