Page 88: Exploring the Active Transformation Chambers

The active transformation chambers are not the failed experiments or research facilities you expected, but working laboratories where successful carcazod transformations are being completed. Your sensors detect three distinct chamber types: The Accelerated Metamorphosis Chambers, where transformation processes that normally take years are compressed into days; The Consciousness Integration Labs, where awareness is modified to accommodate transformed physical forms; and The Environmental Adaptation Spheres, where transformed beings are tested in conditions that match the full range of Martian environments.

As you observe the chambers, you realize that the beings undergoing transformation here are not rogues or failures but carefully selected individuals who have been approved for transformation by an unknown authority. The success rate in these chambers approaches 100%, suggesting a level of understanding that exceeds all faction research programs.

Your analysis reveals that the transformation process in these chambers is fully reversible and controllable, allowing subjects to maintain their original identity while gaining transformation benefits. The chambers represent the perfected form of carcazod transformation technology.

The subjects in the chambers appear to be volunteers who have chosen transformation rather than being forced into it. They show no signs of distress or conflict during their transformation, instead experiencing what appears to be a natural evolution of their capabilities.

You begin to suspect that these chambers are part of a larger program that predates the faction conflicts and represents the original Martian approach to transformation.
