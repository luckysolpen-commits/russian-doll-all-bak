Page 63: Attacking the High Vantage Command Unit

You direct your drone's weapons toward the high vantage command unit that's coordinating the ambush. Your targeting systems lock onto the position, revealing it to be a sophisticated command post built into the tunnel wall, with multiple defensive systems and clear sightlines to all approaches.

As you engage, you discover that the command unit is not just a simple control post but a mobile command center that can reposition itself within the tunnel system. Three key elements of the unit become apparent: The Strategic Control Hub, where the ambush coordination is managed through advanced tactical systems; The Communication Nexus, which maintains contact with all ambush elements across the tunnel network; and The Personal Security Detail, where the unit's commander is protected by elite carcazod-enhanced guards.

Your weapons fire alerts the command unit to your presence, and it begins to relocate to a new vantage point. The unit's mobility systems activate, revealing it to be a transformed tunnel section that can move through the underground like a living organism.

You realize that this is no ordinary ambush - it's being coordinated by someone with access to mobile command technology that predates the current faction systems. The sophistication suggests involvement from the ancient Martian transformation programs or possibly a successful carcazod with command abilities.

The transport convoy you're protecting remains in danger as the command unit relocates. Your decision to engage the command post directly has forced it to reveal capabilities that weren't apparent from your initial scan.
