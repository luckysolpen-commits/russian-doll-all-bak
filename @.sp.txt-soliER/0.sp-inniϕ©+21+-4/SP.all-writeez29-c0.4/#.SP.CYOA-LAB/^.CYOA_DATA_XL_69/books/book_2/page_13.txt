Page 13: Guide of the Unmapped Tunnels

"You've chosen the path of the navigator," says your patchwork-clothed guide. "The unmapped depths of Mars hold countless secrets, and those who master them become the true lords of the underground. Few beings venture where you'll go, and fewer return with their sanity intact."

You're given a specialized navigation drone and a map that's mostly blank spaces marked with warnings in several different languages and symbol systems. Your guide explains: "Three regions have become accessible recently due to geological shifts: the Ancient River Complex, where pre-human tunnels may hold the keys to carcazod transformation; the Quantum Caves, where space and time behave strangely; and the Deep Thermal Vents, where the boundary between the natural and artificial worlds has broken down."

The other members of the guide network are beings who've spent years in the unmapped regions – some have developed enhanced sensory organs to navigate in complete darkness, others have learned to read the subtle vibrations in tunnel walls that indicate various conditions. All have one thing in common: they know paths through the underground that don't appear on any official maps.

Your first assignment is to explore one of these regions and establish a safe route for future travelers. The choice of destination will determine what kind of knowledge you gain and what kind of dangers you'll face in the depths.

The guide hands you a device that pulses with bioluminescent light. "This will help you find your way back, but the real navigation tools are between your ears. Trust your instincts, and remember – the tunnels themselves want to be mapped, but only by those who respect their ancient mysteries."
