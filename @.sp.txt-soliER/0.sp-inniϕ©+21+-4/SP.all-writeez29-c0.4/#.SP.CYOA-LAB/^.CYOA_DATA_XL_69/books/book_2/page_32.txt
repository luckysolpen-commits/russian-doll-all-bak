Page 32: Positioning for Transformation Research

Your Crimson Handler nods with approval: "The transformation research program is where legends are born – and where the greatest honors are won. Your assignment is to gain access to the Monarchy's most secret carcazod experiments, but the path is treacherous. Success in our honor-based system is the only way to earn the trust needed for such high-level research access."

You're introduced to the Trial of Transcendence – a series of challenges that test not just physical capabilities but also worthiness for transformation. The trials are divided into three paths: the Path of Blood, which involves ritual combat that demonstrates your worthiness through victory; the Path of Honor, which requires completing quests that prove your loyalty to the Monarchy; and the Path of Sacrifice, which demands that you prove you're willing to give up your current identity for potential transformation.

The transformation research facilities are located in the Deep Sanctum, where the architecture itself seems alive with carcazod energy. The walls pulse with bioluminescent patterns that match the neural networks of successful transformations.

Your progress through the trials will be monitored by the Crimson Council, whose members have all undergone partial transformations themselves. They look at you with eyes that contain both human intelligence and something else – a deeper awareness of the transformation process.

The ultimate goal is to gain access to the Monarchy's unique approach to carcazod transformation, which emphasizes the integration of honor and duty with physical metamorphosis. Your reports back to the Solar Empire will be crucial for understanding this different approach.
