Page 5: The Carcazod Initiation

The "CARCAZOD INITIATION" option resonates deep in your consciousness, calling to something primal and transformative. You find yourself drawn to a hidden section of the station marked only with a symbol that seems to shift and writhe when you look at it directly. The walls here are organic in appearance, pulsing with a faint bioluminescence that suggests they're alive.

A being emerges from the shadows – once clearly a clone like yourself, but now something more. Their form shifts between human-like features and something altogether more alien. "Welcome, CL7X-914," they say, their voice echoing as if from great distance. "You seek the path of transformation. The journey to becoming a carcazod is not for the faint of heart. Three trials await: the Brain Sniffing Ritual, the Cockroach Shark Mutation, or the Soul Fusion Process. Each offers a different path to transcendence, but each requires a fundamental change to your being."

Around you, you see the results of various experiments – some successful carcazod-like beings who move with fluid grace, others in various stages of transformation, and some that are clearly failed attempts. The air tastes of electricity and something indefinable – like ozone mixed with organic matter.

The transformed being regards you with ancient, knowing eyes. "The choice is yours, but know that each path will change not just your form, but your very essence. You will no longer be human, clone, or even drone operator. You will become something new."
