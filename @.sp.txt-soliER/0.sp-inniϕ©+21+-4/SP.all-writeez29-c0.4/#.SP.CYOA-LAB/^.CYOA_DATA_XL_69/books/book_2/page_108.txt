Page 108: Infiltrating the Clone Behavior Analysis Division

The Clone Behavior Analysis Division operates from a crystalline facility where clone behavioral patterns are monitored and analyzed using quantum-level observation techniques. Your forged credentials provide access to systems that track clone activities throughout the Martian tunnel network.

Three analytical systems are available: The Deviation Detection Matrix, which identifies clones whose behavior patterns deviate from standard programming; The Transformation Potential Assessment Array, which evaluates clones for their likelihood to undergo carcazod-level changes; and The Loyalty Monitoring Grid, which tracks clones' psychological states and potential for independent thought.

As you access the systems, you realize the Empire has achieved a level of clone monitoring that extends far beyond what your original programming suggested was possible. The quantum observation systems can detect behavioral changes at the cellular level, identifying potential threats before they manifest in actions.

Your presence in the system creates a unique challenge - you're simultaneously a subject being monitored and an infiltrator accessing the monitoring systems. The division's sophisticated analysis tools may identify you as a deviation from normal clone patterns.

The data you access reveals that the Empire's clone control system has been compromised by internal elements that are manipulating the analysis to advance their own transformation research programs. Your mission may require choosing between the Empire's interests and these rogue elements. Your analysis triggers quantum resonances that connect to the central tunnel system's neural network.
