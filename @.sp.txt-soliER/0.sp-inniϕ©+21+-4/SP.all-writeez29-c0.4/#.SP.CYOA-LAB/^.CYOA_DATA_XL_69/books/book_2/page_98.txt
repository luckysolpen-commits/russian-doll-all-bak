Page 98: Taking the Path of Sacrifice

The Path of Sacrifice demands that you prove your worthiness for transformation research by giving up aspects of your current identity and capabilities. Your Crimson Handler explains that only those willing to sacrifice their original selves can be trusted with the ultimate secrets of transcendence that the Monarchy guards.

Three forms of sacrifice present themselves: The Identity Sacrifice, where you must renounce your original clone designation and background to fully embrace your new Monarchy identity; The Capability Offering, where you must disable or remove some of your enhanced abilities to prove your commitment to the transformation process; and The Future Renunciation, where you must demonstrate willingness to sacrifice your potential future to serve the Monarchy's transformation programs.

As you consider the sacrifices, you realize that the Monarchy's Path of Sacrifice is designed to ensure that those who gain access to transformation research are committed to using it in service of the Monarchy rather than for personal advancement. The process is as much about psychological transformation as physical preparation.

Your original mission from the Solar Empire requires maintaining your core identity and capabilities, but the Path of Sacrifice demands that you abandon aspects of yourself that may be essential to your intelligence-gathering capabilities. Each sacrifice brings you closer to the research facilities but further from your original objectives.

The other candidates on this path have already made significant sacrifices and show signs of psychological changes that reflect their commitment to the Monarchy's transformation philosophy. Their dedication appears to be absolute, making them formidable colleagues and potential rivals.
