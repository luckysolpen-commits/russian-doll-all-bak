Page 81: Leading the Direct Assault Through the Main Entrance

Colonel Argent's voice crackles over the comm: "Solar Guard, advance as planned. Let them see the power of the Empire." Your team moves toward the main entrance of the Blood Queen Monarchy outpost, where the crimson and gold architecture makes clear this is a fortress designed for both honor and combat.

As you approach, three distinct defensive systems activate: The Honor Gate Guardians, elite soldiers who challenge all intruders to formal combat before allowing passage; The Crimson Defense Grid, an energy barrier that can be selectively permeable based on honor recognition protocols; and The Golden Sentry Network, automated systems that supplement the organic defenses with technological precision.

Your Solar Empire forces must adapt to the Monarchy's honor-based defensive systems while maintaining offensive momentum. The Blood Queen's forces seem to be welcoming the direct assault, as if they've prepared for exactly this type of confrontation.

As combat erupts, you realize the outpost's defenses are not just technological but cultural - the very concept of honor is being weaponized against intruders who don't understand the formal combat protocols that govern Monarchy military operations.

Your team's augmented efficiency clashes with the Monarchy's ritualized combat systems, creating opportunities and dangers that neither side fully anticipated. The direct assault has forced the conflict into the open, but on the defender's cultural terms rather than purely military ones.
