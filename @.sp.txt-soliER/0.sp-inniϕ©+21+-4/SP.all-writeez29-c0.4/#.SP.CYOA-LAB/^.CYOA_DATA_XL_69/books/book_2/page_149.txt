Page 149: Pursuing Resilience Engineering

The Cockroach Shark Mutation chamber completes the Survival Metamorphosis through Resilience Engineering, giving your form the ability to repair itself from almost any damage and return to functionality. Your biological and mechanical systems become self-repairing and self-improving.

Three resilience capabilities emerge: Damage Recovery Systems, where your form automatically repairs biological and mechanical damage at accelerated rates; Structural Adaptation Protocols, allowing your body to modify its structure in response to damage to maintain functionality; and Regenerative Enhancement Engineering, enabling your systems to not just repair but improve themselves through the healing process.

As the resilience engineering completes, you experience the sensation of being able to recover from any injury or damage - your systems automatically identify damage, initiate repairs, and adapt to prevent similar damage in the future.

Your enhanced resilience allows you to survive attacks and conditions that would be fatal to normal beings, continue operating effectively even while damaged, and become stronger and more capable through the healing process.

The resilience engineering provides unprecedented durability, but also requires energy and resources for the repair processes and may make you overconfident in dangerous situations due to your enhanced survival capabilities.
