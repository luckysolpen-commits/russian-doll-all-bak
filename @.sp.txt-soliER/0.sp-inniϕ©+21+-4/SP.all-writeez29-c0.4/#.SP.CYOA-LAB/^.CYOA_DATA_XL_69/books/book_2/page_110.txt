Page 110: Targeting the Carcazod Intelligence Cell

The Carcazod Intelligence Cell operates from a bio-mechanical facility where the boundaries between biological and technological systems have been completely dissolved. This is the Empire's most sophisticated effort to understand and control the transformation process, combining ancient Martian technology with advanced faction research.

Three intelligence operations are active: The Transformation Tracking Network, which monitors all carcazod-related activities throughout the Martian system; The Infiltration Detection Array, which identifies beings with transformation capabilities operating within faction territories; and The Control Development Lab, where methods for managing and directing transformed beings are researched and tested.

As you study the cell's operations, you realize that the Empire's approach to carcazod intelligence is more sophisticated than any other faction's efforts. They've achieved a comprehensive understanding of transformation patterns and capabilities that allows them to predict and influence transformation-related events.

Your presence in the cell puts you in contact with intelligence about transformation that exceeds the knowledge of most high-ranking officials. However, the cell's focus on control and management of transformed beings may conflict with your own transformation goals.

The bio-mechanical systems of the facility respond to your presence with unusual intensity, possibly recognizing your own transformation potential or your true mission objectives. The facility itself may be a form of living intelligence connected to the transformation process.
