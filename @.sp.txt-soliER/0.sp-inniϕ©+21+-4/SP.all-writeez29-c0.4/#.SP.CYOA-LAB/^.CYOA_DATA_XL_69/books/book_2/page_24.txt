Page 24: Infiltrating the Clown Hunting Operation

Your stealth drone brings you to the site of an active clown hunting operation in the Central Zone. The scene is unlike anything in your training – beings who appear to be identical to you are systematically tracking and capturing other beings that have the same basic form but different modifications. The hunters wear a mixture of equipment from all four factions, suggesting this is a truly neutral operation that transcends standard loyalties.

As you observe from concealment, you identify three distinct aspects of the operation that you could investigate: the command structure that coordinates the hunts, the capture methods that seem to involve advanced neural net technology, or the processing facility where captured clowns are taken for what appears to be reconditioning or questioning.

Your stealth systems detect unusual electromagnetic signatures that match the carcazod transformation chambers you've heard about but never seen. The hunters move with a precision that suggests they've been enhanced through some kind of metamorphosis process.

A communication intercept reveals that this operation is being funded by elements from all four factions – they're working together to eliminate rogue clowns who threaten the stability of the entire Martian system. However, your sensor readings suggest that some of these "clowns" may actually be important political figures or research scientists who've gone missing from their respective factions.

The operation's sophistication implies that high-level officials from each faction are aware of and supporting this joint effort, despite the official political tensions between them.
