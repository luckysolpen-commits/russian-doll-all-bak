Page 109: Joining the Cross-Faction Monitoring Section

The Cross-Faction Monitoring Section operates from a neutral facility where intelligence from all four factions is collected, compared, and analyzed for patterns and connections that individual factions might miss. Your credentials grant access to information that no single faction fully possesses.

Three monitoring capabilities are available: The Pattern Recognition Network, which identifies connections and correlations across faction activities; The Joint Operation Tracker, which monitors collaborative efforts between factions that aren't officially acknowledged; and The Transformation Research Monitor, which specifically tracks carcazod development across all faction programs.

As you integrate into the monitoring section, you realize that this facility represents the true center of Martian intelligence operations. The cross-faction analysis reveals the extent of joint operations and shared research that exists beneath the surface of apparent faction conflicts.

Your position provides access to intelligence that could fundamentally change your understanding of Martian politics. However, you must maintain your cover while potentially discovering information that contradicts your original mission briefing.

The section's personnel operate under strict neutrality protocols, which may offer protection for your true loyalties while providing unprecedented access to faction secrets. Your role is to observe and report, not to influence the operations you're monitoring.
