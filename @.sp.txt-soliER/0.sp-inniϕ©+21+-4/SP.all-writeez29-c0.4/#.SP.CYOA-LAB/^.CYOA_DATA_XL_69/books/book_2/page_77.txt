Page 77: Breaching the Quantum Communication Array

The quantum communication array is far more sophisticated than any communication system you've encountered - it doesn't just transmit information but allows for quantum entanglement communication that transcends normal space-time limitations. Your drone's systems struggle to comprehend its full capabilities as you attempt to breach it.

Three distinct communication networks are accessible: The Inter-Faction Quantum Link, which maintains real-time communication between the representatives' governments across vast distances; The Carcazod Monitoring Network, which tracks transformation activities throughout the Martian system; and The Off-World Communication Hub, which suggests the project has implications extending beyond Mars itself.

As you attempt to breach the array, you realize it's not just being monitored but actively controlled from multiple locations simultaneously. The quantum entanglement effects mean that the communication transcends normal interception methods - you're not just breaching a local system but accessing quantum states that exist throughout the entangled network.

Your sensors detect that the array is receiving input not just from Mars but from quantum communication relays throughout the solar system. The scope of Project Carcazod Supremacy may extend far beyond Martian politics to include off-world interests and capabilities.

The breach attempt triggers quantum-level responses throughout the network. The representatives in the main conference may be unaware of your presence, but the quantum communication system itself appears to be aware of the intrusion attempt.
