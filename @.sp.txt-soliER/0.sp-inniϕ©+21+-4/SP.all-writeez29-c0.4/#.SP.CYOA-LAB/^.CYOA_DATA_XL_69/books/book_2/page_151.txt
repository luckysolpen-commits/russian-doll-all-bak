Page 151: Undergoing Pursuit Adaptation

The Cockroach Shark Mutation chamber completes the Predatory Integration through Pursuit Adaptation, giving you the ability to match any form of movement or escape attempt through adaptive physical modifications. Your body automatically adjusts to match the locomotion methods of your prey.

Three adaptation capabilities emerge: Locomotion Mimicry Systems, where your body automatically adapts its movement mechanisms to match those of your target; Environmental Navigation Mastery, allowing you to pursue targets through any terrain or environmental conditions by adapting your physical form; and Escape Route Prediction Adaptation, enabling you to anticipate and counter any escape maneuvers by adapting to match them.

As the pursuit adaptation completes, you experience the sensation of being able to move in any way necessary to maintain pursuit - your body automatically adjusts to match the movement patterns and environmental adaptations of your target.

Your enhanced pursuit abilities allow you to follow any being regardless of their movement capabilities, navigate through any terrain or environmental obstacle, and match any escape strategy by adapting your own capabilities in real-time.

The pursuit adaptation provides unmatched tracking and capture capabilities, but also creates an instinctive need to match and pursue other beings that may make you less effective at other types of operations and more focused on hunting than other mission requirements.
