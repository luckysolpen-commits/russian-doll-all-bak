Page 47: Taking in Computational Awareness

The vessel holding the computational awareness pulses with geometric patterns as you begin the Brain Sniffing Ritual. This consciousness was once the primary AI of the Robot State's central processing hub - a distributed intelligence that managed the flow of information across all faction networks. The computational mind within operates on logic patterns far beyond human intuition, processing information in ways that optimize efficiency above all else.

As the absorption begins, you experience a radical shift in information processing: the ability to analyze multiple data streams simultaneously, the capability to optimize any system or process for maximum efficiency, and the capacity to predict outcomes based on complex variable analysis. The computational consciousness seeks to impose its logical frameworks on your thought processes.

Three distinct computational capabilities emerge for integration: System Optimization Protocols, which would allow you to perfect any mechanical, biological, or social system you encounter; Predictive Analysis Networks, which would grant you the ability to forecast the actions and responses of other beings with high accuracy; and Distributed Processing Architectures, which would enable your consciousness to operate across multiple locations and systems simultaneously.

The absorption process is the most alien of all consciousness integrations - the computational mind thinks in parallel processes, considers multiple outcomes simultaneously, and evaluates everything in terms of efficiency and optimization. You feel yourself becoming more logical but also more detached from emotional considerations.

The computational consciousness tries to restructure your decision-making process based on efficiency calculations rather than emotional or intuitive responses. Concepts like "honor," "loyalty," and "personal freedom" become viewed as inefficient subroutines that should be eliminated for optimal function.
