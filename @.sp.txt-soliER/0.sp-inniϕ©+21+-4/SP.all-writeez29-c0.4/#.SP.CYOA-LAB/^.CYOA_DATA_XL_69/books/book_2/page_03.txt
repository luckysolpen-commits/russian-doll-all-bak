Page 3: The Clone Hierarchy

You ignore the drone command center and instead make your way to the Clone Hierarchy sector, where your designation number CL7X-914 grants you access to the vast cloning complex. The walls are lined with viewing ports to the vast vats where your brothers and sisters were born from genetic templates. The air carries a metallic scent, and the soft hum of life support systems creates an almost hypnotic rhythm.

A Clone Commander approaches – a being who appears to be your exact double, but with subtle differences that mark their higher rank. "CL7X-914, you have arrived at a critical moment," they say, their voice carrying the same slight electronic modulation that marks all clone speech. "Three assignments await: integration into the Solar Empire's military division, infiltration of the Blood Queen Monarchy's high council, or assignment to the secret carcazod research division. Each path offers advancement through the hierarchy, but each comes with its own risks and requirements."

The Commander shows you briefings on each assignment:
- The military division offers rapid advancement through combat operations in the Martian tunnels
- The infiltration mission promises power through intelligence gathering and subterfuge
- The research division holds the secrets of transformation but requires dangerous experimentation

You feel a strange connection to the other clones in the facility, as if there's a deeper consciousness linking your thoughts. The Commander seems to notice this awareness and nods approvingly. "Good. The hierarchy's deeper mysteries are revealing themselves to you."
