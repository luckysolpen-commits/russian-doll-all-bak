CYOD#3: Clones, Drones and Carcazods - Outline

**Setting:** Mars, both surface hubs/cities and extensive underground tunnel systems. Multiple governments: Solar Empire, Blood Queen Monarchy, Red African Union, Robot State. Some travel to Moon base for training/trade.

**Theme:** Clowns hunting clowns with drones, some turning to clowns, others as spies. Clones and clowns reaching high ranks where they can mutate into carcazods through brain sniffing, eating cockroach shark mutations for advanced penetration, stealth, spying and warfare. Mars tunnel system used by clone/clown pilots for policing, military, and heist/raids.

**Main Character:** Player starts as either a clone or drone operator in the Martian underground system

**Introduction:** 
- Introduce the player character in the clone/drones world on Mars
- Explain the underground tunnel system, the different governments, and the conflict between clones, clowns, and the possibility of becoming carcazods
- Present the first major choice that sets their path

**Branching Paths:**
- Path 1: Choose to remain loyal to your assigned government/faction
- Path 2: Decide to become a rogue clown, hunting other clowns or clones
- Path 3: Choose to become a spy for one government against another
- Path 4: Attempt to rise in rank to gain access to carcazod transformation

**Major Story Elements:**
- Exploration of Martian geography (real and fictional elements)
- Underworld tunnel navigation and conflicts
- Drone piloting and clone piloting missions
- Mutations and the path to becoming a carcazod
- Heists, warfare, and infiltration missions
- Possible journeys to the Moon base
- The concept of the SS. SOL and the honorable death for clones

**Multiple Endings:**
- Success as a government agent
- Becoming the ultimate carcazod bio-weapon
- Leading a rebellion of rogue clowns
- Sacrificing yourself for the SS. SOL
- Establishing your own faction in the Martian underground
- Betrayal leading to your downfall
- Becoming a legendary pilot or drone operator

**Structure:** Approximately 120-150 pages with branching choices leading to different outcomes
