Page 60: Investigating the Cloning Chambers

The cloning chambers represent technology far beyond anything used by the current factions - vast organic vessels where entities are grown using genetic templates that include not just physical characteristics but consciousness patterns and transformation potential. Your drone's sensors detect that these chambers are creating beings specifically designed for carcazod transformation from their inception.

Three distinct cloning processes are active: The Prime Carcazod Line, where the first generation of transformation-ready beings are grown; The Hybrid Integration Series, where genetic material from all current factions is combined to create universal infiltrators; and The Consciousness Implants, where awareness itself is grown as a biological entity before being transferred to physical forms.

As you examine the chambers more closely, you realize they were established by the original Martian colonists who discovered that natural reproduction couldn't produce beings capable of surviving the transformation process. These chambers have been operating continuously for centuries, producing generations of beings with increasing transformation potential.

Your drone detects that the cloning processes here are not just creating physical forms but engineering consciousness itself. The beings being grown here are designed to be perfect vessels for carcazod transformation, with neural structures that facilitate the integration of new abilities and awareness.

The organic technology of the chambers pulses with life, responding to your presence as if recognizing your own clone heritage. Some of the developing beings show characteristics similar to your own, suggesting that your original template may have been created here or in a similar facility.
