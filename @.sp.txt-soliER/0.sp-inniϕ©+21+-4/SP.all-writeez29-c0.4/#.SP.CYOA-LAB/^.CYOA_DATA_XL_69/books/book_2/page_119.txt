Page 119: Exploring the Pre-Human Artificial Structures

The pre-human artificial structures represent technology that predates human arrival on Mars, but appears to be specifically designed to work with human and clone biology. Your exploration drone reveals three distinct types of structures: The Integration Chambers, where ancient technology can modify human physiology to match Martian conditions; The Consciousness Expansion Modules, which enhance awareness and cognitive capabilities beyond normal human limits; and The Transformation Preparation Spheres, which appear to have been designed to prepare beings for carcazod-level metamorphosis.

As you navigate these structures, you realize they were built by an intelligence that understood both human nature and the transformation process that would later become central to Martian civilization. The technology seems designed to bridge the gap between human biology and Martian environmental requirements.

Your sensors detect that these structures have been actively maintained since before human arrival, suggesting either extremely long-term planning or continuing operation by unknown entities. Some of the technology shows signs of having been modified or upgraded in ways that incorporate human and clone biology specifically.

The structures respond to your presence with activation sequences that suggest they've been waiting for beings with your specific characteristics. The ancient technology appears designed to work with clone genetics and transformation potential.
