Page 144: Choosing Environmental Merging

The Cockroach Shark Mutation chamber completes the Stealth Adaptation through Environmental Merging, allowing your form to blend seamlessly with tunnel walls and structures. Your body begins to adopt the reflective and textural properties of your surroundings, becoming nearly invisible to all detection systems.

Three merging capabilities emerge: Adaptive Camouflage Mastery, where your form automatically matches the visual, thermal, and electromagnetic properties of surrounding materials; Structural Integration Capability, allowing you to become part of tunnel walls and structures while maintaining mobility; and Detection Evasion Expertise, enabling you to avoid all forms of surveillance and sensing while in merged state.

As the environmental merging completes, you experience the sensation of becoming part of the tunnel system itself - your form flows and adapts to match any surface you approach while maintaining your essential consciousness and capabilities.

Your enhanced stealth abilities allow you to move through any tunnel environment undetected, observe faction operations from concealed positions, and approach targets without triggering any form of detection system.

The environmental merging creates unprecedented stealth capabilities, but also requires constant awareness of your surroundings to maintain the camouflage effect and may limit your ability to interact with the environment while in merged state.
