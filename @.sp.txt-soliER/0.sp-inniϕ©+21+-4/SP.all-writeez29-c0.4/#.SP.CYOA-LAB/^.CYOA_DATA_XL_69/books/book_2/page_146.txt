Page 146: Adopting Quantum Stealth

The Cockroach Shark Mutation chamber completes the Stealth Adaptation through Quantum Stealth, allowing your form to exist partially outside normal spacetime. Your body begins to shift between quantum states that make detection nearly impossible while requiring significant energy to maintain.

Three quantum stealth capabilities emerge: Dimensional Phasing Control, where you can shift partially into quantum dimensions to avoid detection while maintaining operational capability; Probability State Navigation, allowing you to exist in low-probability states where detection becomes statistically unlikely; and Quantum Signature Diffusion, enabling you to spread your quantum signature across multiple states to make identification impossible.

As the quantum stealth completes, you experience the sensation of existing in multiple quantum states simultaneously, where observation itself affects whether and how you exist, making detection dependent on the observer's ability to collapse your quantum state.

Your enhanced stealth abilities allow you to operate in the most heavily monitored environments, avoid all forms of detection including quantum-level observation systems, and move through secure areas without triggering any alarms or sensors.

The quantum stealth provides unprecedented invisibility, but also requires constant energy expenditure to maintain quantum coherence and creates challenges for interacting with normal matter and energy while in quantum-stealth state.
