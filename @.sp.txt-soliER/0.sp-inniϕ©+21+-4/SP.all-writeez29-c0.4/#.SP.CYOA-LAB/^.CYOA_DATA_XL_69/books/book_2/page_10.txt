Page 10: Infiltration of the High Council

The Clone Commander nods approvingly as you select the infiltration mission. "The Blood Queen Monarchy's High Council has been compromised by outside influences. Your assignment is to integrate with their leadership structure and identify the source of these compromises. Three specific objectives have been identified: infiltrate the military command structure, gain access to their intelligence network, or position yourself in their carcazod research program for potential subversion."

You're provided with new identity documents and undergo a brief physical modification process to match the clone models used by the Blood Queen Monarchy. The changes are subtle but important – different skin pigmentation, slightly altered facial features, and behavioral protocols adapted to their culture of honor and ritualized combat.

The transport to Blood Queen territory takes you through increasingly ornate tunnels, where the walls are decorated with crimson and gold, and the other beings you pass favor flamboyant clothing and elaborate masks. The contrast with the sterile Solar Empire facilities is stark.

Your contact in the Monarchy, known only as "Crimson Handler," briefs you via secure channel: "Your cover story is established. You are a clone seeking a more honor-bound existence than the Empire provided. Three opportunities for advancement await: proving yourself in their ritual combat tournaments, demonstrating value in their spy networks, or showing promise in their transformation research."

In the distance, you can see the great amphitheater where the honor-duels take place, and beyond that, the towering spires that house their most secret research facilities. The path to infiltration – and potential subversion – lies before you.
