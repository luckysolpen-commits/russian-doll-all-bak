Page 19: The Electromagnetic West

The electromagnetic anomalies to the west lead your drone Scout-X7 through increasingly metallic passages where the very air seems to vibrate with energy. Your instruments go wild as you enter a vast chamber filled with what appears to be an ancient Martian technology – crystalline structures that pulse with electromagnetic energy, creating fields that distort your sensors and communications.

Captain Vex's voice becomes heavily distorted: "Scout-X7... do you... copy... energy readings... off the scale..."

In the center of the chamber, three distinct technological complexes await investigation: a massive array that appears to control the electromagnetic fields throughout the tunnel system, a bio-mechanical interface that suggests the ancient Martians were experimenting with consciousness transfer, and a quantum processing center that might be the key to understanding how the carcazod transformations actually work.

As you approach these structures, you realize they're not merely ancient technology – they're still active, maintaining a vast network of energy that spans deep beneath the Martian surface. Your drone's systems begin to interface with the ancient mechanisms, and you experience brief flashes of memory or knowledge that aren't your own.

The implications are staggering: this could be the original source of the carcazod transformation process, a discovery that would revolutionize the understanding of both ancient Martian civilization and the current factions' research efforts.
