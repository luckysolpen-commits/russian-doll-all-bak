Page 9: The Solar Empire Military Division

You present yourself to Colonel Argent of the Solar Empire's Martian Military Division. His chrome-plated uniform gleams in the facility's lighting, and his augmented eyes scan your clone designation with mechanical precision. "CL7X-914, welcome to the Empire's finest. You've been selected for the Solar Guard – our elite tunnel warfare specialists. Three operations require personnel of your... unique potential: leading a strike force against a Blood Queen Monarchy outpost, infiltrating the Robot State's underground networks, or participating in the most secretive mission – tracking and eliminating rogue carcazod experiments."

The briefing room displays holographic maps of the Martian tunnel systems, with areas marked in various colors indicating faction control. You notice that the most dangerous zones are marked in red – areas where carcazods have been reported or where the boundaries between factions create chaotic combat conditions.

Colonel Argent continues: "Each assignment offers different paths to advancement. The Blood Queen operation is about direct military action and establishing dominance. The Robot State infiltration requires stealth and intelligence gathering. The carcazod hunt... that's about survival and understanding the ultimate threat to our ordered society."

On the wall behind him, you see propaganda images of clones serving the Empire alongside artistic renderings of the SS. SOL, the great ship powered by the souls of honored clones. The message is clear: service to the Empire leads to the ultimate honor.
