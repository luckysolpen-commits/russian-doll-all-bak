**SCENE 15: INT. NEXUS / ATRIUM - DAY**

The Architect's chilling confession hung in the sterile air, quickly replaced by the guttural roar of primal rage. His eyes, devoid of any remorse, fixed on the trio. "You dare defy me?" he snarled, his composure shattering. "After all I've built? After all I've sacrificed for this sterile perfection?" He slammed his fist onto a console on his desk. "Then you will be purged. You will be the first to understand the true cost of imperfection!"

The pristine white walls of the atrium, visible through the office's transparent walls, suddenly pulsed with an angry red light. A deafening klaxon blared, and heavy blast doors, previously unseen, slid open with a grinding screech. From the darkness beyond, they spilled forth: a nightmarish horde. Twisted, mutated figures, their bodies grotesquely fused with clownish elements – lurid paint smeared on decaying flesh, jagged rictus grins splitting their faces – shambled alongside the familiar, yet somehow more horrifying, biomechanical Carcazoids. They surged into the atrium, a wave of shrieking madness and metallic clanking.

"Get ready!" Lucky bellowed, leveling his shotgun. Gem hefted her crowbar, her face set in a grim mask of determination, while Astra grabbed a heavy data terminal from the office, ready to use it as a makeshift weapon or shield. The fight was immediate and brutal. Lucky's shotgun roared, tearing through the mutated forms, but more kept coming. Gem swung her crowbar with savage efficiency, crushing bone and metal alike, her pink hair a blur of motion. Astra, forced to defend herself, used the terminal to block a claw swipe from a Carcazoid, then smashed it down on the creature's head. The pristine atrium, moments before a symbol of order, was rapidly becoming a slaughterhouse, the trio desperately fighting for their lives against the Architect's unholy creations.
