**SCENE 19: INT. SPACECRAFT - CONTINUOUS**

The interior of The Architect's private craft was as sleek and alien as its exterior. Soft, ambient lighting emanated from the walls, and the cockpit was a symphony of holographic displays and touch-sensitive controls that responded to the lightest gesture. Astra, her eyes wide with a mixture of awe and intense focus, immediately took the pilot's seat. "This interface... it's unlike anything I've ever seen, but the core principles of flight control seem universal. Lucky, strap in behind me. Gem, secure that Mutagen vial. We need to get this thing airborne, *now*."

As Astra's fingers danced across the luminous controls, the spacecraft hummed to life, a deep resonance thrumming through the hull. Outside, the pristine Nexus began to shudder violently. Red emergency lights flashed within the cockpit, and automated warnings, in The Architect's cold, synthesized voice, blared: "WARNING: STRUCTURAL INTEGRITY COMPROMISED. CONTAINMENT PROTOCOL INITIATED. FACILITY SELF-DESTRUCT SEQUENCE COMMENCING." Lucky glanced back at Gem, who gave a shaky thumbs-up, the vial secured. "Got it! Let's go!" Astra initiated the launch sequence. Thrusters flared to life, pushing the trio back into their seats. Below them, the impossibly clean courthouse began to buckle and crack, explosions blooming across its facade as the self-destruct protocol took hold. With a powerful surge, the spacecraft lifted off, piercing the bruised sky, leaving the collapsing monument to a madman's ambition far behind.
