**SCENE 16: INT. ARCHITECT'S LAB - DAY**

Fighting through the carnage in the Nexus atrium, Lucky, Astra, and Gem pushed deeper into the sterile complex. Each corridor they breached was a gauntlet, throwing more of The Architect's twisted creations at them – enhanced Carcazoids with reinforced plating, clowns whose laughter now echoed with the sound of grinding metal. Lucky’s shotgun roared, Astra used salvaged riot shields and improvised chemical concoctions, and Gem’s crowbar became a whirlwind of destruction, but the sheer numbers were relentless. They were being funneled, driven towards a specific point.

Finally, they burst through a blast door into a vast chamber that dwarfed the atrium. This was clearly the heart of The Architect's operation, a titanic lab filled with humming machinery and sparking conduits. At the center, suspended by arcane-looking energy fields, was a colossal, nightmarish bio-mechanical monstrosity. It was a grotesque fusion of metal, flesh, and technology, bristling with weapons and pulsating with raw, dark energy. And within its cockpit, a familiar, chilling figure – The Architect himself, his face a mask of triumphant madness. "You made it," his voice boomed, amplified through the monstrous machine's speakers. "You fought valiantly, little insects. But this is where your journey ends. This leviathan is my masterpiece, the ultimate expression of order and control. And you… you are merely the final test subjects!" The chamber vibrated with the raw power of the machine, signaling the beginning of the final, desperate battle.
