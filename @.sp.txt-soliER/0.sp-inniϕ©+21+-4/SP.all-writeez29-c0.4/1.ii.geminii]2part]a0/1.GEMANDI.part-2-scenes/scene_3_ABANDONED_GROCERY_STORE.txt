**SCENE 3: INT. ABANDONED GROCERY STORE - DAY**

The crumpled map had led them to this: a faded sign for "Groc-Mart" barely clinging to its moorings above a shattered storefront. Lucky signaled for caution, his shotgun sweeping the interior. The air hung thick with the cloying scent of decay and stale goods. Shelves lay toppled, aisles choked with debris and the desiccated husks of what were once vibrant displays. Most of the easily accessible food and medicine had clearly been looted long ago.

"Jackpot, huh?" Gem muttered, kicking aside a rusted can. "Looks more like a graveyard of consumables." Astra, however, was already moving with purpose, her eyes scanning the more hidden corners. "Wait. Look behind this collapsed refrigeration unit. Sometimes, supplies get pushed back or overlooked." With a grunt, Lucky helped her heave the heavy metal carcass aside, revealing a small cache of untouched items. A few precious cans of beans, a sealed bottle of water, and, miraculously, a sealed first-aid kit stocked with bandages and antiseptic. Gem's eyes lit up. "Score! And look, a half-full bag of jerky! Better than nothing!" Lucky nodded, a rare, grim smile touching his lips. "It's not much, but it's a start. Better than starving. Grab what you can, quickly. We don't know how long we have before something else finds us." The brief moment of relief was palpable, a small victory against the overwhelming odds, but the knowledge that every can, every bandage, was a critical resource against the ever-present dangers kept them moving with practiced urgency.
