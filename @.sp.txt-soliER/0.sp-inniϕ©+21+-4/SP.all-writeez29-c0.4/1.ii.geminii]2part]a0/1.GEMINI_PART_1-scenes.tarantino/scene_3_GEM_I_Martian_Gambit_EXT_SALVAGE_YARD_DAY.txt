**GEM&I: Martian Gambit**

**Scene 3**

**EXT. SALVAGE YARD - DAY**

A vast graveyard of rusted metal under a sickly yellow sky. Mountains of crushed cars, skeletal remains of buses, and forgotten construction equipment.

The trio moves through the maze of junk.

LUCKY is in his element, his eyes scanning the wreckage for anything useful. ASTRA follows, her gaze analytical, assessing the structural integrity of potential vehicles. GEM skips ahead, occasionally stopping to admire a particularly colorful piece of graffiti on a rusted panel.

<center>GEM</center>
> Ooh, this one has a certain... post-apocalyptic charm.

She gestures to a school bus that has been completely flattened.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> We need something with a solid frame and a working engine. And armor. Lots of armor.

They round a corner and see it. An old, armored transport truck, the kind used for riot control. It's covered in rust and grime, but its reinforced chassis and heavy-duty tires are intact.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> There. That's our ride.

Astra walks around it, tapping on the armor plates.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> The engine block is shielded. The tires are solid rubber. It's a good choice.

Gem peers into the driver's side window.

<center>GEM</center>
> And it comes with a free air freshener!

She points to a small, pine-tree shaped air freshener hanging from the rearview mirror. It is the only clean thing on the entire truck.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Alright. Let's see if we can get it started.

**FADE OUT.**
