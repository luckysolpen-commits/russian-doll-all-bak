**GEM&I: Martian Gambit**

**Scene 1**

**INT. NEXUS CONTROL ROOM - DAY**

The silence in the NEXUS is the most unsettling part. It's a clean, sterile silence, the kind that hums with the ghost of activity. Polished white walls, gleaming chrome, and massive, dark screens dominate the space. For days, the trio has been catching their breath here, a stark contrast to the ruined world outside.

LUCKY (30s, a man worn thin, his usual resourcefulness frayed at the edges) stands before a massive, floor-to-ceiling window. He gazes out at the skeletal remains of the city.

The sky is a bruised purple. The streets are littered with the grotesque, deflated forms of MUTANT CLOWNS and the chitinous husks of CARCAZOIDS. The virus worked, but it was a slow, creeping death.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> It's not enough. We cut the head off the snake, but the body is still thrashing.

ASTRA (30s, fiercely intelligent, focused) looks up from a terminal she has commandeered. Charts and data streams scroll across the screen, a language only she can fully comprehend.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> The mutagen is effective, but its dispersal is inefficient. It's like trying to fumigate a skyscraper with a single can of bug spray. We need a delivery system. Something comprehensive, automated, and relentless.

GEM (20s, a free-spirited artist with a wild streak and bright pink hair) is tinkering with a captured clown's JOY-BUZZER, sending weak sparks from its prongs. She perked up.

<center>GEM</center>
> So, like, a million little robot exterminators?

Astra nods, a faint smile touching her lips.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Exactly.

**FADE OUT.**
