**GEM&I: Martian Gambit**

**Scene 7**

**INT. ARMORED TRUCK - NIGHT**

The truck is pulled over on the side of the road, its engine idling. The floodlights illuminate a small circle of relative safety in the vast darkness.

Inside, the trio tends to the aftermath of the fight.

LUCKY is welding a new patch of armor over a dented section of the hull. The sparks from the torch are the only bright light in the cramped space.

ASTRA is cleaning a long gash on Gem's arm. Gem winces, but doesn't complain.

<center>GEM</center>
> You'd think for a bunch of clowns, they'd be funnier.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> There's nothing funny about tetanus. Hold still.

Lucky finishes welding and lifts his mask. He looks tired.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> That's the last of the welding rods. We need to find more supplies. Soon.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> According to the map, there's a town about twenty miles up the road. It's small, but it might have a hardware store.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> We'll head there at first light. No more driving at night.

He looks at Gem, his expression softening.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> You did good up there.

<center>GEM</center>
> I know.

She winks, then immediately winces as Astra applies a disinfectant to the cut.

The trio sits in silence for a moment, the only sound the hum of the engine. The constant threat of the world outside hangs heavy in the air.

**FADE OUT.**
