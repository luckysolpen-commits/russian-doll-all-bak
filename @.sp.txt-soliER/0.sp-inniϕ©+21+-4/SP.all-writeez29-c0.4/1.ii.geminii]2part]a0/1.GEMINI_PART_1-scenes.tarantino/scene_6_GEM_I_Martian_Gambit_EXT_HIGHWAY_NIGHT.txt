**GEM&I: Martian Gambit**

**Scene 6**

**EXT. HIGHWAY - NIGHT**

The only light comes from the truck's powerful floodlights, cutting a path through the oppressive darkness. The skeletal remains of trees line the road, their branches like grasping claws.

INT. ARMORED TRUCK

The trio is on high alert. The quiet of the day has given way to a tense, watchful night.

Suddenly, a high-pitched giggle echoes through the darkness. It is a sound that is both childish and deeply unsettling.

<center>GEM</center>
> I hate that sound.

Astra's eyes are glued to the sensor tablet.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> We've got movement. All around us.

LUCKY's hands tighten on the steering wheel.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Here we go.

From the darkness, they emerge. A pack of CLOWN THIEVES. They are feral and emaciated, their faces painted with grotesque, smeared smiles. They ride on jury-rigged motorcycles, their engines screaming like banshees.

They swarm the truck, their laughter a cacophony of madness. One of them leaps from his bike onto the side of the truck, his fingers scrabbling for a handhold.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Hold on!

Lucky swerves the truck, trying to shake the clown off. The clown hangs on, his face a mask of insane glee.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> He's trying to get to the fuel tank!

Gem, without a word, climbs onto the roof of the truck through a hatch. She is armed with a long metal pole.

<center>GEM</center>
> Hey, Bozo!

The clown looks up, and Gem swings the pole, knocking him off the truck and into the darkness.

The other clowns continue their assault, throwing makeshift explosives that burst against the truck's armor in showers of sparks.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> That's it. I'm done with this.

He slams his foot on the accelerator. The truck roars forward, smashing through the motorcycles and sending clowns flying. The battering ram with the snarling clown face does its job with brutal efficiency.

The remaining clowns scatter, their laughter fading into the darkness.

**FADE OUT.**
