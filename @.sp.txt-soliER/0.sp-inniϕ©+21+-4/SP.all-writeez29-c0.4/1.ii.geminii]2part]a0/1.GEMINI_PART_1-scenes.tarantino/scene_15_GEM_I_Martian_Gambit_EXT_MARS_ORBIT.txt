**GEM&I: Martian Gambit**

**Scene 15**

**EXT. MARS ORBIT**

The Seraph's Folly hangs in the orbit of Mars. The Red Planet fills the viewport, a vast, ochre desert under a thin, wispy atmosphere. It is both beautiful and intimidating.

INT. SERAPH'S FOLLY (COCKPIT)

The trio is at their stations, preparing for their approach.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> I'm hailing Olympus Port.

She opens a com channel. A moment of static, then a calm, professional voice responds.

<center>OLYMPUS PORT CONTROL (V.O.)</center>
> Seraph's Folly, this is Olympus Port Control. We have you on our scopes. Welcome to Mars.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Olympus Port, we are a refugee vessel from Earth. We request permission to land.

<center>OLYMPUS PORT CONTROL (V.O.)</center>
> We are aware of your situation, Seraph's Folly. The Black Queen Conglomerate extends its deepest sympathies for the tragedy on your homeworld. Permission to land is granted. Transmit your approach vector and we will guide you in.

The voice is smooth, reassuring, almost... too perfect.

<center>GEM</center>
> They sound nice.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Too nice.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Sending the vector now. Let's see what kind of welcome they have in store for us.

She looks at the image of Mars on the screen, her expression a mixture of hope and apprehension.

**FADE OUT.**
