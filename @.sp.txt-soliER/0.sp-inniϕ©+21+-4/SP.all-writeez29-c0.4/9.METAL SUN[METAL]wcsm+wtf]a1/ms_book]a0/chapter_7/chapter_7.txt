Chapter 7: Metal Mafia and Unobtainium, Sol's Brutality

As Lucky, Astra, and Gigishixmix began the arduous task of deciphering the cryptic messages from the ancient documents, their focus was brutally shattered by a chilling event unfolding in the Metal Sun's public square. A collective gasp rippled through the gathered crowd as SOL, his eyes gleaming with an unsettling, almost manic intensity, assembled his latest batch of CLONE SONS. The air grew thick with a palpable dread, a silent horror that intensified as Sol, with a twisted sense of purpose, began to slaughter them one by one.

"These are the wages of SIN!" Sol's voice, amplified and echoing across the square, boomed with a terrifying conviction. "Let this be a warning to all who stray from the path of purity!" The senseless brutality was almost too much to bear, a stark reminder of Sol's escalating madness. Gem, who had arrived moments before, watched, her worst fears realized, as her son RILEY was spared. His rapid growth and advanced abilities, once a source of hope, now served as a stark, chilling reminder of Sol's twisted intentions for him.

Lucky felt a deep, gnawing sense of unease. He had grown too close to Gem and her troubled past, and now he was witnessing the horrifying consequences of Sol's obsession firsthand. Astra and Gigishixmix stood frozen in shock, their bond with the clones, however brief, shattered by Sol's monstrous act. The once-peaceful Metal Sun was now irrevocably tainted by the darkness of Sol's obsession, a place where even life itself was a disposable commodity in his warped vision.

As the dust settled on the carnage, Sol vanished into the shadows, leaving behind a trail of devastation and a chilling warning to the people: "SIN" would not be tolerated, and he would stop at nothing to eradicate it. The public square, once a vibrant hub, was now a silent testament to his terrifying power.

In the aftermath, as the shock slowly began to recede, Lucky's mind, still reeling from the atrocity, began to connect the dots. The ancient documents Gigishixmix had found, coupled with the recent intelligence about Solenoid Industries, hinted at something far more sinister than mere wormhole experiments. Whispers had circulated for months about the "Metal Mafia," a shadowy organization that dealt in rare and dangerous materials, operating just beneath the surface of the Metal Sun's seemingly orderly society. And the most coveted of these materials was Unobtainium, a substance rumored to possess incredible, almost mythical properties, capable of amplifying energy and manipulating quantum fields.

Could Sol's brutal display be a message, not just to the populace, but to the Metal Mafia? Was he asserting his dominance, clearing the path for his own acquisition of Unobtainium? The pieces began to fall into place. Solenoid Industries' wormhole project, Riley's unique abilities, and now the Unobtainium – it all pointed to a grand, terrifying scheme. The Metal Mafia, with their control over this rare resource, would undoubtedly be a key player, either as an ally or an obstacle. The discovery of the ancient map, now more than ever, felt like a desperate race against time. They had to find the source of Unobtainium, and understand its connection to Sol's plans, before he unleashed something truly catastrophic upon the Metal Sun and beyond.
