INT. METAL SUN - GEM'S QUARTERS - NIGHT

GEM sits alone in her quarters, the holographic projection of RILEY, now appearing older and more imposing, flickering before her. He is growing at an alarming rate, his eyes holding a disturbing intensity.

She touches the projection, a complex mix of maternal love and profound unease on her face. Her relationship with SOL, the father of Riley, is a tangled web of manipulation and a strange, undeniable pull. Yet, her heart aches for LUCKY, the man she truly cares for, now lost.

GEM
(To herself, whispered)
What have I done?

FLASHBACK - INT. SOL'S CHAMBERS - DAY (BRIEF)

Sol, in his SUPER SON POWERSUIT, stands over a younger, more vulnerable Gem. He holds a glowing vial. Riley, as an infant, is in a containment unit nearby.

SOL
(Voice cold, commanding)
This genetic accelerant will ensure his rapid development. He will be my greatest creation. And yours.

Gem looks conflicted, but nods, a silent tear tracing her cheek.

END FLASHBACK

INT. METAL SUN - GEM'S QUARTERS - CONTINUOUS

Gem clenches her fist. Riley's image flickers, then solidifies, looking directly at her.

RILEY (Holographic)
Mother. My power grows. The Metal Sun... it feels fragile.

Gem recoils slightly. His words are not those of a child, but of something ancient and powerful. She knows Sol is training him, but to what end? And how much of Sol's darkness has already seeped into her son?

She thinks of Lucky, stranded, and the guilt gnaws at her. She made choices, driven by fear and a desperate hope for a future with Sol that never materialized. Now, those choices threaten everything.

FADE OUT.
