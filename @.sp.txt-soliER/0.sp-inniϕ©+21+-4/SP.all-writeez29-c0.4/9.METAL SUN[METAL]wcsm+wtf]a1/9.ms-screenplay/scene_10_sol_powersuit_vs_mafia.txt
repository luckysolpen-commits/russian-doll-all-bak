INT. METAL SUN - METAL MAFIA HIDEOUT - DAY

Word of the Unobtainium discovery spreads like wildfire through the underbelly of the Metal Sun. In a grimy, neon-lit hideout, EDGE, a hulking figure with cybernetic enhancements, grins menacingly.

He stands before a massive, dormant MECHA – the METAL MAN MECHA. Its metallic skin is scarred, its eyes dark.

EDGE
(To his cronies)
Unobtainium, eh? The ultimate power source. And Sol thinks he can keep it to himself? Not on my watch.

He turns to the Metal Man Mecha, a glint in his eye.

EDGE (CONT'D)
Time to wake up, old friend. We've got a shipment to intercept.

Meanwhile, Sol, now clad in a gleaming "SUPER SON POWERSUIT," effortlessly fends off a skirmish with a small group of Metal Mafia thugs. His Sol Pen creations, sleek and efficient, move with deadly precision.

SOL
(To himself, a cruel smile)
Let them try. My power grows with every sin I purge.

INT. METAL SUN - LUCKY'S WORKSHOP - DAY

Lucky, in his own workshop, struggles with his less advanced SOL PEN. It sputters, occasionally emitting weak energy bursts. He's trying to craft an UNOBTAINIUM SUIT, but progress is slow.

LUCKY
(Frustrated)
Come on, you piece of junk! I need more power!

Astra and Gigishixmix watch, concerned. The Metal Mafia's harassment of Lucky has intensified since Sol's public display of power. They've been picking on him, knowing he's not as formidable as Sol.

ASTRA
They're getting bolder, Lucky. We need to be careful.

GIGISHIXMIX
(Drunkenly burping)
Don't worry, Lucky-wucky! Gigishixmix will protect you!

Lucky manages a weak smile, but his gaze is fixed on the Unobtainium suit he's trying to build. He knows he's at a significant disadvantage. Sol, sensing Lucky's vulnerability, has been toying with him, sending his minions to harass and intimidate Lucky at every turn. The once-peaceful streets of the Metal Sun are now a battleground.

FADE OUT.
