EXT. PLANET X - DESOLATE LANDSCAPE - DAY

Lucky, his Unobtainium suit now patched with scavenged materials, navigates the harsh terrain of Planet X. The twisted metal trees offer little shelter from the acrid winds. He hunts for sustenance, his movements precise and efficient. Survival has become his sole focus.

He finds a small, glowing crystal, a remnant of the Extremophiles' presence. He holds it, and a faint, almost imperceptible hum resonates through his hand. He tries to amplify the Halo connection, desperate to reach Astra.

LUCKY
(Whispering to the crystal)
Astra... can you hear me? I'm still here.

INT. METAL SUN - ASTRA'S SHELTER - DAY

Astra, her face gaunt with worry, clutches her own Halo. She feels a faint flicker, a ghost of Lucky's presence. She tries to push through the static, to send him a message of hope.

ASTRA
(Whispering)
Lucky... I hear you. Don't give up. I'm trying.

But the Metal Mafia's blockade of the wormhole is stronger than ever. Edge, fueled by his obsession, has reinforced it, turning the wormhole into a one-way trap. Astra pounds her fist on the ground in frustration.

GIGISHIXMIX, her Halobody now a faint, flickering outline, places a comforting hand on Astra's shoulder.

GIGISHIXMIX
(Weakly)
He's a survivor, Astra. He always has been.

EXT. PLANET X - NIGHT

Lucky sits by a meager fire, the glowing crystal his only source of light. He looks up at the alien sky, a sky without the familiar Metal Sun. He thinks of Astra, of Gem, of the life he left behind. He knows he must adapt, must find a way to thrive in this new, hostile world.

He closes his eyes, focusing on the faint connection to Astra. He sends her a single thought: *I will survive.* 

FADE OUT.
