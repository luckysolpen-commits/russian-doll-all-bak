dark and empty, its beauty fading, its spirit

felt, the longing for human connection, for the
sound of footsteps and voices within its walls.
It was during one of these periods of
despair, Seraphina revealed, that the Midnight Bloom
had first appeared, a beacon of hope, a
promise of renewal, its roots drawing sustenance from
the Chateau's deepest emotional currents.
