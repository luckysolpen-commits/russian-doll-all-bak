SCENE 2: MEETING THE HAREM

[INT. TIME DOJO RECEPTION - DAY]

Lucky steps into the Time Dojo, a vast, open space with polished bamboo floors and intricate paper screens. The air hums with a gentle, melodic chime. Before he can fully take in the grandeur, three figures emerge from behind a shimmering screen, their presence radiating grace and beauty.

MEI-LING (19, elegant, with a serene yet commanding presence, her long black hair adorned with delicate jade pins) is the first to approach. Her eyes, the color of warm honey, fix on Lucky, then drift to the Fuzzballs, a soft smile gracing her lips.

LING-LI (18, vivacious and playful, with a cascade of chestnut hair and eyes that sparkle with mischief) bounces forward, her gaze immediately captivated by Zick and Zack. She giggles, reaching out a hand, which Zick immediately nuzzles.

XI-XI (17, shy and gentle, with a quiet strength, her dark braids interwoven with silk ribbons) trails slightly behind her sisters, her gaze shyly meeting Lucky's before darting away, a faint blush rising on her cheeks.

MEI-LING
(Her voice like wind chimes)
Welcome, traveler. We have been expecting you. Your Fuzzball companions precede your reputation.

Lucky, slightly flustered by their collective beauty and the attention, manages a small bow.

LUCKY
Uh, hi. I'm Lucky. And these are Zick and Zack.

Ling-Li claps her hands, delighted.

LING-LI
They're even cuter in person! Are they really made of pure time energy?

Zack lets out a soft purr, rubbing against Ling-Li's hand. Xi-Xi, emboldened, steps closer, her eyes wide with fascination as she observes the Fuzzballs.

MEI-LING
We are Mei-Ling, Ling-Li, and Xi-Xi. It is our honor to welcome you to the Time Dojo. We will be your guides during your stay.

Lucky feels a warmth spread through him. He'd expected a stern, martial arts academy, not this welcoming, almost familial reception.

LUCKY
Thank you. It's... a lot to take in. This place is amazing.

LING-LI
Oh, you haven't seen anything yet! Come, we'll show you around. There's so much to explore.

Mei-Ling gestures towards a grand archway. Ling-Li takes Lucky's arm, her touch light and friendly, while Xi-Xi walks a little behind, occasionally glancing at him with a shy smile. The Fuzzballs, sensing the friendly atmosphere, begin to explore their new surroundings, occasionally zipping ahead, then returning to Lucky's shoulders. Lucky, with his new companions, begins his journey into the heart of the Land of Time.
