SCENE 12: REUNION AND WARNING

[INT. TIME HOTEL - LUXURY SUITE - DAY]

Lucky gasps, his eyes snapping open. He's lying on a plush bed in the Time Hotel suite, a soft, silken blanket covering him. The scent of temporal lilies fills the air. He coughs, water still in his lungs, and struggles to sit up.

MEI-LING
(Her voice filled with relief)
Lucky! You're awake!

He looks up to see Mei-Ling, Ling-Li, and Xi-Xi gathered around him, their faces etched with concern. Mei-Ling is holding a warm compress to his forehead, Ling-Li is wringing out a damp cloth, and Xi-Xi is gently stroking Zick and Zack, who are nestled on his chest, purring softly.

LUCKY
(Hoarsely)
What... what happened? I thought...

LING-LI
We found you. Arianna brought you back. You were... not looking so good.

XI-XI
(Softly)
She said you were trying to retrieve the artifact from the Lake of Echoes.

Lucky remembers the Statue Samurai, the crushing pressure, the darkness. He shivers.

LUCKY
Arianna... where is she? She just left me there?

Mei-Ling's expression darkens slightly.

MEI-LING
Arianna is... complicated. She has her own methods. She believes in pushing people to their limits.

LUCKY
(Frustrated)
She just left me to drown!

LING-LI
(Sighs)
She knew you wouldn't. She has a strange way of teaching. But she also warned us. She said the artifact requires... a sacrifice. Not of life, but of something deeper.

Lucky looks at them, confused.

LUCKY
A sacrifice? What are you talking about?

XI-XI
(Her voice barely a whisper)
To obtain the artifact, you must confront your greatest fear. It is not enough to simply defeat the guardians. You must overcome what truly holds you back.

Lucky thinks back to the lake, the feeling of helplessness, the fear of drowning. Was that his greatest fear? Or was it something else, something deeper, more personal?

MEI-LING
Arianna believes that true mastery of Time Fu comes not from strength, but from understanding oneself. The Lake of Echoes reflects not just the physical world, but the inner world as well. The Statue Samurai... they are manifestations of your own doubts and fears.

Lucky processes this. It makes a strange kind of sense. He had been fighting them physically, but perhaps the battle was meant to be internal.

LUCKY
So, I have to go back. And face... whatever it is.

LING-LI
We will help you. We will train you. You have an innate connection to time, Lucky. We saw it when you shielded Xi-Xi. You just need to learn how to control it.

Xi-Xi nods in agreement, her eyes filled with quiet determination. Zick and Zack, sensing Lucky's renewed resolve, begin to glow brighter, their purrs growing louder. Lucky looks at the three women, their unwavering support a comforting balm. He had almost given up, but their belief in him reignited a spark. He would go back to the lake. He would face his fear. And he would get that artifact.
