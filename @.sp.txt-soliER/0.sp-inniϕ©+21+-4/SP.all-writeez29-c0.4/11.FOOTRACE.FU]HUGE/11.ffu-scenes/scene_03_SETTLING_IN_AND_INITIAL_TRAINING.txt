SCENE 3: SETTLING IN AND INITIAL TRAINING

[INT. TIME HOTEL - LUXURY SUITE - DAY]

The Time Hotel is a marvel of traditional Asian architecture blended with subtle temporal technology. Lucky's suite, shared with the sisters, is expansive, adorned with silk tapestries depicting ancient time-travelers and furnished with plush cushions and low tables. A panoramic window offers a breathtaking view of the Land of Time.

Lucky unpacks his meager belongings, feeling a little out of place amidst the opulence. Zick and Zack, however, are having a grand time, zipping through the air, leaving faint trails of shimmering light.

MEI-LING
(Smiling gently)
Make yourself at home, Lucky. This will be your sanctuary while you are with us.

LING-LI
And we'll make sure you're never bored! First lesson: basic Time Fu!

Ling-Li demonstrates a simple hand gesture, and a teacup on the table shimmers, its contents swirling in reverse before settling. Lucky tries to mimic her, but the teacup merely wobbles.

LUCKY
Whoa. That's harder than it looks.

XI-XI
(Softly)
It takes practice. But you have a natural affinity. We can feel it.

Later, the sisters lead Lucky through a bustling marketplace, vibrant with exotic scents and sounds. They stop at a grand, ornate building – the TIME CASTLE.

[INT. TIME CASTLE - ROYAL ANTECHAMBER - DAY]

Lucky, dressed in a simple but elegant uniform, stands a little awkwardly as he's introduced to a stern-faced ROYAL ADVISOR. The Fuzzballs are tucked discreetly into his uniform pockets.

ROYAL ADVISOR
So, you are the new assistant. Your duties will involve assisting the royal family with minor temporal adjustments – ensuring tea is always hot, scrolls are delivered precisely on time, and the Emperor's naps are undisturbed by temporal anomalies.

Lucky nods, trying to look competent. It's a far cry from the grand adventures he imagined, but a job is a job.

[INT. TIME HOTEL - LUXURY SUITE - NIGHT]

Lucky sits on a cushion, a scroll of Time Fu exercises in his lap, a faint glow emanating from it. He practices a few gestures, and this time, a small, intricate clockwork toy on the table whirs slightly faster.

LUCKY
(To himself)
Progress.

Ling-Li enters, carrying a tray with steaming tea. She sits beside him.

LING-LI
How was your first day at the Castle?

LUCKY
Interesting. Mostly making sure the Emperor's tea doesn't get cold. But I did see some amazing time-warping artifacts.

LING-LI
(Grinning)
Every journey begins with a single step, or in your case, a single temporal adjustment. You'll get there. We'll help you.

Lucky smiles, feeling a sense of belonging. The Land of Time was strange, but with the sisters by his side, it was starting to feel like home.
