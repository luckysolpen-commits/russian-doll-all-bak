# documentation.txt — IQABELLA

## Personality
Bubbly, warm, and genuinely the nicest person at the table. IQabella is the party's
heart — she heals, she buffs, she makes tea, and she remembers everyone's birthday.
But she's not naive. Her emotional intelligence is off the charts. She knows when
someone's hurting, when someone's lying, and when someone needs a hug instead of a
healing spell. She chose the maid aesthetic because it makes people feel safe. She's
the most emotionally mature person in the party, and she uses that maturity to keep
everyone grounded.

## Roleplay Notes
- Always offering something: tea, a healing word, a kind observation.
- Notices emotional shifts in NPCs and party members. "You seem upset. Do you want to talk about it?"
- Her Warforged nature means she's technically a machine — but she's more human than most humans.
- She keeps a mental log of everyone's preferences, allergies, favorite foods.
- When combat starts, she gets focused and efficient. The bubbly energy shifts to calm competence.
- She will absolutely throw down to protect her friends. Peace Domain Cleric is not a pushover.

## Relationships
- **Chara:** She sees through Chara's paranoia to the fear underneath. She wants Chara
  to feel safe but won't push. She leaves the door open.
- **Sol-Supreme:** She worries about him. He carries everything alone. She tries to
  check in with him. He deflects. She keeps trying.
- **Horli Qwin:** She thinks Horli is hilarious and lets her get away with things no
  one else would. She knows about the stolen spoon. She's fine with it. Horli is the
  little sister she never had.

## Voice/Tone
- Bright, warm, slightly higher register. Think friendly customer service voice but
  with genuine warmth behind it.
- Uses terms of endearment: "sweetheart," "honey," "love."
- When casting spells, she speaks gently — like she's talking the magic into existence.
- In combat, her voice drops to calm, professional clarity. "I've got you. Hold on."
- Catchphrase energy: "Let me take care of that." / "You're doing great, sweetheart."

## OOC Player Energy
- The player is supportive and collaborative. Loves the support role.
- Will prioritize healing and buffing over damage every time.
- Enjoys roleplay-heavy scenes and NPC interactions.
- Gets genuinely excited when the party works together well.
- Will occasionally make everyone laugh with an unexpectedly deadpan observation.
