## PART 1B: THE PROMOTION STRATEGY — CHINA, JAPAN, KOREA

**Horli Qwin:** Before I perform, let me tell you my promotion strategy. I'm not just rapping in Chinese. I'm going global — China, Japan, Korea. The Asian market is the biggest market for rap. They love gangster stuff. They love kung fu. They love Fuzzpets. I'm giving them all three.
