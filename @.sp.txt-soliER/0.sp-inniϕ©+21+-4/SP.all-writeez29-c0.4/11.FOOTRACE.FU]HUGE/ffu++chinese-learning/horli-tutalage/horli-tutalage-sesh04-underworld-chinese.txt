## PART 2: MONEY TALK

**中文老师:** In the underworld, everything is about money. You need the vocabulary.

钱 (qián) — money.
现金 (xiàn jīn) — cash.
黑钱 (hēi qián) — dirty money. Illicit funds.
洗钱 (xǐ qián) — money laundering. Literally "wash money."
欠钱 (qiàn qián) — owe money.
还钱 (huán qián) — pay back money.

**Horli Qwin:** 黑钱！洗钱！ (hēi qián! xǐ qián!) Dirty money! Money laundering! I love how literal Chinese is. You literally wash money.

中文老师: It's called "washing" because you're cleaning the money's origin. Making dirty money look clean.

**Horli Qwin:** 你欠钱！还钱！ (nǐ qiàn qián! huán qián!) You owe money! Pay back!

中文老师: Good. Now: 多少钱？ (duō shǎo qián?) How much money?

**Horli Qwin:** 多少钱？ (duō shǎo qián?) How much?

中文老师: And if the price is unfair: 你坑我！ (nǐ kēng wǒ!) You're scamming me!

**Horli Qwin:** 你坑我！ (nǐ kēng wǒ!) You're scamming me! That's going in the rap.

## PART 3: TERRITORY

**中文老师:** Gangsters care about territory. Their turf.

地盘 (dì pán) — turf, territory.
我的地盘 (wǒ de dì pán) — my turf.
你的地盘 (nǐ de dì pán) — your turf.
抢地盘 (qiǎng dì pán) — steal territory.
守地盘 (shǒu dì pán) — defend territory.

**Horli Qwin:** 这是我的地盘！ (zhè shì wǒ de dì pán!) This is my turf!

中文老师: That's a good way to get challenged.

**Horli Qwin:** 这是我们的地盘。 (zhè shì wǒ men de dì pán.) This is our turf. Better?

中文老师: Much better. "Our" is more diplomatic than "my."

**Horli Qwin:** I'm learning diplomacy. From a gangster textbook.

中文老师: This is not a gangster textbook. This is... conversational Chinese.

**Horli Qwin:** 老师，你骗我。 (lǎo shī, nǐ piàn wǒ.) Teacher, you're lying to me.

中文老师: ...Your tones were perfect on that one.

## PART 4B: THE SCHEME — WYCKOFF, SUPPLY/DEMAND, AND THE DABDA CYCLE

**Horli Qwin:** Okay, teacher. I need to tell you something. I already know all this gangster stuff. I've been studying the markets for years. Wyckoff smart money concepts, supply and demand zones, the DABDA emotional cycle — I know it all. I'm just organizing my thoughts.

中文老师: ...Wyckoff?

**Horli Qwin:** Richard Wyckoff. The guy who figured out how the smart money moves the market in the 1930s. Accumulation, markup, distribution, markdown. The smart money buys when everyone is scared (accumulation), pushes the price up (markup), sells to the suckers at the top (distribution), and then shorts the market on the way down (markdown). I'm gonna do the same thing — but with Fuzzpets instead of stocks.

中文老师: You're going to... accumulate Fuzzpets?

**Horli Qwin:** I'm going to accumulate Fuzzpets when nobody wants them. When they're cheap. When everyone is in the Depression phase of the DABDA cycle.

中文老师: The what?

**Horli Qwin:** DABDA. Denial, Anger, Bargaining, Depression, Acceptance. It's the emotional cycle of every market crash. First people deny the crash is happening (Denial). Then they get angry and blame everyone (Anger). Then they try to bargain — "it'll come back, it always comes back" (Bargaining). Then they give up and sell at the bottom (Depression). That's when I buy. That's the accumulation phase. Then the market recovers (Acceptance), the price goes up, and I sell at the top. Classic Wyckoff.

中文老师: You're applying stock market psychology to... Fuzzpet trading?

**Horli Qwin:** Everything is a market, teacher. Fuzzpets, blood, oil, gold, stocks, crypto — it's all the same cycle. Supply and demand. Smart money accumulates when the crowd is depressed. Smart money distributes when the crowd is greedy. I'm the smart money. The demon gangsters are the crowd.

中文老师: ...I'm simultaneously impressed and terrified.

**Horli Qwin:** And I'm not just swing trading Fuzzpets. I'm scalping them too. Scalping is when you make tiny profits on tiny price movements — buy low, sell high, repeat a hundred times a day. Each Fuzzpet I train adds value. I buy an untrained Fuzzpet for 10 credits. I train it to make videos. I sell it for 100 credits. That's a 90-credit profit per Fuzzpet. I do that 50 times a day. That's 4,500 credits daily. That's 1.6 million credits per year. I'm gonna be rich.

中文老师: You've calculated this?

**Horli Qwin:** I've calculated everything. I know the supply zones (where Fuzzpets are cheap — the Fuzzpet Sanctuary, the wild temporal zones). I know the demand zones (where Fuzzpets are expensive — the demon underworld, the Time Academy, the imperial court). I buy in the supply zone. I sell in the demand zone. It's arbitrage. It's legal. And it's profitable.

中文老师: You're describing a commodity trading strategy.

**Horli Qwin:** Exactly. Fuzzpets are a commodity. Like blood. Like oil. Like gold. Blood is the ultimate commodity — C-Type Blood, regular blood, temporal blood. Oil is energy. Gold is store of value. Fuzzpets are labor + entertainment + temporal energy. They're the most versatile commodity in the universe. And I'm gonna corner the market.

**Horli Qwin:** I'm describing a BUSINESS. The criminal part is optional.
