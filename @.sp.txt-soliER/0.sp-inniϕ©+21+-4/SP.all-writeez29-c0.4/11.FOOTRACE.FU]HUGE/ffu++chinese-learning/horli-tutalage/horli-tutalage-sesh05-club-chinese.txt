## PART 1: CLUB VOCABULARY

**中文老师:** Let's start with the basics.

酒吧 (jiǔ bā) — bar.
夜店 (yè diàn) — nightclub.
音乐 (yīn yuè) — music.
跳舞 (tiào wǔ) — dance.
节奏 (jié zòu) — rhythm.
节拍 (jié pāi) — beat.

**Horli Qwin:** 酒吧！夜店！音乐！跳舞！节奏！节拍！ (jiǔ bā! yè diàn! yīn yuè! tiào wǔ! jié zòu! jié pāi!) Bar! Nightclub! Music! Dance! Rhythm! Beat!

中文老师: Now: 喝酒 (hē jiǔ) — drink alcohol.

啤酒 (pí jiǔ) — beer.
白酒 (bái jiǔ) — baijiu. Chinese liquor. Very strong. 40-60% alcohol.
鸡尾酒 (jī wěi jiǔ) — cocktail.
红酒 (hóng jiǔ) — red wine.

**Horli Qwin:** 我要喝白酒！ (wǒ yào hē bái jiǔ!) I want to drink baijiu!

中文老师: Baijiu will destroy you.

**Horli Qwin:** 我确定。 (wǒ què dìng.) I'm sure.

中文老师: ...Okay. 给我来一杯白酒。 (gěi wǒ lái yī bēi bái jiǔ.) Bring me a glass of baijiu.

**Horli Qwin:** 给我来一杯白酒！ (gěi wǒ lái yī bēi bái jiǔ!) Bring me a glass of baijiu!

## PART 2: GETTING SOMEONE'S ATTENTION

**中文老师:** In a club, you need to get people's attention. Loud music. Crowded room. You need the right words.

嘿！ (hēi!) Hey!
喂！ (wèi!) Hey! (More urgent.)
美女！ (měi nǚ!) Beautiful girl! (How you address a woman you don't know.)
帅哥！ (shuài gē!) Handsome guy! (How you address a man you don't know.)

**Horli Qwin:** 嘿！喂！美女！帅哥！ (hēi! wèi! měi nǚ! shuài gē!) Hey! Hey! Beautiful girl! Handsome guy!

中文老师: Be careful with 美女 and 帅哥. They can be friendly or creepy depending on your tone.

**Horli Qwin:** 美女～ (měi nǚ~) — friendly. 美女！ (měi nǚ!) — creepy.

中文老师: Exactly. Tone matters.

**Horli Qwin:** I have great tone. I'm a rapper.

中文老师: You have loud tone.

**Horli Qwin:** Same thing in a club.

## PART 3: DANCING — THE UNIVERSAL LANGUAGE

**中文老师:** Dancing doesn't need words. But talking about dancing does.

跳舞 (tiào wǔ) — dance.
跳得好！ (tiào de hǎo!) Good dancing!
一起跳舞！ (yī qǐ tiào wǔ!) Let's dance together!
你的节奏很好。 (nǐ de jié zòu hěn hǎo.) Your rhythm is very good.

**Horli Qwin:** 一起跳舞！ (yī qǐ tiào wǔ!) Let's dance together!

中文老师: Now: 说唱 (shuō chàng) — rap.

**Horli Qwin:** 说唱！ (shuō chàng!) Rap! This is my language!

中文老师: 歌词 (gē cí) — lyrics.
押韵 (yā yùn) — rhyme.
节拍 (jié pāi) — beat.

**Horli Qwin:** 我的歌词很好。我的押韵很好。我的节拍很好。 (wǒ de gē cí hěn hǎo. wǒ de yā yùn hěn hǎo. wǒ de jié pāi hěn hǎo.) My lyrics are good. My rhymes are good. My beats are good.

中文老师: Your confidence is good.

**Horli Qwin:** 谢谢！ (xiè xie!) Thank you!

## PART 4: RAPPING IN CHINESE — THE BASICS

**中文老师:** Now let's learn how to count beats in Chinese. This is essential for Chinese rap.

一拍 (yī pāi) — one beat.
两拍 (liǎng pāi) — two beats.
三拍 (sān pāi) — three beats.
四拍 (sì pāi) — four beats.

**Horli Qwin:** 一拍！两拍！三拍！四拍！ (yī pāi! liǎng pāi! sān pāi! sì pāi!) One beat! Two beats! Three beats! Four beats!

中文老师: Now a simple Chinese rap structure. Four bars. Each bar has four beats.

我是 Horli Qwin，来自时间学院，
(wǒ shì Horli Qwin, lái zì shí jiān xué yuàn,)
一拍两拍三拍四拍，我的功夫最强，
(yī pāi liǎng pāi sān pāi sì pāi, wǒ de gōng fu zuì qiáng,)
恶魔大哥，给我来杯白酒，
(è mó dà gē, gěi wǒ lái bēi bái jiǔ,)
我的说唱最好，你们都比不上！
(wǒ de shuō chàng zuì hǎo, nǐ men dōu bǐ bù shàng!)

**Horli Qwin:** Let me try:

我是 Horli Qwin，来自时间学院，
一拍两拍三拍四拍，我的功夫最强，
恶魔大哥，给我来杯白酒，
我的说唱最好，你们都比不上！

中文老师: ...Your pronunciation was correct. Your rhythm was correct. Your rhyme scheme was... acceptable.

**Horli Qwin:** "Acceptable" is Horli Qwin for "fire."

中文老师: No, it isn't.

**Horli Qwin:** 你比不过我！ (nǐ bǐ bù guò wǒ!) You can't compete with me!

中文老师: That was a good use of 比不过.

**Horli Qwin:** I know. I'm learning.

## PART 5: MAKING CONNECTIONS — NETWORKING IN CHINESE

**中文老师:** Clubs are about connections. Meeting people. Building relationships.

认识你很高兴。 (rèn shi nǐ hěn gāo xìng.) Nice to meet you.
你是做什么的？ (nǐ shì zuò shén me de?) What do you do?
我们交换联系方式吧。 (wǒ men jiāo huàn lián xì fāng shì ba.) Let's exchange contact information.
微信是多少？ (wēi xìn shì duō shǎo?) What's your WeChat?

**Horli Qwin:** 认识你很高兴！你是做什么的？我们交换联系方式吧。微信是多少？ (rèn shi nǐ hěn gāo xìng! nǐ shì zuò shén me de? wǒ men jiāo huàn lián xì fāng shì ba. wēi xìn shì duō shǎo?) Nice to meet you! What do you do? Let's exchange contact info. What's your WeChat?

中文老师: Perfect. That's a complete networking conversation.

**Horli Qwin:** What if they're a demon gangster?

中文老师: The same words work. 认识你很高兴 is universal.

**Horli Qwin:** 认识你很高兴，魔王大哥！ (rèn shi nǐ hěn gāo xìng, mó wáng dà gē!) Nice to meet you, demon king big brother!

中文老师: ...That's actually very polite.

**Horli Qwin:** I'm a polite rapper.

## PART 5B: ONLINE PROMOTION — HOW TO BE FAMOUS WITHOUT TALKING TO PEOPLE

**Horli Qwin:** Okay, so here's something nobody knows about me. I'm actually... shy. Like, really shy. In person, I get nervous. I overthink. I don't know what to say. The whole loud rapper thing? That's a persona. On stage, I'm fearless. Off stage, I'd rather text than talk.

中文老师: ...I can see that, actually.

**Horli Qwin:** So online promotion is perfect for me. I can be bold behind a screen. I can edit my words before I send them. I can be the loudest person on the internet and the quietest person in the room. That's my brand.

中文老师: Okay. Let's learn the vocabulary for online promotion.

网红 (wǎng hóng) — internet celebrity, influencer. Literally "net red."
粉丝 (fěn sī) — fans. Literally "vermicelli" (noodles), but slang for fans.
点赞 (diǎn zàn) — like (a post). Literally "click praise."
关注 (guān zhù) — follow. Literally "pay attention."
转发 (zhuǎn fā) — share/repost. Literally "turn send."
评论 (píng lùn) — comment.
热搜 (rè sōu) — trending search. Hot search.
流量 (liú liàng) — traffic (web traffic, views). Literally "flow volume."

**Horli Qwin:** 网红！粉丝！点赞！关注！转发！评论！热搜！流量！ (wǎng hóng! fěn sī! diǎn zàn! guān zhù! zhuǎn fā! píng lùn! rè sōu! liú liàng!) Influencer! Fans! Likes! Follow! Share! Comment! Trending! Traffic!

中文老师: Now the strategy phrases.

我要发视频。 (wǒ yào fā shì pín.) I'm going to post a video.
这个视频会火。 (zhè ge shì pín huì huǒ.) This video will go viral.
给我点赞！ (gěi wǒ diǎn zàn!) Like my post!
关注我！ (guān zhù wǒ!) Follow me!
转发我的视频！ (zhuǎn fā wǒ de shì pín!) Share my video!

**Horli Qwin:** 我要发视频。这个视频会火。给我点赞！关注我！转发我的视频！ (wǒ yào fā shì pín. zhè ge shì pín huì huǒ. gěi wǒ diǎn zàn! guān zhù wǒ! zhuǎn fā wǒ de shì pín!) I'm going to post a video. This video will go viral. Like my post! Follow me! Share my video!

中文老师: Your energy is very... influencer.

**Horli Qwin:** That's because behind a screen, I'm not shy. I'm a monster. Online, I can be the loudest person in every room simultaneously. I can post in Chinese, Japanese, Korean — all at once. My Fuzzpet content creation empire runs 24/7. I don't have to talk to anyone. The Fuzzpets make the videos. I just edit and post.

中文老师: Your Fuzzpets make the videos?

**Horli Qwin:** Yeah. I train them — each one has a specialty. One does dance videos. One does cooking videos. One does kung fu tutorials. One does restaurant reviews in the demon underworld. They're like little AI agents — I give them a prompt, they make the content, I post it. I'm the CEO of Fuzzpet Media.

中文老师: That's... actually a very smart business model.

**Horli Qwin:** I know. I'm not just a rapper. I'm a media mogul. A shy media mogul. The internet is perfect for me.

中文老师: 你会成功的。 (nǐ huì chéng gōng de.) You will succeed.

**Horli Qwin:** 谢谢老师！ (xiè xie lǎo shī!) Thank you, teacher!
