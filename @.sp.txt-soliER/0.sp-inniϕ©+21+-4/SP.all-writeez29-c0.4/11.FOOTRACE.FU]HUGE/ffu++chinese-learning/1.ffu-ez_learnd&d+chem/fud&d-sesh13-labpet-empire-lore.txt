1. **Design guide RNA** — , eng, design, ... tx, 设计, shè jì, ... xt, the RNA sequence to match the target gene
2. **Cas9 cuts DNA** — , eng, Cas9 enzyme, ... tx, Cas9酶, Cas9 méi, ... xt, cuts the DNA at the target location
3. **Cell repairs** — , eng, cell, ... tx, 细胞, xì bāo, ... xt, repairs the break, either disabling the gene (NHEJ) or inserting new DNA (HDR)

- **Discovered:** 2012 by Jennifer Doudna 珍妮弗·道德纳, Zhēn nī fú Dào dé nà, and Emmanuelle Charpentier 埃马纽埃尔·沙尔庞捷, Āi mǎ niǔ āi ěr Shā páng jié,

- **First therapeutic use:** 2019 — sickle cell disease , eng, sickle cell disease, ... tx, 镰状细胞病, lián zhuàng xì bāo bìng, ... xt,
- **Controversy:** 2018 — He Jiankui 贺建奎, Hè Jiàn Kuí, edited twin babies' genes (CCR5) for HIV resistance. Widely condemned.
