## 📖 PLOT OUTLINE

### Phase 1: Invitation & Arrival
- Party receives a formal invitation from the twins. Delivered by a rolling labpet.
- They arrive at the 12-story lab. Check in on Floor 1. Get lab coats. Safety briefing (in Chinese).
- Meet the twins: Dr. Venom (poison specialist) and Dr. Panacea (healing specialist).

### Phase 2: Lab Training (Floors 2-6)
- **Floor 2:** Basic compounds. Make painkillers and energy boosters. Test on labpets.
- **Floor 3:** Healing compounds. Make burn cream, paralysis cure, vaccines. Test on injured labpets.
- **Floor 4:** Enhancement compounds. Make perception boosters, life extension. Test on willing labpets.
- **Floor 5:** Toxin compounds. Make poisons, tear gas, confusion agents. Test on enemy-simulated labpets.
- **Floor 6:** Resurrection Lab. The twins reveal the resurrection serum. It's dangerous. One labpet dies during testing. They bring it back. The party is horrified and amazed.

### Phase 3: The Test Begins
- The twins announce: ", eng, now, ... tx, 现在, xiàn zài, ... xt, , eng, battle, ... tx, 战斗, zhàn dòu, ... xt,"
- They release their modified ferrets: , eng, poison fang, ... tx, 毒牙, dú yá, ... xt, ferrets.
- The ferrets are horrifying: stretchable bodies, work together as arms, poison fangs, coordinated attacks.
- The battle destroys the lab. Equipment smashes. Chemicals spill. Alarms blare.

### Phase 4: The Battle
- **Ferret 1 (Venom's):** , eng, venomous snake, ... tx, 毒蛇, dú shé, ... xt, — long, thin, strikes from distance. Poison fangs inject , eng, paralysis toxin, ... tx, 麻痹毒素, má bì dú sù, ... xt,
- **Ferret 2 (Panacea's):** , eng, iron armor, ... tx, 铁甲, tiě jiǎ, ... xt, — short, thick, heavily armored. Uses its body as a whip. Can stretch 10 meters.
- The ferrets work together: one stretches to grab, the other strikes. They use the lab environment — swinging from pipes, bouncing off walls, knocking over chemical tanks.
- The party must use their Time Fu + Fuzzpets + newly-made compounds to fight.
- Security alarms trigger: , eng, alarm, ... tx, 警报, jǐng bào, ... xt, Red lights. Sprinklers. Chemical fires.

### Phase 5: The Escape
- The lab is collapsing. Chemical reactions are cascading. The twins are still fighting.
- The party must escape from Floor 6 to Floor 12 (rooftop) while the lab self-destructs.
- Each floor presents a new hazard: chemical floods on Floor 3, fire on Floor 5, gravity failure on Floor 8, temporal distortion on Floor 10.
- They reach the rooftop. Temporal aircraft waiting. The twins, defeated but impressed, grant them the next belt.

### Phase 6: Graduation
- Earn , eng, red belt, ... tx, 红带, hóng dài, ... xt,
- Unlocks: 5 Fuzzpets, 5 combo slots, 5 Chronoments each.
- The twins give a final speech — one in Chinese, one in broken English. They respect the party now.
