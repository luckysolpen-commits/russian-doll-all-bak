## ⚔️ TIME FU MARTIAL ARTS

Time Fu is the core combat system. Every fighter manipulates time during combat:

| Move | Chinese | Pinyin | Effect |
|------|---------|--------|--------|
| **Slow Strike** | 慢打 | man da | Slow the enemy's perception of time. Your hits land before they can react. |
| **Fast Step** | 快步 | kuai bu | Accelerate your own movement. Dodge attacks by existing 0.5 seconds ahead. |
| **Time Clone** | 时间分身 | shi jian fen shen | Create a temporal duplicate of yourself. Both attack simultaneously. |
| **Rewind** | 倒带 | dao dai | Reverse 3 seconds of time. Undo a mistake. Heal wounds. |
| **Freeze** | 冻结 | dong jie | Stop time in a 2-meter radius. Enemy can't move. You can. |
| **Accelerate** | 加速 | jia su | Speed up your Fuzzpet's attack cycle. Double damage for 6 seconds. |
| **Decelerate** | 减速 | jian su | Slow down incoming attacks. Arrows, fireballs, fists — all move through syrup. |
| **Temporal Palm** | 时间掌 | shi jian zhang | Strike with concentrated temporal energy. Ages the target's armor to rust. |

Each party member has their own Time Fu style:
- **CHARA:** Tactical Time Fu. Calculates temporal vectors. Uses , eng, predict, ... tx, 预判, yù pàn, ... xt, to plan 10 moves ahead.
- **Horli Qwin:** Chaotic Time Fu. Random temporal bursts. Unpredictable. Uses , eng, speed up, ... tx, 加速, jiā sù, ... xt, for acrobatic flips.
- **IQabella:** Healing Time Fu. Uses , eng, rewind, ... tx, 倒带, dào dài, ... xt, to undo damage. Uses , eng, freeze, ... tx, 冻结, dòng jié, ... xt, to protect allies.
- **Sol-Supreme:** Engineering Time Fu. Builds temporal constructs. Uses , eng, time clone, ... tx, 时间分身, shí jiān fēn shēn, ... xt, to create multiple Circuit duplicates.

## 🎲 NEW FORMAT

All Chinese vocabulary uses this format:
```
, eng, English, ... tx, 汉字, pinyin, ... xt,
```

Example:
```
**Mother Tmojizu:** First word: , eng, entropy, ... tx, 熵, shāng, ... xt, , eng, hint, ... tx, 提示, tí shì, ... xt, Disorder in a system.
```

The `, eng,` at the start and `, ... xt,` at the end mark the Chinese learning segment. English voices read the pinyin. 中文老师 reads everything including the character.
