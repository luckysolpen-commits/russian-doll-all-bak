EXT. AFGHANISTAN DESERT CITY - NIGHT

SOUND of relentless gunfire, explosions, screams

The battle rages. Lucky and Astra are a whirlwind of motion, their combat training kicking in. Lucky's tactical cap has been blown off in the firefight — his platinum mohawk is exposed, absurdly out of place in a war zone, but somehow he makes it work. They move with brutal efficiency, taking down terrorists and Shadow Syndicate members alike.

Kaito's men, realizing they've been double-crossed, turn their weapons on the USSF and the newly revealed terrorists. The lines are blurred, alliances shattered.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (To Astra, panting)
> We need to get out of here!

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Agreed! This is a meat grinder!

They fight their way through the chaos, dodging bullets, leaping over bodies. The desert city is a crumbling ruin, a testament to years of conflict.

They are vastly outnumbered. More terrorists pour in from all directions, their faces contorted with fanaticism.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (Grabbing Lucky's arm)
> This way!

She pulls him towards a dilapidated building, its entrance partially obscured by rubble. They burst through the door, slamming it shut behind them, the sounds of the battle momentarily muffled.

They lean against the door, gasping for breath, the adrenaline still coursing through their veins.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> What now?

<center>ASTRA</center>
> We find a way out. And we figure out what that "nuke" is.

Suddenly, Astra's eyes dart upwards. She grabs Lucky again, pulling him towards a rickety staircase.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Up here!

They scramble up the stairs, the sounds of the battle growing louder again as they ascend. They burst onto the rooftop – a flat, exposed surface.

Below them, the battle continues, a swirling vortex of violence. But on the rooftop, they are exposed.

A group of terrorists, led by a particularly menacing figure, appears from the opposite side of the roof, their weapons trained on Lucky and Astra.

<center>TERRORIST LEADER</center>
> The device! Hand it over! Or we detonate the mini-nuke! This building, this city... it all goes! Our cover is blown!

Lucky and Astra are cornered. The mini-nuke. It's real. And it's here.

Just as the Terrorist Leader raises his hand to give the order, a blinding flash of light erupts from the sky.

LASERS. Precise, surgical, and utterly devastating. They cut through the terrorists, vaporizing them instantly, leaving only smoking craters where they stood.

Lucky and Astra stare, stunned.

Above them, a massive, sleek UFO hovers silently. Its lights pulse with an otherworldly glow. It descends slowly, an energy field emanating from its underside.

The energy field envelops Lucky, Astra, and the mini-nuke, lifting them effortlessly into the air.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (Disoriented, looking at Astra)
> What... what is happening?!

Astra, despite the surreal situation, manages a faint, almost knowing smile.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (Voice barely a whisper)
> Don't worry, Lucky. We've been here before.

The UFO ascends rapidly, carrying them and the mini-nuke into the night sky, leaving the chaos of the Middle East far below.

FADE TO BLACK.
