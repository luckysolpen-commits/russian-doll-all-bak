EXT. EARTH - CLEAN ZONE "000010001000" - OBSERVATION DECK - DAY (FUTURE)

SOUND of wind, the hum of energy barriers, the distant chaos of Clown World beyond the walls

Lucky — now SOL — stands at the observation deck of the Clean Zone. His white mohawk catches the sunlight. His Exo Suit has changed — darker, more refined, the original template that his future self wore. The SolPen is holstered at his hip, warm, alive, an extension of his will.

He carries centuries of knowledge now. Two lifetimes merged into one. He remembers being Lucky — the ranch, the horses, the polo, the crops, the peace. And he remembers being Sol — the Moon, the clones, the manipulation, the centuries of preparation, the loneliness of knowing what's coming.

ASTRA stands beside him. She knows. She can see it in his eyes.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> You're different.

<center>SOL (LUCKY)</center>
> I'm more. And less. I'm Lucky. And I'm Sol. And I'm something new that neither of them was alone.

<center>ASTRA</center>

<center>SOL (LUCKY)</center>

<center>SOL (LUCKY)</center>
> But I'm also the man who built a civilization inside the Moon. Who trained clone soldiers. Who manipulated governments and criminals and heroes to create the conditions for survival. Who carried the weight of centuries alone.

<center>ASTRA</center>

<center>SOL (LUCKY)</center>

<center>GEM</center>

<center>SOL (LUCKY)</center>
> Phase One: We use the Clean Zone's resources to root out the Carcazod infiltration. System by system. Infrastructure by infrastructure. The SolPen can detect and neutralize them at the quantum level.

<center>ASTRA</center>

<center>LAUREN</center>

<center>SOL (LUCKY)</center>

He looks at each of them. Astra. Gem. Lauren. His team. His family. His reason.

<center>SOL (LUCKY)</center>
> I have a choice to make. I can follow the path my future self laid out — the one where I become the Sol who guides humanity from the shadows, manipulating, planning, carrying the weight alone. Or I can forge a new path. One where we fight together. Where no one carries the weight alone. Where we win as a species, not as the project of one man.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> What's your choice?

Sol — Lucky — smiles. It's Lucky's smile. Warm. Mischievous. Human. But it's Sol's certainty too.

<center>SOL (LUCKY)</center>

The team stands together. The Clean Zone hums behind them. Clown World rages beyond the walls. The Carcazods infiltrate. The Silent Shadow approaches.

But for the first time in centuries, the future is uncertain. Not predetermined. Not planned. OPEN.

And in that openness, there is hope.

Sol raises his SolPen. It glows — white, brilliant, alive with the power of creation, the power of clone souls, the power of a future that hasn't been written yet.

<center>SOL (LUCKY)</center>
> Let's build something new.

The SolPen's light fills the sky.
