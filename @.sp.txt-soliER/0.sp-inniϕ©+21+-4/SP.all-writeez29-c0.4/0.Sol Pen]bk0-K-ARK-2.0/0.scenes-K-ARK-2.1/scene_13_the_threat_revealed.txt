INT. MOON BASE - VARIOUS LOCATIONS - WEEKS LATER

SOUND of SolPen energy pulses, clone drill commands, the hum of advanced technology

A MONTAGE of Lucky's accelerated training across the Moon Base:

* INT. TRAINING ROOM - Lucky stands before a holographic display, the SolPen now an extension of his will. He creates increasingly complex objects: intricate gears, delicate machinery, small functional drones that buzz around the room. His chrome silver mohawk catches the holographic light.

* INT. COMBAT SIMULATION ARENA - Lucky trains with elite clone forces, using the SolPen to create defensive barriers, offensive projectiles, and temporary structures. His movements become fluid, confident. He's starting to look like a warrior.

* INT. TECH LAB - Astra assists Lucky with technical specifications and design. She's fascinated by the SolPen's mechanics, her scientific mind eager to understand every nuance. Lucky learns to manipulate existing matter, reshaping and reconfiguring it with a flick of the wrist.

* INT. ARMORY - Lucky receives the HALO BIO SOFTWARE HUD — a sleek, almost invisible device that integrates directly with his optic nerve. It overlays digital information onto his vision: SolPen energy levels, structural integrity of creations, potential weaknesses in opponents. He blinks, and the interface comes alive.

* INT. FITTING CHAMBER - Lucky dons the LIGHT EXO SUIT — a form-fitting, lightweight armor that enhances his speed, strength, and agility, with a direct neural link to the SolPen. He moves like a phantom, a blur of enhanced human capability. His gold chains fit perfectly over the suit's chest plate.

* INT. OBSERVATION DECK - GEM watches Lucky's training from a distance. Her expression is unreadable, but there's a new intensity in her gaze. She makes a subtle adjustment to a small handheld device — almost imperceptibly transmitting data to someone. A brief, telling glance exchanged with Sol confirms her true nature: she is a spy, gathering intelligence, her loyalty to Sol unwavering.

* INT. MESS HALL - Lucky, Astra, and the clones share a meal. Lucky watches them drink Type C blood from metallic vials — the crimson liquid that fuels their society. He tries it himself. The effect is immediate: clarity, energy, a rush of oxygen to every cell. He understands now why it's the lifeblood of Mars.

* INT. CORRIDORS - As Lucky walks between training sessions, he catches glimpses of something in the shadows. Something TRANSPARENT. Reptilian. Moving just at the edge of perception. He turns — nothing there. But the feeling persists: they're being watched by something ancient and alien.

Weeks turn into months. Lucky's mastery of the SolPen is undeniable. He is no longer just a tech mogul; he is a force of creation, a living weapon.

His mohawk changes with his mood — silver for focus, crimson for combat, gold for confidence. It's become his signature, his battle standard.

FADE OUT.
