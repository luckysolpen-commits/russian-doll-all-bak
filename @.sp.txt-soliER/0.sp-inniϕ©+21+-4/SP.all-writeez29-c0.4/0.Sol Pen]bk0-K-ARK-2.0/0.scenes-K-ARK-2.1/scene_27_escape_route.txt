INT. MARTIAN COLONY - ESCAPE ROUTE/COLLAPSE - DAY

SOUND of groaning metal, cracking rock, distant explosions, alarms blaring

The Black Queen's inner sanctum is in chaos. The battle has taken its toll, and the entire section of the Martian colony is beginning to COLLAPSE. Dust and debris rain down from the ceiling, and massive structural beams groan under immense pressure.

<center>BLACK QUEEN</center>
> (Voice echoing, distorted)
> If I cannot have this colony, then no one will!

Lucky, Astra, and Gem are caught in the maelstrom. The floor beneath them cracks, and a chasm opens up, revealing the dark depths of the colony's foundations.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> She's initiating a self-destruct sequence! Or the damage is too extensive!

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Doesn't matter! We need to get out! Now!

They race through the crumbling corridors, dodging falling debris and navigating newly formed obstacles. Lucky's crimson mohawk is now dusted red with Martian dust — he looks like a warrior king fleeing a fallen kingdom. Alarms blare, and emergency lights flash erratically, casting long, distorted shadows.

<center>GEM</center>
> (Scanning her HUD)
> The primary escape route is compromised! Secondary access tunnels are our only option!

They encounter pockets of panicked REBEL SOLDIERS, who are also trying to escape the collapsing structure. Some are crushed by falling debris, others are consumed by the widening chasms.

Lucky uses the SolPen to create temporary force fields, shielding them from falling rock and shoring up unstable sections of the corridor. He also creates temporary bridges across gaps, allowing them to continue their desperate flight.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (Gritting his teeth)
> This way! The main ventilation shaft!

They reach a massive ventilation shaft, its grates twisted and broken. It's a long, dark drop.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> It's too far! We'll never make it!

Lucky extends the SolPen, and with a surge of creative energy, he rapidly constructs a series of interlocking, spiraling ENERGY RAMPS within the shaft, creating a makeshift slide.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Hold on!

They slide down the energy ramps, the wind rushing past them, the sounds of the collapsing colony fading behind them. They emerge into a lower level, relatively stable, but still shaking.

<center>GEM</center>
> (Panting)
> That was... exhilarating.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (Wiping dust from his face)
> Just another Tuesday.

They look back at the section of the colony they just escaped. With a final, deafening ROAR, the entire upper section implodes, sending a massive cloud of red dust billowing into the Martian sky.

They are safe, for now. But the battle has left its mark, both on the colony and on them.

FADE OUT.
