INT. MOON BASE - TRAINING ROOM - DAY

SOUND of whirring machinery, soft clicks, focused concentration

The training room is vast, sterile, yet filled with an almost artistic energy. Lucky stands before a holographic display, the SolPen now in his hand. It feels lighter than he expected, almost an extension of his own will.

Sol stands beside him, observing, offering precise, concise instructions.

<center>SOL</center>
> Focus your intent, Lucky. Visualize the object. The SolPen translates thought into form.

Lucky closes his eyes, picturing a simple cube. He extends the SolPen. A shimmering, translucent cube materializes in the air before him, then solidifies into a perfect, metallic form.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Incredible.

<center>SOL</center>
> A rudimentary beginning. The SolPen draws its power from... unique sources. The collective consciousness of the clones. Their very essence.

Lucky glances at the clones in the room, a new understanding dawning on him. Their "souls" are the fuel for this device. A chilling thought, yet undeniably powerful.

A MONTAGE of Lucky's training begins:

*   He creates increasingly complex objects: intricate gears, delicate machinery, even small, functional drones that buzz around the room.
*   He practices with Astra, who, while not wielding a SolPen, assists him with technical specifications and design. She's fascinated by the device, her scientific mind eager to understand its mechanics.
*   He learns to manipulate existing matter, reshaping and reconfiguring it with a flick of the wrist.
*   He trains with the elite clone forces, using the SolPen to create defensive barriers, offensive projectiles, and even temporary structures in simulated combat scenarios. His movements become fluid, confident.
*   He receives the HALO BIO SOFTWARE HUD, a sleek, almost invisible device that integrates directly with his optic nerve. It overlays digital information onto his vision, showing him the SolPen's energy levels, structural integrity of his creations, and even potential weaknesses in his opponents.
*   He dons the LIGHT EXO SUIT, a form-fitting, lightweight armor that enhances his speed, strength, and agility, while also providing a direct neural link to the SolPen. He moves like a phantom, a blur of enhanced human capability.

During the montage, subtle hints of Gem's true nature are woven in. She is often seen observing Lucky's training, her expression unreadable. Perhaps a brief, almost imperceptible communication with Sol, a shared glance that speaks volumes. She is a spy, gathering intelligence, her loyalty to Sol unwavering.

Weeks turn into months. Lucky's mastery of the SolPen is undeniable. He is no longer just a tech mogul; he is a force of creation, a living weapon.

FADE OUT.
