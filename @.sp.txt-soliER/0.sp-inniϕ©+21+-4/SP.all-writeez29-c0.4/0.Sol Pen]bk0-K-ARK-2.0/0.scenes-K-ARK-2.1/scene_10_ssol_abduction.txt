INT. UFO / SSOL - SPACE

SOUND of a low, resonant hum, a sense of vastness

Lucky and Astra are suspended within a shimmering energy field inside the UFO. The interior is sleek, minimalist, and impossibly advanced. No visible controls, no seams. Just pure, unadulterated technology.

Lucky struggles against the energy field, but it holds him gently, firmly.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (Frantic)
> Astra! What is this place?! What do you mean, "we've been here before"?!

Astra, now free of the energy field, floats gracefully towards him. She looks calm, almost serene.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Not us, Lucky. Not exactly. But this technology... it's familiar.

Suddenly, a figure materializes before them. SOL. He stands tall, his form obscured by a voice-modulating mask and a thin, almost imperceptible exo suit. He radiates an aura of immense power and intelligence.

<center>SOL</center>
> Welcome aboard, Lucky Shepherd. And Astra. Your mission has been... revised.

His voice is deep, resonant, and utterly devoid of emotion.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (Struggling)
> My mission? What are you talking about? Who are you?!

<center>SOL</center>
> I am Sol. And you are now part of my team. The CIA's petty squabbles are irrelevant. The true threat is far greater. And you, Lucky, are essential to stopping it.

He gestures towards the mini-nuke, which is now floating in its own containment field nearby.

<center>SOL</center>
> This "sensitive equipment" is merely a distraction. A crude imitation of true power. But it served its purpose. It brought you to me.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (Eyes narrowing)
> You orchestrated this? The CIA, Kaito, the terrorists... it was all a setup?

<center>SOL</center>
> A necessary catalyst. The stakes are too high for subtlety. Earth is in peril. And only those with the vision to see beyond the mundane can save it.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Peril? What peril? And what makes you think I'll help you?

Sol's masked face seems to tilt, as if in amusement.

<center>SOL</center>
> You will help me, Lucky. Because you are a builder. A creator. And what I offer is the ultimate canvas. The power to shape reality itself.

He extends a hand, and a shimmering, ethereal object materializes in his palm – a device that looks strikingly similar to the prototype Lucky showed Kaito, but infinitely more refined. The SOLPEN.

<center>SOL</center>
> This is the SolPen. And you, Lucky Shepherd, are destined to wield it.

The energy field around Lucky dissipates. He floats freely, staring at the SolPen, then at Sol, then at Astra, who now looks at him with a mixture of awe and concern.

The SSOL begins to move, accelerating through the vastness of space, heading towards a distant, glowing orb – the Moon.

FADE OUT.
