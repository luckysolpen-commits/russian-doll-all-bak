INT. MOON BASE - LUCKY'S QUARTERS - NIGHT

SOUND of the low hum of the base, Lucky's restless movements, the faint pulse of his HALO HUD

Lucky is in his spartan, yet comfortable, quarters. His chrome silver mohawk is disheveled — he's been pacing for hours. The mysterious message replays in his mind. "He's lying to you, Lucky. About everything." The sender's encryption, his own design, gnaws at him. Only a handful of people knew that signature. And one of them was…

He stops, a sudden realization hitting him. He pulls up his HALO HUD, accessing the base's internal network. He starts cross-referencing data, looking for anomalies, for anything that doesn't quite add up.

He finds it. Subtle discrepancies in Sol's energy readings, inconsistencies in the historical data of the Silent Shadow's movement, and a curious, almost imperceptible, data stream originating from within the base, but not registered to any known Lunarian system.

He zooms in on the data stream's origin point. It leads to a restricted sector, one he hasn't been granted access to. A sector marked "Genesis Chamber."

But there's more. Buried deep in the data stream, he finds something that makes his blood run cold.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (murmuring to himself)
> What the hell is this?

The data stream contains biological signatures. Ancient ones. Fossil DNA. Reptilian. And it's being MERGED with clone neural patterns. Someone is splicing ancient reptilian brain matter into the clone genome.

He traces the merged neural patterns. They're not random. They're TARGETED. The reptilian neural signatures are designed to create a PSIONIC LINK — a mind control pathway that extends beyond the Moon. Beyond the SSOL.

It reaches Mars.

And on Mars, the signal connects to two specific neural signatures: THE RED QUEEN. And her daughter, PRINCESS MEGAN8R.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (whispering)
> He's controlling them. Through reptilian brains.

Just then, ASTRA enters, carrying a data pad. She looks tired, but determined.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Lucky, I've been reviewing the clone neural architecture. There's something I don't understand. The clones' consciousness is supposed to power the SolPen, but I'm reading a secondary neural pathway. Something that's not part of the original design.

She trails off, noticing Lucky's intense focus on his HUD, the troubled expression on his face.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> What is it?

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (without looking up)
> The message. It wasn't a random glitch. Someone sent it. Someone who knows my old encryption. And they're telling me Sol is lying.

Astra's eyes widen. She glances at the door, then back at Lucky, her voice dropping to a whisper.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Lying about what?

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Everything. The Silent Shadow. The SolPen. The clones. And the Red Queen.

He turns the HUD to show her the data stream, the "Genesis Chamber" designation, and the reptilian neural merge data.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> This data stream. It's not part of the main network. It's hidden. And it's carrying reptilian fossil DNA signatures. Ancient ones. Millions of years old. They're being merged with clone brains.

Astra studies the data, her scientific mind quickly grasping the implications. Her face pales.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> This is... this is neural parasitology. The reptilian brain matter — it's not just being added. It's creating a CONTROL PATHWAY. A psionic link.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (pointing to the Mars connection)
> And the link extends to Mars. To the Red Queen. And her daughter. Sol isn't just growing clones. He's building a mind control network using ancient reptilian brains.

Astra's hands tremble.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> The transparent beings we've been seeing. The reptilian shadows. They're not just hiding on the Moon. They're the SOURCE. Their fossil brains are being used to control people on Mars.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> The Red Queen. Her rebellion. Her cooperation with us. Is any of it real? Or is she being controlled?

Astra looks at him, her eyes wide with horror.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> What if she doesn't know? What if she thinks she's acting freely, but the reptilian neural link is pulling her strings?

Lucky stands. His chrome silver mohawk catches the HUD's glow.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> We need to get into that Genesis Chamber. We need to find out what he's hiding. And we need to find out if the Red Queen is a person or a puppet.

Astra nods, her fear replaced by a steely determination. She pulls out a small, sleek device from her pocket — a universal bypass key, a relic from her own past as a corporate spy.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> I know a way in. But we have to be careful. If Sol finds out…

<center>LUCKY</center>
> He won't. Not if we're smart. And fast.

FADE OUT.
