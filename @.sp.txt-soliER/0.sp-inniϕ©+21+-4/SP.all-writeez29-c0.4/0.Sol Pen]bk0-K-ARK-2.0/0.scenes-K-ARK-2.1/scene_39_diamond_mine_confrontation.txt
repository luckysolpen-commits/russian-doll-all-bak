INT. CLEAN ZONE "000010001000" - DIAMOND MINE - CENTRAL CHAMBER - CONTINUOUS

SOUND of the diamond mine's deep hum, the crackle of holographic displays, the weight of destiny

Future Sol stands at the edge of the diamond chamber. Lucky faces him. Astra, Gem, and Lauren stand behind Lucky — his team, his choice, his family.

The holographic displays around them show everything: Earth's Carcazod infiltration, the clown chaos, the Clean Zones, the Silent Shadow's approach, and beyond — threats Lucky can't even comprehend yet.

<center>FAKE LUCKY</center>
> I've been orchestrating events for centuries. Every moment that led you here was calculated. The CIA blackmail. Kaito's syndicate. The abduction. The Moon. Mars. All of it. A path to this moment.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> You used everyone.

<center>FAKE LUCKY</center>
> I SAVED everyone. Without my manipulation, you'd be dead in Afghanistan. The Carcazods would have consumed Earth unopposed. The Silent Shadow would have arrived to a divided, weakened humanity. I made you strong. I made you READY.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> You made him a weapon.

<center>FAKE LUCKY</center>
> (turning to her)
> A weapon is what you need when the enemy is a force of nature. The Carcazods aren't evil. They're CONSUMPTION. The Silent Shadow isn't malicious. It's ENTROPY. You don't negotiate with entropy. You fight it. With everything you have.

Lucky steps closer. His navy mohawk catches the diamond light.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> The gold. The guns. The hoarding. It's not just preparation. It's fear. You're afraid that even with all your planning, it won't be enough.

Future Sol is quiet. For the first time, the mask of certainty slips.

<center>FAKE LUCKY</center>
> I've seen futures where we lose. Thousands of them. In most of them, humanity is consumed. Erased. Forgotten. In a handful — a HANDFUL — we survive. And in every one of those, YOU are the one who makes it happen. You wield the SolPen. You unite the factions. You fight the Shadow.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> And what happens to YOU?

Future Sol smiles. It's sad. Resigned.

<center>FAKE LUCKY</center>
> I cease to exist. When you take my place — when you become Sol — the timeline corrects. I become a memory. A possibility that was realized. My consciousness merges with yours. Everything I know, everything I've learned, everything I've carried for centuries — it becomes YOURS.

<center>GEM</center>
> He'll die.

<center>FAKE LUCKY</center>
> He'll become. There's a difference.

Lucky stares at his future self. The man who carried the weight of centuries. Who manipulated, planned, sacrificed, and suffered — all for this moment. All for humanity's survival.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> If I do this... if I become you... do I lose myself? Or do I become more?

<center>FAKE LUCKY</center>
> Both. Neither. I can't explain it. You have to experience it. But I promise you this: you won't be alone. I'll be with you. Everything I am. Everything I've been. It'll all be yours.

Lucky closes his eyes. He thinks of the ranch. The horses. The robots. The genetically perfect crops. The marijuana greenhouse. The peace he left behind.

He thinks of the Moon. The transparent reptilian beings in the shadows. The Type C blood. The SolPen. The clones whose souls power creation.

He thinks of Mars. The Red Queen. The basketball game. The punk rock clown executions. The SSOL disappearing into silence.

He thinks of Earth. The clowns. The Carcazods. The Clean Zone. The diamond mine. The gold and guns stockpiled for a war that never ends.

He opens his eyes.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> I'll do it. Not because you planned it. Not because the timeline demands it. Because it's the right thing to do. That's MY choice. Not yours.

Future Sol nods. Tears — actual tears — run down his face.

<center>FAKE LUCKY</center>
> That's exactly what I hoped you'd say.

He extends his hand. Lucky takes it.

The SolPen in Lucky's holster GLOWS. The diamond mine shakes. The holographic displays flare. Future Sol's body begins to DISSOLVE — not into nothing, but into LIGHT. Pure, brilliant light that flows into Lucky, through the SolPen, into his very being.

Lucky screams. Not in pain. In TRANSFORMATION.

Astra watches, tears streaming down her face. Gem holds her hand. Lauren stands at attention, witnessing something she'll carry for the rest of her existence.

When the light fades, Lucky stands alone. But he's different. Older. Wiser. His eyes carry centuries.

His mohawk is WHITE.

He is Sol now.

<center>SOL (LUCKY)</center>
> (voice deeper, carrying the weight of two lifetimes)
> We have work to do.

FADE OUT.
