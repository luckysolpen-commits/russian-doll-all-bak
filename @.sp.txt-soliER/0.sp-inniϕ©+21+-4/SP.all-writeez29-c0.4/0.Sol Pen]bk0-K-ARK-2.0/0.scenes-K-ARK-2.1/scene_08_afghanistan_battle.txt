EXT. ABANDONED AIRFIELD BASE - AFGHANISTAN - NIGHT

SOUND of wind, distant desert sounds, a sense of desolate quiet

A vast, desolate landscape under a star-strewn sky. An abandoned airfield base, its runways cracked and overgrown. A single, battered cargo plane sits on the tarmac, its engines cold.

Lucky and Astra are there, dressed in tactical gear, blending into the shadows. Lucky's platinum mohawk is tucked under a tactical cap — but the gold chains are still there, peeking out from his body armor. He can't fully hide who he is. Astra looks tense, her hand resting on a holstered weapon.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> This feels wrong, Lucky. Dealing with these people...

<center>LUCKY</center>
> We have to play the game, Astra. Get close to the source. Find out what this "sensitive equipment" really is.

Kaito arrives with a contingent of his SHADOW SYNDICATE GUARDS, heavily armed. They are also dressed for combat. The air is thick with anticipation.

<center>KAITO</center>
> Our buyers will be here soon. They're... particular about punctuality.

Suddenly, the silence is shattered by the WHIRR of approaching helicopters. Not Kaito's. Not the buyers'.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Incoming!

From the darkness, a squad of US SPECIAL FORCES operatives descends, fast-roping from the helicopters. They are precise, efficient, and heavily armed.

<center>USSF COMMANDER (O.S.)</center>
> CIA! Drop your weapons!

Chaos erupts. Gunfire. Explosions. The airfield becomes a war zone. Kaito's men return fire, caught off guard.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (Shouting over the din)
> This wasn't part of the plan!

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (Gritting his teeth)
> When is it ever?!

Amidst the gunfire, a panicked voice from one of the Shadow Syndicate guards cuts through the noise.

<center>SHADOW SYNDICATE GUARD</center>
> GET THE NUKE!

Astra's eyes widen in shock.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> What NUKE?!

Lucky's gaze snaps to Kaito, who looks furious, betrayed.

<center>KAITO</center>
> This is not part of our deal!

The USSF are closing in. Terrorists, previously unseen, emerge from hidden bunkers, joining the fray, their weapons blazing. It's a three-way battle, a maelstrom of violence.

Lucky and Astra find themselves back-to-back, fighting for their lives.

FADE OUT.
