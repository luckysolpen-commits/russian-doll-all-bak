INT. CLEAN ZONE "000010001000" - ENTRANCE GATE - DAY

SOUND of energy barriers humming, the murmur of the elite, the contrast between chaos outside and order inside

Clean Zone "000010001000" is the richest, cleanest, most advanced safe zone on Earth. Its walls are pristine white — SolPen-constructed barriers that no Carcazod can penetrate. Inside: a super-advanced civilization. Outside: chaos.

Only the richest snobs are here. The powerful. The connected. The ones who bought their way in when the world started falling.

Lucky stands at the entrance gate. But something is WRONG.

Because standing on the other side of the gate — inside the Clean Zone, welcomed, protected — is LUCKY.

Same face. Same build. Same gold chains. But this Lucky's mohawk is pure white. His expression is cold. Calculating. And beside him, equally welcomed, equally protected, is GEM.

Not the Gem who traveled with Lucky from Mars. Another Gem. Or the same one, but on the wrong side.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (staring through the gate)
> What the hell is this?

ASTRA, the real Gem, and Lauren stand behind him. All three are stunned.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Is that... you?

<center>LUCKY</center>
> It's wearing my face.

The Fake Lucky — SOL IN DISGUISE, Lucky from the future, the architect of all of this — steps forward. His voice is Lucky's voice, but older. Wiser. More certain.

<center>FAKE LUCKY</center>
> Hello, Lucky. I've been expecting you.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (voice rising)
> Who ARE you?

<center>FAKE LUCKY</center>
> You know who I am. I'm you. From the future. The you that survives all of this. The you that wins.

<center>GEM (REAL)</center>
> (stepping forward, betrayed)
> And me? The Gem inside — is that me too?

<center>FAKE LUCKY</center>
> That's the Gem who understood the plan. Who stayed. Who helped me prepare.

The Fake Lucky gestures. Behind him, inside the Clean Zone, Lucky can see STASHES. Massive quantities of GOLD BARS. Crates of ADVANCED WEAPONS. SolPen constructs. Enough resources to arm an army.

All hidden inside a huge DIAMOND MINE that the Fake Lucky apparently owns and has headquartered within the Clean Zone.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> You're hoarding. While the world burns, you're hoarding gold and guns.

<center>FAKE LUCKY</center>
> I'm PREPARING. For the conflict that comes AFTER this one. The Carcazods are just the beginning. The Silent Shadow is coming. And when it arrives, humanity will need resources. Weapons. Leadership.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> You ordered the clowns to hunt us. To bring us here.

<center>FAKE LUCKY</center>
> I needed you alive. I needed you HERE. Inside the Clean Zone. Where I can protect you. Where you can fulfill your destiny.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> My destiny is MY choice. Not yours. Not Sol's. Not anyone's.

<center>FAKE LUCKY</center>
> (smiling sadly)
> That's what I said too. Once. Before I understood.

The energy barrier between them hums. Lucky can feel the SolPen in his holster, pulsing, wanting to act. But this isn't a fight he can win with force. Not yet.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> I'm not coming in there. Not like this. Not as your guest. Your prisoner. Your puppet.

<center>FAKE LUCKY</center>
> Then come as my equal. When you're ready. When you understand.

The Fake Lucky turns and walks away, into the Clean Zone, into his diamond mine, into his hoarded empire of gold and guns.

Lucky stands at the gate, staring at the life he could have. The safety. The protection. The easy way out.

He turns to Astra. To Gem. To Lauren.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> We're going in. But not through the front door.

FADE OUT.
