INT. MOON BASE - GENESIS CHAMBER - CONTINUOUS

SOUND of humming machinery, the gentle bubbling of liquid, the deep resonant pulse of ancient neural activity

The heavy doors to the Genesis Chamber slide open with a hiss, revealing a room bathed in a soft, blue-green light. Lucky and Astra step inside, Astra's bypass key still in hand.

The chamber is vast — far larger than Lucky expected. Rows upon rows of translucent pods line the walls, each containing a developing clone in fetal position. But these aren't ordinary clone pods.

Each pod is connected to a SECONDARY CHAMBER — smaller, darker, containing something that looks like a FOSSILIZED BRAIN. Reptilian. Ancient. Millions of years old. The fossil brains pulse with a faint, sickly green light, and thin neural cables connect them to the clone brains above.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (whispering, horrified)
> My god... It's a parasite system. The reptilian fossil brains are being merged with the clone neural pathways. They're not just powering the clones — they're CONTROLLING them.

Lucky moves to a console at the far end of the room. It's the source of the hidden data stream. The screen displays a complex neural map:

REPTILIAN FOSSIL BRAIN → CLONE NEURAL MERGE → PSIONIC TRANSMISSION ARRAY → MARS RELAY → RED QUEEN NEURAL SIGNATURE → PRINCESS MEGAN8R NEURAL SIGNATURE

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (reading aloud)
> "Nurture Sequence Initiated. Subjects: Red Queen Neural Template, Princess Megan8R Neural Template. Status: Accelerated Growth. Psionic Link: ACTIVE."

He turns to Astra, his face grim.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> He's not just studying them. He's GROWING replacement neural templates. Backup versions of the Red Queen and her daughter. Controlled by reptilian fossil brains. If the real ones go rogue, he can swap them out.

Suddenly, Sol's voice echoes through the chamber, calm and collected.

<center>SOL (O.S.)</center>
> I see you've discovered my little secret.

Sol enters the chamber, flanked by two guards. He doesn't seem angry, merely... disappointed. His masked face reflects the green pulse of the reptilian fossil brains.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (confronting him)
> The reptilian fossil brains. The mind control. The Red Queen. How much of her is real? How much is YOURS?

Sol pauses. For the first time, Lucky senses something behind the mask — not guilt, but the weight of an impossible decision.

<center>SOL</center>
> The Red Queen is real. Her consciousness is her own. Her daughter is her own. But the reptilian neural link... it's a SAFETY MEASURE.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> A safety measure? You're CONTROLLING them!

<center>SOL</center>
> I'm MONITORING them. The reptilian fossil brains don't control — they OBSERVE. They create a neural mirror, a window into the Red Queen's mind. I can see what she sees. Know what she knows. But I cannot MAKE her do anything.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Then why grow replacement neural templates? Why the "Nurture Sequence"?

Sol gestures to the pods. The green light pulses rhythmically, like a heartbeat from the dawn of time.

<center>SOL</center>
> Because the reptilian beings are not extinct, Lucky. They are HIDDEN. On the Moon. On Mars. On Earth. Transparent. Ancient. Patient. Their fossil brains are not fossils — they are DORMANT. And if the wrong mind activates them, they wake up.

He steps closer to Lucky.

<center>SOL</center>
> The Red Queen's cooperation with you was genuine. Her hatred of the clowns is real. Her desire to save Mars is authentic. But she carries something inside her — a reptilian neural seed, planted before she was born. If it activates, she becomes THEIR vessel. Not mine. THEIRS.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (stunned)
> So the Genesis Chamber isn't about controlling her. It's about PROTECTING her. From the reptilians.

<center>SOL</center>
> And preparing for the worst. If the seed activates, I need a backup. A clean neural template. One without the reptilian contamination. That's what these pods are. Not weapons. INSURANCE.

Lucky stares at the pods. At the reptilian fossil brains. At the neural cables connecting ancient minds to modern ones.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> The transparent beings we keep seeing. The ones in the shadows. They're watching her too.

<center>SOL</center>
> They're watching EVERYONE. They've been watching since before humanity walked on land. The Carcazods were their experiment. The clowns are their distraction. And the Red Queen... she is their chosen vessel.

Sol turns to leave. He pauses at the door.

<center>SOL</center>
> The mission to Mars is more critical than ever. You've seen what's at stake. The Red Queen is your ally. But she carries a bomb inside her mind. And the reptilians are waiting for the right moment to detonate it.

He exits. The guards remain.

Lucky and Astra stand in the green light of the Genesis Chamber, surrounded by the pulsing reptilian fossil brains and the clone pods they're parasitizing.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (quietly)
> When she cooperated with you. When she took you to the basketball game. When she executed the clown rebels. Was any of it her? Or was it the seed?

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (after a long pause)
> I think it was her. I think she's fighting the seed every day. And I think she doesn't know it's there.

He looks at the neural map one more time. At the Red Queen's signature. At the reptilian link.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> We need to get to Mars. We need to find her. And we need to remove that seed before the reptilians wake it up.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> How do you remove a reptilian neural seed from someone's brain?

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (touching his SolPen)
> The same way we do everything else. We build something new.

FADE OUT.
