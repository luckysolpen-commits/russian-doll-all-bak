INT. MARTIAN COLONY - POWER CORE/GENERATOR ROOM - DAY

SOUND of a deep, resonant hum, crackling energy, distant alarms

Lucky and Astra descend into the heart of the Martian colony – the POWER CORE. Lucky's mohawk has shifted to a deep crimson — a color that matches the pulsating generator ahead. He's got a fresh vial of Type C blood clipped to his belt, the crimson glow illuminating his Exo Suit as they move. It's a massive, cavernous chamber, dominated by a pulsating, glowing GENERATOR that throbs with raw energy. Cables as thick as tree trunks snake across the floor and up the walls, feeding power to the entire rebel complex.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> This is it. If we can disable this, their defenses will crumble. Their new Carcazods will be cut off from their energy source.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (Eyes scanning the complex security)
> Easier said than done. Looks like they've got a guardian.

From the shadows, a colossal, heavily armored CARCAZOD emerges. This isn't a mutated human; it's a purebred monstrosity, a walking tank of chitin and muscle. Its eyes glow with an malevolent intelligence, and its roar shakes the very foundations of the chamber.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (Voice tight)
> That's a new one. And it looks like it's been specifically engineered to protect this core.

The Carcazod charges, its massive fists slamming into the ground, sending tremors through the floor. Lucky and Astra split up, dodging its initial assault.

Lucky extends the SolPen, creating a series of rapidly deploying ENERGY MINES in the Carcazod's path. The creature barrels through them, shrugging off the explosions, but its momentum is momentarily disrupted.

Astra, meanwhile, targets the generator's exposed conduits with her energy weapon, but the blasts merely spark harmlessly against its reinforced plating.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> It's shielded! We need to overload it from the inside!

Lucky realizes the strategy. He needs to create a conduit for Astra's energy. He uses the SolPen to rapidly construct a complex, crystalline structure, a temporary energy siphon, directly connected to one of the generator's main power lines.

The Carcazod, recovering, lunges at Lucky. He barely dodges, the creature's claws tearing through the air where he stood moments before. He retaliates by creating a massive, solid ENERGY WALL, momentarily trapping the beast.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Astra! Now!

Astra aims her weapon at the SolPen-created siphon. She unleashes a sustained, powerful blast. The siphon glows brighter, channeling the energy directly into the generator's core.

The generator begins to OVERLOAD. Lights flicker, alarms blare louder, and sparks fly from every connection point. The Carcazod, sensing the impending collapse, roars in frustration, slamming against the energy wall, trying to break free.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> It's working! But we need to get out of here!

The energy wall holding the Carcazod begins to crack. Lucky, with a final surge of SolPen power, reinforces it just long enough for them to make their escape.

They race out of the power core as the chamber behind them erupts in a blinding flash of light and a deafening EXPLOSION. The entire colony shudders.

FADE OUT.
