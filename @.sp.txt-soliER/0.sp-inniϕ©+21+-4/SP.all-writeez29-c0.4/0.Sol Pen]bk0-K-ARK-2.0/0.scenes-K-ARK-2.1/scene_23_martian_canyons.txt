EXT. MARTIAN CANYONS - DAY

SOUND of howling Martian wind, the roar of engines, energy blasts

The red dust of Mars whips around Lucky, Astra, and Gem as they hurtle through a labyrinthine network of canyons. Their Light Exo Suits — supercharged by the Type C blood coursing through their veins — allow them to bound across the uneven terrain with incredible speed. Lucky's matte black mohawk streams behind him like a battle flag. They are being relentlessly pursued.

Behind them, two sleek, heavily armed REBEL SKIMMERS, designed for rapid Martian traversal, close in. Their energy cannons spit crackling bolts of plasma, narrowly missing the trio.

<center>GEM</center>
> (Into comms, strained)
> They're gaining! Their propulsion systems are superior in this environment!

<center>ASTRA</center>
> (Panting)
> Lucky, do something! We're exposed!

Lucky glances back, the skimmers filling his HUD. He extends the SolPen, its tip glowing with intense energy. He doesn't create a weapon, but a solution.

He points the SolPen at a narrow chasm ahead. With a surge of power, he rapidly materializes a series of interlocking, temporary ENERGY BRIDGES, shimmering constructs that span the gap. They are just wide enough for them to cross.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Go! Go! Go!

Astra and Gem leap across the first bridge, then the second. Lucky follows, the bridges dissolving behind them as soon as they clear, denying the skimmers a path.

The lead skimmer, unable to stop, crashes into the canyon wall where the bridge once was, exploding in a shower of sparks and debris. The second skimmer swerves, its pilot cursing.

<center>REBEL PILOT (V.O.)</center>
> He's manipulating the terrain! Get him!

Suddenly, from above, a flock of mutated, bat-like FLYING CARCAZODS descends, their leathery wings beating against the thin atmosphere. Their screeches are guttural, terrifying.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Aerial threats! They're flanking us!

Lucky pivots, the SolPen now a defensive tool. He creates a rapidly expanding, translucent ENERGY DOME around them, just as the Carcazods dive-bomb, their sharp claws scraping against the shimmering barrier.

The second skimmer opens fire again, its energy blasts impacting the dome, causing it to ripple and strain.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (Gritting his teeth)
> I can't hold this forever! We need cover!

He spots a narrow cave entrance in the canyon wall. With another burst of SolPen energy, he rapidly expands the opening, creating a temporary, reinforced tunnel.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Inside! Now!

They dive into the makeshift tunnel as the energy dome shatters behind them. The skimmer's blasts and the Carcazods' attacks slam into the rock face, sending dust and debris flying.

Inside the tunnel, they are safe, for now. Lucky leans against the rough rock, panting, the SolPen still glowing faintly in his hand.

<center>GEM</center>
> That was... resourceful, Lucky.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> (A grim smile)
> Just getting started. They want a chase? We'll give them a chase.

FADE OUT.
