**Planets and Locations**

*   **Earth:** The starting point of the story. A technologically advanced world, but one that is also plagued by conflict and corruption.
*   **The Moon:** Home to a hidden lunar society led by Sol. The moon base, Lunaria, is a vast, underground city filled with clone soldiers and advanced technology.
*   **Mars:** The site of a clone rebellion led by the Black Queen. The Martian colony is a desolate, windswept outpost.

**Spaceships**

*   **SSOL:** Sol's mothership. A massive, technologically advanced spacecraft capable of interstellar travel. It serves as a mobile command center, a self-sustaining ecosystem, and houses advanced training facilities and research labs.

**Items**

*   **SolPen:** A 3D digital printer that uses "clone souls as power." It is a powerful and versatile tool that can be used to create a wide variety of objects.
*   **HALO Bio Software HUD:** A holographic heads-up display that provides the user with a variety of information and tactical data.
*   **Light Exo Suit:** A lightweight, form-fitting suit that provides the user with enhanced strength, speed, and agility.
*   **Universal Bypass Key:** A device that can be used to bypass electronic locks and security systems.

**Weapons**

*   **Energy Blades:** Melee weapons that are used by Princess Megan8r.
*   **Nanobots:** Microscopic robots that can be used to disable electronic systems.

**Factions**

*   **The Lunarians:** A technologically advanced society that lives on the moon. They are led by Sol, and their goal is to create a "Clean Zone" on Earth, free from chaos and conflict.
*   **The Clone Rebels:** A group of clones who have rebelled against their creators. They are led by the Black Queen, and their goal is to create a new society on Mars.
*   **The Shadow Syndicate:** A powerful criminal organization that is led by Kaito.
*   **The CIA:** A ruthless government agency that is led by Agent Thompson.

**Miscellaneous**

*   **Carcazods:** A mysterious alien race that is a threat to the "Clean Zone."
*   **The Clean Zone:** A region of space that Sol is trying to create, free from chaos and conflict.
