Finally, as the clock struck 2 AM, Lucky decided it was time to call it a night. He excused himself from the party, citing an early meeting with investors the next day. The staff and guests alike bid him farewell as he made his way back to his private quarters.

With a tired grin, Lucky climbed onto his private plane, a sleek black jet that would take him back to his ranch in style. As the engines roared to life, he settled into the plush leather seat, feeling the weight of the day lift off his shoulders.

The flight was smooth and quiet, the only sound being the hum of the engines and Lucky's occasional chuckle as he replayed the highlights of the party in his mind.

Upon landing at the ranch, Lucky stretched his arms above his head, feeling the gentle night breeze rustle his hair. He ambled out to the porch, where a warm fire crackled in the stone pit. A cold drink awaited him on the swing, and he sank into the cushions with a satisfied sigh.

Just as he was starting to unwind, Lucky's eyes flicked to the small device in his hand – the one that allowed him to keep an eye on the casino from afar. He glanced at it briefly, then switched it off, not wanting to ruin the peace and quiet of the night.
