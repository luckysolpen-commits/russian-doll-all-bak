SCENE 3

EXT. LUNAR SHUTTLE LAUNCHPAD - NIGHT

SOUND of powerful engines spooling up

A massive, sleek LUNAR SHUTTLE stands bathed in the glow of floodlights. The air hums with anticipation. Lucky, Astra, Gem, and the Korean tear-it users (JIN, MIN, YOON) are dressed in new, pristine space suits, their faces a mixture of excitement and determination.

MR. KIM, accompanied by a small entourage of his own, shakes Lucky's hand.

MR. KIM
(Firmly)
My investment is significant, Mr. Lucky. I trust your team will prove worthy of it.

LUCKY
(Smiling)
We won't disappoint, Mr. Kim. We're calling ourselves "THE CHAEBOL" now. A new era.

Mr. Kim offers a rare, slight smile. He then turns to Jin, Min, and Yoon.

MR. KIM (CONT'D)
Ensure our interests are protected. And learn everything you can.

JIN
(Bowing slightly)
Yes, sir.

As they board the shuttle, Astra looks back at the distant, shimmering lights of Dorchester, a pang of nostalgia in her eyes.

ASTRA
(To Lucky)
Goodbye, Dorchester. For now.

LUCKY
(Putting a hand on her shoulder)
Hello, Moon. And beyond.

INT. LUNAR SHUTTLE - CONTINUOUS

The interior of the shuttle is luxurious, designed for comfort during the long journey. Lucky, Astra, and Gem are seated together, while the Koreans are engrossed in their own advanced tablets.

GEM
(Grinning)
Ten trillion won. Can you believe it? We're going to be rich!

ASTRA
(Thoughtful)
It's not just about the money, Gem. It's about what we can do with it. The possibilities.

Lucky gazes out the viewport as Earth shrinks behind them, a beautiful, fragile marble. The moon, a crescent of light, grows larger ahead.

LUCKY
(Quietly)
It's about a new adventure. A new frontier.

FADE OUT.
