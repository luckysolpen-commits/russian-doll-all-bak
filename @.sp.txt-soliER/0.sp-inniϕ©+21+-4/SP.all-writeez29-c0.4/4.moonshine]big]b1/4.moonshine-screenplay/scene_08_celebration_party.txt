SCENE 8

INT. LUNAR BASE - RECREATIONAL AREA - NIGHT

SOUND of upbeat music, laughter, clinking glasses

The recreational area is transformed into a vibrant party space. Holographic projections of Earth and the asteroid belt swirl around the room. The Chaebol team, along with other lunar base personnel, are celebrating.

EDGE, ever the showman, is at the center of a group, holding a bottle of champagne aloft.

EDGE
(Toasts)
To new discoveries! To old friendships! And to Mr. Kim's very generous funding! May our pockets be as full as our hearts!

Cheers erupt. Glasses clink. Lucky, Astra, and Gem raise their glasses.

LUCKY
(To Astra, smiling)
To us.

ASTRA
(Her eyes sparkling)
To our next adventure.

Gem clinks his glass against theirs, beaming.

GEM
To a killer team!

Jin, Min, and Yoon are showing off their latest VR gaming moves on a dance floor, their synchronized movements drawing a crowd. They laugh as they try to outdo each other.

Jin approaches Edge, a mischievous glint in his eye.

JIN
(To Edge)
Another stunt, Edge? For the camera?

EDGE
(Grinning)
Always, my friend! Always!

Edge grabs a nearby hoverboard and performs a series of daring, low-gravity tricks, much to the delight of the crowd. Lucky watches, a genuine smile on his face this time. Edge is still a showboat, but he's *their* showboat.

Later, Lucky and Astra slip away from the main party, finding a quieter corner near a panoramic window overlooking the lunar landscape.

ASTRA
(Sighs contentedly)
This is nice. A moment of peace.

LUCKY
(Nodding)
It is. You know, I never thought I'd be here. On the moon, mining asteroids, celebrating with a demon-fighting stuntman.

Astra laughs, leaning her head on his shoulder.

ASTRA
Life's full of surprises. But I wouldn't want to be here with anyone else.

Lucky puts his arm around her, gazing out at the stars.

LUCKY
Me neither, Astra. Me neither.

FADE OUT.
