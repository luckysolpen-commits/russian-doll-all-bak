SCENE 18

INT. THE DIABOLUS - DEMON'S LAIR - CONTINUOUS

SOUND of guttural growls, crackling energy

The team stands at the entrance of a colossal, nightmarish palace. Its walls are made of a pulsating, organic substance, and the air is thick with malevolent energy. Kofi and his two humanoid robots lead the way, their weapons at the ready.

DR. PATEL
(Whispering)
This is it. The source of the energy.

EDGE
(Grinning, pulling out a sleek, futuristic energy pistol)
Alright, big guy! Time to meet your new landlords!

Kofi turns, his eyes flashing.

KOFI
Caution, Mr. Danger Man! This is not a place for bravado. We must proceed with respect.

Edge ignores him, striding forward. He fires a warning shot into the cavernous space. The energy bolt dissipates harmlessly against the organic walls.

Suddenly, a towering, grotesque DEMON emerges from the shadows. Its body is a mass of writhing, pulsating tendrils, its eyes glowing with ancient malevolence. It lets out a guttural GROWL that shakes the very foundations of the palace.

Edge, undeterred, fires again, this time directly at the demon. The energy bolt strikes its chest, but the demon merely absorbs it, its tendrils seeming to grow larger, more vibrant.

DEMON
(Voice like grinding stone, echoing)
Foolish mortals. You seek to provoke that which feeds on your aggression.

DR. PATEL
(Panicked)
Edge, stop! You're agitating it!

But Edge is caught up in his own hubris. He fires a rapid succession of shots, his face contorted in a desperate attempt to assert dominance.

EDGE
(Shouting)
Come on, you overgrown squid! Is that all you got?! I've faced bigger!

The demon lets out a terrifying ROAR. Its tendrils lash out, moving with impossible speed. One tendril smashes into Kofi's humanoid robots, crushing them instantly into twisted metal and sparking wires.

Kofi, with a cry of rage, lunges forward, his spear glowing. He strikes the demon, but the blow is absorbed. The demon backhands him, sending him flying across the chamber, crashing against the organic wall.

Edge, seeing the raw power of the demon, finally understands his mistake. Fear, cold and absolute, grips him. He turns, his bravado shattered.

EDGE
(Stammering)
Retreat! Retreat!

He scrambles back, followed by Dr. Patel and the UN officials, their faces pale with terror. They flee the lair, leaving Kofi, battered but alive, and the destroyed robots behind.

FADE OUT.
