SCENE 9

INT. LUNAR BASE - ASTRA'S QUARTERS - NIGHT

SOUND of soft, rhythmic breathing

Astra is asleep in her bunk, the room dimly lit by the soft glow of the lunar night outside her viewport. Suddenly, a shimmering, ethereal light fills the room. Astra's eyes flutter open.

Standing at the foot of her bed is LOONA TWILIGHT (ageless, regal, her form composed of shifting light and color), accompanied by two smaller, silent HOLOGRAPHIC FIGURES.

ASTRA
(Groggily, then with a jolt of recognition)
Twilight? What... what are you doing here?

LOONA TWILIGHT
(Her voice a melodic whisper, echoing slightly)
Forgive the intrusion, Astra. But there is a matter of grave importance. Something we have observed from the deeper currents of the cosmos.

Astra sits up, fully awake now, a chill running down her spine. Loona Twilight's visits are never casual.

ASTRA
What is it? Is it about the asteroid belt?

LOONA TWILIGHT
(Nodding, her form rippling)
Indeed. One of the celestial bodies you have been studying. You call it... Asteroid-6.

Astra frowns. Asteroid-6 was one of the less remarkable ones they'd scanned, mostly rocky, nothing of significant value.

ASTRA
Asteroid-6? What about it?

LOONA TWILIGHT
It is not what it appears. It moves with an unnatural velocity, far exceeding its gravitational parameters. And its energy signature... it is unlike anything we have ever encountered. It is hollow, Astra. And it emanates a dark energy.

Astra's eyes widen. She remembers a faint anomaly in the initial scans, dismissed as a sensor glitch.

ASTRA
(Whispering)
Hollow? A dark energy? What do you mean?

LOONA TWILIGHT
(Her voice growing more somber)
We believe it is not a natural formation. It is... a vessel. And it is accelerating. Towards Earth.

Astra gasps, her mind racing to comprehend the implications. A vessel? Heading for Earth? This was beyond anything they had prepared for.

ASTRA
(Stammering)
A spacecraft? From... from where? What is it?

LOONA TWILIGHT
(Her form flickering slightly, as if under strain)
Its true nature is veiled. But the dark energy... it is malevolent. Ancient. Whatever lies within, it is not benign. And if it reaches Earth...

Loona Twilight's form begins to fade, the holographic figures dissolving around her.

LOONA TWILIGHT (CONT'D)
You must warn them, Astra. The fate of your world rests on your swift action.

And then, she is gone, leaving Astra alone in the dim light, the chilling words echoing in her mind. The soft hum of the lunar base suddenly feels ominous.

FADE OUT.
