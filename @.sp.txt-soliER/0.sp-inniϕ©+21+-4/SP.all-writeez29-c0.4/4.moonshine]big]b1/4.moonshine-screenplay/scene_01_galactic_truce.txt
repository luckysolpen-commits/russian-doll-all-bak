SCENE 1

INT. MAKESHIFT COMMAND CENTER - LUNAR BASE - DAY

SOUND of distant, fading explosions

Dust motes dance in the harsh, utilitarian light of a temporary command center. Makeshift screens flicker with news reports – headlines screaming about the UN/USA TREATY and the sudden, unexpected end to the galactic war.

LUCKY (30s, charismatic, weary but with a spark in his eyes) stares at a screen, rubbing his temples. He wears a patched-up combat suit, still bearing the grime of recent battle.

ASTRA (30s, sharp, perceptive, Lucky's partner) stands beside him, her expression a mix of relief and suspicion. She gestures to a news feed.

ASTRA
(Quietly)
China's threat. That's what did it. Forced their hand.

GEM (30s, energetic, always looking for an angle) paces, a wide grin spreading across his face. He's already shedding his combat gear, revealing a surprisingly clean jumpsuit underneath.

GEM
(Excited)
Can you believe it? A truce! Just like that! No more Carc-Zillas, no more NewKorean skirmishes... for now, anyway.

Lucky sighs, running a hand through his hair.

LUCKY
It feels... unfinished. Like the calm before another storm.

Across the room, a group of NEWKOREAN SOLDIERS, still in their sleek, advanced armor, eye Lucky's team warily. Their leader, a stern-faced CAPTAIN (40s), approaches.

CAPTAIN
(Korean, translated)
Your forces are withdrawing, yes? Our orders are to hold position until further notice.

Lucky nods, offering a tired smile.

LUCKY
Same here, Captain. Looks like we're all in limbo.

The Captain studies him for a moment, then nods curtly and returns to his men. The tension in the room, though lessened, is still palpable.

Astra turns back to Lucky, her voice low.

ASTRA
So, what now? Go back to Dorchester? Try to pick up the pieces?

Lucky looks around the command center, at the weary faces of his team, at the uncertain NewKoreans. A new thought, a glimmer of possibility, begins to form in his mind.

LUCKY
(A faint smile)
Maybe... maybe we don't go back to picking up pieces. Maybe we start building something new.

FADE OUT.
