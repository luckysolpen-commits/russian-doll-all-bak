SCENE 2

INT. LUNA'S LUXURY HABITAT - LUNAR BASE - NIGHT

SOUND of soft, ambient music

The habitat is sleek and futuristic, with panoramic views of Earth hanging like a blue marble in the blackness. LUNA (30s, sharp, confident, dressed in elegant, flowing attire) stands before a holographic display, which projects intricate diagrams of asteroid belts and mineral compositions.

Lucky, Astra, and Gem are seated at a polished table, along with MR. KIM (50s, impeccably dressed, shrewd, the Korean representative). Jin, Min, and Yoon are also present, observing with keen interest.

LUNA
(Smiling warmly)
Gentlemen, and ladies, thank you for joining me. I believe I have an opportunity that will redefine your understanding of wealth.

Gem leans forward, eyes gleaming.

GEM
We're listening, Luna. Always open to redefining wealth.

LUNA
(Gesturing to the holographic display)
For years, the focus has been on terrestrial resources. But the true treasure, the untapped fortune, lies out there. In the newly discovered asteroid belt.

The display shifts, highlighting specific asteroids, their projected mineral content glowing.

LUNA (CONT'D)
We're talking rare earth elements – neodymium, dysprosium – crucial for our burgeoning electric vehicle and renewable energy sectors. And precious metals: platinum, palladium, rhodium. The demand is skyrocketing.

Mr. Kim, who has been silently observing, finally speaks, his voice calm but authoritative.

MR. KIM
And the investment? The projected returns?

LUNA
(Confidently)
An initial investment of $500 million. This covers establishing a lunar base, state-of-the-art extraction equipment, and a world-class team. As for returns... we're projecting at least 500% within the first five years. Dividends distributed quarterly.

Astra exchanges a look with Lucky. The numbers are staggering.

ASTRA
That's... ambitious. What about the logistics? Extraction, refining, shipping?

LUNA
(Her smile unwavering)
We have a comprehensive plan. The lunar base will be our hub. Missions to select asteroids, resources transported back here for refining, then shipped to Earth. We've already secured preliminary agreements with several major corporations.

Gem lets out a low whistle.

GEM
Five hundred percent... that's not just redefining wealth, Luna. That's rewriting the rulebook.

Lucky, who has been quiet, absorbing every detail, finally speaks, his voice firm.

LUCKY
We're in. We want in on this opportunity.

Luna's smile widens, a genuine warmth in her eyes.

LUNA
Excellent. I'll send over the detailed financial projections and business plan for your review. We can discuss the specifics further.

Mr. Kim, however, holds up a hand.

MR. KIM
(To Lucky)
My investment, Mr. Lucky, will be substantial. Ten trillion won. I will require... due diligence. A personal inspection of the lunar operations. My team will accompany yours.

Lucky nods, understanding the weight of the request.

LUCKY
Understood, Mr. Kim. We welcome your oversight.

FADE OUT.
