SCENE 7

INT. LUNAR BASE - RESEARCH LAB - DAY

SOUND of scientific equipment humming, soft beeps

The research lab is a hive of activity. Asteroid Alpha-2 and Asteroid Gamma-3, now much smaller, are secured on examination tables. Holographic displays project intricate molecular structures and energy readings.

Astra, Gem, Jin, Min, and Yoon are meticulously analyzing the asteroid samples. Astra operates a QUANTUM COMPUTER, its crystalline core glowing softly, while Gem uses a NUCLEAR SCANNER, its beam sweeping over the asteroid's surface.

ASTRA
(Excitedly)
Readings confirm high concentrations of platinum and iridium! Even trace amounts of... antimatter? That's unprecedented for a natural formation of this size!

GEM
(Whistling)
Antimatter? Luna wasn't kidding about the potential. This is beyond anything we projected.

Jin and Min are operating advanced EXTRACTION ARMS, carefully siphoning off refined minerals into containment units. Yoon is meticulously cataloging the findings.

YOON
(To herself)
Gamma-3 shows an unusual crystalline structure. High potential for new rare earth elements. Edge was right about this one.

Speaking of Edge, he enters the lab, still in his jumpsuit, but now with a pair of stylish, futuristic goggles pushed up onto his forehead. He surveys the scene with a proprietary air.

EDGE
(Grinning)
See? Told you these rocks had personality! And profit!

Lucky enters, carrying a data pad. He looks at the glowing displays, a sense of wonder on his face.

LUCKY
(To Astra)
This is incredible, Astra. We're literally unlocking the secrets of the universe.

ASTRA
(Smiling, tired but happy)
And the secrets of a very lucrative market, Lucky. Mr. Kim is going to be very pleased.

Edge claps Lucky on the back.

EDGE
Damn right he is! We're not just miners, boss. We're cosmic prospectors! And we're about to hit the motherlode!

FADE OUT.
