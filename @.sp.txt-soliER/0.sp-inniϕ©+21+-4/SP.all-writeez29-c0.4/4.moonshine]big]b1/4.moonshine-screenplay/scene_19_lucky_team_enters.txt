SCENE 19

INT. THE DIABOLUS - ENTRY POINT - DAY (DEMON REALM)

SOUND of otherworldly wind, distant groans, now with a hint of despair

Lucky, Astra, Gem, Jin, Min, and Yoon stand at the entry point to The Diabolus, watching the retreating figures of Edge and the UN team. The air is heavy with the demon's malevolent energy, now more palpable than ever.

GEM
(Shaking his head)
Idiots. Absolute idiots. They just made it worse.

ASTRA
(Her face grim)
He's feeding. We can feel it. The violence... it's making it stronger.

Lucky looks at his team, his gaze resolute. The fear is there, but so is a quiet determination.

LUCKY
(Voice firm)
Alright. Our turn. We go in. And we don't fight it. We appease it.

LOONA (Hologram, appearing beside them, her form more solid now)
(Her voice calm, but with an underlying urgency)
It will demand trials. Tests of your worthiness. It seeks understanding, not destruction. But it will be cunning. And it will exploit your weaknesses.

JIN
(Nervously)
Trials? What kind of trials?

LOONA
(Her gaze sweeping over them)
Trials of wit, of courage, of self-sacrifice. It will force you to confront your deepest fears, your greatest desires. And it will offer temptations.

Lucky takes a deep breath, then looks at Astra.

LUCKY
Ready?

ASTRA
(Nodding, a flicker of resolve in her eyes)
As I'll ever be.

They step forward, into the swirling darkness of The Diabolus. The landscape shifts around them, the organic structures seeming to twist and writhe, forming a labyrinth of illusions.

Suddenly, the path ahead splits into three. Each path shimmers with a different, unsettling light. One glows with a seductive, golden hue. Another pulses with a cold, blue light. The third is shrouded in an oppressive, inky blackness.

DEMON (V.O.)
(Voice echoing from all directions, a low rumble)
Choose. Your first trial begins. Choose wisely, mortals. For your choices will define your fate.

Lucky looks at his team. They are in this together. He points to the path shrouded in darkness.

LUCKY
(To his team)
We go through the darkness. It's the only way to truly face it.

FADE OUT.
