SCENE 11

INT. EARTH - VARIOUS NEWS CHANNELS - DAY

SOUND of frantic news anchors, alarm sirens

Multiple news channels flicker across a split screen. The tone is one of escalating panic. Graphics of a menacing, dark asteroid with a glowing red core dominate the screens. Text overlays read: "ASTEROID-6: THE DIABOLUS?" and "IMMINENT THREAT TO EARTH?"

NEWS ANCHOR 1 (O.S.)
...and in an unprecedented move, the United Nations has officially labeled Asteroid-6 as "The Diabolus," citing its erratic trajectory and anomalous energy readings as a direct and immediate threat to global security.

NEWS ANCHOR 2 (O.S.)
...scientists worldwide are scrambling to understand the implications. Early projections suggest a potential impact zone that could trigger a new ice age, wiping out life as we know it...

NEWS ANCHOR 3 (O.S.)
...panic buying has begun in major cities, with reports of civil unrest and widespread fear. Governments are urging calm, but the public is demanding answers...

INT. LUNAR BASE - COMMAND CENTER - DAY

Lucky, Astra, Gem, Jin, Min, and Yoon watch the news feeds, their faces grim. The comms room is still locked down, but the news has found its way to them.

GEM
(Voice hollow)
So much for keeping it quiet, Edge.

Edge Danger Man stands apart, watching the screens with a mixture of dread and a strange, almost desperate resolve. His usual swagger is gone, replaced by a tense stillness.

ASTRA
(To Lucky)
They know. Somehow, they know. Loona Twilight's warning... it wasn't just for us.

LUCKY
(His jaw tight)
No. It was for everyone.

Edge turns, his eyes hard.

EDGE
Alright. This changes things. We can't just sit here. The UN is sending a team. And if they find out about... everything... it's over. For all of us.

JIN
(Confused)
What do you mean, 'everything'?

Edge ignores him, his gaze fixed on Lucky.

EDGE
I'm going to meet them. I'm going to cut a deal. We offer our expertise, our resources. We become the solution. And in exchange... they keep their noses out of our past. My past.

Astra steps forward, her voice firm.

ASTRA
Edge, you can't be serious. We're scientists, not diplomats. We need to focus on understanding this thing, not making backroom deals.

EDGE
(His voice rising, a desperate edge to it)
You don't understand! This isn't about science anymore! This is about survival! My survival! If they dig into my past, I'm finished! Australian prison, penniless... my family...

He trails off, a raw vulnerability in his eyes that quickly hardens into defiance.

EDGE (CONT'D)
I'm taking charge. This is a fight-or-flight situation, and I'm fighting. I'm going to make them an offer they can't refuse.

Lucky watches him, a dawning realization. Edge isn't just reckless; he's cornered. And a cornered animal is the most dangerous.

FADE OUT.
