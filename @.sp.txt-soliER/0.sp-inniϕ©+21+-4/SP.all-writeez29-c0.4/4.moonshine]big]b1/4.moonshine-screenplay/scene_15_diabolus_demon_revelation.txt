SCENE 15

INT. LUNAR BASE - SECURE LAB - NIGHT

SOUND of SoulPens humming, low thrumming

Lucky, Astra, Gem, Jin, Min, and Yoon are gathered around a central console. Several SOULPENS, intricate devices glowing with an inner light, are connected to a holographic projector. The projector displays a complex, swirling image of The Diabolus, far more detailed than any previous scan.

LUCKY
(Intensely)
Boost the SoulPen output. I want every nuance, every energy signature.

Jin adjusts a dial, and the holographic image sharpens, revealing pulsating veins of dark energy within the asteroid. The thrumming sound intensifies.

ASTRA
(Her eyes wide with a mixture of awe and dread)
It's... it's not just an asteroid. It's a living entity. A construct.

GEM
(Staring at the display)
And those energy readings... they're not just dark. They're... malevolent. Like a predatory intelligence.

Loona's holographic form flickers into existence beside them, her expression grave.

LOONA
(Her voice a somber whisper)
My earlier warnings were incomplete. The Diabolus is not merely a vessel. It is a prison. And what is imprisoned within... is a demon.

Silence descends upon the lab, broken only by the hum of the SoulPens. The revelation hangs heavy in the air.

LUCKY
(Voice barely audible)
A demon? You mean... literally?

LOONA
(Nodding slowly)
Its essence is pure malevolence. And its nature... it feeds on conflict. On violence. If you attempt to destroy it with force, with weapons...

MIN
(Finishing her sentence, horrified)
It will feed. It will grow stronger.

YOON
(His face pale)
And then... it will devour Earth. To become larger. To spawn... demon aliens.

The holographic image of The Diabolus seems to pulse with a new, terrifying life. The implications of their backup plan, the one involving high-powered Tear-It energy, suddenly become horrifyingly clear.

ASTRA
(Her voice filled with despair)
We can't use the Tear-It weapons. We can't agitate it. It would be suicide. For everyone.

Lucky stares at the demonic image, the weight of the world pressing down on him. Their most desperate solution has just become their greatest threat.

LUCKY
(His voice firm, despite the fear)
Then we don't destroy it. We go inside. We find another way.

FADE OUT.
