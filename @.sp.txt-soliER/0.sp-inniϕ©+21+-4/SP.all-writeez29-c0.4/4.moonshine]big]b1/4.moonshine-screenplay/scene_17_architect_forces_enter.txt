SCENE 17

EXT. THE DIABOLUS - ENTRY POINT - DAY (DEMON REALM)

SOUND of otherworldly wind, distant groans

The exterior of The Diabolus is no longer a simple asteroid. It is a swirling vortex of dark energy, pulsating with an ominous light. Several sleek, advanced SHIPS, unlike any seen before, pierce the swirling darkness, followed by the UN's more utilitarian vessels.

INT. ARCHITECT'S SHIP - BRIDGE - CONTINUOUS

Edge Danger Man, Dr. Patel, and the UN officials are on the bridge of one of The Architect's ships. The interior is minimalist, yet incredibly advanced. Holographic displays show the swirling chaos outside.

DR. PATEL
(Awe-struck)
This is... beyond anything I've ever seen. The energy readings are off the charts.

EDGE
(Grinning, a touch of nervousness in his voice)
Just another day at the office, Doctor! Though I admit, this office has a certain... demonic charm.

A figure enters the bridge. This is KOFI (40s, imposing, clad in ceremonial African regalia, carrying a traditional, yet futuristic, spear). He is flanked by two towering, silent HUMANoid ROBOTS, their forms sleek and powerful.

KOFI
(Voice deep, resonant)
We are entering the realm. Prepare for atmospheric shift.

The ship shudders as it passes through an invisible barrier. The swirling darkness outside gives way to a landscape of twisted, organic structures. The sky is a sickly green, and the ground beneath them seems to writhe.

UN OFFICIAL 1
(Pale)
What is this place?

KOFI
(Calmly)
The Demon Realm. A place of ancient power. My people have guarded its secrets for generations. The Architect believes its energies can be... repurposed.

Edge looks at Kofi, a flicker of surprise in his eyes. This isn't just a security detail; Kofi is clearly more than he seems.

EDGE
(To Kofi)
Repurposed, eh? Sounds like my kind of project!

Kofi turns to Edge, his eyes unreadable.

KOFI
This is not a project, Mr. Danger Man. This is a sacred trust. We are here to ensure the balance is maintained. And to claim what is rightfully ours.

Dr. Patel watches the exchange, a new layer of complexity added to her understanding of The Architect's involvement. This isn't just about technology; it's about ancient power and hidden agendas.

FADE OUT.
