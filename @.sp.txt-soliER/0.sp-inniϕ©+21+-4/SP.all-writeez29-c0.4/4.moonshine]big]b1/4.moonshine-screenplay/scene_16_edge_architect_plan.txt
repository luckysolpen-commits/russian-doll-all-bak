SCENE 16

INT. LUNAR BASE - UN CONFERENCE ROOM - DAY

SOUND of confident voices, holographic projections

Edge Danger Man stands before Dr. Patel and the UN officials, a new holographic projection of The Diabolus behind him. This projection, however, highlights potential entry points and internal structures, not demonic energy.

EDGE
(Beaming)
...and thanks to our accelerated analysis, we've identified multiple access points! The Diabolus isn't just a threat, ladies and gentlemen. It's a treasure trove! A cosmic vault, waiting to be opened!

Dr. Patel nods, a glint of commercial interest in her eyes. She's no longer just concerned with planetary defense; the potential for profit is clear.

DR. PATEL
(To Edge)
And the internal composition? Your earlier reports were... vague.

EDGE
(Waving a hand dismissively)
Early days, Doctor! We've since refined our scans. We're looking at unprecedented mineral deposits. And something else... something truly unique. A power source, perhaps. Or a new form of energy.

He gestures to a new figure entering the room. This is a stern-faced, impeccably dressed MAN (40s), radiating an aura of quiet authority. This is THE ARCHITECT'S REPRESENTATIVE.

ARCHITECT'S REPRESENTATIVE
(Voice smooth, controlled)
My employer, The Architect, has been following your progress with great interest, Dr. Patel. He believes this 'Diabolus' holds the key to unlocking new frontiers in energy and propulsion. He is prepared to offer substantial funding and resources for an exploratory mission.

Edge grins, a triumphant look at Dr. Patel. His deal is paying off.

DR. PATEL
(To the Representative)
And what would The Architect expect in return?

ARCHITECT'S REPRESENTATIVE
Exclusive access to any discoveries made within. And a share of the intellectual property. We believe this could revolutionize warp drive technology. And perhaps... antimatter containment.

Edge's eyes gleam. This is bigger than he imagined. Not just immunity, but a piece of a potentially world-changing discovery.

EDGE
(To Dr. Patel)
It's a win-win, Doctor! We get the resources, the funding, and the expertise to neutralize the threat. And Earth gets a technological leap forward!

Dr. Patel considers the offer. The UN's budget is stretched thin, and the public is demanding action. The Architect's resources could be invaluable.

DR. PATEL
(To the Representative)
We will require a detailed proposal. And full transparency on your intentions.

ARCHITECT'S REPRESENTATIVE
(A slight, knowing smile)
Of course. Our intentions are purely... scientific. And commercially viable. We believe this is a unique opportunity to turn a global threat into a global asset.

Edge claps his hands together, already envisioning the headlines.

EDGE
Then it's settled! We go in! The Diabolus, here we come!

FADE OUT.
