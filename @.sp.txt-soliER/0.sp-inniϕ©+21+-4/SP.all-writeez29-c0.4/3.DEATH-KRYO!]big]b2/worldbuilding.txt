**Worldbuilding: DEATH-KRYO!**

**1. Key Locations/Settings:**

*   **The Soul Pen:** A lavish, high-tech underwater nightclub. A marvel of engineering with transparent walls, bioluminescent flora, and a mix of human and mer-people clientele. It serves as a symbol of Lucky's initial success and the setting for the inciting incident.
*   **Underwater Ruins:** Ancient, forgotten cities beneath the ocean, now covered in coral and seaweed. Eerie and beautiful, they hold secrets and serve as temporary hideouts or sources of cryptic clues.
*   **Dorchester's Lair:** A massive, hollowed-out asteroid, hidden in a remote sector of the ocean. A chaotic but functional space, blending high-tech equipment with pirate aesthetics and ancient artifacts. It reflects Dorchester's personality and resources.
*   **New Atlantis:** A stunning underwater city, a blend of ancient Greek and modern Asian architecture. Bustling, multicultural, and a hub of both legitimate and illicit activities. It represents the complex political landscape of the underwater world.
*   **Sub-Korea Research Institute:** A front for New-Korea and Sub-Korea's operations in New Atlantis. An industrial, high-tech facility where sub-robots are manufactured and where Carc-Zilla is initially housed. It symbolizes the technological prowess and potential dangers of the Korean factions.
*   **Sub-Korea (City):** A futuristic underwater city, seamlessly integrated with the natural environment. Sleek metallic buildings, transparent travel tubes, and a harmonious coexistence of various aquatic beings. It represents a technologically advanced and politically significant underwater civilization.
*   **Korean-Style Villa (Seoul):** A serene oasis on the surface, nestled in lush green hills. It serves as a safe house and training ground for Lucky's team, symbolizing a temporary calm before the storm and the blending of cultures.
*   **UN Command Center (Geneva):** A sprawling, multi-level, high-tech complex on the surface. A fortress of global power, representing the UN's control and military might.
*   **The Deep Ocean (Abyssal Plains):** The dark, immense, and largely unexplored regions of the ocean. A place of immense pressure, strange bioluminescent life, and the rumored dwelling place of ancient Carcazod strains. It becomes the battleground for the war for the deep.
*   **Asteroid Field (Void's Embrace):** A distant, resource-rich cluster of asteroids in space. Represents the next frontier for Lucky and his team, promising new challenges and opportunities beyond Earth.
*   **The Architect's Lair (Unknown):** A dark, minimalist, and cold room dominated by holographic screens. It symbolizes The Architect's detached, calculating nature and his cosmic ambitions.

**2. Key Items/Technology:**

*   **Soul Pen Device:** Lucky's signature invention. A device capable of harvesting and channeling raw 'soul energy' (life force/emotional energy). Initially used for curing the Carcazod plague, later adapted for combat and interfacing with other technologies.
*   **Carcazod Cure:** The medical breakthrough developed by Lucky, which initially brings him fame and fortune. Its success is the catalyst for the UN's interest in his company.
*   **Sub-Robots:** Small, spider-like machines used by New-Korea and Sub-Korea for various purposes, including surveillance, security, and potentially military applications. They represent the Korean factions' technological advancement and their subtle control.
*   **Carc-Zilla:** The UN's secret weapon. A monstrous, bio-mechanical creature, a weaponized Carcazod. A perversion of nature, capable of immense destruction and controlled by Sáoirse.
*   **Oceanic Current Manipulator:** A crystalline device developed by New-Korea, capable of manipulating ocean currents to create massive storms, whirlpools, and disrupt naval forces. It requires a specific sonic frequency for activation, which Lucky can provide.
*   **Tear-It Cards / Tear-It Decks:** A unique form of magic developed by the Koreans. Intricately designed cards infused with the energy of human emotions and life force. They act as conduits to amplify the user's magical abilities, allowing them to tap into elemental forces (wind, fire, water, etc.) and channel their inner strength. The science behind them involves bio-luminescent materials resonating with emotional frequencies.
*   **Pursuit Submarine:** A sleek, high-speed submarine provided by New-Korea for chasing Sáoirse. Equipped with advanced navigation and tactical systems.
*   **Stealth Suits:** Advanced suits worn by Lucky's team for infiltration, capable of rendering them nearly invisible and silent.
*   **Energy-Bladed Dagger:** Dorchester's signature weapon, capable of slicing through armor.
*   **Bio-Enhancements (Astra):** Astra's personal technological upgrades, providing enhanced senses, strength, agility, and the ability to interface with computer systems.

**3. Factions/Organizations:**

*   **United Nations (UN):** The primary antagonist organization. Portrayed as a corrupt, power-hungry entity seeking global dominion, particularly over the oceans. They are willing to commit atrocities (framing Lucky, attempting genocide of merpeople, weaponizing Carcazod) to achieve their goals. Led by Sáoirse.
*   **New-Korea / Sub-Korea:** Powerful Asian factions operating in the underwater world. Technologically advanced, they have formed an alliance with the clone-merpeople and oppose the UN's expansionist agenda. They are pragmatic and willing to form alliances for mutual benefit.
*   **Clone-Merpeople:** A peaceful, advanced civilization of mutant clones living in the deep ocean. They are targeted by the UN for extermination but are protected by their alliance with New-Korea. They possess unique abilities and a deep understanding of the ocean.
*   **New Korean Resistance:** A group of skilled individuals (Ji-Hyun, Soo-Jin, Hye-Mi, Mi-Sun) who provide sanctuary, training, and support to Lucky's team. They represent the human element of the resistance against the UN.
*   **Spy-K:** Lucky's core team of female operatives (Gem, Astra, Spike). Highly skilled, loyal, and adaptable, they are his primary support in combat and infiltration.
*   **Black Market Asteroid Mining Venture:** A new, lucrative venture in space, hinting at future conflicts and opportunities beyond Earth. Run by Luna.

**4. Key Concepts/Phenomena:**

*   **Carcazod Plague:** The initial global threat that Lucky cures, leading to his fame. Its weaponized form (Carc-Zilla) becomes a major threat.
*   **Soul Energy / Blood and Souls:** A mystical/scientific concept. The Soul Pen harvests it, and Tear-It cards channel it. It represents the life force and emotional intensity that can be harnessed for magical and technological purposes.
*   **Urban Fantasy Elements:** The integration of magic (Tear-It cards, elemental manipulation) into a technologically advanced, futuristic setting, particularly within the Korean context.
*   **Political Intrigue:** The constant maneuvering and power struggles between the UN, New-Korea, and other global powers, often leading to unexpected truces and betrayals.
*   **Genetic Engineering / Cloning:** The underlying scientific basis for the clone-merpeople and the UN's attempts to weaponize biological entities.
*   **The Deep Black:** The unexplored, high-pressure regions of the deep ocean, home to ancient and powerful entities, and a strategic location for hidden bases and resources.
