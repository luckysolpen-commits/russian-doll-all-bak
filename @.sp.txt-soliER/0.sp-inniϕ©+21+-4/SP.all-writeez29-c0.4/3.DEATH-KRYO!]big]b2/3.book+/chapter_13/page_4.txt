**INT. UNKNOWN LOCATION - NIGHT**

The room was dark, minimalist, and cold, a stark contrast to the vibrant world they had just left. A single, massive holographic screen dominated one wall, displaying a complex web of data streams, financial transactions, and news feeds. On one of the feeds, a small blip, a tiny speck of light, represented Lucky's new asteroid mining venture, heading towards the 'Void's Embrace' cluster, a distant, uncharted region of space.

"So, my dear brother." The Architect's voice, chillingly similar to Lucky's, yet colder, more calculating, filled the silent room, a low, satisfied hum. "You escape one cage, only to fly into another. Always seeking, always striving. Always so predictable." He stepped into the faint, ethereal light cast by the holographic screen. He was the spitting image of Lucky, a perfect mirror image, yet his eyes were devoid of warmth, filled instead with a predatory intelligence, a cold, calculating gleam that spoke of vast, unseen ambitions, of a hunger for power that transcended mere mortal desires. He wore a sleek, dark suit, perfectly tailored, a shadow against the flickering light, a figure of silent menace.

He gestured, and the holographic screen shifted, displaying a detailed analysis of the Soul Pen device, its intricate energy signatures, its immense potential, its hidden power. "Your little toy," The Architect continued, his voice dripping with a condescending amusement. "So much potential, wasted on petty squabbles. But soon, it will be mine. And with it, the true power of the 'blood and souls' will be unleashed. A power that will reshape the universe." His words were a chilling prophecy, a glimpse into a future far more terrifying than any plague or war, a future where he reigned supreme.

He brought up another feed, showing a shadowy figure, barely discernible, moving through a crowded market on a distant, alien world. It was a figure that resembled Sáoirse, but subtly different, perhaps a clone, or an echo, a pawn in a much larger, more intricate game, a game he controlled.
