**Page 10**

They jumped into the tunnel, one by one, the cold, murky water a shocking, unwelcome embrace. Lucky was the last to go. He paused for a fleeting, heart-stopping moment, his eyes fixed on the monstrous, terrifying form of Carc-Zilla, its red eyes burning with an intelligent, calculating malice. A look of grim, unwavering determination settled on his face. This wasn't just about survival anymore. It was about stopping this abomination, about preventing the UN from unleashing this horror upon the world. It was about protecting his legacy, his cure, from being twisted into a weapon of mass destruction.

They were swept away by a strong, turbulent current, tumbling through a network of dark, claustrophobic underwater caves. The sound of the battle, the blare of the alarms, and the monstrous, terrifying roars of Carc-Zilla faded behind them, replaced by the rushing, roaring sound of water and the occasional, jarring scrape of rock against their suits. The journey was disorienting, a chaotic, violent tumble through the earth's ancient, forgotten arteries.

Finally, the current lessened, and they came to a rest in a large, quiet cavern. They were battered and bruised, their suits scuffed and scratched, but they were alive. The air was still, the water calm, a stark, welcome contrast to the chaos they had just escaped.

"Did we lose them?" Gem asked, her voice hoarse, her chest heaving with exertion.

"For now," Dorchester replied, her eyes scanning the cavern, ever vigilant, ever suspicious. She pulled herself onto a rocky ledge, her movements still fluid and graceful despite the ordeal.

Lucky, his gaze fixed on Dorchester, a cold, hard certainty settling in his gut, felt a surge of anger. "You knew about this," he accused, his voice low and dangerous. "You knew about Carc-Zilla. You knew what they were building."

Dorchester met his gaze, her expression unreadable, her emerald eyes giving nothing away. "I had my suspicions," she admitted, her voice a low, dangerous purr. "Now we know for sure. The UN isn't just trying to control the underwater world. They're trying to create a new plague. A weaponized Carcazod. And that, Lucky," she said, her voice dropping to a deadly whisper, "changes everything."
