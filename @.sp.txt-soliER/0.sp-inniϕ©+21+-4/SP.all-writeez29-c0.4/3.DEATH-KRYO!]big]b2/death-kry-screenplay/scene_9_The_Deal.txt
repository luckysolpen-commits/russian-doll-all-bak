**SCENE 9**

**INT. SUB-KOREA NEGOTIATION CHAMBER - DAY**

The negotiation chamber is less formal than the council chamber, but no less serious. It's a sleek, minimalist room with comfortable seating and holographic displays that project data and agreements.

LUCKY, DORCHESTER, GEM, SPIKE, and ASTRA sit across from the NEW-KOREA GENERAL and a MER-PEOPLE DIPLOMAT. Raven is quietly observing from a corner, occasionally taking notes on a tablet.

NEW-KOREA GENERAL
(projecting a holographic document)
Our proposal is simple: an alliance. We provide the resources, the technology, and the strategic support. You provide the unique expertise to operate the current manipulator and, of course, your... unconventional methods.

LUCKY
(leaning forward)
And our terms are equally simple. We deploy the device. We control its activation. And we retain a piece of the technology. A full schematic, and access to its core programming. For research purposes.

The General and the Diplomat exchange a glance. It's a bold demand.

MER-PEOPLE DIPLOMAT
(a calm, measured voice)
Such a powerful device... its secrets are closely guarded.

DORCHESTER
(a wry smile)
And Lucky's secrets are even more so. He's the only one who can make your toy sing. A fair exchange, wouldn't you say?

The General considers this, then nods slowly.

NEW-KOREA GENERAL
Agreed. A full schematic, and access to the core programming. Provided the device is used solely for defensive purposes against the UN's aggression.

LUCKY
(nodding)
Understood. And if anything goes wrong, if this alliance proves to be a trap, or if your intentions shift... we walk away. No questions asked, no repercussions.

SPIKE
(interjecting)
And our people. The clone-merpeople. They get full protection. No more experiments, no more provocations.

MER-PEOPLE DIPLOMAT
(a genuine smile)
That is our primary objective. Their safety is paramount.

GEM
(to Astra)
Can you verify these schematics? Make sure there are no hidden backdoors?

ASTRA
(already tapping on her comms)
On it. I'll need a secure connection to their network.

NEW-KOREA GENERAL
(to Astra)
Access granted. Our systems are open to you. We believe in transparency in this alliance.

Lucky looks at Dorchester, who gives him a subtle nod. He extends his hand across the table.

LUCKY
Then we have a deal.

The General shakes his hand firmly. The Mer-People Diplomat also shakes his hand, a sense of hope in his eyes.

NEW-KOREA GENERAL
Excellent. The deployment window is narrow. We must move quickly. Our intelligence indicates a major UN naval exercise is scheduled for tomorrow. A perfect opportunity.

LUCKY
(a determined glint in his eye)
Then let's get to work.

**FADE OUT.**
