**SCENE 3**

**EXT. UNDERWATER RUIN - NIGHT**

LUCKY and SPY-K swim through the dark ocean, the only light coming from the soft glow of their suits. They move with purpose, putting distance between themselves and the Soul Pen.

They arrive at a vast, ancient underwater ruin. The remains of a forgotten city, its buildings now covered in coral and seaweed. It's both beautiful and eerie.

ASTRA
This should be a safe place to lay low for a while.

They swim into one of the larger, more intact structures, a former grand hall of some kind.

**INT. UNDERWATER RUIN - CONTINUOUS**

Inside, the water is still and silent. The only sound is the gentle hum of their equipment.

GEM
(pacing)
This is a setup. They wanted to frame you.

LUCKY
But why? The UN got what they wanted. They have my company.

SPIKE
Maybe that's not all they wanted. Maybe they wanted you out of the picture for good.

Lucky runs a hand through his hair, frustrated and angry.

LUCKY
I need a drink.

He swims off to explore the ruin, needing a moment to himself.

Astra is examining the walls, her bio-enhanced eyes scanning for anything out of the ordinary.

ASTRA
There's something here. Faint energy signature.

Gem and Spike join her. Astra points to a section of the wall, where a series of strange symbols are faintly etched into the stone.

GEM
What is it?

ASTRA
I'm not sure. It's old. Very old.

Lucky returns, having found nothing to drink.

LUCKY
Find something?

He looks at the symbols, his brow furrowed in concentration. He recognizes them.

LUCKY (CONT'D)
It's a cipher. A pirate's cipher.

He traces the symbols with his finger, his mind working quickly. He mutters the words as he deciphers them.

LUCKY (CONT'D)
Dorchester's... game... afoot.

SPIKE
Dorchester? The pirate?

GEM
I thought she was just a myth.

LUCKY
She's real. And if she's involved in this... then we're in a whole new level of trouble.

He looks at the cryptic message, a sense of dread washing over him. This is no longer just about clearing his name. This is about survival.

**FADE OUT.**
