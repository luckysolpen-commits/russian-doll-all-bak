**SCENE 1**

**INT. THE SOUL PEN - NIGHT**

The Soul Pen is a breathtaking underwater nightclub, a marvel of engineering and opulence. Bioluminescent flora casts an ethereal glow on the transparent walls, revealing the deep ocean and its inhabitants outside. Inside, a lavish party is in full swing. The elite of this new world, a mix of humans, mer-people, and cyborgs, dance to a pulsating beat.

LUCKY (25), handsome, roguishly charming, and dressed in a sharp, tailored suit, stands on a small stage, a microphone in hand. He's the man of the hour, the genius who cured the Carcazod plague and sold his company, Cerberus Inc., to the United Nations for an astronomical sum.

GEM, ASTRA, and SPIKE, the formidable members of Spy-K, watch from the crowd, their eyes scanning the room, ever vigilant. They are Lucky's friends and protectors.

QUEEN SOPHIA, a regal and graceful mer-person, watches her daughter, a young, vibrant mermaid, dance joyfully.

LUCKY
(grinning)
Thank you! Thank you all for coming. Tonight, we celebrate a new beginning. A world free from the Carcazod plague. A world where we can finally start to rebuild.

The crowd cheers. Lucky raises his glass.

LUCKY (CONT'D)
But this isn't just my victory. It's ours. To all of you who believed in me, who fought alongside me... this is for you.

He takes a sip of his drink. The party continues, the music swells. Lucky steps down from the stage and is immediately surrounded by well-wishers.

Suddenly, the music cuts out. The lights flicker and die, plunging the club into near darkness, save for the faint glow of the bioluminescent plants.

A collective gasp ripples through the crowd. Then, silence.

Five figures, clad in black tactical gear, emerge from the shadows. They are UN COUNTER-FORCE AGENTS, their faces obscured by helmets. They move with a chilling, synchronized precision.

Panic erupts. The crowd scrambles, trying to flee. The UN agents ignore the chaos, their focus fixed on one target.

One of the agents raises a weapon. A single, silenced shot rings out.

Queen Sophia's daughter falls to the floor, a look of shock on her face. She is dead.

The agents move towards Lucky. One of them points a finger at him.

UN AGENT
(a synthesized voice)
Lucky is guilty.

The agents disappear as quickly as they arrived, melting back into the shadows.

The emergency lights flicker on, casting a harsh, sterile light on the scene. The joyous celebration has become a scene of tragedy and accusation.

Lucky stands frozen, the eyes of everyone in the room on him. He is no longer a hero. He is a suspect.

**FADE OUT.**
