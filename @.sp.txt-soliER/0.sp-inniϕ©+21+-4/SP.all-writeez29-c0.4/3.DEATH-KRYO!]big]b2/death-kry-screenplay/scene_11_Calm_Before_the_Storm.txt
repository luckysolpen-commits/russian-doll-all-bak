**SCENE 11**

**INT. SUB-KOREA DEPLOYMENT VESSEL - CONTINUOUS**

The vessel is in chaos. Alarms blare, lights flash red. The crew scrambles, trying to stabilize the ship as the ocean outside churns violently. The massive energy anomaly from the malfunctioning device pulses on the main viewscreen, a terrifying, growing vortex of dark energy.

LUCKY
(frantic, still at the console)
I can't shut it down! It's drawing power from everywhere! It's self-sustaining!

DORCHESTER
(grabbing his arm, pulling him away)
We have to get out of here! Now! This thing is going to consume everything!

NEW-KOREA GENERAL (O.S.)
(over comms, distorted)
All personnel, abandon ship! Repeat, abandon ship! The device is unstable!

They scramble to an escape pod, the vessel groaning under the immense strain. Just as they seal the hatch, a massive wave of dark energy engulfs the deployment vessel, tearing it apart. The escape pod is violently ejected, tumbling through the turbulent water.

**EXT. OCEAN SURFACE - DAY**

The escape pod breaches the surface, bobbing amidst a sea of debris. The sky above is clear, a stark contrast to the chaos beneath the waves. The sun shines down, warm and inviting.

They are rescued by a New-Korea patrol boat, which quickly ferries them to a nearby coastal city.

**INT. KOREAN-STYLE VILLA - DAY (SEVERAL DAYS LATER)**

The villa is a serene oasis, nestled amidst lush green hills overlooking the ocean. Traditional Korean architecture blends with modern comforts. The air is filled with the scent of blooming flowers and the distant sound of waves.

LUCKY, GEM, SPIKE, ASTRA, and DORCHESTER are recovering. They are bruised, but alive. The near-catastrophe has left them shaken, but also forged a deeper bond.

They are welcomed by the women of the NEW KOREAN RESISTANCE. JI-HYUN, with her calm demeanor and sharp eyes, greets them warmly. SOO-JIN, mischievous and quick-witted, offers them tea. HYE-MI, gentle and nurturing, tends to their minor injuries. MI-SUN, fiery and energetic, prepares a delicious meal.

JI-HYUN
(a soft smile)
Welcome. You are safe here. We heard of your... incident. The device is contained, for now. But it was a close call.

LUCKY
(still haunted)
Too close. We almost destroyed everything we were trying to protect.

DORCHESTER
(a rare moment of vulnerability)
I miscalculated. I didn't realize the UN had tampered with the device's core programming. They wanted it to fail, to create a disaster they could then 'solve'.

SOO-JIN
(handing Lucky a cup of tea)
Such is the way of the UN. They create the problems, then offer the solutions. But here, we have our own solutions.

She gestures to a beautifully crafted wooden box on a nearby table. Inside are dozens of intricately designed cards, each glowing with a faint, internal light. These are TEAR-IT CARDS.

LUCKY
What are these?

JI-HYUN
These are our heritage. Our power. The Tear-It Deck. They harness the energy of 'blood and souls'. Not in a macabre way, but through the resonance of intense human emotion and life force. Each card is attuned to a specific elemental force, a specific aspect of the human spirit.

HYE-MI
(gently)
They are a conduit. A way to channel our inner strength, our will, into the world. To mend, to protect, to fight.

MI-SUN
(grinning)
And to kick some serious UN butt!

Lucky picks up a card. It feels warm in his hand, almost alive. He looks at his Soul Pen device, then back at the cards.

LUCKY
Can they... work together? My Soul Pen harvests and channels raw soul energy. These cards seem to refine it, direct it.

JI-HYUN
That is what we hope to discover. Your Soul Pen, combined with our Tear-It Decks, could be a formidable force. We have much to teach you. And much to learn from you.

Over the next few days, the villa becomes a hub of activity. They share stories, laugh, and begin to train. Gem, with her emotional intensity, takes to the Tear-It cards naturally, able to conjure small gusts of wind or sparks of fire. Astra, with her bio-enhancements, finds a surprising synergy between her tech and the elemental magic.

Spike, ever the pragmatist, focuses on how the magic can enhance her combat skills. Dorchester, intrigued by the raw power of the Soul Pen, begins to experiment with channeling its energy through her own unique combat style.

Lucky, meanwhile, is fascinated by the science behind the Tear-It cards. Ji-Hyun explains that the cards are made from a unique bio-luminescent material that resonates with specific emotional frequencies. When a user focuses their intent and channels their life force (their 'blood and soul'), the card amplifies that energy, allowing them to manipulate the elements.

They set up a training ground in a secluded part of the villa's grounds. Clone practice dummies, surprisingly resilient and agile, are their opponents. They are not just simple targets; they are programmed to simulate various threats, including mer-clones, forcing the team to adapt and push their limits.

LUCKY
(wiping sweat from his brow after a particularly intense session)
These dummies are tougher than they look.

SOO-JIN
(grinning)
They have to be. The real fight will be even tougher. We need to be ready.

As they train, the news plays softly in the background. SAOIRSE, the ruthless leader of the UN, appears on screen, her voice dripping with self-righteousness, condemning the recent 'natural disaster' and vowing to bring the perpetrators to justice.

DORCHESTER
(her eyes narrowing, a dangerous glint in them)
Justice. She wouldn't know justice if it bit her in the face. It's time we paid her a visit.

She looks at Lucky, a plan already forming in her mind. A plan to assassinate Sáoirse. Little does she know, Sáoirse has her own plot brewing, a sinister scheme involving Dorchester's sister, Raven.

**FADE OUT.**
