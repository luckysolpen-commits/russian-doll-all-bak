**SCENE 4**

**INT. DORCHESTER'S LAIR - DAY**

DORCHESTER'S LAIR is a sight to behold. It's a massive, hollowed-out asteroid, hidden in a remote sector of the ocean. The interior is a chaotic mix of high-tech equipment, ancient artifacts, and pirate loot. A large viewport shows the deep ocean outside.

DORCHESTER, a woman in her late 30s with sharp, intelligent eyes and a confident smirk, watches as LUCKY and SPY-K are escorted into the main chamber by her crew. She is every bit the legendary pirate.

RAVEN (16), Dorchester's younger sister, watches from the sidelines, her eyes wide with a mixture of curiosity and excitement. She has bright blue hair and an eager grin.

DORCHESTER
(a husky, confident voice)
Lucky. I was wondering when you'd show up. I trust you got my message.

LUCKY
(cautious)
What do you want, Dorchester?

DORCHESTER
Straight to business. I like that. I want to offer you a deal.

GEM
We're not interested in deals with pirates.

DORCHESTER
(laughing)
Oh, I think you'll be interested in this one. You're being hunted. Framed for a murder you didn't commit. I can help you find out who's behind it.

SPIKE
And why would you help us?

DORCHESTER
Let's just say I have a vested interest in taking down the people who are after you. They're a faction that's been making waves in New Atlantis, and I'm not a fan of their methods. Besides, I have a patron who pays well for this kind of information.

She gestures to a large screen, which flickers to life, showing a dossier on the UN agents who attacked the Soul Pen.

DORCHESTER (CONT'D)
These are just puppets. The real players are in the shadows. I can help you find them. But we're going to do it my way.

LUCKY
What's your way?

DORCHESTER
(a mischievous glint in her eye)
A game. A game of cat and mouse. We'll hunt the hunters. It'll be fun.

Raven giggles, clearly excited by the prospect.

RAVEN
Dorchester says I need practice with lies and tricks.

Lucky looks at Dorchester, then at Spy-K. He doesn't trust her, but he knows they're out of options.

LUCKY
What's the first move?

DORCHESTER
(smiling)
We go to New Atlantis. And we make a little noise.

**FADE OUT.**
