**SCENE 12**

**INT. KOREAN-STYLE VILLA - NIGHT**

The villa is quiet, save for the soft hum of the air conditioning. LUCKY, DORCHESTER, JI-HYUN, SOO-JIN, HYE-MI, and MI-SUN are gathered around a holographic map of the world, specifically focusing on a heavily fortified UN complex in Geneva.

DORCHESTER
(pointing to a section of the complex)
Sáoirse's command center. Heavily guarded, but not impenetrable. I've been feeding her false intelligence for weeks, making her believe I'm working on a top-secret project for her. She trusts me.

LUCKY
(skeptical)
She trusts a notorious pirate?

DORCHESTER
(a wry smile)
She trusts a mercenary who gets results. And I've been giving her results. Small, controlled leaks of information that make her look good, while subtly undermining her rivals within the UN.

JI-HYUN
So, you're suggesting a direct assault? An assassination?

DORCHESTER
(nodding)
It's the only way to cut the head off the snake. Sáoirse is the driving force behind the UN's genocidal agenda. Without her, their plans will crumble.

SOO-JIN
(tapping on the holographic map, bringing up schematics of the complex)
Security is layered. Biometric scans, sonic dampeners, energy shields. And a personal guard detail that's rumored to be composed of augmented super-soldiers.

MI-SUN
(cracking her knuckles)
Sounds like fun.

HYE-MI
(concerned)
This is incredibly dangerous, Dorchester. Sáoirse is not to be underestimated.

DORCHESTER
(her eyes hard)
I know. But the longer she remains in power, the more innocent lives are at risk. We have to stop her.

LUCKY
(looking at the schematics)
How do we get in? Even with your intel, it's a fortress.

DORCHESTER
(a glint in her eye)
That's where my 'top-secret project' comes in. I've convinced her I'm developing a new, undetectable infiltration system. She's given me access to her personal labs within the complex. A back door, if you will.

She projects a new schematic, showing a hidden access tunnel leading directly to Sáoirse's private quarters.

LUCKY
(impressed)
Clever. Very clever.

DORCHESTER
(a shadow crosses her face)
There's a complication. Sáoirse has been... asking about Raven. She seems to have taken an interest in her unique abilities. I've been deflecting, but I don't know how much longer I can keep her at bay.

Ji-Hyun and Soo-Jin exchange worried glances. The implication is clear: Sáoirse might be planning to kidnap Raven.

LUCKY
We need to move fast then. Before she makes her move.

DORCHESTER
(a determined nod)
Agreed. We strike during the next UN security summit. Sáoirse will be present, and her personal guard will be spread thin, overseeing the event.

They begin to strategize, discussing entry points, escape routes, and contingency plans. The mood is grim, but resolute. They know the stakes are incredibly high.

**INT. SAOIRSE'S PRIVATE QUARTERS - GENEVA - NIGHT (SIMULTANEOUS)**

SAOIRSE (50s), elegant and ruthless, sips a glass of wine, looking out at the city lights. Her personal assistant, a nervous-looking man, stands respectfully by.

ASSISTANT
Madam Director, the reports on the asteroid mining venture are in. Luna's projections are... substantial.

SAOIRSE
(a cold smile)
Excellent. And the girl? Raven? Has Dorchester brought her in yet?

ASSISTANT
Not yet, Madam Director. Dorchester has been... evasive. She claims the girl is too young, too untrained.

SAOIRSE
(her eyes narrowing)
Untrained? Or too valuable to share? No matter. I have my own methods. Prepare a discreet extraction team. I want the girl. And I want her brought to me. Intact.

ASSISTANT
(nervously)
Yes, Madam Director. Immediately.

Sáoirse turns back to the window, her smile widening. She raises her glass.

SAOIRSE
To new acquisitions. And to the future of the United Nations.

**FADE OUT.**
