**SCENE 16**

**EXT. THE DEEP OCEAN - CONTINUOUS**

The pursuit sub, despite Soo-Jin's resonant frequency attack, is still struggling against the sheer number of Carc-Zillas. Sáoirse, though momentarily disoriented, has regained control, her dark energy flaring, pushing the horde back into a relentless assault.

LUCKY
(gritting his teeth, Soul Pen glowing)
We're not going to make it! There are too many!

JI-HYUN
(straining, channeling elemental energy)
I can try to create a localized thermal shock! Maybe disrupt their bio-mechanics!

She focuses, her hands glowing. A wave of intense heat, then extreme cold, emanates from the sub, causing a small pocket of Carc-Zillas to convulse and momentarily freeze. But the effect is fleeting, and the horde quickly closes in.

DORCHESTER
(at the helm, dodging a massive Carc-Zilla claw)
It's not enough! We need a bigger distraction!

Suddenly, a series of high-frequency SONAR PINGS echo through the deep. They are not from Sáoirse, nor from the New-Korea forces. They are distinct, powerful, and unfamiliar.

SOO-JIN
(at her console, eyes wide)
Incoming transmission! From the surface! It's... a global broadcast!

The main viewscreen flickers, overriding the tactical display. A stern-faced ANCHORWOMAN appears, her voice cutting through the chaos.

ANCHORWOMAN (V.O.)
...we interrupt this broadcast with breaking news. Sources confirm that a historic, albeit unexpected, truce has been signed between the United Nations and the United States of America. This unprecedented agreement comes amidst escalating global tensions, with reports of a direct threat of intervention from the People's Republic of China if a peaceful resolution was not immediately reached.

The words hang in the air, surreal and jarring amidst the ongoing battle. The Carc-Zillas, as if sensing the shift, begin to slow. Their movements become less aggressive, more confused. Sáoirse, on her command Carc-Zilla, looks up at the viewscreen, her face a mask of disbelief and fury.

SÁOIRSE
(a guttural roar)
No! This cannot be! My victory! My destiny!

The New-Korea forces and the merpeople, equally stunned, cease their attacks. The vast, chaotic battlefield falls into an uneasy, almost silent, stalemate. The millions of Carc-Zillas drift aimlessly, their malevolent glow dimming.

LUCKY
(lowering his Soul Pen, bewildered)
It's over? Just like that?

DORCHESTER
(a bitter laugh)
Politics. The ultimate weapon. More effective than any Carc-Zilla.

**EXT. SURFACE - DAY (SEVERAL DAYS LATER)**

The world has returned to an uneasy, anticlimactic peace. News channels are abuzz with analysis of the sudden truce. Sáoirse's whereabouts are unknown, but the UN has issued a statement about her 'retirement' due to 'health reasons.' The Carc-Zillas have retreated to the deepest parts of the ocean, their threat neutralized by the political maneuverings of global powers.

LUCKY, GEM, SPIKE, ASTRA, DORCHESTER, and RAVEN are back at the Korean-style villa. The training dummies are gone. The Tear-It cards are neatly stacked. The atmosphere is one of quiet reflection, almost disappointment.

LUCKY
(staring out at the ocean)
All that fighting... all that risk... for a treaty signed in a boardroom.

GEM
(shrugging)
That's how the world works, Lucky. Sometimes the biggest battles are fought with pens, not weapons.

Suddenly, Astra's comms unit BEEPS. She checks it, her eyes widening.

ASTRA
(a spark of excitement in her voice)
Lucky! Gem! You won't believe this. It's Luna. She's got a proposition. A black market asteroid mining venture. Says she's made us privy to it.

GEM
(a grin spreading across her face)
Asteroid mining? That sounds... profitable.

LUCKY
(a weary smile)
After all this, I could use a change of pace. And a change of fortune.

DORCHESTER
(a knowing look)
Luna, you say? She always did have a knack for finding opportunities in the most unexpected places. Just be careful, Lucky. The void of space can be even more treacherous than the deep ocean.

Raven, who had been quietly listening, looks up, a mischievous glint in her eye.

RAVEN
Can I come? I'm really good at finding hidden things.

Lucky looks at his friends, then back at the vast, open ocean. The war for the deep may be over, but a new adventure, one promising riches and unknown dangers, is about to begin.

**FADE OUT.**
