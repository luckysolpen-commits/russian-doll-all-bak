INT. SCRIBE'S HOLLOW - UPPER CHAMBER - DAY

SOUND of wind whistling through broken windows, dust motes dancing in sunbeams

Elara places the warm SOULPEN on a carved wooden desk. Its surface is etched with ancient, swirling runes, mirroring those on the tower's exterior. The pen seems to thrum with a faint, internal light.

Her INKLING, perched on the desk beside the pen, pulses faintly, its shimmering form momentarily solidifying as it senses the Soulpen's magic.

ELARA
(whispering)
What are you?

She hesitates, then presses the nib to a scrap of parchment. Ink, black and viscous, flows from the pen as if from an unseen well. Her hand moves, not entirely under her control, forming words.

CLOSE UP - The words appear on the parchment:
"The Soulpen wakes when the world breaks."

Elara GASPS, dropping the pen as if burned. It clatters on the desk. The Inkling recoils slightly, its form rippling. Elara stares at the glowing words, which pulse faintly before fading into the parchment, leaving it blank.

Elara picks up the pen again, her hand trembling. This time, she writes deliberately.

CLOSE UP - The words appear:
"Who am I?"

The pen scratches, and new words form.

CLOSE UP - The words appear:
"Elara Veyn, daughter of Lirien, bearer of the unbroken thread."

Elara frowns, confused by the cryptic answer. She tries again, her voice a desperate whisper.

ELARA
Where is my mother?

The pen scratches again, the ink flowing with a chilling finality.

CLOSE UP - The words appear:
"Beyond the veil, where ink meets bone."

Elara's stomach twists. The words echo in the silent chamber, a cold dread settling over her. The Inkling shimmers, its form darkening slightly, as if reflecting Elara's unease.
