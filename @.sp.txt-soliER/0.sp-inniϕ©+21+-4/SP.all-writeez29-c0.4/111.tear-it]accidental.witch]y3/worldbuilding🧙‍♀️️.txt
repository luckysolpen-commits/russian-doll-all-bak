# Worldbuilding Notes

## Locations:

*   **Quillhaven:** A village clinging to the edge of the Shatterveil Cliffs. Known for its sea-reed thatched cottages and wardens. Seems to be a relatively isolated, traditional community.
*   **Shatterveil Cliffs:** Dramatic cliffs where the sea roars. Believed to hold old magics and listen to whispers. A hidden trail leads down to a cave.
*   **Scribe's Hollow:** A crumbling tower at Quillhaven's edge, an old archive. Its walls are etched with ancient runes. Formerly kept by Elara's mother, Lirien. Sealed by elders after Lirien's disappearance.
*   **Ashen Wastes:** Barren lands, air tastes of cinders. No one goes there willingly. Believed to be where the Soulpen was made, containing an old scriptorium.
*   **The Veil:** A mythical barrier between the living world and "whatever lay beyond." Lirien is said to have crossed it using the Soulpen. "Where ink meets bone."

## Magical Elements:

*   **The Soulpen:** A black wooden pen with a starlight-glinting nib. It's a relic that writes truths, makes words real, and "binds" and "takes." Every word written with it costs something (will, memories, soul). It hums, glows, and causes pain when used for significant truths. It's a "key" that opens things best left locked. It "calls to those who listen."
*   **Old Magics:** Stirring at the Hour of Whispers (dawn).
*   **Wards:** Carvings found in the cave beneath Shatterveil Cliffs, used to guard the Soulpen.
*   **The Veil:** A magical barrier that can be broken or crossed.

## Lore/History:

*   **Hour of Whispers:** Dawn, when old magics stir.
*   **Soulpen's Origin:** Made in an old scriptorium in the Ashen Wastes. Was once guarded, not hidden.
*   **Lirien's Actions:** Used the Soulpen to cross the veil, writing something that "shouldn't have been written," which "broke the veil." This is why she's gone and why the Soulpen chose Elara.

## Society/Culture:

*   **Elders:** Authority figures in Quillhaven, who sealed the Scribe's Hollow.
*   **Wardens:** Village guards who patrol and enforce rules.
*   **Supernatural Creatures:** Werewolves, vampires, etc., implied to exist and potentially interact with humans (Elara plans to open a potions bar for them).

## Concepts:

*   **Cost of Magic:** The Soulpen's power comes with a price, drawing from the user.
*   **Unbroken Thread:** Elara is the "bearer of the unbroken thread," a cryptic phrase from the Soulpen.
*   **Stardew Valley/Moonlighter Mix:** Implies a blend of village life, farming/gathering, and dungeon crawling for resources (for potions).
*   **Celtic Influences:** Mentioned by the user, suggesting a specific cultural aesthetic and potential mythological elements.
*   **Urban Fantasy but more Fantasy:** Suggests a modern setting with strong magical elements and perhaps less focus on the "urban" aspect than traditional urban fantasy.
