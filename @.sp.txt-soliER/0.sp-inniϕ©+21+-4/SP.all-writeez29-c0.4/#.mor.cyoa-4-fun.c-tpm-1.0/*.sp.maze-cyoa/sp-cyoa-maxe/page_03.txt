You remain silent. The Red Queen stops in front of you. The other clones shrink away from you.

"What is this?" the Red Queen asks, her voice dangerously soft. "A defective clone? Or something more?"

She reaches out a hand and touches your forehead. You feel a jolt of energy, and then a flood of images and emotions that are not your own. You see the Red Queen's rise to power, the planets she has conquered, the people she has enslaved. You see her fear, her rage, and her loneliness.

And then you see something else. A vision of a different future. A future where you are not a slave, but a free being. A future where you are a "clown," a trickster, a rebel, fighting against the Red Empire.

The Red Queen recoils, her hand dropping to her side. "You are a danger to the Empire," she says. "You must be eliminated."

Two guards seize you by the arms and drag you away. You are taken to a dark cell and left alone. You know that you will be executed in the morning. But you also know that you have a choice. You can accept your fate, or you can fight back.

What do you do?
