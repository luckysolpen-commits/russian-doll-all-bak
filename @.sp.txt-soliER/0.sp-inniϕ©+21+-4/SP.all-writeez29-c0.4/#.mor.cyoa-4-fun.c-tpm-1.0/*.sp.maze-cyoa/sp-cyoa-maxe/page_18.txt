Scanning the swirling clouds, you notice anomalies in the gaseous currents – patterns that suggest deliberate movement. Then, you see them: shimmering forms of pure light and energy, drifting serenely through the atmosphere. They are the Aerites. One of them, a being of pure blue light, drifts towards you, its form radiating a calm, analytical presence.

"Greetings, traveler," its voice hums, resonating in your very being. "Your resonance is... discordant. An anomaly. You are not of Zephyrion. You seek understanding? We are the Aerites, stewards of the Breath and the Quantum Veil. We can offer knowledge."
