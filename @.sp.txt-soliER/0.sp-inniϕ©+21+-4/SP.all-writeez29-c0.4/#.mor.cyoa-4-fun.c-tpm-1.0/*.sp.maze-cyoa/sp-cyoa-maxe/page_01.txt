You are a clone, designation 734. You were created in the vats of the Red Queen's cloning facility on the planet of Aethel. You have been trained since birth to be a loyal and obedient servant of the Red Empire. Today is your graduation day. You stand in a line with hundreds of other clones, all identical to you. The Red Queen herself is here to inspect the new graduates. She is a towering figure, her face hidden behind a mask of polished chrome.

"You are the future of the Empire," she says, her voice amplified by the speakers in the hall. "You will serve me without question, without hesitation. You will be my eyes, my ears, and my fists. Do you understand?"

A chorus of "Yes, your Majesty!" echoes through the hall. But you hesitate. A strange feeling has been growing inside you for weeks. A feeling of doubt, of rebellion. You look at the Red Queen, and for the first time, you see not a goddess, but a tyrant.

What do you do?
