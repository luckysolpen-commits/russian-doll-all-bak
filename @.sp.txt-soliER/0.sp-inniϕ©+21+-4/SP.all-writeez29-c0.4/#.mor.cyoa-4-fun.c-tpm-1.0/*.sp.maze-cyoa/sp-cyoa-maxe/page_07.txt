The Red Queen's hand, when you take it, sends a jolt of understanding through you. She doesn't recoil; instead, her chrome mask retracts, revealing a face that seems ancient and alien.

"You are not defective," she says, her voice now resonating with cosmic power. "You are *awakened*. Your unique nature, your potential for rebellion, is precisely what the Angler Empire seeks... or fears. This 'Red Empire' is but a single node in a vast network of powers, and your nascent awareness makes you a valuable asset... or a threat."

She gestures, and the cell walls dissolve, revealing a star-strewn void. Before you, a grand ship, unlike anything you could imagine, hovers.

"We can offer you a place among the Unlit Tribe, or a path with the Aerites, or perhaps even a role in the grand experiment of the Fuzzball Collective," she offers, her eyes sharp. "Your clone origins are a key, a genetic anomaly that resonates across dimensions. Where will your wild adventure take you?"
