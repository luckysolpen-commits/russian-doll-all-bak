The Red Queen ushers you into a dazzling display of quantum mechanics. Reality bends, and you find yourself on a vast, gaseous planet under a swirling, aurora-filled sky. This is Zephyrion, home of the Aerites. They are beings of pure energy and gas, their forms shimmering with vibrant colors. A being of pure blue light, radiating intellect, approaches you.

"Greetings, traveler," its voice hums. "I am a representative of the Aerite Conclave. We perceive your unique resonance, a frequency outside the Empire's grasp. You seek knowledge? We study the fundamental forces – the Breath, the Quantum Veil. We can teach you about the universe's true chemistry."
