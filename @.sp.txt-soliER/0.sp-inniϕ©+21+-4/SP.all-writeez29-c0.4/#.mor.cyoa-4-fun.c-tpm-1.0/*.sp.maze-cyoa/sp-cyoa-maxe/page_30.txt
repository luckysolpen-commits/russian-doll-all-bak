You express caution. "Your knowledge seems vast, almost overwhelming. How can I trust that your intentions are benign?" Aetheron's blue light flickers. "We understand your apprehension. Our pursuit of knowledge is pure. We seek to comprehend, not to control. Your own journey has shown you the dangers of unchecked power and manufactured order. We offer understanding; the choice to accept it is yours. Consider what true freedom means – is it the absence of control, or the mastery of oneself and the universe?"
