The Red Queen's ship docks with a colossal vessel that dwarfs even her craft. You are escorted by stern, chrome-masked guards into the heart of the Angler Empire's fleet. The interior is a marvel of efficient, ordered technology. You are brought before a high-ranking official, whose face bears the distinctive Angler features – a luminous lure and sharp, intelligent eyes.

"We have been expecting an anomaly like you," the official states, their voice smooth and authoritative. "Your genetic signature is potent. You will be trained. Your potential for cross-dimensional resonance makes you valuable. You will serve the Empire."

Your wild adventure begins now as an agent of a vast, multi-versal power.
