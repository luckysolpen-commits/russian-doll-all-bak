# CYOA Book Structure: The Weaver of Gilded Secrets

## Project Goal:
Create a ~70-page interactive choose-your-own-adventure book based on the "Weaver of Gilded Secrets" concept, using the spider avatar. The book will feature branching narratives, sensory details from a spider's perspective, and will culminate in one of several possible endings or paths (Silent Witness, Saboteur, Hitchhiker, Mutation, Cyborg).

## Format Inspiration:
- Narrative text describing the current situation.
- Clear choices, presented as numbered options, leading to specific page numbers.
- Each page will include a "Spider-Sense HUD" (Hydration, Silk Reserves, Intel).

## Page Outline (Conceptual - ~70 pages total):

**Page 1: The Awakening**
  Description: You are an ancient, intelligent cellar spider living in the molding of Castle Aethelgard. You awaken to the sounds of the castle gates opening.
  Choices:
    - Stay in the Great Hall chandelier. -> Page 2
    - Retreat to the vents. -> Page 3

**Page 2: Great Hall Chandelier**
  Description: Perched in the chandelier, you observe the arrival of guests. Heat from lights is a minor risk.
  Choices:
    - Focus on the Tech Titan's arrival. -> Page 4
    - Focus on the Pop Icon's arrival. -> Page 5
    - Focus on the Spies' arrival. -> Page 6

**Page 3: Vents and Whispers**
  Description: In the dark, cool vents, you smell copper and overhear whispers about "The Red Room."
  Choices:
    - Follow the whispers towards the Red Room. -> Page 7
    - Explore deeper into the vent system. -> Page 8

## Potential Branching Paths & Endings:
- **Core Path:** Interactions within the castle, observing secrets.
- **Wildcard - Mutation:** Encountering a glowing substance, gaining enhanced abilities.
- **Wildcard - Cyborg:** Discovered by tinkerer, integrated into a mechanical spider.
- **Endings:** The Silent Witness, The Saboteur, The Hitchhiker, outcomes from Mutation/Cyborg paths.

## Implementation Notes:
- Each page will be a separate text file or section, clearly labeled with its page number.
- Cross-referencing between pages will use the "Page #: [Page Title]" format for easy navigation.
- The structure will be iteratively developed to ensure logical flow and reach the target page count.
