You are a castaway, waking up on a pristine, yet unnervingly quiet, beach. The last thing you remember is a deafening roar, the tearing of metal, and then… nothing. Your head throbs, and a chilling amnesia has wiped your slate clean. Who are you? How did you get here? The questions swirl in your mind like the waves lapping at your feet. The sun beats down, and the air is thick with the scent of salt and unfamiliar blossoms. Before you lies the vast, indifferent ocean, and behind you, a dense, impenetrable jungle. Your survival, and perhaps your sanity, depends on your next move.

What do you do?
