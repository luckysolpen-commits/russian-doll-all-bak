As you reach out to the Eye of the Island, an overwhelming surge of pure energy engulfs you. You feel your consciousness expand infinitely, merging with the island's vast awareness and its raw power. You gain an unprecedented understanding of its cycles, its purpose, and its control over reality. This path leads to becoming a powerful, yet potentially enslaved, entity.
