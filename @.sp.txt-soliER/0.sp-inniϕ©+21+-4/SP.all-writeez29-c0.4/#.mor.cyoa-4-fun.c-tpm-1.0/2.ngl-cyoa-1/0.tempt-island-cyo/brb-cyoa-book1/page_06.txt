You cautiously follow the strange, rhythmic sound deeper into the jungle. The vegetation grows thicker, and the air becomes cooler and more humid. The sound grows louder, resolving into a low, resonant hum that seems to vibrate through the very ground. Pushing aside a curtain of vines, you discover the entrance to a hidden cave, almost perfectly concealed by the dense foliage. The humming emanates from within.

What do you do?
