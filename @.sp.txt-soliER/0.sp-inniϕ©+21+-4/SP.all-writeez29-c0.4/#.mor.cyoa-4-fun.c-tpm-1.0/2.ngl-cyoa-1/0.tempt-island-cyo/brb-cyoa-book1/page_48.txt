Following the clues from the journal and your own developing intuition, you journey towards what feels like the heart of the island's power. You find a hidden caldera, nestled deep within the island's interior. At the center of this caldera floats a radiant, pulsating orb of pure light – the 'Eye of the Island.' It hums with immense energy and seems to be the source of the island's cyclical nature and the 'reset' mechanism.

What do you do?
