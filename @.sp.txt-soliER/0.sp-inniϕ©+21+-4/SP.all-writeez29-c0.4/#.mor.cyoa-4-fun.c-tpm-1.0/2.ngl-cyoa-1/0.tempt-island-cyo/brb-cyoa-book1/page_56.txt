The River Folk accept your offer of help. They equip you with a fishing spear and some basic supplies, then integrate you into their defense strategy against Silas. You participate in setting up ambushes along the riverbanks and helping to fortify their main village. Your actions earn you their trust and respect, and you begin to learn about their way of life, their knowledge of the river, and their deep-seated distrust of Silas and his growing influence.
