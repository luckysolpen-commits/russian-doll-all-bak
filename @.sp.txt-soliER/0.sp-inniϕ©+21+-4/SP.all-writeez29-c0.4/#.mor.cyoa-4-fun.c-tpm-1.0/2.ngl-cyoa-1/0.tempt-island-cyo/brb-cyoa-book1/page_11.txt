You cautiously enter the hidden cave. The air inside is cool and damp, carrying the scent of moss and something earthy. The low hum you heard outside is much clearer in here, a deep, resonant vibration that seems to emanate from the very depths of the cave. As your eyes adjust to the dim light filtering in from the entrance, you can make out the rough shape of the cave extending far back into darkness. Faintly, from the deeper recesses, you can hear what sounds like hushed chanting.

What do you do?

*   If the prospect of an unknown, dark cave filled with chanting unnerves you, and you decide to retreat back to the relative safety of the beach, turn to page 2.
