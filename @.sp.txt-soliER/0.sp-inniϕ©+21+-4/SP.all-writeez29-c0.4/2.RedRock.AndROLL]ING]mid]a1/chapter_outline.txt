# Novel Chapter Outline: Red Rock & Roll

**Overall Goal:** 15-20 chapters, 75k-100k words (approx. 4000-5000 words per chapter, 10-15 pages per chapter).

---

**ACT I: THE GILDED CAGE**

**Chapter 1: The Entrepreneur's Facade**
*   **Based on:** SCENE 1: INT. TRITON TOWER OFFICE - DAY
*   **Focus:** Introduce Lucky Starr, his current life as a biotech entrepreneur in Atlantis Prime. Establish the advanced underwater city and the threat of the Carcazods. Show his internal conflict and the facade he maintains.
*   **Word Count Target:** 4500 words

**Chapter 2: Whispers in the Nova**
*   **Based on:** SCENE 2: INT. CLUB NOVA - NIGHT
*   **Focus:** Introduce Gem and Ratchet. Their conversation about the fragile peace and the "Vampire" problem on Mars. Hint at the political tensions and the desperation of the Martian clones.
*   **Word Count Target:** 4500 words

**Chapter 3: The Mercenary's Restlessness**
*   **Based on:** SCENE 3: INT. MERCENARY HIDEOUT - NIGHT
*   **Focus:** Introduce Astra and her crew. Their feelings of obsolescence and the lingering desire for action. Discussion of Martian factions and the power vacuum.
*   **Word Count Target:** 4500 words

**Chapter 4: The Red Queen's Fading Grip**
*   **Based on:** SCENE 4: INT. QUEEN'S THRONE ROOM (MARS) - DAY
*   **Focus:** Introduce the Red Queen and Zara. Their tense relationship and the unstable climate on Mars. The Queen's struggle to control her "Vampire" guards and maintain power.
*   **Word Count Target:** 4500 words

**Chapter 5: A Desperate Arrival**
*   **Based on:** SCENE 5: INT. TRITON TOWER - HANGAR BAY - DAY
*   **Focus:** The arrival of the ship from Mars. The quarantine and the circumstances that bring Lucky, Gem, and Astra together. The revelation of the Queen's accelerated invasion plan, twisted by Zara.
*   **Word Count Target:** 4500 words

**ACT II: THE DEVIL'S DEAL**

**Chapter 6: Detained Diplomacy**
*   **Based on:** SCENE 6: INT. UN DETENTION CELL - DAY
*   **Focus:** The trio's detention by UN forces. Their request for an audience with the Queen, who is also on Earth.
*   **Word Count Target:** 4500 words

**Chapter 7: An Unlikely Alliance**
*   **Based on:** SCENE 7: INT. QUEEN'S QUARTERS - DAY
*   **Focus:** The Queen's desperate meeting with the trio. Her proposal for an alliance: help her stop Zara, and she'll share technology.
*   **Word Count Target:** 4500 words

**Chapter 8: Playing the Game**
*   **Based on:** SCENE 8: INT. ABYSSAL BIOTECH LAB - NIGHT
*   **Focus:** The trio's discussion of the Queen's offer. Their decision to play along to gather intel on Zara's faction and the mutagen.
*   **Word Count Target:** 4500 words

**Chapter 9: Covert Operations (Montage Expanded)**
*   **Based on:** SCENE 9: MONTAGE
*   **Focus:** Expand on the trio's "work" with the Queen. Gem and Ratchet gathering intel. Lucky tracking supply lines. Astra training mercs. Show their individual skills and growing synergy.
*   **Word Count Target:** 4500 words

**Chapter 10: Zara's Deception**
*   **Based on:** SCENE 10: INT. ZARA'S FLAGSHIP (ORBIT) - DAY
*   **Focus:** Zara's briefing to her commanders. Her plan to deliver a modified, weaponized mutagen to the UN, framing the Queen.
*   **Word Count Target:** 4500 words

**Chapter 11: The Race Against Time**
*   **Based on:** SCENE 11: INT. QUEEN'S WAR ROOM - DAY
*   **Focus:** The trio and the Queen discovering Zara's plan. The realization that they must race to deliver the *real* mutagen and peace agreement to the UN envoy ship.
*   **Word Count Target:** 4500 words

**Chapter 12: Martian Skirmish**
*   **Based on:** SCENE 12: INT. MARS SUPPLY DEPOT - DAY
*   **Focus:** The ground battle on Mars. Queen's loyalists vs. Zara's forces to get the mutagen sample to a launchpad.
*   **Word Count Target:** 4500 words

**Chapter 13: Orbital Chaos**
*   **Based on:** SCENE 13: EXT. SPACE - DAY
*   **Focus:** The massive space battle. Queen's fleet vs. Zara's armada. Focus on providing cover for the STARLIGHT SERENADE.
*   **Word Count Target:** 4500 words

**Chapter 14: The Desperate Run**
*   **Based on:** SCENE 14: INT. STARLIGHT SERENADE - COCKPIT - DAY
*   **Focus:** The trio's desperate run through the blockade aboard the Starlight Serenade, carrying the package.
*   **Word Count Target:** 4500 words
