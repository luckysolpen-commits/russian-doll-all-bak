### **CHARACTER SHEET**

**PROTAGONISTS**

*   **LUCKY STARR**
    *   **Archetype:** The Charming Hustler
    *   **Description:** A former space mercenary in his 40s who has successfully rebranded himself as a slick, wealthy biotech entrepreneur in the new underwater world. He's opportunistic, charming, and driven by profit, but retains the sharp survival instincts and piloting skills from his past. His primary motivation is securing his comfortable new life, but he can't escape the pull of a high-stakes game.
    *   **Key Item:** His company, 'Abyssal Bio-Tech,' and his luxurious office in the Triton Tower.

*   **GEM**
    *   **Archetype:** The Pragmatic Specialist
    *   **Description:** A clone in her 30s with a covered cybernetic arm, Gem is a brilliant security consultant and hacker. She is pragmatic, cautious, and deeply analytical. Having seen the worst of the clone experience on Mars, she trusts few people and always looks for the logical angle. Her loyalty is hard-won but fierce.
    *   **Key Relationship:** Her network of clone contacts, primarily Ratchet.

*   **ASTRA**
    *   **Archetype:** The Restless Warrior
    *   **Description:** A natural-born leader in her 30s, Astra is a seasoned mercenary who feels obsolete in the "peaceful" new world. She is direct, loyal to her crew, and yearns for a life of purpose and action. She is the tactical heart of the trio, skilled in ground combat and small-unit leadership.
    *   **Key Motivation:** A search for purpose beyond being a bodyguard for the wealthy.

**ANTAGONISTS**

*   **ZARA**
    *   **Archetype:** The Ruthless Visionary
    *   **Description:** A charismatic and disciplined Martian commander in her 40s. She sees the Red Queen's rule as decadent and weak, believing only her brand of military order can bring stability. She is a master propagandist and strategist, willing to do whatever it takes to achieve her vision of a strong, unified Mars that dominates Earth.
    *   **Goal:** To supplant the Queen and control both Mars and Earth, believing her rule is for the greater good.

*   **THE ARCHITECT**
    *   **Archetype:** The Unseen Puppeteer
    *   **Description:** A mysterious, hyper-intelligent entity who communicates only through text and voice modulation. The Architect is a master manipulator who sees individuals, factions, and even species as pieces in a grand cosmic game.
    *   **Goal:** To force evolution through conflict, creating a "common enemy" to unite humanity against and accelerate its development.

**SUPPORTING CHARACTERS**

*   **THE RED QUEEN (QUEEN SOPHIA)**
    *   **Archetype:** The Deposed Monarch
    *   **Description:** The former ruler of Mars, now a desperate fugitive. She is regal and intelligent but has been broken by the loss of her kingdom and the instability of her "Vampire" guards. She is forced to make deals with devils to survive, her morality compromised by her desperation to reclaim her throne.

*   **RATCHET**
    *   **Archetype:** The Streetwise Informant
    *   **Description:** A clone contact of Gem's who operates in the underworld of Atlantis Prime. He is a survivor, trading in information and favors to get by. He provides the trio with crucial intelligence from the Martian underground.

*   **DIRECTOR VANCE**
    *   **Archetype:** The Bureaucrat
    *   **Description:** A high-ranking official in the UN Reconstruction Authority. She is stern, by-the-book, and represents the cautious, political nature of the new Earth government.

*   **DR. ARIS**
    *   **Archetype:** The Conscientious Scientist
    *   **Description:** A brilliant but junior UN scientist who first discovers the Carcazod DNA hidden within the mutagen, unraveling the Architect's plot.
