**Title:** Red Rock & Roll

**Logline:** A washed-up space mercenary, a cybernetically enhanced clone, and a renegade aristocrat must navigate a three-way cold war between a vampiric Martian queen, a ruthless corporate warlord, and a fledgling underwater human government to stop a genetically engineered plague from finishing off what's left of humanity.

**ACT I: THE GILDED CAGE**

*   **SCENE 1: INT. TRITON TOWER OFFICE - DAY**
    *   INTRODUCTION to LUCKY STARR, now a slick biotech entrepreneur in the underwater city of ATLANTIS PRIME. He's on a holo-call, schmoozing a UN official for a grant. We see the sprawling, advanced underwater city through his window. ESTABLISH the new status quo: humanity lives in high-tech underwater cities, safe from the CARCAZODS who can't swim.

*   **SCENE 2: INT. CLUB NOVA - NIGHT**
    *   GEM, now a security consultant, meets with an old clone contact, RATCHET. They discuss the fragile peace and the "Vampire" problem on Mars—clones whose blood supply has been cut off, forcing them to resort to desperate measures.

*   **SCENE 3: INT. MERCENARY HIDEOUT - NIGHT**
    *   ASTRA reconnects with her old mercenary crew. They're restless, feeling obsolete in this new, "civilized" world. They talk about the Martian factions and the power vacuum.

*   **SCENE 4: INT. QUEEN'S THRONE ROOM (MARS) - DAY**
    *   The RED QUEEN is on a tense call with ZARA, her rival. The climate on Mars is still unstable, and her "Vampire" guards are a constant source of instability. She's losing her grip.

*   **SCENE 5: INT. TRITON TOWER - HANGAR BAY - DAY**
    *   A ship arrives from Mars, carrying a desperate message for the Queen (who is on Earth). The trio (Lucky, Gem, Astra) are brought together by circumstance as the ship is quarantined. They learn the Queen's plan to invade Earth has been accelerated and twisted by Zara.

**ACT II: THE DEVIL'S DEAL**

*   **SCENE 6: INT. UN DETENTION CELL - DAY**
    *   The trio is detained by UN forces. They request an audience with the Queen, who is also on Earth for diplomatic reasons.

*   **SCENE 7: INT. QUEEN'S QUARTERS - DAY**
    *   The Queen meets the trio. She's desperate. Her guards are vampires, Zara is moving against her, and her original invasion plan is shot. She proposes an alliance: help her stop Zara and stabilize her rule, and she'll share her technology to help Earth.

*   **SCENE 8: INT. ABYSSAL BIOTECH LAB - NIGHT**
    *   The trio discusses the Queen's offer. It's a trap, but it's their only way to get ahead of the situation. They decide to play along to get intel on Zara's faction and the mutagen.

*   **SCENE 9: MONTAGE**
    *   The trio "works" with the Queen.
    *   Gem and Ratchet use their network to gather intel on Zara's forces.
    *   Lucky uses his corporate access to track supply lines.
    *   Astra trains a small squad of mercs for a potential strike.

*   **SCENE 10: INT. ZARA'S FLAGSHIP (ORBIT) - DAY**
    *   ZARA briefs her commanders. She plans to deliver a modified, weaponized version of the mutagen to the UN, framing the Queen and seizing control of both Martian and Earth forces.

*   **SCENE 11: INT. QUEEN'S WAR ROOM - DAY**
    *   The trio and the Queen discover Zara's plan. They realize it's a race to deliver the *real* mutagen and the peace agreement to the UN envoy ship in orbit.

*   **SCENE 12: INT. MARS SUPPLY DEPOT - DAY**
    *   A ground battle erupts on Mars as the Queen's loyalists fight Zara's forces to get the last shipment of supplies (and the mutagen sample) to a launchpad.

*   **SCENE 13: EXT. SPACE - DAY**
    *   A massive space battle. The Queen's small fleet vs. Zara's armada. It's a chaotic, brutal fight focused on providing cover for one ship, the STARLIGHT SERENADE, to break through and reach the Earth envoy.

*   **SCENE 14: INT. STARLIGHT SERENADE - COCKPIT - DAY**
    *   The trio, aboard the ship, makes a desperate run through the blockade, carrying the package.

**ACT III: THE ABYSS GAZES BACK**

*   **SCENE 15: INT. UN ENVOY SHIP - DAY**
    *   The trio delivers the package. They are immediately put against Zara and the Queen in a tense diplomatic standoff in front of Earth's leaders.

*   **SCENE 16: INT. UN COUNCIL CHAMBERS - DAY**
    *   The debate rages. Zara's propaganda vs. the Queen's desperate plea vs. the trio's hard evidence. Earth's leaders are torn.

*   **SCENE 17: INT. ATLANTIS PRIME - MEDICAL BAY - DAY**
    *   UN scientists, analyzing the mutagen, discover its true potential—and its terrifying dangers. They also discover a hidden bio-marker... something not human or Martian.

*   **SCENE 18: EXT. ATLANTIS PRIME - PERIMETER - NIGHT**
    *   A Carcazod, larger and more intelligent than any seen before, is spotted near the city's perimeter. It's testing the defenses. It seems to be... evolving.

*   **SCENE 19: INT. UN COUNCIL CHAMBERS - DAY**
    *   News of the evolving Carcazod threat reaches the council. The petty squabbles are interrupted. They realize the bio-marker in the mutagen is Carcazod. Zara or the Queen (or both) were trying to control them. The Architect's influence is revealed.

*   **SCENE 20: INT. TRITON TOWER OFFICE - NIGHT**
    *   The factions are forced into a fragile, temporary alliance against the new Carcazod threat. Lucky looks out his window at the dark abyss, a new, more dangerous game beginning. He gets a one-word message on his private comm: "Hello." - from The Architect.
