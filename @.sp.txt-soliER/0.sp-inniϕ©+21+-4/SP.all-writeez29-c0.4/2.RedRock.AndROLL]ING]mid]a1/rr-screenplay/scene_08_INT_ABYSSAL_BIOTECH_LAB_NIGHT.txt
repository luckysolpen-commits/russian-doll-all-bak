**SCENE 8**

**INT. ABYSSAL BIOTECH LAB - NIGHT**

SOUND of bubbling liquids, humming machinery

The lab is a state-of-the-art facility, filled with holographic displays and complex scientific equipment. This is Lucky's domain.

Lucky, Gem, and Astra are alone. The mood is tense.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> A deal with a deposed queen who keeps vampires for pets. What could possibly go wrong?

<center>ASTRA</center>
> She's desperate. Desperate people are predictable. And dangerous.

<center>GEM</center>
> She's also our only source of intel. Zara is moving now. The UN is on the fence. If we don't act, Zara wins. And the Queen is right--she won't stop at Mars.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> So we play along? Become her royal agents?

<center>ASTRA</center>
> We use her. We get her intel, find out what she knows about Zara's forces, her version of the mutagen, everything. We get ahead of the situation.

<center>GEM</center>
> It's a trap.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Of course it's a trap. The question is, for whom?

Lucky pulls up a holographic map of the solar system. He highlights Mars and Earth.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Zara wants Earth's support. The Queen wants her kingdom back. The UN wants to avoid a war. And we're in the middle.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> We need to control the outcome. And that means getting our hands on the mutagen. The real one.

<center>GEM</center>
> If it even exists.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Oh, it exists. A biological key to unlock the planet? That's not war. That's the biggest business opportunity in human history.

He looks at Gem and Astra.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> We're in. We'll be her 'allies.' But we play our own game. We find out what she knows, we get the mutagen, and we stop Zara. For our own reasons.

Gem and Astra nod in agreement. The unlikely alliance is formed.

**FADE OUT.**
