**SCENE 20**

**INT. TRITON TOWER OFFICE - NIGHT**

SOUND of the deep, resonant HUM of the city

Lucky stands at his panoramic window, looking out at the sprawling underwater city. The lights of Atlantis Prime seem more fragile now, more vulnerable.

He swirls a glass of amber liquid, the same as in the beginning, but his posture is different. The easy confidence is gone, replaced by a weary tension.

The door opens and Gem and Astra enter.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> The UN has formed an emergency council. They've... requested Zara's assistance.

<center>GEM</center>
> A temporary, unified command. All factions against the new threat.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> A fragile alliance, bought and paid for by the Architect.

He takes a drink.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> He played us all. Every single move.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> So what now? We join the happy family? Fight alongside Zara?

<center>LUCKY</center>
> For now. We fight the monsters. But we don't forget who created them.

Gem looks out at the dark abyss beyond the city lights.

<center>GEM</center>
> He's still out there. Watching.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> He'll make a mistake. They always do.

Lucky's private comm-link on his desk chimes. A single, text-only message appears on the screen.

It reads: "Hello."

Below the message is the stylized 'A' of the Architect.

Lucky, Gem, and Astra look at the message. The new war has already begun.

Lucky picks up his glass, a grim determination in his eyes. He looks out at the abyss, at the darkness that holds the new enemy.

**FADE TO BLACK.**
