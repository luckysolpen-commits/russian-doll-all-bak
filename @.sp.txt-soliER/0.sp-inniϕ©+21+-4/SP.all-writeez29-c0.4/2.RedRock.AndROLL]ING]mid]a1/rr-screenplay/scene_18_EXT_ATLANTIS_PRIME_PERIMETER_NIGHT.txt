**SCENE 18**

**EXT. ATLANTIS PRIME - PERIMETER - NIGHT**

SOUND of deep ocean currents, the distant HUM of the city

The abyssal plain. The water is black, pierced only by the lights of Atlantis Prime in the distance.

A lone submersible patrols the city's perimeter fence, a massive, electrified barrier.

**INT. SUBMERSIBLE - COCKPIT - NIGHT**

Two bored UN PILOTS are at the controls.

<center>PILOT 1</center>
> Anything on sonar?

<center>PILOT 2</center>
> Same as last sweep. A few whale-sharks, some... squid... nothing.

Suddenly, a proximity alert beeps.

<center>PILOT 1</center>
> What was that?

<center>PILOT 2</center>
> Probably just a glitch. This thing is older than my grandfather.

He taps the console. The beeping stops.

Then, a massive shape drifts into the submersible's lights.

It's a CARCAZOD. But not like any they've seen before. It's easily twice the size of a normal one, its hide is thicker, more armored. And its eyes... they glow with a malevolent intelligence.

The pilots stare in stunned silence.

<center>PILOT 1</center>
> Sweet mother of...

The Carcazod doesn't attack. It floats there, observing the submersible, the fence. It seems to be... thinking. Testing.

It reaches out a clawed hand and touches the perimeter fence. A massive jolt of electricity arcs through the water. The Carcazod recoils, but it doesn't flee. It roars, a silent, terrifying display of fury.

Then, as quickly as it appeared, it turns and disappears into the blackness.

The pilots look at each other, their faces ashen.

<center>PILOT 2</center>
> (into comms)
> Command... you are not going to believe this.

**FADE OUT.**
