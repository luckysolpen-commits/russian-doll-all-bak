**SCENE 9**

**MONTAGE**

**A) INT. QUEEN'S WAR ROOM - DAY**

The trio stands with the QUEEN around a holographic map. She points to strategic locations, explaining Zara's tactics. Gem and Astra listen intently, while Lucky seems more interested in the technology running the display.

**B) INT. CLUB NOVA - NIGHT**

GEM and RATCHET sit in a shadowy booth. Ratchet slides a data chip across the table. Gem palms it, her eyes scanning the club.

**C) INT. ABYSSAL BIOTECH LAB - DAY**

LUCKY is on a holo-call with a smarmy corporate contact. He's tracking shipping routes, supply chains. He cross-references the information with the Queen's intel, a web of connections forming on his screen.

**D) INT. MERCENARY HIDEOUT - DAY**

ASTRA trains her small squad of mercs. They are practicing close-quarters combat, their movements precise and deadly. They are no longer soft.

**E) INT. QUEEN'S WAR ROOM - DAY**

The QUEEN watches the trio, a flicker of hope in her eyes. But there's also a hint of calculation. She is using them, just as they are using her.

**F) INT. ABYSSAL BIOTECH LAB - NIGHT**

GEM plugs the data chip from Ratchet into the main computer. Encrypted files appear on the screen. She begins to decrypt them.

<center>GEM</center>
> I'm in.

Lucky and Astra gather around.

On the screen, they see it: shipping manifests, troop movements, and a bio-signature they don't recognize.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> What is that?

<center>GEM</center>
> It's the mutagen. Zara's version. And it's already on its way to a flagship.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> She's not going to wait for the UN's approval.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> She's going to force their hand.

The trio looks at the screen, the pieces of the puzzle falling into place.

**END MONTAGE.**
