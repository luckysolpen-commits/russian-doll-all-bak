**SCENE 15**

**INT. UN ENVOY SHIP - CONFERENCE ROOM - DAY**

SOUND of tense silence

The room is large and official, dominated by a polished table. On one side sits ZARA, flanked by her commanders. She looks calm and confident.

On the other side sits LUCKY, GEM, and ASTRA. They are exhausted, still wearing their battered gear. The container with the mutagen sits on the table in front of them.

At the head of the table sits the UN CHAIRMAN, a man with the weight of the world on his shoulders.

<center>CHAIRMAN</center>
> We have... a situation. Two competing narratives. Two queens, one dead, one very much alive. And a sample of a mutagen that could either save or doom us all.

<center>ZARA</center>
> The situation is simple, Chairman. The late Queen was a terrorist. She and her criminal associates
> (she gestures to the trio)
> attempted to deploy a biological weapon. I stopped them. I am here to offer you the technology that will save your planet.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> She's lying.

<center>ZARA</center>
> The businessman speaks. You have no authority here.

<center>GEM</center>
> We have evidence. The Queen's final broadcast. The logs from the Starlight Serenade.

<center>ZARA</center>
> Fabricated. All of it.

<center>CHAIRMAN</center>
> We have two samples of the mutagen. One from you, Commander Zara. And one from... these individuals. Our scientists are analyzing them now.

<center>ZARA</center>
> I welcome the scrutiny. My mutagen is a gift. Theirs is a weapon.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> The only weapon here is you.

The Chairman raises a hand for silence.

<center>CHAIRMAN</center>
> We will have the truth soon enough. Until then... we wait.

The two factions stare at each other across the table, the fate of worlds hanging in the balance.

**FADE OUT.**
