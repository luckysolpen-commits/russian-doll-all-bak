**SCENE 10**

**INT. ZARA'S FLAGSHIP - BRIDGE - DAY**

SOUND of a calm, efficient bridge hum

The bridge is militaristic and spartan. Everything is grey and functional. ZARA stands in the center of the bridge, looking out at the starfield on the main viewscreen.

Her commanders, all sharp and disciplined, stand at their stations.

<center>ZARA</center>
> Report.

<center>COMMANDER 1</center>
> The package is secure in the bio-lab, Commander. Ready for transport.

<center>ZARA</center>
> And the UN envoy?

<center>COMMANDER 2</center>
> On schedule. They are eager to receive our... gift.

<center>ZARA</center>
> They are weak. They crave a simple solution, and we will provide it. They will see the Queen as a chaotic warmonger, and us as the saviors of Earth.

<center>COMMANDER 1</center>
> And the Queen's new... associates?

Zara smiles, a cold, thin smile.

<center>ZARA</center>
> The mercenary, the clone, and the businessman? Let them play their little games. They are a distraction. By the time they realize what is happening, we will already have won.

She turns to her commanders, her eyes burning with conviction.

<center>ZARA</center>
> For too long, Mars has been ruled by a decadent, unstable monarchy. We will bring order. We will bring strength. And we will start with Earth.

She looks back at the viewscreen, at the distant blue marble of Earth.

<center>ZARA</center>
> Prepare the shuttle. It's time to deliver the future.

**FADE OUT.**
