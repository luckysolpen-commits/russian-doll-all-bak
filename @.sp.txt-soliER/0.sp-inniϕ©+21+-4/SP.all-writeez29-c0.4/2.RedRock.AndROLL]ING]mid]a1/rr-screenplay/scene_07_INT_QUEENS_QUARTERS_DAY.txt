**SCENE 7**

**INT. QUEEN'S QUARTERS - DAY**

SOUND of faint, distant city HUM

These are luxurious quarters, but they feel like a cage. The windows show a view of the underwater city, but the glass is reinforced.

The RED QUEEN stands by the window, her back to the door. She looks regal, but smaller, more vulnerable than she did on her throne.

Lucky, Gem, and Astra are escorted in. The door slides shut behind them, leaving them alone with the Queen.

She turns to face them. Her eyes are filled with a desperate intensity.

<center>QUEEN</center>
> The mercenary, the clone, and the... businessman. An unlikely trio of saviors.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> I prefer 'concerned investor.' And we're not here to save you.

<center>QUEEN</center>
> No. You're here because you're trapped. Just like me. Zara has played her hand. She will paint me as a warmonger, and Earth will back her, giving her the legitimacy she needs to seize my throne.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> And why should we care?

<center>QUEEN</center>
> Because her rule will not be confined to Mars. She has a... modified version of the mutagen. A weapon. Once she has consolidated power, Earth is her next target.

<center>GEM</center>
> You were planning to invade Earth yourself.

<center>QUEEN</center>
> My plans have... changed. My guards... the climate... I have lost control. I am a queen with no army and no kingdom.

She steps forward, her composure cracking.

<center>QUEEN</center>
> I need your help. You have connections here. The skills. Help me stop Zara. Help me expose her to the UN.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> What's in it for us?

<center>QUEEN</center>
> Everything. My technology. My resources. The real mutagen, not the weaponized version Zara possesses. It can rebuild Earth's biosphere. It can cure diseases. It can save your world. All I ask is for my own in return.

She looks at them, her desperation laid bare.

<center>QUEEN</center>
> Form an alliance with me. It is the only way any of us survive this.

The trio exchange glances. A deal with the devil.

**FADE OUT.**
