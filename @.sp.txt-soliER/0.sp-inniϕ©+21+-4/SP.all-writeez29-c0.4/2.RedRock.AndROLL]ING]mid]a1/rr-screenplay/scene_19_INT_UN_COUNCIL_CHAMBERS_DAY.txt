**SCENE 19**

**INT. UN COUNCIL CHAMBERS - DAY**

SOUND of a panicked, chaotic crowd

The chamber is in an uproar. The image of the new, intelligent CARCAZOD is on the main screen.

The CHAIRMAN is trying to restore order.

<center>CHAIRMAN</center>
> Order! We need order!

Dr. Aris, the scientist from the medical bay, is at the podium. He looks terrified.

<center>DR. ARIS</center>
> The bio-marker in the mutagen... it's a perfect match for the new Carcazod variant. This isn't just a cure for Earth, it's a control agent for them.

All eyes in the chamber turn to ZARA. Her calm, confident facade is gone. She looks genuinely shocked.

<center>ZARA</center>
> That's... impossible. The mutagen was a Martian discovery. It has nothing to do with... them.

<center>GEM</center>
> (from the viewing box)
> She's lying.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> No... I don't think she is. Look at her. She had no idea.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> Then who?

The Chairman looks at Zara, then at the trio.

<center>CHAIRMAN</center>
> Someone created this mutagen. Someone has been playing us all.

Suddenly, a new voice fills the chamber. It's smooth, calm, and utterly chilling. It seems to come from everywhere at once.

<center>ARCHITECT (V.O.)</center>
> It is remarkable, is it not? The intricate dance of survival. So much passion. So much... potential.

Every screen in the chamber flickers, and then a single, stylized 'A' appears.

<center>ZARA</center>
> Who is that?

<center>LUCKY</center>
> The Architect.

<center>ARCHITECT (V.O.)</center>
> You have all played your parts beautifully. The Queen, the Warlord, the Heroes. But the game is over. It is time for the next stage of evolution. And I am its architect.

The delegates are in a panic. The guards are searching for the source of the broadcast.

<center>CHAIRMAN</center>
> What have you done?

<center>ARCHITECT (V.O.)</center>
> I have given you a common enemy. A reason to unite. A reason to evolve. You're welcome.

The broadcast cuts out. The chamber is left in stunned, terrified silence.

**FADE OUT.**
