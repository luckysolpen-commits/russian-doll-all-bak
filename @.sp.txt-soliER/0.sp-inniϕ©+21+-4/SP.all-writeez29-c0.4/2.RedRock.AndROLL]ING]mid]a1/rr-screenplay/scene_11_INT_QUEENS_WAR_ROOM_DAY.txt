**SCENE 11**

**INT. QUEEN'S WAR ROOM - DAY**

SOUND of frantic, overlapping voices

The war room is in a state of controlled chaos. The Queen, Lucky, Gem, and Astra are gathered around the holographic map.

<center>GEM</center>
> Zara's not waiting. She's moving the mutagen to a shuttle. She's going to deliver it directly to the UN envoy.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> She's cutting us out of the deal. Forcing a decision.

<center>QUEEN</center>
> She will show them a weapon and call it a cure. They will be desperate enough to believe her.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> We can't let that shuttle reach the envoy. We need to intercept it.

<center>QUEEN</center>
> My fleet is in disarray. Her forces outnumber mine three to one.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Then we don't fight a war. We win a race.

He points to a location on Mars.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> You have a sample of the real mutagen here. In a secure lab.

<center>QUEEN</center>
> Yes. But it's deep in contested territory.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> We need to get it. And we need to get it to the envoy before Zara does.

<center>GEM</center>
> It's a suicide mission.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> It's our only shot. We create a diversion, a big, loud, messy battle. And while Zara's forces are occupied, a single ship makes a run for the envoy.

<center>QUEEN</center>
> My people will fight. They will die for this chance.

<center>ASTRA</center>
> We'll lead the strike team on the ground. Get the sample.

<center>GEM</center>
> I'll run comms. Coordinate the fleets.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> And I'll... arrange the transportation.

He looks at the Queen.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> You have a ship. Small, fast, unassuming. The Starlight Serenade.

<center>QUEEN</center>
> It's a diplomatic courier. Unarmed.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> Exactly. No one will see it coming.

The four of them look at each other. The impossible plan is set.

**FADE OUT.**
