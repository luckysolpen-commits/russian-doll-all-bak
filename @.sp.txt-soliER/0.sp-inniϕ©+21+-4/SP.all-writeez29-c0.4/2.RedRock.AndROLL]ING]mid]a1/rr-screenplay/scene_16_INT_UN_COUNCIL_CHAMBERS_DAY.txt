**SCENE 16**

**INT. UN COUNCIL CHAMBERS - DAY**

SOUND of heated debate, murmuring crowds

The chambers are a grand amphitheater, packed with delegates from every nation of the new underwater world. The atmosphere is electric with tension.

Lucky, Gem, and Astra watch from a private viewing box.

On the main floor, ZARA is giving a powerful, charismatic speech.

<center>ZARA</center>
> ...we stand on the precipice of a new era! An era of strength, of unity, of purpose! The Queen's archaic monarchy is dead. A new, stronger Mars is ready to be an ally to Earth. To share our resources, our technology. To help you rebuild.

She is met with a wave of applause.

<center>LUCKY</center>
> She's good.

<center>GEM</center>
> She's selling a fantasy.

Next, a recording of the late QUEEN's final broadcast is played. Her defiant, desperate plea for help.

The mood in the chamber shifts. There is sympathy, confusion.

Finally, the UN CHAIRMAN takes the floor.

<center>CHAIRMAN</center>
> We have heard the words. We have seen the evidence. But the most important testimony comes not from the living or the dead, but from the science.

He gestures to a screen. Two DNA helixes appear.

<center>CHAIRMAN</center>
> Our scientists have analyzed both samples of the mutagen. They are... nearly identical. Both have the potential to heal our world. But both also have the potential for great destruction.

He looks at Zara, then towards the trio's viewing box.

<center>CHAIRMAN</center>
> The question is not which is the weapon and which is the cure. The question is, who do we trust to wield such power?

The chamber erupts in debate. The delegates are divided. The fate of the alliance hangs on a knife's edge.

**FADE OUT.**
