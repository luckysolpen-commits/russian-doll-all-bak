As they boarded the freighter, Lucky took one last look at
-the shimmering cityscape of Atlantis Prime. It was a
-beautiful, fragile thing, a testament to humanity’s
-resilience. And it was in danger.

He felt a surge of protectiveness, a feeling he had not
-expected. He was not just fighting for himself anymore. He
was fighting for them. For all of them.

He looked at Astra, at Gem, at the faces of his new crew.
He saw the same determination, the same resolve, in their
-eyes. They were all in this together. They were all
-fighting for the same thing.

He smiled. For the first time in a long time, he felt like
he was a part of something bigger than himself. He felt
like he was home.
