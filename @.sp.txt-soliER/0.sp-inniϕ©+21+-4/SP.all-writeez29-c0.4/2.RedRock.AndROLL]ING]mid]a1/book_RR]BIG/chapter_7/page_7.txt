The Queen watched them, a mixture of hope and fear in her
-eyes. She had unleashed a force she did not fully understand,
a team of ghosts from a forgotten war. She had placed the
-fate of her world in their hands.

She knew it was a gamble. A desperate, reckless gamble.
But it was the only gamble she had left.

She prayed that she had made the right choice. She prayed
that they would succeed. She prayed that they would not
-become the very monsters they were fighting.

But as she watched them, as she saw the fire in their eyes,
the determination in their faces, she felt a flicker of
-something she had not felt in a long time. Hope.

Maybe, just maybe, they could pull it off. Maybe, just
-maybe, they could save them all.

And so, she waited. She watched. And she hoped.
