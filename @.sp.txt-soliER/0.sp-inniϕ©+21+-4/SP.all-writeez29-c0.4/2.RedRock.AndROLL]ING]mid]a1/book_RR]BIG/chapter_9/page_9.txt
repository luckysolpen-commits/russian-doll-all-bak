They floated in the silence of space, the only sound the
-faint hum of the life support systems. They watched as
Zara’s fleet, a swarm of deadly predators, swept past them,
-their sensors searching for a prey that was no longer there.

It was a tense, nerve-wracking wait. But it worked.

When the last of Zara’s ships had disappeared from their
-sensors, they powered up the *Serenade* and made a run for
the UN envoy ship.

They were a tiny, insignificant speck in the vastness of
-space. But they were a speck with a purpose. A speck that
-carried the fate of two worlds.

They were the last, best hope for a universe on the brink
of destruction. They were a team of misfits, of outcasts,
of survivors. They were a family.

And they were about to deliver a message of hope.
