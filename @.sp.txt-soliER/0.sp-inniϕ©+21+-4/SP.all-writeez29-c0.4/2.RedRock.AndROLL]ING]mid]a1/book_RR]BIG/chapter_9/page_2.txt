Explosions rocked the depot, sending showers of sparks and
-debris into the thin Martian atmosphere. A cargo container,
-struck by a stray plasma bolt, erupted in a fiery inferno.
Ratchet saw one of his men fall, a smoking hole in his chest,
and a surge of cold fury coursed through him. Zara would pay
for this. They all would.

He was not a soldier. He was a survivor. A scavenger. A
-man who had spent his life in the shadows, avoiding trouble.
But now, he was a leader. A commander. And he would not let
his people down.

He fought with a desperate, savage intensity. He was not
as strong or as fast as the younger clones, but he had
-experience. He had cunning. And he had a will to survive
that was second to none.

He was a cornered rat, and he was fighting for his life.
