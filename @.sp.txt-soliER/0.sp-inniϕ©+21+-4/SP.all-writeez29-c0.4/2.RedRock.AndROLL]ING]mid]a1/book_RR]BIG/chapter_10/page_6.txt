Astra expertly maneuvered the *Starlight Serenade* into the
-yawning maw of the *Diplomat One*’s hangar bay. The ship
-shuddered as it docked, the clamps locking into place with a
-resounding thud. As the ramp extended, Lucky, Gem, and Astra
-stood ready, the container held firmly in Lucky’s grasp.
They had made it this far. The desperate run was over. But the
-real battle, the battle for truth and the future of humanity,
was just beginning.

They were met by a squad of heavily armed UN security guards,
-their weapons trained on them. Rostova stood behind them, her
-face a mask of grim determination.

“You have five minutes to convince me that I haven’t just
-made the biggest mistake of my career,” she said, her voice
-cold, hard.

Lucky nodded. “That’s all we’ll need.”
