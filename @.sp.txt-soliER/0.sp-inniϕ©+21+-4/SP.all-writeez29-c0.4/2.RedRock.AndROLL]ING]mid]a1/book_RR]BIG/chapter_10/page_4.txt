Rostova’s face paled. “Zara’s ship? But… our intelligence

“That’s Zara for you,” Lucky said grimly. “Always ahead of
the curve. She’s not just sending a package, Commander. She’s
-sending a message. A very deadly one.”

The *Diplomat One*’s internal alarms began to blare, a shrill,

-mutagen, she’s going to fire it!”

“Open the bay doors, Commander!” Lucky pleaded, his voice
-urgent. “You have to trust us! If Zara’s mutagen reaches
your ship, it’s over. For all of us.”
