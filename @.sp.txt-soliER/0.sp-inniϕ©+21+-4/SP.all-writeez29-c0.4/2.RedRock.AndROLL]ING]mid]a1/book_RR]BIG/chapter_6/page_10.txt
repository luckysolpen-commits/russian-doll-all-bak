They had a plan. A desperate, reckless plan, but a plan
-nonetheless. They would go to Mars. They would infiltrate
Zara’s facility. They would steal the mutagen. And they
-would use it to expose her, to turn her own weapon against
her.

It was a suicide mission. But they were the only ones who
-could do it. They were a team of ghosts, of outcasts, of
-survivors. They were the last, best hope for a universe
on the brink of destruction.

As they prepared for their mission, Lucky looked at the
-faces of his crew. He saw the fear, the determination, the
-unwavering loyalty. He saw the family he had lost, and the
-family he had found again.

He was not the same man he had been all those years ago.
He was a better man. A man who was willing to fight for
-something more than just himself. A man who was willing to
-die for the people he loved.

He was Lucky Starr. And he was ready for his final gamble.
