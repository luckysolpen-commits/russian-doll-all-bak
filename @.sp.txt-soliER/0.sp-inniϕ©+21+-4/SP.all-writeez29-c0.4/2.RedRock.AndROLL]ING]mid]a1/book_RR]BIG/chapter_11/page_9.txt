The plan was desperate, reckless, but it was the only one
they had. They would use the mutagen, the very weapon that
had been designed to destroy them, to save them. They would
-modify it, re-engineer it, turn it into a weapon that could
-be used against the Carcazods.

It was a race against time. The Carcazod was getting closer.
The fate of Atlantis Prime, of Earth, of all of humanity,
-hung in the balance.

But they were ready. They were united. They were a team.

They were the last, best hope for a universe on the brink
of destruction.

And they would not fail.
