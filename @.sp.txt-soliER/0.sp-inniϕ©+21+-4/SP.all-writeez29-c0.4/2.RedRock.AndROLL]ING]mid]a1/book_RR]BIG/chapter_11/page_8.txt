As they worked, a new dynamic began to emerge. The old
-animosities, the old rivalries, began to fade away. They
were no longer enemies, but allies. They were a team.

Lucky found himself working alongside Zara, the woman he had
-sworn to destroy. He saw the brilliance of her mind, the
-ruthlessness of her ambition. And he realized that they were
-not so different, she and he.

Astra found herself fighting alongside the Red Queen’s
-loyalist soldiers, the very people she had once considered
her enemies. She saw their courage, their loyalty, their
-unwavering determination. And she realized that they were
-all just soldiers, fighting for what they believed in.

Gem found herself working with the UN’s top scientists, the
-very people who had once hunted her, who had seen her as
-nothing more than a machine. She saw their dedication, their
-brilliance, their passion for knowledge. And she realized
that they were all just searching for the truth.

They were a new kind of alliance, a new kind of family.
Forged in the crucible of a common threat. United by a
-common purpose.
