INT. UNITED NATIONS HEADQUARTERS - NEW YORK - DAY

SOUND of urgent chatter, news reports playing on multiple screens

GENERAL ARMSTRONG, a stern-faced military leader, stands before a large holographic map of the solar system. Red markers blink ominously around the Architect's mine camp.

ARMSTRONG
(Pounding a fist on the table)
A Dyson Sphere! He's building a Death Star, right under our noses! This cannot stand.

DELEGATE CHEN
(A Chinese diplomat, calm but firm)
General, we must consider the implications. A direct military confrontation with the Architect's technology could be catastrophic.

ARMSTRONG
And allowing him to complete this… Metal Sun? That's an even greater catastrophe! He speaks of transcendence, but I see only a madman bent on global domination.

SCIENTIST DR. ELARA VANCE
(A brilliant but cautious astrophysicist)
His energy readings are off the charts. If he truly intends to harness antimatter on that scale, the potential for a catastrophic spacetime distortion is immense. We're talking about an existential threat.

ARMSTRONG
(Turns to a comms officer)
Contact Lucky's team. They're already on site. Tell them the UN is dispatching a rapid response unit. We need to shut this down, now.

COMMUNICATIONS OFFICER
(Typing rapidly)
Sir, I'm getting a message from Lucky. He says they've uncovered the Architect's true intentions. It's worse than we thought.

Armstrong's face hardens. The holographic map glows brighter, the red markers expanding.

FADE OUT.
