INT. ARCHITECT'S GRAND ARENA - AFRICA - DAY

SOUND of cheering crowds, triumphant fanfare

A colossal, open-air arena, carved into the African landscape, gleams under the harsh sun. Thousands of spectators, a mix of humans and various alien species, fill the stands. At the center of the arena, a massive holographic display flickers with images of powerful, sleek MECHA.

The ARCHITECT stands on a raised platform, a charismatic smile on his face, addressing the roaring crowd. He wears an elaborate, almost regal, white and gold uniform.

ARCHITECT
(Voice booming, amplified across the arena)
Citizens of the solar system! For too long, humanity has been bound by petty squabbles, by fear of the unknown! But no more! Today, we usher in a new era of unity, of technological marvel!

He gestures to the holographic display, which now shows his "Metal Horses" and the "Black Eyes Metal Dragon."

ARCHITECT (CONT'D)
Behold! The pinnacle of mecha engineering! My magnificent creations, designed not for war, but for spectacle! For the advancement of all sentient life!

The crowd roars its approval. The Architect raises his hands for silence.

ARCHITECT (CONT'D)
And to celebrate this new dawn, I propose a grand competition! The Equestrian Games! A series of mecha olympics, where the finest robotic combatants from Earth's United Nations and the formidable forces of China will pit their prowess against my own!

On a large screen behind him, the logos of the UN and China appear, followed by images of their respective mecha. The crowd murmurs with excitement.

ARCHITECT (CONT'D)
After these glorious games, we shall negotiate, not with threats, but with a vote! A vote from every citizen of this solar system, to decide our collective future!

Lucky, Gem, and Astra, disguised in the crowd, exchange worried glances. The Architect's showmanship is undeniable, but his intentions remain deeply unsettling.

FADE OUT.
