INT. UNDERGROUND REBEL BASE - EARTH - DAY

SOUND of frantic comms chatter, distant rumbling

The air in the cramped, makeshift rebel base is thick with tension. Maps of the solar system, riddled with glowing red lines indicating the Metal Sun's expansion, are projected onto every available surface. Lucky, Gem, and the remaining Koreans are gathered around a central holographic display, their faces grim.

LUCKY
(Pounding a fist on a console)
He's building it too fast! The Icarus is a self-replicating nightmare. We can't keep up with its construction rate.

GEM
(Adjusting her glasses, pointing to a section of the holographic map)
And the energy output… it's stabilizing. He's almost at critical mass. Once the Metal Sun is fully operational, his control will be absolute.

Suddenly, a secure comm channel opens. The face of EDGE DANGERMAN, rugged and weary, appears on the holographic display. He's surrounded by what looks like a makeshift industrial setting, sparks flying in the background.

EDGE
(His voice raspy, but urgent)
Lucky. It's Edge. I'm on the Metal Sun. I'm leading the Metal Mafia now – a union of laborers. We've been forced to work on this… monstrosity.

Lucky's eyes widen in surprise. Edge Dangerman, the former leader of the underworld, now a union boss?

LUCKY
Edge? What's going on? What do you mean forced?

EDGE
(A bitter laugh)
He's got us by the short hairs, Lucky. The Architect. He's promising us a new world, a new order. But it's a lie. He's building this thing to launch world police bots. To enslave humanity. To make everyone take his damn Mark of the Beast.

Gem and the Koreans exchange horrified glances.

EDGE (CONT'D)
And it's worse than you think. Mars and the Lunar Colony… they're backing him. They see it as their chance to finally break free from Earth's influence. They're providing resources, manpower, even some of their own tech.

Lucky's jaw tightens. The scale of the Architect's deception, and the depth of his allies, is staggering.

LUCKY
(His voice low, filled with resolve)
We have to stop him, Edge. Before it's too late.

EDGE
(Nodding, a flicker of hope in his eyes)
That's why I'm calling. The UN… they're desperate. They know they can't fight him head-on. They're reaching out to anyone who can help. They want you, Lucky. They want your team. You're the only ones who've ever stood a chance against him.

Lucky looks at Gem and the Koreans. The weight of the world, and the entire solar system, rests on their shoulders. The distant rumbling of the Metal Sun's construction seems to intensify, a relentless march towards a dystopian future.

FADE OUT.
