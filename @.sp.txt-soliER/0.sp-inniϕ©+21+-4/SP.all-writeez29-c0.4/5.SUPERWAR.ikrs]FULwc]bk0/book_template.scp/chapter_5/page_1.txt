The Architect was not merely showcasing his engineering prowess; he was sending a message, a chilling demonstration of his power and his willingness to use it.

The ground beneath their feet trembled with the force of the ongoing conflict, a constant tremor that vibrated through their very bones. It was a scene of utter chaos, a maelstrom of destruction that filled them with a chilling sense of dread. The Architect, observing from his private skybox, watched with a detached intensity, his face a mask of impassive detachment, his eyes betraying no emotion.
