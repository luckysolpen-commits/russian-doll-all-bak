Their chance to show the Architect that humanity, even without his twisted enhancements, was still capable of greatness. The Metal Sun, his ultimate weapon, was still under construction, its ominous presence a constant threat, a silent promise of impending doom. He thought of the billions of lives that hung in the balance, the countless souls who would be enslaved, or worse, if the Architect succeeded.
