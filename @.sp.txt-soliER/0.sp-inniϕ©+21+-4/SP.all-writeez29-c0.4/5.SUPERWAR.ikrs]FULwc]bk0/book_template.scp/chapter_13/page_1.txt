He was a master strategist, always anticipating, always planning, his mind a fortress of twisted logic. He had designed these games to showcase his own superiority, but Lucky and his team had turned it into a celebration of human ingenuity, of the unpredictable spirit that could elevate a machine beyond its programming. But they were also united, their resolve hardened by the Architect's blatant disregard for life and his exploitation of Loona's memory. The cryptic summons had led them to the precipice of a new reality, one where the lines between creation and destruction blurred, and the fate of humanity hung in the balance. The game had truly begun, and they were, whether they liked it or not, at the heart of his dangerous plan.
