"Alright, Anglers, you heard the doctor!" Jax's voice cut through the
chaos, a rallying cry for the weary team. "Form a circle
around the Heartstone! We're going to give this 'Darkness' a taste
of its own medicine!" The team didn't hesitate. They fought
their way back to the village center, a tight, disciplined unit
moving with a single purpose. They formed a protective ring
around the corrupted crystal, their backs to each other, facing the
oncoming horde. The creatures, sensing a shift in the battle, redoubled
their assault, a frenzied wave of black claws and gnashing teeth.

"We can't hold them off for long!" Hana yelled, her pulse
rifle glowing red-hot. "Whatever you're going to do, do it
now!" Jax took a deep breath, his gaze meeting the eyes
of his teammates around the circle. He saw fear, yes, but
he also saw courage, determination, and an unbreakable bond of camaraderie.
"Focus your energy," he commanded, his voice resonating with a power
that seemed to come from the very core of his being.
"Channel the light of your anglers. Let it flow, let it
merge, let it become one." One by one, the Anglers closed
their eyes, their minds turning inward. They reached deep within themselves,
touching the source of their unique powers, the inner light that
made them who they were.

Jax's angler, the steady beacon of leadership, pulsed with a calm,
golden light. Kael's, the sharp beam of strategy, shone with a
brilliant, analytical silver. Zara's, the vibrant antenna of communication, radiated
a warm, empathetic violet. Jack's, the practical horn of mechanics, glowed
with a solid, dependable orange. Luna's, the crystalline structure of science,
emitted a cool, inquisitive blue. Hana's, the protective shield of security,
shimmered with a fierce, unwavering green. And Kato's, the dark orb
of espionage, surprisingly, began to glow with a soft, pearlescent white,
the hidden light of a spy who fought for the greater good.

The individual lights began to weave together, a tapestry of color
and energy, a symphony of the soul. They flowed towards the
center of the circle, merging into a single, blindingly brilliant beam
of pure, white light. The beam struck the Heartstone, and for
a moment, there was only silence, a deafening, absolute stillness. The
horde of creatures froze, their mindless rage replaced by a sudden,
instinctive fear. The 'Darkness' within the Heartstone recoiled, a wounded
beast facing a power it could not comprehend, a light it
could not extinguish.
