The alien’s message was simple, and profound. They did not offer an answer, but a new way of looking at the question. They spoke of a third path, a path that was not of silence, or of revelation, but of a quiet, persistent, and ultimately irresistible resistance. They would not expose the Sol Pen, but they would fight them. They would not start a war, but they would wage one. A secret war, a war of whispers and shadows, a war fought not with fleets and armies, but with hope, and courage, and a single, defiant song.

The crew of the ‘Straylight,’ their purpose renewed, their family reunited, set a new course. They were no longer just a freighter, but a ghost ship, a phantom, a rumor on the solar winds. They were the secret soldiers in a secret war, the silent guardians of a fragile peace. They were the Unlit’s unlit, the darkness that fought the darkness, the shadow that hunted the shadows.
