The crew of the ‘Straylight’ were not heroes. They were not soldiers. They were just a group of people trying to make their way in a messy, beautiful, and dangerous universe. But they were also the children of the revolution, and they would not stand by and watch as the galaxy they loved was plunged back into the darkness. They would not let the ghosts of the past destroy the future they were all fighting so hard to create.

They began to investigate, to follow the trail of whispers and rumors that the Sol Pen had left behind. Their journey took them to the darkest corners of the unlit void, to forgotten worlds and dangerous, lawless places. They were hunted by pirates, and bounty hunters, and the remnants of Vorith’s fallen empire. They were betrayed, and beaten, and left for dead. But they never gave up.

They were a small, insignificant ship in a vast, uncaring universe. But they were also a spark of light in the darkness, a single, defiant note of hope in a symphony of despair. They were the Straylight, and their story was just beginning.
