Their investigation led them to a shocking discovery. The Sol Pen were not just a ghost from a forgotten war. They were a vast, shadowy organization that had been operating in the unlit void for millennia, their influence extending to every corner of the galaxy. They were the secret puppet masters behind a thousand different conflicts, a thousand different wars, a thousand different acts of terror. They were the architects of the very chaos that the Unlit Tribe had fought so hard to embrace.

The crew of the ‘Straylight’ had stumbled upon a conspiracy that was so vast, so ancient, so deeply ingrained in the very fabric of the galaxy, that to expose it would be to shatter the fragile peace that the Unlit Tribe had fought so hard to create. They were faced with an impossible choice: to reveal the truth and risk plunging the galaxy into a new, more terrible war, or to keep the secret and allow the Sol Pen to continue their reign of terror, their silent, unseen manipulation of a thousand different worlds.
