The galaxy was stunned into silence. The Unlit Tribe, the loose coalition of worlds and individuals who had fought so hard for their messy, beautiful, and necessary freedom, were now faced with a new, and terrifying, choice. They could unite, they could fight, they could risk everything they had built for a chance at a true, lasting peace. Or they could retreat, they could hide, they could hope that the storm would pass them by.

The choice was not an easy one. The galaxy was a messy, beautiful, and chaotic place, and the Unlit Tribe was not a monolith. There were those who argued for war, for a final, decisive battle against the forces of darkness. And there were those who argued for peace, for a quiet, patient resistance, for a belief in the slow, inevitable triumph of light over darkness.
