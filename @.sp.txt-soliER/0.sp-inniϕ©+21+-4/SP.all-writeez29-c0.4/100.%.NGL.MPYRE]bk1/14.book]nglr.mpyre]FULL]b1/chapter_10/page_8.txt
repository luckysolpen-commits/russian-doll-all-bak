The Sol Pen, their secrets exposed, their power shattered, were faced with an enemy they could not defeat. They could not conquer a galaxy that refused to be conquered. They could not control a people who refused to be controlled. They could not silence a song that was being sung by a billion different voices, in a thousand different languages.

They retreated into the unlit void, their empire in ruins, their power broken, their beautiful, orderly, and ultimately lifeless ideology shattered by the messy, beautiful, and necessary chaos of a universe that would not be tamed.

The war was over. The galaxy was free. And the crew of the ‘Straylight,’ their mission complete, their purpose fulfilled, flew on, a small, insignificant ship in a vast, beautiful, and wonderfully, gloriously, and eternally messy universe.
