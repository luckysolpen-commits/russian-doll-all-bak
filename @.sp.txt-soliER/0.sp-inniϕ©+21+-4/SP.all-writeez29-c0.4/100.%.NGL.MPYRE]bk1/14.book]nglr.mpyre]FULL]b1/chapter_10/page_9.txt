But the universe is a story that is always being written, a song that is always being sung. And the most beautiful, most interesting, and most necessary notes are the ones that are yet to come. The Sol Pen were defeated, but they were not destroyed. The galaxy was free, but it was not at peace. The war was over, but the struggle, the messy, beautiful, and necessary struggle for a better, more interesting, and ultimately more honest universe, would never end.

And the ‘Straylight,’ a small, insignificant ship crewed by a band of misfits and outcasts, would be there for all of it. They were the children of the revolution, the guardians of a fragile peace, the secret soldiers in a secret war that was no longer a secret. They were the Unlit’s unlit, the darkness that fought the darkness, the shadow that hunted the shadows. They were the Straylight, and their story was just beginning.
