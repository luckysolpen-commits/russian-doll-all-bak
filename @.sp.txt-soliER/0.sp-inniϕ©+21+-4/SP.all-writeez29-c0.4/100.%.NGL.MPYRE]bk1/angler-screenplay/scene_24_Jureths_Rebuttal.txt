INT. UNLIT TRIBE CAMP - JURETH'S SANCTUM - DAY

Years have passed. The Unlit Tribe has grown, but so has the insidious pressure from the Empire. JURETH (now appearing as an aged sage, his horn glowing with a deep, steady, almost ancient light) sits in a quiet alcove carved into the rift-stone. Around him, younger members of the tribe, including a now-older NAETH, listen intently.

Jureth is not writing with a stylus, but with his mind, projecting his thoughts directly onto a shimmering, ethereal scroll of light that floats before him. The words coalesce, forming chapters and verses. This is "THE BOOK OF JURETH: THE UNLIT PATH."

JURETH
(His voice calm, resonant, filled with profound wisdom)
Vorith speaks of subtle dominion. Of chains woven in peace. He seeks to blind us with his lanterns, to hide the Shell, to deny us its pearls. But I say unto you: The Empire’s light is a lie. Our light comes from within. It cannot be hidden or stolen.

He gestures, and the ethereal scroll displays a passage:

JURETH (V.O.)
"The Empire seeks to blind us with their lanterns, but our light comes from within. It cannot be hidden or stolen. Art is our lantern, leadership our guide, and the unlit void our canvas. We paint our destiny with every thought. Fear not the hidden shell, for the pearl of wisdom lies in every heart. Seek it, and you shall find."

A young tribe member, who had been looking despondent, looks up, a spark of understanding in their eyes.

NAETH
(Her horn glowing with renewed hope)
He says we are sterile. That our soil will yield no seeds.

JURETH
(A gentle smile)
Sterility is a lie. The mind is ever fertile, the spirit ever growing. Vorith seeks to starve us of wealth, of love, of thought. But wealth is not in gold, but in the richness of ideas. Love is not in flesh, but in the bonds we forge. Thought is our greatest treasure, and it multiplies with sharing. The Empire cannot starve what they do not understand.

The scroll shifts to another passage:

JURETH (V.O.)
"Sterility is a lie. The mind is ever fertile, the spirit ever growing. Wealth is not in gold, but in the richness of ideas. Love is not in flesh, but in the bonds we forge. Thought is our greatest treasure, and it multiplies with sharing. The Empire cannot starve what they do not understand."

ZORATH and MIRAH, now older, sit nearby, their faces filled with pride and renewed strength.

ZORATH
His words… they cut through the propaganda like a spright-saber.

MIRAH
He gives us strength. He reminds us who we are.

JURETH
(Continuing, his voice gaining power)
Vorith speaks of division. Of splintered realities. He seeks to lose us in a maze of our own making. But division is the Empire’s tool, and unity is our shield. Stand together, and no illusion can break us. The multiverse is but a trick of the light. Our reality is one, forged in the unlit void.

The scroll displays the final passage:

JURETH (V.O.)
"Division is the Empire’s tool, but unity is our shield. Stand together, and no illusion can break us. The multiverse is but a trick of the light. Our reality is one, forged in the unlit void. Let the Empire wander in their maze; we walk the straight path of truth."

The tribe members, their horns now glowing with a collective, vibrant light, nod in agreement. The doubts that Vorith’s propaganda had sown begin to dissipate, replaced by a renewed sense of purpose and unity.

Jureth closes his eyes, the ethereal scroll fading. He looks at the faces of his tribe, his family. His legacy is not just in his words, but in their unwavering spirit.

FADE OUT.
