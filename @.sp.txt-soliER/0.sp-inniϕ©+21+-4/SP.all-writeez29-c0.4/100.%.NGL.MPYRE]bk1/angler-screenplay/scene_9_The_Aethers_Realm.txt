EXT. AETHERS' REALM - LANDING ZONE - DAY

The XVS shuttle descends through a sky of swirling, iridescent mists onto a world of breathtaking, almost impossible beauty. Floating islands of crystalline rock drift lazily above oceans of liquid light. Ethereal flora glows with soft, shifting colors, and the air is filled with a gentle, melodic hum.

Jax, Kael, Zara, Jack, Luna, Hana, and Kato disembark. The serene atmosphere is palpable, almost overwhelming.

ZARA
(Her horn glowing with a soft, receptive light)
It’s… peaceful. Almost too peaceful. The energy readings are incredibly stable, yet… there’s a dissonance. A subtle undercurrent of… unreality.

LUNA
(Scanning, her scientific horn emitting gentle pulses)
No overt corruption. No parasitic entities. No malicious frequencies. The biological and environmental data are pristine. Yet, the official report stated "Dreams."

As they walk, they encounter the AETHERS. They are beings of pure light, shimmering and translucent, their forms constantly shifting like living nebulae. They float gracefully, their expressions serene, but their eyes are unfocused, distant. Some are murmuring to themselves, their voices soft and melodic, but the words are nonsensical, disjointed.

JACK
(Watching an Aether try to catch a beam of sunlight with its bare hands)
They look… happy. But they’re not really here, are they?

HANA
(Her security horn scanning for threats, finding none)
No physical threat. But their minds… they’re somewhere else.

Jax turns to Zara.

JAX
Zara. This is your domain. Can you reach them? Understand what these "Dreams" are?

Zara nods, her horn beginning to pulse with a complex array of empathetic frequencies. She approaches an Aether, extending a hand, her horn mirroring the Aether’s shifting colors.

ZARA
(Softly, her voice resonating with compassion)
Greetings. We are travelers. We sense… a journey within you. Can you share your path?

The Aether blinks slowly, its form shimmering. It begins to project images directly into Zara’s mind: vivid, beautiful landscapes, impossible creatures, moments of intense joy and profound sorrow. But they are disjointed, shifting rapidly, without logic or narrative.

ZARA (CONT'D)
(Wincing, pulling back slightly, her horn flickering)
They’re… overwhelming. Not just images, but emotions. Memories. They’re living in these dreams. They’re so real to them.

KAEL
(Observing Zara’s reaction)
Manipulative, as the report stated. Not malicious in intent, perhaps, but certainly controlling. They’re trapped in their own minds.

KATO
(Quietly, his horn almost invisible, observing the Aethers)
They are not trapped. They are content. The dreams are their reality. To break them free… would be to shatter their world.

Luna approaches, her scientific horn analyzing the Aether’s projected thoughts.

LUNA
He’s right. Their neural pathways have rewired to prioritize these dream states. It’s a form of self-induced coma, but one they perceive as blissful. Any attempt to forcibly remove them could cause severe psychological trauma. Or worse.

Jax looks at the serene, dreaming Aethers, then at his team. This is a different kind of battle. One that requires understanding, not force.

JAX
(To Zara)
Then we need to understand the dreams. Find the thread that binds them. The key to waking them, without destroying them.

Zara looks at the Aether, then back at Jax, a new resolve in her eyes. Her horn glows with a steady, determined light.

FADE OUT.
