INT. NEUTRINO PRIME - XVS SHUTTLE - DAY

The Anglers are back in their shuttle, preparing for departure. The mood is a mix of relief and weariness. Neutrino Prime, visible through the viewport, now glows with renewed vibrancy.

ZARA
(Smiling, watching the planet)
They’re recovering. The bioluminescence is stronger than before. It’s beautiful.

LUNA
(Checking her readings)
The parasitic energy is completely gone. Their cellular structures are regenerating at an accelerated rate. Remarkable.

JACK
(Wiping grease from his hands)
Another day, another save. Who knew being a cosmic plumber would be so… fulfilling?

HANA
(A rare, soft smile)
Fulfilling, perhaps. But never easy.

Kael, ever the strategist, is already reviewing data on a holographic display.

KAEL
(Frowning)
Not entirely easy, Chief. Our intervention here seems to have… accelerated a pre-existing conflict.

Jax turns to him, a questioning look on his face.

JAX
What do you mean, Lieutenant?

KAEL
(Tapping the display, showing new data streams)
While we were dealing with the Darkness, our long-range sensors picked up increased activity. The Neutrinos, now free from the entity’s influence, are mobilizing. Against the sinqid-fishpeople. A long-standing territorial dispute, apparently. Our presence seems to have tipped the scales.

LUNA
(Concerned)
So, we cured one ailment, only to exacerbate another?

Suddenly, the shuttle’s comms crackle to life. VORITH PRIME’s authoritative voice fills the cabin.

VORITH PRIME (O.S.)
Anglers. Report. Mission status on Neutrino Prime.

JAX
(Activating the comms)
Mission successful, sir. The parasitic entity has been neutralized. Neutrino Prime is recovering.

VORITH PRIME (O.S.)
Excellent. As expected. However, new intelligence has emerged. A significant energy signature, previously masked, has just flared on a distant world. Coordinates incoming. You are to proceed immediately.

Jax’s brow furrows. No time to rest. No time to process.

JAX
(Hesitantly)
Sir, with respect, the situation here on Neutrino Prime is… evolving. A conflict with the sinqid-fishpeople is brewing.

VORITH PRIME (O.S.)
(Voice hardening)
That is a local matter, Commander. Your mandate is multiversal. This new signature is of far greater concern. It indicates a widespread systemic corruption, unlike anything we’ve encountered. The Hydroverse requires your immediate attention elsewhere.

The new coordinates appear on Kael’s display. The planet is a swirling mass of chaotic energy.

KAEL
(Reading the data)
Energy readings are… off the charts. Pure chaos. This isn’t just a localized issue, Commander. This is… profound.

Jax looks at his team. The initial relief has vanished, replaced by a grim understanding. The weight of their role, as cosmic peacekeepers, is heavier than ever.

JAX
(To Vorith Prime, with a sigh of resignation)
Understood, sir. We are en route.

He cuts the communication. The shuttle’s engines hum to life, pulling away from the now-vibrant Neutrino Prime, leaving its brewing conflict behind.

FADE OUT.
