INT. IMPERIAL OBSERVATORY - DAY

A vast, dimly lit chamber filled with humming machinery and holographic displays. Star charts and energy signatures flicker across the walls. VORITH (30s), a rising, ambitious officer in the Angler Empire, stands before a massive central console. His horn glows with a cold, calculating light, sharper and more intense than Vorith Prime’s. He is a descendant of Vorith Prime, embodying the Empire’s rigid adherence to order.

A junior officer, ENSIGN THANE (20s), nervously approaches Vorith.

THANE
Sir, the latest reports from the XVS Angler team. Mission success on Apex, but… anomalies in their internal communications.

Vorith turns, his gaze piercing.

VORITH
Anomalies? Elaborate, Ensign.

THANE
(Swallowing hard)
Subtle shifts in their neural signatures. Particularly from Angler Jureth. His horn’s energy output is… unusual. Highly resonant. And there are… philosophical discussions. About the nature of the Empire’s light. The “unlit void.”

Vorith’s horn pulses, a flicker of irritation. He gestures to a holographic display, and Jureth’s neural signature appears, a chaotic, vibrant swirl amidst the more orderly patterns of the other Anglers.

VORITH
(Voice low, dangerous)
The unlit void. A dangerous concept. The Empire’s light is order. It is truth. Any deviation is… chaos.

THANE
He’s also been… writing, sir. A personal log. It seems to be evolving into a treatise. He calls it “The Book of Jureth.”

Vorith’s eyes narrow. He accesses Jureth’s log. Excerpts of Jureth’s writings appear on the display:

JURETH (V.O.)
The Empire’s light… it is a filter. It shows us only what it deems safe. But beyond the light, there is a vastness. A truth that embraces both shadow and light.

Vorith scoffs.

VORITH
(To himself)
Naive. The void is not truth. It is emptiness. A vacuum that must be filled with order.

He scrolls through more of Jureth’s entries, his horn pulsing with growing suspicion. He sees references to the “Darkness” on Neutrino Prime, the “Madness” on Xerxes, the “Dreams” on Aethers – not as external threats, but as reflections of the Empire’s own suppressed truths.

VORITH (CONT'D)
(To Thane)
And the others? Are they susceptible to this… philosophy?

THANE
Commander Jax maintains his usual stoicism, sir. Lieutenant Kael’s strategic patterns remain consistent. But Dr. Luna’s scientific curiosity seems… piqued. And Agent Kato’s signature is, as always, unreadable.

Vorith’s gaze sharpens. He knows Kato’s reputation.

VORITH
(To himself, a dangerous glint in his eye)
Unreadable. Or complicit.

He turns back to the main console, his horn glowing with renewed purpose.

VORITH (CONT'D)
Continue to monitor them. Every neural flicker. Every communication. I want to know who else is being swayed by this… “unlit path.” This dissent will not be tolerated. The Hydroverse requires absolute order. And I will ensure it.

Thane nods, a shiver running down his spine. Vorith’s ambition is palpable, a cold, relentless force.

FADE OUT.
