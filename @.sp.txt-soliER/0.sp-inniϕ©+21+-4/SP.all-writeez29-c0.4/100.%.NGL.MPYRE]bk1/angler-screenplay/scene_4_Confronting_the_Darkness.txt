INT. NEUTRINO PRIME - DEEP CAVERN - DAY

The Anglers descend into a vast, echoing cavern, the air thick with an oppressive, chilling presence. The bioluminescence here is almost entirely extinguished, replaced by an inky blackness that seems to absorb all light. The few Neutrinos present are completely inert, their forms barely visible.

LUNA
(Voice hushed, scanning)
The energy signature is immense here. It’s not just a drain; it’s a parasitic entity. Feeding on their life force, their very essence.

Suddenly, the darkness coalesces. A swirling, amorphous mass of shadow rises from the cavern floor, tendrils of inky blackness reaching out. This is the "DARKNESS."

JAX
(Drawing his spright-saber, its blade glowing fiercely)
Form up! Hana, perimeter! Jack, any weaknesses?

HANA
(Activating a shimmering energy shield, positioning herself to cover the team)
On it! Nothing gets past me!

Jack quickly scans the cavern walls, his horn pulsing with diagnostic light.

JACK
(Pointing to a series of crystalline conduits running along the cavern ceiling)
Those conduits! They’re channeling the planet’s core energy. This thing’s tapping into it, amplifying itself!

KAEL
(Eyes darting, formulating a plan)
If we can disrupt its energy source… Luna, can you overload those conduits? Jack, can you reroute the flow?

LUNA
(Already moving towards a conduit, her horn glowing with scientific intensity)
I can attempt to reverse the polarity, but it’ll require precise calibration. One wrong move, and we could destabilize the entire cavern!

JACK
(Grinning, already pulling out a multi-tool)
Precise calibration is my middle name, Doc! Just tell me where to plug in!

The Darkness lunges, a massive tendril of shadow lashing out at the Anglers. Jax meets it head-on, his spright-saber clashing against the shadowy mass, pushing it back with sheer force.

JAX
(Straining)
Keep it busy! Hana, cover Luna and Jack!

Hana’s shield flares, deflecting smaller tendrils that try to bypass Jax. Kato, silent and unseen, moves like a ghost, distracting the Darkness with quick, precise strikes, drawing its attention away from Luna and Jack.

Luna works feverishly at the conduit, her horn glowing brighter and brighter. Jack, with incredible speed, connects his multi-tool to a series of intricate mechanisms, sparks flying.

LUNA
(Sweat beading on her brow)
Almost there! Jack, prepare for the surge!

JACK
(Gritting his teeth)
Ready when you are, Doc!

With a final, powerful surge, Luna activates the conduit. A blinding flash of pure energy erupts from the ceiling, coursing through the conduits and directly into the swirling Darkness. The entity shudders, its form flickering violently.

The Darkness lets out a guttural, non-verbal SCREAM, a sound of pure agony. It begins to unravel, its tendrils retracting, its oppressive presence diminishing.

JAX
(Pressing the attack, driving it back)
Now! Push it back!

The Anglers, invigorated, unleash a coordinated assault. Jax’s saber slashes, Kael’s strategic strikes find weak points, Hana’s shield pushes, Jack’s tools emit disruptive pulses, Luna’s horn emits a cleansing light, and Kato’s unseen blows hasten its demise.

The Darkness shrinks, collapsing in on itself, until it is nothing more than a wisp of smoke that dissipates into the air. The cavern slowly begins to regain its bioluminescence, a faint, hopeful glow returning to the walls. The inert Neutrinos stir, their lights flickering back to life.

LUNA
(Exhausted but triumphant)
It’s gone. The balance… it’s restored.

Jax looks at the reviving Neutrinos, a weary smile on his face. The mission is complete.

FADE OUT.
