INT. IMPERIAL OBSERVATORY - DAY

Vorith, now a high-ranking official, stands before a vast holographic display, his horn glowing with cold, calculating intensity. The display shows the growing energy signature of the Unlit Tribe by the rift, a chaotic, vibrant anomaly in the Empire’s meticulously ordered Hydroverse.

ENSIGN THANE stands beside him, looking nervous.

THANE
Sir, the Unlit Tribe’s presence is… expanding. Their energy signature is becoming more defined. They’re attracting others.

VORITH
(A sneer playing on his lips)
A minor infestation. A philosophical rash. They believe their “unlit void” is a path to freedom. They are mistaken. True freedom lies in absolute order. In the absence of choice.

He gestures, and a new holographic projection appears: a beautifully bound, ancient-looking tome titled "The Art of Subtle Dominion."

VORITH (CONT'D)
I have authored a new treatise. A declaration of war, disguised as a philosophical discourse. It will be disseminated throughout the Hydroverse. To every Angler. Every citizen. Every potential sympathizer.

Thane reads the projected excerpts, his eyes widening in alarm.

THANE
“To conquer without battle is the supreme art. Let the enemy believe in peace while you weave their chains.” Sir… this is… insidious.

VORITH
(Smiling, a chilling, humorless expression)
Precisely. Why waste resources on open conflict when you can dismantle an enemy from within? Their “unlit path” is a path to sterility. Sexual, financial, intellectual.

He gestures to another section of the display, showing complex algorithms and data streams.

VORITH (CONT'D)
We will implement new trade policies, masked as economic cooperation, that will subtly restrict their resources. We will introduce cultural exchanges that sow doubt about their traditions. We will spread whispers, rumors, pitting their own leaders against each other.

THANE
But… the Shell, sir? The pearls of wisdom they seek?

VORITH
(His horn glowing with triumph)
The Shell will remain hidden. Its pearls, locked away. Ignorance is a powerful shackle. We will control their access to wisdom, and thus, control their destiny. A tribe without growth is a tribe doomed.

He gestures to a final projection: a fragmented, chaotic image of the Hydroverse, worlds splintered and isolated.

VORITH (CONT'D)
And if all else fails, we will splinter their reality. Create illusions of choice, paths that lead nowhere. In the multiverse of the mind, they shall be lost, each to their own delusion.

Thane shudders. This is a war unlike any he has ever known. A war for the very soul of the Hydroverse.

THANE
(Voice barely a whisper)
This is… a war without end, sir.

Vorith turns, his cold gaze fixed on Thane.

VORITH
(A chilling smile)
Only for those who resist. For those who embrace order, it is eternal peace. The Unlit Tribe will learn. One way or another.

FADE OUT.
