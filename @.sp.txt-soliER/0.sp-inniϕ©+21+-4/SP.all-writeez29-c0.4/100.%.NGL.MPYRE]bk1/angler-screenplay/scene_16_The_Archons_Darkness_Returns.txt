INT. XVS SHUTTLE - BRIEFING ROOM - DAY

The Anglers are gathered, a palpable tension in the air. A holographic projection of a familiar, yet subtly altered, Neutrino Prime hangs in the center of the room. Its bioluminescence is muted, flickering with an unhealthy, almost sickly glow.

JAX
(His voice grim)
The Archons. A new report indicates a resurgence of the parasitic entity we encountered on Neutrino Prime. But this time, it’s different. More widespread. More… intelligent.

LUNA
(Scanning the holographic data, her scientific horn pulsing with concern)
The energy signature is identical to the original “Darkness,” but amplified. It’s not just draining life force; it’s actively manipulating neural pathways. Inducing fear. Doubt. Paranoia.

KAEL
(His strategic horn flickering rapidly)
It’s learning. Adapting. And the reports indicate it’s targeting specific individuals. Amplifying their deepest insecurities.

ZARA
(Her horn dimming, a flicker of unease in her eyes)
So, it’s not just a physical threat. It’s a psychological one.

Hana tightens her grip on her spright-saber, her security horn scanning the room, as if expecting the Darkness to manifest there.

HANA
We faced it before. We can face it again.

JAX
(Looking at each of his team members)
This time, it’s personal. The reports indicate it preys on the mind. It will seek out your fears. Your doubts. Your weaknesses.

Jack shifts uncomfortably, his horn’s steady pulse faltering slightly. He thinks of his occasional self-doubt, his fear of failure.

LUNA
(Her horn’s light wavering)
It could exploit our past experiences. The cost of control on Nexus. The painful truth on Apex. It could twist our successes into failures.

Zara’s horn dims further. She remembers the Aethers, the burden of delivering hard truths. What if her communication, her empathy, could be turned against her?

Kato, usually impassive, is subtly more withdrawn. His horn, almost invisible, seems to absorb the ambient light, as if trying to hide from an unseen threat. He thinks of the secrets he carries, the information he’s withheld.

Jax feels a familiar weight in his chest. The burden of leadership. The fear of making the wrong decision. His horn, usually a beacon of unwavering resolve, flickers with a momentary uncertainty.

JURETH (O.S.)
(From the corner of the room, his voice quiet but clear)
It’s not just fear. It’s the unlit void. It’s what the Empire tries to suppress. The doubts they don’t want us to acknowledge.

The Anglers turn to Jureth, who has been listening intently. His horn, which had been flickering erratically since his visions, now glows with a strange, almost defiant light.

JURETH (CONT'D)
The Darkness… it’s a reflection. A mirror of what we deny within ourselves. The Empire’s narrative of perfect order… it creates these shadows.

Jax looks at Jureth, then back at the holographic projection of the corrupted Neutrino Prime. The young Angler’s words resonate with the unease they’ve all been feeling.

JAX
(His voice low, acknowledging the truth in Jureth’s words)
Then we face it. Not just with our weapons, but with our truths. We confront our own shadows.

The Anglers look at each other, a new understanding passing between them. This mission will be different. It will be a battle not just for a world, but for their very souls.

FADE OUT.
