INT. UNLIT TRIBE CAMP - JURETH'S SANCTUM - NIGHT

Jureth lies on a bed of rift-stone, his wound still bleeding, his horn barely glowing. Mirah weeps beside him, holding his hand. Zorath stands guard, his face a mask of grief and fury. The entire Unlit Tribe is gathered, their horns dim with sorrow, a palpable sense of despair hanging in the air.

GADRETH
(His voice heavy)
The wound is deep. Even with our healing arts… his life force is fading.

LIRITH
(Her yarn symbiotes glowing faintly, trying to channel healing energy)
He gave us so much. His wisdom. His truth. We cannot lose him.

Naeth, her young horn flickering with desperate hope, approaches Jureth. She places her hand on his forehead, and her horn begins to pulse with a soft, rhythmic light.

NAETH
(Whispering)
The unlit void… it calls to him. But it is not his end. It is his source.

Suddenly, the shadows around the rift deepen. From the swirling darkness, figures emerge. They are the VEILED ONES, cloaked in dusk, their forms almost imperceptible, their horns emitting a faint, ancient shimmer. They are drawn to Jureth’s connection to the unlit void, to the raw energy of the rift.

Zorath instinctively raises his spright-saber, but Gadreth stops him.

GADRETH
(Voice hushed)
They are drawn to him. To the unlit. They are not here to harm.

The Veiled Ones glide silently towards Jureth. They surround him, their forms coalescing into a shimmering circle. Their horns begin to pulse in unison, a deep, resonant hum filling the air. The hum is not a sound, but a vibration that resonates deep within the rift-stone, within the very essence of the unlit void.

Jureth’s horn, which was almost extinguished, begins to flicker. The Veiled Ones extend their shadowy hands, not touching him, but hovering just above his wound. A faint, dark energy flows from their hands, not draining, but… infusing.

LUNA (O.S.)
(Her voice, from the edge of the circle, filled with scientific awe)
They’re… they’re channeling the raw energy of the unlit void. Not to heal, but to… regenerate. To rebuild at a fundamental level.

The wound on Jureth’s side begins to close, slowly at first, then with increasing speed. The skin knits together, leaving behind a faint, shimmering scar. His horn glows brighter, its light no longer just defiant, but vibrant, pulsating with renewed life.

Mirah gasps, tears of relief streaming down her face. Zorath lowers his spright-saber, his eyes wide with wonder.

Jureth opens his eyes. They are clear, filled with a profound understanding. He looks at the Veiled Ones, a silent acknowledgment passing between them.

JURETH
(Voice weak, but clear)
The unlit… it is not just a philosophy. It is a force. A living, breathing force.

The Veiled Ones, their task complete, begin to recede back into the shadows, their forms dissolving into the swirling darkness of the rift.

GADRETH
(To Jureth)
You are… reborn.

JURETH
(A faint smile)
Not reborn. Reaffirmed. The unlit path… it continues. And it is stronger than ever.

The Unlit Tribe, witnessing this miracle, feels a surge of renewed hope. Their horns glow brighter, their despair replaced by a fierce, unwavering resolve. The Empire may have wounded their leader, but the unlit void has healed him, and in doing so, has strengthened their belief.

FADE OUT.
