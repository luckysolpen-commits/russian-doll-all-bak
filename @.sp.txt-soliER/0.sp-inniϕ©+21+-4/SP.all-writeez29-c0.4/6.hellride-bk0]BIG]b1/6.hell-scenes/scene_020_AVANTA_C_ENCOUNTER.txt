INT. AVANTA.C - VIRTUAL WORLD - DAY

Lucky's exploration leads him to a less frequented sector of Avanta.C, a digital slum of sorts, where data streams are sluggish and the architecture is glitching. Here, the avatars are less polished, their forms often distorted or incomplete, reflecting their real-world struggles.

He observes a small group of these disheveled avatars huddled around a flickering holographic display. They are watching a news feed, but the images are heavily pixelated, the audio distorted. He catches snippets of words: "Empire... control... resistance..."

Suddenly, one of the avatars, a wiry figure with a perpetually twitching eye, notices Lucky. Their avatar's form flickers, revealing glimpses of raw code beneath the surface.

TWITCH (AVATAR)
(Voice a series of crackles and pops)
New here, aren't you? Don't look so shiny for long. Not in this part of the net.

Lucky approaches cautiously. He senses a raw energy from Twitch, a desperate defiance.

LUCKY (AVATAR)
I'm looking for information. About the Empire. About what's really going on here.

Twitch's eye twitches more rapidly. They glance around, as if expecting to be overheard, even in this digital backwater.

TWITCH (AVATAR)
Information costs. But some information... it's worth more than credits. It's worth freedom.

Twitch extends a digital hand, a gesture of invitation, or perhaps a challenge. Lucky looks at the flickering news feed, then at Twitch. He knows this is the beginning of something new, something dangerous.

FADE OUT.
