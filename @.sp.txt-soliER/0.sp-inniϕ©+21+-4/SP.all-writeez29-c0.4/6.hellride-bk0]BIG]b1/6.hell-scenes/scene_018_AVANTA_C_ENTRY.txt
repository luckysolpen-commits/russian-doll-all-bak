INT. AVANTA.C - VIRTUAL WORLD - DAY

Lucky, now embodied in his chosen AVATAR, stands on a shimmering, crystalline plaza. The virtual world of AVANTA.C stretches before him, a breathtaking panorama of impossible architecture and vibrant, pulsating energy.

Skyscrapers made of pure light pierce a sky that shifts through a spectrum of colors. Rivers of data flow through transparent conduits, connecting floating islands of information. Other AVATARS, in a myriad of forms, move through the space, some interacting, some simply observing.

Lucky takes a tentative step, testing the feel of his new digital body. It feels fluid, powerful. He can sense the vastness of the network, the endless possibilities within this virtual realm.

He sees marketplaces where digital goods are traded, forums where information is exchanged, and hidden corners where illicit activities undoubtedly take place. This is a new frontier, a world built on code and dreams.

He takes a deep breath, a purely simulated sensation, and a sense of exhilaration washes over him. He has escaped the physical wasteland of Earth, and now, in Avanta.C, he has a chance to truly begin again.
