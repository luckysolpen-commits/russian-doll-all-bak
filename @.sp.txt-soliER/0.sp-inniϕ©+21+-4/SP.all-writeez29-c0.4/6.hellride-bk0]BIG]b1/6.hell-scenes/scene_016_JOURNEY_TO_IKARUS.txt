INT. BLUE BIRD'S SHIP - SPACE

LUCKY's eyes flutter open. He's no longer in the suffocating darkness of the Caramelle nest. He lies on a comfortable cot within a sleek, minimalist spaceship. The gentle hum of engines vibrates through the deck.

He sits up, his body still aching but the crushing weakness gone. He looks around. The BLUE BIRD BOUNTY HUNTER is at the helm, her back to him, her silhouette framed by the vast, star-dusted expanse of space visible through the panoramic viewport.

BLUE BIRD
(Without turning)
Awake, are we? Good. Wouldn't want you missing the view.

Lucky approaches the viewport. Below them, Earth is a distant, swirling marble, scarred by the robot wars. Ahead, a shimmering, artificial light grows larger – ICARUS, the Ellison Island of Earth refugees.

BLUE BIRD
(Cont.)
Ellison Island. Where the desperate and the hopeful gather. Where those who can't afford the official channels try their luck. Viruses, hacking, EMPRIE patrols... it's a gamble.

Lucky watches as the ship expertly navigates a treacherous ASTEROID FIELD, dodging massive chunks of rock with practiced ease. The Blue Bird's movements are fluid, precise.

BLUE BIRD
(Cont.)
They're always watching. The EMPRIE. Looking for illegals. We'll have to be careful. Blend in.

The ship descends, piercing the hazy atmosphere of Ikarus. Below, a sprawling, makeshift city comes into view – refugee camps, cobbled-together habitats, a testament to humanity's resilience.

INT. REFUGEE CAMP - DAY

The ship lands on the outskirts of a bustling refugee camp. The air is thick with the smells of smoke, cooking, and desperation. A cacophony of languages, haggling, and distant music fills the air.

Lucky follows the Blue Bird through the crowded, winding streets. They move like shadows, avoiding direct eye contact, blending into the chaotic flow of humanity. Makeshift stalls line the paths, selling everything from scavenged tech to questionable food.

They reach a secluded, grimy alleyway. Figures lurk in the shadows, their faces obscured. This is the black market, the true heart of Ellison Island.

BLUE BIRD
(Low, urgent)
Your contact.

She gestures to a figure, cloaked and hooded, who steps forward. The contact's hand extends, revealing a small, glowing DATA CRYSTAL.

BLUE BIRD
(Cont.)
This contains your authorization code. An unauthorized black market bot account. Your ticket to Avanta.C.

Lucky takes the crystal, his heart pounding. This is it. His chance to leave Earth, to start anew. He looks at the crystal, then back at the Blue Bird.

LUCKY
Thank you.

The Blue Bird gives a curt nod, a hint of something unreadable in her eyes. She melts back into the shadows, disappearing as quickly as she appeared.

Lucky stands alone in the alley, the data crystal warm in his hand. The sun dips below the horizon, casting long, distorted shadows. His destination awaits: Avanta.C, the fabled virtual world.

FADE OUT.
