INT. UN HEADQUARTERS - DAY

EDGE DANGERMAN, a man hardened by the new world, stands before a holographic projection of the METAL SUN. He addresses a council of grim-faced UN OFFICIALS.

EDGE DANGERMAN
(Voice laced with urgency)
The Architect is building the Metal Sun at an alarming rate. He plans to launch world police bots that will enslave humanity. We can't let this happen.

A UN OFFICIAL, weary and defeated, shakes his head.

UN OFFICIAL
Our forces are no match for the Icarus' technological might.

Edge turns to LUCKY, who stands silently, observing the projection.

EDGE DANGERMAN
Lucky, we need your help.

Lucky's expression is grim. He knows the odds are stacked against them, but he also knows he has to act.

LUCKY
I'll do what I can.

Edge nods, a hint of gratitude in his eyes.

EDGE DANGERMAN
We have reason to believe that Mars and the Lunar Colony are supporting the Architect's plans. We need your help to stop them.

Lucky's mind races with strategies. He accepts the challenge, knowing he has to act fast before it's too late.

FADE OUT.
