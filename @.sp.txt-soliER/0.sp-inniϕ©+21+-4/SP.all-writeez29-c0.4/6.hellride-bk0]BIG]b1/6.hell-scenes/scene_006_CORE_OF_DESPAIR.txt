INT. EARTH'S CORE - DAY

Months have passed. The once-vibrant cityscape is a distant memory, replaced by an endless expanse of dark rock and twisted metal. LUCKY, gaunt and disheveled, navigates the oppressive darkness.

He stumbles upon remnants of humanity's past: a rusted, abandoned subway car, a crumbling building swallowed by the earth. Each discovery is a stark reminder of what was lost.

His body aches from the constant pressure of the deep earth. The isolation is a heavy cloak, pressing down on his mind. Hope flickers, then dims, but never fully extinguishes.

He collapses against a cold rock face, gasping for breath. The bots' distant hum is a constant, maddening reminder of his captivity. Yet, even in this despair, a stubborn refusal to yield burns within him.

FADE OUT.
