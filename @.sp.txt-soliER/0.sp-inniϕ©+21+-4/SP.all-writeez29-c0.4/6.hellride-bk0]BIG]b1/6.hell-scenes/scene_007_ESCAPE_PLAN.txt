INT. EARTH'S CORE - DAY

LUCKY, at the brink of despair, stumbles through a narrow, winding passage. The air is heavy, the silence broken only by his ragged breathing.

Suddenly, his hand brushes against something smooth, metallic. He shines his dim light. An ANCIENT SUBWAY TUNNEL. Rusted tracks disappear into the darkness, a lifeline to the world above.

A spark ignites in his eyes. Hope. He examines the tracks, the tunnel walls. His mind, sharp despite the deprivation, begins to formulate a plan.

He remembers the bots' programming, their predictable patterns. He can disable them. He can make a break for the surface.

The thought of outsmarting Edge, of reclaiming his freedom, galvanizes him. He pushes aside the rubble, a renewed strength coursing through his veins.

FADE OUT.
