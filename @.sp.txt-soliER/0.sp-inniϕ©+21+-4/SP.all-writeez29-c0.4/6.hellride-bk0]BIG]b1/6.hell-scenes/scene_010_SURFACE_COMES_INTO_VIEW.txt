EXT. EARTH SURFACE - DAY

LUCKY emerges from the mouth of the subway tunnel, blinking against the harsh light. The sky is not the vibrant blue he remembers, but a dull, hazy expanse, choked with pollution. The air is thick with the acrid smell of industry and decay.

The once-familiar cityscape is a skeletal ruin, twisted metal and crumbling concrete stretching as far as the eye can see. The silence is profound, broken only by the distant, metallic clanking of unseen bots.

He takes a deep, ragged breath, the polluted air burning his lungs. But a triumphant grin spreads across his face. He made it. He's out of the core.

He looks up at the hazy sky, a sense of overwhelming freedom washing over him. The world above is a wasteland, but it's *his* wasteland now. He is free.

FADE OUT.
