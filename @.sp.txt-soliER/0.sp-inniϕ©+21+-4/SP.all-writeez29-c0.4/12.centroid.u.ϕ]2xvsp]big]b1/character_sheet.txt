Characters:
- Lucky: An ancient being, eternally young, protagonist. Opted not to change form.
- Astra: An ancient being, eternally young, protagonist, Lucky's companion. Opted not to change form.
- Professor Pylorus: Wise mentor, teaches about Soul Bank, invites to Dustopia. Has silver hair and kind eyes.
- Professor Aurora: Teaches Creation Class, eyes like the morning sun.
- Piper: Fellow student, initially struggles, finds her footing. Gentle soul with hair that flows like the wind.
- Atlas: Fellow student, white hair, eyes like stars. Appears as a teenager despite being ancient.
- Zephyr: Proprietor of "Mystic's Muse" in Diagonal Alley. Eccentric individual with wild gray hair and piercing green eyes.
- Lyra: Cartographer of the Multiverse in Diagonal Alley. Enigmatic individual with eyes that burned like stars.
- Maria Blochroachez: Shy but talented young artist, winner of Tear It Card competition.
- Supreme Emperor Sol: Leader of Metal Sun.
- Lucky's Family: Wives, concubines, friends, clones, clowns, Luna (all brought back from Metal Sun).
