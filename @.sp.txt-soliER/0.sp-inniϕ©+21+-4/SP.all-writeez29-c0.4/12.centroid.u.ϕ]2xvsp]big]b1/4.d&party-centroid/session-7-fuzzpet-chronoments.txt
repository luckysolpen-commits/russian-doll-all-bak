**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** The week after the Dustopedia blurred into a rhythm of classes, Stardust Ball practice, and late-night Chimochio sessions. Temporal combat. Using Time Fu techniques.

**LUCKY:** *smiling* Time twins. We both invented it. At the same time. In different timelines. *he shrugs* It's what we do.

Behind her, a cage of chrono dust pets waited — tiny, rare fuzzpets with fur the color of twilight and eyes that ticked like clocks. They chirped in temporal frequencies, sensing shifts in time before they happened.

**PROFESSOR PYLORUS:** Welcome to Fuzzpet Chronoments. Today, you'll learn to manipulate time through fuzzpets. Each of you will bond with a chrono dust pet — a companion that senses temporal shifts before they occur. You'll create temporal bubbles, slow and accelerate time in small areas, and practice Time Fu techniques. *she pauses* And you'll spar. Temporal combat. Using the techniques you've learned. Begin.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** The 4-party each took a chrono dust pet. CHARA's pet ticked rapidly, its fur shifting from midnight blue to crimson. It sensed danger before it happened. IQabella's pet ticked softly, its fur glowing like dawn. It sensed warmth before it arrived. Horli's pet ticked in rhythm, its fur shifting colors like a disco ball. It sensed beats before they dropped. And Sol's pet ticked erratically, its fur flickering between gold and black. It sensed... something. Something unstable. Something unraveling.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *watching his chrono dust pet* It's ticking wrong. It's sensing... instability. *he looks at his hand. It flickers again. He hides it.*

**LUCKY:** *watching Sol, saying nothing* ...

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Professor Pylorus led them to the temporal training arena — a flexbox container where time flowed at different speeds in different zones. The main axis moved forward at normal speed. The cross axis moved backward. The corners accelerated. The center slowed. It was a temporal maze. And they had to navigate it using their chrono dust pets.

**PROFESSOR PYLORUS:** Your chrono dust pet will sense temporal shifts before they occur. Follow its frequency. Create temporal bubbles to protect yourself. Slow time in dangerous zones. Accelerate time in safe zones. And when you reach the center — spar. Use Time Fu. Fight with time.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** CHARA went first. Her chrono dust pet ticked rapidly, sensing danger in the accelerated corners. She created a temporal bubble — a sphere of slowed time — and moved through the maze with tactical precision. She reached the center in record time.

**CHARA:** *in the center* Temporal bubble stable. Time slowed by 0.618x. Ready to spar. *her chrono dust pet ticks in a combat frequency*

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** IQabella went next. Her chrono dust pet ticked softly, sensing warmth in the safe zones. She created a temporal garden — a zone of accelerated time where flowers bloomed and wilted in seconds. She moved through the maze with gentle precision. She reached the center with a tray of tea — 175 degrees, for focus.

**IQABELLA:** *in the center* Temporal garden stable. Time accelerated by 1.618x. Ready to spar. *her chrono dust pet ticks in a calming frequency*

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Horli went third. Her chrono dust pet ticked in rhythm, sensing beats in every zone. She created a temporal dance floor — a zone where time moved to the beat of her music. **HORLI:** *in the center* Temporal dance floor stable. Time moves to the beat. Ready to spar. *her chrono dust pet spins on the turntable*

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Sol went fourth. His chrono dust pet ticked erratically, sensing instability everywhere. He created a temporal bridge — a zone where time flowed in Golden Ratio patterns, connecting the main axis to the cross axis. He moved through the maze with mathematical precision. He reached the center with equations covering every surface.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *in the center* Temporal bridge stable. Time flows at φ-frequency. Ready to spar. *his chrono dust pet ticks erratically*

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Lucky went last. His chrono dust pet ticked in perfect harmony with Sol's — the same frequency, the same resonance. He created a temporal loop — a zone where time folded back on itself, creating a self-sustaining cycle. He moved through the maze with casual precision. He reached the center with a smile.

**LUCKY:** *in the center* Temporal loop stable. Time folds at φ-squared. Ready to spar. *his gold spiral dust pet chirps in harmony with Sol's*

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Professor Pylorus activated the sparring protocol. The temporal arena shifted. The zones became combat zones. The temporal bubbles became shields. The temporal bridges became weapons. And the sparring began.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** CHARA sparred with IQabella — tactical precision vs. gentle precision. CHARA's temporal bubbles blocked IQabella's temporal garden. IQabella's tea calmed CHARA's chrono dust pet. They drew.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Horli sparred with Lucky — chaotic precision vs. casual precision. Horli's temporal dance floor disrupted Lucky's temporal loop. Lucky's φ-squared resonance stabilized Horli's beat. They drew.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** And then Sol challenged Lucky.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *aggressively* Lucky. Sparring match. Now.

**LUCKY:** *confused* Uh. Sure. You okay, man?

**SOL-SUPREME:** *not answering* Begin.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Sol attacked first. He used advanced Time Fu techniques — temporal acceleration, temporal deceleration, temporal displacement. He pushed hard, fast, relentless. Lucky defended — casual, defensive, not trying to win. He parried Sol's attacks with temporal loops, deflected his strikes with φ-squared resonance, and held his ground.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *pushing harder* Fight back! Use your Time Fu! Use your SoulPen! Use something!

**LUCKY:** *still defensive* Sol, what's going on? Why are you so intense?

**SOL-SUPREME:** *using advanced techniques* Because you're always ahead of me! You always know what's coming! You always have the answer! And I don't know why! *his hand flickers — a full second of dissolution* I don't know why!

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Lucky saw the flicker. He saw the desperation. He saw the fear. And he understood. Sol wasn't fighting him. Sol was fighting the unknown. The time twin connection. The thing he couldn't calculate, couldn't solve, couldn't control.

**LUCKY:** *softly* Sol. Stop. We're time twins. We're the same. You don't have to fight me.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *pushing harder* Yes I do! Because if I don't, I'll never know why you're always ahead! Why you always know! Why you always— *his hand flickers again. Longer this time. Two seconds.*

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Professor Pylorus intervened. She stepped into the arena, her silver hair catching the temporal light, her robes shifting between deep blue and violet. She placed a hand on Sol's shoulder.

**PROFESSOR PYLORUS:** Sol. You're fighting the wrong battle.

**SOL-SUPREME:** *breathing hard* I'm fighting the only battle that matters. I need to know. I need to understand. I need to—

**PROFESSOR PYLORUS:** You will. Eventually. But not today. Today, you rest. Today, you accept that some things can't be calculated. *she looks at his flickering hand* Some things can only be felt.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Sol stormed out of the arena. His chrono dust pet followed, ticking erratically. Lucky watched him go, his gold spiral dust pet chirping in a worried frequency.

**LUCKY:** *quietly* He's getting worse. The flickering. It's getting worse.

**PROFESSOR PYLORUS:** *quietly* I know. The time twin connection. It's destabilizing his temporal signature. Every hour, another piece of him flickers. *she pauses* He'll need the temporal body suit soon. Or he'll dissolve completely.

**LUCKY:** *nodding* I know. I'm working on it. *his gold spiral dust pet chirps in agreement* I need to go back in time. To fix this. To save him. To save all of us.

**PROFESSOR PYLORUS:** *nodding* You will. But not today. Today, you rest. Today, you prepare. Today, you accept that some things can't be fixed yet. *she smiles* Tomorrow, you fight the finals. Tomorrow, you win. Tomorrow, you prepare for the impossible.

**MOTHER TMOJIZU:** Lucky nodded. He walked out of the arena, his gold spiral dust pet chirping softly. And Sol, for the first time, didn't push him away.

They know something's coming. *her chrono dust pet ticks in a warning frequency* I need to tell someone. But who?
