**Chapter 1: Fresh Start**

Lauren's eyes fluttered open, the bright red hue of her hair the first thing she 
noticed. She rubbed her temples, trying to shake off the haze. A faint hum filled the 
air, like a gentle buzz in her mind. Where was she? The last thing she remembered 
was... nothing. No memories, no name, no past.

Lauren sat up, taking stock of her surroundings. She was in a small, Spartan room with
gray walls and a single, flickering light above. A small table held a simple, 
utilitarian meal: a protein pack, some water, and a packet of stimulants. The air 
reeked of disinfectant.

A small, discreet console on the wall beeped softly, flashing a message in a language 
Lauren didn't understand. She reached out to touch it, but hesitated. This might be 
important information, but she had no context to decipher it.

As she pondered, a gentle knock at the door interrupted her thoughts. The room's sole 
occupant, a soft-spoken woman with a kind face and a badge reading "Astra," entered 
the room.

"Good morning, Lauren," Astra said, her voice warm. "I'm your... escort, I suppose. 
You're on Mars, now. We'll be getting you settled in shortly."

Lauren's brow furrowed. "Mars? What do you mean?"

Astra smiled gently. "You'll find out soon enough. Just for now, let's focus on 
getting you acclimated to your new life. There's a lot to learn, and I'm here to help 
you through it."

Lauren nodded, though her mind remained foggy. Who was she? What had happened to her 
before this... start?

"Follow me," Astra said, already turning towards the door.

As Lauren stood up, the room's hum died away, leaving only an unsettling silence. She 
followed Astra out into a bright, crimson-hued world...
