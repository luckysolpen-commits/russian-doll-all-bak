INT. AVANTA.C - NORTHERN MOUNTAINS - ELDRADOR RUINS - DAY

The air grows colder, thinner, as Lucky, Astra, and Kael ascend the rugged, snow-dusted slopes of the northern mountains. Jagged peaks pierce a sky the color of bruised plums. The path is treacherous, strewn with loose rocks and patches of ice. A biting wind WHISTLES through the crags.

They finally reach the ancient ruins of ELDRADOR. It's a desolate, haunting place. Crumbling stone structures, half-buried in snow and overgrown with skeletal trees, cling to the mountainside. The silence here is profound, an eerie absence of sound that makes the hairs on Lucky's neck stand on end. It feels like the land itself is holding its breath.

LUCKY
(Voice low)
This place gives me the creeps.

ASTRA
(Her hand on her wand, eyes scanning the shadows)
Ancient magic. And not the friendly kind. Stay alert.

Suddenly, a low GROWL echoes from the gnarled trees ahead. Then another, and another. From the shadows, a pack of snarling WOLVES emerges, their eyes glowing with an unnatural, predatory light. They are larger than normal, their fur matted and their teeth long and sharp. They move with a chilling coordination, fanning out to surround the trio.

KAEL
(Drawing a gleaming, ornate SWORD from beneath his robes with surprising speed)
Looks like our welcoming committee has arrived.

**COMBAT INITIATED!**

The wolves lunge. Kael moves with a fluid grace Lucky hadn't expected, his sword a blur as he parries the first wolf's attack, then thrusts, sending it yelping back.

LUCKY (V.O.)
/ability ThunderousFury

Lucky charges, his Blade-Shield of Valor glowing. He slams into the lead wolf, sending it sprawling. He follows up with a series of quick, precise strikes, forcing the pack to keep their distance.

ASTRA (V.O.)
/spell ArcaneBlast

Astra raises her upgraded Spellbook of Ancient Shadows. A swirling vortex of purple energy erupts from her hands, expanding rapidly and engulfing several wolves. They howl in pain as the arcane energy tears at them, some dissolving into shimmering pixels.

Kael is a whirlwind of motion, his sword flashing. He moves with an efficiency that speaks of countless battles, dispatching wolves with brutal precision. He's not just a merchant; he's a highly skilled combatant.

LUCKY
(Dodging a lunge, then striking)
He's good!

ASTRA
(Casting another spell)
Too good for a merchant!

After a fierce, short battle, the last wolf falls. The silence returns, broken only by their ragged breathing. The ground is littered with shimmering wolf-pelts and faint, dissipating magical residue.

KAEL
(Wiping his blade clean, his expression unreadable)
Just a hobby. Now, about that expedition.

They press on, deeper into the ruins. Soon, they come upon a clearing, a makeshift CAMPSITE. It's a scene of frantic, sudden abandonment. Tents are ripped, supplies are scattered, and a half-eaten meal sits on a makeshift table, covered in a thin layer of snow.

Lucky spots a series of SCRAPS OF PARCHMENT, pinned to a broken tent pole with a rusty dagger. They're covered in hurried, almost frantic scrawl.

LUCKY
(Picking up a note, his brow furrowed)
Guys, I think we have a problem. The expedition wasn't just lost. They were searching for something... or someone.

Astra kneels, examining the scattered supplies. Her gaze narrows as she picks up a discarded, heavily encrypted data pad.

ASTRA
(Voice grim)
This isn't just a camp. It's a research outpost. And these notes... they're talking about an ancient artifact. Something hidden deep within these ruins. Something powerful.

Kael steps closer, his eyes gleaming with a predatory interest as he scans the notes over Lucky's shoulder.

KAEL
(A low, almost eager tone)
Sounds like we might have stumbled upon a treasure trove... or a tomb. Let's hope it's the former.

FADE OUT.
