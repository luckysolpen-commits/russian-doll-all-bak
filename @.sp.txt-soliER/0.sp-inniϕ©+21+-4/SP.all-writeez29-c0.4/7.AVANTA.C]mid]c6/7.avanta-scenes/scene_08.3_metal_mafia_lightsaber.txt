INT. AVANTA.C - ILLEGAL FURRY SERVER - DARK ALLEY NETWORK - NIGHT

After their victory in the Beat Saber arena, Lucky (duck form) and Astra (swan form) are approached by three mysterious figures emerging from the shadows. The figures are clearly members of the METAL MAFIA, recognizable by their metallic, armored avatars and glowing red accents.

METAL MAFIA LEADER
(A metallic, synthesized voice)
Well, well... what have we here? Two upstarts thinking they can make names for themselves in our territory?

LUCKY
(Duck form tensing up)
We're not looking for trouble.

METAL MAFIA LEADER
(Chuckling darkly)
Trouble found you. Word's already spread about your Beat Saber victory. And we don't take kindly to outsiders trying to make a name for themselves without paying the proper... respects.

ASTRA
(Swan neck extended, defensive stance)
We didn't know this was your territory. We're just trying to survive and level up.

METAL MAFIA LEADER
Then let's see what you're really made of. A little lightsaber demonstration to test your worth.

The alley transforms into a virtual ARENA with a metallic, industrial design. The walls gleam with chrome and neon. Three lightsabers materialize on the ground - one blue, one red, and one purple.

METAL MAFIA LEADER (CONT.)
First one to disarm their opponent wins. Loser either pays tribute to the Metal Mafia... or gets their account banned from the legal servers permanently.

LUCKY
(Picking up the blue lightsaber)
I've seen some of these in the old movies. How hard can it be?

ASTRA
(Selecting the purple lightsaber)
Just remember what we learned in the Beat Saber arena. Rhythm, timing, and coordination.

The three Metal Mafia members activate their red lightsabers. The duel begins with an intense clash of blades.

METAL MAFIA MEMBER 1
(lunging forward with a wide arc)
Think you can hack your way through this, amateur?

LUCKY
(Duck form surprisingly agile)
/ability DuckDodgeParry

Lucky blocks the attack with his lightsaber but uses his webbed feet for enhanced stability. He moves with surprising grace for his new form.

ASTRA
(Gliding between attacks with her swan agility)
/spell LightShield

Astra creates a protective barrier of light that deflects attacks while she strategizes. Her enhanced speed from her swan form gives her an edge in evasion.

METAL MAFIA MEMBER 2
(Attacking Astra from behind)
Got you now, swan!

ASTRA
(Flipping away with elegant movement)
Not so fast!

She counters with a precise strike that disarms the member temporarily.

LUCKY
(Seeing an opening)
Time to end this!

Lucky combines his duck form's stability with his newfound saber skills, engaging in a fierce duel with the leader. Sparks fly as the blue and red sabers clash repeatedly.

The battle intensifies. Despite being outnumbered, Lucky and Astra's teamwork from the Beat Saber battles gives them the edge. They fight back-to-back, covering each other's weaknesses.

LUCKY
(To Astra, during the battle)
On my mark, we use the combo we practiced!

ASTRA
(Nodding gracefully)
Now!

They execute a synchronized attack - Astra creates a light distraction while Lucky flanks and disarms the Metal Mafia Leader.

METAL MAFIA LEADER
(Lowered saber, grudging respect)
Impressive. You've got skills. And you work well as a team. The Metal Mafia remembers this. Don't make us your enemy, but don't expect us to make this easy for you either.

The three Metal Mafia members retreat into the shadows, leaving Lucky and Astra standing victorious in the arena.

LUCKY
(Duck form breathing heavily)
That was... intense.

ASTRA
(Swan form nodding)
We're definitely making a name for ourselves. But it sounds like we're on their radar now.

FADE OUT.
