INT. AVANTA.C - MISSION BOARD - DAY

Lucky, Astra, and Kael stand before a massive, glowing MISSION BOARD in the town square. It's a dynamic, holographic display, constantly updating with new quests, bounties, and community announcements. Players mill about, scanning the offerings, some excitedly accepting quests, others grumbling about low rewards.

Kael, with an air of casual confidence, gestures towards a prominent listing that pulses with a faint, urgent red light.

KAEL
(Smoothly)
Ah, here we are. "The Lost Expedition." A rather intriguing proposition, wouldn't you agree? High risk, but potentially even higher reward.

Lucky and Astra exchange a glance. Kael had clearly been tracking their interests, or perhaps even influencing the board's offerings. They maintain their facade of casual interest.

LUCKY
(Reading the holographic text)
"Rumors circulated about an expedition that vanished while exploring the nearby mountains..."

ASTRA
(Finishing the sentence, her voice flat)
"...The board offered a substantial reward to anyone who could uncover what happened to the missing explorers and retrieve any valuable artifacts they might have left behind."

Kael's smile is unwavering.

KAEL
Precisely. A classic tale of adventure and mystery. And given your... demonstrated capabilities, I thought it might be a perfect fit for our burgeoning partnership.

Lucky taps the holographic display, bringing up the detailed mission briefing. The text expands, revealing more information.

**MISSION BRIEFING: THE LOST EXPEDITION**

**OBJECTIVE:** Retrieve any valuable artifacts or clues from the lost expedition's campsite. Ascertain the fate of the missing explorers.

**LOCATION:** The expedition's last known location is in the northern mountains, near the ancient ruins of **Eldrador**. (A holographic map of a rugged, mountainous region with a glowing red marker appears).

**HAZARDS:** Reports indicate that the area is infested with giant spiders and packs of aggressive wolves. (Images of monstrous spiders and snarling wolves flash briefly on the screen).

**REWARD:** 5,000 Gold Coins, 2,500 Experience Points (each), and a rare, unidentified artifact upon successful completion.

Lucky and Astra absorb the information. The hazards are significant, but the reward is substantial, especially the promise of a rare artifact. More importantly, this quest offers a chance to observe Kael in action, to see if his actions align with his smooth words.

LUCKY
(Looking at Astra, a silent question in his eyes)
What do you think?

Astra gives a subtle, almost imperceptible nod. The risk is worth the potential intel on Kael.

ASTRA
(To Kael, a hint of challenge in her voice)
Sounds like a job for us. But you'll be pulling your weight, Kael. We don't carry dead weight.

KAEL
(A theatrical bow)
My dear Astra, you wound me. My skills are at your disposal. Consider me your humble, yet highly effective, support.

He accepts the quest on the board. A confirmation chime sounds, and the red light on the listing turns green, indicating it's been taken.

Lucky, Astra, and Kael turn and head towards the northern gate of the city, the holographic map of Eldrador's treacherous mountains already forming in their minds. They are ready to face whatever dangers lie ahead, but the unspoken question hangs heavy in the air: What was Kael's true motive for joining them? And what secrets might the Lost Expedition truly hold?

FADE OUT.
