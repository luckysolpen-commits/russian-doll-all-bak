## AI Use Cases (By Priority)

### Tier 1: Immediate (Phase 0-1)

#### 1. AI Quest Matching
- **What:** Quest board app uses AI to match quest posters with quest takers.
- **How:** NLP analyzes quest descriptions. Matches based on skills, interests, availability.
- **Value:** Increases quest completion rate. Makes the quest board actually useful.
- **Build:** Fine-tune open-source LLM (Llama, Mistral) on quest data.
- **Cost:** $500-2,000/mo (cloud compute, API calls).

#### 2. AI Session Assistant (DM Tool)
- **What:** AI helps DMs run TPMOJIO, D&D, and other sessions.
- **How:** Real-time rules lookup, NPC voice suggestions, plot hook generation, session summarization.
- **Value:** Lowers barrier to entry for new DMs. Makes sessions better for everyone.
- **Build:** RAG system over TPMOJIO rules, session transcripts, lore documents.
- **Cost:** $1,000-3,000/mo (compute, storage).

#### 3. AI Content Generation
- **What:** AI generates session transcripts, character art, lore content, social media posts.
- **How:** Fine-tuned models on existing TPMOJIO content. Human review before publishing.
- **Value:** 10x content output. Consistent brand voice.
- **Build:** Fine-tune LLM on session transcripts. Use Stable Diffusion/Flux for art.
- **Cost:** $500-1,500/mo (compute, API calls).

#### 4. AI Customer Service
- **What:** AI chatbot handles FAQs: hours, menu, membership, events, quest board.
- **How:** RAG over cafe knowledge base. Escalates to human for complex questions.
- **Value:** 24/7 support. Reduces staff burden.
- **Build:** Open-source chatbot (Rasa, Botpress) + LLM API.
- **Cost:** $200-500/mo.

### Tier 2: Medium-Term (Phase 1-2)

#### 5. AI Personalization
- **What:** App recommends quests, events, games, and products based on user behavior.
- **How:** Collaborative filtering + content-based recommendations.
- **Value:** Increases engagement, membership retention, retail sales.
- **Build:** Standard recommendation engine. Start simple, iterate.
- **Cost:** $500-1,500/mo.

#### 6. AI Inventory Management
- **What:** AI predicts food/beverage demand, retail stock needs, merch trends.
- **How:** Time series forecasting on sales data. Seasonal adjustments. Event-based predictions.
- **Value:** Reduces waste. Prevents stockouts. Optimizes ordering.
- **Build:** Standard forecasting models (Prophet, ARIMA) + custom adjustments.
- **Cost:** $300-800/mo.

#### 7. AI Event Planning
- **What:** AI suggests optimal event types, times, and pricing based on historical data.
- **How:** Analyzes attendance, revenue, engagement data. Suggests improvements.
- **Value:** Maximizes event revenue. Reduces planning time.
- **Build:** Analytics dashboard + recommendation engine.
- **Cost:** $500-1,000/mo.

#### 8. AI Streaming Assistant
- **What:** AI helps streamers at the cafe: auto-captions, scene switching, chat moderation, highlight detection.
- **How:** Computer vision + NLP + audio processing.
- **Value:** Makes streaming accessible to beginners. Improves quality for pros.
- **Build:** Integrate existing tools (OBS plugins, Whisper, moderation bots).
- **Cost:** $500-1,500/mo.

### Tier 3: Long-Term (Phase 3-4)

#### 9. AI NPC (In-Store Experience)
- **What:** Physical AI character in the cafe that interacts with customers.
- **How:** Robot/avatar + LLM + voice synthesis. Knows TPMOJIO lore, cafe menu, quest board.
- **Value:** Unique experience. Draws visitors. Generates social media content.
- **Build:** Robot hardware + LLM + voice cloning. Start simple (screen-based), evolve to physical.
- **Cost:** $5,000-20,000 (hardware) + $1,000-3,000/mo (compute).

#### 10. AI Lore Engine
- **What:** AI generates new TPMOJIO sessions, Clone Chronicles episodes, HARD_VVAR battles — consistent with existing lore.
- **How:** Fine-tuned LLM on all session transcripts, lore documents, character profiles.
- **Value:** Infinite content. Fans can generate their own sessions. New revenue stream.
- **Build:** Fine-tune large model. Human curation layer. Community voting on best outputs.
- **Cost:** $2,000-5,000/mo (compute, storage).

#### 11. AI Community Moderator
- **What:** AI moderates Discord, quest board, social media. Detects toxicity, spam, harassment.
- **How:** NLP classification + human review queue.
- **Value:** Safe community. Reduced moderation burden.
- **Build:** Open-source moderation tools + custom fine-tuning.
- **Cost:** $300-800/mo.

#### 12. AI Business Intelligence
- **What:** AI analyzes all business data and provides actionable insights.
- **How:** Natural language queries over business data. Automated anomaly detection. Predictive analytics.
- **Value:** Better decisions. Faster responses. Competitive advantage.
- **Build:** Data warehouse + BI tool + LLM interface.
- **Cost:** $1,000-3,000/mo.

## AI Ethics & Guardrails

### Principles:
1. **AI enhances, never replaces, human connection.** The cafe is about people. AI is the tool, not the product.
2. **Transparency.** Customers know when they're interacting with AI. No deception.
3. **Human review.** AI-generated content is reviewed before publishing. AI recommendations are suggestions, not decisions.
4. **Data privacy.** Customer data is never sold. AI models are trained on anonymized, aggregated data.
5. **Open source.** AI tools we build are open source. Community can audit, improve, fork.

### Red Lines:
- **No AI replacing staff.** AI augments staff. It doesn't replace them.
- **No AI-generated food.** AI can predict demand. It doesn't make the coffee.
- **No AI surveillance.** Cameras are for security, not behavior tracking.
- **No AI manipulation.** AI doesn't manipulate pricing, recommendations, or quest matching for profit maximization. It optimizes for community value.
