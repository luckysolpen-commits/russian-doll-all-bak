# TPMOJIO CAFE — RP SIMULATION SESSION 2: "THE FIRST MEETING"
## Phase 0: Pre-Launch — "Four People, Zero Dollars, One Plan"
### Players: Sol-Supreme (as Lucky), IQabella, Chara, Horli Qwin | DM (Mother Tmojizu)
### Setting: A free Zoom call. Four broke dreamers. One shared document. No money.

---

**Mother Tmojizu:** Welcome back, ducklings. All four of you now. The quest board brought you together. Now you're here — on a Zoom call, because none of you can afford to meet in person. Lucky has $47. IQabella has a Starbucks apron and a dream. Chara has a law degree and a soul that's dying at a corporate firm. Horli Qwin has a microphone, a notebook of bars, and a venue that keeps canceling her open mic nights.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Tonight, you meet. For the first time. As a team. What do you say?

**Sol-Supreme:** [in character] Thank you all for being here. I know none of us have money. I know this sounds crazy. A cafe. With no capital. No space. No experience running a business. But I have the quest board. It's live. Two quests. One of them is this cafe. And you three answered. So... I'm not starting with a building. I'm starting with us.

**IQabella:** [in character] I've been making tea for six years at a place that calls it "artisan" and serves it in paper cups with corporate logos. I know how to source leaves. I know how to train a barista. I know how to make a space feel like home. What I don't know is how to do any of it without a budget. So... what's the plan, Lucky?

**Chara:** [in character] I can handle the legal. Licenses, permits, leases, LLC formation, operating agreements. I can do all of it pro bono. What I can't do is quit my job yet. I have student loans. I have rent. I have a boss who thinks I'm "a good fit for the firm." I'm not. I'm a good fit for a community. But I need to be smart about this.

**Horli Qwin:** [in character] I'll be honest — I showed up because I thought this was gonna be a rap cafe. Like, literally. But the more I read about this quest board thing, the more I'm like... this is bigger than rap. This is community. This is connection. This is the thing I've been looking for my whole life. So I'm in. But I need to know: what do I do *now*? Not when the cafe opens. Now. Tonight. This week.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] Good questions. All of you. Now — Lucky. You're the builder. You posted the quest. What's Phase 0?

**Sol-Supreme:** [in character] Phase 0 is: prove people want this before we spend a dollar. I'm not asking anyone to quit their job. I'm not asking anyone to invest money. I'm asking us to build the community first. The quest board is step one. Step two is... we use it. We post quests. We take quests. We grow the user base. We prove that people will use this thing. And when we have 100 users, 500 users, 1,000 users — then we have leverage. Then we can pre-sell memberships. Then we can find a location. Then we can build the cafe. But not before.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Strategy. You're laying out a bootstrap plan with no budget.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 17. Plus 4 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. Lucky pulls up a shared document. He types:

```
PHASE 0: PRE-LAUNCH (Months 1-6)
Goal: 1,000 quest board users. 100 cafe membership pre-signups. $0 spent.

Lucky: Build quest board features. Add DM-player matching. Calendar. Attendance.
IQabella: Design the cafe menu. Source ingredients. Build a tea/coffee brand.
Chara: Draft LLC paperwork. Research permits. Find low-cost location options.
Horli Qwin: Build audience. Open mics. Social media. Community outreach.

Milestone 1 (Month 2): 100 quest board users.
Milestone 2 (Month 4): 500 quest board users. 50 membership pre-signups.
Milestone 3 (Month 6): 1,000 quest board users. 100 membership pre-signups.
```

**Mother Tmojizu:** He shares it. The four of you read it. Silence. Then:

**IQabella:** [in character] I can do the menu. I'll start a tea blog. Free. I'll review teas, share recipes, build an audience. People who love tea will find the blog. The blog will link to the quest board. The quest board will link to the cafe dream. It's a funnel. Free.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll CHA Content Creation. You're building an audience from nothing.

**IQabella:** [rolls] 19. Plus 3 is 22.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 22. IQabella starts the blog that night. "The Tea Quest." First post: *"Why Starbucks Tea Is a Crime Against Humanity (And What to Do About It)."* It's funny. It's honest. It's passionate. She shares it on Reddit, Instagram, TikTok. Within a week, it has 5,000 views. Within a month, 50,000. People comment: *"Where can I get tea like this?"* She replies: *"Soon. Join the quest board."*

**Chara:** [in character] I'll handle the legal structure. LLC. Operating agreement. IP licensing. I'll make sure the quest board stays open source. I'll make sure the cafe brand is protected. I'll make sure that if this fails, we all walk away clean. And if it succeeds, we all own it equally.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Legal Strategy. You're structuring a company with no money.

**Chara:** [rolls] 20. Plus 4 is 24.

**Mother Tmojizu:** NATURAL 20. Chara drafts the LLC operating agreement that weekend. Four equal members. 25% each. No capital contributions required. Sweat equity only. The quest board is MIT licensed — anyone can use it. The cafe brand is trademarked — only the LLC can use it commercially. The IP is Creative Commons — the community owns it. It's clean. It's fair. It's bulletproof.

**Chara:** [in character] It's done. We're an LLC. Four equal members. No money in. No money out. Just sweat equity. If we fail, we dissolve. If we succeed, we split it four ways. And the community owns the IP. Always.

**Horli Qwin:** [in character] Okay, okay, okay. Y'all are doing the legal and the blog and the code. What am I doing? I'm a rapper. I don't code. I don't do legal. I don't review tea. What's my quest?

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll CHA Community Building. You're figuring out your role.

**Horli Qwin:** [rolls] 18. Plus 3 is 21.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 21. Horli Qwin leans into the camera. She smiles. She says:

**Horli Qwin:** [in character] I'm the voice. I'm the energy. I'm the one who fills the room. I'm starting a podcast. "The Quest Board Podcast." Every week, I interview someone who posted a quest. The library DM. The barista. The lawyer. The coder. The cosplayer. The kid who found their first D&D group because of this board. I'm telling their stories. And every episode ends with the same line: *"The quest board is free. The cafe is coming. Join the quest."*

**Mother Tmojizu:** [smiles] That's your quest. And it's the right one.

**Sol-Supreme:** [in character] I'm building the quest board features. DM-player matching. Calendar. Attendance. I'm making it the best free community tool on the internet. And I'm adding one more thing: a membership pre-signup page. Not a payment page. Just an email. "If we build this cafe, would you join? $15/month. No commitment. Just let us know." I'm putting it on the quest board. I'm putting it on the blog. I'm putting it on the podcast.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Development. You're building the pre-signup system.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 15. Plus 4 is 19.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 19. You build it. Simple. Clean. One field: email address. One button: "Join the Quest." You deploy it. You link it everywhere. The quest board. The blog. The podcast. The Reddit posts. The Instagram bio. The TikTok profile. Everywhere.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The first week: 12 pre-signups.
**Mother Tmojizu:** The second week: 34.
**Mother Tmojizu:** The third week: 67.
**Mother Tmojizu:** The fourth week: 112.

**Mother Tmojizu:** 112 people who said: "If you build this cafe, I'll join."

**Sol-Supreme:** [in character] 112. That's... that's real. That's not a dream anymore. That's 112 people who believe in this.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] It is.

**Mother Tmojizu:** End of Session 2.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The team has a plan. Phase 0. Six months. 1,000 quest board users. 100 membership pre-signups. $0 spent.

**Mother Tmojizu:** The quest board has features: DM-player matching, calendar, attendance tracking.
**Mother Tmojizu:** The blog has 50,000 views.
**Mother Tmojizu:** The podcast is launching.
**Mother Tmojizu:** The LLC is formed. Four equal members. Sweat equity only.
**Mother Tmojizu:** The pre-signup page has 112 emails.

**Mother Tmojizu:** [quietly] Four broke dreamers. One shared document. 112 believers. And a plan.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Did you learn anything, ducklings?

**Sol-Supreme:** [in character] "I learned that you don't need money to build. You need a plan. And a team."

**IQabella:** [in character] "I learned that people are hungry for real tea. And real community."

**Chara:** [in character] "I learned that legal doesn't have to be expensive. It just has to be fair."

**Horli Qwin:** [in character] "I learned that my voice is my superpower. And I'm gonna use it."

**Mother Tmojizu:** [warmly] You did. All of you. Phase 0 has begun. The quest board is growing. The pre-signups are climbing. The cafe is still a dream. But dreams with plans become roadmaps. And roadmaps with execution become reality.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Sleep well, ducklings. The quest board is live. The team is working. And the cafe... the cafe is coming.
