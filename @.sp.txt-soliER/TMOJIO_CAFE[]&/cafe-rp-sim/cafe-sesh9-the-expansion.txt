Revenue shares with cafe. Network nodes in every room. Each hotel room is a node. 20 rooms = 20 new nodes per location.

**Mother Tmojizu:** Roll INT Hospitality Architecture. You're building a hotel for night-owl builders.

**Sol-Supreme:** [rolls] 19. Plus 4 is 23.
