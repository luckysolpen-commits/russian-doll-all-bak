## Why This Revision Exists

The original sessions used "pre-signups" ambiguously. This was confusing. The distinction matters:

**The software is LIVE.** ~600 active users. 150 nodes. 80K BSR/week flow. Research published. AR battles running. Soul Pen stories publishing. Creator shops transacting. No "pre" about it. It's real. It's now.

**The pre-signups are for CAFE MEMBERSHIP.** These are people who said: *"When you open a physical cafe, I'll join as a paying member ($15/month)."* This is not software validation. This is **community-funded real estate**. 241 people × $15/month = $3,615/month in guaranteed recurring revenue from day one. That covers rent. That covers utilities. That makes the cafe viable before the doors open.

**It is not illegal to have users.** It's open source software. People run nodes. They earn BSR. They spend BSR. They research. They battle. They write. They drink tea. That's it.

**It is not illegal to pre-sell memberships.** It's a commitment. No money changes hands yet. Just an email saying "I'll join when you open." When the cafe opens, they get charged. If the cafe never opens, nothing happens. No risk. No securities. No regulated activity.

This revision corrects the record.
