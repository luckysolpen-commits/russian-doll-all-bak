C. OPERATION DECOUPLING (PAL):

---------------------------
A. HARDCODED ADJACENCY:
   - The 'interact.c' operation has a hardcoded +/- 1 adjacency check.
   - PAL standard recommends that "reach" or "interact_radius" should be a piece state defined in the PDL.
   
B. CONSOLE OVERWRITE (FIXED):
   - Previously, multiple 'console_print' calls in assembly would overwrite each other. 
   - I have fixed this by updating console_print.c to APPEND mode and having playrm_module clear the buffer per keypress.

3. HOW TO REACH 100% COMPLIANCE:
