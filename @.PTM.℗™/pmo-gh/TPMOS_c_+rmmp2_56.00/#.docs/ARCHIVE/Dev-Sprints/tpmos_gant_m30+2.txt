### IMPORTANT NOTE ON ESTIMATE CONSERVATISM

**The 4-person/18-month estimate ($1M-$1.5M) is LIBERAL/OPTIMISTIC.**

This estimate assumes:
- Highly skilled, senior-level developers with minimal ramp-up time
- Efficient agile processes with low overhead
- Minimal bureaucratic friction or decision delays
- "Small startup premium" — team accepts standard market rates for the
  opportunity to work on breakthrough technology
- Low turnover, sustained productivity throughout 18 months

**Reality Check:** For a project of this novelty and complexity, these
assumptions are aggressive. A more CONSERVATIVE/GENEROUS estimate would
apply a 1.5x-2.0x multiplier to account for:
- Learning curve on novel TPM architecture
- Debugging complex cross-platform issues
- Iteration cycles and false starts
- Team ramp-up and onboarding time
- Meeting/coordination overhead at scale

**Generous Estimate Range: $1.5M - $3.0M** (applying 1.5x-2.0x to base)
